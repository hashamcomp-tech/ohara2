The Great Solaris Auction House wasn’t a building, it was a monument.

It dominated the central plaza of the capital’s trade district, a colossal structure of white celestial marble and veins of solid gold.

Towers shaped like sunbursts pierced the sky, and the main entrance was a set of doors thirty feet tall, carved from a single piece of fire-opal that shimmered with captured daylight.

The plaza around it teemed with life. Cultivators in the colors of a dozen different sects and clans mingled with merchants in rich silks and guards in polished armor.

The air thrummed with conversation, the clink of spirit stones, and the low, powerful hum of countless restraining formations.

Mei led them through the crowd like a queen parting waves. People instinctively moved aside, their eyes widening as they took in her robes, her aura, the undeniable air of absolute power that radiated from her. Whispers followed in their arrival.

"The Azure Dragon Sect’s Mei Nightrider..."

"A Saint Realm expert here in person?"
