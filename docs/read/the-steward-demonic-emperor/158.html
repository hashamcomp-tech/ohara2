Chapter 158, Unstoppable Speed

Sticking out his chest, Lin Zitian felt so full of himself being the center of attention.

What he didn’t see was the suspicion in their eyes.

“5th elder, if Elder Lin wishes to fight him, let us owe him this favor and we’ll repay it back in the future.” Squinting, Huangpu Qingyun no longer second-guessed it and agreed with Lin Zitian.

Lin Zitian cupped his hands to Huangpu Qingyun in joy, “I wouldn’t dare. This is something I should be doing anyway. For the second young master to grant me this honor is enough.”

Huangpu Qingyun’s words revealed how much importance he gave himself. Lin Zitian was overjoyed Merry Woods now had a powerful backer.

“But second young master...” 5th elder spoke with revulsion. Though his face had an understanding smile when turned to Huangpu Qingyun.

As if the second young master cared about this pawn called Lin Zitian! He was only used as bait to see what other tricks Zhuo Fan had up his sleeves.

At the least, they’d make some early plans on how to deal with them.

Snickering inside, 5th elder cupped his hands at Lin Zitian, “Elder Lin, thank you for your trouble.”

“You’re too kind, 5th elder.”

Lin Zitian’s face had a glittering smile plastered on it. Like the bottom of the barrel got hailed as the number one genius. While Zhuo Fan could barely contain his mockery.

[There’s no worse fool out there than a clueless lab rat!]

[Fighting such a moron is a total waste of time.]

Zhuo Fan didn’t waste words and capitalized on their inattention by jumping backward. His Lightning Wings were primed into sharpened daggers to break out of this encirclement.

This time he wasn’t aiming for the air, but the wall behind him.

As the wall was the closest to him, his action would leave the other unable to react. This would ultimately allow him to escape Drifting Flowers Arena and take to the skies.

He would be soon as free as a bird, in his own element. While also offsetting the three’s Profound Heaven speed from catching up.

Huangpu Qingyun shouted in alarm, “Damn it! He wants to run!”

He cursed Zhuo Fan inside for his cunning nature. One careless moment and he would slip away!

By the time he and 5th elder got to the hole in the wall, Zhou Fan had the advantage of the open sky outside.

“Ha-ha-ha, Huangpu Qingyun, old 5th, we will meet again if fate allows it.” Zhuo Fan sped up and left his laughter behind.

Huangpu Qingyun and 5th elder gnashed their teeth but didn’t pursue.

They knew it was too late already.

But Zhuo Fan once again omitted Lin Zitian’s name.

It was clear disregard!

Lin Zitian’s grew irate, [This prick thinks of escaping with me from Merry Woods here? Absurd!]

Whoosh!

Lin Zitian’s figure vanished. Not even Huangpu Qingyun and 5th elder who were the closest, noticed his movements.

The next thing everyone knew, he was behind Zhuo Fan and cutting off the latter’s retreat.

So fast!

Zhuo Fan was startled somewhat. Lin Zitian was in the Profound Heaven Stage but his speed was far above it.

[He isn’t an elder of Merry Woods for nothing. Although...]

[Since when is speed everything? You’re nothing more than a paper tiger to me.]

Grinning, Zhuo Fan didn’t slow down at all, going straight for Elder Lin.

This had the elder fuming with rage.

“You little shit, you will pay for your insolence!” Like an enraged lion, Lin Zitian roared and charged as well.

But Zhuo Fan wasn’t one to mind, mocking him in fact as his Lightning Wings were sharpened for impact, “Your funeral!”

The clash already had a conclusion in everyone’s minds.

Zhuo Fan was a monster yet only a Bone Tempering cultivator. He could face off against the impressive 5th layer of Profound Heaven body cultivator, Hell Valley’s 5th elder by himself. Not to mention he killed You Guiqi in one hit.

[You, Lin Zitian, are no body cultivator, even weaker than You Guiqi. Yet, you dare face such a freak? Truly a moron.]

“Let’s go together, 5th elder!” Huangpu Qingyun spoke.

Startled, 5th elder didn’t understand, “Second young master, we won’t be able to save him even if we do join.”

“Fool, what saving? He might not last a hit, but as a Profound Heaven expert he can delay that brat long enough for us to catch up!”

Huangpu Qingyun already bolted with the 5th elder laughing right behind him.

Zhuo Fan had long guessed their plan and his mouth perked into a peculiar smile.

Whoosh~

Just when they were about to clash, Zhuo Fan used Mystifying Phantom Step again, only to appear behind Lin Zitian.

“What?!”

Huangpu Qingyun and 5th elder stopped in shock. Zhuo Fan’s cackle came from behind Lin Zitian, “Ha-ha-ha, your plan was for me to waste time on this idiot, right? You counted your chickens too soon!”

“Damn it, he figured it out!” Huangpu Qingyun scowled.

Long Jiu’s group now realized as well. Lin Zitian wasn’t there to attack Zhuo Fan, but to stall him enough for the other two to catch up.

It was clearly out of Lin Zitian’s intentions, ending up used by Huangpu Qingyun.

Regent Estate was indeed an expert in manipulation. But Zhuo Fan saw through it with a glance, hailing his skills a cut higher.

In this three against one battle, they did not fight with only their fists but with their minds. If one side lost either aspect, defeat was imminent.

Long Jiu admired Zhuo Fan even greater now. If it were him in Zhuo Fan’s shoes, he would’ve attacked Lin Zitian and fell in Huangpu Qingyun’s trap.

But now the situation resolved and Zhuo Fan was free to leave.

However, before anyone could breathe a sigh of relief, Lin Zitian punched Zhuo Fan as he roared, “Punk, you dare look down on me?”

“Humph, you seriously don’t know when to quit. Since you want death, I shall oblige!”

Finding Huangpu Qingyun and 5th elder stock still from the corner of his eye, Zhuo Fan grinned. Killing intent flared in his eyes and thrust at Lin Zitian.

Huangpu Qingyun was filled with regret.

Who knew Lin Zitian was so thickheaded, still in pursuit? If they knew, they wouldn’t have stopped. Now, however, it was too late to go after him again. All they could do was watch Lin Zitian throw away his life for nothing.

Lin Zitian didn’t seem afraid of the 6th-grade demonic treasure coming for his life. He even had time to mock, “Brat, don’t you know power is useless when you can’t even land a hit?”

Zhuo Fan didn’t seem to catch his drift. But then Lin Zitian vanished half a meter away from him and the Lightning Wings hit naught but air.

“How?!”

Zhuo Fan was in shock. It was impossible to move so fast without build-up. Only his Mystifying Phantom Step could do it, allowing him to blink short distances.

What he failed to detect, however, was the ripple in space. This was a burst over short distances.

But this burst was so fast that not even a Profound Heaven expert’s speed was its match.

In his shock, Lin Zitian appeared again with a mocking smile as he punched Zhuo Fan’s chest. With Lightning Wings in mid-attack, Zhuo Fan could only use his arm to block.

“Humph, regardless of how fast Merry Woods is, your power is nothing. With such childish punches, not even a hundred will help. Can one man’s punch make any difference?”

“He-he-he, ever heard of constant efforts always prevail?”

Snorting, Lin Zitian mocked Zhuo Fan but his heart was furious as he added more power into his punch. But Zhuo Fan didn’t care, as Lin Zitian was indeed punching like a child. The most he could do was add his Yuan Qi on his punch to rattle Zhuo Fan’s organs.

Zhuo Fan was flabbergasted to discover this paper tiger sent him flying. He planted into a rock and ended up buried under the rubble.

This astonished everyone around, making them reassess Elder Lin. To send the freakish Zhuo Fan flying earned him the title of peak expert in the Profound Heaven Stage.

Some couldn’t even believe the opportunist Lin Zitian had such power in him.

Even Huangpu Qingyun and 5th elder were rooted in shock.

Rumble!

Zhuo Fan jumped out of the rubble, unharmed, but his arm looked inflamed.

Raising his eyes on Lin Zitian, Zhuo Fan was now serious, “It seems I’ve underestimated you, Elder Lin!”

“Humph, you better believe it! We, the Merry Woods, have become one of the seven houses thanks to our strength!” Lin Zitian put away his fist as his eyes flashed with pride.

All around looked at Lin Zitian with newfound respect. His figure never looked so imposing.

Despite all that, Lin Zitian sneakily put his hand behind him and rubbed the pain away as he sucked a cold breath.

His hand was purple...
