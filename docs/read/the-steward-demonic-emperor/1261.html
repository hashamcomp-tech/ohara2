Chapter 1260, Surrounded

Translator: StarReader

Editor: CutieBinkie

Proofreader: Papatonks

A month later, inside Devil Mountain, Zhuo Fan came before a stone door, “Demonic Emperor sir, we’ve taken three cities and 2,350 prisoners.”

“Send them in already!”

A gruff voice echoed, followed by loud booms as the stone door rumbled open.

Zhuo Fan walked in. Zhao Chen was panting like a beast, his eyes bloodshot from madness, as he roared, “Where are they? Give them to me!”

Whoosh~

People appeared in the dark room out of nowhere, covering the floor.

Zhao Chen’s eyes were frenzied, setting his black energy on them like a tide to consume them.

He was too deep into his madness and thirst to cultivate to notice Zhuo Fan smirking as he left.

His own aide didn’t even say goodbye.

“Corpse explosion!”

A cold shout boomed and the black energy released a terrifying ripple.

Zhao Chen jumped to his feet, slow to react.

Boom~

The explosions were endless, all over the black energy, sending deadly shockwaves all around. The blasts were so strong they flattened the top of Devil Mountain.

Thick dust spread around, swallowing all the eerie, yet lavish, buildings below.

Whoosh~

The wind whistled as someone moved through the thick dust, landing on the ground and soaking it with his blood.

“The Wraith Emperor!”

Zhao Chen raised his sinister face, his eyes bleeding, and roared, “Wraith Emperor, I’m going to pulverize you for what you did!”

“Ha-ha-ha, but what about the others?”

A cackle responded as five figures landed before him, the other five emperors.

Zhao Chen’s eyes flashed staring hard at them, “So decrepit fools like you ganged up just so to play me? I should’ve known none of you are worth crap. First you ganged up on my master, and now on me!”

“Zhao Chen, did you already forget about your share when we attacked your master? Don’t call us evil when it suits you. You’re no better!”

The Sword Emperor mocked him. The Wraith Emperor cackled, “Zhao Chen, don’t be quick to insult others when you’re just as vile. It’s so poetic, if you think about it. This is karma for everything you did to your master, ha-ha-ha…”

“Karma?”

Zhao Chen exclaimed, “You couldn’t have entered my lands without someone finding out, especially on Devil Mountain. There are countless arrays on it that would react to your coming. What is going on?”

All looked at him with disdain, turning to one side and making Zhao Chen look there as well. He gasped, “You?”

“Yes, it is I, Demonic Emperor sir!”

Zhuo Fan smirked with a bow, “I’m thankful for all your care, in giving me final word over your lands and Devil Mountain so that I could get the Eight Emperors inside. Forgive me.”

Zhao Chen’s face twitched, “Zhuo Fan, you rotten traitor, I’ve given you everything and this is how you repay me, by selling me out? God damn bastard…”

Zhao Chen shook and a powerful aura shot for Zhuo Fan. A huge black palm loomed over him.

Zhuo Fan paled and fled back.

The Sword Emperor intervened, striking with his sword, “Sky Breaker!”

Whoosh!

A blinding light flashed and turned into a three hundred meter sword, clashing with the huge black palm.

Boom!

The sky rumbled under their power, with the Sword Emperor frowning and falling back ten steps and looking at the other in shock.

Zhao Chen shuddered and coughed blood.

“Sword Emperor, how is it?”

The others rushed to his side, looking at Zhao Chen in worry. It was beyond their expectation to find that Zhao Chen was stronger than him, just like Zhuo Yifan.

[A Sovereign’s inheritance is so incredible!]

The Sword Emperor panted, looking grave, “He’s not the same old weak brat. We have to be careful. It’s fortunate we acted in time and gave him heavy wounds or it would be us who’d pay the price later.”

Zhao Chen raged as he glared.

“Zhuo Fan!”

Zhao Chen’s face was covered in blood, “I’ve been nothing but good to you. Why would you sell me out? I’m wounded because you let the Wraith Emperor fiddle with the corpses and trigger the trap as I absorbed them. What’s an ungrateful bastard still doing standing here?”

Zhuo Fan stepped up, “Demonic Emperor sir, everyone has a price. I came under your wing for a career, but as you become stronger and stronger, one day you won’t need me. I’d avoid being expendable. And the Eight Emperors’ terms are quite enticing, making me one of them forever. I had no reason to refuse. So, ha-ha, forgive me.”

“Screw your apology! You’re dead for betraying me!”

Zhao Chen struck with a palm, a black dragon enveloping him. He was going to kill them all, especially Zhuo Fan.

“Steward Zhuo, not to worry. He can’t harm you.”

The Sword Emperor shouted, shielding a sneering Zhuo Fan.

[They said the same thing to Zhao Chen then, but didn’t they still attack him now? Their promise isn’t worth crap.]

The others surrounded Zhuo Fan to shield him, unaware of his thoughts. When Zhao Chen drew closer, they struck, “Zhao Chen, you dare mention others when you betrayed your own master? Compared to Steward Zhuo’s act, yours is heresy. You have no right to speak!”

“I can betray while no one else can betray me!”

Zhao Chen focused all his strength into his palms, making the dragon roar and shoot at them.

“I’ll refine all of you with the Nether Script, ha-ha-ha…”

“You won’t succeed!”

The Sword Emperor released his Imperial Mark, “Decimate, Supreme Sword!”

“Haunt, Elusive Wraith!

“Unite heaven and earth, Enchanting Jasper!”

…

Whoosh~

Five Imperial Marks took the skies, making contact with the black dragon in a resounding explosion. A hole got punched in the endless darkness, bringing light.

The six Imperial Marks coming together felt like the Sacred Domain was going to crumble.

The five shuddered back with red faces and blood at their mouths. Zhao Chen got pushed back a dozen steps and spat so much blood, he soaked the ground.

The Wraith Emperor laughed, “Zhao Chen, we know your cultivation method is great, but you won’t get to refine us so long as we don’t let you surround our soul. We just need to match your every blow with all our strength. In your condition, you won’t be able to take the shock. A few more times and your body will break. Demonic Emperor, give it up, it’s over!”
