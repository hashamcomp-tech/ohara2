Chapter 4: Swipe It All!

"No, we must buy all of them." Stan Harrison was very firm.

’Was Stan Harrison giving her such a huge gift because he liked her?’

Maya’s cheeks flushed slightly, and she felt a bit embarrassed, her face showing shyness.

"Buy them all? All the clothes in this shop added together cost over one million dollars. Can you afford that?" Leo mocked Stan disdainfully.

Stan didn’t say a word, but handed his bank card to the waiter and said, "Swipe the card."

[This transaction is 1,260,000 dollars. You have received a double rebate of 2,520,000 dollars.]

"He really swiped the card?" Leo couldn’t believe what he was seeing. Even he couldn’t come up with over one million dollars, yet Stan Harrison had spent over one million dollars without even blinking. This was very shocking.

Leo felt a bit flustered at this moment. Wasn’t Stan Harrison just a penniless loser? How did he suddenly transform into a rich tycoon?

Lily found it hard to accept this reality. Looking at Stan Harrison’s departing back, she actually felt some regret over breaking up with him.

Meanwhile, back at the Zimmerman Family.

Maya was a treasured daughter of the Zimmerman family, so they kept tabs on her.

So when she went out with Stan, the Zimmermans had definitely noticed and were trying to investigate his background. After all, it was unlike their daughter to go out with anyone.

"Have you found out clearly? Who was eating with my daughter?" the head of the Zimmerman family asked in a cold voice.

"It is a college student named Stan Harrison."

"A college student with no background dares to hit on my daughter? Go send him my prized vintage wine and give him a hint."

"Understood," the man in black replied respectfully before quietly leaving.

...

Back at the dormitory, Stan Harrison received a message from Lily admitting her mistake.

"Stan Harrison, I was wrong before. I shouldn’t have broken up with you. I was just momentarily possessed. Can you forgive me?"

A cold sneer appeared on the corner of Stan Harrison’s mouth. Lily was truly shameless; upon discovering he had money, she actually came crawling back to him.

If it were before, perhaps Stan Harrison would have forgiven Lily.

But now, it was impossible to forgive her. He was no longer the simp he was before. This time, he’ll make sure that his relationship with girls is the other way around, with girls simping over him, not him simping over girls...

"Get lost," he replied coldly, not even batting an eye.

Lily was completely shaken by how blatantly Stan had rejected her... Considering how he had treated her before she broke up with him, she knew he genuinely loved her, and he was quite handsome himself, but she just couldn’t be with someone without money, so she had to leave...

But knowing his feelings for her previously, when she came back, even though she was pleading so earnestly, she had some confidence that he would accept her back, but now everything had been completely shattered...

Meanwhile, after sending that one word, Stan Harrison blocked Lily on Snapchat.

Stan Harrison’s mind was now completely occupied by the system. He had already spent money on Maya Zimmerman twice; the next time he could spend money would be in seven days.

He had to bind another heroine; only then could he earn more consumption rebates. He couldn’t just hang himself on one tree.

Bored, Stan Harrison began scrolling through TikTuk to find a woman with a beauty rating of 8 points or higher.

Soon, Stan Harrison locked onto a woman, Sarah, the third-ranked campus belle of Peak University, whose beauty was on par with Maya Zimmerman.

He just happened to swipe onto Sarah’s video.

Sarah had posted a video: "My family is in crisis, I need to borrow one million dollars. Is anyone willing to lend it to me? I guarantee I will pay it back, and I can write an IOU."

In the comment section, there was also Sarah’s Snapchat.

Seeing this video, Stan Harrison was overjoyed. He had been worrying about how to establish a connection with Sarah, and he hadn’t expected to stumble upon her video asking for money.

As expected, anything that can be solved with money is not a problem.

Stan Harrison added Sarah on SnapChat.

"Hello, may I borrow one million dollars?"

"Yes, but I need to have a video call with you."

Only through a video call could he bind the relationship with Sarah as a consumption rebate target. As for borrowing money?

Never mind one million dollars, even if it were two million dollars, Stan Harrison would lend it all the same.

In less than a minute, Sarah’s video call came through.

In the video, Sarah was wearing a pink pajama set. Although it was just pajamas, it still couldn’t hide her beautiful face. Her eyes were slightly damp, as if she had been crying.

She didn’t know how many video calls this had been, but everyone just wanted to see her in person, and no one actually intended to lend her money.

Some even thought she was joking, that all this was just content to go viral, not knowing how serious she was. She was very serious about this, considering that she would write and sign an IOU document to acknowledge the debt...

At this point, after trying for so long, Sarah’s mood was at an all-time low. She was only trying her luck when she made the video call to Stan.
