Chapter 3: Regret Begins!

Inside the bubble tea shop.

Stan and Maya sat across from each other.

In front of them, there was a cup of overpriced viral bubble tea.

And Maya was the one who paid for it!

At this moment, Maya rested her hands on the table, her fair, jade-like palms cupping her delicate chin like a lotus flower.

"Stan, thank you for the tip yesterday."

Maya thanked him in a soft, gentle voice.

For a moment, Stan was captivated. The reputation of Maya being the campus belle was certainly no exaggeration; both her voice and her face were incredibly alluring.

This beauty completely outclassed Lily by more than a few blocks.

Fortunately, Stan had willpower; anyone else would have already been soul-snatched by Maya.

"How about this, let me buy you an outfit as a token of my thanks."

Maya brought Stan to a CUCCi men’s clothing store.

Just as they walked into the clothing store.

They ran into acquaintances.

Leo had his arm around Lily’s shoulders, and the two were picking out clothes.

Suddenly, Lily’s gaze turned icy; seeing Stan was like seeing a mortal enemy.

"Stan, are you still pestering me? We’ve already broken up, please have some self-respect."

Stan frowned slightly. It was truly unlucky; he was out shopping with the campus belle, and he had to run into his ex-girlfriend.

"You’ve misunderstood, I’m just here to buy clothes," Stan explained.

Hearing that Stan was there to buy clothes, Lily scoffed disdainfully: "Buy clothes? Can you even afford the clothes here?"

She knew exactly what Stan was worth. A man who only ate at street stalls, would he come to buy clothes worth over ten thousand dollars? Impossible.

Stan shook his head, not bothering to explain.

Leo, with his arm around Lily’s waist, held up a suit jacket and asked the Waiter: "How much is this?"

"Thirty-two thousand dollars."

"Only thirty thousand? That’s so cheap, I’ll take it..."

Leo had deliberately chosen a relatively expensive piece of clothing to show off his wealth in front of Stan. He held Lily’s waist and stood in front of Stan, flaunting his status with a provocative air.

"Some people won’t be able to afford clothes this expensive in their entire lifetime." Leo deliberately drew out his voice, implying something.

Maya walked up to Stan holding a black jacket, "I picked out a piece of clothing for you. Do you like it?"

"I like it."

"Please wrap this up." Maya handed the clothes to the Waiter and said slowly.

Leo looked astonished.

The garment in Maya’s hand was no ordinary item; it was a limited-edition luxury piece, the most expensive item in the shop, costing over a hundred thousand dollars.

Although Leo had money, he couldn’t bear to buy something so expensive.

’What was going on? Wasn’t this person the campus belle? How did she get so close to Stan?’

After seeing Maya, Leo suddenly felt that Lily by his side wasn’t quite as appealing.

Whether it was her looks or her figure, Maya outclassed Lily by more than a few blocks.

"Maya, what a coincidence?" Leo took the initiative to greet Maya.

Maya just gave Leo a cold glance and ignored him.

Leo gritted his teeth in embarrassment and glared fiercely at Stan.

A loser like Stan had actually hooked up with the Peak University campus belle, which made Leo extremely unhappy.

"Maya, where are you going? My Audi is parked outside, do you want a ride?"

Leo tried to hit on her again.

"No need."

Maya coldly refused.

She had seen the conflict between Leo and Stan just now, and Stan was the man who had tipped her nearly five million.

Maya wouldn’t offend Stan just because of Leo.

Maya linked her arm with Stan’s and said in an intimate tone: "Having Stan accompany me is enough."

The corners of Stan’s mouth curled up; he felt a bit smug inside. Maya was so good at handling things; this one sentence gave him a huge boost of face.

While obviously she’s like this because of his money, it was still a good thing that Stan can take advantage of...

Leo clenched his fists tightly, his eyes turning red with frustration. He was unwilling to accept it; a stinking loser like Stan had actually hooked up with the campus belle.

Maya brought Stan to the CUCCi women’s clothing store next door and bought a few pieces of clothing for herself.

Leo, meanwhile, brought Lily along to the women’s clothing store as well.

"Lily, whatever clothes you like, just buy them. Don’t worry about them being too expensive, I have money."

As he spoke, Leo deliberately glanced at Stan, implying something.

Stan shook his head silently with a smile, ’Leo is just too childish, is this what Lily left me for... So funny seeing him childishly trying so hard to irk me...’

Meanwhile, Maya took her bank card and went to the counter to pay.

Seeing Maya checking out, a cold sneer appeared on Leo’s face. He walked up to Stan and mocked him: "Stan, why are you letting a woman pay the bill?"

"What? Are you too poor to have any money?"

"Leo my love, the clothes here start at ten thousand. A loser like Stan simply can’t afford them." Lily chimed in mockingly.

"Oh, if you hadn’t mentioned it, I would have almost forgotten; Stan is just a penniless, stinking loser." Leo and Lily sang in unison to humiliate Stan.

’Could he endure this?’ they thought, not knowing how bad they were embarrassing themselves.

Originally, Stan didn’t want to show off, but this Leo had gone too far.

It seemed he had no choice but to show off today.

Stan moved casually toward Maya.

Leo, standing to the side, was stunned: "What’s going on? Is this guy going to pay the bill?"

Seeing Stan walking toward her, Maya teased: "What’s wrong? Are you going to pay for me?"

Stan shook his head, "No."

A look of disappointment flashed in Maya’s eyes. Thinking about it, that made sense; she and Stan only had an ordinary relationship. Although Stan had tipped her nearly five million in her livestream, they were still just acquaintances. It was their first time out eating together, and Stan had no obligation to buy her clothes.

"Putting on an act, you broke loser, still can’t afford the clothes, can you?" Leo mocked.

"You scared me, I actually thought this guy was going to pay!" Lily also breathed a sigh of relief.

How could Stan possibly pay?

You see, a piece of clothing Lily bought was only twenty thousand dollars. Even with a double rebate, it would only return forty thousand dollars. No matter how you calculated it, it was a loss.

Since he was going to show off, he might as well do it big.

"I’ll buy every piece of clothing in this store." Stan waved his arm, pointing at the clothes in the room.

The atmosphere instantly went quiet.

"All the clothes?"

The Waiter suspected they had misheard and asked again.

"That’s right, all of them."

Since he was going to pay, he definitely had to spend as much as possible.

You have to understand, he only had two opportunities for consumption rebates per week; if he spent too little, it would be a huge loss.

Maya grabbed Stan’s arm and said in a gentle tone: "You don’t need to buy so many clothes, it’s too wasteful. Just buying one piece is enough."

[Maya: Favorability 34]

[Maya: Favorability 37]

[Maya: Favorability 40]

[Maya: Favorability 49]

[Maya: Favorability 53]

[Favorability surpassed 50; next consumption will receive a five-fold rebate.]

She said she didn’t need him to buy them, but Maya’s favorability was skyrocketing.

Stan was somewhat excited; it had only been two days, and her favorability had already surpassed 50. If things continued to develop like this, reaching 100 favorability wasn’t just a dream.

He was one step closer to becoming a billionaire tycoon.

"No need to be polite with me, it’s just a little pocket change, not wasteful at all."

"Forget it, just buying one piece is fine," Maya said again.

Stan sighed inwardly. ’One piece of clothing?How could that work? Wouldn’t that stop me from making more money?’
