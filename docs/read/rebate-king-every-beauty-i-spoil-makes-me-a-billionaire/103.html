Chapter 103: Emperor In The Room

Sarah’s breathing had slowed into the deep, steady rhythm of sleep. She was curled against him, one arm draped across his chest, her face pressed into the curve of his shoulder with the boneless trust of someone who had stopped worrying about anything the moment she’d closed her eyes.

Stan lay still for a while, listening to the quiet sounds of her apartment, the hum of the refrigerator, the distant murmur of traffic, the soft tick of a clock in the hallway. Then, carefully, without disturbing her, he reached for his phone on the nightstand and opened TikTuk Live.

He had unfinished business.

The first thing he did was change his username.

A Streak of Green on the Head had served its purpose, but the novelty had worn off weeks ago and the name had started to feel like a joke that had overstayed its welcome. He trimmed it down to a single word.

[Sir Streak.]

Clean. Short, but instantly recognizable to anyone who’d been paying attention to TikTuk Live’s leaderboards over the past week.

The second thing he did was search for Sophie Youngs.

He found her stream buried deep in the platform’s lower tiers, the kind of positioning that came from having almost no viewer base, no algorithmic momentum, and no whale backing to push her up the rankings.

Sophie had been streaming for close to two weeks now, according to Zack, but the numbers told a bleak story. Low viewership. Minimal gifting. The kind of traction that made most new streamers quietly give up within the first month.

Stan tapped into the room.

Sophie was on screen, different from the poised, perfectly composed campus belle he was used to. She was sitting cross-legged on her bed in an oversized hoodie, hair pulled into a messy bun, talking to her sparse audience with the slightly self-conscious energy of someone who was still figuring out how to be natural in front of a camera.

The production quality was minimal. The lighting was whatever her bedroom ceiling fixture provided. The chat moved at the leisurely pace of a stream with fewer than three hundred viewers.

She was trying. That much was clear. But trying wasn’t enough on a platform where attention was currency and currency required whales.

Stan activated Emperor status the moment he entered the room.

The notification blazed across Sophie’s screen, and across every active stream on the platform, in bold red characters.

[Sir Streak has activated Emperor status in Sophie Youngs’s room.]

Sophie’s eyes widened. Her sentence trailed off mid-word.

The chat, which had been drifting along at a sleepy pace, suddenly jolted to life.

People were flooding in from other streams, drawn by the Emperor notification, driven by curiosity and the electric possibility that the platform’s most legendary whale was about to do what he did best.

Sophie stared at the chat, her lips slightly parted.

Streak. Formerly known as a Streak Of Green, the tycoon who dropped twenty million on Xenia in a single night.

The mysterious figure whose identity nobody on the platform had ever confirmed. The man whose name alone could move a streamer from obscurity to the front page.

He’s in my room.
