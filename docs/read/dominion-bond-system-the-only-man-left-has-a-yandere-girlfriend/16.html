Meanwhile at an apartment near the Gate, Reiji had crashed into the apartment. He was now on the floor covered in rubble only his hands were visible.

"Cough.... Cough..." Reiji coughed as he sat up from the rubble.

"Mmmm.." He winced as he slowly got up.

"Man, that was insane..." Reiji muttered to himself as he struggled to stand up straight. He genuinely felt he had left his soul back at the dungeon. "What was I thinking... a body as weak as mine could handle such speed..."

Immediately as he went through the portal and just took a step to start running he accidentally crashed into this apartment.

The speed was just insane, it was surely at the level of a beginner D-Rank awakener or adventurer.

"Thank God no one was home, otherwise this would have been much worse..." Reiji stood up as he walked out of the apartment. There was no way he was running again.

As he dragged himself along the sidewalk he suddenly heard the sound of vehicles approaching. It seemed the awakeners were already out and everybody was returning back to their stations.

"Fuck." Reiji’s instincts immediately kicked in and as soon as he took a step, he regretted it dearly.

BOOM!

Reiji crashed straight into the wall of an alley.

"Argh...."

Reiji clutched his head as he dizzily stood up, the world doing a complete 360 in his head.

Despite his dizziness, he was able to see that the apartments that should have been dark suddenly had lights on. It was clear that the commotion he caused had woken up most people.

Windows started opening one after another.

"What was that sound?"

"Did a monster escape?"

"Call the authorities!"

Reiji’s face twitched hearing the panic spreading around. His instincts kicked in again and this time he just gave up entirely.

BOOM!

This time he crashed straight into a lamppost, knocking it over.

Reiji didn’t know whether to laugh or cry.

He felt bullied but the irony was that he was the one who bullied himself by adding 30 whole points to his Speed stat.

He slowly got up despite the dizziness and pain. He noticed that it was only a few blocks from his apartment.

He dragged his broken body toward his apartment.

If anybody saw him now they would think he was a monster that had escaped from a dungeon or some kind of failed science experiment.

Reiji reached his apartment and clawed his way up toward the window like a thief, or more like an animal.

He opened the window, closed it and plopped straight onto the ground.

[....]

"..."
