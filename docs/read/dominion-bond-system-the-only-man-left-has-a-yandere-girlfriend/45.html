Chapter 45: Vel The Chubby Yellow Cat

The Velsra walked through the door, and Reiji prepared himself for something exciting.

But as it passed through, nothing happened.

"Maybe he got erased."

Thinking about it, Reiji’s grin widened as he rushed through the door.

Reiji looked around, but the Velsra was nowhere to be seen. "It really works! This might be the ultimate jackpot for killing monsters."

"What works, Your Majesty?"

Reiji froze. ’No. It’s a freaking hallucination...’

"Please do tell this humble servant of yours." Vel’s voice sounded again.

Reiji slowly turned his head.

"Why the hell didn’t you die?"

"Why would I die? Wait... you wanted me to die?!" Vel stared at Reiji as he slowly backed away.

"Well, duh..."

"Why, My Lord?"

"For a noble cause."

"What noble cause would make you let me give up my life by walking through a door?"

"For science..."

"Who is she?!"

"She’s not a she. I mean, it’s not a she."

"Then why would I die for something that isn’t even a person?"

Reiji rubbed his temples, not wanting to explain.

BOOM!

The door closed with a thunderous sound and disappeared.

Reiji stared at the familiar living room and felt at peace. After all the fighting and near-death experiences, he was finally home.

"Wow..."

Vel stared at the house in awe.

It wasn’t the first time he had seen a cave, but it was definitely the most beautiful and well-designed one he had ever seen.

"Hmmm... As expected of My Lord," Vel muttered.

"Hey, come back here."

Reiji sounded annoyed as Vel walked around the house with his dirty bare feet.

"I think I should send you back to the Palace of the Dominion King."

"Huh?"

"My palace."

"Oh... But I want to stay by your side, My Lord."

Vel gave him a pitiful look.

"The way you look, if my girlfriend sees you, she’ll probably kill you before you can run away."

Vel felt his spine tingle.

"But what’s a girlfriend?"

"Uhm... A girlfriend is like a wife you haven’t married yet."

"Isn’t that a fiancée?"

"Yeah. Something like that."

Reiji responded half-heartedly.

"Is she as strong as you, My Lord?"

"Though I may be strong, when it really comes down to it, she’s stronger than two of me combined."

Vel’s face darkened.

In his understanding, if his Lord was already a monster, then his girlfriend had to be an even bigger monster.

’What the hell did I get myself into?!’

"Uhm... My Lord?"

"Hmmm... Hey, can you shapeshift?"

Reiji asked as he read Vel’s profile on the system screen with great curiosity.

[Servant (1)] — [Vel]

[Species: Velsra (Grand Elder Velsra) (???)]

[Age: 1278]

[Skills: Shapeshift (???), Eternal Youth (???), Xe (???)]

[Title: The Last]

’Even the system doesn’t know its rank, or even the ranks of its skills... This thing might actually be something more than it appears to be.’

Reiji decided not to ask any questions about it for now.

"Yes, but only once every two years, My Lord."

"Can you do it now?"

Reiji picked up his phone from the couch and began scrolling.

"Yes. But I won’t be able to turn back into my original form until two years later..."

Vel’s voice became nervous as he looked around, worried that his Lord’s girlfriend might suddenly appear.

"Turn into this."

Reiji showed Vel a picture of a cute, chubby yellow cat.

"M-My Lord..."

"What? Don’t like it?"

Reiji had picked this type of cat because Hina loved chubby cats. He had also picked it because of the cat’s color since it matched Vel’s.

"I love it, My Lord. It’s very cute..."

The Velsra said, wiping tears from his eyes as a golden light immediately enveloped him.

After the light faded, Vel had completely transformed into the exact cat from the picture.

It looked as though the picture itself had come to life.

"Hmmm..."

’My Lord?’

’Huh? How are you doing this?’

’I also don’t know, My Lord. As I transformed, I felt our connection become closer...’

’Hmmm... This is actually very handy.’

"Can you still talk?"

"Yes... It appears so, meow."

Reiji cringed as he stared at Vel.

"What the hell did you just do?"

"I swear it was not me, My Lord. It came out before I could stop it, meow."

"Are you trying to piss me off?"

"No, My Lord, meow."

Reiji gritted his teeth in anger.

If not for Vel’s current form, he would have given him a good beating.

’Wait... Does it copy the behavior of the creature whose form it takes?’

Reiji’s hypothesis was immediately confirmed when Vel began purring.

"M-M-My Lord... Meow... I feel very sleepy." Vel yawned. "C-Ca... Can I please sleep, My Lord... meow?"

Without waiting for Reiji to respond, Vel immediately jumped onto the couch and fell asleep.

"..."

’I guess so...’ Reiji thought as he went to the kitchen to whip up breakfast.

When he checked his phone, it was already 2 a.m., but that wasn’t going to stop him from eating.

After preparing a meal for himself, Reiji realized he had made two servings.

He didn’t know if it was because he missed Hina or because Vel was here.

Either way, he was going to give it to Vel instead of wasting the food.

A few minutes later, the food was served.

Purrrr...!

Vel purred, stretching his limbs exactly like a cat.

"Good morning, My Lord, meow."

"Yeah, good morning, furball."

"How long was I asleep, meow?"

"Only an hour."

"I’m sorry, My Lord. I didn’t have control over my body and suddenly became so tired, meow."

"No problem, take this..."

Reiji served Vel a bowl of food.

"My Lord? Meow."

Vel stared at the food that had been served in a bowl on the ground for him.

"Eat. It’s yours."

Vel stared at Reiji, and his respect and affection for him increased. "You don’t know how long it has been since I ate real food, meow."

"Huh? Why?"

"After I was kidnapped and locked in that damn cave, the only thing I could eat was sour fruit, meow." Vel let out a sigh.

Reiji brows knitted as he stared at Vel. "Now you’ve seen food, why are you hesitating? Do you think I poisoned your food?!"

"No, no! Certainly not, Your Majesty, meow."

Vel quickly shook his head.

"I’ll eat it right away, meow."

As the food entered Vel’s mouth, his eyes couldn’t help but widen in pure bliss.

"Youm Mayesty... It’sss suuu good, meeooww."

"Close your mouth and chew, you shameless goblin."

Vel didn’t even mind Reiji’s taunt and continued devouring the food.

"Slow down. There’s more if you want..."

~Ding! ~Dong!

Reiji immediately tensed up as he stared at the door.

Hina was supposed to come back in three months, and it hadn’t even been a week.

Even if Hina decided to run away from the trip, Reiji knew his own mother would track her down and drag her back.

"Who the hell is at the door?"
