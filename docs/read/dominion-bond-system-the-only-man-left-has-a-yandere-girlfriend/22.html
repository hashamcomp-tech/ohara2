Chapter 22: Separation

"Tell me you didn’t pack my son’s entire wardrobe... fufu," Kaori teased as Hina dragged her suitcase over to the car.

A sleek black Mercedes-Benz was parked quietly outside the house, its polished body reflecting the evening glow.

Kaori stood beside the car with a playful smile on her beautiful face. Her long black evening dress hugged her figure gracefully while the golden floral designs near the sleeves gave her an elegant and luxurious aura.

"It’s nothing..." Hina replied softly.

"Hand it over to the driver..."

The lady dressed in a sharp black suit immediately rushed over to collect the suitcase.

Before Hina could refuse, she was suddenly pulled into the car by two young girls.

"Hina-san!"

Both girls hugged Hina tightly. They were Reiji’s little sisters.

The twins were treated like princesses by the family. Both of them wore stunning dresses with small floral patterns on them. They had long silky black hair like their mother and though they were still little, their adorable faces were already pretty enough to rival Hina in the future.

Hina smiled softly and ruffled both their heads. "My two little troublemakers."

"We missed you so much..." Both of them looked around but didn’t seem to see someone.

"...Hina-san, where is Nii-chan?" Yuna, the elder of the twins, asked.

"Uhmm.... Reiji went to another country for an exam..." Hina lied to them.

The twins stared at Hina with their big black eyes full of suspicion for a moment before breaking into sweet smiles.

"Nii-chan is such a big meanie..." Yui said angrily with a pout.

"Too bad for him, we got Hina-san with us..." Yuna chimed in with a smirk as she grabbed Hina’s arm even tighter.

’Rei-kun is not a meanie, you little rascals. He just doesn’t want to complicate things, otherwise he would’ve come to hold you guys.’ Hina thought while ruffling their heads even harder.

After a while, Kaori and the driver got into the car and the female driver started the engine. The vehicle roared to life as they left the apartment.

"Mom..?"

"Yes dear..." Kaori responded casually.

"How long is the trip...?" Hina asked. Even now she already missed Reiji so much and they had only just left the house.

"I’m sorry, I totally forgot to tell you. Well, it might last for three months... maybe even longer..." Kaori said very casually.

"...Eeeehhh?!"

Hina’s eyes widened in shock. Beneath them were tears she was desperately trying to hold back.

"I know it’s too short... I wish it could be like one year, but since I still have the company to look after, I can only spare three months." Kaori said with a sigh.

The corner of Hina’s mouth twitched slightly.

’A year?!?! No way, I’ll die!! Three months is already too much!!’

But Kaori didn’t know what Hina was thinking, so the journey continued as the car sped off toward the direction of Hina’s House.
