Chapter 305

Vol 2 Chapter 182: Unconscious Charm

"Are you going to rest here? The ground is very cold." That gentle and charming voice came from in front of him again, and then a folded blanket was taken by those flawless fingers and lightly placed before the youth.

"This will be a bit better." Sylutia guessed the other party probably hadn't brought cold-proof supplies. After all, after delving this deep into the labyrinth for so long, even if they had some, they were likely left outside.

"Th-thank you." Even though the girl before him might be an illusion, the red-haired youth still hoarsely uttered these words.

Seeing the other party had calmed down, Sylutia stood up again, lightly shifted her feet, and beautiful butterfly wings unfurled from her back.

She flew around this spacious room, searching for an exit.

According to the rules she guessed, the labyrinth builder's intention was probably for her and that red-haired youth to fight to the death, and after the victor emerged, a new passage or door would appear.

But she clearly had no thought or intention of indiscriminately killing the innocent. Such a method of opening doors definitely wouldn't work, so this time she could only find a way to break through on her own.

Sylutia blinked her eyes, briefly switched her Aspect, and then used her special [Alien Star Perception] and [Discernment Eyes] to scan the environment here.

Several minutes later, she finally discovered the details of the mechanisms arranged here and tried activating a small section of them.

Accompanied by clicking sounds, a wall on one side of the room opened, revealing a forward passage.

Great, now I can continue again.

Sylutia thought about walking into it, but remembering something, she turned back, descending before that youth. The other party was still holding the thick blanket Sylutia had given him, constantly stroking it, seemingly trying to determine whether this was an illusion or some kind of reality.

Seeing him still not fully clear-headed, the young girl had to lightly cough once.

"I'm leaving first. After I finish exploring the labyrinth, I'll come back to pull you out. That will be safer." After she finished speaking, she found Gallas was still somewhat mentally unclear, but there was no way to keep waiting and explaining right now.

"Rest well." Having said that, she lightly leaped up and flew into the opened passage.

Gallas stared blankly at her departing figure, then unconsciously followed Sylutia's instructions, unfolding the blanket, wrapping himself in it, and slowly falling asleep.

Sleeping for a while will be fine. I must be dreaming today.

In this dangerous abyss labyrinth, this red-haired youth gradually entered a sweet dreamland.
