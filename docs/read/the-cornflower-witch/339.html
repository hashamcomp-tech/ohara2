Vol 2 Chapter 213: The Secret of the Eternal Daylight White Bird

"Master, what exactly is the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird'? The 'White Bird' I saw before, was that it?"

In the room, the Eternal Daylight White Bird, who had been lying on the bed, suddenly sat up. Her eyes were wide open, her gaze sharp, staring at the girl sitting by the bedside.

The girl sitting there was the Eternal Daylight White Bird's master, a woman with long silver hair and deep blue eyes. She looked at her disciple, a hint of helplessness in her eyes, and sighed softly.

"Master, answer me, Master." The Eternal Daylight White Bird grabbed her master's hand, her expression anxious.

"Alright, alright, I'll tell you." The master patted the Eternal Daylight White Bird's hand, her voice gentle. "But before that, you need to calm down."

"Okay, I'll calm down." The Eternal Daylight White Bird took a deep breath, forcing herself to relax.

"Good." The master nodded, then slowly began to speak. "The 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' is a very special existence. It is not a creature, nor is it a divine beast. It is a 'concept'."

"Concept?" The Eternal Daylight White Bird was stunned.

"Yes, concept." The master's expression turned serious. "The 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' represents 'eternal daylight', symbolizing endless light and hope. It is a power born from the collective faith and longing of all living beings in this world."

"Born from... faith and longing?" The Eternal Daylight White Bird murmured, her eyes filled with confusion.

"That's right." The master continued, "In this world, there is a legend. It is said that when the world falls into darkness and despair, the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' will appear, bringing eternal daylight and driving away all darkness."

"So... the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' is a savior?" The Eternal Daylight White Bird asked.

"Savior?" The master smiled slightly, shaking her head. "No, it's not a savior. The 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' is more like a 'symbol', a symbol of hope. Its existence itself gives people the courage and strength to persevere."

"Then... what about me?" The Eternal Daylight White Bird pointed at herself. "What am I?"

"You..." The master looked at her disciple, her eyes complex. "You are the 'vessel' of the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird'."

"Vessel?" The Eternal Daylight White Bird's body trembled.

"Yes, vessel." The master's voice was low. "The 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' is a concept, a power, and it needs a vessel to manifest in this world. And you are the vessel chosen by it."

"Chosen... by it?" The Eternal Daylight White Bird's voice trembled.

"That's right." The master nodded. "From the moment you were born, you were destined to become the vessel of the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird'. This is your fate, something you cannot escape."

"Fate..." The Eternal Daylight White Bird lowered her head, her hands clenched tightly.

"Don't be sad." The master reached out, gently stroking the Eternal Daylight White Bird's head. "Although this is your fate, it is also your glory. Because you will become the hope of this world, the light that drives away darkness."

"But... I don't want to." The Eternal Daylight White Bird raised her head, tears welling in her eyes. "I don't want to become some 'vessel', I just want to be an ordinary person, living an ordinary life."

"I understand." The master sighed softly. "But sometimes, fate does not allow us to choose. Since you have been chosen, you must shoulder this responsibility."

"Responsibility..." The Eternal Daylight White Bird murmured, her heart in turmoil.

"Yes, responsibility." The master's expression turned serious again. "As the vessel of the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird', you have a responsibility to protect this world, to protect all living beings. This is your mission, something you must accomplish."

"I... understand." The Eternal Daylight White Bird closed her eyes, tears streaming down her face.

"Good child." The master hugged the Eternal Daylight White Bird, her voice gentle. "I know this is hard for you, but please believe that you are not alone. I will always be by your side, supporting you, helping you."

"Master..." The Eternal Daylight White Bird hugged her master tightly, crying like a child.

After a long time, the Eternal Daylight White Bird finally calmed down. She wiped her tears and looked at her master. "Master, can you tell me more about the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird'? I want to know everything about it."

"Alright." The master nodded, then began to explain in detail. "The 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' is a very ancient existence. According to legend, it was born at the beginning of the world, when the first ray of light appeared."

"Born at the beginning of the world?" The Eternal Daylight White Bird was shocked.

"Yes." The master continued, "At that time, the world was shrouded in darkness, without a trace of light. It was the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' that brought the first ray of light, illuminating the world and giving birth to all life."

"So... the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' is the source of light in this world?" The Eternal Daylight White Bird asked.

"That's right." The master nodded. "The 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' is the source of light in this world, the foundation of all life. Without it, the world would fall into eternal darkness, and all life would perish."

"Then... why does it need a vessel?" The Eternal Daylight White Bird was puzzled.

"Because the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird' is too powerful." The master explained, "Its power is so immense that no ordinary body can withstand it. Only a special 'vessel' can bear its power and allow it to manifest in this world."

"And I am that special 'vessel'?" The Eternal Daylight White Bird pointed at herself.

"Yes." The master nodded. "Your body is special, possessing a constitution that can accommodate the power of the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird'. This is why you were chosen."

"I see." The Eternal Daylight White Bird lowered her head, falling into thought.

"Also." The master added, "As the vessel of the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird', you will gain immense power. This power will allow you to protect this world and protect all living beings."

"Power..." The Eternal Daylight White Bird murmured, a complex look in her eyes.

"Yes, power." The master said seriously, "But you must remember that power is a double-edged sword. If used properly, it can protect; if misused, it can destroy. So you must learn to control this power, not be controlled by it."

"I understand." The Eternal Daylight White Bird nodded. "I will learn to control this power and use it to protect the people I want to protect."

"Good child." The master smiled with relief. "I believe you can do it."

"Master." The Eternal Daylight White Bird suddenly looked up, her eyes determined. "I have decided. I will accept this fate and become the vessel of the 'Eternal Daylight White Bird'. I will use this power to protect this world and protect all living beings."

"Are you sure?" The master looked at her disciple, her eyes filled with concern.

"I'm sure." The Eternal Daylight White Bird nodded firmly. "Although this is not the life I wanted, since fate has chosen me, I will face it bravely. I will not run away, nor will I give up. I will use my own strength to change this fate and create the future I want."

"Good!" The master's eyes shone with admiration. "This is my disciple, the Eternal Daylight White Bird I am proud of!"

"Master." The Eternal Daylight White Bird smiled, a genuine smile appearing on her face for the first time. "Thank you. Thank you for telling me all this, and thank you for always being by my side."

"Silly child." The master hugged the Eternal Daylight White Bird again, her voice choked with emotion. "You are my disciple, of course I will always be by your side."

"Mm." The Eternal Daylight White Bird nodded, hugging her master tightly.

At this moment, she made up her mind. No matter what difficulties lay ahead, no matter what challenges she faced, she would move forward bravely. Because she was the Eternal Daylight White Bird, the hope of this world, the light that drives away darkness.

She would use her own strength to protect this world and protect all living beings.

This was her mission, her responsibility, and her glory.
