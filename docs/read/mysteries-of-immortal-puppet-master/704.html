Chapter 704

Chapter 704 -217: New Iron Han Armor_3

The maximum speed of the Golden Swallow is very high, reaching an almost exaggerated level. Its downside is its lack of explosive power and slow acceleration.

However, when placed on the Iron Han Armor, these drawbacks disappear. This is because its acceleration and explosive power can be strongly boosted by the Golden Core and Talisman!

This is where the true power and excellence of Mechanical Techniques lie—they can balance strengths and weaknesses.

Since the wings of the Golden Swallow are involved, it is naturally most suitable to use materials associated with the Gold Element.

At the beginning of the design, Ning Zhuo didn’t have any special requirements for materials; he first used one of the most widely available materials—Gold Essence.

Modifying the shape was very easy, and Ning Zhuo quickly managed it using a Mechanical Platform.

He didn’t rush to add Talismans and Formations; instead, Ning Zhuo first wore it for low-altitude testing.

The initial test was successful.

The shape of the Iron Han Armor is excellent, capable of cutting through the atmosphere and effectively reducing wind resistance.

Ning Zhuo began designing the Formations.

The most fundamental core of the Formations is the energy core.

This must be designed first to allow subsequent extensive external expansion.

Since it is no longer the Foundation Establishment Pill but the Golden Core, the old parts of the energy core must be discarded.

Considering the undefined properties of the Golden Core, Ning Zhuo decided to use the structure of the Five Elements Golden Core Disk.

This idea comes partly from the old slots for the Foundation Establishment Pill, and partly from the Five Elements Spiritual Conversion Disk.

The Five Elements Spiritual Conversion Disk is one of the items Ning Zhuo acquired while traversing the Lava Fairy Palace.

He had used it several times and was deeply impressed.

Combining these two design concepts, Ning Zhuo integrated them and quickly created the Five Elements Golden Core Disk.

The disk has a slot for embedding the Golden Core. As long as the Golden Core belongs to one of the Five Elements, the disk can convert its mana into the other four elements.

After designing the energy core part, Ning Zhuo proceeded to derive outward formations.

He arranged the Sharp Arrow Formation on the wings of the Golden Swallow for breaking through the sky. On the waist and abdomen, he placed the Evergreen Array for endurance. Inside the Iron Han Armor, numerous Returning Flow Arrays were inscribed for balancing and regulating the internal and external pressure of the armor. Fire Spray Arrays were designed on the soles to accelerate the armor.

On the helmet, he arranged the Earth Shield Array for enhanced defense.

The Mechanical Platform played a powerful role again, allowing Sun Ning and others to quickly inscribe various Array Patterns on all the mechanical components.

Once assembled, the new Iron Han Armor retained only the internal spine portion, with the rest replaced by new Treasure materials.

After linking the Array Patterns one by one, Ning Zhuo began activating the Arrays in sequence.

All the Arrays passed in one go without any issues.

Sun Lingtong sincerely exclaimed, “If we were to set up the Arrays, we would probably mess up one or two. The Mechanical Platform is really useful!”

With the initial product ready, Ning Zhuo wore it and immediately conducted tests.

Since each Array was designed with the utmost conservativeness, the test results were very good.

After more than thirty tests, there were only five issues. Among them were minor ones, such as uneven fire propulsion of the soles that needed adjustment. For long-distance flights, the knees tended to bend, causing high wind resistance and heating up. This required solving the local high temperature problem, otherwise the knees would be damaged after a battle.

The uneven fire propulsion was easily fixed. Ning Zhuo drew a new Array Pattern, linking two sole Arrays to adjust their fire propulsion levels.

Additionally, Ning Zhuo modified a small part of the sole Arrays to make them sense each other. This also helped adjust the uneven propulsion.

With these two approaches combined, the design ensured that even if Array Patterns were damaged during battle, there would be alternative solutions for emergencies.

After extensive modifications, Ning Zhuo added the Frost Return Ring on the knees, perfectly pairing with the Returning Flow Arrays to solve the overheating issue.

The Fire Spray Arrays on the soles were upgraded to the Fire Tongue Formation, which had more than ten times the propulsion capability of the previous one.

The Sharp Arrow Formation on the wings of the Golden Swallow was upgraded to the White Tiger Breaking Army Formation, primarily using Runes to serve as the Array Eye.

The Evergreen Array on the waist and abdomen was externally housed, freeing up the original space for the Returning Flow Array.

Ning Zhuo specially designed a Mechanical Rope, arranging the Evergreen Array on the rope. After wrapping it around the waist, activating the Evergreen Array benefited the entire mechanism.

The Returning Flow Array, originally limited to the interior of the Iron Han Armor, was upgraded to the Dual Yin Return Flow Array, combining internal and external Yin Yang, which further facilitated the regulation and balance of the armor’s internal and external pressure.

The performance of these Arrays was increased by an average of tenfold, massively boosting the armor’s overall performance.

However, after testing, Ning Zhuo discovered another problem.

The Golden Eyes material couldn’t withstand such strong performance, causing the wings of the Golden Swallow to bear the most pressure and break down first.

Ning Zhuo first changed the materials and altered the structure of the wings from two-fold to three-fold!

Once the wings no longer failed, Ning Zhuo optimized the curvature of the wings in subsequent tests.

He made tolerance corrections to the Five Elements Golden Core Disk.

He arranged twelve Divine Splitting Mirrors on the spine separately, making control easier.

Thus, the Iron Han Armor was reborn!
