Chapter 822 -277: “Myriad Phenomena Scripture”_2

Now, that’s the impression Li Xiangshang gives Qing Chi.

After swearing a solemn oath, Li Xiangshang looked earnestly at Qing Chi: “There, little girl, are you relieved now?”

Qing Chi’s tone softened a bit: “Who are you calling little girl? Don’t call me little girl, I have a name, it’s Qing Chi.”

Li Xiangshang: “Ah? You’re far younger than me, I’m even older than your grandfather, isn’t it proper to call you little girl?”

Qing Chi rubbed her forehead: “Anyway, just call me Qing Chi. Also, remember the oath you just made.”

“I’m teaching you, when interacting with people, don’t go around calling them little girl or kid, it’s easy to offend!”

Li Xiangshang’s eyes widened, looking as if he learned something new: “Is that so?”

Qing Chi’s eyes shifted slightly: “Now that I’ve taught you, how will you repay me?”

Li Xiangshang exclaimed: “This…”

This time it was Qing Chi’s turn to wave her hand: “Since you’re going to the Yang Enhancement Institute, why not take me with you as a repayment?”

Li Xiangshang: “Is that okay?”

Qing Chi impatiently nodded: “Of course! Just take me, and if anyone stops me, just be fierce with them.”

Li Xiangshang hesitated: “Isn’t that improper? I’m new here, I don’t want to offend people.”

Qing Chi: “If you can’t be fierce with others, then just stand there silently and stare at them expressionlessly!”

Li Xiangshang thought for a moment: “That I can do.”

Qing Chi then followed Li Xiangshang into the Yang Enhancement Institute.

The first step Li Xiangshang took when he arrived at White Paper Immortal City was to pay respects to the Paperman Avatar of the White Paper City Lord. This time he had come to the Yang Enhancement Institute, who had already been notified from above.

The Yang Enhancement Institute welcomed Li Xiangshang, but had a different attitude towards Qing Chi.

But when they tried to stop Qing Chi, they saw Li Xiangshang nodding with piercing eyes.

Yang Enhancement Institute Cultivator: ?!

Li Xiangshang: …

After a moment of eye contact, the cultivator of the Yang Enhancement Institute realized: “So you two are together, please, come in, come in.”

Qing Chi was overjoyed and followed Li Xiangshang further into the Yang Enhancement Institute.

She had been here before and knew the way, midway through she parted ways with Li Xiangshang.

Before leaving, she patted Li Xiangshang’s arm: “Alright, I’m off now, big guy, you’re quite nice. Next time I’m here, I’ll look for you to guide me again.”

Li Xiangshang immediately beamed: “Miss Qing, you’re the first friend I made here, feel free to call upon me for help.”

The cultivator at the side of the Yang Enhancement Institute could only show a wry smile.

Qing Chi arrived at Ning Zhuo’s residence, tidied up her appearance a bit, took a deep breath, and nervously approached Ning Zhuo’s door.

“Little Ma, Little Ma, I’m here to see you.” She called softly.

No response.

Qing Chi bit her lip and gently knocked on the door with her knuckle: “Little Ma, it’s me, Qing Chi, your Qingqing.”

No response.

Qing Chi pressed her ear to the door, using a spell to eavesdrop, but there wasn’t a sound from inside the room.

Qing Chi’s face sank slightly, her eyes narrowing, a glint of green flame flickered deep in her eyes.

She started pounding on the door, her force increasing.

Bang bang bang…

“Little Ma, open up!”

“Are you still trying to hide from me?”

“I’m right at your door!”

“Open the door for me, hurry up and open it!!”

“If you don’t open the door, I won’t leave today!”

“You brat, do you dare to open the door, do you even have the courage to face me? You jerk!”

Qing Chi’s voice grew louder, her emotions increasingly agitated, she even began kicking the door.

The neighbor next door finally couldn’t bear it: “Stop causing a ruckus, girl. Jiao Ma isn’t here!”

Qing Chi shouted angrily: “You trying to cover for him?!”

The neighbor was startled: “No, no. He left early yesterday morning and didn’t return at night.”

Qing Chi let out a sound of realization, turning her anger into joy, it turned out Jiao Ma wasn’t deliberately avoiding her.

She asked about Jiao Ma’s whereabouts, but the neighbor didn’t know, shaking his head repeatedly.

Qing Chi bit her lip, unwilling to let it go, having come all the way to the Yang Enhancement Institute: “I’ll find the higher-ups at the Yang Enhancement Institute and ask them!”

Ning Zhuo had gone to find Wen Ruanyu.

Following his guidance, Sun Lingtong smoothly arrived at the seventh Paperman Army, finding Meng Yaoyin’s set-up.

But to activate the Array Plate, Myriad Images Mana was needed.

The two were stuck at this point, and Sun Lingtong could only patiently wait for good news from Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo had tried his best, and had left the Yang Enhancement Institute yesterday to find Wen Ruanyu and took the initiative to seek guidance.

“Last time, Senior Wen mentioned the possibility of joining the Myriad Images Sect, and also mentioned the Flying Cloud Conference. The more I ponder, the more tempted I become.”

“Recently, I learned that any cultivator who joins the Myriad Images Sect must practice the Myriad Phenomena Scripture.”

“I wonder if Senior Wen could teach me a part of the Myriad Phenomena Scripture beforehand, so I can start cultivating and have a better chance at the Flying Cloud Conference?”

This reason was indeed very appropriate!

Wen Ruanyu himself recommended talents and had the merit of introducing them.

Hearing Ning Zhuo’s request, he was very pleased and immediately agreed: “My Myriad Images Sect includes everything, embracing all, the initial content of the Myriad Phenomena Scripture is not restricted from spreading.”

“I’ll teach you the Qi Refining and Foundation Establishment chapters of the Myriad Phenomena Scripture right now!”

Ning Zhuo smoothly received the Myriad Phenomena Scripture and did not return to the Yang Enhancement Institute.

He went to the South Gate of White Paper Immortal City and once again entered the Paperman Giant Statue. Only with the Qianmo Lamp, could he borrow the Life Hanging Thread and establish contact with Sun Lingtong.

“Boss, it all went well, I got the Myriad Phenomena Scripture.” Ning Zhuo said joyfully.

Sun Lingtong cheered: “Little Zhuo, I knew you could do it!”

He received the teachings, first memorized them, and then began to ponder.

The Myriad Phenomena Scripture starts systematically, saying: “In the beginning, chaos separated, clarity from murkiness undetermined. The innate Mixed Element Qi, and thus the Mixed Element Scripture. Mixed Element Qi transforms into all things, hence Myriad Phenomena. Myriad Phenomena is the pivot of heaven and earth, the transformation of Yin and Yang…”

“I have seen the stars falling into the sea, witnessed thunder splitting into spring buds, realizing Myriad Phenomena is nothing but Qi transforming shape. Thus, I wrote this scripture to accommodate form into formless, to prove the truth in the illusory.”

Sun Lingtong delved deep into study, his heart filled with growing joy, increasingly intense.

“This Myriad Phenomena Scripture indeed lives up to its status as a supreme technique of a powerful sect! Its high and far-reaching aspirations surpass most techniques.”

“Cultivating this scripture can form Myriad Image Mana. This mana can simulate and evolve the power of any cultivation technique in the world.”

Sun Lingtong infused the Myriad Elemental Mana, and the Array Plate finally activated!

The Array Plate’s mysterious and contained aura quickly spread out, enveloping the entire Paperman Army inside.

The next moment, the Paperman Army began to move slightly, producing a rustling sound.
