Chapter 689 -209: Ning Zhuo vs Tian Shuang

“Bai Shuang, look at you, such a pitiful sight!”

“You really embarrass me.”

Earth Ghost is laughing, blatantly mocking and ridiculing Heavenly Ghost.

Boom, boom, boom…

The assault launched by Liu Guanzhang grows increasingly urgent and fierce.

Tian Shuang keeps retreating.

He is heavily protected by Magic Treasures and Spells, but the fierce attack of the Three Generals, empowered by their Military Power, repeatedly shakes him and finally breaches his limit, causing a three-foot-long wound on his right arm.

The wound continuously spills a large amount of frost fragments.

These fragments drift and scatter in the sky, shining brilliantly.

Possibly due to the pain, Tian Shuang finally, in that moment, suppresses the countless Buddha Thoughts interfering in his Divine Sea and reluctantly activates a Magic Treasure.

Magic Treasure—Ghost Lantern!

This is an immensely popular and classic Magic Treasure among Ghost Cultivators.

According to the nature of each Ghost Cultivator and the captured Ghosts, the Ghost Lantern exhibits different powers and efficacy.

The Ghost Lantern Tian Shuang uses now imprisons frost-type evil spirits.

He holds the lantern in one hand, it spins rapidly, the frost ghosts inside constantly transform, forming images of imprisoned ice prisons on the white paper surface of the lantern.

The light emitted by the lantern shines near the Three Generals, immediately forming ice prisons around them.

These prisons carry powerful binding and interfering forces, preventing the Three Generals from fully exerting their attacks, and their assaults weaken greatly after breaking the ice prisons.

Tian Shuang thus stabilizes the situation.

“Heh heh heh,” Earth Ash sneers coldly, emerging from the ground.

He aims from afar at the Iron Bucket Formation of the Three Generals’ Camp, waving his sleeves, casting wave after wave of ghastly green ghost fire.

Spell—Ghost Flame Scorches the World!

The ghost fire burns silently, surging like waves of green tide, swallowing the Iron Bucket Formation of the Three Generals’ Camp.

The Three Generals sense the danger, immediately reduce the use of Military Power, reassigning most of it to the soldiers to enhance their defense.

The ghost fire clashes with the Iron Bucket Formation, like waves hitting rocks, instantly dispersing to the sides.

Soon, more ghost fire rushes in, engulfing the entire Iron Bucket Formation.

“Hold the line, buy time for the three generals!” Chen Zhi roars, boosting morale.

This low-rank talent, who emerged from the selection process of Iron Skin Armor Technique, has shone in his own way after gaining the recognition and limited cultivation from the Three Generals.

Despite the raging and perilous ghost fire, the Iron Bucket Formation holds firm like an impregnable fortress.

Just when the Three Generals breathe a sigh of relief, Earth Ghost spits out a brown bone ball.

The bone ball extends and expands in the air, transforming into a massive hollow door frame in seconds.

A rapid vortex churns within the frame, its black spiral center spinning hundreds of times before abruptly enlarging—Ghost Gate Pass!

Inside the door frame lies the murky red Netherworld scenery.

“You have many people, but I have more ghosts! Hahaha,” Earth Ash laughs thrice, waving his hand.

Countless evil ghosts emerge from the Netherworld, connected by Ghost Gate Pass.

These evil ghosts are engulfed in flames, with Evil Qi surging around them. Some flames are bright red, others deep crimson.

Among the flames’ tongues and shadows, twisted, wailing human faces occasionally appear.

A humanoid lava skeleton supports the entire flame mass, making the fire itself form a vague human shape.

A fist-sized Karma Fire Core floats within the chest cavity, the true core.

“Fire Evil Ghosts, burn and kill them all!”

Numerous Fire Evil Ghosts endlessly rush out of Ghost Gate Pass, attacking the soldiers in the Three Generals’ Camp.

“Hold on!” Chen Zhi feels unprecedented pressure, his face ashen, gritting his teeth to endure.

The soldiers have already formed the Iron Bucket Formation, but under the relentless assault of the Fire Evil Ghosts, they suffer heavy casualties.

Earth Ghost’s brutal tactics immediately take effect, causing Liu Guanzhang’s faces to change dramatically.

Although the Three Generals know it’s the enemy’s strategy, they can’t help but “fall into the trap” and retreat.

Their cultivation levels are all at Golden Core Level, depending solely on the Battle Formation organized by numerous soldiers and the gathered Military Power to counter Nascent Soul Level enemies.

Thus, the soldiers in the Three Generals’ Camp are indispensable.

“Think you can escape?!” Tian Shuang roars sharply.

Having been suppressed by three mere Golden Core Level individuals for so long, he regains his senses, breaths deeply, and feels utterly humiliated.

The Three Generals want to return and salvage the crisis, but how can Tian Shuang let them leave easily after gaining the upper hand?

Instantly, Tian Shuang counters, forcibly battling against the Buddha Thoughts in his Divine Sea, temporarily blocking the Three Generals.

“Not good!” Seeing the situation, Ning Zhuo immediately decides to continue fighting.

The condition in his mind is worse than Tian Shuang’s, the Buddha Thoughts almost completely filling it.

However, this does not hinder him from rescuing the Three Generals’ Camp.

“By the grace of Buddha, heaven holds compassion!”

“These ordinary people are all living beings, worthy of saving.”

Ning Zhuo immediately pulls out a Magic Artifact Level Somersault Cloud, sits on it, and swiftly flies above the Three Generals’ Camp.

Overlooking the battlefield, he casts Five Elements Magic continuously.

At once, raging torrents rise, earth and stones churn, trees grow wildly, golden light slashes wildly!

Many Fire Evil Ghosts are injured and destroyed by Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo’s intervention instantly relieves the pressure on the Three Generals’ Camp.

He then deploys his Mechanical Army, engaging the Fire Evil Ghosts from the side.
