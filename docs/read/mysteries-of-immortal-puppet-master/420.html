Chapter 420: Chapter 61: Finger-Guessing Game (Two in One)

Great Struggle Peak.

Behind the mountain, within the Myriad Demons Cave.

Ning Zhuo stood up, wobbling, and sincerely thanked Linghu Jiu with a cupped fist salute.

“Little brother owes this fortuitous opportunity entirely to Brother Linghu’s generosity. Such kindness, Ning Zhuo will remember in his heart, never daring to forget!”

The Five Organs Temple Spiritual Divine Skill!

The complete Cultivation Technique reached the Refining Void Level. It is apparent that the Five Elements Divine Master is on the same level as the Three Superior Sect Masters.

...

Although the Three Superior Sect Techniques that Ning Zhuo practiced came from the Three Superior Sect Masters, they were currently only at the Foundation Establishment level. However, the complete “Five Organs Temple Spiritual Divine Skill” had now been obtained by Ning Zhuo.

This gain was extraordinarily astonishing!

Linghu Jiu shook his head and smiled, “My role was minimal, this was your opportunity. You should know, to inherit the legacy of a sage, one must catch the eye of that sage. If they don’t take a liking to you, they will definitely not teach you anything.”

“Ning Zhuo, your ability to continuously receive the legacy for such a long period indicates that the sage who imparted this Cultivation Technique to you favors you greatly. That’s one thing.”

“Secondly, if your comprehension was not up to par, after imparting a part of it, the sage would feel you’re unable to understand and would cease the transmission.”

“So, everything you’ve gained, you gained through your own strength. The role I played was far smaller than you imagine.”

At this point, Linghu Jiu looked at the two empty wine jars and sighed, “When I received the Lonely Sword Scripture, it only cost me a jug of Netherworld brew.”

“The Cultivation Technique you’ve obtained is of immense value, congratulations. Have you considered starting anew with it?”

Ning Zhuo shook his head.

The Five Organs Temple Spiritual Divine Skill was an ancient technique, of which Ning Zhuo had never even heard of the Five Elements Divine Master.

Ning Zhuo, versed in literature, was familiar with the talented people and sages of his era and the few generations before.

Ning Zhuo was certain he had never come across the name of the Five Elements Divine Master. This indicated that the person must be from an even more ancient time.

Ning Zhuo straightforwardly said, “The Cultivation Technique I primarily practice comes from the Lava Immortal Palace and originates from the Three Superior Sect Masters. Its grade is in no way inferior to this technique. ”

“I have an affinity for Mechanical Techniques; the Three Superior Sect Techniques are especially suited to me.”

“Futhermore, this newly acquired method is an ancient technique, the style and contemporary language differ greatly. In ancient times, Spiritual Energy was sparse, but in the current era, it is abundant; it is likely that ancient techniques are inferior to modern ones.”

There seemed to be an issue of adapting to the environment, and another was the order of the transmission of Cultivation Techniques in the Cultivation World, which has been continuously improving and renewing.

Even if a Sect was destroyed and a family wiped out, sages in the Netherworld still had control. Once a descendant with a thin bloodline performed a sacrifice, it was very likely they would receive their ancestor’s legacy.

Of course, the Three Superior Sect Techniques also had shortcomings. That was, Ning Zhuo only knew the Foundation Establishment and Qi Refinement parts; to obtain the content of the subsequent techniques, he would still have to go to the Lava Immortal Palace.

Additionally, the grade of the Three Superior Sect Techniques might not reach the Refining Void Level. Although they were created by the Three Superior Sect Masters, it was not certain that all techniques created by a Refining Void Level master reached the Refining Void Level.

Nevertheless, the Five Organs Temple Spiritual Divine Skill only targeted the Qi Sea, while the Three Superior Sect Techniques aimed at cultivating all three Dantians. Just from this point, it greatly surpassed the other.

“I will use this technique as a reference, this will be very helpful for my future practice of the Three Superior Sect Techniques.”

Ning Zhuo told Linghu Jiu, “The name of this Cultivation Technique is ‘Five Organs Temple Spiritual Divine Skill’, originating from the sage, Five Elements Divine Master. Brother Linghu, let us ponder over it together.”

Then, ah, Ning Zhuo recited the opening content of the Cultivation Technique.

Linghu Jiu hurriedly extended a hand, refusing, “Hold on, Young Master Ning Zhuo, this is your opportunity, not mine.”

“One should not casually transmit techniques!”

“This technique delves deeply into the mysteries of the Five Elements and is not suitable for me.”

“As I’ve said before, my contribution was just some Netherworld brew. It does not merit such a great reward!”

Ning Zhuo was startled, “This…”

The Nascent Soul Level Divine Sense of Lin Bufan was flaring intensely, “Foolish! Jiu’er is foolish, ah.”

“In the past, the Five Elements Divine Master dominated the world, controlling the Five Elements as if a sovereign descended upon the Earth. This was his primary Cultivation Technique; even if it is of ancient style, it contains the profound principles of the Five Elements.”

“In today’s world, it is still of enormous value. If brought to our Sect, it would be a legacy to stabilize the Sect!”

Lin Bufan was getting impatient as well.

His Divine Sense spread to the Great Struggle Peak, and at first glance, he saw his senior disciple, pouring Netherworld brew generously for Ning Zhuo, an outsider.

And Ning Zhuo, that lad, was drinking it without reserve, pound by pound, thoroughly enjoying himself!

Lin Bufan’s vision darkened.

Then, he saw Ning Zhuo wanting to share the Divine Skill, only to be directly refused by Linghu Jiu, infuriating him to the point where he wanted to rush to Great Struggle Peak, hang his senior disciple up, and thrash him.

“Who taught you to be so nobly principled?! Is this how I taught you?!”

Lin Bufan locked onto Ning Zhuo, still holding out hope.

But Ning Zhuo loudly praised Linghu Jiu’s noble character and didn’t mention sharing the Divine Skill anymore.

Lin Bufan’s temples twitched, “Ning Zhuo, that little punk, is really too clueless.”

“The Netherworld brew can be set aside, but the Five Organs Temple Spiritual Divine Skill…”

“I took the opportunity to have Ning Zhuo meet Jiu’er, not expecting him to gain such a great advantage! Ah, what is this all about?”

Ning Zhuo knew that this time, he had reaped a huge benefit.

He couldn’t help but be filled with emotion, “Before, there was Lin Shanshan, and now Linghu Jiu, Ten Thousand Medicine Valley truly is a good place, really my blessed land.”

He had never expected to have such an enormous gain!

Originally, Ning Zhuo merely wanted to meet and probe Linghu Jiu, the prospective Sect Leader of the Ten Thousand Medicine Sect.

But then, his opportunity arrived.

“Ah, when fortune comes, it truly can’t be blocked.”

Ning Zhuo wished he could immediately go into seclusion, to carefully comprehend the newly acquired Divine Skill. However, leaving now would be really awkward; it was just too ungracious.
