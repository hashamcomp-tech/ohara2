Chapter 1113: Chapter 416: Is It Really Just Luck?_2

For Situ Xing himself, he would never perform so brilliantly.

The reason is obvious; Situ Xing just isn’t that "righteous" of a person.

"What kind of temperament does the current Hall Master of the Evil Slaying Hall have? It’s certain, he must greatly admire Ning Zhuo!"

"If delayed, and we act later, I’m afraid it will be too late."

With this in mind, Situ Xing finally made up his mind and summoned Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong.

With a serious expression, he spoke to them with flattering words.

"Daoist Fellow Shen, Daoist Fellow Lin! We have formed this society, with the three of us as its pillars. From days of yore, our bond was made, our alliance as solid as gold and stone. Today, we gather at Yuntai to strive at the Feiyun Conference, aiming to soar in the nine skies. Since our ventures began, each of you has proven your valor, especially valued is our united spirit, working as a single unit."

"By heaven’s fortune, Daoist Fellow Shen has recommended a genius like Ning Zhuo, whose talent matches ours. His brilliance is indeed our great fortune!"

"Yet I observe that Ning Zhuo’s position is unsettled, which is a grave mistake! Should rumors spread across the lands, accusing us of overlooking distinguished talents and arrogantly snubbing the worthy, wouldn’t it tarnish our clan’s reputation?"

"My resolve is set; I shall choose an auspicious day to challenge Ning Zhuo to a contest at the Starfall Platform. If I lose, I will step aside and let Ning Sheng take the lead—willingly, witnessed by heaven and earth!"

Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong were both silent for a moment.

The two had long known Situ Xing, how could they not understand his true intent?

That talk of conceding the top position if defeated? Such formalities, hahaha, just listen and be done with it!

A cold glint flashed in Shen Xi’s eyes. Internally, he translated it as—I’m worried that this Ning Zhuo kid will usurp my power, so I need to deal with him first! I want to fight him, aiming to bring him down and remain the boss. You see, we three have a good relationship, you both have to support me.

Lin Jinglong, nurtured by his family since childhood, naturally deciphered Situ Xing’s true intentions as well.

He found it somewhat amusing: "Situ Xing’s eloquent display is a rare sight. It shows how much pressure Ning Zhuo has put on him, making him say such formalities to us both!"

"Hmm... indeed. Both Shen Xi and I have interacted with Ning Zhuo; I even fought him once."

"Ning Zhuo was personally recommended by Shen Xi to join."

Before the fight, actively rallying people, establishing allies, and creating a numerical advantage is a common maneuver for the Righteous Path. The new star of the Righteous Path, Situ Xing, is doing just that!

Situ Xing stared at Shen Xi, pressuring him to state his position.

Shen Xi pondered and said, "A snake cannot function without a head, such is the Heavenly Dao. Since the society’s inception, I have tirelessly followed Brother Situ’s lead. The chief’s position must not be coveted by outsiders."

"Though Ning Zhuo was recommended by me, our meeting was but by chance, how can it compare to the bond I have with Brother Situ?"

"Now, I shall personally pen a letter to Ning Zhuo, agreeing as follows: First, the position of chief belongs to Daoist Fellow Situ by consensus, and it is unshakable. Second, I willingly step back to the fourth position, granting the second position to Ning Zhuo."

"Thus, with all finding their place under the harmonious symphony of phoenixes, would it not be a boon to our alliance?"

"For the pillar not to fall, I am willing to follow; as long as our flags point in the same direction, if we work together, how can our grand endeavor fail?"

Seeing that Shen Xi was willing to support him, Situ Xing’s gaze softened.

"Ning Zhuo was brought in through his recommendation. If I clash with Ning Zhuo, Shen Xi sitting on the second seat could very well just sit back and watch the tigers fight. Even if Ning Zhuo wins, his position won’t be shaken."

Situ Xing was satisfied with Shen Xi’s stance.

But he was not pleased with Shen Xi’s suggestion.

Writing letters back and forth is too slow. It also gives time for Shen Xi and Ning Zhuo to communicate and react, likely leading to unexpected complications!

Situ Xing was very aware that summoning the two of them urgently was partly to launch a surprise attack, catching them off guard.

Situ Xing never completely trusted Shen Xi.

In the past, he suffered a loss within Shen Xi’s formation.

This time, in creating the alliance, he specifically made things difficult for Shen Xi to confirm the latter’s willingness to remain subordinate before feeling assured.

"What if Shen Xi is merely buying time and is actually leveraging Ning Zhuo to wear me down, seizing the opportunity to rise... it cannot be discounted."

Situ Xing shifted his gaze towards Lin Jinglong.

Lin Jinglong’s attitude was very firm.

He clearly expressed his concern that confrontations in the Martial Arts Hall disregard life and death. Should anything happen, how would the Situ Family and Ning Family react? Such an outcome would be terribly troublesome.

Even if no one dies, should there be serious injuries that lead to missing the Feiyun Conference, it would surely invite ridicule from outsiders.

Now, our mountain peak is quite fortunate to have exceptional talents like Situ Xing and Ning Zhuo. This is a rare situation and an opportunity that should be greatly treasured and seized.

In my opinion, Ning Zhuo doesn’t occupy any significant position, which suggests he has no interest in such matters. We should regard him simply as a Guest Elder-like figure.

From the beginning to the end, he has never participated in any decision-making affairs on this mountain peak.

Moreover, if the two of you were to face off, it would take considerable time to prepare, wouldn’t it? This would consume too much energy and time, and let’s not even mention the Feiyun Conference; even the current Xingyun Trials would lead to missing many opportunities!

A glint of brilliance flashed in Situ Xing’s eyes.

Lin Jinglong, surprisingly, was the one who firmly refused!

This once again exceeded Situ Xing’s expectations.

He originally thought Lin Jinglong would be more supportive of him than Shen Xi. As the third chair, Lin Jinglong should have entirely pure support for him.

"Lin Jinglong keeps saying he’s acting for the bigger picture, worried about both me and Ning Zhuo being mutually injured..."

"He once sparred with Ning Zhuo. Is his current attitude a testament to Ning Zhuo’s formidable combat strength?"

"By cultivation level, Ning Zhuo is merely at Foundation Establishment Mid-Stage. Is Lin Jinglong really valuing him so highly?"

Situ Xing’s expression turned cold.

He wanted the support of both, and while Shen Xi clearly expressed support, Situ Xing was not reassured. Lin Jinglong expressed opposition, fundamentally supporting neither side.

All because Ning Zhuo’s presence poses no threat to their interests!

Situ Xing shook his head slowly, his voice deep: "Brother Jinglong is overthinking! I have my own plans, how could I miss a penny’s worth? Ning Zhuo is extraordinary, my heart is indeed eager. Through battle, we can truly understand each other’s character. Do you both have no interest?"

Seeing Situ Xing express himself like this, Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong no longer had words of objection, willing to follow the arrangements.

"In that case, could Brother Shen please deliver this challenge letter?" Situ Xing handed the already prepared challenge letter to Shen Xi.

Shen Xi received the letter with swift movements.

After bidding farewell to Situ Xing alongside Lin Jinglong, they walked the return journey together.

Lin Jinglong transmitted thoughts with Divine Sense to express dissent to Shen Xi: Situ Xing and Ning Zhuo are both exceptional talents. If they could unite, I believe we would achieve remarkable results at the Feiyun Conference.

When two tigers fight, one side is bound to sustain injuries. The Myriad Images Sect does not prohibit life-or-death battles, you and I both know Situ Xing’s temperament, and Ning Zhuo’s true nature is unfathomable, should a fatality occur, our great situation now would be ruined.

Shen Xi, you’re muddle-headed, how could you agree so readily?

Shen Xi smiled slightly and first transmitted thoughts to ask Lin Jinglong: You’ve suffered losses at the hands of Ning Zhuo. Don’t you want to explore Ning Zhuo’s true nature? We know Situ Xing very well, but our acquaintance with Ning Zhuo is relatively short. By expressing this, you’re actually defending an outsider.

Lin Jinglong chuckled: "Shen Xi, don’t try to probe me. I used to be wary of Ning Zhuo, viewing him with caution. However, now, look at his history, Confucian cultivators hold him in high esteem, awarding him first place twice consecutively in The Three Questions of the Heart of the Evil Slaying Hall, and he answered them powerfully!"

"Ning Zhuo is a righteous person! A principled individual! He would be our most reliable ally in battle."

"At least when fighting alongside us, I’m not worried about him stabbing us in the back. But Situ Xing’s pride is unyielding, he prefers to keep everything in his own control... If massive benefits were at stake, wouldn’t it be natural for him to draw his sword against us?"

Hearing such a response from Lin Jinglong, Shen Xi felt reassured, and promptly said: "Since you think so, then let me tell you."

"Situ Xing has made up his mind, if I were him, I’d strike while the time is ripe."

"Even if we expressly oppose it, we cannot stop him."

"It’s inconvenient for us to intervene, and in this world, there are many cultivators who can stop him."

Lin Jinglong felt a stirring in his heart: "Do you mean..."

Shen Xi responded with a smile full of profound meaning: "The Shen Family has their mortal enemies in the Pi Family, doesn’t the Situ Family have theirs too?"

Lin Jinglong: "The Situ Family has many rivals, but who is the most suitable?"

Just as Shen Xi was about to offer his insight, a cultivator released a powerful aura, descending from the sky, calling out, "I bring a challenge letter from Miss Zhu Fenxiang. Where is Situ Xing? Does he dare accept this letter?!"

Lin Jinglong and Shen Xi were momentarily at a loss, looking at each other.

Lin Jinglong transmitted thoughts with Divine Sense, expressing awe: "The Zhu Family and the Situ Family are indeed mortal foes, this challenge letter is a matter of family honor; Situ Xing will unquestionably accept. This saves us a lot of trouble. Our luck is quite good, and so is Ning Zhuo’s, haha."

Shen Xi fell silent, with Ning Zhuo’s smiling visage surfacing in his mind.

"Is it really... just luck?"
