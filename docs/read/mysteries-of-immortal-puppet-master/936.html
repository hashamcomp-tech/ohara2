Chapter 936: Chapter 331: Shadow of the Silent Extinction Buddha

In His eyes, this cultivation army was merely a gathering of little ants.

Then, He pierced through the array’s concealment and detected the Returning Ruins Black Ball.

Finally, His gaze fell upon the mighty barrier formed by the expansive Earth-Lung Miasma.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion mused, "A ship sinking deeper, a dead wood to green anew. A lost game conceals the heavens’ play, a glimmer becomes a war star."

"So the meaning of the first half of this revelation is like this."

He, in essence, was the Life Buddha Wheel of the Silent Ghost Monk, primarily using the Divine Sense of the Silent Ghost Monk, aided by the Divine Power of the Silent Extinction Buddha.

This was the technique of invoking the gods!

Much like before the battle, when the Qing Jiao Army activated the festival camp to invite the Night Patrol Deity for assistance.

To the Silent Ghost Monk, this battle was crucial.

He was already a sworn enemy of the Lord of Wanchuan. If the Lord of Wanchuan succeeded in his path, gains and losses would shift, possibly marking the turning point of his future demise.

Therefore, he had to give his all in this battle to intercept the Lord of Wanchuan’s path!

Like Yin Jiu and the Lord of Wanchuan, he too had channels for divination.

However, while the former two invited others for divination, he prayed to Buddha and was granted a revelation.

When the Silent Ghost Monk cast the Life Buddha Wheel and was sealed by the Wanchuan Prefect Seal, the Lord of Wanchuan keenly sensed something amiss, smelled the scent of conspiracy.

His instincts were indeed correct!

Though the Silent Ghost Monk’s main body was injured and retreated, his Life Buddha Wheel preserved a great power, reserved for a final comeback!

Now, the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion became the greatest force in this decisive battle.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion reached out and immediately grasped the Returning Ruins Black Ball!

The Returning Ruins Finger was originally released by the Silent Ghost Monk. Although much was consumed, it was still worth using.

Wherever the Returning Ruins Black Ball went, everything was devoured and erased.

It resembled a terrifying eraser, clearing a straight line of anything in its path.

Ning Zhuo watched as it shot towards him, and a strong survival instinct broke through his desolate mindset, prompting him to risk flying away from his original position.

The surroundings were quite harsh, but fortunately, Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong had practiced flying skills diligently for a time.

Just after Ning Zhuo flew away, the Returning Ruins Black Ball brushed across, directly pulverizing the invisible hub.

Ning Zhuo’s Confucian skill greatly interfered with the invisible hub but couldn’t destroy it. The Returning Ruins Black Ball was entirely different, directly swallowing the invisible hub whole, instantly effective!

Without the support and hold of the invisible hub, the entire array space began to completely collapse.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion paid no heed to Ning Zhuo’s life or death, considering him an insignificant ant.

After grasping the Returning Ruins Black Ball, He infused a new Returning Ruins Finger power into it, causing it to expand by thirty percent.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion gently pushed the Returning Ruins Black Ball to the immense barrier of Earth-Lung Miasma.

Hiss... Hiss hiss hiss...

A subtle, dense, teeth-gnashing and chilling sound of dissolution arose.

The Earth-Lung Miasma barrier immediately collapsed, greedily swallowed by the Returning Ruins Black Ball, relentlessly torn into that deep, lightless extinction.

However, the power of the Returning Ruins Black Ball rapidly weakened in the consumption, its size continuously shrinking.

When it devoured a passage through the entire barrier, it directly dissipated at the endpoint.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion followed this massive passage, boldly breaking into the core battlefield!

Luo Si was astounded.

He was teetering on the brink of defeat, after enduring so much hardship, he’d awaited not Ning Zhuo, but the Buddha.

But regardless, he was now rescued.

Luo Si swiftly retreated.

The Lord of Wanchuan sighed, the Earth Evil God abandoned Luo Si, facing the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion directly.

"Silent Ghost Monk, you cultivate the Buddhist Scripture, yet remain deluded? Your body is already heavily wounded and retreating, how much power do you have left now?" The Lord of Wanchuan voiced.

The power of the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion indeed was dwindling, mainly needed to constantly suppress the Wanchuan Prefect Seal.

He had just utilized the Returning Ruins Black Ball, adding force precisely, just enough for the Returning Ruins Black Ball to penetrate the Earth Barrier, no more, no less. Such an extreme frugal combat stance had already revealed His details.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion let out a sneering laugh, "Lord of Wanchuan, how good can your condition be? The main body can only watch, and your Divine Separation... has it even been stripped of its underwear?"

In the midst of speaking, the Earth Evil God deflated once again, shrinking by a tenth.

Lord of Wanchuan: ...

The Nine Abyss Drain Water pouch applied to Divine Separation by Luo Si had already been withdrawn by the Earth Evil God. Luo Si reclaimed half of the Wanchuan bridging line.

But the root of the Earth Evil God’s deflation was still within the Wangchuan Immortal City. Though there was no longer the Nine Abyss Drain Water pouch, it addressed the symptom but not the cause.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion naturally wasn’t fooled by a few words and continuously approached the altar while speaking.

The Earth Evil God let out a low roar, halfway borrowing the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion.

Boom boom boom...

The clash between both sides instantly turned into intense fighting.

The Black Cloud Battlefield was rapidly collapsing.

Previously, Ning Zhuo exerted all his strength, deploying Confucian skill, only to greatly influence it. But the Returning Ruins Black Ball pulverized the invisible hub, completely obliterating it.

This caused the array to lose its greatest anchor, akin to a tower already wobbling and unstable, its foundational roots suddenly disappeared, its fate was one of collapse and ruin.

And the Black Cloud Battlefield, being the result of a great array, naturally couldn’t hold on.

Ning Zhuo once again embarked on a difficult escape.

This was already an immeasurable number of times.

With mere Foundation Establishment cultivation level, being involved in such a battlefield was indeed too perilous. Most of the time, he was frantically running for his life.

However, compared to himself, he was more worried about the Qing Jiao Army.

"After all, I still have the Iron Han Armor to protect me. But these people don’t have it; they might end up falling straight down from the cloud top heights."

"Huh?!"

The next moment, Ning Zhuo showed a hint of pleasant surprise.

Dark light shone on him, and an eerie wind arose out of nowhere, entwining his limbs.

This scene felt familiar to Ning Zhuo, as the Night Traveling Divine Technique was at play!

"So much time has passed, I didn’t expect the Night Traveling Divine Technique to still be functioning."

"The Night Patrol Deity we summoned is quite extraordinary!"

Ning Zhuo no longer worried about the Qing Jiao Army’s situation.

They ascended into the clouds relying on the Night Traveling Divine Technique. But when they entered the Black Cloud Battlefield, the enemy activated the great array, suppressing the Night Traveling Divine Technique, forcing the Qing Jiao Army to proceed on foot.

But now, the Heavenly Phase Array was mostly destroyed, leaving only the core part of the altar intact.

Moreover, on the Lord of Wanchuan’s side, beset by attacks from the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion, he no longer had the time for other concerns, hence the Qing Jiao Army no longer faced suppression and once more had the ability to fly.

"Qing Chi, lead the Qing Jiao Army and descend. It’s too dangerous here!" Ning Zhuo ordered Qing Chi from afar through the Life Hanging Thread, using Divine Sense Transmission.

The Qing Jiao Army was still too frail.

In such massive-scale decisive battles, apart from initially contributing, most of the time they could only serve as providers of military power.

With a bit of bad luck, brushed by a Transforming Spirit Level maneuver, the entire army could be wiped out.

Let alone, regarding the Night Traveling Divine Technique, who knows when it might be exhausted; apart from the Golden Core Level and a few cultivators, other cultivators in the Qing Jiao Army might meet their end falling.

Ning Zhuo still needed to provide military power, it’s a prudent move to have them descend first.

Qing Chi immediately accepted the order, retrieving the festival camp and tossing it to Jiao Ma’s father.

She escorted the Qing Jiao Army’s descent, halfway entrusting the army to Jiao Ma’s father, then soared and returned.

"Qing Chi, listen..." Ning Zhuo immediately noticed Qing Chi’s change through the Life Hanging Thread.

Qing Chi’s eyes burned like fire, her stance resolute: "No! I want revenge, make the Lord of Wanchuan pay in blood."

"Also..."

"Little Ma, I’m left with you. I want to help you, fight alongside you!"

"Even if it means dying, we die together!"

Ning Zhuo’s heart trembled slightly, his expression complex.
