Chapter 479: Chapter 96: Treating Petrification Dao Injury_3

Ning Zhuo offered a few modest words, then suddenly had an inspiration.

“I can’t control it myself, but I can rely on the Five Elements Qi Regulation Art to precisely manipulate the shares of Mana. Then design two Spells, one Fire-attribute and one Wood Element, to constrain the effects of the Earth Element Magic.”

“Fire produces Earth, Wood overcomes Earth.”

“Through the relationship of generation and conquest, I’ll finally be able to finely tune Earth Element Magic.”

With that thought, Ning Zhuo waved his hand, “Hold on, I’ve got a great idea.”

How to explain?

...

When one has a profound understanding of the Five Elements Realm, one can do as one pleases!

The two Spells he designed, although both could not be precisely controlled, acted like rulers, restricting the performance of the Earth Element Magic.

Zhang Zhongyi, witnessing Ning Zhuo construct two entirely new Spells, was greatly shaken and exclaimed in disbelief, “Are you also a master of Fire-attribute and Wood Element?”

“Could it be that you possess the Innate talent of Burning Earth Transformation, or a life born from the three attributes of Wood, Fire, and Earth?”

Ning Zhuo chose to conceal some truths, “I do possess Innate talent, but it has never been measured.”

Zhang Zhongyi took a deep breath, sized up Ning Zhuo, and clicked his tongue in wonder for a moment.

Ning Zhuo utilized three Spells, while Zhang Zhongyi prepared the herbs. Together, they rapidly restored Sun Lingtong’s left arm.

As sensation returned, Sun Lingtong gripped his left hand with a face full of joy.

Suddenly, a shock went through his heart, and he sensed something.

Seeing that Ning Zhuo was about to cast a Spell on his right arm, Sun Lingtong quickly hopped back to dodge.

“Wait, wait,” he called out.

“Big brother, do you feel discomfort?” Ning Zhuo instantly tensed.

But Sun Lingtong just laughed merrily, raising his left hand and boasting, “Quick, look, do you notice something different?”

Ning Zhuo enveloped it with his Divine Sense, his expression changing slightly, “Big brother, your Cultivation Technique primarily cultivates the Divine Sea, with only some attention to the other two Dantians.”

“But now, your left hand’s flesh and bones are much stronger than before, clearly different from the rest of your body. Why is this?”

Sun Lingtong winked at Ning Zhuo, “Little Zhuo, do you remember the Cultivation Technique you recently acquired by accident?”

Ning Zhuo was stunned, and the title “Heavenly Born Earth Nourished Fetal Breath Great Method” instantly came to mind.

His curiosity grew.

This time, Sun Lingtong conveyed the details to Ning Zhuo through a Spirit Transmission, “While I was idling in the Mechanical Dragon, I kept pondering the ‘Heavenly Born Earth Nourished Fetal Breath Great Method’ and gradually had some insights.”

“Suddenly one day, I thought maybe I could rely on this method to alleviate my injury. After all, if the body grows stronger, it also gains more resistance.”

“So, I lay in the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat, letting my whole person enter the state of Fetal Breathing.”

“To my surprise, after the Stone Skin was destroyed, my left hand’s fleshly body became far superior to the rest of my body. Tell me, can I use this affliction to cultivate my physique?”

“Big brother, this is…” Ning Zhuo was at a loss for words.

However, Ning Zhuo was used to it.

Sun Lingtong was always like this, full of bizarre and wonderful ideas.

Ning Zhuo examined Sun Lingtong’s left arm closely and briefly explained his Cultivation thoughts to Zhang Zhongyi.

Zhang Zhongyi stroked his beard and smiled, “There are actually many examples of using one’s own ailment for Cultivation.”

“For instance, the Cave Master of the Six Cave Sect, Blood Shadow, suffered from Blood Poison Illness since childhood. By practicing a Demon Path Cultivation Method, his Cultivation Level soared rapidly.”

“Another example is Young Master Shengxu, who was weak and sickly, with Innate Five Organs being extremely frail, even incomplete, but he rather formed a Weakness Qi Cave, attracting everything from the outside.”

“And there are some Cultivation Techniques, like the Vajra Unbreakable Skill, where one must constantly beat oneself to create injuries, so the body keeps repairing itself, becoming stronger and stronger.”

“If it’s really as Friend Sun says, then there may be no need to rush to heal.”

Sun Lingtong laughed heartily, “Indeed, this misfortune has turned into a blessing. Little Zhuo, you can also try it, the feeling of being petrified is quite unique.”

Ning Zhuo’s heart throbbed with excitement.
