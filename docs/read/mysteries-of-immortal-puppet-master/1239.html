Chapter 1239: Chapter 479: Ning Zhuo Returns the Treasure

Next, there was no suspense. The quantity of Haoran Xuan produced by Ning Zhuo far surpassed others, and the quality was as high as Heaven itself, leaving a group of cultivators in awe.

Even Gu Qing, upon realizing this, ceased his desire to win, no longer depleting his precious Vast Energy but instead exercising restraint.

Ultimately, Ning Zhuo won the top spot in this round with an overwhelmingly exaggerated lead, leaving no room for dispute!

When Situ Gu announced the results, he looked at Ning Zhuo with complex emotions. He wanted Gu Qing to win, but Ning Zhuo’s performance was too astonishing, making it impossible for him to show any bias.

However, although this round was over, Ning Zhuo’s actions were not yet finished.

He took out the White Rainbow Righteousness Festival again, held it in his hand, and displayed it to everyone, informing them that he was able to produce Vast Energy thanks to the Evil Slaying Hall lending him their treasure.

Yet what virtue does he have to hold onto such a treasure for long?

Therefore, upon winning this time, he wanted to return this treasure and thank the Evil Slaying Hall!

"Fellow Daoists, if interested, you may accompany me as witnesses." Ning Zhuo smiled and publicly invited.

"Let’s go together, let’s go together!" Many cultivators responded.

Seeing there was more excitement to witness, a large number of cultivators stayed.

The three rounds had already made Ning Zhuo the most popular cultivator. Initially, it was Ning Zhuo’s refining of the Top-grade Mountain and River Page that made many people want to buy them, driven by interest. Then, Ning Zhuo, who was not a Confucian Cultivator, managed to embarrass Gu Qing, an outsider, winning many hearts. Now, Ning Zhuo publicly recited poetry, and his Vast Energy was like a White Rainbow Piercing the Sun — what a righteous person he is!

Everyone felt at ease dealing with Ning Zhuo and wanted to make such a friend.

The cultivators who came to watch also wanted to form a virtuous connection!

Ning Zhuo purposefully rode a cloud, flying in the front, heading straight for the main hall of the Evil Slaying Hall.

Among the audience, Zhong Dao’s subordinates saw Ning Zhuo’s open declaration and proactive actions; they were so excited that their bodies slightly trembled, hastily using their methods to send messages ahead.

The main hall of the Evil Slaying Hall.

Zhong Dao listened as the core members in the hall continued to exchange and discuss, all striving to come up with solutions to resolve the current predicament.

The message arrived.

Zhong Dao’s heart stirred: "Ning Zhuo has announced that he is coming to my place to publicly return the White Rainbow Righteousness Festival?"

Originally, after this small test of Haoran Xuan, Zhong Dao intended to reclaim the White Rainbow Righteousness Festival.

This is one of the foundations of the Evil Slaying Hall, an undisputed treasure. Previously, lending it to Ning Zhuo was already an exception, primarily because Zhong Dao wanted to test Ning Zhuo.

Now that Ning Zhuo was returning it voluntarily, it showed that Zhong Dao found him to be very sensible.

"The key is, he returns it publicly and has deliberately brought a group to bear witness..." Zhong Dao immediately understood Ning Zhuo’s intention.

"This young man indeed has a clever mind."

"This is a political showcase."

"He wants to maximize the use of the White Rainbow Righteousness Festival!"

Zhong Dao knew of Ning Zhuo’s performance in Xuan Armor Cave, and this time, realizing Ning Zhuo’s intention, he no longer harbored any doubt as before.

The White Rainbow Righteousness Festival provided Ning Zhuo with the strongest proof!

On this basis, Zhong Dao increasingly admired Ning Zhuo.

To defeat evil, righteousness naturally must be stronger and wiser. With such excellent innate talent as Ning Zhuo’s, even at Foundation Establishment Mid-Stage, he is already extraordinary. He will undoubtedly grow stronger in the future. And possessing such seasoned righteous methods—isn’t that wisdom?

"If he does not stray from this path, he will certainly become a pillar of the Righteous Path in the future!" Zhong Dao’s expectation for Ning Zhuo’s future rose higher.

"Since that’s the case, why not cooperate with him on this occasion?"

Thinking of this, Zhong Dao secretly transmitted thoughts with Divine Sense to instruct the guards at the main entrance to prepare.

Ning Zhuo’s presence was grand along the way, with the size of the entourage continuously growing, approaching the entrance of the Evil Slaying Hall main hall.

Three cultivators stood outside early to await Ning Zhuo’s arrival.

Seeing Ning Zhuo, they promptly went to greet him, stating they were aware of Ning Zhuo’s purpose and invited him and the cultivators to follow them.

When Ning Zhuo and everyone arrived at the main hall, Zhong Dao had already activated the grand formation of the hall, creating a large array space within.

Ning Zhuo stepped into the grand hall, looked up to see Zhong Dao sitting on the main seat of the high platform, with cultivators on both sides, from Golden Core to Nascent Soul, each with different expressions, revealing an aura of sharpness.

Ning Zhuo walked steadily forward, his wide sleeves draped like clouds, bowing respectfully to Zhong Dao, and his voice clear as the chime of jade: "This junior Ning Zhuo, graciously received the Hall Master’s kindness, borrowing the treasure to combat evil. Having won first place today, I have come specifically to return it. The White Rainbow Righteousness Festival is here, returned intact!"

Zhong Dao stroked his beard, smiling with a voice that echoed through the hall: "Good! Young friend Ning Zhuo’s Vast Energy today goes straight to the skies, illuminating all phenomena. Such splendor is rarely seen by this old man."

Ning Zhuo bowed again, his eyes as clear as autumn waters: "This junior’s qualifications are clumsy, fortunately receiving the opportunity bestowed by the Hall Master. While holding the festival, I felt the Vast Energy swelling in my chest, as if seeing the heroic posture of past sages slaying evil and upholding the path, as if hearing the teachings of past saints and sages supporting righteousness. The Hall Master’s guidance is an unforgettable grace!"

After speaking, he offered up the White Rainbow Righteousness Festival with both hands.

Zhong Dao made a gesture with his hand, using Divine Sense to bring the White Rainbow Righteousness Festival back into his own hand.

He looked at Ning Zhuo with even more approval: "Good."

"Do you know: The abundance of Righteous Energy lies in sincerity; the steadfastness of the Dao heart is precious for its endurance. Although today’s Vast Energy is already world-shaking, the days are long ahead, you still need to: be diligent like standing above an abyss, practice unceasingly like treading on thin ice, progress daily like spring sprouts bursting forth, and always maintain your original intention in order to achieve completion!"

Ning Zhuo solemnly responded: "This junior remembers the Hall Master’s teachings: to take the past sages of the Evil Slaying Hall as models, to take the protection of all living beings as my mission, to take upholding the righteous path as my lifelong pursuit. Though facing death nine times over, I will not regret it, facing a thousand hardships, I will still strive forward!"
