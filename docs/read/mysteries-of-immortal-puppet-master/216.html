Chapter 216

Ninth level peak!

Ning Zhuo felt stronger than ever before.

When he was at the eighth level, he frantically underwent purification, completely eliminating foreign qi from his body, solidifying his foundation.

Now at the ninth level, he found that the purification of the Three Treasures cultivation technique still needed to continue.

His cultivation level had improved too quickly, it needed to be consolidated.

In this aspect, he had to slow down.

As the saying went, sharpening the axe does not delay the woodcutter’s work. Otherwise, haste makes waste!

"While refining and purifying my ninth-level mana, I need to find a Foundation Establishment method. There should be one in the Magma Fairy Palace!"

According to Ning Zhuo’s speculation, after pushing open the third Buddha gate and passing through three checkpoints, the Foundation Establishment method should be there.

"A Foundation Establishment method alone seems insufficient. In many books, people seek Foundation Establishment items to increase their chances of success as much as possible."

Not everyone could succeed in Foundation Establishment.

And for someone like Ning Zhuo, who cultivated the Three Elixir Fields simultaneously, the difficulty of Foundation Establishment was far beyond ordinary.

Ning Zhuo didn’t have much confidence.

Most of his confidence came from the Three Ancestral Masters.

According to Ning Zhuo’s thoughts, since the Three Ancestral Masters had left these three cultivation techniques, it wouldn’t be immaturely conceived techniques specifically left to harm future generations.

"Judging by their character, they should have left many auxiliary items for Foundation Establishment as rewards."

Ning Zhuo had already arranged the purification of his ninth-level mana properly.

When he was at the eighth level of cultivation, he had saved the usage of the Light Profound Ice-Purifying Snow Talisman, reserving it for the ninth level.

"With the addition of Pure One Pills, formation, the Clear Source Treasure Mirror, and the Dragon Tornado, the purification and refining of mana will be completed quickly!"

Preparing for Foundation Establishment, refining a Golden Core level mechanical monkey, and exploring the Magma Fairy Palace were the three major tasks in front of Ning Zhuo.

After considering for a moment, he decided to focus on exploring the Immortal Palace next!

The reason was simple.

Whether it was Foundation Establishment or refining a Golden Core level mechanical monkey, both tasks done solely by Ning Zhuo would yield low results.

If he gained anything from exploring the Immortal Palace, it would greatly help with both of these tasks.

He then conjured layers of floating clouds to cover himself, and his soul entered the Immortal Palace.

By now, even if he entered the palace alone and was discovered, he wouldn’t be highly suspicious.

However, this time, after entering the Immortal Palace, he did not see anyone else.

This was also one of the reasons Ning Zhuo had purposely sold the subsequent cultivation techniques before!

Almost everyone was crazily cultivating, striving to achieve the sixth-level peak as soon as possible to push open the second Dao gate.

Ning Zhuo alone broke through over a dozen levels.

The entire Immortal Palace was empty, as if time had turned back to when he first explored the palace.

When he arrived at the third central hall, he unhesitatingly chose the third Buddha gate and pushed it open.

Dragon Rock Immortal Palace, main hall.

The Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit was so shocked to see Ning Zhuo push open the Buddha gate that it fell from its throne, landing heavily on the floor.

It never expected that Ning Zhuo had actually cultivated to the ninth-level peak.

How did he get there so quickly?!

The Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit was in extreme shock.

The Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit had not observed this secret.

Since Ning Zhuo used the Floating Cloud Handkerchief to conceal himself, the Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit had consciously reduced its observation intensity by a lot.

Its insight was derived from the Magma Fairy Palace, extremely overbearing, able to penetrate the floating clouds and see Ning Zhuo’s real condition.

But doing so would greatly consume the cloud layers, arousing Ning Zhuo’s vigilance.

The Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit didn’t want to be crude, worrying that this little monster Ning Zhuo would discover its presence because of this.

Therefore, it was only at this moment that the Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit truly recognized Ning Zhuo’s actual cultivation level.

However, the shock did not end there!

After Ning Zhuo pushed open the third Buddha gate and entered the first checkpoint, he found that the process to pass the checkpoint was cumbersome and time-consuming.

Thus, Ning Zhuo used the Buddha Heart Demon Seal, directly backed out, and returned to the central hall.

From the central hall, he continued to push open the third Dao gate and the third Demon gate.

The difficulty of the checkpoints behind the Dao gate was very high, and Ning Zhuo didn’t have confidence at the moment.

The first room behind the Demon gate was a special checkpoint.

By employing the Demon-infused Bloodline Technique, cultivators could pass numerous tests, ultimately obtaining different quality Blood Path Diagram Plates.

After considering comprehensively, Ning Zhuo still returned to the Buddha gate route.

From start to finish, this route was the most suitable for him!

Essence Sea ninth-level peak!

Qi Sea ninth-level peak!

Seeing Ning Zhuo passing through the three gates as if drinking water, the Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit was completely numbed with shock!

"What exactly has happened to Ning Zhuo?"

"Could it be the previous dual cultivation?"

The Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit had paid attention to the battle in the underground base.

At that time, the Ghost Mist Whirlpool had not completely obscured its insight.

Yet, even though the Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit knew that Ning Zhuo had previously interacted with Yang Chanyu, a Golden Core level cultivator, the extent of Ning Zhuo’s advancement seemed quite abnormal.

The Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit was an insider, it knew the detailed contents of the Three Superior Sect Techniques.

The Three Superior Sect Techniques, starting from the seventh level to the ninth level, demanded vigorous mana accumulation. The extent of cultivation accumulation was three times the sum of the early and mid-stages!

Ning Zhuo and Yang Chanyu had once generated Yin-Yang energy, but the process did not last long.

"Why is the effect so good?"

"Is it because of Ning Zhuo’s talent, or because Yang Chanyu was actually dual cultivating for the first time?"

However, these speculations were no longer important.
