Chapter 1217: Chapter 466: Zhu Guizhi vs Ning Zhuo (Bonus +4)_2

Zhu Guizhi’s eyes flashed with a chill: "Indeed, what just happened was Ning Zhuo using his full power. The combat strength unleashed by his mechanical puppet is clearly different from before."

Noticing this, Zhu Guizhi became even more furious and urged Lord Qingling to intensify the fierce attack.

But now with Ning Zhuo in control, the battle took on a completely different aspect.

While fighting, Snow Core Control·Xie exhibited extraordinary spirit movement, and simultaneously used Golden Core Power to condense new magic ice tendons to make up for the deficiency.

Furthermore, the Nascent Soul Level treasure Eternal Silence Mystic Ice at its core was slowly being consumed, completing the construction of the Hexagonal Mysterious Ice Armor.

Zhu Guizhi appeared surprised: "Ning Zhuo exerted his full power, and even with Lord Qingling alone, despite using divine skills, it’s hard to suppress him!"

"This young man is arrogant, but he has the backing."

"Among the mechanism materials, I sensed the aura of Eternal Silence Mystic Ice. No wonder the ice frost qi carries a sense of eternality, continuously eroding the divine body."

Zhu Guizhi understood Lord Qingling quite well, knowing that the latter had no strong trump cards left. According to the current trend, he would inevitably be overturned by Ning Zhuo.

Zhu Guizhi once again performed a hand seal, mana gushing and transforming into arcs of electricity at her fingertips, constantly jumping and flashing.

"Summon——Golden Light Saintess, wield the Eye of Heavenly Punishment!"

Crack!

A dazzling lightning bolt struck down without warning, and almost instantly, it materialized into a new divine avatar.

Electric Mother·Golden Light Saintess!

She wore golden misty clothes, overlaid with a moon white immortal ribbon, her eyes sharp as lightning. In her hands, divine power transformed into a pair of divine artifact-like items — the Lightning Mirror.

The left mirror was deep like night, the right mirror was dazzling as day.

The Electric Mother’s avatar floated mid-air, the two mirrors in her hands converged.

Divine Technique — Heavenly Thunder!

A dazzling electric bolt, as thick as a water barrel, erupted with a thunderous roar, directly aiming at Snow Core Control·Xie.

The Electric Mother’s avatar possessed Golden Core Level combat strength. Against two opponents, the battlefield situation immediately changed dramatically.

Snow Core Control·Xie completely fell into disadvantage, with bolts of thunder crashing down, shattering her Hexagonal Mysterious Ice Armor into fragments.

Ning Zhuo felt a slight joy in his heart and attempted full control, sensing his own limits.

"Fighting two is indeed too overwhelming."

He struggled to stabilize Snow Core Control·Xie’s position, his divine sense severely depleted, exerting all means to resist the enemy’s attack wave while repairing himself, overwhelmed both internally and externally.

"How do you feel now, Young Master Ning Zhuo?" Zhu Guizhi smiled slightly.

She suspected that Ning Zhuo might have a second Golden Core puppet, but it didn’t matter!

Ning Zhuo was just one person.

There’s a difference between fully controlling one mechanical puppet and two.

"This is the beauty of the Divine Summoning Technique!"

"Divine avatars fight on their own, without requiring me, a cultivator, to be distracted."

"Mechanical techniques are still inconvenient."

Ning Zhuo chuckled: "Mrs. Zhu, my means are not limited to this."

He patted the storage waist belt and placed Snow-colored Girl·Hui into the battlefield.

She was seven feet three inches tall, gracefully poised, with her body covered in flowing, radiant Three-color Enamel Armor. Most strikingly, beneath her pair of main arms, she had another two pairs of secondary hands.

All three pairs and six arms suddenly unfolded.

Snow-colored Girl·Hui hovered in mid-air, her six mechanical hands simultaneously forming seals, movements graceful and precise, like dancing on ice but with a lethal hidden danger.

She joined the battle directly facing the Electric Mother’s avatar, which had just displayed its divine power!

"Oh? Indeed there is a second Golden Core puppet." Zhu Guizhi immediately looked over with concern.

Ning Zhuo appeared to control, but actually allowed Snow-colored Girl·Hui to fight independently.

Several frost spells were used to counter the Thunder God Technique.

Zhu Guizhi watched for a moment and immediately noticed the difference between Snow-colored Girl·Hui and Snow Core Control·Xie. The former’s actions were more precise, limited to singular targets, while the latter tended towards large-scale attacks.

Snow-colored Girl·Hui’s more spirited performance led Zhu Guizhi to mistakenly believe that she was Ning Zhuo’s primary battle puppet.

"However, after deploying two, Ning Zhuo’s control power has indeed dropped significantly." Zhu Guizhi was misled by appearances, oblivious to Ning Zhuo gradually relaxing control over Snow Core Control·Xie.

Training warriors, now is a good opportunity!

It’s not easy to train warriors using the divine avatars at the Golden Core Level.

The cost of divine summoning is still paid by Zhu Guizhi, making it even more worthwhile.

Snow-colored Girl·Hui used softness against hardness to temporarily stabilize the situation against the Electric Mother’s avatar.

Snow Core Control·Xie faced the Earth God Avatar and began to gradually regain control.

Zhu Guizhi once again saw a dire trend and immediately used divine sense to communicate with the Electric Mother’s avatar.

Divine Technique — Flowing Light Instant Shadow!

In an instant, the Electric Mother’s avatar became a streak of electric light, its speed reaching an extreme.

Ning Zhuo’s heart trembled, immediately intervening in the battlefield.

At the critical moment, he decisively controlled Snow-colored Girl·Hui, casting defensive magic, narrowly avoiding defeat.

Fast!

So fast!

Too fast!!

The Electric Mother’s avatar was like a startling swan, traversing the battlefield, single-handedly suppressing Snow-colored Girl·Hui and Snow Core Control·Xie without suspense.

"Still lacking potent frost spells!" Ning Zhuo recognized the weakness.

The spells mastered by Snow-colored Girl·Hui and Snow Core Control·Xie were preset, fixed in number and content, exposed when faced with electric strikes.

Both mechanical puppets suffered severe damage, their bodies in tatters.

"Young Master Ning Zhuo, perhaps you should deploy a third Golden Core mechanism," Zhu Guizhi tested and provoked.

Ning Zhuo snorted coldly: "There’s no need for that."

The next moment, Snow-colored Girl·Hui activated her innate ability, first freezing Snow Core Control·Xie in ice, then enveloping herself in the same.

This passive defense earned Ning Zhuo precious time to repair their mechanical bodies.

Meanwhile, the power of Ice-jade Hands within the ice possessed healing efficacy, effectively treating spiritual nature.

Though their spiritual nature was heavily protected, it had sustained damage during the intense battle.

The power of Ice-jade Hands, though not highly targeted, offered some healing, like how Yuan Dasheng’s spiritual nature was sustained by such ice before.

Seeing this, Zhu Guizhi’s eyes gleamed, assuming Ning Zhuo had exhausted his options, forced into passive choices.

The Electric Mother’s avatar thought the same, leading the charge, fiercely striking the ice containing Snow Core Control·Xie.

In the fierce battle, Snow-colored Girl·Hui, despite entering late, had more body damage than Snow Core Control·Xie due to material limitations.

Under the severe interference from the Electric Mother’s avatar, electricity penetrated the ice, striking Snow Core Control·Xie’s mechanical body.

This also exposed the Electric Mother’s avatar.

"You’ve fallen into my trap." At the critical moment, Ning Zhuo boldly triggered Snow Core Control·Xie’s self-destruct.

In an instant, the previously accumulated offensive was unleashed by Ning Zhuo, transforming into a vast cold wave, covering the entire martial arts arena within moments.

The Earth God·Qing Ling Gong suffered severe damage, barely holding on.

And the Electric Mother’s avatar nearest to it was directly blown apart!

The self-destruct transformed Snow Core Control·Xie, rapidly restored to its original state under Ning Zhuo’s manipulation, at the cost of the swift consumption of the core’s Eternal Silence Mystic Ice.

Ning Zhuo smiled faintly: "Mrs. Zhu, you should summon the third god now."

Zhu Guizhi: ...

After a moment of silence, she decisively conceded.
