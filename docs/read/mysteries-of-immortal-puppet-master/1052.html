Chapter 1052: Chapter 387: [Songtao Qi-Nurturing Technique]

Ban Ji clutched his heart, gasping for a long time before calming his mind.

But the icy look on his face couldn’t be changed.

His plans had been severely disrupted!

He subconsciously looked up to the sky, feeling a deeper mystery and malice related to the Human Tribulation.

"That Che Zhuzi is a member of the Myriad Images Sect, yet this Silk Lady, an outsider, dares to make a move against him."

"Even if it’s just seduction, once discovered, the consequences would be dire! If Che Zhuzi realizes what’s happening, it’s perfectly reasonable for him to kill Silk Lady."

"This guy is too bold!"

Ban Ji felt regret.

"These scattered Demon Cultivators, even if they know the rules of the Myriad Images Sect, only understand superficially and can’t grasp the details and nuances!"

"Once the case is exposed, if they trace it back to me, I’d suffer serious repercussions."

"Damn it."

Ban Ji clutched his fists tightly.

He needed to plan for the worst and find the best countermeasures.

If Silk Lady gets caught, he can’t expect her to keep silent for his sake; the greatest possibility is mutual incrimination.

In short, don’t count on the bottom line of Demon Cultivators.

"In fact, even if Silk Lady is killed, there are always ways to Soul Search in this world."

"No way!"

"I must quickly sever my ties with this matter..."

Ban Ji frowned deeply, thinking of this, a chill ran from the depths of his heart.

Clearly, if he were to completely sever ties with the four Demon Cultivators now, he wouldn’t be able to recover the heavy funds he invested, nor use this means to control and entangle Ning Zhuo.

"Is this the Human Tribulation? Forcing me to face it actively?"

"No!"

Ban Ji’s expression turned fierce: "I just need to sever ties with Silk Lady and immediately sign contracts with the other three to prevent them from breaking the unspoken rules and incriminating me after an incident."

"But no matter what, Che Zhuzi cannot be touched."

Ban Ji was well aware that if he insisted on executing his original plan, the consequences could be quite severe!

Even if Ning Zhuo wasn’t involved, once the Myriad Images Sect investigated and discovered Ban Ji orchestrated Silk Lady’s actions, and ultimately he benefited from Che Zhuzi’s Xingyun Trial.

What would the Myriad Images Sect think?

Ban Ji knew very well: according to the style of the Myriad Images Sect, they would certainly punish him severely without mercy!

"Void Spider Silk, my Void Spider Silk..."

Ban Ji lamented in his heart, having to painfully give it up.

He felt extremely aggrieved because this time Ning Zhuo didn’t even intervene, and the real obstacle stemmed from himself!

Ning Zhuo woke up refreshed, the fatigue completely gone.

"My soul is a Human Soul of a million, and my physical body has undergone multiple trainings, almost fully bearing the Human Soul of a million!"

The foundation of the soul and body revealed their power at that moment.

Past accumulation and efforts had not been in vain.

His previous state was so poor that Ning Zhuo finally had the strength to review.

"This time, I used almost all the means at my disposal to learn, achieving considerable results."

"I understood the structure of the twelve forms of Helical Muscle in the Demon-infused Bloodline Technique, which consume less Blood Qi than the basic Condensation Method while increasing strength by at least thirty percent."

Ning Zhuo finally tore away the veil over the Demon-infused Bloodline Technique, revealing its true form.

"Head hanging from the beam helps eliminate distractions. Stabbing the thigh generates penetrating thoughts, aiding in overcoming learning difficulties."

"When Divine Sense is exhausted, it can passively consume Literary Style, generating streams of Literary Energy to nourish the Upper Dantian and extend study time."

"The Luoshu Book Pages are like always dialing the correct abacus, ensuring that every calculation and direction I take is correct."

"The combination of Bitter Ink and Ideograph Brush greatly enhances their efficacy, providing me with higher levels of hidden knowledge beyond words and pictures, allowing for rapid insight."

"The downside is leaving a bitterness in the Divine Sea’s Upper Dantian."

"It’s a pity. If I had the True Scripture left by the Three Sects Elder of the Demon-infused Bloodline Technique, the combination of Bitter Ink and Ideograph Brush would be extremely effective."

"What I’m studying now is merely what I’ve personally copied into a Jade Slip."

Even Jade Slips made by Golden Core Level Cultivators practicing the Demon-infused Bloodline Technique are better than those Ning Zhuo crafted personally.

Because others’ understanding always differs from one’s own.

With the combination of Ideograph Brush and Bitter Ink, Ning Zhuo could acquire this implied meaning through the true scripture.

"It’s really a shame!"

"The Three Sects Elder once left a true copy of the Demon-infused Bloodline Technique, a true scripture!"

"In the end, without my knowledge, it became materials for Yuan Dasheng’s refining."

The intent contained in the true scripture wouldn’t be ordinary profound meaning but True Intent.

Cultivators can absorb True Intent directly, comprehending the various mysteries in the true scripture’s cultivation techniques, making it very convenient.

This also implies the high value of true scriptures, as few Cultivators can produce them, with almost no circulation in the market.

Ning Zhuo instinctively twirled his fingers, deducing the review to the end.

"The help from Literary Style is not insignificant either."

"Unfortunately, I can’t practice Confucianism concurrently; otherwise, I could convert Literary Style into more and higher quality Literary Energy!"

"From observing the small, one can know the considerable advantage of Confucian Cultivators in learning."

"Therefore, mastering the Pine Wave Cultivation Technique is essential."

"Moreover, even though Song Taosheng allowed me to absorb the mountain’s Pine Wave Energy at any time, this behavior seemingly takes advantage but potentially carries greater costs."

"The free ones are always the most expensive!"

"Therefore, I still need to personally seek pine trees, perform planting, and strive for self-sufficiency."
