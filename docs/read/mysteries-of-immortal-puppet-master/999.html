Chapter 999: Chapter 362: I Don’t Like Conflict, I Resolve Conflict

Fire Element Heart Temple!

This was one of the Five Viscera Spirit God Techniques utilized by Ning Zhuo, resolving the troubles within Gongsun Yan’s Divine Sea.

The various angers and irritabilities within his Divine Sea were all caused by heart fire, imbued with the essence of fire-attribute.

The Fire Element Heart Temple transforms the heart into a divine temple, allowing Ning Zhuo’s Divine Sense to reside in the divine statue, enhancing his control over the fire attribute, becoming the master of fire!

This places the individual above the fire attribute, manipulating it at will, essentially placing personal will above the principles of heaven and earth. No wonder the Five Elements God Master incurred the wrath of the heavens with this technique. His attempt to pass on his cultivation technique has always been in vain, almost impossible to propagate until he encountered Ning Zhuo.

When Ning Zhuo uses this Divine Technique, he can fully control the fire attribute within himself.

Now, using it on Gongsun Yan, he could naturally fully control all fire-attribute-related aspects within him.

All the anger, irritability, and similar emotions were swept away by Ning Zhuo utilizing the Five Elements Life and Death Fan, absorbed completely into his Fire Element Heart Temple.

Then, they were suppressed mercilessly by the divine statue within the heart temple, not even a ripple could arise.

Compared to Gongsun Yan, Ning Zhuo’s soul, Divine Sea, and spirit far exceeded. It’s hard to imagine such a vast difference appearing between two cultivators of the Foundation Establishment level.

This was already part of Ning Zhuo’s treatment plan, but when Ning Zhuo controlled it to this extent, a small unexpected incident occurred.

Innate Talent——True Self!

Ning Zhuo’s third innate talent suddenly activated, a profound power instantly swept through the Fire Element Heart Temple, eradicating all anger and irritability!

Ning Zhuo was secretly astonished, soon followed by a surge of joy.

He originally intended to suppress it first and then use other methods to dissipate these external angers and irritabilities. Unexpectedly, the activation of the third innate talent instantly eliminated this internal threat.

After pondering briefly, Ning Zhuo immediately understood some things: "These angers, irritabilities, etc., all belong to Gongsun Yan, not to me."

"So under the emphasis of the True Self talent, these foreign objects were all eradicated. Because they were not mine, merely influences from the outside world!"

"This means that numerous mind-influencing methods from the outside world, if applied to me, would directly trigger the True Self talent, greatly reducing their efficacy or rendering them entirely ineffective?"

This was undoubtedly a small but pleasant surprise for Ning Zhuo.

Even though Ning Zhuo had acquired this innate talent, it was still an unexpected event, leaving him with little knowledge about the True Self talent.

Of course, he wanted to understand more, but wasn’t he pressed for time?

For him, there were so many more important things. When he led the Qing Jiao Army from the Netherworld back to the Mortal World, he faced many urgent matters needing his attention.

For instance, quickly reaching a new arrangement with the White Paper City Lord to maintain the Qing Jiao Army’s position in the Yin Tide Black Swamp.

Also, finding time to quickly reach the Myriad Images Sect to participate in the Feiyun Conference.

Additionally, secretly infiltrating the Shuyu Pavilion to retrieve all sorts of relics left behind by Meng Yaoyin, providing the most solid material foundation for the healing by Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin.

Doing all these, Ning Zhuo also found the opportunity to elevate his Qi Sea cultivation by one layer.

Having mastered his second Lifebound Technique, his first choice was naturally to refine his Lifebound Magical Artifact. After all, this path would significantly enhance his strength.

In contrast, delving into innate talents was not the same. Ning Zhuo truly did not have much understanding of the True Self talent. The gains here were not apparent, making it naturally not a priority.

"My mysterious talent, previously warned about by the Five Elements God Master, will be set aside for now."

"However, the True Self talent and the Early Wisdom talent can both be explored. It’s just that the timing isn’t right currently."

If innate talents are developed appropriately, they can evolve into Divine Skills, greatly enhancing their power.

The Divine Power Life Thread mastered by Ning Zhuo played a tremendous role in his cultivation and battles in various aspects.

"That being said, I have another Divine Power, Stone Cast Earth Treasure, but it doesn’t have much use."

This cannot be helped.

The latter appears to be a technique for management.

The effectiveness of Stone Cast Earth Treasure can indeed transform the target item, but the process is too slow to be effective in combat.

It can be considered a more powerful, comprehensive, portable version of Shuyu Court.

Ning Zhuo noticed Gongsun Yan’s fiery gaze fixed on the Five Elements Life and Death Fan in his hand and couldn’t help but smile slightly: "Do you want it?"

Gongsun Yan was too eager and nodded involuntarily.

Having just performed this action, he immediately regretted it, startled, realizing he was firmly in Ning Zhuo’s grasp.

Ning Zhuo lightly shook the fan surface: "This cannot be given to you."

This is a Lifebound Magical Artifact, engraved with the talismanic imprints of Lifebound Techniques.

If it fell into the hands of others, they could study these talismanic imprints to deepen their understanding of Ning Zhuo’s Lifebound Techniques, thereby penetrating a portion of the Five Elements Qi Regulation Art.

Lifebound Techniques are the essence of a cultivation technique; to understand Lifebound Techniques is to understand the essence of a cultivation technique.

Denied, Gongsun Yan swallowed hard and immediately felt that Ning Zhuo’s smile was filled with mockery.

He snorted coldly, attempting to appear strong, but ended up saying: "What must I do to obtain this treasure?"

Don’t blame him for such humility; the help of the Five Elements Life and Death Fan to Gongsun Yan is truly too significant!
