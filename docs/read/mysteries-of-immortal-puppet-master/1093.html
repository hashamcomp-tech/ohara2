Chapter 1093: Chapter 407: This Young Man Is the Hope of the Righteous Path

Purple Thunder Peak stood majestically above the sea of clouds.

This towering main peak, akin to a coiled dragon, with its head held high towards the sky. Surrounding it were several slightly shorter secondary peaks, akin to guards holding their halberds.

The mountain body of Purple Thunder Peak appeared a deep purple-black, faintly covered with mysterious patterns, countless in number, flowing with a subtle yet noble cold light.

Above the peak, shrouded all year round by lingering purple clouds, where lightning danced like purple serpents, magnificent and mysterious.

As one of the deputy peak masters of Purple Thunder Peak, Meng Wuyan was currently climbing the mountainside.

Even for him, climbing the mountain required each step to be firm, without the qualification to use mana to cloud ride.

Upon reaching a protruding mountain cliff pavilion, Meng Wuyan paused, folded the list in his hand into a flying message, and sent it out from afar.

"Before the Purple Extreme Hall, with the Thunder Pool listening at command, Deputy Peak Master Meng Wuyan, in awe and humility, respectfully reports before the esteemed Peak Master of our peak:"

"I, graced by favor, humbly occupy the deputy position, vigilant day and night, fearing to betray the heavy trust of the Peak Master and the sect’s expectations. Now that the ’Xingyun Trial’ has reached its first stage, talents gather like colorful clouds."

"I personally investigated and closely observed their actions and abilities. I found several individuals, though of various and humble origins, possessed extraordinary talents, deeply rooted spirit roots, and steadfast determination. Indeed, they are unpolished jade and hidden gems, the qilin and phoenix of the future. I respectfully present their highlights to the Peak Master:"

"Firstly, Xiao Caiting. Daughter of a loose cultivator from the mountains, with spiritual sense penetrating the mysterious. Despite not having studied advanced thunder skills, only relying on a thread of minuscule inheritance and natural spiritual rhinoceros, she managed in the ’Thunder Attraction and Mechanism Recognition’ trial, with a faint finger movement, to predict the trajectory changes of the seven-layered thunder talisman, drawing the purest strand of Purple Qi of Heavenly Yang into her body, with a faint shadow of a thunder beast emerging around her, a naturally formed unpolished jade suitable for Thunder Dao cultivation."

"Firstly, Situ Xing. This individual hails from the Situ Family, a noble lineage descendant, with stars as his chess and sword light painting. His swordsmanship subconsciously aligns with the All Heaven Star Orbit, and during the Xingyun Trial’s hundred weapons contention, he broke through the ’Nine Luminaries Illusion Star Array’ with one sword, showcasing his brilliance, a proud star spirit, outstanding and unparalleled."

"Firstly, Huang Dang. Descendant of a famous general from Earth Wall Country, with bones like iron, and will like a rock. With a body tempered a thousand times and unbending courage, during the ’Nine Tribulations Thunder Dao’ trial, he withstood three layers of thunder strikes, charred but unyielding, instead drawing a thread of earth dragon vein energy to nourish himself, transforming tribulation into strength, with resilience rarely seen."

...

"Firstly, Ning Zhuo. Only at Foundation Establishment Mid-Stage cultivation, not a Confucian cultivator, yet in the Confucian cultivator trials, won dual top honors! Furthermore, during the Evil Slaying Hall trial, passed the Three Questions of the Heart, claiming the top spot again! Therefore, awarded the treasure-level Merit and Achievement Slaughter Tower as a prize!"

"Subordinate daringly presents the request: to win the hearts of these unparalleled talents, the future stars, one cannot be constrained by common norms, nor moved by mundane benefits. What sets Purple Thunder Peak beyond others is this Purple Sky Divine Thunder Dojo, the unrivaled opportunity of the Thunder Pool on the mountain summit attracts the elites of the universe."

"This unparalleled opportunity, to behold the sacred ground and face the Heavenly Dao directly, far exceeds thousands of spirit stones and countless Taoist books! It would surely make them feel our peak’s sincere thirst for talents and accommodating spirit. Know that our Purple Thunder Peak is not only the supreme of Thunder Dao, but also the unrivaled dojo for nurturing talents and carving a future! Thus, the good birds choose their tree, the qilins and phoenixes come in ceremony, and the great path is anticipated, with our peak’s prosperity soon to come!"

"Subordinate deeply understands that this request may be presumptuous, yet for the sect’s considerations, for the future’s planning, my heart burns like fire, unable to bear withholding! Hopes the Peak Master discerns the sincere loyalty of this humble servant and grants grace and approval. Then fortunate is the sect, fortunate is the great path!"

"Subordinate Meng Wuyan, in endless awe and awaiting commands to the utmost!"

"Bows again!"

The flying message quickly disappeared beyond Meng Wuyan’s perception.

Meng Wuyan paused, gazing up at the peak, silently waiting.

The mountain body of Purple Thunder Peak trembled slightly, emitting a long sound, akin to a dragon’s roar.

The mountain sound surged, rushing straight into the sky.

The purple cloud quivered, and in the next moment, the firmament suddenly darkened, neither twilight nor dusk.

An intensely condensed Emperor Purple rapidly spread within the purple cloud, permeating throughout. This purple, deep and heavy, far surpassing all worldly luxurious silks and gemstones.

The layers of Emperor Purple clouds churned continuously, becoming thick and majestic, like an imperial crown’s tassels, layering down with strict order. In the center of the purple cloud, a bottomless purple micro vortex eye slowly opened, silently gazing over the world with authority.

Even though Meng Wuyan had witnessed such scenes many times, he still couldn’t help but feel a sense of oppression, awe, yearning, and reverence in his heart.

Meng Wuyan’s purple eyes squinted more and more, watching the rapid expansion of the thunder serpents within the cloud eye, transforming into dragon shadows.

The purple intention in Meng Wuyan’s eyes grew increasingly intense.

Suddenly, he felt a stir in his heart, silently said, "It’s here."

In an instant! A brilliant heavenly thunder struck down from the core of the vortex eye!

The brilliance of the thunder seemed to condense the will of the heavens and earth, its core was an extremely dazzling white-hot edge, with the edges flowing with purple fire capable of smelting stars.

Judging yin and yang, organizing the universe, rewarding and punishing all things, with supreme authority!

The thunder struck onto the indestructible Mystic Iron Rock Bone of Purple Thunder Peak!

"Rumble——!!!"

It was a sound as if the framework of heaven and earth was shaken. The struck mountain rock instantly transformed into molten purple jade glaze, and then the thunder transformed into a surging stream of light, washing down the mountain body like a flood.

The Emperor Purple stream light activated the vein-like thunder marks on the mountain body instantly.

Millions of thunder marks lit up, as if countless dragons roamed, and just in an instant, dimmed as the stream light departed. But even just a moment of this magnificent scene brought enough shock to Meng Wuyan, bringing tears to the eyes of the powerful Nascent Soul level demon cultivator.
