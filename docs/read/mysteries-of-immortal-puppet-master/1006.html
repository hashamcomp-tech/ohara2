Chapter 1006: Chapter 365: The Young Man’s Fortune: A Surplus of Magic Treasures

The solid ice that froze Yuan Dasheng originated from the means of one of the commanders of the Forbidden Army under the Lord of Wangchuan.

This is not only of the ice attribute but also incorporates the principle of mysterious Yin.

Ning Zhuo can penetrate the ice attribute with his Water Realm, but he doesn’t quite understand the principle of mysterious Yin. Therefore, he cannot devise a spell against solid ice based on the Five Elements Realm.

And the yellow dust covering Mengye Tiger from the inside out, although it is of the Earth Element, it originates from the Divine Separation of the Lord of Wangchuan.

Thus, it is no longer merely Earth Element, but it retains the mighty powers of the divine skills from the Lord of Wangchuan’s avatar.

The Five Elements Life and Death Fan cannot shake the divine skills.

After all, it’s just a Magic Artifact, not a Magic Treasure.

Ning Zhuo is not lacking in materials for creating a Magic Treasure; the problem is that his Lifebound Techniques have not yet matured. Even with the shell at the magic treasure level, he cannot unleash its power.

Instead, this will interfere with and wear down the talisman marks engraved by the Lifebound Techniques due to the strong physical properties of the magic treasure itself.

Only when Ning Zhuo’s Three Elixir Fields all reach the Golden Core Level can he possess a Lifebound Magical Treasure, not the current Lifebound Magical Artifact.

Since the path of the Five Elements doesn’t work, Ning Zhuo examines his possessions to see if he can find a breakthrough from them.

The Buddha Heart Demon Seal has always loomed large in his Divine Sea, bringing Ning Zhuo great unexpected joy in the battles against Heaven and Earth Ghosts and the Lord of Wangchuan.

But its grade is too high and too mysterious for Ning Zhuo to truly control it even now.

"With my cultivation enhancing, the potency of the Buddha Heart Demon Seal’s passive counterattack also increases significantly. After all, it needs to borrow my power."

Magic Artifact, Magic Treasure, Spiritual Treasure, Dao Treasure.

"From current observations, the Buddha Heart Demon Seal obviously lacks spiritual nature and can’t be considered a Spiritual Treasure. It belongs to the strongest category within magic treasures."

Even among magic treasures, there are many differences.

Generally speaking, Golden Core and Nascent Soul-level cultivators use magic treasures.

The differences between magic treasures are much like the disparities between Golden Core and Nascent Soul Cultivators, sometimes worlds apart.

In terms of quality, the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat possesses stronger spiritual nature and can be considered a quasi-spirit treasure.

Ning Zhuo primarily uses it to cultivate the body. Thanks to Sun Lingtong’s developed evil path usage, the efficiency of the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat’s Body Refinement technique is extremely high.

"I must find an opportunity to repair the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat first."

The Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat is still damaged, with many surface cracks.

Previously, Ning Zhuo lacked the ability to repair it himself and thought, "Eventually I’ll burn it; practical use is enough, repairing it is unnecessary."

But now, Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin has clearly advised in the message Jade Slip that Ning Zhuo should preserve the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat and don’t need to burn it.

Ning Zhuo’s plans for the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat underwent a thorough change.

During this time, Ning Zhuo has been using the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat the most.

There’s no choice, as his physical foundation needs to be strengthened to withstand the enormous pressure of millions of human souls.

If this pressure is left unchecked, the body will keep getting damaged due to the soul.

Besides the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat, there are several other magic treasures, all used for cultivation.

Such as the Soldier Helmet, Evil Ghost Retribution Cauldron, and Yin Yang Qi Pot.

Strictly speaking, the Soldier Helmet is a military treasure. Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong use it every time, paired with ’Gao Sheng’s Legacy’, resulting in extraordinary effects.

This cultivation method also has drawbacks; you must fully grasp the previous understanding before proceeding to the next use.

Otherwise, even if understood, it won’t differ much from previous content.

"Boss Sun can proceed to the next step; he’s already mastered his Light Body and Swift Wind Formation. But I’m still a bit short."

What Ning Zhuo previously understood was the Heroic Return Skill.

This military art has extraordinary effects, far surpassing the Light Body and Swift Wind Formation, requiring more time for complete comprehension.

"This military art is strong, but also limited. For instance, in the battle at the Wangchuan Underworld, the Qing Jiao Army suffered little loss, so I couldn’t gain much benefit."

"The true pairing must be with the Mechanical Army!"

This scheme involves considerable difficulty.

Ning Zhuo has already successfully created an army once, but the Qing Jiao Army is aligned with favorable celestial times and forces, with support from various parties, eventually successfully formed.

Ning Zhuo has already emptied his savings for this.

At present, Ning Zhuo cannot simply insert the Mechanical Army into the Qing Jiao Army because he is its deputy commander.

The type of the Qing Jiao Army restricts this possibility.

Unless Ning Zhuo joins an army whose type recognizes mechanical artifacts. Or perhaps, as in Liangzhu Country, directly join the national army, achieving it in one step.

The Evil Ghost Retribution Cauldron is a soul dao treasure that can consume ghosts and mass produce soul essence. When paired with the other three treasures, it significantly enhances the soul foundation.

At White Paper Immortal City, Ning Zhuo used them to enhance his soul cultivation to the height of millions of human souls.

Currently, he can’t use them—Ning Zhuo has paused his soul cultivation and is actively deepening his physical foundation.

Regarding the Yin Yang Qi Pot, it is a demon treasure.

A cultivator possessing demon blood can truly unleash its power, which doesn’t suit Ning Zhuo.

In the Qi fortune contest, Liu Er had no choice but to gift this treasure to Ning Zhuo. Ning Zhuo originally planned to explore the wonders of Yin Yang Qi Pot, trying to research a method for dual cultivation with himself.

Unexpectedly, upon arriving at White Paper Immortal City, he discovered that the contemporary White Paper City Lord desired this demon treasure much more than him!

In the future, Ning Zhuo will rely on this demon treasure to convert the Yin Qi of the White Paper City Lord into Yang Qi. Engaging in these Yang Qi transactions, the White Paper City Lord has no room to refuse, ensuring absolute success!
