Chapter 491: Chapter 101: Ning Zhuo’s New Divine Skill_3

The void itself is very dangerous to tangible objects.

Even Nascent Soul Cultivators find it difficult to traverse the void with their physical bodies.

The bodies of those at Nascent Soul Level also struggle to resist the erosion and wear of the void.

The Nascent Soul itself can barely manage it.

The true value of the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon is revealed once more!

With the black curtain gone and the view suddenly brightened, the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon arrived directly at the bottom of the cave, before the stone ball.

...

The map of the Mechanical Dragon was not precise, but as long as one had journeyed through a place, there would be records, the most accurate of landmarks.

With landmarks, one could travel through the void and arrive directly at the destination.

“I’m back, hahaha, I, Sun Lingtong, am back again!” Sun Lingtong stood with hands on hips, his expression exhilarated, his attitude arrogant.

Ning Zhuo’s gaze was also fiery.

He had already mastered the method to heal Dao Injuries and had been successful once; he no longer feared the “traps” here.

Before coming here, he had even sought Zhang Zhongyi to prepare some herbal medicine in advance.

Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong smeared the herbs all over their bodies and instantly became two black figures covered in medicine mud.

According to the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon’s detection, only Sun Ning and another person were in the cave, with no other living creatures.

The two flew out of the Mechanical Dragon, each carrying a pickaxe, and began the vigorous excavation at the bottom of the cave where the stone ball lay.

During their absence, the stone ball had “grown” back to its original state.

The stone skin was still extremely fragile, easily chipped away and destroyed, and as large flakes fell off, the inner layer was revealed once again.

Treasure light overflowed!

Various kinds of treasures piled together, dazzling Sun Ning and his companion, their hearts pounding with excitement.

With so many treasures, they would become suddenly rich, escape poverty, enjoy financial ease, and face a bright future!

The stone ball began to quiver slightly, like a creature stripped naked, starting to absorb the Earth Element Spiritual Energy around it.

Spiritual Energy clung to its surface, solidifying and forming a new stone skin to envelop the inner stone ball.

Sun Ning and his partner, of course, would not allow this to happen; they immediately swung their pickaxes, chipping away at the new stone skin.

But when it came to the inner layer of the stone ball, neither the pickaxe nor the Magic Artifacts, even when Sun Lingtong used a dagger to execute his Sword Skill, could budge the inner stone monolith at all.

Not to mention knocking any of the treasures loose.

Sun Lingtong was still very enthusiastic.

They had come prepared, of course.

“Little Zhuo, it’s your turn next,” said Sun Lingtong, stepping back slightly.

Ning Zhuo nodded, laughing, “Boss, just watch.”

He took out the Staff Head.

Over twenty Staff Heads were placed on the stone ball, fixed in place due to the new stone skin.

Ning Zhuo began to draw Talismans around these Staff Heads and design an Array.

Sun Lingtong cooperated from the side to prevent the Earth Element Spiritual Energy from condensing into a new stone skin, which would cover Ning Zhuo’s Array.

A moment later, Ning Zhuo completed his task.

After checking for errors, he stretched out his arms, and his fingers spread, releasing from his fingertips thin silvery threads.

The Life Hanging Thread connected to the ten most crucial Staff Heads.

As Ning Zhuo slowly infused Mana, the Staff Heads one by one activated, serving as the Array Eye and Array Heart, and in turn triggered the surrounding Talismans, then finally utilizing the entire Array in full.

The Array emitted the light of the Five Elements.

Thanks to Ning Zhuo’s mastery within the Five Elements Realm, he was able to design such an Array in a short amount of time.

Of course, the Mana refined by the Five Elements Qi Regulation Art was also just right.

The previous time, through Zhang Zhongyi’s Silver Needle, he felt the essence of petrifaction, which was also a crucial experience, giving Ning Zhuo a great revelation.

Ning Zhuo closed his eyes tightly, fully sensing the principles within the inner layer of the stone ball.

Divine Skills—Stone Cast Earth Treasure!

The reason the stone ball was so difficult to mine was precisely because the Divine Power Stone was continuously releasing its might.

Ning Zhuo felt deeply and continued to understand.

Even with Divine Skills, he faced no comprehension barrier.

There was no helping it.

His profound knowledge in the Earth Element Realm meant he faced no obstacles in understanding Divine Skills.

He knew the principles, and Divine Skills and Spells are applications of these principles, a type of intricate combination of Mana with the principles.

“I’ve learned it, I’ve learned it!”

The joy of learning made Ning Zhuo’s lips slightly curl upward.

In his mind, the embryonic form of the Divine Skill Stone Cast Earth Treasure had already formed and was growing rapidly!
