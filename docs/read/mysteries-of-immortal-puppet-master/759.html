Chapter 759 -244: Why Do I, Ning Zhuo, Need Zhuangyang?

Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong are flying rapidly in the sky, chasing each other.

“Faster, faster!” Sun Lingtong shouted loudly, but even pushing Iron Han Armor to its limit, he still couldn’t shake off Ning Zhuo.

After all, both mechanical battle armors were made by the same person, using the same design and materials, naturally their performance is similar.

Sun Lingtong’s eyes shifted: “Little Zhuo, Iron Han Armor should be able to go faster, right?”

Sun Lingtong spoke with certainty.

He also studied the Technique Classics left by Ning Zhuo’s mother and has considerable expertise in Mechanics.

“Yes.” Ning Zhuo always spoke frankly with Sun Lingtong, “Iron Han Armor, being armor worn on our body, has safety limits set by me. Within these limits, no matter how it’s used, it’s very stable.”

“But beyond the safety line, it bears unmanageable risks.”

Sun Lingtong anxiously said, “Don’t say anymore, quickly, Little Zhuo, tell me how to break the limit.”

Ning Zhuo chuckled softly: “It’s simple, you use your Mana and infuse it into the Array Eye of the Yin Yang Array, while at the same time, use your Divine Sense to invest into the bird-shaped symbol on the Golden Core Wheel.”

“Thus, you can lift the restriction.”

As Ning Zhuo finished speaking, there was a deafening explosion, and then he saw Sun Lingtong burst through the air, stirring a wind wave, his speed skyrocketing, quickly pulling hundreds of steps away from him.

“Hahaha!”

“This feels great.”

“Little brother, you’re just eating dust behind my butt.” Sun Lingtong shouted, full of excitement.

After flying far away, he turned back and circled around Ning Zhuo, hooting, with a very arrogant attitude.

Ning Zhuo snorted: “Big brother, I can also break the limit.”

After saying that, he also lifted the safety line, rapidly boosting the performance of Iron Han Armor.

Sun Lingtong gave a strange cry and flew ahead.

Ning Zhuo pursued relentlessly: “Watch me catch you!”

The two raced in the high sky, their speed increasing ever faster.

The headwind also grew stronger, Ning Zhuo quickly felt breathless, immediately mobilizing his Mana.

White Tiger Breaking Army Formation!

He activated this formation, the Golden Swallow wings erupted with platinum light, slicing through the air ahead, producing a continuous ripping sound.

The mechanical armor on his body began to turn red, and the temperature within the iron armor rose sharply.

Ning Zhuo then activated the Earth Shield Array on his helmet, covering his whole body in a thin yellow translucent shield, defense multiplied several times.

The mechanical armor no longer turned red-hot, and the temperature inside the iron armor immediately suppressed.

But good times didn’t last long, as the speed increased, Ning Zhuo felt a pressure enveloping his whole body, making it difficult to breathe.

He then pushed the Dual Yin Return Flow Array within the mech to the limit, finally balancing the pressure inside and out.

“This is my first time flying at such a speed.”

Ning Zhuo tightened his heart, flying while checking the interior condition of Iron Han Armor.

He found that the groove on the Five Elements Golden Core Wheel was emitting dazzling brilliance, its Golden Core energy consumption rate had increased more than three times.

Even with the White Tiger Breaking Army Formation paving the way, he must use his own strength to counteract the resistance of breaking through the air.

Besides being strenuous, even a slight adjustment in posture could affect the flight trajectory.

Right in front, Sun Lingtong was waving his arms and legs, his flying path was extremely turbulent, practically bouncing in the high sky.

But he was playing happily, constantly cheering.

Ning Zhuo obediently kept his legs together, arms down, pressed tightly against both sides of his body.

Mechanical clasps joined his arms and torso temporarily, fixing them together.

His whole body was like an arrow, not even lifting his head but looking straight ahead, causing wind resistance to drop drastically, speeding up even faster.

Meanwhile, back fins like fish fins extended from his rear legs, assisting Ning Zhuo in controlling the flight direction.

“Whoa!” Sun Lingtong shouted, diving headfirst into the nearby cloud layer like a headless fly.

Ning Zhuo seized the chance to surpass him, speeding forward.

“Big brother, I’ve overtaken you.” Ning Zhuo laughed heartily.

“You little punk, don’t be cocky!” Sun Lingtong instantly emerged from the clouds, he also adjusted his flying posture, no longer flailing his limbs, but grasping tight pursuit like Ning Zhuo.

Flames shot from his palms and soles, trailing a flame tail several yards long behind him.

Sun Lingtong’s speed surged!

Ning Zhuo’s heart skipped a beat, hurriedly transmitting thoughts via Divine Sense, reminding: “Big brother, take it easy. I’ve never conducted such an extreme test.”

Sun Lingtong smiled broadly: “Isn’t that perfect? Little Zhuo, take this!”

With that, he directly charged towards Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo quickly dodged.

Sun Lingtong whizzed past, the air wave blowing Ning Zhuo diagonally out a dozen steps.

Sun Lingtong’s spirited laughter came through: “Little Zhuo, can you still keep up with me?”

Inside armor, Ning Zhuo’s eyes became sharp: “Humph, big brother, don’t forget, this Iron Han Armor was designed personally by me. Can I lose to you?”

His competitive spirit arose, he too summoned a flame tail several yards long, charging towards Sun Lingtong.

Sun Lingtong didn’t look back, his Divine Sense coverage was extensive, immediately noticing Ning Zhuo following, his mouth involuntarily curled up, genuinely pleased.

“Little Zhuo, you’ve really changed.”

“In the past, you would never act like this.”

“Throughout this journey, especially the battlefield experience at Liangzhu Country, ignited your iron will and bravery. You’re a sixteen-year-old boy, it’s right to have such passion.”
