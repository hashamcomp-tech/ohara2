Chapter 531: Chapter 123: Invoking the Dragon King

The moment before.

“Report! General, the No. 6 Armory has been emptied, all the Elixir Pills inside were taken by the Stone Monster!”

A Deputy General at the Golden Core Level had received the latest military intelligence. Clenching his teeth in secrecy, he quickly withdrew from the besieging troops and arrived at the No. 6 Armory.

He opened his Storage Waist Belt and, using his Divine Sense, filled the armory with shields.

Moon Phase Shields!

These circular shields had a surface smooth as a mirror, pure and flawless. Their rims were outlined with a circle of white silver lines, and the entire shield emitted a soft halo.

“Little Zhuo, the No. 6 Armory has been restocked with military supplies,” Sun Lingtong immediately called out after detecting this within the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon.

Not long afterward, “Old Monster in Stone” visited again, taking away all the Moon Phase Shields.

This type of weapon was also a classic, with numerous functions.

It could absorb moonlight, causing various moon phases to appear on the surface of the shield.

These moon phases included: Waxing Moon, Crescent Moon, New Moon, Waning Moon, Waning Crescent Moon, Deficit Crescent Moon, Full Moon, Full Moon.

Depending on each moon phase, the power of the Moon Phase Shield would differ; if used in conjunction with a Battle Formation, there would be many ingenious uses.

After collecting all the Moon Phase Shields, Ning Zhuo murmured to himself, “This is the third time, isn’t it?”

“Yes,” Sun Lingtong nodded.

No sooner had they stolen a large amount of military supplies than the White Jade Camp replenished them.

Sun Lingtong’s expression was strange: “Though they are restocking military supplies to maintain Military Power, I can’t help but feel it’s like they’re aiding the enemy.”

Ning Zhuo said, “They’re worried that ‘Old Monster in Stone’, the Nascent Soul Level Cultivator, might join forces with Lord Long.”

“So, they’re using this method to hold us back.”

“It must be said, this is a very calm and rational approach. Shuangjing, despite his lack of experience on the battlefield, is no mediocrity to be appointed as the Main General of the White Jade Camp.”

Under such circumstances, this was undoubtedly very advantageous for Sun Ning and his partner.

They had been somewhat worried before, concerned that if they stole all the military supplies, or took the majority of them, it could drain the military strength and lead to Lord Long crushing the White Jade Camp.

Now, the timely restocking by the officers of the White Jade Camp had dispelled Sun Ning and his partner’s concerns.

This led to—they stole even more merrily.

As they targeted their next objective, a phoenix-like cry resounded through the heavens and the earth.

Then, they saw a blazing arrow, moving so fast it left a dazzling, fiery trail in their vision.

The arrow struck Lord Long squarely in the chest; even with shoulder armor of Spiritual Treasure level, it could not stop the arrow!

This sudden change alarmed Sun Ning and his partner.

“Lord Long… is he going to fall in battle?”

“He won’t die, he’s a Nascent Soul Level Cultivator, and the Nascent Soul can travel through the void. The White Jade Camp hasn’t secured the skies.”

“Mu Lan… Red Heart Arrow Divine Skills… truly formidable! With such a technique, no wonder she mobilized the entire army to come reinforce the White Jade Camp.”

The expressions of Sun Ning and his partner were very complex.

The moment before, they had worried that their rampant theft of military supplies might burden the White Jade Camp and lead to its defeat.

But the next moment, Lord Long suffered a fatal injury!

Sun Lingtong sighed: “On the battlefield, there are too many factors affecting the outcome. Even someone as strong as Lord Long could suddenly suffer a heavy blow. If we with our small stature were involved, we would be ground into dust in no time.”

“It’s better for us this way, with less risk, and we can steal a large amount of treasure.”

Ning Zhuo nodded, expressing his sentiment: “Therefore, when we act, it’s best not to stand out unnecessarily. Humility and caution are always right.”

Lord Long halted and looked down at his chest.

His physical cultivation was not enough for him to survive without his heart.

The breath of life was fading quickly.

Lord Long fell into an extremely tense state, he suddenly looked up to the sky, raising his arms and shouting loudly, “Earth engenders vitality, an endless cycle. Heed the destiny, for the Dragon God descends!”

“Great Ginseng Dragon King, please grant your follower the power to return to the heavens!!”

The next moment, the divine object carried by Lord Long, the Ginseng Whisker, grew wildly, spreading and turning into countless whisker vines that penetrated his body, filling his veins and wrapping around his internal organs.

In the end, they converged in the cavity of his left chest, condensing into a wooden heart.

As soon as the heart took shape, it began to throb violently.

At the same time, the Ginseng Whiskers weaved into a net, sealing both the front and back of Lord Long’s heart cavity.

“AHHHHH—!” Lord Long screamed to the sky, wailing in agony.

His body was already towering and robust, surpassing the norm, and now it swelled again, transforming him into a giant!

“Come on, you ants, feel the might of my master.”

Lord Long’s entire flesh turned wooden, his voice becoming ancient and profound.

Boom.

He stomped his right foot on the ground, immediately creating a deep crater in the mountainous surface.

The ground shook violently, and at the same time, numerous Ginseng Whisker vines burst out from below the earth, piercing through the soldiers’ limbs and then wrapping around them.

The battle formation of White Jade Camp was thrown into chaos, and the vital energies of the Cultivators were drained by the Ginseng Whisker vines, which were then redirected to Lord Long, causing his combat strength to soar once more!

Two Golden Core Deputy Generals couldn’t dodge in time; one was grabbed by Lord Long’s gigantic hand and directly crushed to pieces, while the other was kicked away, his bones shattered, and sent flying high out of the entire battlefield.

“Ginseng Whisker’s energy, draining life and breath, Ginseng Whisker displays its might!”

Lord Long’s voice, majestic like a thunderous bell, resounded in all directions.

The next moment, thousands more Ginseng Whisker vines burst out from his body, launching a frenzied attack on the surroundings!

The Ginseng Whiskers, like dragons or pythons, had an exceptionally fierce offensive.

Cultivators from White Jade Camp were strung up by the Ginseng Whiskers, lifted into the air, and quickly turned into armored corpses.

With this strike, over three hundred people perished!

Shuangjing’s complexion changed drastically.

The entire White Jade Camp was terrified, their military power plummeting, falling below the threshold. The casualties were too heavy, and the entire battle formation collapsed.

With the support of the battle formation power, military power, and national power, the disintegration of the battle formation and collapse of military power caused Shuangjing’s combat strength and presence to plummet rapidly, utterly enfeebled!

Sun Lingtong’s eyes widened: “Lord Long has risen up again!”

Contrarily, it was White Jade Camp that collapsed.

The might of the Ginseng Whisker Dragon King and Divinity Transformation was truly terrifying.

Red Flower Camp.

Zhang Zhongyi supported Mu Lan, both of them dumbfounded.

He was just cheering moments ago, but now he witnessed the total disintegration of White Jade Camp and the massacre of countless soldiers.

Sun Ning exchanged a glance with another, and in the next instant, both sprang into action, lunging towards Shuangjing.

Earth Element Technique!

The Old Monster in Stone traveled underground.

Lord Long also made his move, his loins whipping around more than a dozen Ginseng Whisker vines, bringing gusty winds, lashing at Shuangjing.

Shuangjing dodged frantically, at critical moments even rolling on the ground, utterly disheveled, with none of the elegance befitting someone from a noble family.

“It’s too late!”

A Ginseng Whisker burst out from beneath the ground, its huge shadow looming over Shuangjing.

With life hanging by a thread, Shuangjing hastily threw out the “Gao Sheng’s Legacy” he held, struggling to mobilize the military power to block the Ginseng Whisker.

Shuangjing fled in a panic.

The Main General’s escape further shattered the remnants of morale.

Lord Long’s gaze locked onto Shuangjing, initiating pursuit.

Meanwhile, the “Gao Sheng’s Legacy” fell from the sky, neatly caught by a Stone Ball.

Refining Void Level!

This is a Refining Void Level warrior’s treasure!!
