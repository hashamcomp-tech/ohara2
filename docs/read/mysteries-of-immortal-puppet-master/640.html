Chapter 640 -187: Iron Stream Flat Country!

Ning Zhuo’s long-range combat abilities must also take into account two Divine Temples of the Viscera.

The Earth Element Spleen Temple and Fire Element Heart Temple not only provide substantial support to Ning Zhuo’s cultivation of his Qi Sea but also enhance his combat power, specifically in earth and fire elements.

After evaluating his own abilities, Ning Zhuo once again estimated the value of the treasures he possessed.

The strongest magic treasure, without a doubt, is the Buddha Heart Demon Seal.

This trump card has helped Ning Zhuo multiple times, especially during his time at Fire Persimmon Immortal City.

Even after acquiring many treasures to this date, none could shake its status.

Beneath the Buddha Heart Demon Seal, the second place should be the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon.

This mechanical artifact, of Magic Treasure Level, has brought immense help to Ning Zhuo during his travels across the world.

Its ability to traverse the void allows Sun Ning and him to advance or retreat at will, possessing great strategic significance. It has saved their lives several times.

The Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon has also suffered considerable damage, and each time Ning Zhuo spares no expense to repair it immediately.

Now, the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon has undergone significant changes in appearance compared to when it was first forged.

“The one ranked third would be the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat.”

This magic treasure has begun to show a trace of spiritual nature, and given time, it may ascend to the level of a Spiritual Treasure, inherently surpassing the Buddha Heart Demon Seal.

For the time being, it greatly aids in the physical cultivation of Sun Ning and him.

Despite the short time they have practiced, the results are surprisingly good. It is useful now and will undoubtedly be of great use in the future.

Ranked fourth is the Yin-Yang Qi Pot.

This demon treasure, which can convert Yang Energy and Yin Qi into each other, is highly mysterious and holds vast potential for development.

Ning Zhuo imagines cultivating with himself, where this treasure may play a pivotal role in the future.

“Even if this idea is not realized, just the fact that it can better resolve the issues with Mu Lan’s cultivation technique has considerable value.”

Regarding this matter, Ning Zhuo has always kept it from Mu Lan.

Their relationship with Mu Lan is based on mutual benefit; he has yet to expend energy to plan for this.

As a cultivation treasure, the Yin-Yang Qi Pot is ranked behind the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat in Ning Zhuo’s heart, and there’s a reason for this.

The reason being—the Yin-Yang Qi Pot is a demon treasure.

It is more suitable for Demon Cultivators and somewhat for Half-Demons. For someone like Ning Zhuo, to exhibit the same effects as a demon treasure, his consumption of Mana would be several times that of a Demon Cultivator.

And if Ning Zhuo were to use only his Mana, he could not unleash the full power of the Yin-Yang Qi Pot.

Fifth and sixth places are the Soldiers and Generals share the same helmet and Gao Sheng’s Legacy.

Both of these Magic Treasure Level existences share the same plight—they are both terribly damaged.

The Soldiers and Generals share the same helmet originally was a complete set of armor, but Ning Zhuo only has a helmet.

Gao Sheng’s Legacy is in poor condition with severe content fragmentation.

Only when both are combined, they have practical value for Sun Ning and him.

There’s also a drawback: these two treasures are inconvenient for Ning Zhuo to use openly.

The Soldiers and Generals share the same helmet originated from Lu Hongtu, while Gao Sheng’s Legacy was robbed from Shuangjing by the “Old Monster in Stone”. If Ning Zhuo were to use them, it would be akin to exposing himself.

As for Magic Treasure Level items in Ning Zhuo’s possession, there are also the Soul-stirring Drum and Netherworld Ghost God Seal.

Both of these were obtained from the Golden Core Cultivator Qi Bai of the Soul Devouring Sect.

These two treasures are nearly as good as none.

They are specifically suitable for Ghost Cultivators; Ning Zhuo’s Mana does not match them well, preventing the exhibition of their full capabilities.

Being from the Demon Path and a major sect, the Soul Devouring Sect, Ning Zhuo does not wish to show off these two treasures. Travelling around with a grave responsibility and mere Foundation Establishment Cultivation, he does not want to invite more trouble.

There are also two other reasons.

One is that using these treasures from the Demon Sect would negatively affect Ning Zhuo’s image on the Righteous Path.

The second is that these two treasures have already become a part of the Netherworld Envoy, Qi Bai.

Beyond these, there is also a Black Iron Cloud of Magic Treasure Level.

Initially, Ning Zhuo purchased it from the Cloud Whale Trading Team at Fire Persimmon Immortal City, intending to equip Yuan Dasheng with it to endow him with some air combat capability.

The idea was beautiful, but the reality was less satisfactory.

On one hand, Yuan Dasheng’s Spiritual Nature is not as good as the Mengye Tiger; relying on him alone makes it difficult to maneuver the Black Iron Cloud in combat.

On the other hand, Ning Zhuo, after engaging in actual combat, realized he does not need Yuan Dasheng’s assistance in air combat. Being adept at long-range combat himself, continuously using the Five Elements Magic is usually enough to keep the enemy busy.

“So, this was a misguided investment!”

Compared to magic treasures, Ning Zhuo owns even more Magic Artifacts.

The Cushion, Tornado Windmill, Five Elements Ruler, and Calming Bell used for cultivation once brought great help to Ning Zhuo.

Nowadays, the help these Magic Artifacts provide has greatly diminished; some are even on the verge of being phased out.

One type of Magic Artifact is the most numerous.

That is the Magic Mirror.

The Foundation Establishment Chapter of the Mirror Stand Spirit Communication Technique records the crafting and usage methods of eight types of Magic Mirrors.

Currently, Ning Zhuo has extensively manufactured the Vajra Mirror, Divine Splitting Mirror, and Lime Mirror.

The next most numerous are the Yugang Armor.

After the Ning Clan branch intervened in Yugang Mountain, causing an abundance of raw materials, and with the aid of the former refining facilities of the Zheng Family, the production increased significantly.

As the clan leader of the branch family, Ning Zhuo only had to give the word, and his subordinates naturally went all out, entrusting all inventory to Lin Bufan, who then transferred it to Ning Zhuo.

Therefore, even after equipping Red Flower Camp and Three Generals Camp, Ning Zhuo still has a considerable surplus on hand.

Ning Zhuo’s Divine Sense swept over his storage equipment and found some Magic Artifacts that he almost forgot due to their infrequent use.

There are over a dozen Magic Artifact Level poison smoke black balls, which Ning Zhuo once used in Fire Persimmon Mountain, and they proved quite effective.
