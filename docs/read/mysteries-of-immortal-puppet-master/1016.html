Chapter 1016: Chapter 370: Snow-Colored Spirit Movement

Pi Fugie, although a genius and the face of the Pi family, is only at Foundation Establishment.

Ning Zhuo, despite also being at Foundation Establishment, has cultivation weaker than his. Yet, he has killed Golden Core, Nascent Soul, and has even fought against Transforming Spirit Level!

Pi Fugie’s eyelids twitched, feeling Ning Zhuo’s surging confidence, he became even more reluctant to fight.

For a moment, he was at a loss, regretting having greeted Shen Xi and his group, leading to now having no way to retreat.

If he really retreats without fighting, once it reaches his family, Pi Fugie’s days will certainly be tough, and he’ll lose face.

"Hahahahaha!" Just then, the Tiger Demon beside Pi Fugie laughed loudly.

"Such a mere child, speaks so big. Let I, the Lord, meet you!"

This Tiger Demon has outstanding power and was just recently recruited by Pi Fugie.

Similar to how Shen Xi had once befriended Ning Zhuo, Pi Fugie deliberately approached the Tiger Demon. Both initially had the same intention. However, over time, Ning Zhuo displayed his own strength, prompting Shen Xi to wisely adjust immediately, treating Ning Zhuo as an equal.

Seeing the Tiger Demon laugh, Pi Fugie’s eyes lit up.

The Tiger Demon clenched his fists, solemnly addressed Pi Fugie: "Young Master Pi, although we’ve only met a few times, you gifted me with a Soul Spirit Tattoo, I should exert effort to help you regain your standing!"

Pi Fugie breathed a sigh of relief, feeling joyful inside, this is undoubtedly the best response—he wouldn’t have to fight, providing an explanation to his family.

Pi Fugie immediately promised: "This is your first time striking, regardless of win or loss, there will be rewards!"

Ning Zhuo sneered: "No matter how much the rewards, a dead cultivator can’t receive them, right?"

The Tiger Demon glared at Ning Zhuo: "You little thing, your words are truly bold. When I defeat you, I won’t kill you immediately; I’ll swallow you alive. Then, I wonder if you’ll still be so arrogant?"

Ning Zhuo put away the Five Elements Life and Death Fan, wearing a look of disdain: "You, a mere demon cultivator, what virtue and capability do you have to fight me?"

"For me to strike, it would make more sense to at least cut down Pi Fugie."

The Tiger Demon laughed heartily, pointing his finger at Ning Zhuo’s nose: "You’re scared! You’re timid and afraid of death, not daring to accept my life-and-death martial duel!"

"Even if you don’t accept it, I’ll spread around that you are cowardly and shy away from a fight!"

"I know, you righteous path cultivators cherish reputation. Just wait then."

Ning Zhuo sighed: "Alright then. Since you’re seeking death, I won’t stop you."

"If you can defeat my Mechanical Puppet, then I’ll step down and personally grant you your death."

Shen Xi glanced sideways, thinking, "Mechanical Puppet?"

Both teams entered the Martial Arts Hall. Since the Tiger Demon initiated the challenge, as per customary rules, he would pay the Spirit Stone fee.

Ning Zhuo chose a room.

The room was equipped with an Array, once everyone crossed the door, they entered the Array Space.

During the wait before entering, Shen Xi urgently transmitted thoughts: "Brother Ning, you may not be entirely aware. The Pi family primarily practices the ’Seven Talisman Demon Skin Manual,’ capable of using techniques like Paper Folding and Talisman Making, using souls as the primary material to imprint talismans on their own skin."

"At their strongest, they can simultaneously record seven talismans, regardless of similarity."

"These talismans deploy swiftly, almost like lifebound techniques in speed. Additionally, the talismans often complement each other, forming a combination."

"Though the Tiger Demon joined not long ago, Pi Fugie has the capability to carve Demon Talismans onto him."

"Judging from the Tiger Demon’s earlier statements, Pi Fugie, to recruit him, has already imprinted one talisman. They are likely delaying in order to imprint a second one now."

"For the upcoming battle, be careful. Using the Mechanical Puppet to probe the Tiger Demon and the talismans is a prudent approach."

Shen Xi knows Ning Zhuo’s understanding of the Five Elements far surpasses the ordinary. As for just how much, he has yet to fully gauge it.

In his opinion: Ning Zhuo is about the same age as him, perhaps slightly older, having such profound mastery in the Five Elements realm at this age is quite remarkable. Ning Zhuo should have dedicated a considerable amount of time and energy to the Five Elements.

As for Mechanical Techniques, he likely isn’t as adept.

Thus, he had already predicted the upcoming battle: Ning Zhuo would use the Mechanical Puppet to probe the Tiger Demon’s details. If the situation was favorable, he could even consume and weaken the opponent’s strength. Then, Ning Zhuo would deploy the Five Elements Life and Death Fan and Five Elements Magic to defeat the Tiger Demon, securing victory.

Ning Zhuo slightly waved his hand: "Brother Shen, don’t worry, it’s merely a small cat."

Shen Xi praised: "Brother Ning, bold indeed."

Su Lingkou silently curled his lip, thinking: "My cousin kindly advised you, yet you don’t appreciate it. With such arrogance, if you lose later, the embarrassment might be even greater."

"Sigh, hopefully, you can win."

"Should be able to win, right?"

Su Lingkou, having never seen Ning Zhuo in action, thus lacked much confidence in Ning Zhuo.

After a while longer, Pi Fugie and the Tiger Demon entered the room.

The Tiger Demon entered the Martial Arts arena, Pi Fugie stood outside with Shen Xi and Su Lingkou, watching.

The Tiger Demon’s eyes glowed brightly, elated.

He thought: "Challenging proactively is the right choice! To win this battle, the young master gifted another Demon Talisman, greatly boosting my strength!"

"This battle, I will surely win. Boy, consider this your gift for my advancement."

He extended his finger, teasingly gesturing towards Ning Zhuo, immensely provocative.

Ning Zhuo, expressionless, lightly waved his hand, releasing a Mechanical Puppet from his Storage Waist Belt.

The puppet was six feet three inches tall, female in shape, with six arms, and a graceful posture.

Its exterior was covered with a layer of three-colored enamel armor, its cheeks were snow porcelain masks, and ice silkworm silk woven into black tresses.

Snow Maiden Hui!

As soon as it appeared, its dazzling appearance attracted everyone’s attention.
