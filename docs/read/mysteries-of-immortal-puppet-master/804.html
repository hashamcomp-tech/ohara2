Chapter 804 -268: Jade Bell True Array_2

Sun Lingtong lay motionless on the ground, as if his whole body had fallen apart.

The Silent Ghost General quietly looked down at him.

Beside him, the Peanut Ghost General, wearing a paper white flower on his head, spoke for the Silent Ghost General: “Little Foundation Establishment, my lord asks, how do you feel now?”

“You should know, just now when you were attacked, there wasn’t a single sound, reaching outside the chariot.”

“This is exactly my method.”

“With this method, attacking inside White Paper City, even if for half an hour, no one might notice.”

“Do you still think we are going to our deaths?”

Sun Lingtong wanted to retort, but he had no energy to open his mouth, not even able to roll his eyes.

In just a few breaths’ time, every second felt like an eternity, he suffered torment, paying the due price for his insubordinate action.

But at the same time, he had gambled right.

These Golden Core Ghost Generals, despite having the power to crush Sun Lingtong, chose to hold back, unwilling to trigger military rules for a mere Foundation Establishment.

The Yin Soldiers’ army crossed the Yin Yang, finally reaching the Yin Tide Black Swamp Wetland.

The six Golden Core Ghost Cultivators slipped away silently.

Sun Lingtong, with his limbs weak and sore, climbed down from the war chariot, stayed with the main force, and quietly awaited the attack on the city wall.

During this time, he tried again to contact Ning Zhuo, still without success, causing his anxiety to grow.

Fortunately, it wasn’t long before the Main General gave the order, mobilizing the entire army to attack the north gate of the White Paper Immortal City.

At this moment, the military art of marching couldn’t be concealed any longer.

Sun Lingtong immediately contacted Ning Zhuo, informing him of the intelligence he had confirmed.

Ning Zhuo, who had been anxious due to losing contact with Sun Lingtong for a long time, felt both surprised and relieved upon hearing his response: “Boss, rest assured, I had already requested help before, everything is ready now.”

Sun Lingtong chuckled: “Fight well, and don’t spare those six Ghost Generals, make sure to deal with them thoroughly for me!”

Ning Zhuo understood the boss’s anger wasn’t without reason, his gaze immediately becoming sharp, gritting his teeth: “Understood.”

Inside White Paper City, in the alleys near the south gate.

The Peanut Ghost General’s ears had turned into blooming flowers, he listened intently: “The battle at the north gate is fierce, as soon as our army started, they used war chariots to charge the city.”

The Silent Ghost General immediately nodded, raised his hand, and walked in the front.

The other five closely followed behind.

The Silent Ghost General maximized the innate power to all around, allowing them to run at full speed, even breaking through walls, without making a sound.

They broke through in a straight line, soon reaching the paperman giant statue in front of the south gate.

Just as they were about to strike and destroy the paperman giant statue, they heard a young voice: “Activate the formation!”

In the next moment, twenty-eight musical spells suddenly appeared on the ground, while twelve Green Nether Jade Marrow Nails buried deep underground gleamed brightly.

Four thick turquoise pillars with Simurghs holding bells rose from the ground. On these copper pillars, there were carvings of Simurghs spreading their wings, and the protruding beaks held golden threads, from which hung the Seven Treasure Golden Bells. On the bell’s surface were countless intricate talismans, densely packed, with the entirety of the “Divine Sound Exterminating Demon Curse” etched on the small bell!

The six Golden Core Ghost Generals rushed to the paperman giant statue, as if willingly plunging into the formation, instantly falling into the Array Space.

The six Golden Cores looked around, seeing blue skies and jade land, the sky clear. Four copper pillars occupied the four directions, countless Simurghs circling them.

On the horizon, there stood a humanoid mountain, with large clusters of white clouds replacing the head.

“Damn it!”

“There’s a trap here!!”

The Golden Core Ghost Generals panicked slightly.

The Silent Ghost General raised a hand, the Peanut Ghost General immediately voiced for him: “Silent boss says, don’t panic. Our previous attack failed, and the enemy deployed a formation here, weren’t we expecting this?”

The Golden Core Ghost Generals were immediately comforted.

“Silent boss is right.”

“With formations come breakthroughs, what’s there to panic about?”

“First, probe around, see what kind of formation this is!”

The Ghost Generals attempted to break the formation.

They didn’t immediately attack the obvious copper pillars, but instead rushed towards the giant mountain ahead.

However, no matter how far they ran, the distance to the giant mountain didn’t shorten.

Some Ghost Generals attempted to pull away from the giant mountain, running backwards, only to find after a long run that the giant mountain appeared behind them!

The Ghost Generals suddenly had a new understanding: “This formation has the power to distort space, not something that can be breached just by pulling.”

They used several rounds of magic treasures and spells, attacking randomly, but the sky was towering, the jade ground resilient, all to no avail.

Finally, having given up any hope, they braced themselves to attack the four copper pillars.

Simurghs flew around the copper pillars, engaging them in fierce battle, the combat was intense.

The Ghost Generals were under great psychological pressure, knowing they had to race against time, every strike was a heavy blow.

Soon, most of the bronze Simurghs were eliminated.

Their attacks made the copper pillars tremble.

The Peanut Ghost General exclaimed with surprise: “We’ve detected it, these four copper pillars are the Array Eye, break them and the Array Heart and Array Foot will appear.”

Ning Zhuo sat cross-legged in the Array Heart, controlling the formation.

Suddenly, two Golden Core Ghost Generals appeared behind him.

The leader was the Silent Ghost General!

The two Golden Cores attacked with full force, shredding Ning Zhuo into countless pieces of paper and shreds.

“It’s a fake!” The two Ghost Generals felt a chill in their hearts.

“It’s a fake!” Ning Zhuo and Wen Ruanyu, hidden in the dark, also felt a chill, realizing that the Silent Ghost General and another in the formation were all illusions.

This batch of Golden Core Ghost Generals was skilled in tactics; knowing the Array Heart was the giant mountain, the Array Eye the four copper pillars, they still executed tactical deception.

Their strategy was very well executed, but Ning Zhuo also prepared adequately, the “Ning Zhuo” in the Array Heart was merely a portrait substitute by Wen Ruanyu.

“Such an innate silence!” Ning Zhuo admired inwardly.

He saw clearly that it was actually another Ghost General launching the stealth attack, but it was the Silent Ghost General’s innate power that made this operation possible.

“Senior Wen, make your move.” Ning Zhuo reminded.

The actual host of the formation was Wen Ruanyu.

Wen Ruanyu immediately infused mana, activating the Jade Bell, a magic treasure guarding the Array Heart.

The Jade Bell resonated in the air, bursting out waves of sound.

The sound waves transformed into light blue ripples, swiftly expanding, covering the Formation Space.

All Golden Core Ghost Generals’ mana churned like boiling water, their bodies emitted a pale green light, forcing them to manifest semi-transparent outlines.

This green light didn’t interfere with their mana, yet the Silent Ghost General felt a heavy heart!

He secretly exclaimed something was wrong, shocked to find his innate power severely countered. It might still work outside the formation, but inside, he could no longer remain silent.

In this situation, they’d have to openly attack the formation.

Wen Ruanyu sneered, activating another layer of the formation’s transformations.

The golden bells hanging from the Simurghs’ mouths suddenly rang out, the sound crisp and dense, like rain crashing on tiles.

The four massive copper pillars released intense bronze light. Within the light, they rapidly restored.

The Golden Core Ghost Generals tried to strike, but within the bell sound, their senses reversed, hearing thousands of horses neigh, seeing countless Simurgh illusions, their mana became sluggish, hard to mobilize.

“This is the Jade Bell True Array!” As the formation displayed its dual power, the Ghost Generals finally uncovered its roots.

“Watch your feet!!” a Ghost General yelled in warning.

But it was too late.

Wen Ruanyu activated the third transformation of the formation, the twelve Jade Marrow Nails at the Array Foot invoked the Jade Deficiency Thunder Skill, piercing the acupuncture points at the sole of the attackers’ feet.

Boom, boom, boom!

Several Ghost Generals immediately had their legs blown off.

“Retreat, retreat quickly!” a Ghost General screamed in panic.

But the Array Space isolated inside from outside; how could they retreat so easily now?
