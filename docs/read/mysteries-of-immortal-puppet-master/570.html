Chapter 570 -153: Giving the Treasure to Ning Zhuo (Two in One)

Ning Zhuo simultaneously cultivates three Dantians, causing his cultivation time to be at least three times that of normal cultivators.

But in fact, it’s far more than that.

Because the Three Superior Sect Techniques are too superior, this top-tier cultivation technique, each required cultivation time is far beyond ordinary techniques.

Ning Zhuo showed no complacency: “Although I have saved nine years of grinding effort overnight, it’s nothing at all.”

“Foundation Establishment Level 6: Unobstructed Field Opening, Treasure Medicine Generation, Dantian Shines, Abnormal Phenomenon Seed Talisman, Inner Golden Light, Transforming from Void to Real… I have only just reached this stage.”

“There’s still a long way to go.”

Ning Zhuo pondered his own cultivation situation.

“I cultivate the Three Superior Sect Techniques.”

“Among them, the Demon-infused Bloodline Technique, relies on extracting the blood of my clan members, paired with Heavy Blood Ape·Great Victory to speed up cultivation. Alternatively, through the Fetal Breathing Technique and Spirit Boat, I can boost the physical body. Although it’s a different path, it leads to the same end.”

“In terms of the Five Elements Qi Regulation Art, I possess profound mastery of the Five Elements Realm, innovated the Five Organs Temple Spirit Divine Skill, and have now achieved the Earth Element Spleen Temple, greatly accelerating my cultivation speed. But I still lack the Ten-Thousand-Year-Old Fire Spirit, Earth Core, Water Essence, and Gold Essence to respectively cultivate the other four organs temples.”

“On the Mirror Stand Spirit Communication Technique, although I have not found a shortcut, my progress has been quite good in this aspect, and I haven’t encountered any bottlenecks so far. It shows my inherently kind and benevolent nature, adept at calmly addressing issues, thus quite suited for this Buddhist Scripture.”

“But compared to the national power brick, the difference is quite clear!”

“From the current perspective, cultivating with the national power brick is the right path.”

“Using the national power to increase cultivation level has no risks or side effects.”

“No wonder, in the cultivation world, the nation is the top force.”

After using up four national power bricks, Ning Zhuo profoundly appreciated the allure of national power!

A sharp light flickered in Ning Zhuo’s eyes.

He traveled the world, deeply feeling the misery of insufficient cultivation. Weak cultivation made many things very inconvenient for him.

“If I could get more national power bricks, that would be great.”

Thinking this, he felt an even stronger anticipation for the upcoming great battle.

“By distinguishing myself in the great battle, I may possibly obtain more national power bricks again.”

“I might also use the troops from Liangzhu Country to opportunistically seek my mother’s relic.”

It could be said that he could cultivate and rescue his mother simultaneously.

Therefore, the line involving Old Monster in Stone must not be abandoned.

The previous ambush battle had already completely confirmed the huge advantage of this identity. Previously, as a Military Adviser in the Three Generals Camp and now as Mu Lan’s husband, many things have become increasingly inconvenient. Once he assumes the identity of Old Monster in Stone, some challenges troubling him can be tackled from another angle!

“Perhaps, using the identity of Old Monster in Stone, I could even prematurely obtain mother’s gift from the hands of the Six Cave Sect.”

Of course, Ning Zhuo knew this action required extreme caution.

The identity of Old Monster in Stone was unrelated to Ning Family by a long shot; a rash connection could lead to numerous suspicions.

Especially Lu Hongtu, the Great Cave Master of the Six Cave Sect, who is very strategic, sharp-eyed, and quite familiar with Old Monster in Stone. If Ning Zhuo acts too hastily, it could likely provoke his suspicion and investigation.

It might happen that one time, when impersonating Old Monster in Stone, he gets ambushed by Lu Hongtu.

During the Treasure Medicine Generation stage of cultivation, Ning Zhuo must continuously shift spirit, energy, and essence, melding them to form the treasure medicine.

This is the most significant content of this stage of cultivation!

Ning Zhuo has an exceptional advantage in this area, that is, simultaneous cultivation of the three Dantians.

This allows each of his Dantian not only to be exercised, having a robust foundation, hence capable of easily involving the cultivation of the other two Dantians for integration.

If only one Dantian were primarily cultivated, the other two being considerably weak, not to mention the difficulty in engaging the cultivation level, even if it’s engaged, it would be sparse, and the fused treasure medicine would be even more scarce.

Ning Zhuo stopped cultivating, slowly stood up, packed up the cushion and cloud smoke, lifted the tent curtain, and walked outside.

At that moment, the sky was clear without a hint of clouds.

Ning Zhuo slightly squinted his eyes, then slowly opened them.

A gentle breeze brushed his face, he looked up to the sky.

Just overnight, his cultivation level broke through Unobstructed Field Opening, sparing him nine years of harsh grinding, and entered the Treasure Medicine Generation stage.

However, this immense breakthrough made him feel even more insignificant.

The vast sky seemed boundless, the earth silent, and he felt like a mere speck of dust within it.

Ning Zhuo, stationed at the Three Generals Camp, heard training noises, occasionally including Guan Hong’s cold shouts and Zhang Hei’s severe curses.

Being sixteen with the Treasure Medicine Generation realm is quite rare, and Ning Zhuo further cultivates three Dantians!

But compared to Liu, Guan, Zhang, all at the Golden Core Level, even if he were rare, he was still merely at the early stage of Foundation Establishment.

Everyone believes that given time, he will succeed.

But the cruelty and contradiction of reality lie in that it doesn’t treat you gently because your future is bright.

It’s not that the world revolves for me, but that I must adapt to the wind.

It was never about the world accommodating an individual, but about the individual actively adapting to this harsh external world.

The current Ning Zhuo must adapt, endure his weak cultivation while traveling the world, facing a world filled with Golden Cores and Nascent Souls.

“Fortunately, with the development of the Cultivation Hundred Skills up to now, there are countless ways to achieve cross-level battles!”

Cultivation is the pursuit, development, utilization, and innovation of the truths of heaven and earth.

The process of cultivation is a continual exploration and transformation of the cultivator’s spirit, energy, and essence, wherein the use of power by the cultivator also gradually escalates and qualitatively changes. Initially, it’s just True Qi, then True Qi transforming to liquid, and finally, True Qi solidifying, gathering into a Golden Core!
