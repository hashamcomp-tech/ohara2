Chapter 1242: Chapter 480: Heart Fire Essence (Part 2)

The entire motion resembled Flowing Clouds.

Upon touching the Monster Path materials, Gu Qing’s movements halted, losing his former demeanor.

During the handling of the Chaotic Ink Stone, it suddenly seeped with an inky stench, the ink resembling a centipede crawling towards his heart along Gu Qing’s arm.

Gu Qing dared not be careless, hurriedly pinching his Finger Technique to suppress it. An absurd thought appeared inexplicably in his Divine Sea: "If I cut off the little toe on my left foot and threw it into the papermaking process, perhaps I could refine a rare item?"

He suppressed the thought, completed dealing with the Ink Stone, and proceeded with the Delusional Spider Silk.

His nerves were like guitar strings, plucked fiercely, bringing a burning pain from the depths of his mind. Each burning sensation generated illusory memories, such as being drugged by brothers and ruthlessly exploited in a coma, or being a wild boar scrambling for food in a stone trough...

Having processed the three Monster Path materials, Gu Qing ground his teeth, sweating profusely, his expression twisted.

He had to stop, sitting cross-legged on the spot, to operate his Main Cultivation Method.

Soon, Mana surged within him, his body swiftly relaxed.

After Three Circulations, he reopened his eyes, the blood color in his pupils faded, his body almost restored its previous appearance, but a light grey pattern had appeared on his forehead.

Gu Qing’s Divine Sense enveloped his whole body, pausing on the light grey pattern.

He understood the secret within: "I am not a Monster Path cultivator; dealing with such materials means being subjected to the erosion of Monster Path principles in both body and mind."

"The erosion of these principles is temporary. As long as I promptly operate my Main Cultivation Method, I can replace those principles."

"However, certain Monster Path principles still linger, requiring more time for clearing and replacement."

"This is the water-grinding technique, time-consuming and labor-intensive; it’s not something I can do now."

With great effort, all the materials in Gu Qing’s hands transformed into a pool of colorful pulp, reaching the final gateway in refining the Heart Fire Spirit Paper.

In Gu Qing’s Lower Dantian Essence Sea, his Literary Heart trembled, releasing a burst of Essence Blood.

The Essence Blood dispersed in mid-air, transforming into a blood mist.

As the blood mist fell into the pulp, the liquid suddenly boiled!

The boiling liquid completely lost control. It first condensed into a crying face, then collapsed into a funnel shape, and eventually, a three-legged ink crow emerged, wings spread in an attempt to fly.

Finally, this segment of paper pulp turned pitch black, solidifying, resembling a forcibly pieced-together entity of a crying face, a funnel, and a crow, incredibly bizarre and ugly.

Gu Qing couldn’t help but feel a weight on his heart.

The first wave of papermaking today, he had failed.

"I had control over everything I could."

"But when it comes to Monster Path, I can only rely on Qi Fortune."

Handling Monster Path materials, the success rate never reaches tenfold. Even if Zhao Hansheng himself were to operate it, it would be the same. Only the failure probability would be much lower than Gu Qing’s.

"So, how about Ning Zhuo?" In Gu Qing’s heart, Ning Zhuo was already his greatest enemy.

His Divine Sense swept to the rear, spotting Ning Zhuo still processing materials.

Gu Qing observed for a while, noticing Ning Zhuo’s technique was simple and his movements clumsy, clearly attempting the refinement for the first time.

Seeing this, Gu Qing dared not slacken.

"Again like this." He mumbled softly in his heart.

This familiar scene was indeed too recognizable. He had seen similar checkpoints before in Mountain and River Page, Heavenly Star Paper, and Haoran Xuan’s trials.

Gu Qing controlled his breathing for a moment and started the second wave of papermaking.

He succeeded the second time. The quality of the spirit paper was Intermediate.

The third attempt failed again, the pulp converting into a puddle of writhing eyeballs, which then scattered.

The fourth, fifth times...

He gradually reached his potential.

Not long after, over twenty sheets of Heart Fire Essence had piled beside Gu Qing, the lowest being Mid Grade, with three sheets featuring crimson cloud patterns—nearing Top Grade.

"How about Ning Zhuo?" Gu Qing raised his head again, his pupils suddenly contracting.

Before Ning Zhuo, over thirty sheets of Heart Fire Essence were neatly stacked!

"This?!" Gu Qing’s heart shook violently.

After experiencing Haoran Xuan’s unexpected change, Gu Qing quickly accepted this fact, simultaneously having a strong suspicion in his heart: "How did he do it?"

Gu Qing focused intensely, discovering that Ning Zhuo’s technique was much smoother than when he checked first. But even so, many detailed aspects still revealed the rawness from first contact.

Ning Zhuo’s refining pace was not fast, forced to pause constantly to control breathing.

Gu Qing observed for a while, feeling Ning Zhuo was average, gaining nothing.

He had no choice but to suppress his doubts and continue paper making.

On the high platform among the pine branches, Li Guanyu lightly tapped his Jade Ruler on his knee, his gaze reflecting the figures of Gu Qing and Ning Zhuo.

"Gu Qing prevails due to his Confucian Cultivation foundation, Wenxin Blood has a natural affinity with orthodox materials, processing speed is ahead by thirty percent."

"Ning Zhuo is clearly refining Heart Fire Essence for the first time, he progresses quickly, his technique becoming increasingly adept, which shows his considerable skill in Artifact Refining, and solid foundational skills. Therefore, to a great extent, he compensates for the lack of experience."

"But the most crucial part is the final step!"

"In ’Essence Blood Shaping,’ Ning Zhuo’s success rate is astonishingly high! Out of ten attempts, he fails a maximum of two times, and among the failures, it’s rare to see completely alienated bizarre products."

"But how long can his luck last?"

Even though Ning Zhuo is ahead now, Li Guanyu still cannot determine the ultimate winner of this challenge.

"Involved with Monster Path materials, making Heart Fire Essence becomes uncontrollable. Especially the final step, even I must rely on luck."

"Although Ning Zhuo has overshadowed Gu Qing, this refinement of spirit paper is truly a unique endurance race."

"The level of Monster Path corrosion continues to accumulate."

Thinking of this, some cultivators in the arena could no longer endure.

In the southeast corner, a cultivator in a yellow robe suddenly stuffed Chaotic Ink Stone into his mouth, chewing. He laughed while chewing, ink oozing from his teeth, then began dancing, singing, laughing.

On the west side, a female cultivator handling Monster Path materials abruptly tore open the left side of her chest garment, thrusting her fingertip into her flesh, shrieking sharply.

While to the north, an elderly cultivator made a mistake while processing materials, causing his entire body to soften like a wax figure, his facial features slowly sliding to the back of his head, leaving his face blank.

"It’s happening." Li Guanyu sighed softly, a wine gourd in his sleeve flew up into the air.

Li Guanyu murmured: "In drunkenness, Qiankun vast, within the bottle, sun and moon last."

Liquor sprayed out from the gourd, turning midair into a misty azure rain.

The rain automatically split in the air, accurately dropping into the mouths of cultivators showing abnormalities. The rain threads melted upon touching lips, bringing the rich aroma of aged wine, mixed with the resonance of Confucian cultivation scripture.

The stone-swallowing cultivator fell backwards, snoring like thunder.

The female cultivator, permeated by the wine spirit, collapsed to the ground, utterly inebriated, giggling foolishly.

The elderly cultivator’s rear face burped alcohol, awakening as if from a dream, hastily pushed his facial features back to their original positions in panic.

More and more people exhibited peculiar states, each differing, nearly no two alike.

Many cultivators realized something was amiss, recalling Li Guanyu’s earnest advice before the start, those rational or scared immediately stopped their actions.

Ning Zhuo also sensed these situations.

He gained a deeper understanding of this paper-making competition: "On the surface, it tests manufacturing techniques, but in reality, it’s even more of a challenge to the cultivator’s physical body and soul foundation."

Ning Zhuo’s physical body had been constantly cultivated, with deep foundation. His soul of a million people, accumulating robustly.

All of this supported Ning Zhuo, providing him with great assistance.

"And also the cultivator’s cultivation technique. The higher the quality of the technique, the more principles it represents, enabling it to substitute, overthrow temporary Monster Path principles."

Ning Zhuo reaped the benefits of the Three Superior Sect Techniques once again.

Of course, Gu Qing’s cultivation techniques weren’t far inferior.

Gu Qing continued to hold fast.

A period of time had passed, yet Ning Zhuo couldn’t widen the gap with Gu Qing.

At forty or fifty years of age, Gu Qing’s age advantage allowed his physical body and soul accumulation to slightly exceed Ning Zhuo’s.

Gu Qing also had his Confucian cultivation advantage, and upon realizing Ning Zhuo’s lead, he began taking pills persistently.

"Do I need to utilize the other two cultivation techniques?" Ning Zhuo hesitated.

By doing so, his secret of cultivating the Three Elixir Fields would likely be exposed, which was disadvantageous for him.

Of course, he also knew this secret could only be maintained for a period.

Myriad Images Sect’s investigation of him would surely reach Fire Persimmon Immortal City.

"Before exposure, it’d be best if I could use this informational advantage to defeat some genius cultivators in the Martial Arts Arena."

As Ning Zhuo hesitated, an abrupt change occurred.

From his Storage Waist Belt emerged an intangible force of attraction.

The attracting force targeted Ning Zhuo’s body and mind, eradicating the stubborn Monster Path principles.

In an instant, these Monster Path principles were swept away!
