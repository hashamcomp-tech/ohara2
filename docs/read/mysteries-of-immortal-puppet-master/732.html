Chapter 732 -232: Capturing Two Generals, Finally Securing the Carriage

Two armies deliberately bypassed the Jin Tun River and encountered the Red Flower Camp midway.

“General Mu Lan!” Liu Er was delighted and transmitted these thoughts with his Divine Sense.

Mu Lan nodded slightly to him, then looked at Ning Zhuo with surprise and joy: “Husband, this time let us fight side by side!”

Ning Zhuo wanted to remind Mu Lan not to call him husband. However, amidst the ongoing fierce battle, this was clearly not the right time to correct her. He could only nod and signal Mu Lan to follow him.

Continue the assault!

Military Art — Soldiers value speed!

Liu Er seized the opportunity, consuming military power, causing the speed of the Three Generals Camp to soar.

Wood Element — Root Vine Entanglement.

Ning Zhuo’s Divine Sense covered the left flank. As his spell was cast, dozens of enemy cultivators were entangled by vines.

“Now’s our chance, release the arrows!” Mu Lan’s eyes flashed with sharp light as she immediately ordered.

The Red Flower Camp released a wave of arrow rain.

Military Art — Falling Flower Arrow!

Petals fell from the Red Flower Dharma Image, with giant petals breaking into countless fragments, pouring down like a heavy rain.

“Ahhhhh…” The enemy cultivators, entangled by the vines and unable to dodge, were shot one after another. After screaming in agony, they lost their lives.

The three armies coordinated skillfully, one strike followed by another, trusting each other, creating promising results.

“Little Zhuo, Lin You is in that position!” Sun Lingtong hid within the Mechanical Dragon, continuously correcting Ning Zhuo’s target positions.

“Charge this way!” Ning Zhuo had become the leader figure. At his command, the three armies adjusted their direction.

This was because Ning Zhuo was the core linking the three armies!

The three armies joined forces and carved out a bloody path.

“This is the advantage of having superior numbers.” Ning Zhuo sincerely sighed.

This was much easier than leading the charge alone.

Of course, other reasons played a part as well. For instance, at this moment, Liangzhu Country’s morale was high and they were in an offensive stance, naturally aiding the charge. Additionally, high-ranking figures like Lord Long and Lu Hongtu were all engaged in life-and-death battles, unable to adjust formations and positions, making it difficult to respond promptly to the combined attack of Liu Guanzhang, Ning, and Mu.

The distance between Ning Zhuo and Lin You was steadily closing.

Lin You was in the Mechanical Carriage, busy with the battle ahead, still unaware of Ning Zhuo’s proximity.

“Look over there!”

“We can’t let this enemy force act recklessly anymore.”

“Intercept them!”

A unit of troops from Thousand Peak Forest noticed Ning Zhuo’s group’s might and immediately took action, displaying their skills.

Multiple spells covered them, the firepower fierce, stopping Ning Zhuo’s three armies in their tracks.

It was the Xuanwu Army!

Ning Zhuo gazed into the distance, his eyes shimmering sharply.

This army was led by a Nascent Soul Level commander and was one of the top three outstanding armies of Thousand Peak Forest.

Thousand Peak Forest had not established a country, so this army could only gather military power, unable to mobilize national power. Since the start of the great war, the Xuanwu Army had been holding their position, continuously launching long-range spell attacks, achieving remarkable results, causing significant losses to Liangzhu Country’s soldiers.

In terms of positioning, the Xuanwu Army was akin to the Fire Cloud Camp of Liangzhu Country, both excelling in wide spell coverage and powerful long-range attacks.

The difference was that the Fire Cloud Camp specialized in fire-attribute spells, their attacks fierce, capable of igniting intense firestorms. The Xuanwu Army’s spells, deriving from witchcraft, often bizarre and uncanny, were less grand but highly unpredictable and tricky to defend against.

“How to handle this?” Ning Zhuo was contemplating the strategy when Mu Lan had already taken the initiative.

Military Art — Flower Roll Bud Core.

The Red Flower Dharma Image split into two phantoms, first falling over the Three Generals Camp and the Mechanical Army.

Then, the Red Flower Dharma Image emitted a strong suction force, pulling in various spells.

Each time a certain amount of spells were absorbed, the petals of the Red Flower Dharma Image would contract inward, forming a flower bud shape.

Layers of petals curled inwards, absorbing spells one after another, firmly protecting the three armies below.

The Red Flower Camp alone managed to shield all three armies, showcasing an impressive performance!

However, the Red Flower Camp’s military power was rapidly dwindling and couldn’t sustain this for long.

No need for Mu Lan to speak the reality, Ning Zhuo, Liu Guanzhang all understood well.

“I’ll do it!” Zhang Hei roared, his voice like a thunderclap.

Military Art — Grand Army Pressure!

With a push of his palms, he used up half of the military power of the Three Generals Camp and his own mana.

A formation of Black Water soldiers materialized out of thin air, then surged forward like a black river, charging towards the Xuanwu Army.

“Oh?” Ning Zhuo had not been closely interacting with Liu Guanzhang recently and learned about Zhang Hei’s progress for the first time.

In the past, Zhang Hei had to rely on the Black Snake Spear to execute this military art.

But now, even with his Black Snake Spear broken in half, he could perform it on his own, a considerable improvement!

Seeing the Black Water soldiers charging, the Xuanwu Army had to divert some firepower to cover them.

Although the commander of the Xuanwu Army was of barbarian origin, he led commendably, calm and composed.

Under his command, the Xuanwu Army maintained firm control over the surrounding area of the battlefield.

They quickly intercepted and killed all the Black Water soldiers, about to redirect most of their offensive back towards Ning Zhuo’s group.

At this moment, a figure stormed into the front of the Xuanwu Army.

This person was engulfed in violent gales, with wind blades flying everywhere, destroying everything around.

It was Chen Lingfeng in a state of frenzy!

Chen Lingfeng himself possessed Golden Core peak combat strength. Now, in complete madness, he fully unleashed the power of his innate talent, boosting his combat strength to Nascent Soul Level.

He spared no one, wreaking havoc, troubling both enemy and ally alike.

There were attempts to join forces against him, but the chaotic battle made coordination difficult. Moreover, Chen Lingfeng’s speed was incredibly fast, his movements unpredictable, and his position constantly shifting. For the time being, there was no effective way to deal with him.
