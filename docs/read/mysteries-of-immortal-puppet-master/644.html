Chapter 644 -189: Netherworld, Poisonous Scheme

“Even a middle-grade side attack won’t work?!” Seeing the Iron Deer Mountain God kneeling in defeat, the Mountain God of Purple Luminous felt an immense heaviness in his heart.

Next to him, the Red Sleeves Fairy observed him.

He sighed and looked back: “Red Sleeves, I’ve hosted you for a few drinks as well. Don’t look at me with those eyes, it’s quite unnerving.”

The voice of the Red Sleeves Fairy was soft and delicate, but her words were ice-cold: “It’s your turn now.”

“Once I’m done with you, my task will also be finished, and I can go to the Army Division to claim my reward.”

“Could you hurry up and make your delivery?”

The Mountain God of Purple Luminous was quite speechless.

After a few moments of silence, he let out a sigh: “Fine, escort me then.”

The Red Sleeves Fairy then burst into laughter, promptly unleashing her Divine Skills of Moving Mountain.

“You too, go ahead!”

The last one, Purple Luminous Giant Wood Mountain, appeared before the steel torrent.

The Mountain God hovered in the air above the summit, bringing his hands together, opening the dojo, making the entire Purple Luminous Giant Wood Mountain emit a lustrous purple glow.

The purple radiance enveloped the steel torrent, immediately emitting a piercing hissing sound as billows of steam rose from within it.

The strength of the steel torrent was endless, and soon, it overwhelmed the Purple Luminous Giant Wood Mountain.

However, unlike the previous giant tree mountain ranges, even as the Purple Luminous Giant Wood Mountain crumbled, it merely transformed into countless purple glowing specks, scattering in the air.

The complexion of the Mountain God of Purple Luminous turned pale, but with a smile on his face, he reached out and gathered all the purple glowing specks into his body.

“Eh?” Many of the Nascent Soul Level cultivators who saw this scene were mildly surprised.

A sharp light flickered in Zhu Xuanji’s eyes: “This Mountain God has some plans. He’s actually relying on the enemy’s sharp edge to aid in the dissolution of his mountain body, transforming it into the foundation for his own Divine Body.”

He quietly took note of the Mountain God of Purple Luminous in his heart.

Lin Bufan nodded in agreement: “To achieve this is no easy feat. It seems that the Mountain God of Purple Luminous is likely a being born from the mountain range who proceeded to cultivate into a God. Or perhaps, it’s a God using the body of the mountain through a soul-seizing Cultivation Technique.”

Du Tiechuan also remembered this Purple Luminous Mountain God.

“He does have some methods.” He controlled the entire army, with the widest and most profound perception.

Although the primary Heavy Armor army had breached the Purple Luminous mountain peak, many purple luminous specks had adhered to them.

These specks quickly expanded among the armor and shadows, turning into patches of purple luminance.

Under the searing of these patches, the body temperature of the soldiers rose swiftly, their physical strength was doubly exhausted, and their body’s water was rapidly evaporated.

After observing for a while, Du Tiechuan gained new insight: “This must be some form of light poison!”

After successively breaching four large mountains, each with a Mountain God and their dojo, the momentum of the Heavy Armor Main Army dropped by twenty percent.

Among the soldiers, those with shallow foundations were already feeling tired.

“This won’t do.” Du Tiechuan had already firmly memorized the topographic map of Thousand Peak Forest.

He knew that the main force had only covered thirty percent of the journey, and they hadn’t even completed half the distance to their destination.

Thus, he unhesitatingly took out the National Power Bricks.

He threw the National Power Bricks directly behind him.

Over twenty dark bricks smashed into the black iron torrent and immediately dissolved, transforming into surging forces, providing greater support to the Heavy Armor Army!

“Oh, oh, oh!”

The soldiers let out excited cheers, feeling a powerful force being infused, sweeping away their fatigue and reinvigorating their spirits.

The steel torrent regained its peak prowess and continued its charge into the heart of Thousand Peak Forest.

“They’re aiming for the Dragon King Giant Wood Mountain, we absolutely cannot let them succeed!” Lord Long’s face was covered with frost.

Next to him, Lu Hongtu reassured with his Divine Sense transmission: “Lord Long, please be at ease, the Heaven and Earth Ghosts are already in position.”

Lord Long did not hesitate and directly ordered: “Let them take action! I hope their performance is worth their price.”

The Heaven and Earth Ghosts received the message but did not act immediately, choosing instead to continue observing.

Seeing no action from Lord Long after a lengthy wait, he angrily issued orders, questioning through the transmissions.

The Heaven and Earth Ghosts sneered at this and paid no heed until after Lord Long had urged them thrice, and then the White-clothed Ghost Cultivator Tian Shuang finally smiled lightly: “The time has come.”

The Black-clothed Ghost Cultivator Earth Ash was even more straightforward: “Let’s do this!”

They stood on each side, positioning themselves atop two mountain ranges.

Tian Shuang frequently manipulated Finger Techniques with both hands, eventually crossing his hands over his chest as his Mana gushed forth like a burst dam at this moment.

A ghastly and extremely cold Ghost Energy converged around him.

He chanted lightly, with the Spell echoing around the peak: “Ghosts of darkness, Tian Shuang conjures the soul, open my ghost gate, unfold the Netherworld.”

In the next moment, the Ghost Energy burst into flames, turning into a deep blue Ghost Fire.

The Ghost Fire, as large as a city, soared up high, piercing the sky.

During this process, the Ghost Fire spread, forming the outline of a huge gate.

On the other mountain peak, Earth Ash stomped his feet, and a three-dimensional Array appeared around him immediately.

The Array operated, spewing forth countless Ghost Energy from the Netherworld.

With a deep shout, he proclaimed: “Hell’s Nether Spring, Earth Ash gathers shadows, open my ghost gate, welcome lost spirits.”

In the next instant, the Ghost Energy transformed into numerous dust particles, spreading across the ground, following the traces of Ghost Fire created by Tian Shuang, climbing upwards along the path, filling the entire outline of the huge gate.

The giant gate quickly took a defined form, turning solid.

Tian Shuang and Earth Ash simultaneously activated their Combined Attack Divine Ability, merging the two forces within the woods swiftly.

Both Ghost Cultivators shouted together: “Qiankun interchanging, yin and yang reversed!”

The Ghost Fire and the Ghost Dust vanished abruptly, perfectly merging into a new force.

At this moment, the grand ghost gate was fully materialized.

On the surface of the ghost gate, there was an enormous skull.

The deep eye sockets of the skull and its mouth full of sharp teeth.

It let out a ghostly laugh, opening its mouth wider and wider, eventually stretching so far that it squashed the rest of the skull face, leaving only the vast mouth, which continued to expand to the size of the gate frame.
