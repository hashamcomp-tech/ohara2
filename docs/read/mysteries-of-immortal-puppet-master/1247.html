Chapter 1247: Chapter 483: Inheriting Dao Jade Page (Part 1)

The Five Elements are metal, wood, water, fire, and earth; the Five Internal Organs are the heart, liver, spleen, lungs, and kidneys. The Five Elements correspond to the Five Internal Organs, each performing its role, generating and overcoming each other, endlessly circulating...

This is the content of the "Five Internal Organs Temple Spirit Divine Skill," which can be said to gather the essence of heaven and earth, harmonizing the wonders of the Five Elements and deeply embodying Dao and principle.

After the Five Elements God Master taught it to Ning Zhuo, Ning Zhuo could not cultivate it.

The big-headed youth had already cultivated the Three Superior Sect Techniques, with all three Dantians occupied, so he had to modify the "Five Internal Organs Temple Spirit Divine Skill" into a spell.

This spell consists of five sections, corresponding to the Five Elements of metal, wood, water, fire, and earth. To succeed in cultivation requires the consumption of Five Elements True Essence of at least ten thousand years.

From another perspective, it’s essentially about utilizing the principle of Ten Thousand Grade Five Elements True Essence, applying it to one’s own Five Internal Organs.

The cost is high, but the effect is excellent. During his time in Liangzhu Country, Ning Zhuo, relying on three elements of this, had repeatedly made stunning performances on the battlefield.

Now, having arrived at the Myriad Images Sect, he still perfectly controlled the three-color flames from the very start.

Once he activated the Spirit God within the Five Internal Organs Temple, he could master the Five Elements Magic to the utmost. Thus, any Five Elements Magic he attempted for the first time, Ning Zhuo could perform proficiently, saving him from much practice of spells.

With such flames, Ning Zhuo smoothly passed the second Chapter.

Zhao Hansheng read the third Chapter aloud: "The measure of warp and weft corresponds to the celestial balance. Huang-gu paused the shuttle, borrowing the craftsmanship of Tian Sun; Lu Ban cut off his hand, imitating the loom of creation. Weave the clouds and mist as the weft, stitch the stars as the warp. Initially forming like the River Map appearing and disappearing, upon further inspection resembling the Luoshu’s floating and sinking..."

All the cultivators: ??

Ning Zhuo listened carefully, his brows tightly knit, and initially judged that this step of papermaking involves at least the fields of astrology, mathematical calculations, and the craft of weaving.

"The ’measure of warp and weft corresponds to the celestial balance’ explains the corresponding relationship between astrology and weaving."

"The ’Luoshu’ does not refer to the Taoist Artifact Luoshu. It should signify numerical calculation."

"No wonder there’s a mechanical loom at the booth."

Ning Zhuo directed his gaze at the booth in front of him, and with the third Chapter came new treasures and tools transmitted.

The main framework of the loom is all metal, with a dark silver luster, embedded with twenty-eight gems arranged according to the constellations. The shuttle is even more exquisite, completely transparent, with a rotating seven-colored light inside.

Cultivators with knowledge recognized it: "This is a loom made of Star Meteor Iron, and what’s sealed inside the shuttle should be Rainbow Light True Essence?"

Ning Zhuo caressed the loom, and his Divine Sense explored it, quickly gaining a thorough understanding. After all, he is a Mechanism Cultivator, and this is precisely his area of expertise.

More cultivators directed their Divine Senses towards Gu Qing.

Gu Qing began.

He extracted fine threads from the Wenguang Paste. After the tempering of the second Chapter, these threads were as tough as silk, yet lighter and softer.

The threads were arranged on the loom, with eighty-one vertical threads and eighty-one horizontal threads.

Gu Qing’s weaving speed noticeably slowed down. He was well aware that this step was crucial. The number of threads was limited, and if there were too many mistakes, he would have to start refining from scratch.

Slow work yields fine craftsmanship.

Gu Qing’s every stitch was incredibly precise. As he wove each intersection, he softly chanted runes that landed on the fabric, causing the corresponding gems on the loom to flicker lightly.

Gradually, on the woven paper embryo base, a faint Nine Palace grid pattern emerged.

"Is this Nine Palace Numerology? This is the most obscure and profound among all formations." Many cultivators who saw this scene felt despair.

As Gu Qing wove the formation into the paper embryo, Ning Zhuo was also studying.

Having mastered the loom, Ning Zhuo furrowed his brow, encountering trouble: "My Wenguang Paste from the beginning was not as good as Gu Qing’s. After the tempering of the second Chapter, it’s only the size of a fist."

According to Ning Zhuo’s understanding of the third Chapter, he discovered that his base material might only yield complete thread lines numbering around a hundred.

Whereas the requirement of the third Chapter calls for eighty-one threads.

"There’s very little room for error."

"This is my first attempt at papermaking, yet I can only afford to make mistakes a little over twenty times."

"Weaving techniques..."

This is one of the Cultivation Hundred Skills, but Ning Zhuo is not adept at it.

"However..."

Ning Zhuo dug into his memory, quickly discovering that among the Demon Cultivators he had absorbed, someone was involved in this path.

He revisited the knowledge, then began operating the mechanical loom.

At the same time, he transmitted thoughts through Life Hanging Thread to Sun Lingtong, "Boss, assist me!"

"On my way." Sun Lingtong had long been ready, immediately activating his Spiritual Eyes, drawing upon the Luoshu family’s enhancement effect, and transmitted it through Life Hanging Thread to Ning Zhuo.

With the aid of the Luoshu, mechanical loom, and the memory of the Demon Cultivator, Ning Zhuo’s weaving speed was even slower than Gu Qing’s.

The warp is vertical, the weft is horizontal; the warp is heaven, the weft is earth; heaven and earth interweave, forming the world.

The important thing is not the tangible threads but the intangible principles described in the form of a formation.

Weaving is classified as one of the Cultivation Hundred Skills, not merely for the structure but for weaving principles and materials together, forming a unified whole.

The deeper level of weaving is a deeper internal reorganization and arrangement.

This is not something Ning Zhuo can currently reach.

Every cultivation technique is vast and profound, enriched, expanded, elevated, and accumulated by countless predecessors and sages.

Fortunately, the weaving techniques involved in this papermaking are not that advanced.

Three Two Hours later, Ning Zhuo wiped the sweat from his forehead and looked at the finished product in front of him, exhaling a breath of impure air.
