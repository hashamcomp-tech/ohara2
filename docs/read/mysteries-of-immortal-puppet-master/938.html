Chapter 938: Chapter 332: Withered and Flourishing, Sorrow Shared (Part 2)

Boom boom boom...

Both sides engage in close combat.

The Earth Evil God’s fists are like heavenly drums, driving millions of turbid currents to hammer the Buddha Illusion! Each punch falls, causing the space to shock violently as if a tattered fabric, producing unbearable groans!

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion’s lifeless, wooden body is deeply shaken. The Buddha Light surrounding it is extinguished countless times, the Buddha’s body damaged, dented, and filled with numerous cracks.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion’s body shrinks slightly, it extends its arms, using the Buddha Palm Seal, and could only defend for a moment.

Wangchuan Immortal City.

"Quickly, follow me!"

"The enemy is right in here!!"

"Surround them, don’t let them escape, the Prefect’s orders are that they must die! No matter who it is!!!"

A large number of cultivators encircle the area, surrounding Zeng Jide and Sun Lingtong completely.

Sun Lingtong takes a sharp breath, "Senior, we only took a little something, how did it become like stirring up a hornet’s nest?"

Zeng Jide reveals a solemn expression, gritting his teeth, "Interesting! This stone is quite important to the Lord of Wangchuan."

As he speaks, his Stealing Technique doesn’t stop and he steals another one.

Splat.

In the distant core battlefield, the newly awakened Earth Evil God deflates once more.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion gains the upper hand.

The Earth-Lung Miasma barrier vibrates, another Earth Lung Tuberculosis disintegrates, transforming into an Earth Elemental flood, transmitted by the Great Array.

Roar!

The Earth Evil God rises, spouting a yellow-brown light pillar, the attack range is short, shattering half of the Buddha Illusion’s shoulder.

Another Earth Lung Tuberculosis is removed by Zeng Jide.

Splat.

The Earth Evil God deflates, the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion slaps its top, collapsing its entire skull, reducing the black smoke it spouts significantly.

Roar!

Splat.

Roar!

Splat...

The Lord of Wangchuan’s eyes are filled with bloodlust, raging inside: "No matter who is stirring things up behind my back! After this battle, I will muster the nation’s strength to pursue you, I must strip you and smash your bones, leaving your soul scattered!"

He has never hated someone so much.

More than Yin Jiu and Ning Zhuo, more hateful and angry.

This truly unsettles one’s mind.

The Earth Evil God fluctuates in power, greatly disrupting its combat rhythm. Even if it fights with all its strength, it only delays the advance of the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion doesn’t fare well, much of its power is needed to suppress the Wangchuan Prefect Seal.

This National Treasure becomes increasingly restless as it nears the Lord of Wangchuan’s true body.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion has no alternatives, knowing it can only achieve swift victory!

It waits patiently, even though its power is strong, it is water without a source. In contrast, the Lord of Wangchuan, though heavily wounded in body, is actively accumulating strength for a decisive battle. Additionally, the Earth Evil God can rely on the Earth Phase Array for continuous replenishment.

It may deflate, but ultimately replenishes more.

The Earth Evil God’s fist strikes the Buddha Illusion, cracking the Buddha’s body and dimming the Buddha Light.

The Buddha Illusion’s palm imprint also brands the Earth Evil God’s divine body, leaving an indelible print.

Both sides fight desperately amidst explosions, Buddha Light and Earth Energy tightly entwined, fiercely exhausted, shattering the space. Countless pieces of space fly about like shattered glass.

Eventually, this battle group pushes to the front of the Wangchuan Tributary.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion has shrunk again and again, its figure only one-third of before. Its wooden Buddha form is filled with cracks, its shoulders collapsed, chest sunken, Buddha Light no longer, only scattered light spots spilling from wounds like leaking essence.

The Earth Evil God is also battered and stumbling, with a broken arm needing urgent replenishment. The Buddha Palm Seal left several large wounds, especially on its back, almost breaking its spine, shocking to witness.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion joins its hands at its chest, its mouth remains closed, yet an extinction Zen Chant resonates through the cosmos, shaking souls!

Invisible ripples violently expand, not only freeing it from the Earth Evil God in front but also impacting the Wangchuan Tributary, causing towering water waves, forming raging waves.

The Zen Chant crashes like an invisible heavenly blade, threatening to split the Wangchuan Tributary into two.

The Wangchuan Tributary, which made Ning Zhuo hesitate, is dealt with by the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion in one move.

"Come." The Lord of Wangchuan’s true body launches furiously.

He races against time to rejuvenate, struggling to sleep, but has accumulated strength for a decisive battle.

In an instant, the Wangchuan Tributary roared with anger, forming into a flood dragon amidst the raging waves, pouncing towards the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion neither dodged nor evaded, instead spreading open its palms, extending its arms, pouring nearly all battle power into the effort.

Its form became dim and lifeless, yet the Silent Extinction Zen became even more pure and vast.

Buddhist Law—Decay and Prosperity Share the Same Sorrow!

The water flood dragon seemed to embrace the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion with joy, but upon nearing, it withered like dried vegetation, its power plummeting.

Contrarily, the Buddha Illusion’s aura soared, as if absorbing the water flood dragon’s strength, its might steadily rising.

The Lord of Wanchuan was surrounded by a ghostly light that surged to the heavens, with the Wangchuan Illusion raging behind him. He dedicated all his strength, engraved with a solemn and grave expression, steadfastly staring at the formidable foe before him.

The battle power he had painstakingly accumulated rapidly depleted like the flood dragon charging forward relentlessly.

But in the next moment, the Earth Evil God pounced from behind.

It roared explosively, its divine body suddenly collapsed, compressing to the extreme, transformed into a huge meteor.

Boom...

The meteor hurled forward, blazing with dark yellow flames, trailing a long tail of black smoke.

This was a sacrificial strike!

The meteor crashed towards the Buddha’s back, resisted by the flourishing Buddha Light, gradually slowing its speed, yet persistently approaching.

The Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion was under a pincer attack, besieged from both sides.

Its Decay and Prosperity Share the Same Sorrow could constrain the enemy and enhance itself, but such an offensive exceeded its conversion limit.

The Buddha’s body flickered, like the shadows of candlelight in a stormy wind.

Joy flashed in the eyes of the Lord of Wanchuan, finally seeing a chance for victory in this deadly struggle!

Wanchuan Immortal City.

Zeng Jide shouted, once again offering his lifebound magical treasure.

The Three-handed Monkey Buddha Statue suddenly shone with light, and Buddha Light gathered to form a monkey’s paw, directly grasping the entire giant stone.

In the next moment, Sun Lingtong heard a sharp scream from the monkey’s head, and the monkey’s paw retracted, bringing the entire giant stone back!

The giant stone was massive, yet it became smaller during the retrieval process.

Ultimately, Zeng Jide withdrew the lifebound Buddha Statue, holding a miniature sculpture of the giant stone that was several times smaller.

Zeng Jide groaned, his aura plummeting. One hand cradled the sculpture while the other grasped Sun Lingtong’s arm.

"Let’s go!"

Sun Lingtong felt dazed, as if enveloped in a multitude of colorful flowers.

Almost within the next breath, a group of cultivators from the City Lord’s Mansion burst in.

"Don’t let anyone escape... Where are they?!"

The cultivators’ expressions underwent dramatic changes.

Altar Battlefield.

The Earth Evil Meteor suddenly jolted, with its fierce burning dark yellow flame rapidly dimming, reduced to a small flicker.

The meteor compressed to its utmost swiftly disintegrated, countless fragments scattered, billows of miasma scattered uncontrollably in all directions.

The pressure on the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion sharply decreased, reviving its majesty!

It straightened itself forward, extending its arms, in an embracing gesture, advancing towards the Lord of Wanchuan.

Water flood dragons and giant waves relentlessly slammed against its face, making the Buddha Illusion grow increasingly dim, with the cracks on its surface multiplying and intertwining.

The Lord of Wanchuan continuously retreated, his face ghastly pale, with a touch of desolation.

His eyes remained sharp, filled with fury, tinged with resentment and unwillingness, but quickly turned to doubt and fear.

He understood the state of battle clearly, realizing that he could no longer endure.

If he didn’t retreat, he would be engulfed by the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion, perishing and vanishing! The esteemed Prefect was about to stake his life on this rebellious endeavor.

"Well, Silent Ghost Monk, I didn’t expect you to be the ultimate victor."

"I won’t forget the enmity of obstruction; we cannot coexist under the same sky, and I’ll remember that!"

In the next moment, the Lord of Wanchuan spat out a mouthful of essence, activating the Heavenly Phase Formation, teleporting himself out, disappearing from sight.

Without his support, the torrents reversed, and the flood dragons exploded entirely.

The pressure from the front on the Silent Extinction Buddha Illusion drastically dropped, allowing it to turn and attack the Earth Evil God.

Its five fingers spread, its Buddha’s hand growing larger, seemingly encompassing the Qiankun!

Under the Buddha’s hand, it covered the meteor’s divine body, gripping fiercely.

Boom——!!!!!!!
