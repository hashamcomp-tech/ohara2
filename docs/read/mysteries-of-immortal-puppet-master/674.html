Chapter 674 -201: Ning Zhuo: I Want to Test My Limits_3

Yan Jin swung his fists and palms, triggering the bell’s sound and launching a ferocious attack on the two giants.

The giants moved slowly but took large strides, pushing through the bell’s sound, charging towards the light shield, and swinging their fists, continuously pounding on it.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

Each strike was full of power, causing severe cracks to appear on the light shield.

Yan Jin exerted all his strength, making the giant bell shake left and right, unleashing surging sound waves to counter the attacks.

The two giants, refined by the bell’s sound, had their arms fall off, their fists turned into mush, and their bodies suddenly shortened by half, as they were cut in half at the waist.

In the sky, the Vermilion Bird-shaped flame reformed and surged towards Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo multitasked, forming a finger technique with his left hand while reaching for the Vermilion Bird fire with his right.

With his actions, another torrent sprung forth from thin air.

The torrent spiraled upwards, transforming in the air to become a flood dragon.

With its mouth wide open, the flood dragon precisely bit down on the Vermilion Bird fire.

Boom!

With an explosion, both the water dragon and the fire bird perished together.

On the other side, Ning Zhuo completed the finger technique with his left hand.

Like a sword, his joined fingers shot out a streak of golden light from his index and middle fingers.

The golden light was brilliantly radiant, as swift as a shooting star, striking the light shield in the blink of an eye from the moment Yan Jin saw it.

The light shield only lasted for half a breath before being pierced through.

The golden light, losing none of its momentum, struck the giant bell, creating a small hole in it.

Yan Jin’s expression changed drastically, transmitting thoughts via divine sense, questioning Ning Zhuo, “How is such power possible? This doesn’t match the intel! Have you been hiding your strength all along?!”

Ning Zhuo let out a hearty laugh, feeling a great sense of achievement, mimicking Sun Lingtong with his hands on his hips, “You guessed right!”

“Senior Yan, did you think I came here alone just to take a reckless gamble?”

“Naturally, I am prepared!”

“To tell you the truth, even if Luo Chen arrives, I have ways to deal with him.”

He boasted grandly.

Yan Jin had knowledge of Ning Zhuo’s intel, but the latter’s current combat strength indeed far exceeded the information revealed.

It wasn’t that Ning Zhuo had held back his power before, but that he had made rapid progress during this period.

All along, his Heroic Return Skill had been restricted by the foundation of his physical body, making it difficult to fully augment his power.

But through the evil path usage of the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat, Ning Zhuo’s physical foundation had skyrocketed, making his strength incomparable to before.

Over five hundred Mechanical Puppets were destroyed, boosting his combat power to Golden Core Level, surpassing past peaks, and reaching a top-tier Golden Core Level!

Yan Jin’s mind wavered along with the giant bell.

“Calm down!”

“I still have assistance from Luo Chen and Flowing Flame Fairy, I’m not fighting alone.”

The next moment, Flowing Flame Fairy transmitted a message through the formation, “Great Craftsman Yan, you must hold on. The Three Generals Camp is moving again, marching in my direction.”

The three generals waited for a moment, then noticed the bell’s sound decreasing and the fire curtain slowing. Liu Er made a snap decision, leading the army forward, encountering half the obstacles as before.

Flowing Flame Fairy was too preoccupied to care for herself.

Yan Jin quickly asked Luo Chen, “My lord, have you made any progress in capturing the wood-embedded fire?”

Luo Chen’s face was dark, “The wood-embedded fire is extremely slippery, almost as if it has a spiritual nature, I haven’t caught it yet. You hold on for now!”

Yan Jin was speechless.

Ning Zhuo’s eyes sparkled, reflecting the flames and the giant bell.

“Boss, I feel I still have energy left, I haven’t reached my physical limits. I want to test my limits.”

Sun Lingtong giggled, “Little Zhuo, go ahead and play, I’ll keep watch around you. If Luo Chen comes, I’ll let you know.”

Ning Zhuo released three hundred Mechanical Puppets.

Ning Zhuo then commanded the puppets to form the Light Body and Swift Wind Formation.

The Mechanical Army moved like the wind, as Ning Zhuo flew above them, performing a finger technique, his left hand emitting the previous golden light of the sword finger, while his right palm faced the sky, summoning a massive meteor.

“Senior Yan, take this!” The youth’s momentum surged again, his excited and thrilling voice echoing across the battlefield.

Yan Jin: “…”
