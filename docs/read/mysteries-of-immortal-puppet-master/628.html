Chapter 628

According to the military order issued by Commander Du Tiechuan, even if the Red Flower Camp used up their entire allocation, the heavy armor obtained would still not cover all the soldiers of the Red Flower Camp.

As the great battle is approaching, these days all the armies have been expanding recruitment and undergoing frequent military training.

This put Ning Zhuo into a difficult position of making a choice.

To choose Mu Lan or to choose Liu Er?

The more Ning Zhuo thought about it, the harder it became to decide.

Both were Golden Core Level Cultivators.

In terms of combat power, Mu Lan was significantly superior.

Once she deployed the Divine Skills Red Heart Arrow with the weapons Red Phoenix Bow and Fiery Heart Arrow, her combat power would soar to an exaggerated level—reaching the peak of the Nascent Soul stage!

As for Liu Er's combat power, it was also considerable, ranking at the frontline of the Golden Core Level.

In terms of identity, Mu Lan was the sole heir of Mu Shang General Mansion in Liangzhu Country, undoubtedly a high-level figure in Liangzhu Country. However, the situation of the entire Mu Shang General Mansion was worrisome, coveted by the court. Even Liangzhu Country's Monarch had attempted to dissolve it.

Liu Er also had a notable identity, and in fact an even greater background—he was a royal relative of the Blood Slaughter Dynasty. However, such an identity would be exalted within the Blood Slaughter Dynasty, but by now, it was not highly valued, being merely a title.

Even Liu Er's own half-demon, half-human bloodline was not well received by today's mainstream Cultivation World.

In terms of financial capability.

Mu Lan was poor, Liu Er was even poorer.

In terms of relationships.

Even though Mu Lan and Mu Shang General Mansion were in a precarious situation, they still had some connections in the court of Liangzhu Country.

Liu Er was weak in this aspect, but through the Yin Yang Qi Pot, Ning Zhuo could vaguely feel that Liu Er's background was not as simple and shallow as it appeared on the surface.

Mu Lan and Ning Zhuo were in a marital relationship, but in essence, it was an alliance of interests.

The alliance of interests between Liu Er and Ning Zhuo was even closer and more solid than that between Mu Lan and Ning Zhuo. Because Ning Zhuo had begun investing in the Three Generals Liu Guanzhang quite early on.

Up to now, Ning Zhuo had always been the biggest financier of the Three Generals Camp!

Ning Zhuo fell into contemplation without speaking, subconsciously touching the Mechanical Ring on his finger.

Unfortunately, in this situation, it didn't involve his own safety, and the Mechanical Ring remained still without any reaction.

Ning Zhuo smiled faintly, "Generals, I have a somewhat immature idea."

"Have you both considered... merging into one army?"

Ning Zhuo glanced at Mu Lan, then turned to Liu Er, "Our heavy armor is limited, only enough to cover a majority of the soldiers."

"Under these circumstances, the soldiers from the Three Generals Camp would be eliminated from their roles and merged into the Red Flower Camp."

"After this great war is over, we can separate again, rebuild and reorganize, and that would also be acceptable."

Mu Lan's eyes brightened, "That's quite a good method."

"Under normal circumstances, my Red Flower Camp would absolutely not accept so many outsiders. Mainly because the Red Flower Battle Formation requires experienced hands, needing years of training."

"For us, it's not about having more soldiers the better."

"But this time for the great battle, we are to undertake Commander Du's Military Art Iron Stream Flat Country. Hence, the Red Flower Battle Formation cannot be formed, and there might even be a possibility of winning after one battle. So, bringing in many outsiders is also acceptable."

On the contrary, Liu Er showed a troubled expression, "General Mu Lan, military strategist, please give me some time to think it over."

Mu Lan scoffed coldly, "Then you'd better decide quickly."

She no longer looked at Liu Er, instead turning her gaze to Ning Zhuo, "Husband, let's not talk about other things for now, let's go to the Quartermaster to secure our full quota first."

Ning Zhuo clasped his hands together, forced a bitter smile and said, "General Mu Lan, you can just call me by my name or military title. Our relationship is but a twist of fate, not to be taken seriously."

Mu Lan waved her hand dismissively, "I am the daughter of a military family, I've never cared about such trivial matters as a title."

"Warfare is perilous; compared with one's life, what is mere reputation worth?"

"Life and death is the matter of utmost importance!"

"Let's go now."

As Mu Lan kept urging, Ning Zhuo had no choice but to apologize to Liu Er and leave with Mu Lan.

Liu Er had an unchanging expression, but felt considerably heavy-hearted as he started on his return to the Three Generals Camp.

"I carry the King's Order, and from a young age, I've been determined to establish a nation for Demon Cultivators."

"My master advised me that it's best to maintain an independent establishment."

"If I can't lead the army on my own, it will be difficult to assign the merits to me. After all, I am nothing but a cross-breed."

"Ah, Master, where are you? What should I do?"

Liu Er estimated that the best scenario would be if Ning Zhuo favored the Three Generals Camp, entrusting them with all the heavy armor he owned.

But when Liu Er put himself in another's place, he felt the likelihood of this was slim to none.

"Ning Zhuo and Mu Lan are husband and wife after all. Besides, Mu Lan's noble lineage and background are far superior to mine."

"Even though Ning Zhuo is young, passionate, but part of a powerful family, he has sufficient foundation to weigh the pros and cons."

"If he ultimately chooses to lean towards the Red Flower Camp, I wouldn't be surprised, and would completely understand."

"What should I do?"

Ning Zhuo has offered too much support to the Three Generals Camp, and at this moment, Liu Er realized that he simply had no grounds to persuade Ning Zhuo.

Liu Er then thought of the Yin Yang Qi Pot.

"If I were to gift this item, could it possibly make Ning Zhuo more inclined towards us?"

He shook his head with a bitter smile.

Just as he was about to reach the gate of the Three Generals Camp, a new idea struck Liu Er.

He thought of someone.

"Perhaps, I could seek his assistance?"

White Jade Camp.

Shuangjing stood in front of the Main General's tent, with a welcoming smile, awaiting Ning Zhuo's arrival.

"Friend Ning Zhuo, please come in," Shuangjing gestured with his hand invitingly.

Ning Zhuo promptly bowed in respect, expressing that receiving such a treatment from the great Shuangjing himself was indeed an honor beyond expectation.
