Chapter 786 -260: One Battle Three!_2

“Watch out!” she warned hastily.

A moment too late.

The Butcher Ghost General had already charged in front of Ning Zhuo, like an out-of-control carriage, crashing directly toward him.

Given Ning Zhuo’s small frame, not even reaching a third of the Butcher Ghost General’s size, if he were truly hit, he’d likely be smashed to pieces, sent flying high up, and die spitting blood in mid-air!

Wen Ruanyu couldn’t help but squint her eyes, unwilling to see Ning Zhuo’s tragic plight.

But in the next moment, a flash of mirror light.

A wall of Redsun Copper abruptly stood between the Butcher Ghost General and Ning Zhuo.

Upon closer inspection, it turned out to be a Mechanical Monkey.

What a monkey!

It rose from the ground, nearly two zhang tall, like a small building, with a powerful build, closely matching that of the Butcher Ghost General.

Casted with Wanji Xuan Iron, safeguarded entirely with Redsun Copper, its diamond-made tusks were as long as half an adult’s arm, with wind-fire wheels embedded in its calves, exceeding six feet in diameter.

The Mechanism Core Ball Furnace fired within the monkey, causing a stream of high-temperature air to erupt.

The Blood Oil Bottle and Blood Spring Jug in its abdomen formed a circulation, emitting a faint blood mist.

It was indeed the Heavy Blood Ape· Great Victory!

Bang!

The Butcher Ghost General collided solidly with Yuan Dasheng’s chest.

Yuan Dasheng took one step back, completely absorbing the impact.

Instead, the Butcher Ghost General felt dizzy from the collision.

Bam.

Yuan Dasheng delivered an uppercut to the Butcher Ghost General’s abdomen.

The Butcher Ghost General doubled over from the strike, wide-eyed and glaring hard, almost popping out his eyeballs.

He hastily waved his butcher’s knife, striking back blindly.

Yuan Dasheng sidestepped, then turned around, swinging his elbow like a spear, directly hitting the Butcher Ghost General in the face.

In that instant, the Butcher Ghost General’s head jerked back, his face battered into a dent.

Yuan Dasheng assumed a stance and kicked with his leg.

As he exerted force, the wind-fire wheel on his calf abruptly spun, raising a trail of flames, doubling the power of his kick.

Bam!

Another dull thud, sending the Butcher Ghost General flying.

Bam bam bam…

The Butcher Ghost General flew backward, crashing through two walls and numerous facilities in the workshop.

Then, he was buried under the collapsed wall bricks and stones.

Wen Ruanyu’s tension instantly eased, feeling quite relieved, “Almost forgot, Jiao Ma still hid a Golden Core level mechanical puppet.”

“This Golden Core level mechanism is indeed ferocious.”

Roar!

The Butcher Ghost General’s roar shattered smoke and dust as he strode out angrily.

Yuan Dasheng had already pounced in front of him.

Bam bam bam…

A barrage of attacks landed on the Butcher Ghost General.

The butcher’s knife in the Butcher Ghost General’s hand had already slipped during his flight, leaving him to fight recklessly with fists and feet against Yuan Dasheng.

The two exchanged blows fiercely, each strike filled with force, generating gusty wind sounds.

Inside the Heavy Blood Ape· Great Victory, the twenty-seven bronze pillars on the Mechanism Core Ball Furnace surface expanded and contracted rapidly like living creatures, consuming Red Fire Essence continuously, and billowing a large amount of steam.

The embedded wind-fire wheels in its calves spun swiftly, whistling with the wind, stirring up flaming tails.

As they fought, the Butcher Ghost General fell into a disadvantage.

“What the hell! This Mechanical Monkey’s martial abilities surpass mine?!” The Butcher Ghost General was greatly shocked, trying to turn the tables, only to be anticipated by the Mechanical Monkey time and again.

“Great control!” Wen Ruanyu observed the battle while entangled with the Mirror Makeup Ghost General.

In his view, Yuan Dasheng was clearly a mechanism, showing such profound martial skills was all thanks to Ning Zhuo.

“Could Jiao Ma actually be a Mechanism Cultivator?”

“This doesn’t match the intelligence at all,” Wen Ruanyu remarked.

Ghost Technique — Nether Soul Cast Iron.

The Butcher Ghost General bellowed, employing the Ghost Technique, greatly enhancing his defense. Yuan Dasheng’s blows landed on him, clanging like metal.

The Mirror Makeup Ghost General was startled, “To actually force the Butcher to use this Ghost Technique. But not bad, with this boost, victory is assured!”

The Mirror Makeup Ghost General knew this was a powerful trump card of her comrade. She had witnessed its prowess many times in past battles.

Sure enough, with this stance, Yuan Dasheng’s attacks became feeble.

The Butcher Ghost General was about to turn the tide and vent his anger when unexpectedly, a spell struck him.

Wood Element — Root Vine Entanglement!

Wood Element Liver Temple!!

Vivid green vines burst from the ground, directly entwining the Butcher Ghost General on the spot.

The Butcher Ghost General couldn’t move, forced to unleash another Ghost Technique, violently cutting the green vines.

But though the vines were severed, the parts entwining the Butcher Ghost General slithered like serpents, binding his arms, tugging at his thighs, distorting his every movement.

In this way, the Butcher Ghost General became vulnerable, easily handled by Yuan Dasheng, pummeled with a ringing sound, becoming a mere punching bag.

“How is this happening?!” The Mirror Makeup Ghost General was stunned, so far from her expectations.

Ning Zhuo merely cast a Wood Element Spell, effortlessly thwarting the Butcher Ghost General’s comeback attempt.

Inside the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon, Sun Lingtong felt deeply resonated.

It seemed like he was witnessing how, within the Lava Fairy Palace, Ning Zhuo and Yuan Dasheng collaborated closely to overcome challenges.

“In such a short time? Little Zhuo’s strength has grown to this level.”

“Such tremendous progress!”

“The Ghost General was a Golden Core, yet was powerless against this {human and monkey combo} duo.”

Watching Ning Zhuo and Yuan Dasheng’s performance, Wen Ruanyu was visibly uplifted, thinking, “We can win! I see a chance for victory!”

“Who knew Jiao Ma’s combat strength would turn out to be so formidable.”
