Chapter 1031: Chapter 377: Borrowing a Brush

"What’s going on here?" The trio of Bai Liu Kong were stunned, their gaze seemingly glued to the Golden Core in Ning Zhuo’s hand.

Their eyes struggled to tear away from the Golden Core and turn to Ning Zhuo, now looking at him differently than before.

Ning Zhuo had demonstrated his power!

After all, it’s their first meeting; one must acknowledge the appearance before the person.

Ning Zhuo carefully observed the change in their expressions, knowing his strategy had succeeded.

"I achieved the top spot in the Feiyun trial, acquiring the Ideograph Brush, yet I am not a Confucian Cultivator, but an outsider."

"Such circumstances easily stir jealousy and resentment among the Confucian Cultivators towards me."

"Therefore, it’s essential to display outstanding strength, making them feel: even though I’m an outsider, as a formidable figure, winning is only natural, nothing strange."

This was Ning Zhuo’s calculation.

Of course, he originally had the intention of trade.

Innate Technique!

Using innate talent as a source, supplemented by some means to leverage even stronger or more profound powers.

To cast spells, it’s not only reliant on mana; many treasures can be utilized.

Ning Zhuo’s Weather Balloon·Ice Crystal Snow can consume soul, transforming into a fierce ice and snow.

Ning Zhuo witnessed Kong Ran using Early Wisdom Talent to perform techniques during Chu Xuangu’s Feiyun trial.

He always used Early Wisdom Talent in the simplest way.

For the development of Early Wisdom Talent, Ning Zhuo always wanted to do it well. This Early Wisdom Talent could elevate to Divine Skills in the future!

"Any innate talent needs to be developed, and there are techniques for development. Once developed, it becomes Divine Skills, greatly benefiting the cultivator!"

Ning Zhuo understood this long ago.

Previously, in Fire Persimmon Immortal City, he wanted to develop Early Wisdom Talent but was entirely inadequate. Moreover, there were significant risks. After all, he also had to disguise his identity, constantly endure, waiting for the right opportunity to seize Lava Fairy Palace.

After the struggle for Lava Fairy Palace temporarily settled, Ning Zhuo left Fire Persimmon Immortal City and began to travel the world, mainly aiming to revive his mother, leaving no time for talent development. He barely kept up with his cultivation.

The Confucian trial brought Ning Zhuo four rewards. First, the Ideograph Brush, second, Ning Zhuo’s status was raised, brilliantly debuting in the Confucian circle! Third, Ning Zhuo witnessed Chu Xuangu’s Righteous Path techniques and was eager to learn. Fourth, he learned that Kong Ran not only has Early Wisdom Talent but also corresponding talent techniques!

Kong Ran retracted his gaze, then slowly shook his head.

"Daoist Fellow Ning Zhuo, the Myriad Phenomena Cradle is not simple!"

Ning Zhuo raised his brows slightly, silently remembering the name of this spell.

Kong Ran: "After using Myriad Phenomena Cradle, my perception of the world’s myriad things becomes akin to a newborn baby. It’s like encountering for the first time, gaining more insights and guidance."

"As the saying goes: Human emulates Earth, Earth emulates Heaven, Heaven emulates Nature."

"Everything carries principles and reason, providing guidance to cultivators."

"Because of this, some cultivators can observe the scenery and grasp Dao, can see a mountain stone and learn a world-stabilizing spell!"

Kong Ran brought his point back: "The essence of Myriad Phenomena Cradle is treating heaven and earth as my cradle. Everything is enlightening."

"I often use it to read Confucian classics; each time benefits me, providing new perspectives."

"This is a powerful learning tool!"

Kong Ran shook his head and said, "Actually, what my family has documented is not just this innate technique. There are other methods centered around developing Early Wisdom Talent."

After laying such groundwork, Kong Ran revealed his intention: "If you can sell the Ideograph Brush to me, I’ll agree to exchange Myriad Phenomena Cradle with you."

"Of course, we can discuss the specific transaction price in detail!"

Ning Zhuo did not hesitate, immediately shaking his head: "The Ideograph Brush will not be sold."

"Mr. Chu Xuangu has high expectations for me; how could I let him down?"

"Absolutely not."

On one side, Liu Fushu chimed in: "I say, Daoist Fellow Ning Zhuo, you’ve already cultivated other magic techniques, unless you’re dual practicing."

"Otherwise, you won’t possess Literary Heart, Literary Palace, or Literary Energy. Without these, you’re not truly a Confucian Cultivator."

"As for Mr. Chu Xuangu’s expectations of you, I privately believe it’s just the gentleman’s experimentation. You needn’t be so serious."

Kong Ran felt grateful when Liu Fushu spoke up for him.

Ning Zhuo let out a long sigh.

Often, he pities why cultivators cannot benefit from practicing multiple cultivation techniques? Like him, who only practices three, is an exception among exceptions.

Ning Zhuo is eager to learn, genuinely wanting to experience what it’s like to become a Confucian Cultivator.

In fact, he wanted to study the "Myriad Phenomena Scripture."

However, unfortunately, his three dantians already have cultivation techniques. In White Paper Immortal City, he could only let Sun Lingtong practice on his behalf.

Returning to his senses, Ning Zhuo saw Kong Ran looking at him expectantly once more; he shook his head again.

Liu Fushu and Kong Ran were unaware of the inner situation.

Currently, the Confucian Cultivator group of the Myriad Images Sect, entrusted by Wen Ruanyu and based on their own interests, is committed to supporting Ning Zhuo fully.

Chu Xuangu had already accomplished his task, at least during his Feiyun trial, he accomplished it.

Under such circumstances, if Ning Zhuo drops the ball himself, how will he explain to the Confucian Cultivator group? How to explain to Wen Ruanyu?

Though Ning Zhuo didn’t secretly communicate via Divine Sense Transmission with Chu Xuangu the entire time, he personally felt Chu Xuangu’s earnest intention. In response, Ning Zhuo should also display full sincerity.
