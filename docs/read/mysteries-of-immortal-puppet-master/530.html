Chapter 530: Chapter 122: Red Heart Arrow_3

Shuangjing transmitted his Divine Sense thoughts, boosting morale.

Morale indeed got boosted by him somewhat.

Golden Core Deputy Generals, following Shuangjing’s train of thoughts, also felt fortunate one by one.

“Go take out the military equipment from the Storage Bag, replenish it, lure Old Monster in Stone to continue stealing, and buy us time!” Shuangjing resolutely ordered.

How insignificant money seems compared to life and death!

Without life, what use is all the wealth?!

Shuangjing knew this move was wise, but the emotions of sorrow and hatred were still hard to dispel, accumulating more and more.

White Tiger Sky-tearing Sword Qi!

With the amplification of Military Power, the sharp sword qi once again inflicted a wound on Lord Long’s back.

Blood streamed out of the wound!

Although Lord Long had a Spiritual Treasure protecting him, it was only his right shoulder pad, and the defense was not comprehensive, strongest at the right shoulder pad.

As the battle prolonged, White Jade Camp suffered hundreds of casualties, which allowed Shuangjing to clearly understand Lord Long’s situation.

Lord Long stepped back, attempting to distance himself to facilitate healing.

But he was still within the Battle Formation, surrounded by enemies on all sides.

In the next moment, Golden Core Deputy Generals swarmed in, with dozens of punches and kicks, making Lord Long stagger.

“Old Monster in Stone, stop stealing treasures!”

“Defeat them, and I promise you half of these spoils of war are yours!”

Lord Long transmitted his Divine Sense thoughts, full of threats: “Have you forgotten, you are blessed with Divine Decree!”

Lord Long thought, Old Monster in Stone had never unleashed his full power.

But for Sun Ning and his companion, they were more than giving their all? Squeezing themselves time after time, when exhausted to death, they also reminisce about the horror of poverty, using it to whip themselves to break through limitations.

The same was true for the Stone Ball.

The Stone Ball itself could not move, and just rolling repeatedly was already beyond its limits.

Thus, Divine Decree for the three, had already satisfied the terms, no longer a constraint.

Lord Long called dozens of times, getting no response from Old Monster in Stone, he was furious and shouted from outside the formation: “Chi Tengyi, what are you dawdling for? Hurry and help me!”

Nascent Soul Level Cultivator of the Ancient Vine Tribe, Chi Tengyi, and her husband, Golden Core Cultivator Cang Vine, had both already joined the battle.

But they and several other Golden Core Cultivators were intercepted by Red Flower Camp, trapped within the Red Flower Dharma Image.

If Red Flower Dharma Image can trap Ma Feitui, naturally, it can trap these cultivators as well.

Chi Tengyi is not a Demon Cultivator, not physically strong, and cannot break through with brute force.

However, she and the other Golden Core Cultivators together continued casting spells, their erosion of the Dharma Image far exceeded that of Ma Feitui.

Military Art—Reward the Three Armies!

Mu Lan’s face was pale as she held the military order, continually maintaining this Military Art.

The efficacy of the Military Art was simple and straightforward, consume Spirit Stones to increase Military Power!

“This can’t go on!”

“Our military expenses are already tight, this battle has greatly depleted them, and if it continues for a while longer, even next month’s military salary will be hard to dispense.”

Wars are like this, once it reaches a stalemate phase, it’s a terrifying depletion of resources.

Determination flashed in Mu Lan’s eyes.

She drew a long bow, in the span of a breath, she readied her bow with an arrow.

A Magic Treasure Level weapon, known as Soldier Treasure—Red Phoenix Bow, Fiery Heart Arrow!

Utilizing her Innate talents to transform into Divine Skills—Red Heart Arrow!

Military Power, Battle Formation, National Power perfectly supported!

Mu Lan released her hand.

The bowstring snapped, sounding like a phoenix cry!

An arrow shot out, the sky shook, and the heavens split.

The arrowlight was dazzling, like a giant pillar of the sun, shooting into the heavens, light as brilliant as a rainbow, making all pale in comparison!

When Lord Long reacted, it was already too late.

He looked down at his chest, where the flesh was charred black, a hollow spot, his heart incinerated in an instant.

For a moment, the entire field fell silent.

Mu Lan slumped, her hands powerless, the long bow fell to the ground, her body staggered, nearly collapsing, fortunately, Zhang Zhongyi supported her just in time.

“My wise niece, no, General Mu… Divine Power!” Zhang Zhongyi was extremely excited.

He never expected that Mu Lan, merely at the Golden Core Level, would develop her Innate talents into Divine Skills.

Red Heart Arrow!

Using almost all her vitality as the guide, a desperate must-hit arrow!
