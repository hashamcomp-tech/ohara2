Chapter 878: Chapter 303: Colossus Ning Zhuo

"I feel myself brimming with power!"

Ning Zhuo was filled with excitement.

This was the first time in his life he was officially controlling the Paperman Giant Statue.

Because of a prior deal with the White Paper Immortal City Lord, this Paperman Giant Statue had become Ning Zhuo’s property. After the deal, the White Paper City Lord taught him the specific method of control.

Afterward, Ning Zhuo secretly practiced a few times, but it was always done discreetly, never diving too deeply.

After the crisis erupted at the South City Gate, Ning Zhuo immediately rushed over. Instead of joining the battle first, he dove into the Paperman Giant Statue and activated it.

Upon activation, Ning Zhuo felt himself inflate like a balloon, turning into a giant within breaths, and then donned a layer of paper clothing.

Initially, he could still feel the difference between the paper clothing and himself.

But soon, this sense of difference vanished without a trace, and he completely merged with the Paperman Giant Statue.

Ning Zhuo’s spirit elevated drastically; he could no longer feel his soul or body, only the Paperman Giant Statue.

From the outside, after the Paperman Giant Statue kicked away the Golden Core Ghost General, it began to change rapidly.

It turned ghostly with a fierce appearance, its white paper body exuding Yin Qi. The Yin Qi, like ink, transformed from void to real, turning into a layer of black iron armor, arming the Paperman Giant Statue.

Ning Zhuo kicked away the Golden Core Ghost General as easily as a person kicking away a mouse.

He pressed the attack, striding forward in big steps, then lifted his foot for a fierce stomp.

Bang.

His terrifying foot power created a deep footprint in the tiles of the White Paper Immortal City.

The stirring airwaves, like a gust of strong wind, blew away the Scarlet Ghost General.

The Scarlet Ghost General, chilled to the core, looked at the deep footprint: "If I were hit by that, I would certainly be gravely injured and on the brink of death!"

Yang Sanyan’s Divine Sense reached him, saying to Ning Zhuo, "Ning Zhuo! Don’t blindly pursue them; these Golden Core Ghost Generals are mine to handle! You should deal with the big one!"

"Alright." Ning Zhuo agreed straightforwardly.

That flying kick earlier, though it hit the Long Axe Ghost General, was unexpected, catching the opponent off guard.

When it comes to actual attack, the Paperman Giant Statue’s power is astonishing, but its speed is relatively slow, making each move easy for others to anticipate.

Thus, the subsequent stomp allowed the Scarlet Ghost General to easily evade.

"Dealing with these little things is indeed inconvenient."

"Regrettably, the Paperman Giant Statue doesn’t really possess such methods."

Having experienced the battle of Liangzhu Country, Ning Zhuo naturally possessed sufficient combat prowess.

He understood that Yang Sanyan’s words were spot-on, so he decisively abandoned the pursuit of those few Ghost Generals and strode toward the Death Bone Giant Spirit.

Boom boom boom...

The giant Ning Zhuo sprinted, and every footfall on the tiles left deep footprints, causing the ground to shake.

Bang!

With a resounding crash, Ning Zhuo directly collided with the Death Bone Giant Spirit, tackling it to the ground.

Underneath the massive giants, buildings were instantly crushed and flattened.

Tie Guzheng watched with twitching eyelids, quickly sending a Divine Sense message: "Don’t fight here, move away from the city gate!"

The giant Ning Zhuo stood up first, and the Death Bone Giant Spirit quickly followed.

The latter swung its arm, punching from left to right, aiming for Ning Zhuo’s head.

The giant Ning Zhuo dodged the counterattack with a low stance, grabbed the Death Bone Giant Spirit, and executed an over-the-shoulder throw.

Boom.

The Death Bone Giant Spirit slammed heavily into the ground, causing a cloud of dust, and another dozen or so buildings collapsed, paper scraps flying everywhere.

Ning Zhuo was about to press the attack when he suddenly heard someone shout, "Watch out!"

A gust of wind rushed from behind his head.

Ning Zhuo instinctively ducked, rolling to evade the heavy blow from behind.

This was the second Death Bone Giant Spirit!

Its fist, like a siege ram, missed and smashed heavily into a three-story paper building that had yet to fully collapse!

Boom!

The paper building, as if lit by a fuse, deformed and expanded violently, then exploded into numerous fragments, pieces of paper.

These debris flew out like a meteor shower, crashing into low residential houses in the distance, causing secondary damage.

Ning Zhuo rolled on the ground for a distance, crouched, and turned back to look.

Seeing the two Death Bone Giant Spirits standing together, his heart sank, "How do I fight this?"

His gaze subconsciously shifted, focusing on the Ghost Cultivator in another direction.

It was Sang Le Youling.

Sang Le Youling was still maintaining her Path of Silence Candle.

The candle was burning fiercely, already consumed by two-tenths.

"I must take her down!"

"Otherwise, the Death Bone Giants will only increase in number!!"

Ning Zhuo swept his gaze and saw that Yang Sanyan and the Bone Tomb Taoist were still locked in fierce combat with the Golden Core Ghost Generals, unable to break through for the moment.

Ning Zhuo decided to take action himself.

But just as he got up, he was surrounded by the two Death Bone Giant Spirits.

Boom boom boom...

The three massive creatures engaged in fierce, close-quarters combat.

"Having a bigger body is also inconvenient!"

Lacking this kind of combat experience, Ning Zhuo was entangled by the two Death Bone Giant Spirits.

Turning, punching, retreating, dodging sideways, using force against force... Even though he was one against two, Ning Zhuo quickly grasped the situation, gaining the upper hand.

The Death Bone Giant Spirits, while powerful and heavily defended, lacked much intelligence and even basic Martial Arts, giving Ning Zhuo several advantages in close combat.

But the good situation was short-lived, as the arrival of a third Death Bone Giant Spirit directly shifted the dynamics.

The giant Ning Zhuo was pushed back step by step.

He retreated into a cluster of buildings, every step crushing a residential house.

"Time to deploy Martial Arts!"

The Paperman Giant Statue had some methods built-in.
