Chapter 526

Chapter 526 -120: “Old Monster in Stone” Makes a Move!_3

But the attack from the Qi Soldier just now exceeded Ning Zhuo’s reaction limit; logically, he should not have had even the time to activate the Targeted Teleportation Talisman with his Divine Sense.

“Thank goodness for the Mechanical Ring.”

Ning Zhuo’s complexion was pale, filled with emotions of having just had a narrow escape.

It turned out that before the battle began, anticipating the myriad crises on the battlefield, he had proactively established mechanisms for defense and escape.

Linking the Mechanical Ring, endowed with the power of Piercing Heart Insight, to the Targeted Teleportation Talisman, he created a simple Formation. As soon as the Spiritual Nature within the ring perceived a threat to Ning Zhuo’s life, it would trigger the Talisman.

It was this mechanism that saved Ning Zhuo’s life.

“It was too dangerous!”

“This is what it means to be on the battlefield.”

“One moment we were pursuing our victory, the next a powerful enemy seized our flaw, nearly leading to my death.”

Ning Zhuo wiped his broad forehead.

His forehead was covered in fine, cold sweat.

“Little Zhuo, you’re finally here!” Sun Lingtong noticed the commotion and hurried over.

He was unaware of Ning Zhuo’s brush with death, his face full of excitement: “I can’t believe I waited this long for you to arrive!”

“What shall we do now?”

Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong were burdened with a Divine Decree, required to act against the armies of Liangzhu Country to satisfy the requirements set forth by the Decree in order to survive this calamity.

Subsequently, after discussing, Sun Ning had Sun Lingtong take the Mechanical Dragon far from the troops, waiting for an opportune moment.

Ning Zhuo immediately used his Divine Sense to inform Sun Lingtong of the latest situation on the battlefield.

Sun Lingtong clenched his teeth: “Mu Lan is so unscrupulous, shall we target them this time?”

Ning Zhuo shook his head: “What benefit is there in attacking Red Flower Camp? Their bones are still very hard!”

He had long since set his sights on a target: “We go directly to White Jade Camp. Those offspring of noble families are the richest.”

Although displeased with Mu Lan, Ning Zhuo remained calm, not allowing personal emotions to influence him.

He continued: “After participating in the battle for a while, I’ve confirmed from opponents such as Chen Lingfeng that the terms of the Divine Decree are not excessively strict.”

“This time, by playing the role of Old Monster in Stone and looting the military supplies and weapons of White Jade Camp, we should be able to meet the demands of the Decree.”

Sun Lingtong, already eager, rubbed his hands together: “I’ll move out the Stone Ball, Little Zhuo, you should quickly recover some strength, we must set off soon! If we’re too late, we might miss the chance!”

Ning Zhuo’s condition wasn’t bad.

He swallowed some Elixir Pills, allowing the medicinal energy to aid his recovery, and then entered the Dragon Head Cabin.

Sun Lingtong had already moved the Stone Ball out, allowing the Mechanical Dragon to hide within the Stone Ball.

Ning Zhuo opened his mouth and spat out the Earth Element Spleen Temple, causing it to combine with the Stone Ball.

The three of them revived their old strategy, “Old Monster in Stone” reentered the scene.

The power of the Divine Decree was activated in an instant, feeling like a rope tightening around the necks of Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong.

With “Old Monster in Stone” completed, the true constraints of the Divine Decree were finally revealed!

“Hurry, we’re going to hit White Jade Camp!” Sun Lingtong was itching to get started.

The Stone Ball sank into the ground, advancing rapidly.

With a thought, Ning Zhuo cast the Earth Element Technique, significantly increasing the speed of the Stone Ball and ensuring its secrecy.

White Jade Camp battlefield.

Shuangjing was struggling to hold on, using Military Power to resist Lord Long. Deputy Generals were directing soldiers, sometimes assisting Shuangjing, sometimes defending against the attacks of other Golden Core Cultivators.

White Jade Camp suffered heavy casualties, its warriors enduring the agony of attacks from both inside and outside.

Had it not been for Mu Lan leading Red Flower Camp, arriving in time to alleviate much of the external pressure on White Jade Camp, it would have already collapsed.

A Stone Ball suddenly emerged from the ground next to a Mana Pool.

Swoosh.

The Mana Pool was swallowed by the Stone Ball, vanishing from sight.

“What, what is that?!” The soldiers guarding the Mana Pool from White Jade Camp were dumbfounded.

“Attack!” someone shouted, and the surrounding people, as if waking from a dream, lashed out.

But with the main military forces of White Jade Camp concentrated on dealing with Lord Long, the attack from these soldiers was like a mere tickle to “Old Monster in Stone.”

The “Old Monster in Stone” did not linger for the fight. In the next moment, it burrowed into the ground and fled!
