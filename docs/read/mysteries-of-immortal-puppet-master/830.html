Chapter 830: Chapter 280: Ning Zhuo’s Scalp Tingling_3

The crowd burst into laughter again.

Qing Chi’s puffed-up appearance belied a hidden gratitude.

To rally the military spirit, it must be announced publicly and accepted by others to be carried out. At the same time, its political significance is extraordinary, equivalent to confirming Qing Chi as the next successor.

No one opposed.

Qing Yan’s prestige and deep control over kinship were evident.

Of course, the most crucial aspect is their close-knit relationship, their strong blood ties, making them truly one family.

One family speaks easily among themselves.

Ning Zhuo walked out of the Complete Book Pavilion.

White Paper Immortal City is the largest cultivator stronghold in the Yin Tide Black Swamp Wetland, where the Myriad Images Sect, Soul Devouring Sect, and other super forces have stations, and the Complete Book Pavilion naturally has one as well.

"Distinguished guest, walk safely," the Tower Master personally escorted Ning Zhuo out.

This time, Ning Zhuo entered the Complete Book Pavilion mainly to acquire a military art called the Spirit Bearing Demonic Gathering Technique, spending a considerable sum.

The market price for military arts is often higher than other spells.

On one hand, when military arts are practically applied, they often display strong power that many magic techniques cannot achieve. On the other hand, those who choose military arts are often from big and powerful families, people with authority. Those without power or position have no interest in military arts.

Ning Zhuo did not return to the Yang Enhancement Institute. He had learned that Qing Chi often visited him, which annoyed him greatly.

Ning Zhuo has been hiding in the Paperman Giant Statue for cultivation recently.

On one hand, he manages the Qianmo Lamp, allowing him to contact Sun Lingtong at any time. On the other hand, the Paperman Giant Statue was left by his mother, giving him an inner sense of peace here.

But this time, an unexpected incident occurred.

Just as Ning Zhuo was about to step into the Paperman Giant Statue, Qing Chi suddenly appeared.

She excitedly said, "Ha ha, Little Ma, so you’re here! I finally caught you!"

Ning Zhuo frowned instantly, his expression indifferent: "Miss Qing, I’ve already explained in the Flying Message that my identity has changed, and it’s over between us."

Li Xiangshang stood behind Qing Chi: "Young man of the Human Clan, do you know how much effort Miss Qing Chi has put in to see you!"

Ning Zhuo snorted coldly: "You seem quite skilled at meddling."

He knew of Li Xiangshang’s existence, as such information was easy to gather.

"You can leave now, do not disturb my cultivation," Ning Zhuo said, waving his sleeve to dismiss them.

Qing Chi proudly laughed: "Little Ma, look at this!"

She revealed a token with the White Paper City Lord’s seal.

Ning Zhuo frowned, appearing puzzled.

Qing Chi said, "I have a passage token, I can come and go as I please, you can’t control me!"

Ning Zhuo: "How could that be?"

Qing Chi stood tall, urging the formation of military spirit: "You are now gathering military spirit! Do you see?"

Li Xiangshang added: "We have reported to City Lord and received her approval and appointment. Now, Miss Qing Chi is already the Deputy Commander of the City Guard Army."

Ning Zhuo stared intently at Qing Chi, as if trying to penetrate the ghost clan girl before him.

He suddenly spoke: "The first step in building an army is to gather military spirit. That can... be quite challenging."

Qing Chi wiped her nose: "To me, it’s a piece of cake."

She clenched her fist again, declaring to Ning Zhuo: "Little Ma, I am now highly valued by the City Lord! Watch, I will take you back sooner or later!"

Ning Zhuo: ...

He thought to himself: "So, this is how it is?"

When he recalled the spell he had just bought, he couldn’t help but feel chills.

Seeing him silent, Qing Chi thought he was stunned by her, and couldn’t help feeling complacent: "Little Ma, I came here to see you only to tell you this."

"I don’t want to disturb your cultivation, I’m glad you didn’t participate in that Spirit Contract Treasure Refining Array!"

"I’m leaving, take care in your cultivation."

"Wait," Ning Zhuo suddenly interrupted, "You must gather military spirit, not necessarily on the battlefield. There are many remnants of military sinister energy here, it was a place of fierce battles in the past."

"I have a copy of ’Spirit Bearing Demonic Gathering Technique,’ which can help you collect military sinister energy, allowing you to gather military spirit even off the battlefield!"

Li Xiangshang exclaimed: "’Spirit Bearing Demonic Gathering Technique’? This spell is very rare, it’s fantastic. Now, Miss Qing, your speed in gathering military spirit should triple at least!"

Qing Chi deeply gazed at Ning Zhuo, almost shedding tears: "Little Ma, I knew you hadn’t forgotten me!"

Now, Ning Zhuo’s skin was feeling even more prickly.
