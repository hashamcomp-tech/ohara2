Chapter 1156: Chapter 437: The Righteous Path’s Eloquence (Part 3)

While making his case, Ning Zhuo successfully highlighted the dominance of Ban Ji and others, creating more external pressure.

"Compared to Ban Ji, I prefer Ning Zhuo more!"

"Hmph, although the Ban Family is of the Righteous Path, Ban Ji is a disgrace from the Demon Path. The Ban Family cherishes his talent, but overlooks character, which is wrong."

"A person who cheats is especially hateful!"

"It’s not simple for Ning Zhuo to gain the recognition of the Confucian Cultivators. Key to this, is that he passed The Three Questions of the Heart in the Evil Slaying Hall! Let’s help him together!"

Under The Three Questions of the Heart in the Evil Slaying Hall, Ning Zhuo’s outstanding performance was a heavy weight, tipping the inner balance of everyone.

Zhang Wangxing and others were shocked.

They were surrounding Ning Zhuo moments ago, just seeing hope, when the next moment, they themselves were surrounded.

A gleam flashed in Ning Zhuo’s eyes: "The bronze sword broke, but it was a turning point, triggering insecurity in everyone’s hearts. Therefore, they have risen to attack!"

Ning Zhuo seized the momentum to counter-attack, shouting, "Zhang Wangxing! You despicable people, colluding in advance, openly cheating, leaning on the Ban Family, bullying us Loose Cultivators!"

"Doing such things of the Evil Path shall receive punishment!"

"Do you think this is outside?"

"No, this is Myriad Images Sect, Xuan Armor Cave, this is the land of the Righteous Path!"

"Fellow Daoists, let them experience the might of our Righteous Path!!"

Ning Zhuo’s shout received a resounding response from the crowd.

Various bronze weapons surged wildly, forcing Zhang Wangxing and others to huddle miserably, with injuries mounting and perils surrounding.

Zhang Wangxing yelled, "You listen to Ning Zhuo so much, are you all his lapdogs?!"

Ning Zhuo immediately responded, "Heavenly Dao above, justice is evident, you despicable demons are hired by the Ban Family, openly cheating, and still trying to sow discord?!"

Zhang Wangxing yelled again, "We have nothing to do with the Ban Family, slandering us without evidence, are you still of the Righteous Path?"

Ning Zhuo laughed loudly, "Shameless thief, still trying to deny and argue, take this!"

He saw Zhang Wangxing was a focal point, and immediately sent out his own Mysterious Armor.

In mid-air, Green Flowing Copper transformed into a small leg, following a flaw, and the sole directly collided with Zhang Wangxing’s face.

Mysterious Armor——Old Cold Leg!

Zhang Wangxing’s face caved in, and simultaneously a chilling coldness seeped, eroding his flesh, making his flaws bigger.

The next moment, numerous bronze weapons shot down, piercing him, leaving countless holes in his flesh.

Zhang Wangxing screamed in pain, no longer able to support himself, his injuries accumulated to the limit, and was transported away by the Great Array.

Without his support, the remaining cultivators were also severely injured and eliminated.

Ning Zhuo did not relax at all, taking advantage of the momentum, rushed towards Ban Ji, shouting, "Fellow Daoists, eradicate evil thoroughly! Together, take down the true culprit behind the scenes!"

Ban Ji was just a step away from defeating his opponent, about to seize the bronze sword, when he heard Ning Zhuo’s shout, turned around to see the latter leading a vast team of nearly ten men charging at him!

Ban Ji’s expression froze: "..."

At the edge of the cliff, smoke curled, clouds swirled.

Ban Jie had already brewed the tea, pouring it out, only holding an empty cup under her nose, gently sniffing the fresh tea aroma.

She thought to herself, "At this moment, Ning Zhuo should be besieged close to elimination, right?"

"Although his background is mysterious and strong, he is ultimately alone and weak."

"Ban Ji should be able to seize the opportunity, defeat others, capture the Mysterious Armor, and gather swords and blades."

At this moment, a subordinate rushed over, delivering the latest intelligence in haste.

Ban Jie saw her subordinate’s anxious expression, her heart sank, and she slightly changed color upon hearing the intelligence.

The scenario she imagined appeared, but then disappeared.

Now instead, it’s Ban Ji and the Rust Disaster Craftsman who are being besieged, in quite a dangerous situation!
