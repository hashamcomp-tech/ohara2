Chapter 1197: Chapter 457: Dedication to Conscience

Ning Zhuo sat cross-legged on the cushion, with various thoughts clashing intensely in his Divine Sea.

The mechanical structure diagram of Snow Core Control·Xie was completely embedded within his mind.

This was originally his design.

The refinement of Artifact Refining and Mechanical Techniques, coupled with Ling Mo’s lifetime of experience and memory, had greatly amplified Ning Zhuo’s depth of knowledge.

Regarding the design of Snow Core Control·Xie, he now had a better idea!

"When I previously designed this Mechanical Puppet, the main idea was centered around the low-level talent of [Snow Soul Frost Spirit], striving for the Mechanical Body to utilize this talent as much as possible."

"But at that time, I was constrained by my limitations in the Ice Attribute, Artifact Refining, and Mechanical Techniques, making this Mechanical Combat Puppet achieve Golden Core level combat power, yet it was not an excellent design."

"The [Eternal Silence Profound Ice Body Casting Demon Technique] will be my second core in this design!"

Once the design tone was set, new primary materials naturally appeared in Ning Zhuo’s view: Eternal Silence Mystic Ice!

This is a unique ice crystal from Frost Roar Valley, whose quality ranges from Qi Refining level all the way to Integration level! Moreover, the cultivation world generally believes that the deepest parts of Frost Roar Valley likely contain Mahayana level Eternal Silence Mystic Ice!!

Above Mahayana is Cross Tribulation and Become Immortal, which is the ultimate power of the cultivation world.

This is the face of the world’s wonder.

Every world wonder is the top resource site on the earth, the biggest cultivation holy land, but at the same time, it is also a death trap.

Massive cultivators often gather around each world wonder to conduct mining operations.

The vast majority of them, for their entire lives, can only collect from the outskirts of the world wonder. And many suffer countless diseases due to prolonged exposure to the invasion of principles in their bodies.

"I hope the Commerce Hall sells Golden Core level Eternal Silence Mystic Ice." Ning Zhuo estimated the possibility was not small.

North Wind Country and Flying Cloud Country are nearly adjacent, with the buffer zone dominated by the Yin Tide Black Swamp of White Paper Immortal City.

After Wen Ruanyu reported for duty, the entire Myriad Images Sect’s upper echelon was alarmed and wanted to seize White Paper Immortal City. Apart from the soul path resources inherent to White Paper Immortal City, there’s also huge strategic geographical value.

The cleverest part is that Flying Cloud Country cannot intervene in this competition.

As soon as the Cultivation Kingdom makes a move, it touches sensitive nerves and will inevitably trigger countermeasures from North Wind Country. A slight miscalculation, escalating the tension, leads to clashes between the two countries.

Myriad Images Sect, as a superpower, is able to intervene above the baseline. Of course, it must have legitimate reasons, and the intensity must be strictly controlled.

"Perhaps I can also try Nascent Soul Level Eternal Silence Mystic Ice?"

After selecting the primary material, Ning Zhuo strides forward along this line of thought.

"Modify Eternal Silence Mystic Ice, making the new primary material the base, to carry the spiritual nature of the innate [Snow Soul Frost Spirit]."

"[Snow Soul Frost Spirit] can create ice; with the base, it can’t create Eternal Silence Mystic Ice, but can it supplement?"

"This is a truly feasible plan!"

Ning Zhuo did not use Luoshu for calculation, relying solely on his intuition, he felt it would work.

He believed in his intuition.

This feeling was strong; it was his body’s response after withstanding the related principles of Artifact Refining.

Ning Zhuo’s thoughts flowed smoothly and began to unfold.

"The framework must possess both strength and spiritual conductivity... Ten Thousand Years Ice Heart Iron for the bone, Mystic Ice Marrow for the marrow, roughly in this direction. The specific choice among the similar materials needs testing."

"The technique to modify the body in [Eternal Silence Profound Ice Body Casting Demon Technique] offers significant reference value."

"Moreover, I can refer to Demon-infused Bloodline Technique! How about manufacturing Ice Magic Power’s blood vessels?"

"Thus, not only can it conduct Mana, support the frame, but the structure of blood vessels itself can exert force and retract."

"Good, that’s a great idea."

Ning Zhuo cheered for himself internally, his heart stirred with joy.

He continued to think deeply.

Countless thoughts intertwined like waters, flowing unrestrained through his Divine Sea.

He retained some of Snow Core Control·Xie’s old structures, such as Hexagonal Mysterious Ice Armor, and expanded upon them greatly.

As a result, the main materials for the new Snow Core Control·Xie were completely changed, the body construction method was significantly improved, and the combat style changed accordingly.

"The characteristic of Eternal Silence Mystic Ice is to absorb sound, accumulate continually, and upon reaching the limit, it erupts violently, forming a terrifying sound wave cold tide."

"The Hexagonal Mysterious Ice Armor and the new ice-sculpted body will possess such characteristics — absorbing attacks, accumulating strength, breaking limits to erupt, forming sound wave cold tides!"

"After the Mystic Ice Armor and ice-sculpted body are consumed, use the innate [Snow Soul Frost Spirit] to create new Mystic Ice, supplementing themselves."

"Wonderful, wonderful."

This design combines "destruction" and "rebirth."

Every fierce attack by enemies accumulates power for an even more terrifying future eruption!

He imagines seeing, on future battlegrounds, how the new Snow Core Control·Xie breaks again and again under enemy attacks, and how it continuously rises from the ashes of cold tides, becoming the nightmare all enemies cannot dispel.

Enemies will find themselves in an awkward situation: Attack Snow Core Control·Xie, and it will continue building power. Don’t attack, and he will strike them.

"Haha, this problem is for the enemies to solve."

Ning Zhuo continued his research.
