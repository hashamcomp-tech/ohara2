Chapter 937: Chapter 332: Withering and Flourishing, Shared Sorrow

There was a moment when Ning Zhuo had the idea to use Life Hanging Thread. He wanted to forcibly control Qing Chi, making her avoid the final battleground.

But immediately, he sighed softly, with a complex gaze, waiting quietly for Qing Chi to join him.

On one hand, he particularly understood Qing Chi. Ning Zhuo could endure for his mother in Fire Persimmon Immortal City for over ten years, how could he not understand Qing Chi’s determination for revenge for her father?

On the other hand, Ning Zhuo felt a deep sense of guilt towards Qing Chi. After all, he had disguised himself as Jiao Ma, deceiving this Ghost Clan girl for a long time, though it was with good intentions, it indeed involved a lot of manipulation.

As for Qing Yan’s death...

Is there any war without deaths?

"My journey is one of righteous cause. It’s for the survival and prosperity of countless beings in White Paper Immortal City and Yin Tide Black Swamp Wetland!"

"The Qing Family was originally part of White Paper Immortal City’s faction, similar to the Jiao Family, voluntarily betting everything on White Paper Immortal City, being the most steadfast city protection group."

"The evolution of fate allowed the Qing Family to form an army naturally, which is already a great gift."

"Having gained such benefits, the Qing Jiao Army is supposed to play a role here!"

This is not child’s play.

Which of Ning Zhuo, Sun Lingtong, Luo Si is not risking their lives? Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin and Grey Bone Elder have spent extensive time and energy, laboriously planning and plotting.

Honestly, having participated to this extent, it’s already a huge favor that the Qing Jiao Army has suffered only minor casualties.

This is the result of Ning Zhuo, Luo Si, and others trying their best to protect them.

The black clouds roil endlessly, but compared to before, they are no longer tight, the cracks grow wider and more numerous, allowing the Qing Jiao Army and Qing Chi to pass through easily.

Most of the Heavenly Phase Array has already collapsed. Having passed through the initial and most dangerous moments, Ning Zhuo and Qing Chi can already face the remaining chaos calmly.

They successfully converge at high altitude and immediately follow the passage, rushing into the Altar Battlefield.

Boom boom boom...

Violent explosion sounds roar like thunder, indicating the fierce battle within.

The power of the Returning Ruins Black Ball is extraordinary, wherever it corrodes, pitch-black marks remain on the Miasma barrier. Despite the Earth-Lung Miasma’s surge, trying to replenish it, all are blocked and dispelled by the remaining black energy.

Thus, this passage remains stable for the time being.

Just reaching the middle of the passage, they saw Luo Si slowly flying in.

Ning Zhuo and Qing Chi were delighted and hurriedly greeted him.

The three successfully converged.

Luo Si was in extremely poor condition, and after seeing Ning Zhuo, he nodded slowly and closed his eyes.

Luo Si understood: The illusion of Silent Extinction Buddha rushing over was certainly Ning Zhuo’s handiwork. Ning Zhuo did not let Luo Si down.

Despite only a short time together, Luo Si has an extraordinary trust in Ning Zhuo.

He directly abandoned the outside world, entrusting his life and death to Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo quickly supported him, lifting him to fly together.

Luo Si was severely corroded by the Earth-Lung Miasma, his injuries terrifying, his Nascent Soul lethargic and dim.

Thankfully, he operated his Cultivation Technique with all his strength, beginning to generate new Mana.

Mana began to spread rapidly, reclaiming the lost ground within, starting to expel the Earth-Lung Miasma step by step from the body.

Seeing this, Ning Zhuo and Qing Chi discussed briefly and didn’t rush in. They first took Luo Si out of the passage, returning to the outside world.

The fight inside was fierce, Ning Zhuo and Qing Chi were just at Foundation Establishment, so it’s better to observe for a while.

The illusion of Silent Extinction Buddha sat cross-legged, with Buddha Light radiating all around, penetrating all Miasma.

Taking advantage of the Miasma cover, the Earth Evil God charged in, roaring loudly and swinging its fists with all its might.

The sound of fists like thunder, the force like stars!

The illusion of Silent Extinction Buddha then transformed into Buddha Palm Seals, pressing softly, overcoming hardness with softness, dispelling the force and Miasma invisibly, returning to silence.

Poof.

The Earth Evil God suddenly deflated, a crack appeared on its Divine Body, spewing Earth-Lung Miasma, causing its divine form to tremble and its breath to wilt.

The Earth Evil God suddenly felt weakened and could only retreat. Its head had a hole, spitting endless black smoke.

The black smoke was so dense it formed a sticky black rain, emitting a stench and the aura of decay, enveloping the illusion of Silent Extinction Buddha.

Buddha Light and black rain countered each other as the illusion continued forward.

Lord of Wangchuan’s main body showed a tense expression, urgency in his heart, subconsciously looking toward Wangchuan Immortal City: "Has the message not been delivered yet? It’s time!"

Wangchuan Immortal City.

Zeng Jide used Stealing Technique and stole another Earth Lung Tuberculosis, suddenly an anomaly occurred.

The massive stone in front suddenly shook violently, emitting a mighty yellow light. For a moment, the surrounding gravity doubled, almost causing an old and young Bukong Sect member to kneel.

"What’s happening?!" Sun Lingtong was startled and uncertain.

Zeng Jide then discovered that within the residual Earth Lung Tuberculosis in the stone, one had suddenly disintegrated, transforming into an incredibly pure and vast Earth Element torrent, disappearing from the original place along the Array, and being teleported out.

This Earth Element torrent encompassed mighty Earth Element Power, vast Earth-Lung Miasma, and surging Earth Evil power...

Although two-tenths were consumed in teleportation, the remaining eight-tenths were rapidly infused onto the Earth Evil God.

"Roar——!!"

The Earth Evil God seemed to be injected with strong medicine, it roared to the sky!

Every wound on its body quickly healed and even reshaped under the infusion of the Earth torrent. The speed was visible to the naked eye.

Its previously wilting breath surged like a volcanic eruption, rapidly reaching a certain peak.

With a violent momentum, it intercepted the path of the Buddha illusion again.
