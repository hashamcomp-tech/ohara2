Chapter 719 -224: Full Army Assault_2

“The Old Monster in Stone has been discovered!”

“He has finally appeared!”

“Is this true?”

“Bi Tengyi is absolutely certain!”

“Then proceed as planned!!”

The reaction at the Dragon King Temple was extremely swift.

Because they had long imagined scenarios involving the Old Monster in Stone, and had set up numerous contingency plans. Some of them had even rehearsed these plans.

They couldn’t afford to ignore it.

In their eyes, the Old Monster in Stone was at the Divinity Transformation Level, a crucial figure affecting the overall situation of the Thousand Peak Forest.

During the Old Monster in Stone’s disappearance, they had searched frantically but found nothing.

Now, the Old Monster in Stone had finally surfaced!

In all the plans, the first thing that the Dragon King Temple always did was to offer incense and report to the Ginseng Whisker Dragon King.

The Dragon King’s statue received the message, its eyes immediately glazed with brilliance, coming alive from its dead state!

Moments later, Lord Long received the latest orders.

“The Old Monster in Stone?!” He suddenly stood up from his seat.

Upon learning of the Old Monster in Stone’s emergence, the Ginseng Whisker Dragon King immediately mobilized!

Lord Long also received the latest command—to launch a full-scale attack on the Liangzhu Country’s camp without any delay.

“Everyone, heed my order, full attack on the enemy!!” Lord Long roared, his voice echoing across the battlefield.

Zhao Xi: ?!!

Liangzhu Country’s army: ??!

The battle was raging, and the Thousand Peak Forest’s side was beginning to gain the upper hand. How could they suddenly launch a full-scale attack?

This was very strange!

Not only did Liangzhu Country feel this way, but the Thousand Peak Forest Alliance was also momentarily stunned and looked towards Lord Long, unsure of what he was doing.

Lord Long, however, responded to the countless doubting eyes with a stern gaze, “Full attack, no mistakes!”

He ordered again.

“Hahaha. This is more like it!” The first to react was none other than the contemporary Elephant King, Xiang Yue.

Xiang Yue stopped laughing, abandoned Shuang Ling, and charged fiercely towards the Liangzhu Country’s fortified camp.

He was overwhelmingly powerful, breaking through the air with an incredibly fierce stance, like a cannonball piercing forward!

Zhao Xi suddenly stood up and promptly activated the array.

The Gray Moon Flying Shield Formation!

Seven hundred silver shields ascended into the sky, their surfaces flowing with moonlight, with light blades formed of Geng Metal Qi, integrated for both offense and defense!

Bang bang bang.

Xiang Yue charged headlong, smashing through a cluster of shields.

“This is the kind of battle I like. Hehehe…”

On the other side, Li Jiuju abandoned Mo Yechen and flew higher.

His lips split to the ears as he chuckled sinisterly. The Rot Immortal Poison Basket tilted, and three hundred thousand rot immortal grubs poured down like a tar stream, engulfing the sky, cascading towards the military camp!

Zhao Xi and others’ faces turned pale.

Zhao Xi reactivated the Gray Moon Flying Shield Formation, sending out a thousand silver shields that circled and combined above the camp into a complete moon disc.

The Geng Metal Qi formed into countless blade whirlwind.

The first wave of insects collided with the light blades, disintegrating with the sounds of shattered shells akin to hailstones smashing onto jade, relentlessly.

Crack, crack.

In the camp’s fort, the Array Plate of the Gray Moon Flying Shield Formation showed cracks, rapidly expanding and spreading all over its surface within breaths.

“I see it!” Among the Thousand Peak Forest Alliance, the Poison Scorpion Cave Master suddenly fired a venomous scorpion tail needle.

The needle moved silently, mingling with the insect swarm to approach successfully, then exploded instantaneously, piercing through the void, directly hitting the Array Plate.

The Array Plate shattered.

The Gray Moon Flying Shield Formation immediately became chaotic, and both Xiang Yue, who was obstructed, and the bug swarm that was intercepted hurdled again.

The shattered shields scattered into countless glittering fragments, showering the battlefield. The dozen Array Masters manning the formation died instantly from backlashes.

The Geng Metal Qi dispersed like the wind.

Thunder Net Locking Sky Formation!

In the next moment, countless thunder serpents burst from the fort walls, violet-white electric light woven into hexagonal grids.

Gu Ya’s wolf claw markings glowed green, his muscular right arm suddenly tripled in size.

“Wolf Fang Break!”

He roared, his wolf fang club engulfed in green light, fiercely smashing into the thunder net.

Boom.

In the next moment, the explosion’s smoke engulfed Gu Ya and the surroundings.

The Poison Scorpion Cave Master gently flicked her purple scarf, flying above the battlefield.

Her tail needle slowly etched a scorpion-shaped talisman in mid-air.

Following this, the five Array Masters responsible for the Thunder Net Locking Sky Formation bled from all seven orifices, and the formation’s apparatus, the Red Copper Azure Dragon Column, inclined, electric chains spasming like dying giant serpents.

Rushing towards them, Xiang Yue directly tore apart the electric net with his hands, continuing to charge like a primordial beast!

Li Jiuju’s bug swarm followed a bit slower, the electric net started to recover, only to be engulfed by the swarm, both factions fiercely counteracting.

“Activate the Bihai Soldier Tide Array!” Zhao Xi’s hairpin shattered, his long hair flying in the wind.

His face was drenched in cold sweat as he crazily pushed the formation.

Nine streams merged into dragons, rising from the surrounding moat, their roars stirring waves as high as a hundred meters.

The torrents rose to the sky, forming a water screen barrier swiftly.

Just as the barrier was about to close completely, Xiang Yue crashed through, entering the water screen ahead.

Martial Arts—Breaking Army Stance!

Xiang Yue charged headlong, deploying a military-defeating martial technique, each water dragon attempting to block him, but their force dispersed, allowing Xiang Yue to break through each one!

The delay led the bug swarm to crawl onto the water screen, which began ravenously gnawing.

“Release arrows, release arrows!” A general urgently commanded.

The city wall immediately released a torrential rain of arrows.

Employing military art, the rain of arrows was extraordinarily dense, almost entirely focusing on Xiang Yue.

Divine Skill—Vajra Unbreakable!

Xiang Yue’s body emitted golden light, his defense soaring to the maximum, enduring the arrow rain while continuing to charge fiercely.

“This didn’t stop him?!”

On the wall, the generals changed facial colors, horrified.

Xiang Yue’s ferocity exceeded their imagination.

As they anxiously prepared to face Xiang Yue’s collision with the wall, a mechanical puppet suddenly appeared before him.

Boom!

Their collision sent the mechanical puppet flying backwards.

But it quickly steadied itself, regaining its posture.

The mechanical puppet faintly resembled Shuang Ling, but its body was serpent-like flexible, shrouded in delicate clothing, sprinkled with starlight, water vapor swirling beneath the robe.

Nascent Soul Level!

Xiang Yue snorted coldly, expelling a hot breath from his nostrils, charging forth again.

Boom boom boom…

He stomped the ground, each step pressing deep footprints.

The mechanical puppet moved towards Xiang Yue, like a baby diving into an adult’s embrace.

Martial Arts—Steel Fist.

Xiang Yue’s fists turned iron-black, their durability skyrocketing, delivering fierce blows.

The mechanical puppet raised its palm, directly intercepting Xiang Yue’s steel fists.

Instantly, from the puppet’s palms emerged thousands of hanging threads.

The hanging threads enveloped the steel fists, combining martial arts’ soft palm techniques, forming a mechanical technique—Cloud Binding Hand · Thousand Threads Bind!

Xiang Yue sensed his steel fists striking a cotton-like mass, his enormous strength rapidly dissipating.

For the first time since entering the battlefield, Xiang Yue showed a hint of surprise, reassessing the puppet before him.

“Countering rigidity with flexibility! This is the Soft Bone Mysterious Concubine, surely Shuang Ling’s trump card.”

“Three thousand six hundred segmented articulations, each embedded with ‘Force Dispelling Runeworks’, freely diverting impact direction.”

“Internally filled with ‘Nine Curves Corridor’ Liquid Mercury, channeling seventy percent absorbed force elsewhere.”

“The outer robe seems ordinary but is woven from Star Silk, naturally structured to disperse force, altering external force direction and trajectory.”

“Combined, the puppet’s core should be Mermaid’s Tears.”

Ning Zhuo watched eagerly.

Though the Soft Bone Mysterious Concubine temporarily halted Xiang Yue, it heavily taxed Shuang Ling’s energy and divine sense.

To counter rigidity with flexibility, she needed to employ refined combat skills.

This strain led to the collapse of the previously maintained scene by Shuang Ling.

Nascent Soul cultivators who had been stalemated with Shuang Ling now gained freedom and quickly distanced themselves.

Shuang Ling was evidently a tough opponent; better to attack others first, achieving merits and victories, a more pragmatic approach.
