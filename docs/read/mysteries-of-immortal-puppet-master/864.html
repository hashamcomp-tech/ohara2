Chapter 864: Chapter 296: Full Force Military Building

Outside the White Paper Immortal City.

Yin Soldier Camp.

Sun Lingtong scanned the surroundings, transmitting everything she saw and heard to Ning Zhuo through the Life Hanging Thread.

Ning Zhuo "saw" a large number of Yin Soldiers using an ointment.

The Yin Soldiers opened the ointment and then evenly applied it on themselves.

When the ointment was slapped on, it often emitted the sound of beings dying miserably.

These sounds were absorbed into the Yin Soldiers’ bodies and quickly dissipated.

Sun Lingtong explained in a timely manner: "I found out that this type of elixir pill is called Death Echo Ointment."

"It’s mainly made of Netherworld Grass, Moon Shadow Dew, and Ghost Flower Mud. Now the entire Yin Soldier army regularly applies this ointment daily."

"At first, there’s no effect, but with continued application, it gradually builds up, eventually transforming the soul body with the sound of death, resulting in Heavy Armor Protection."
