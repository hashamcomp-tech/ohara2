Chapter 688 -208: Triggering the Treasure Seal_2

Ning Zhuo was easily tricked by Tian Shuang, letting out a groan of pain and instinctively trying to stop the Flame Cloak spell. But at this moment, a flash of spirit light suddenly appeared in his Divine Sea.

The spirit light was like a bolt of lightning, cutting through the sky of the Divine Sea, severing nearly a thousand Mirror Intents along its path.

Ning Zhuo invested a portion of his Divine Sense, infusing it into his heart.

The statue inside the Fire Element Heart Temple immediately became lifelike, the features vividly resembling Ning Zhuo.

With this change, Ning Zhuo immediately felt his control over the Fire-attribute spell rise to another level.

He quickly reduced the power of the flames, making them match the current cold invasion.

“Hmm?” Sun Lingtong was both surprised and pleased.

He was very clear about Ning Zhuo’s situation, knowing that although the latter’s Five Elements Realm was quite advanced, many of his spells were not well-practiced, and their practical combat effectiveness was another matter.

Especially such hastily created spells, which were highly targeted, but often lost their flexibility.

Tian Shuang had rich combat experience and sharp vision, immediately noticing Ning Zhuo’s somewhat rigid spellcasting. Thus, he used his unique method to retract his Divine Skills, easily forcing Ning Zhuo into a perilous situation again.

But he did not expect Ning Zhuo to gain control of the spell at the critical moment and adapt to the dramatic changes in the cold.

In fact, even Ning Zhuo himself did not expect it.

“In my haste, I used the existence of the Fire Element Heart Temple and was able to design this Fire-attribute spell just in time.”

“Because it was the Source Fire from the Fire Element Heart Temple, with a high degree of participation, I could indirectly adjust the spell’s power by controlling the Fire Element Heart Temple. Amazing!”

This small breakthrough in combat was like opening a new window for Ning Zhuo.

Tian Shuang quickly adjusted his Divine Skills’ power, making it fluctuate between strong and weak, while Ning Zhuo also immediately responded to the challenge, causing the flames of his Flame Cloak to alternately grow and shrink.

Tian Shuang tested it and confirmed that Ning Zhuo could withstand this layer of offensive, a strange look appeared on his face: “This kid’s situation is peculiar, as if he has mastered some secrets and has considerable combat potential.”

“However, if you think you can easily block my Divine Skills like this, you are too naive!”

Even though he had forced Ning Zhuo to this point, Tian Shuang had never unleashed the full power of this Divine Skill.

The fully unleashed Frost Soul Gaze could generate extreme cold, directly penetrating a cultivator’s Upper Dantian Divine Sea, freezing all thoughts!

Tian Shuang took a deep breath and immediately stopped holding back, fully activating his Divine Skills.

In an instant, terrifying coldness, like a waterfall from the sky, descended into Ning Zhuo’s Upper Dantian, bringing boundless cold, freezing all the Mirror Intent along the way.

These mirror-like thoughts shattered one after another, their countless fragments submerged by the cold and turned to nothing.

Ning Zhuo’s cultivation time was too short, only a mere ten or so years. Usually, he never encountered a bottleneck in Divine Sea cultivation and always thought things were fine.

Now, facing a powerful enemy like Tian Shuang, his significant flaws in Divine Sea protection were immediately exposed.

The terrifying coldness penetrated without any obstruction, making Ning Zhuo completely unprepared.

He was good at using the Five Elements Realm to design spells, but when it came to his own Divine Sea, it fell under the realm of Divine Arts. Most of his spells were ineffective.

Ning Zhuo couldn’t think of a solution, and after only delaying for a few breaths, most of the Mirror Intent in his Divine Sea had been frozen, slowing his thinking by several multiples.

This situation plunged him into the abyss of defeat completely!

At this moment, all his thoughts and ideas were about to be frozen and killed, completely losing the ability to think.

No matter how strong the Five Elements Realm was, without the ability to think, no spells could be conceived.

“Little Zhuo!” Sun Lingtong couldn’t hold back, ready to intervene and pull Ning Zhuo into the Ten-Thousand-Li Traveling Dragon to escape.

At that moment, an item in Ning Zhuo’s mind suddenly radiated boundless Buddha Light.

The Buddha Light, like the blazing sun, illuminated everything, clearing all coldness and icy intentions.

Unexpectedly, it was the Buddha Heart Devil Seal!

This treasure passively triggered, emitting endless Buddha Light, protecting Ning Zhuo’s Divine Sea.

In the Buddha Light, countless Buddha Thoughts appeared instantly.

In a few breaths, thousands of Buddha Thoughts filled Ning Zhuo’s Divine Sea.

Ning Zhuo’s thinking ability quickly recovered!

“That was close!”

“Just a little more and I would have lost completely.”

Tens of thousands of Buddha Thoughts in the Buddha Light shone brilliantly, while the Buddha Light surged upwards, counterattacking towards Bai Shuang.

“Ah!” Bai Shuang screamed, hurriedly covering his eyes.

From his tightly closed eye slits, large amounts of blood overflowed.

In the blood, there was even overflowing Buddha Light!

Bai Shuang, being a Ghost Cultivator, was restrained by Buddhist techniques. Using Divine Skills that formed such a close link with Ning Zhuo, he was counterattacked by the Buddha Light, causing severe damage to his eyes.

He certainly couldn’t use the Frost Soul Gaze again in a short time.

Sun Lingtong shouted: “Little Zhuo, a great chance, strike while he’s down! Let’s go all out!”

Ning Zhuo shook his head: “Fighting and killing, right and wrong, all are illusions, what meaning is there?”

Sun Lingtong: ?!

Even though Bai Shuang had closed his eyes, his vision was still filled with large patches of Buddha Light.

This Buddha Light also shone into his Divine Sea, driving away his other thoughts and replacing them with many Buddha Thoughts.

“These damn… Oh, I!”

Bai Shuang began repenting in his heart: “I truly deserve to die.”

“Why did I harm others, harming such a young and beautiful life?”

“I am not human; what I did was against the natural order! Why can’t I be kind to others?”

“May all beings be peaceful… Ah, nonsense.”

At the end of his thoughts, Bai Shuang forcibly twisted his thinking and cursed fiercely in his heart.

“These damn Buddha Thoughts, get out of my Divine Sea!”

Bai Shuang didn’t launch a new attack, and Ning Zhuo didn’t take the opportunity to strike, instead releasing the Floating Ice Jade Hands to urgently heal himself.

The two sides fell into an eerily peaceful standoff.

“We’ll attack in turns!” Liu Er shouted.

The confrontation between the Three Generals Camp and Earth Ash had entered a state of fierce white heat.

Liu Er, Guan Hong, and Zhang Hei broke out of the Iron Bucket Formation, attacking Earth Ash in turns.

This Earth Ghost of the Heaven and Earth Double Ghosts immediately activated a spell to shift away.

He didn’t use the ordinary Earth Escape Technique, but a higher-requirement, more effective Earth Walking Technique!

All members of the Three Generals Camp had participated in ambush battles and had seen Old Monster in Stone rely on the Earth Walking Technique, well aware of its powerful uses.

So, as soon as they saw the Earth Ghost using such a spell, they immediately changed their target.

“Aargh, White Ghost, take my spear!” Zhang Hei rushed into the sky, thrusting his spear at Tian Shuang, killing intent apparent.

Tian Shuang kept his eyes closed, maintaining his Divine Sense.

Just as the Black Snake Spear was about to touch the White Ghost’s head, the latter suddenly reached out.

Clang!

Tian Shuang’s hands directly intercepted the powerful Black Snake Spear, producing a crisp metallic impact sound.

Spell—Nine Nether Cold Soul Claw!

Tian Shuang’s fingers condensed into black ice, forming heavy claw gauntlets like cold iron.

The gauntlets were sealed with numerous frozen souls, each strike would bring a freezing wind. The freezing wind would manifest countless painful human faces, wailing mournfully, causing great disruption to the Divine Sense.

The next moment, Tian Shuang swung his claw.

Wooo…

Zhang Hei was hit by the dual assault of the fierce cold air and the wails of the frozen souls, immediately falling from the high sky.

But right afterward, Guan Hong, bolstered by a large amount of military power, followed up to challenge the Heavenly Ghost.

After Guan Hong, it was Liu Er’s turn.

Through several battles, Liu, Guan, and Zhang had developed a sense of tacit understanding. Relying on this tacit understanding, they formed a continuous offensive.

The Heavenly Ghost was quite beleaguered.

Every time he wanted to fight back, the Buddha Thoughts in his Divine Sea would rise, participate in his thinking, and cause disruption.

This hindered his murderous intent, making his thoughts feel restricted. Not to mention, he still had to cast spells.

The Heavenly Ghost was forcefully pushed back by the Three Generals.

Seeing this, Earth Ash laughed heartily.

It was the first time he had seen his best partner in such a predicament.

Hearing Earth Ash’s laughter, Liu, Guan, and Zhang felt a tightness in their hearts, realizing that their opponent had not yet gone all out!
