Chapter 734 -232: Capturing Two Generals, Finally Securing the Carriage_3

“Young Master Ning Zhuo, I, Xu Dali, will forever remember your life-saving grace!” Xu Dali propped his body up with all his strength and expressed his gratitude to Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo nodded at him, casually tossing him a supply of Healing Pills: “General Xu, you may follow behind our troops.”

He would not integrate Xu Dali into his own army, as it would disrupt the formation and coordination of his troops.

Likewise, the Three Generals Camp and the Red Flower Camp could not accept Xu Dali either.

“Thank you, Young Master!” Although Xu Dali had a straightforward personality, he was deeply grateful and respectful towards Ning Zhuo, his lifesaver.

He followed honestly behind Ning Zhuo’s group, taking advantage of their military presence, breathing heavily, and began using other methods to quickly restore his combat strength.

Lin You’s heart tightened as he noticed that Ning Zhuo’s group was not far from him.

This enemy situation drew his full attention.

“Count yourself lucky!” Lin You snorted coldly, letting off the opponent who was barely holding out, decisively maneuvering the mechanical car, and started to relocate with his subordinates.

“Little Zhuo, be careful, he’s about to run!” Sun Lingtong promptly reported.

Ning Zhuo nodded, responding with a hum, and carefully observing Lin You’s direction, he led his troops to attempt an interception.

Lin You immediately sensed Ning Zhuo’s group’s movements.

Instantly, suspicion rose in his heart: “What’s going on? Did I coincidentally end up on their path?”

Ning Zhuo’s long-standing strategy of patience and concealment played a crucial role at this moment. Lin You would never have expected that he was Ning Zhuo’s primary target.

Lin You quickly scanned the surroundings, his gaze fixing on Ju Bing.

He made another move, using Ju Bing’s military formation as a shield to block Ning Zhuo’s group and buy himself some time.

Gu Ya and Ju Bing were the most renowned Beast Taming Cultivators in the Thousand Peak Forest.

Gu Ya commanded a wolf pack, whereas Ju Bing controlled a herd of pigs.

Since the large-scale battle began, Ju Bing hadn’t joined the main camp to participate in the fiercest combat.

This was contrary to his nature, but there was no other choice. In previous battles, many of Ju Bing’s pig pets had been killed by the master and apprentice duo, Ding Pi and Jia Wan.

Ju Bing’s pig herd had significantly dwindled, so he was using them sparingly.

“Hmm?!” He frowned, also noticing that Ning Zhuo’s group was closing in on him.

“Since the battle started, my pig herd is down to forty percent, they can’t suffer any more losses!” Ju Bing started to form an intention to retreat.

The morale of the Thousand Peak Forest also affected him, prompting more conservative decisions.

Thus, the pig herd retreated like a tidal wave, making way for Ning Zhuo’s group with a wide passage.

Ning Zhuo: “Eh?”

Lin You: “What’s the situation?!”

Just as he realized it, Ning Zhuo’s group charged forward, closing the critical distance.

“General Mu Lan, General Liu Er, whoever captures this man will earn my favor!” Ning Zhuo shouted.

Mu Lan was initially indifferent, but upon hearing Ning Zhuo’s promise, his eyes shone brightly.

However, Liu Er reacted even faster, bursting with excitement as he immediately broke away from the main force and charged ahead alone.

Guan Hong and Zhang Hei showed expressions of surprise; they had never seen their big brother so aggressively bold before.

Mu Lan coldly snorted: “My husband’s favor is mine by right!”

She mobilized the battle formation and summoned her Dharma Form, the Red Flower itself flew out with great speed, landing directly on the mechanical car, wrapping Lin You and the vehicle completely.

Military Art—Petal Enclosure!

The Red Flower Dharma Image bore the combat power of a Nascent Soul Level, once trapping a great demon horse, Ma Feitui, of the Nascent Soul Level. Lin You was merely at the Golden Core Level, how could he resist? He was immediately captured alive.

Another captive!

“We got him!” Sun Lingtong cheered in the Dragon Head Cabin, raising his arm in triumph.

Ning Zhuo exhaled a deep breath, his heart settling as immense joy surged within him.

Success at last!

Even at the final moment, he had not revealed his true intent—to retrieve the mechanical car.
