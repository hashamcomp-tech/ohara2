Chapter 872: Chapter 300: Lord of Wangchuan vs Lady Beigui

Earth Element—Sandfall.

Earth Element Spleen Temple!

Ning Zhuo split his focus in two, concentrating on the battlefield while also diving into his own Inner Organ Temple.

The Earth Element statue manipulated it, greatly saving Ning Zhuo’s time practicing spells, allowing for exquisite control upon casting, as if it were instinctual.

Ning Zhuo gazed at the sky, and countless yellow sands gushed out from nowhere.

The sand waterfall surged downward, grand and majestic, spreading along the fortress wall, directly engulfing a large number of skeletal soldiers.

The sands formed a whirlpool quicksand at the base of the wall, swallowing two skeletal bridges before being abruptly stopped and dissipated by enemy generals using ghost technique to break it.

At least three Ghost Generals cast their spells simultaneously, the ghost techniques rose continuously, forming into a giant hand that reached through the air towards Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo did not evade or retreat, standing still, suddenly extending his right hand, his fingers pointed like a sword.

Gold Element—Astral Metal Storm.

Gold Element Lung Temple enchancement!

A large force of Geng Metal Qi spurted from his sword-like fingers. The sharp metal Qi clashed with the ghost hand, immediately halting its momentum.

Three Golden Core Level Ghost Generals shouted in unison, intensifying their mana infusion.

The ghost hand was worn down by the metal Qi continuously but was also nourished by mana, maintaining its massive size and still grabbing toward Ning Zhuo, albeit with greatly reduced speed.

Ning Zhuo chuckled coldly; the statue in the Lung Temple became incredibly alive, manipulating the magic technique to undergo a delicate transformation.

The next moment, the Geng Metal Qi transformed into a whirlwind, spinning rapidly, encircling the ghost hand completely.

The ghost hand was utterly trapped, unable to move, and a few breaths later, it was eradicated by the Geng Metal whirlwind.

The three Golden Core Ghost Generals each grunted, sharing the backlash from the broken spells, sustaining only minor injuries.

Even so, the fact that three at the Golden Core level joined forces and couldn’t overpower a single Foundation Establishment Cultivator was already a massive failure!

Ning Zhuo, taking advantage of the momentum, redirected the Astral Metal Storm downward, swirling around a skeletal bridge about to reach the top of the fortress wall.

Swish, swish, swish, swish...

The skin-prickling sound of slicing rang out densely. The hard skeletons in front of the metal blade storm were like rotten wood, instantly sliced, disassembled, and shredded into a sky full of white bone powder!

The storm swept past, and the bone bridge along with hundreds of skeletal soldiers on it were entirely eradicated, leaving a ground strewn with bone debris!

The Javelin Ghost General, seeing Ning Zhuo’s overwhelming strength, immediately seized a Magic Artifact level long spear, aiming at Ning Zhuo and throwing it directly.

Ning Zhuo’s Mechanical Ring instantly tightened, but he had already noticed the strike without its warning.

Water Element—Vortex Shield Return!

Water Element Kidney Temple enhancement.

A bright blue stream appeared out of nowhere, rapidly enveloping Ning Zhuo, transforming into a massive water shield.

The javelin struck the water shield, instantly inciting it into a fierce vortex.

The water vortex absorbed the javelin, continuously altering its direction. The javelin circled within the water shield and shot out at a slanted angle in another direction.

Bang!

The javelin directly struck the base of another skeletal bridge, demonstrating formidable power, collapsing the bridge.

The Javelin Ghost General was filled with rage and disbelief, not expecting Ning Zhuo to turn his move against them, destroying the tower bridge of the Yin Soldiers’ army. Thankfully, these skeletal soldiers had little intelligence, or their morale would certainly plummet.

Ning Zhuo successfully countered, maintaining the water shield while immediately activating another magic technique.

Wood Element—Root Vine Entanglement.

Wood Element Liver Temple!

Ning Zhuo juggled multiple tasks, performing auxiliary actions to cast the spell while sustaining other spells.

He formed hand seals with his left hand, completing all movements within two breaths, then flipped his hand, palm downward, and forcefully pressed towards the ground beneath the wall.

The ground rumbled violently as countless massive, dark green vines burst through the tough soil, wild and serpentine like living pythons! These vines ignored the cutting of the skeletal soldiers, swiftly wrapping around their leg bones, torsos, and arms, then strangling mercilessly!

Where the vines passed, the skeleton army collapsed in swathes; the scattered bone fragments, with their exposed soul fire, were also eradicated by the vines in their path.

This almost annihilated all the skeletal soldiers near the wall.

Even the skeletal bridges were all destroyed.

This section of the wall, by Ning Zhuo’s power alone, turned from peril to safety. The youth’s incredible performance attracted countless eyes.

The next moment, the amazement in those eyes turned to shock.

Fire Element—Nine Heavens Fire Dragon Technique!

Fire Element Heart Temple enhancement!

Ning Zhuo switched to Fire Element, displaying his adept crowd-control technique.

Three fire dragons roared skyward, soaring from the wall, carrying formidable power, surging nearly a thousand steps forward, incinerating vast numbers of skeletal soldiers yet to approach this segment of the wall!

Five or six Golden Core Level Ghost Generals took action one after another, some using ghost techniques against the fire dragons, while others applied military arts to empower the skeletal soldiers.

Ning Zhuo controlled the fire dragons with precision, avoiding many ghost techniques, maximizing their destructive potential.

After a protracted effort, the Ghost Generals finally eradicated the three fire dragons, each looking rather disgruntled, breathless, and quite disheveled.

Inside the soldier camp within the walls, Qing Chi turned her head towards Ning Zhuo’s direction.

She, along with the Qing Jiao Army, served as reserve forces, yet to take the field, merely on standby.

She witnessed the fire dragons soaring and soaring in mid-air, heard the successive dragon roars, and the thunderous blast from the fire dragon’s ultimate detonation: "Which powerful figure is fighting the enemy?"

She could feel the awe-inspiring presence of the fire dragons, lifting her spirits.

Clearly, such an offensive wasn’t in the style of the Yin Soldiers’ army; it must be a display from their own cultivators.

Ning Zhuo once again cast Five Elements Magic, clearing all skeletal soldiers in front of his section of the wall, leaving a vast expanse of empty plain.
