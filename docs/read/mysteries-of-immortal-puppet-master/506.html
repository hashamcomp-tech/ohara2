Chapter 506: Chapter 107: Horseshoes Trample the Flowers

“Give it a try,” Sun Lingtong said, also feeling very excited.

Ning Zhuo closed his eyes and silently sensed his new spleen.

In a trance, he seemed to enter a temple.

He sat upright on the platform and became the Earth God Statue worshipped in the temple!

From outside the temple, a large amount of blood, essence, and energy constantly drifted in. After falling into the temple and being illuminated by the Earth God Statue, they underwent rounds of purification.

The purification continued unabated, absorbing impurities, settling them down, and enriching the foundation soil beneath the temple.

...

The spleen itself functions to transform food, blood, and defend against diseases. Now, with Ning Zhuo’s own spleen serving as the foundation, the Earth Element Spleen Temple refined from it was even more efficient in this regard!

“It can also absorb and precipitate all impurities, acting like the earth itself—accepting everything, bearing everything, and transforming everything. Through profound accumulation and the erosion of time, it ultimately turns waste into treasure.”

This made Ning Zhuo immediately think of the Divine Power Stone Cast Earth Treasure!

He started to channel Mana, activating the Earth Element Spleen Temple.

Instantly, a surge of powerful force arose from the depths of the spleen and was channeled outward in all directions, actively participating in the vital operations of his entire body.

Ning Zhuo felt as if he was firmly pushed, gradually experiencing an indescribable sense of comfort all over.

He sat down cross-legged and tried to absorb the natural Spiritual Energy around him.

Instantly, a vast amount of Earth Element Spiritual Energy was absorbed, filling up the Earth Element Spleen Temple inside his body.

Such an effect of energy absorption made Ning Zhuo’s heart throb fiercely.

Even as the modifier of this Spell, he was surprised by the practical Cultivation effects!

“The extent of this Spiritual Energy absorption even far surpasses my use of the Tornado Windmill Magic Artifact.”

“No wonder the Five Elements God Master back in the day had such a rapid pace of cultivation despite practising all Five Elements.”

Five Elements Qi Regulation Art!

Ning Zhuo rapidly absorbed and converted the majority of the Earth Element Spiritual Energy into the other four types of Spiritual Energies, maintaining the balance of the Five Elements in his Qi Sea.

“In the future, if I could have a complete Five Elements Five Internal Organs Divine Temple, the speed of my Cultivation will inevitably leap tremendously.”

Ning Zhuo was greatly moved.

“The Five Internal Organs Temple Spirit God Skill I am currently designing must use the essence of the Five Elements and possess Spiritual Nature.”

Without Spiritual Nature, Ning Zhuo couldn’t create external gods.

His Blood Sacrifice of organs was mainly to utilize his own Spiritual Nature to create temples and control external gods.

He had just refined it, and he almost became the god himself, with the deepest control. However, if in the future his Spiritual Nature could not constantly replenish, the Spiritual Nature from Old Monster in Stone would inevitably rise and gradually take dominance, eventually controlling the Earth Element Spleen Temple.

This was the downside of the Five Internal Organs Temple Spirit God Skill!

“Therefore, the Earth Element Spleen Temple must not be detached from the flesh for too long; it needs to frequently return to the body to replenish Spiritual Nature and suppress external gods.”

Ning Zhuo reminded himself inwardly.

“Next comes the crucial part!”

With a thought, Ning Zhuo made his spleen tremble, detaching it from its original position and transforming it into a clump of earthy yellow light.

Ning Zhuo opened his mouth and spat out the light clump.

While controlling the light clump to drill into the Stone Ball, he simultaneously activated the Demon-infused Bloodline Technique, allowing a patch of Blood Vessels to temporarily replace the missing part of the spleen.

The light clump followed the previously drilled fine holes on the Stone Ball, reaching deep into the stone heart.

There, the light clump expanded, filling the void in the stone heart and vaguely forming an Earth Element Temple. The temple doors were wide open, with a god statue sitting inside, bearing a striking resemblance to Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo and his Earth Element Spleen Temple were extremely closely connected. He immediately controlled the Earth Element Spleen Temple, rapidly permeating into the Stone Ball itself.

After a moment, it was as if he had grown an additional organ.

He manipulated the top-grade spirit crystal inside the Stone Ball, which contained Nascent Soul Level Earth Element Power. The power jetted out, immediately making the Stone Ball roll and turn around, extremely agilely.

With a thought from Ning Zhuo, the Earth Element Spleen Temple burst out with a strong suction force and immediately drew back the jetted Earth Element Power, storing it inside the Spirit Stone.

Sun Lingtong, who had been watching all along, saw this scene and struck his palms together forcefully: “Little Zhuo, you’ve succeeded!”

Ning Zhuo also revealed a bright smile: “Boss, with the Earth Element Spleen Temple, I can control the Stone Ball itself as easily as moving my own arm.”

“But I still need to rework its internals, make it into a mechanism, to better utilize it!”

The core layer of the Stone Ball was filled with vast amounts of top-grade Earth Element Spirit Crystals, containing overwhelming Nascent Soul Level Mana, a natural Mana Pool.

Ning Zhuo also planned to create multiple Stone Mirrors.

The Stone Mirror originated from the Mirror Stand Spirit Communication Technique, which uniquely enhances Divine Sense, and in combination with the Stone Ball, they complemented each other well.

Time was extremely tight. Ning Zhuo worked tirelessly for two consecutive days and nights, finally achieving his expected goal.

His face was pale, with two quite evident dark circles under his eyes, as he returned to the Three Generals Camp.

In addition to training the new soldiers, the Three Generals also worried about Ning Zhuo.

After all, Ning Zhuo had taken a significant amount of military funds and disappeared without a trace.

Hearing of Ning Zhuo’s return, Liu Er laughed heartily and personally came out of the camp to welcome him.

Seeing Ning Zhuo, he was momentarily stunned.

Previously, Ning Zhuo was dressed in white, with bright eyes and fair skin, radiantly spirited. Now, the youth appeared haggard, with a pale complexion, puffy eyes, and deep dark circles, as if a female ghost had sucked away much of his Yang Energy.

Liu Er took a deep breath, extended his hands to support Ning Zhuo’s arms: “The strategist has done so much for our army, to the point of such exhaustion!”

“Enter the camp, rest for a few days, properly recuperate, and must not overwork again like this.”

He only cared about Ning Zhuo, not even mentioning anything about Divination.

Such social skills, whether sincere or not, felt genuinely warm to Ning Zhuo at the moment.
