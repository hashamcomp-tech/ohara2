Chapter 691 -210: The Layout (Additional Chapter 1/2 by Book Friend 20210111152859427)

Sun Lingtong’s expression was anxious; she stared intently at the ice pipa: “Little Zhuo, you restrain Tian Shuang, I’ll try to steal this Magic Treasure!”

Ning Zhuo: “By my Buddha, such a Buddha Treasure that brings disaster to the world, the sooner it is eliminated, the sooner it can benefit all beings.”

Sun Lingtong was momentarily speechless: “Alright, Little Zhuo, I’m making my move.”

Just as she spoke, Tian Shuang took a deep breath, pressed his palms together fiercely in front of his chest.

Divine Skill – Mysterious Yin Frozen Realm!

In an instant, centered on Tian Shuang, a hundred-zhang-wide ice mist barrier unfolded.

Within the barrier, fog spread, condensing into countless inverted ice cones. The ice cones were a deep, subtle greenish-black, their surfaces inscribed with eerie talismans. Each ice cone sealed an Ice Prison Weeping Ghost within, making them exceedingly lethal.

Sun Ning and his companion were trapped in the barrier, their hair standing on end.

Sun Lingtong loudly warned: “Little Zhuo, be careful!”

They had already obtained information on the Heaven and Earth dual ghosts, including details about the Mysterious Yin Frozen Realm.

This was essentially a spell, involving myriad mysteries and applications concerning space, ice, and the ghost path.

Tian Shuang grasped the essence of the spell, understanding its principles, thus transforming it into a Divine Skill!

Once this Divine Skill was performed, it could form a massive barrier in an extremely short time. Within the barrier, the cold surged, freezing both body and mind. The talismans on the ice cones not only had a sealing effect but could also cooperate with the Ice Prison Weeping Ghosts for soul exchange.

The Ice Prison Weeping Ghosts were bound in the extreme cold prison, attracting the sympathy of outside beings through their weeping.

Their cries might be heart-wrenching, slight sobs, or mournfully gentle, varying according to the target, most effectively moving specific individuals.

Once such a target was infected, shedding a tear out of sympathy, the Ice Prison Weeping Ghosts would succeed, exchanging their soul with the target’s, thereby escaping the ice prison.

If Ning Zhuo fell prey, his soul would be trapped in an ice cone, while his body would be taken over by a ghost from the ice cone!

The cold surged forward, greatly disturbing Ning Zhuo’s vision.

Countless ice cones shot, piercing through the cold mist, towards Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo maneuvered the Somersault Cloud, dodging swiftly in the air, occasionally flipping, having only the power to evade but not to counterattack.

This was due to the fact that he was still under attack by the sound of the pipa.

“Lord, I will protect you!” At the critical moment, Yuan Dasheng stepped forward again, using his body as a shield to block almost all the ice cones for Ning Zhuo.

Hundreds of ice cones pierced his body, densely packed, even layered upon each other.

The Black Iron Cloud under his feet was also hit, disintegrating immediately! This Flowing Clouds Magic Treasure Level item was destroyed under the terrifying assault of the ice cones.

Yuan Dasheng turned his head with difficulty, looked at Ning Zhuo, and then the light in his eyes quickly faded.

“You’ve worked hard. Come back!” Ning Zhuo sighed, releasing flames to burn.

The ice cones on Yuan Dasheng’s body shrieked and scattered under the burning.

Ning Zhuo took the burned, disfigured Heavy Blood Ape – Dasheng back into his pouch.

Crying sounds emanated from his body.

Though he was dodging everywhere and had Yuan Dasheng’s protection, he was still hit by a few ice cones in the end.

The ice cones pierced his body, the Ice Prison Weeping Ghosts within immediately launching their attack; crying sounds emerged from within his body, making Ning Zhuo’s eyes red in an instant.

“Damn! Little Zhuo, your Divine Sea is filled with Buddha Thoughts.” Sun Lingtong’s face turned pale with shock.

“My Buddha is merciful!” Ning Zhuo’s face was full of sorrow, “As the saying goes, if I do not enter hell, who will enter hell…”

“Bullshit!” Sun Lingtong roared.

At the critical moment, he finally couldn’t hold back and made his move.

Stealing Technique – True Copy Hand!

The few ice cones pierced into Ning Zhuo’s body disappeared instantly, reappearing within the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon.

Ning Zhuo turned danger into safety!

However, because of this incident, Sun Lingtong was exposed to Tian Shuang, Zhu Xuanji, Lin Bufan and Red Sleeves Fairy.

Red Sleeves Fairy uttered a light “huh.”

Lin Bufan frowned: “The one who just made the move didn’t seem to be Ning Zhuo.”

Zhu Xuanji nodded: “Indeed.”

As the Divine Hunter, he already knew Sun Lingtong was beside Ning Zhuo. He had dealt with Sun Lingtong back in the Fire Persimmon Immortal City and was deeply impressed with him.

After all, it was rare in Zhu Xuanji’s life to be openly swindled and grabbed onto on the streets.

Tian Shuang roared: “No matter how many helpers are hidden, they’ll perish in my Mysterious Yin Frozen Realm!”

His Buddha thoughts had been halved, allowing more of his power to be unleashed.

Ning Zhuo controlled the Somersault Cloud, struggling to escape in the Mysterious Yin Frozen Realm.

His Somersault Cloud was only Magic Artifact Level, soon meeting its end.

“Little Zhuo, use mine!” Sun Lingtong immediately lent his Somersault Cloud.

Back in Fire Persimmon Immortal City, Ning Zhuo had bought one for Sun Lingtong as well.

Since Sun Lingtong actively lent it out, Ning Zhuo received it without hindrance.

However, it was clear that this Magic Artifact Level Somersault Cloud wouldn’t hold out for long.

Tian Shuang howled once more and threw out another Magic Treasure.

This was a completely transparent cold jade coffin, displaying an ice-blue luster. The coffin was sculpted from the mysterious yin cold jade of an extreme cold place, crystal clear, exuding bone-chilling cold just by looking at it.

Intricate and ancient talismans were carved around the coffin. The talismans emitted a ghostly blue light.

Ice crystals were embedded along the coffin’s edges, and frost formed natural patterns on its surface, like snowflakes blooming in winter.

The lid was covered in complex array patterns. These patterns intertwined to form an Array. At the center of the array, right in the middle of the lid, was an ice-blue ghost eye.
