Chapter 543

Three Generals Camp.

General Liu Er reviewed military affairs, his brows permanently furrowed in worry.

After he handled the last piece of military duty and placed it at the corner of the table, he couldn't help but let out a long sigh.

He called for his subordinate to convey these commands, then Liu Er lifted the tent flap, glanced towards the camp's main gate, and then let the flap fall back, pacing back and forth with hands clasped behind his back within the tent.

"Why hasn't he returned yet?" murmured Liu Er.

Ning Zhuo had informed Liu Er of his invitation to General Shuangjing's banquet before he left.

Since morning, Liu Er had been distracted and restless.

It wasn't until a subordinate reported Ning Zhuo's return that Liu Er's face finally broke into a smile.

He immediately lifted the tent flap and ran towards the camp's main gate.

Upon meeting Ning Zhuo, he laughed heartily, his smile warm as flame: "Military Adviser, you're back! Good to have you back!"

While welcoming him, Liu Er covertly observed Ning Zhuo's expression.

Seeing Ning Zhuo's furrowed brows and his troubled look, Liu Er's heart skipped a beat, and he quickly asked, "Military Adviser, did General Shuangjing give you a hard time? Don't worry, your three brothers here will back you up."

Ning Zhuo shook his head: "That's not the case."

"It's General Shuangjing inviting me, wanting to recruit me as his retainer."

Liu Er's smile froze and a coldness settled in his heart, fearing the very thing he dreaded might have happened.

But he still managed a strained smile: "This matter requires careful consideration."

"Although General Shuangjing is of noble birth, Military Adviser's background is almost the same."

"But to become a retainer is to lose your freedom…"

"Oh, that wouldn't be the case. He explicitly said I could leave whenever I wish and did not restrict my freedom," Ning Zhuo gestured dismissively.

Liu Er's smile froze again, his heart sinking to the depths, but he still congratulated with a clasped hand gesture: "In that case... that is indeed auspicious. After all, General Shuangjing's lineage is truly distinguished. I must congratulate the Military Adviser, for with General Shuangjing's support, you could surely avenge your family's downfall."

Ning Zhuo shook his head: "I did not agree on the spot."

Liu Er's eyes immediately brightened: "Could it be that the Military Adviser you…"

Ning Zhuo nodded: "I am still considering it."

Liu Er's expression dimmed once more.

Ning Zhuo glanced at the training field within the camp: "Why aren't Generals Guan Hong and Zhang Hei training the troops?"

"Our army has just been established, and after our recent victory capturing a Nascent Soul Cultivator, morale is high. We should be seizing every minute to train the soldiers, preparing for the massive battle that will eventually join the warfront."

Liu Er sighed deeply: "I was just about to seek Military Adviser's advice, when the soldiers became agitated. They are quite dissatisfied, demanding compensation in gold, and wish to renegotiate the previous agreements. My youngest brother took it upon himself to handle the issue."

"As for my second brother, he is in the tent absorbing National Power to enhance his Cultivation Level."

The reward for the three generals, Liu, Guan, and Zhang, apart from promotion, included ten units of National Power.

Ning Zhuo immediately showed interest: "How's General Guan's injury?"

Before, Wu Hen had ambushed Guan Hong, causing a severe injury to Guan Hong's right arm.

As they spoke, a Divine Sense message came through: "Thank you for your concern, Military Adviser. The injury to my right arm is more troublesome than I imagined. I have tried all sorts of methods in rotation, but to no avail."

It was Guan Hong.

Obviously, he had finished his round of Cultivation.

Liu Er frowned and immediately, along with Ning Zhuo, entered the tent to visit Guan Hong.

The tent was dim, lit by a few candles. The candlelight was red, casting a blood-like glow.

Guan Hong wore a deep green robe, but his right arm was exposed, showing an injury noticeably swollen, the skin and flesh tinged with black and purple.

"No wonder!" Liu Er's expression darkened.

"No wonder!" Ning Zhuo's eyes lit up.

Liu Er suddenly bowed with a clasped hand to Ning Zhuo, "Does Military Adviser also dabble in the strange and mysterious ways? I see you possess a scythe, which must be among the finest of the Night Rain Demon Weapons. In the previous ambush, the scythe easily repelled Wu Hen, its power immense! Please, Military Adviser, lend your aid."

Ning Zhuo nodded: "I don't have much experience in the arcane, merely lucked out with a Night Rain Demon Weapon."

"General Guan, may I see your injury?"

Guan Hong nodded: "Please, Military Adviser."

Ning Zhuo examined the wound, his Divine Sense penetrating the injury, finding the black and purple spreading.

It wasn't just spreading through the flesh but was also corroding the surface of the bone.

"Although the wound isn't large, if left untreated, it will increasingly lead to more severe complications over time," Liu Er projected his Divine Sense as well, full of concern.

He and Guan Hong looked towards Ning Zhuo with hopeful gazes.

Ning Zhuo shook his head: "I possess a Night Rain Demon Weapon, adept at slaying, not suited for healing such injuries."

"However, although the arcane is difficult to resolve, the injury is still confined to the right arm. Perhaps, General Guan could rely on the National Power to surge his Cultivation Level and crush it in a single go?"

Guan Hong sighed: "That is a last resort."

Liu Er then spoke up promptly: "Second brother, all ten units of National Power will be yours, I'll take responsibility for my youngest brother's share as well! I'll compensate my youngest brother."

Guan Hong felt touched.

Ning Zhuo, however, was somewhat curious and asked: "These ten units of National Power were awarded by the Royal Capital, can they indeed be concentrated for use by one among you?"

Liu Er nodded: "The imperial edict only specifies that these ten units of National Power are awarded to the three of us, without naming specific individuals or precise allocation, it should indeed be feasible."

"But the four units of National Power you have, Military Adviser, can only be used by yourself."
