Chapter 528: Chapter 122: Red Heart Arrow

Numerous elixir pills were consumed, and during this critical time, both Sun Ning exerted all their strength to recover rapidly.

Soon, they resumed their actions.

According to Ning Zhuo’s plan, instead of stealing the mana pool, they headed towards the armory.

A stone ball suddenly emerged from the ground of the armory, immediately triggering the alarm and the attacks from the defensive magic array.

“Hold firm!” Ning Zhuo employed the stone ball, casting Earth Element Magic!

The surging mana was rapidly consumed, turning into a majestic earthy-yellow halo.

The halo was thin, yet its defenses were substantial, easily upholding against all attacks.

“The armory is under attack!”

“Quick, follow me in to slay the enemy!!”

The cultivators guarding the armory hastily rushed in upon hearing the noise.

Then, they saw a round stone ball quickly tumbling through the armory, wherever it went, helmets and spears all vanished.

“Somebody is stealing the armaments!”

“Not good, stop them quickly!”

“Thief, leave your life behind!”

The cultivators of White Jade Camp were furious, all striking out.

They mobilized military power, and the might of their attacks soared, making the earthy-yellow halo wobble… but that was it.

If these soldiers had been able to mobilize the full military power, they might have posed a threat to the stone ball, but how could that be possible now?

The majority of the military power was continuously mobilized to assist the main general and deputy generals in besieging Lord Long, and occasionally to enhance the defenses of the peripheral cultivators, resisting far-ranged spells from other Golden Core Cultivators of Thousand Peak Forest.

If among these soldiers, there was a Golden Core Level cultivator, under the enhancement of military power and battle formation, they might have damaged the stone ball.

But the reality was, the highest cultivation level among these soldiers was only at the Foundation Establishment Stage.

Ning Zhuo chose an excellent time; White Jade Camp was already too busy to defend thoroughly.

The stone ball toughly resisted all sorts of attacks, circled the armory, and almost all the military equipment was taken away.

Then, under the extremely angry gazes of a dozen pairs of eyes, it suddenly burrowed underground and disappeared once again.

Inside the stone ball, within the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon.

A huge pile of armor and spears had formed a small mountain.

Sun Lingtong fully released his stealing technique, making them extremely compact, otherwise, they wouldn’t fit inside the Mechanical Dragon!

Jade Armor!

Shocking Light Spear!

The armor was mainly milky-white, emanating a layer of soft jade light, combining toughness, rigidity, and exceptional protective abilities.

The armor in its entirety retained its original form, with smooth shoulder guards, sleek lines, the chestplate resembling a circular mirror, full curvature, overall simple and elegant.

The spears were snow-white, with sharp tips flashing cold light, the shafts cold, seven feet long, with barrier-like hoops at the rear end to prevent slipping, which could sometimes also assist in blocking.

Both, the Jade Armor and Shocking Light Spear, were weapons, inherently imbued with Military Arts.

The former came with Beijian Skill, allowing full-body defensive coverage; the latter with Sharp Weapon Skill, adding sharp lethality from the spear to punches and kicks.

These two Military Arts could also be combined, forming the Beisharp Skill!

This art allowed the soldiers’ attacks and defenses to meld together into one, where attack was defense, and defense was attack.

If the enemy punches the armor, it would be as if they are punching the spear tip. Similarly, if they attempt to smash the thin sharp spear tip, it was no different than hitting the solid armor.

Beisharp Skill is a quite classical Military Art, once deployed, it maintains a balance of offense and defense with no weaknesses.

Thus, although these two types of weapons were only at the Magic Artifact Level, not at the Magic Treasure Level, they were still very valuable in the market.

The Jade Armor was priced at forty thousand Low Grade Spirit Stones each, while the Shocking Light Spear was priced at seventy thousand Low Grade Spirit Stones each.

There were more than four hundred sets of Jade Armor and over three hundred eighty Shocking Light Spears.

The total value is roughly forty-two million two hundred twenty-six thousand Low Grade Spirit Stones!

Because they were at the Magic Artifact Level, sealing them away by Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong was much easier than securing the mana pool.

It was just that there were too many of them.

Sun Ning and the other worked frantically, hands blurring, and after completing all the temporary seals, they lay exhausted on the floor, not moving an inch.

“I don’t want to move anymore.” Ning Zhuo’s face was pale, devoid of any color, his eyes full of blood vessels.

His cultivation level was only in the Foundation Establishment Early Stage, considerably lower than Sun Lingtong.

Sun Lingtong struggled to support himself, sitting up with his upper body, encouraging Ning Zhuo: “Little Zhuo, get up quickly. This is a great opportunity, we can’t miss it. Think about how we used to struggle for resources for cultivation.”

Ning Zhuo’s face suddenly changed, memories and painful emotions whipped him like a lash.

“Continue! Let’s continue!!”

White Jade Camp.

Lord Long’s palms flew, accompanied by dragon roars, repeatedly causing Golden Core deputy generals to be blown away.

Shuangjing felt restrained in combat.

Because he wasn’t a Body Cultivator, only cultivating the Qi Sea, making it difficult to directly confront Lord Long.

This was a significant disadvantage in real combat!

Shuangjing could only rely on White Tiger Sky-tearing Sword Qi, continuously attacking Lord Long; the Golden Core deputy generals tried their hardest, acting as human shields, creating opportunities and space for Shuangjing.

The condition of the Golden Core deputy generals grew worse and worse, struggling to hold on.

“What happened? Our military power has decreased so much!” Shuangjing felt uneasy, urgently inquiring.

The subordinates had wanted to report earlier, but there wasn’t an opportunity before.

Seeing Shuangjing unusually interrupting his strikes to inquire, the subordinates hurriedly bowed: “Reporting to the lord, a stone monster invaded our camp, possessing Nascent Soul Level combat power. It’s been stealing military equipment everywhere, and despite the subordinates’ best efforts, it’s difficult to capture.”

“What?!” Shuangjing’s brow furrowed immediately, “A spherical stone monster? Could it be… the Old Monster in Stone from Thousand Peak Forest?”
