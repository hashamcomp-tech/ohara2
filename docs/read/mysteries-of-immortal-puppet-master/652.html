Chapter 652 -192: Ginseng Dragon Whisker_3

Ning Zhuo cast a Gold Element Technique, deeply cutting into the pile of rubble, targeting the lower half of the ginseng dragon whisker.

Gold beats Wood.

However, Ning Zhuo’s Gold Element Technique was only of Foundation Establishment Level, while the ginseng dragon whisker in front of him was of Golden Core Level, and even had a strand of Divinity Transformation Divine Aura.

This resulted in the Gold Element Technique only causing some superficial scratches on its surface. A breath later, it completely healed.

Ning Zhuo remained calm, he was merely testing the spell.

Next, he cast nearly ten different spells in quick succession, covering the spectrum of the Five Elements of metal, wood, water, fire, and earth.

Liu Er watched from the side and was secretly astounded, praising aloud: “Strategist, could it be that your family specializes in Five Elements Magic?”

“The spells you cast, I’ve almost never seen any of them.”

“This makes me curious, just how many Five Elements spells have you mastered?”

Ning Zhuo smiled faintly, but he did not reveal the truth about creating spells on the fly and simply said Liu Er’s praises were undue.

Liu Er shook his head and sighed: “Strategist, you are too modest.”

While conversing, Ning Zhuo did not stop his actions.

“I’ve figured it out.” A moment later, he stopped his hand movements, showing a slight smile filled with a sense of accomplishment.

He then cast three spells in succession: Fire-attribute, Gold Element, and Earth Element.

After these spells were cast in turn, the ginseng dragon whisker shook violently, its movements stiffened, and its combat strength greatly decreased.

Liu Er’s pupils contracted slightly, a tremor in his heart: “Even though these are merely three Foundation Establishment Level spells, they have such a restraining effect on a Golden Core Level ginseng dragon whisker!”

“Ning Zhuo…”

With a solemn expression on his face, Liu Er’s eyes reflected the young visage of Ning Zhuo in white robes.

In his heart, he firmly believed: “Given time, Ning Zhuo is bound to shine spectacularly! Just his mastery in Five Elements Magic alone spells a boundless future for him.”

After being restrained, the ginseng dragon whisker was no longer as fierce and valiant as before.

In the end, Zhang Hei swung his spearhead and toppled it.

Liu Er seized the opportunity to act, casting a spell to seal the ginseng dragon whisker.

“How much would such a treasure material be worth on the market?” Zhang Hei excitedly asked.

Liu Er was not very sure.

Ning Zhuo, stroking his chin, estimated a figure: “Roughly three hundred thousand Mid Grade Spirit Stones.”

Liu Er and Zhang Hei both looked startled, then quickly became excited.

Liu Er, who was also fearful of poverty, asked: “That much?”

Ning Zhuo nodded: “Mainly because this one is so long, its size is substantial. Plus, it has the renown of the ginseng whisker dragon king, perhaps when resold to distant places, it could fetch an even higher price.”

“However, to remove the residual will within the ginseng dragon whisker, that would cost a hefty sum of money.”

Liu Er’s excited expression slightly receded, and he nodded lightly: “Indeed. Not only is the ginseng whisker dragon king of Divinity Transformation Level, but it has also taken the path of the Divine Tao. The will of gods is often very stubborn and lingering.”

Zhang Hei then sighed regrettably: “It’s a pity, none of these ginseng dragon whiskers are ours to claim, they all have to be handed over.”

Liu Er comforted him: “Exchanging them for military merits, and then trading those merits for military supplies, is more suitable for us.”

Zhang Hei shook his head: “Trading for military merits would definitely mean the lowest market price. I, Old Zhang, don’t even need to think to know this.”

Ning Zhuo’s expression showed a slight change.

Liu Er looked at Ning Zhuo: “Strategist, according to our previous agreement, the Three Generals Camp is entitled to thirty percent of the battlefield spoils, which are all yours.”

“And this time, military merits can also be traded.”

“It’s shameful, we have only now begun to repay you.”

Ning Zhuo laughed and waved his hand: “General, you need not take it to heart, you know me.”

Liu Er laughed heartily: “Meeting the strategist is a fortune in my life.”

Zhang Hei also laughed along, adding: “I share the same sentiment.”

For a while, the atmosphere was relaxed and cheerful.

Following Liu and Zhang, Ning Zhuo took down two more ginseng dragon whiskers, and it was already noon.

After Ning Zhuo carefully passed on a few newly created spells to Liu and Zhang, he informed the pair he would go out to explore on his own.

Given Ning Zhuo’s exceptional status within the Three Generals Camp, Zhang Hei had no objections, while Liu Er instructed Ning Zhuo to be extra cautious.

As he entered the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon, Sun Lingtong couldn’t wait to say, “Hurry, it’s time for Old Monster in Stone to make an appearance.”
