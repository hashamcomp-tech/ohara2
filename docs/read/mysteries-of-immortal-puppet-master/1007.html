Chapter 1007: Chapter 365: The Young Man’s Fortune: A Surplus of Magic Treasures

This is also Ning Zhuo’s powerful trump card to restrain and continuously influence the White Paper City Lord, and even the Yin Tide Black Swamp in the future!

The upcoming magic treasures are the Taixu Disintegration Ruler, Golden Light Necklace, Qingbai Jade Ring, etc.

Taixu Disintegration Ruler is the legacy of a Xuan Su Scholar.

First captured by Luo Si, then given to Ning Zhuo. Ning Zhuo realized the arrangement of fate on the Black Cloud Battlefield, using it to break the Yellow Dust Barrier.

The Taixu Disintegration Ruler played a crucial role.

After the Underworld battle, Ning Zhuo also tried to refine it but disappointingly found that he didn’t make a single bit of progress.

"I have yet to find the key."

"Xuan Su Scholar was a great power at the Refining Void level. When he built the White Paper Immortal City and left numerous legacies, secretly arranging the Paperman Army, he must have had a purpose."

Ning Zhuo felt that if he didn’t understand this purpose, he wouldn’t be able to find the key to refining the Taixu Disintegration Ruler.

But for now, by relying on his identity as the Deputy City Lord of White Paper Immortal City, he can still freely use this treasure ruler.

So, whether to refine it or not is not important.

As for the Golden Light Necklace and Qingbai Jade Ring, they are all defense magic treasures. Ning Zhuo exchanged them using his military merits on the battlefield of Liangzhu Country.

In the subsequent battle against the Lord of Wangchuan, Ning Zhuo did not use them.

The reason is simple – even if he simultaneously activated both magic treasures, the additional defense wouldn’t be enough to withstand an enemy’s strike.

During that battle, Ning Zhuo mostly relied on the Iron Han Armor: Golden Swallow, jumping and maneuvering on the line between life and death.

However, in the battle against Lin Jinglong, Ning Zhuo did use the Qingbai Jade Ring.

It worked quite well.

Lin Jinglong didn’t resort to his magic treasures while Ning Zhuo, with the Qingbai Jade Ring, directly placed himself in an undefeatable position. No matter how violently the spring thunder bombarded, it didn’t shake him in the slightest.

Besides these, there is also the stomach of the Bone-piercing Crocodile.

Back then on the ship, Qing Yan gave the stomach to Ning Zhuo. Since its material is of Golden Core level, the stomach itself belongs to the magic treasure level.

The stomach is used to enhance the soul foundation.

As long as you escape the soul and burrow into it, endure the corrosion, and then recover, you can greatly increase the soul’s toughness.

The stomach has a limit on the number of uses, so this Golden Core level magic treasure is of the lowest grade.

Ning Zhuo accepted it but didn’t have the time to use it. When he finally had the time, he had Chef Lao’s dark cuisine and soon after, the soul cultivation four treasures given by the White Paper City Lord.

Compared to these, the stomach’s cultivation effect was very different. Ning Zhuo never used it once and directly left it at the bottom of his chest.

Unused items also include the Dragon Qi Jade Pendant and Wangchuan Prefect Seal.

Ning Zhuo took out the Dragon Qi Jade Pendant, observed it for a while, then put it away.

"This was given by Li Xiangshang. When I ascended the sacrificial altar to seize my innate talents, a crack appeared."

"It must have played a crucial role, greatly helping me."

"It’s a pity that when I led the Qing Jiao Army back to the mortal world, Li Xiangshang had already left White Paper Immortal City."

"However, he’s cultivating by the Cang River, under the command of White-haired Dragon Lord. If there’s a chance in the future to find him for a chat, perhaps I can uncover the true use of the jade pendant."

As for the Wangchuan Prefect Seal, to Ning Zhuo, it is complete uselessness.

"Maybe in the future, I can use it to trade with Lord of Wangchuan?"

"Let’s wait and see."

"Unless Lord of Wangchuan weakens to a certain extent, I won’t use this seal to make a deal with him."

Currently, Wangchuan Underworld is undergoing a full-scale attack by the Ghost Fire Underworld.

Ning Zhuo chose to watch quietly.

"We can’t let Ghost Fire Underworld completely occupy Wangchuan Underworld. With such a large territory annexed, the strength of Ghost Fire Underworld will swell exponentially."

"Yin Tide Black Swamp will face an opponent not as familiar as the Lord of Wangchuan but a more powerful Ghost Fire Underworld."

This is not what Ning Zhuo wants to see.

What he needs is for Wangchuan Underworld and Ghost Fire Underworld to continue battling, for the two giant beasts to become stuck in a stalemate, continuously bleeding out, thus making them too busy to attend to others.

"Therefore, the opportunity to trade this Wangchuan Prefect Seal will only arise when Wangchuan Underworld is on the verge of collapse!"

Upon inspection, Ning Zhuo found that the magic treasures in his possession had exceeded ten.

Of course, this includes national treasures, military treasures, and so on.

Overall, the presence of magic treasures at this level has greatly surpassed the worth of a Foundation Establishment Cultivator.

Not to mention Foundation Establishment Cultivators, even the vast majority of Golden Core Cultivators would pale in comparison.

If considering the quality, whether it’s the Buddha Heart Demon Seal, Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat, Yin Yang Qi Pot, or Wangchuan Prefect Seal, it would make Nascent Soul Cultivators covetously envious.

And when Ning Zhuo calculated these, he deliberately excluded mechanical artifacts, otherwise, it would be even more.

Just Qi Bai’s body alone contains four mechanism parts of the magic treasure level.

Among mechanical artifacts, Ning Zhuo used Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon the most.

Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon helped Ning Zhuo a lot, especially during the Liangzhu Country battle, where it had many performances.

Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon has numerous empty cabins and has always been equipped with great potential for enhancement. Anyone else would probably invest day and night to continuously build it.

But this Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon fell into Ning Zhuo’s hands, only being sparsely built once at Ten Thousand Medicine Sect specifically for Spirit Hidden Willow. Once Spirit Hidden Willow was transplanted, there were just minor repairs made, no construction.

Sun Ning and others were busy traveling and had neither the time nor the leisure.

But now, because of moving various items from Shuyu Pavilion’s cave mansion, Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon has also incidentally started its second round of construction.

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin, Heavy Blood Ape: Big Victory, Shadow Blade: Night Tiger, Hundred-Armed Arhat Armor, Iron Han Armor: Golden Swallow are Golden Core level mechanical puppets.

Among them, Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin grasps peculiar methods, possessing Nascent Soul Level combat power.

Besides these, there are Netherworld Envoy: Qi Bai, Miss Rudie, Mechanical Ring, Floating Ice Jade Hands, Weather Balloon: Ice Crystal Snow.

Following this are some mass-produced matured mechanisms. For example, Explosive Flame Monkey, Runaway Fire Snake, Silent Thunderbird, Golden Swallow, etc.

Then there are some mechanism parts, such as the upper and lower Ruyi Ropes, Hanging-Floating Whip, Riveted Powerful Crossbow, Hangman’s Noose, Half-Alive-Dead Beads, and so on.

Regarding elixirs, many were consumed on the journey by Ning Zhuo. Currently, the last valuable one is Nine Springs Resting Incense, which is one of the soul cultivation four treasures of White Paper Immortal City.

Spirit Food cannot be stored for too long, so there is a large amount of Ice Jade Wine, a small amount of Bitter Cold True Water, etc. The most valuable one is Cold Bone Moon Dew Wine. Like Nine Springs Resting Incense, it is also one of the soul cultivation four treasures.

There are quite a few talismans. Among them, the Triad Gift Treasure Talisman once provided Ning Zhuo significant help, but now it’s no longer useful. Oil Slick and Smooth Talisman, Flowing Clouds and Streaming Water Talisman, etc., even contains some spiritual nature! Targeted Teleportation Talisman, Substitute Talisman provide Ning Zhuo more room for error.

"But previously during the Lord of Wangchuan battle, none of these talismans could be used."

"In high-level combat, the grade of these talismans is still too low."

There are only a few materials left. The previous collection from Heavenly Ghost household was all invested into building Qing Jiao Army.

Some from Western Pure Land, over a hundred Ghost Dao Golden Cores, and a pile of materials dismantled from magic treasures and magic artifacts.

Supreme Kindness is Like Water, Wood Fire has already been consumed.

There’s a large gap in Spirit Stone!

The most valuable one is the Stone Monster’s corpse, which cannot be consumed. Perhaps going to Thousand Peak Forest in the future, it can be used to impersonate the Old Monster in Stone.

Ning Zhuo saw thirteen stalks of Ghost Shadow Bamboo from among them. At the time, he wanted to study Night Tiger’s design plan, keeping these Ghost Shadow Bamboo for future use.

Now that Night Tiger has fully adopted Golden Core level materials, these Foundation Establishment level Ghost Shadow Bamboo no longer have any use.

Regarding books, Ning Zhuo has also gradually collected a small pile. For example, "Ice Carving Secret Scripture", "Curved Snake Bouncing Array Collection", "Yin Bone Technique", "Netherworld Heart Sutra", "Detailed Explanation of Soul Attraction Technique", "Blood Demon Disintegration Small Method", "Dead Moon Sacrificial Song", "Heavenly Burial Ceremony", Jiao Family Jade Slip, "Myriad Phenomena Scripture" (Qi Refinement, Foundation Establishment Chapter), Chef Lao’s legacy, etc.
