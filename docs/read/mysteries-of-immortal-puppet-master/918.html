Chapter 918: Chapter 322: Earth Evil Shatters the Rock (Part 2)

"That ice coffin is the key!" Luo Si was instantly shaken, realizing this point in a split second.

His gaze immediately turned to Ning Zhuo, silently marveling, "As expected, he’s truly my wise lord, discovering the key to victory so quickly!"

Taking this opportunity, Luo Si hurriedly cast a spell to heal himself.

He had been holding back the Earth Evil God alone, almost without any time to rest. Now, with the chance, he naturally seized every second!

"Damn little brat!" the Earth Evil God roared, extremely furious.

Ning Zhuo, positioned behind Luo Si, kept firing mechanical Golden Swallows continuously.

The cost of these mechanical Golden Swallows was almost negligible to the Lord of Wangchuan. Ning Zhuo’s cultivation in the Foundation Establishment Stage was to him like an ant by his feet.

Yet, it was this very ant, utilizing these incredibly cheap mechanical Golden Swallows, that managed to hold back his Earth Evil God clone at this critical moment!

No wonder the Earth Evil God was so frustrated.

Luo Si felt greatly relieved!

Not only did he properly heal himself, but he also had the leisure to stabilize the situation with the Bronze War Chariot.

Otherwise, according to the previous state, the situation of the war chariot was extremely worrying, and Luo Si felt it quite reasonable that it could totally collapse in the next moment.

Earth Element—Earth Evil Shatters Rock.

The Earth Evil God extended his arms, palms facing the sky.

A torrent of mana gushed out from around him, releasing waves upon waves.

The waves of mana quickly spread, scattering through the massive miasma barrier that enveloped the entire altar battlefield.

The Earth-Lung Miasma boiled like it was being heated, violently tumbling, and rapidly condensed, forming gigantic rocks.

The rocks flew like rain, carrying Earth Evil, shooting towards the altar battlefield!

The malevolent energy of the flying stones surged fiercely, with impressive power. They varied in size; the larger ones were as big as dragons and elephants, the smaller ones were at least the size of Luo Si’s Bronze War Chariot.

A large number of flying stones crashed into the Golden Swallow Mechanism Group, causing massive casualties.

Luo Si drove the Bronze War Chariot, dodging here and there in the rain of flying stones, in quite a sorry state.

"If I got hit solidly by one, I might lose my life!" Ning Zhuo broke out in a cold sweat on his forehead, urgently throwing the Mechanical Token.

Mechanism—Iron Han Armor!

The token transformed into a mechanical battle armor.

Golden Swallow cleaves the clouds, Han Armor covers the body!

The Iron Han Armor disassembled, and the Five Elements Golden Core Wheel spun out, attaching to Ning Zhuo’s chest. The spinal bone adhered to his back.

Twelve Divine Splitting Mirrors simultaneously emitted silver light. Under the guidance of the silver light, the arm armor and leg armor precisely fitted into place.

The belt coiled around like a green snake, emitting spirit light, forming a light film that covered Ning Zhuo’s entire body.

Ka ka ka.

The helmet, chest armor, arm armor, leg armor, and the mechanical golden wings on the back, all connected, relatively contracting to fit Ning Zhuo’s physique perfectly.

With the help of the mechanical battle armor, Ning Zhuo darted out, dodging an Earth Evil Shattering Rock, officially beginning a mobile warfare.

Seeing this, Luo Si decisively ordered, "Qing Jiao Army, retreat beyond the miasma barrier!"

Qing Chi gritted her teeth, somewhat hesitant. She saw Ning Zhuo rush into the flying rocks, extremely worried, wanting to fight alongside her beloved!

Qing Yan, however, held Qing Chi’s shoulder, pulling her away, "You can’t fly, going there would only be a burden, get out quickly."

"I won’t!" Qing Chi refused, "If I’m going to die, I’ll die with Xiao Ma!"

Qing Yan exclaimed, "You silly girl, we will definitely win! Fate is on our side!"

"Luo Si’s military order is extremely correct!"

"We need to preserve the entire army, providing military power to those two. That’s what we should be doing. The role we play is also extremely crucial!!"

Qing Chi understood that her father was right and allowed herself to be dragged away.

As she was about to completely withdraw from Qujing, she struggled free, "I’ll stay in the passage to watch Xiao Ma fight. I’ll join the battle if necessary!"

Qing Yan hesitated for a moment, finally gritting his teeth, "Stupid girl, remember—you are our army’s commander, staying alive to command the Qing Jiao Army is more helpful to them!"

"Dad, take this!" Qing Chi took something from her bosom and tossed it to Qing Yan.

Qing Yan received it and saw it was a military equipment Festival Camp.

Qing Yan and Jiao Ma’s biological father took command of the Qing Jiao Army and returned to the previous Black Cloud Battlefield to garrison.

Qing Yan released the Festival Camp, allowing the Qing Jiao Army to garrison and rest.

With the military camp as a shield, the soldiers of the Qing Jiao Army felt much better.

Jiao Ma’s biological father then led a group out of the camp, dispersing around, actively cleaning the battlefield.

Previously they didn’t have time, now they need to plunder as many spoils as possible, to replenish their troops and transform them into military supplies.

Currently, the Qing Jiao Army’s military supplies are already in a crisis.

And with abundant military supplies, they can accomplish more. Whether it’s using the military arts [Money Ghost], [Life-saving Money], or serving as sacrifices on the altar to attract ghost gods for assistance, it’s all possible.

Ning Zhuo also noticed the Qing Jiao Army retreating.

He breathed a sigh of relief, giving a look of appreciation to Luo Si.

Luo Si’s situation was even more dangerous than his own, constantly receiving the Earth Evil God’s focused attention, yet he still commanded the battle with a very clear insight into the situation.

"But like this, I won’t be able to see the status of the Qing Jiao Army."

"If there is a chance later, I must plant Life Hanging Thread on Qing Chi and others first!"

The military unit was not with Ning Zhuo, which made him lose control over the Qing Jiao Army.

"Next, I need to allocate military power!" Ning Zhuo transmitted his thoughts to Luo Si.

Luo Si’s response was concise: "Received."

Just like Qing Yan said, the military power of the Qing Jiao Army is a crucial help. Without the leverage of military power, Ning Zhuo would have no way to pry open Nascent Soul level combat power.

And only by possessing such power, can he meet the minimum standard to join this incredibly dangerous battle.

Luo Si also needed military power assistance.

Without the support of Qing Jiao Army’s military power, he might have already been seriously injured at this moment.

The sky was pitch black, with countless yellow-brown shattering rocks heedlessly speeding everywhere.

Ning Zhuo, wearing Iron Han Armor, resembled a golden light, continuously refracting forward in the air.

From the start, he had unlocked the safety limit of Iron Han Armor, unleashing its maximum speed.

The flames shot from the Fire Tongue Formation were incredibly fierce, and each emergency turn made Iron Han Armor groan.

A gigantic rock the size of an elephant whistled towards him from the side.

Ning Zhuo mobilized his Divine Sense, throwing over a dozen mechanical Golden Swallows, directly colliding into it.

Bang bang bang...

A series of explosive sounds followed, with the mechanical Golden Swallows and the huge rock perishing together.

Countless rock shards flew everywhere, some still hitting Ning Zhuo.

Iron Han Armor was like being hammered continuously, with fine cracks appearing on the Earth Shield Array patterns.

Ning Zhuo went all out, barely maintaining his flight path. The speed of Iron Han Armor was too fast, and the side impact easily deflected the direction.

And in this rain of flying rocks, losing control of the direction would almost certainly result in a collision with a huge rock, ending similarly to the sacrificed mechanical Golden Swallows.

"Faster, just a bit faster!" Ning Zhuo clenched his teeth.

Iron Han Armor dashed crazily through the gaps in the gigantic rocks, constantly flirting with life and death.

The wind resistance was terrifying!

Even though the Golden Wings with the White Tiger Breaking Army Formation cut through the air, Ning Zhuo still felt that many times the entire mechanical battle armor suddenly plunged into sticky mud.

"No, these gigantic rocks are causing the trouble!"

Ning Zhuo immediately realized that some rocks exerted repulsive force, while others attracted objects. The dense Earth Evil emanating from the rocks was also negatively affecting the mechanical battle armor.

The black iron chest armor turned reddish, emitting a charred smell.

Under the influence of Earth Evil, the Earth Shield Array gradually failed, and the temperature inside the mechanical battle armor rapidly increased, like a steamer, making Ning Zhuo sweat profusely, soaking his clothes.

The Dual Yin Return Flow Array also began to have problems. The cushioning air film it formed was as thin as paper, every emergency turn bringing severe dull pain to Ning Zhuo’s body fitted into the armor.
