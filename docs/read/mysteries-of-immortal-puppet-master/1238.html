Chapter 1238: Chapter 478: Already at the Highest Heavens of Green Cloud (Part 2)

Zhong Dao looked at another person: "Lin Xiu, eighty-one boys and girls disappeared in the Iron Hook Mountain Range you’re in charge of. Those Demon Cultivators want to cultivate the ’Nine Sons Mother Blood Evil’, their purpose has long been evident, how come you haven’t achieved any results yet?"

Lin Xiu looked troubled: "Reporting to the Hall Master, I’ve already deployed personnel to search the caves of the Iron Hook Mountain Range, sealed off key routes, and looked for altars related to Blood Evil cultivation. It’s just, it’s just..."

"Shut up." Zhong Dao snorted coldly, "I ask you one thing—can these words you say satisfy the parents who lost their children?"

Zhong Dao’s sharp gaze turned to the third person.

"Yan Feng! In the Heavenly Snow Ice Field you manage, ’Ice Corpse Rebirth’ appeared! What are you guarding? Did these ancient ice corpses get exploited by evil outsiders? Are your array methods just empty appearances? Huh?!"

Yan Feng smiled wryly: "Hall Master, when the news reached me, I wanted to leave the mountain gate and return to guard the Heavenly Snow Ice Field. But isn’t it during the Feiyun Conference now? I can’t leave!"

"Hall Master!" Yan Feng continued, "Actually, we all know this is something unavoidable."

"Every time during the Feiyun Conference, our Myriad Images Sect withdraws its power to guard the Main Mountain Gate."

"Usually, the evil forces we suppress take the opportunity to emerge and rush to create chaos."

"It’s not just me, everyone actually has such concerns, Hall Master."

These words touched everyone’s hearts.

But Zhong Dao became even angrier. He slammed the table and growled, "Do you think only you’re smart, and this Hall Master doesn’t know the situation?!"

"Precisely because of this, precisely because evil forces seize the opportunity to do evil, we must show the strength of the Righteous Path, and deliver a heavy blow to them, ruthlessly killing these evil beings."

"Summoning you today, do you think it’s to hear your excuses or reasons?"

"No!"

"We must face these issues head-on, pool our wisdom, and solve these problems!"

Yan Feng and others were criticized to the point they couldn’t raise their heads.

At this moment, a majestic Platinum Gas Pillar shot up, piercing the sky.

Zhong Dao’s critical voice paused.

The cultivators all looked up, some turned around, and through the open hall doors, they saw the Platinum Gas Pillar.

"What’s happening?"

"Seems like... Vast Energy?!"

"Which Great Scholar made a move? Is it the renowned Zhao Great Scholar from Huazhang Country?"

Everyone speculated internally, gradually feeling the atmosphere becoming strange.

Thinking carefully, why hasn’t Hall Master Zhong Dao continued to criticize them?

They returned to focus and saw Zhong Dao squinting his eyes, gazing into the sky, his expression seemingly much relaxed.

"Not bad, not bad." Zhong Dao murmured.

Even though he personally issued the order to lend the White Rainbow Righteousness Festival to Ning Zhuo, Ning Zhuo’s stunning performance exceeded his expectations.

His previous suspicions were unfounded, instead, it further confirmed the prominence of this young talent Ning Zhuo!

Ning Zhuo not only has a strong sense of justice but also possesses mature and skilled methods. How rare is this?

With the emergence of such an excellent young star in the Righteous Path, Zhong Dao felt deeply satisfied.

"This child is impressive." Zhong Dao murmured, his lips slightly curved, revealing an extremely faint smile.

Astounding!

What did I just witness?

A Hall Master in a fit of anger actually smiled?!

Even though Zhong Dao’s smile was fleeting, those in the hall noticed it. Zhong Dao’s soft whispers were completely caught by their keen ears.

Instantly, a great wave surged in everyone’s hearts as they speculated who this mysterious young person could be that caught Zhong Dao’s favor and calmed his anger.

Surely linked to this Platinum Gas Pillar.

To be connected with Vast Energy, must be a member of our generation. No wonder favored by the Hall Master.

Who is this person?!

The key members of the Evil Slaying Hall all had the same thought; after this assembly, they must fully investigate and identify this individual.

This is a talent indeed!

To calm and make Hall Master Zhong Dao smile during this sensitive period, such a presence is practically a treasure!

This is still the Xingyun Trial; the main phase of the Feiyun Conference has yet to arrive.

During this period, the situation in the original jurisdiction is bound to be troublesome and full of bad news. Zhong Dao’s stance could be imagined, and so could everyone’s stress.

They desperately need Ning Zhuo!

The impact of the Platinum Gas Pillar is like a large rock plunging into the lake, with waves widely spreading.

Fuyao Peak.

Peak Master Lu Zhenshu wore a puzzled expression.

He had attended Zhao Hansheng’s public lectures, interacted privately with Zhao Hansheng, and even had a small spar, so he knew Zhao Hansheng’s aura.

"It’s not Zhao Hansheng, nor is it Duanmu Zhang’s aura."

"Could it be that Gu Qing?"

"If that’s the case, this young man is extraordinary."

Sword Cry Peak.

Peak Master Ling Juejian was honing his Sword Qi, looking up at the sky, brows slightly raised: "Vast Energy, indeed so grand and masculine. Who made the move?"

"If I exert the same force, can my Sword Qi cut this gas pillar?"

Ten Thousand Beasts Peak.

Tuoba Huang was squatting on the ground, feeding the chickens and dogs all around.

He too was drawn by the Platinum Gas Pillar, furrowed his brows, feeling quite displeased.

He knew Duanmu Zhang’s magical power aura, but the Platinum Gas Pillar obviously wasn’t.

"Is it the old scholar from Huazhang Country?"

"Brought his students to participate in the Feiyun Conference, yet never wanted to join our Myriad Images Sect. What do they take our sect for?"

"Now so flamboyant!"

"The old scholar is detestable, bullying our sect’s members, truly arrogant!"

Within the Platinum Gas Pillar, Ning Zhuo slowly opened his eyes.

He felt waves of excitement: "The White Rainbow Righteousness Festival responded to me, Vast Energy proved me."

"I really am a hero of the Righteous Path!"

Even through the Platinum Gas Pillar, Ning Zhuo could feel countless gazes, blazing and focused on him.

"With this event, I will be renowned in the Wanxiang Sect’s Main Mountain Gate, truly entering the eyes of the higher echelons!"

The Evil Extermination Hall lending the White Rainbow Righteousness Festival last night made Ning Zhuo sharply aware: this is a test from Zhong Dao, and simultaneously, it’s Ning Zhuo’s perfect opportunity.

Ning Zhuo knew how to endure, and more importantly how to seize opportunities!

An unexpected chance for fame lay before him, which he grasped tightly, exerting all his strength.

From the beginning to the end, he always believed in himself.

He succeeded.

At this moment, he was the brightest star in the scene, also drawing the gazes of countless cultivators outside, throughout the Wanxiang Sect’s Main Mountain Gate.

"At this moment, I’m virtually the center of attention worldwide!"

"The atmosphere has reached this point..."

With rising emotions, Ning Zhuo began to chant aloud—

"In the Xingyun Trial, I learn the spiritual Chapter, within the White Rainbow, my courage shines."

"Only the righteous energy fills the universe, why need historical tomes keep record of glory."

"The gates of Myriad Phenomena gain new talent, in front of the Evil Slaying Hall, the edge is honed."

"If you ask my lifelong wish, it’s already at the top of Green Cloud!"

In the midst of attention, Ning Zhuo recited a poem, at this precise moment, the platinum radiance gradually dissipated, revealing the true form of the great-headed youth.

He wore a white robe, untarnished by dust, his posture straight like a pine. His youthful face bore eyes as bright as stars, now flashing with a resolute and pure light.

Situ Gu slightly opened his mouth, inwardly marveling: "Righteously dignified... This child will definitely achieve greatness in the future!"

Gu Qing widened his eyes, utterly speechless.

The verses Ning Zhuo chanted played repeatedly in Gu Qing’s ears, making his mind buzz continuously.

He sensed trouble brewing but hadn’t expected Ning Zhuo to cause such a huge commotion!

He was completely dumbfounded.

Gu Qing paused, yet Ning Zhuo didn’t.

He continued crafting spirit paper.

His Vast Energy was nearly inexhaustible, tirelessly infused into the pulp, swiftly transforming it into sheets of Haoran Xuan.

Every single sheet of Haoran Xuan was Top-grade!

Gu Qing used his Vast Energy sparingly, in trickles. Cultivators like Kong Ran borrowed Situ Gu’s Vast Energy, which was diluted and hard to utilize.

Ning Zhuo’s Vast Energy was produced by himself, wielded effortlessly, and the output was so massive it bordered on cheating.
