Chapter 1303: Chapter 508: My Luoshu (Part 2)

All these five-colored dots are influenced by Ning Zhuo.

Blessed with wisdom, Ning Zhuo instantly mobilizes these dots, guiding them into his Five Elements Mana, which eventually converges into his multicolored palm.

The five-colored palm, bolstered by this power, undergoes a qualitative transformation, breaking through bottlenecks, and continuing the refining process of the Luoshu with astonishing efficiency, at least ten times faster than before!

Five Elements Realm, Ice Realm, Master Level Artifact Refining Realm, Master Level Array Formation Realm, Master Level Mechanical Techniques Realm...

These realms help Ning Zhuo understand the principles of Luoshu, turning abstract concepts into concrete, detailed, and rich examples.

Divine Refining, Magic Refining, Blood Refining, these three refining methods consecutively achieve qualitative changes.

After 30 minutes.

The vibration of the Luoshu Book Pages comes to a halt, completely tranquil.

In Ning Zhuo’s Divine Sea, those remaining black and white dots no longer swirl frantically but quietly condense into the shape of the book pages.

An enigmatic connection forms between Ning Zhuo and the Luoshu.

It’s not a master-servant connection but a "resonance"—he understands the Luoshu, and the Luoshu accepts him. From now on, he can unleash the complete power of the Luoshu, wielding a computational force far beyond his capability limits!

"Succeeded! Hahaha." Sun Lingtong stands with hands on hips, throwing back his head in laughter, but his vigorous motion causes his vision to black out momentarily, almost making him fall backward.

Throughout the refining process, he exerted all kinds of Stealing Techniques, providing immense assistance to Ning Zhuo.

"Yes, it succeeded." Ning Zhuo murmurs.

The book pages remain jade-like white, but in his heart, they are entirely different. Those black and white dots are no longer chaotic but orderly; their circulating trajectories are no longer vague and variable but clear and discernible.

This is his Luoshu now.

Two hours have passed, Zhao Hansheng ascends the platform again.

He wears a blue robe, his face gaunt, surrounded by an ethereal Literary Energy.

His gaze sweeps over the dense crowd below, and Zhao Hansheng slightly nods.

His gaze imperceptibly pauses on Ning Zhuo.

Everyone is eagerly awaiting, knowing today is Zhao Hansheng’s last public lecture on Heart Learning.

Zhao Hansheng begins speaking, his voice not loud yet distinctly reaching every ear.

The crowd below listens intently.

Ning Zhuo is no exception. His eyes slightly squint, Divine Sense working with full power, memorizing Zhao Hansheng’s every word.

Traditional Confucian Cultivation emphasizes "Observing Things and Gaining Knowledge", seeking reason externally; whereas Heart Learning advocates "Heart is Reason", seeking internally.

After several classes, this point no longer surprises the cultivators but immerses them further into Heart Learning.

Some ponder, some frown in deep thought, some nod frequently.

After two hours, the class approaches its end.

The session for open questions begins again.

Ning Zhuo actively asks a question, and the overseeing Confucian Cultivator immediately permits him upon seeing him stand up.

All eyes focus on him, each with a subtle expression.

Ning Zhuo’s fame rose significantly before, practically stepping up over Gu Qing. Gu Qing is Zhao Hansheng’s prized student, and it’s well known the relationship between Ning Zhuo and the mentor and student is tense.

Hence, when Ning Zhuo stands, he instantly draws all the attention. Everyone is anticipating Ning Zhuo’s question and even more Zhao Hansheng’s response.

Zhao Hansheng’s gaze settles on Ning Zhuo, his expression calm: "Young friend Ning Zhuo, what question do you have?"

Ning Zhuo clasps his hands in a salute, staring directly into Zhao Hansheng’s eyes, and slowly speaks: "With boldness, I wish to consult Mister about — how does one practice the Knowledge and Action Integration Skill?"

Countless gazes shift between Ning Zhuo and Zhao Hansheng.

Some are excited, some curious, some gloating, some watching coldly.

Zhao Hansheng pauses momentarily.

He knows clearly that without providing a proper answer, he would surely diminish his own reputation, even his credibility.

All the fame and credibility he accumulated over several public lectures would be at risk.

"The Knowledge and Action Integration Skill requires Confucian Cultivation foundation, but telling him wouldn’t matter." Zhao Hansheng muses, knowing Ning Zhuo is not purely a Confucian Cultivator.

The Knowledge and Action Integration Skill is derived from Heart Learning and is recently created, only available in Confucian Skill versions. Unlike methods like Awl-Thorn Vigilance Art, which has ancient versions suitable for almost all cultivators and Confucian versions exclusively for Confucian Scholars.

Yet, Zhao Hansheng harbors doubts in his heart.

This isn’t the first time Ning Zhuo has asked about the Knowledge and Action Integration Skill, but the last time...

It’s deeply imprinted in Zhao Hansheng’s memory — his Jade Pendant vibrated, warning him!

"What if I declare the Knowledge and Action Integration Skill?"

Zhao Hansheng contemplates.

And at that moment, the Jade Pendant hanging from his waist vibrates again!

Zhao Hansheng’s pupils slightly constrict.

Yet his demeanor remains calm, impassive, revealing no emotion.

After a pause, Zhao Hansheng slowly speaks, his voice still composed: "The Knowledge and Action Integration Skill... this skill is the secret transmission of Heart Learning, not something easily conveyed in words. Although I know a bit, I dare not claim mastery. If the young friend is interested, perhaps begin with the Chuanxi Lu, after thoroughly reading, further exploration can be pursued."

"Mister," Ning Zhuo adds, "I have already read the Chuanxi Lu in full, yet it only speaks of principles, not methods. I am dull, unsure of how to turn principles into methods, earnestly asking for Mister’s guidance."

Of course, Ning Zhuo does not want to let go of this opportunity easily, pressing forward.

Zhao Hansheng takes a gentle breath, his gaze distant, briefly looking at the far mountain peaks, then back to Ning Zhuo. He says warmly: "Young friend is talented, why persist in one method? Understand the principles, methods will naturally be clear. Perhaps read several more times, delve deeply, and insights might arise."

Then, without waiting for Ning Zhuo’s further inquiry, he lightly flicks his sleeve: "Next."

The crowd below begins whispering amongst themselves.

Zhao Hansheng’s obvious evasive response greatly lowers their impression of him.

Despite Zhao Hansheng carrying the air of a revered teacher, with a selfless demeanor, when it involves personal grievances, he remains discerning!

Zhao Hansheng sighs internally, having anticipated the crowd’s reaction. One could say Ning Zhuo’s inquiry diminished at least thirty percent of his efforts over the past days.

But the Jade Pendant warns again, Zhao Hansheng understands the need for caution!

Though unaware of the reason why imparting to Ning Zhuo triggers warnings, opting for lesser harm, Zhao Hansheng doesn’t hesitate, once again evading Ning Zhuo’s question.

"Alas!!!" Ning Zhuo deeply sighs, having no choice but to sit down again.

Even knowing his hopes were slim, experiencing the empty disappointment brought a touch of sadness.

The Knowledge and Action Integration Skill is very useful to him.

Even the Confucian version holds great reference value. There might be a possibility to refine this skill in the future, creating a version usable by himself.

Of course, Ning Zhuo’s current foundation is too weak.

"But as long as I absorb some high-grade Confucian Demon Souls, these problems can be fundamentally resolved."

Thoughts of Confucian Demon Souls bring Ning Zhuo’s mind to Qin De.

Confucian Scholars are also discussing Qin De.

"The Saint Thief Scripture, in the name of saints, practices grand theft. He thoroughly read Confucian classics yet read this evil book." Duanmu Zhang deeply sighs.

Situ Gu says: "Qin De’s Saint Thief Scripture, its core argument is ’the saints are the greatest thieves’. He finds countless examples from the classics, proving sages through the ages have ’stolen’ heavenly principles, people’s hearts, and Qi Fortune. This twisted reasoning is indeed difficult to refute using our traditional Confucian theories."

"However, Heart Learning emphasizes inner exploration and conscientiousness. This is in complete opposition to Qin De’s ’external theft’. I believe if Zhao Hansheng debated scripture with Qin De, he could theoretically defeat him directly!"

Song Taosheng continues: "And once defeated, the obstacles hindering our Confucian Cultivator Group’s development will be swept away."

Even though Zhao Hansheng publicly rejects Ning Zhuo’s consultation, causing his reputation to plummet, in Duanmu Zhang, Situ Gu, and the newly inclined Song Taosheng’s view, this is trivial.

The development of Myriad Images Sect’s Main Mountain Gate Confucian Cultivator Group is the major concern!

Discussions about this have already taken place in several rounds.

Today is Zhao Hansheng’s final public lecture, also the moment for the Confucian Cultivators to conclude their judgments.

Go consult Zhao Hansheng!
