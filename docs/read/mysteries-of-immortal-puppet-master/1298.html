Chapter 1298: Chapter 506: Obtaining the Tome

This is the strategy set by Sun Ning and the other!

It doesn’t matter if you can’t calculate it, then just interfere with the Water Array, making what you calculated unable to be realized.

Sun Lingtong was thrilled, his face full of excitement. He wanted to shout for Ning Zhuo but worried he might disturb him, so he could only applaud Ning Zhuo in his heart.

The Array Heart of the Luoshu Water Array is indeed powerful as it is a page of the Taoist Artifact Luoshu. But Sun Ning and the other have their advantages.

One of their advantages is secretly controlling the Shuyu Pavilion. With the array in this Cave Mansion, Sun Ning and the other can prevail with force!

On the other hand, although the Luoshu Water Array’s Array Heart is extremely powerful, the Array Base constructing this Water Array is just those damaged treasures.

The Water Array’s foundation is quite thin!

Sun Ning and the other fully exploited their advantages, pressuring the entire Water Array.

The rotation speed of the Water Vortex became slower and slower, while the Array Patterns became more chaotic.

No matter how the Luoshu Book Pages responded, ultimately its comprehensive strength couldn’t match the opposite side, somewhat overtaxing it.

A fine crack suddenly appeared on the surface of the Water Vortex!

This is the result of the array’s operations being sluggish, reaching a certain stage resulting in an energy fracture.

It’s now!

Ning Zhuo’s eyes shot out brilliance, both hands emerged, ten fingers dancing like butterflies.

Streaks of Mana shot from his fingertips, swiftly etching Array Patterns on the surface of the Water Vortex.

Array inside an Array!

Sun Lingtong’s eyes widened, holding his breath. He knew this move by Ning Zhuo was crucial, as if he could successfully build this array, then even if it failed, it wouldn’t make a huge commotion.

Master-level Array Realm was fully exhibited by Ning Zhuo!

Various methods for setting up an array emerged in an endless stream, revealing the gains Ning Zhuo had received from sweeping arrays previously.

Soon, beads of sweat oozed from the forehead of the big-headed youth, yet his fingers remained as stable as a rock.

One breath, ten breaths, twenty-seven breaths.

When the last Array Pattern was laid down.

Buzz...

The Water Vortex suddenly shook and then slowly stopped rotating.

The Luoshu Book Pages still calculated frantically, but its results could no longer be transmitted to the original array — because the array had been "taken over" by Ning Zhuo’s new array.

And the function of the new array is — useless!

It can be said that Luoshu Book Pages were hollowed out and isolated.

A hint of joy flashed across Sun Lingtong’s face, but soon, he regained a solemn expression.

Because the new array began to change!

Array Patterns twisted, Array Line regrouped, Array Eye shifted — everything happened in an instant. The speed was even faster than the previous Water Vortex.

Sun Lingtong couldn’t help but be shocked: "Turns out, the computational ability of Luoshu Book Pages had never been fully exerted."

Ever since Sun Ning and the other sneaked into Shuyu Pavilion, Sun Lingtong had been studying the Luoshu Book Pages and the surrounding Water Array. Now, upon taking formal action, it truly forced out the upper limit of the Luoshu Book Pages’ abilities.

The new array changed increasingly fast.

Ning Zhuo wore a heavy expression.

The Luoshu Book Pages are the source of numerics; the array Ning Zhuo set up inside the Water Array would surely be comprehended, penetrated, and eroded.

Even if this was Ning Zhuo’s own Mana, it couldn’t hold on for long.

The new array trembled violently, and the Array Patterns twisted like mad.

The Luoshu Book Pages were attacking with full force, trying to take over the new array at maximum speed. Meanwhile, the external Water Array was also exerting force inward.

"After all, it lacks Spiritual Wisdom, only instinct. Luoshu Book Pages, come to me!" Ning Zhuo muttered softly in his heart.

He activated the Array Gap Traveling Shuttle, first following the Water Array’s gaps, rushing through the Array inside an Array towards the Luoshu Book Pages.

The Book Pages subtly trembled, suddenly its form became ethereal, after a brief blur, it split into ten parts.

Nine fakes and one real!

Ning Zhuo took a deep breath, his mind surged with thoughts like lightning, exhausting fiercely, granting him ultimate speed in thinking.

He used the Breaking Array Treasure in his hand one after another, scattering them into the Water Array.

The phantoms of the Book Pages were shattered one after the other, with seven dispersed, at the last moment, there were still three remaining.

The Breaking Array Treasure in Ning Zhuo’s hand was already depleted, in the time taken to recover them, the Book Pages could regroup, reappearing with ten illusions.

At this moment, Sun Lingtong’s heart inevitably hung high!

Because he knew, if this fails, it would be necessary to find another way to plot for the Luoshu Book Pages later. Since the series of changes this time have already become Luoshu’s computational data.

Thus, breaking the Water Array and seizing the Luoshu will become increasingly difficult tier by tier.

"Which one of these three is real?"

At this instant, Ning Zhuo’s gaze was like lightning, aware of his plight — he must immediately choose one. Selecting the true form would achieve great success. Choosing wrongly would be fruitless.

He had no more time, not even a little bit, to think. Fortunately, in this critical moment, the Master Level realm triggered his Spiritual Nature, bringing forth a rush of intuition from his heart.

Yet this intuition was very weak because Ning Zhuo had been basking in the resonance of Luoshu Daoist for breaking the array.

Even as intuition rose, other feelings arose, like viewing flowers in the mist.

At this crucial moment, Ning Zhuo’s experience from sweeping arrays played a role, allowing him to precisely capture the intuitive sensation.

"Great, it’s you!" Ning Zhuo acted decisively.

Mana was sent forth, transforming into a hand imbued with Five Elements Mana, fiercely charging into the Water Array, breaking through the Array inside an Array, directly reaching for the deepest part of the Water Vortex.

The hand with Five Elements Mana directly grabbed the left page.

Luoshu struggled, its essence displayed, indeed it was its true body!

Ning Zhuo took a deep breath, his Divine Sense mobilized, retracting the Five Elements hand.

The Luoshu Book Pages instinctively struggled, even using Dao resonance to erode the hand of Five Elements Mana.
