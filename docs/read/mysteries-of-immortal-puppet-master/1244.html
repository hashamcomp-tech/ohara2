Chapter 1244: Chapter 481: Mu Lan’s Thoughts of Her Husband (2)

Most of the cultivators remained at the scene.

It’s not that they didn’t want to leave, but rather the Monster Path principles took hold of them. They were using Li Guanyu’s spiritual wine, entering a state of drunkenness to release these temporary principles.

Red Mist Valley.

Inside the valley, the glow was even more dazzling than the sunset.

Within the great array, the stalemate of battle had persisted for over ten days.

"Release the arrows!"

Mu Lan shouted fiercely, and the Blood Tide Crossbow from the Red Flower Camp fired in unison. Countless arrows shot into the sky, converging into a blood-colored rainbow.

The rainbow charged fiercely like it was piercing through the sun.

But upon hitting the formation’s light wall, most of it was immediately swallowed, and the remaining power merely shook the array slightly.

A cultivator with a fierce ghost mask sneered, "This Blood Rust Cage Formation specifically counters your Red Flower Army, General Mu Lan, you have absolutely no chance of winning."

"Just surrender obediently."

"If you were within the borders of Liangzhu Country, you might have someone come to aid you. But here, it is beyond Liangzhu’s borders!"

Mu Lan’s face remained calm, her eyes flashed sharply.

This time, she had received imperial orders, setting out from Liangzhu Country and heading straight towards Flying Cloud Country. On one hand, it was the monarch’s command, and on the other hand, she took the opportunity to rendezvous with Ning Zhuo for a deal involving the Ghost Dao Golden Core.

She traveled in disguise, strictly safeguarding the secrecy of the marching route, yet she still fell into an ambush at Red Mist Valley.

On this mission, Mu Lan naturally didn’t bring the entire Red Flower Camp but selected some elites to protect the delegation.

As a result, they were trapped here, having failed several attempts and unable to shake the Blood Rust Cage Formation.

The formation launched a frenzy of attacks, with Mu Lan holding the front, bearing the greatest pressure.

Her movements gradually slowed, her spear technique no longer as fierce as before.

The ghost-masked cultivator seized the opportunity, flicked his sleeve, and shot out a ghastly bone arrow.

The arrow grazed Mu Lan’s left arm, causing her arm armor to shatter, and blood soaked her clothes.

"General!" Zhang Zhongyi gritted his teeth. Although he was Mu Lan’s elder, he only considered himself a subordinate in the army.

"Retreat to the heart of the valley, set up the Turtle Shell Array!" Mu Lan’s voice was hoarse, her aura declining.

The soldiers of the Red Flower Camp followed orders, contracting and using the crossbow carriage as the core, with shields layered on top of each other. However, the Blood Rust Cage Formation continuously extracted the blood qi of everyone, leaving the whole army pale-faced, showing signs of being unable to hold on for long.

The ghost-masked cultivator laughed heartily. With a wave of his hand, two squads immediately entered the array, charging from the left and right.

"Mu Lan, today marks the extinction of your General Mu Mansion’s lineage!"

The situation was like eggshells on the verge of breaking, but the entire army had no wavering, all showing the resolve to fight to the death.

Mu Lan sat cross-legged in the formation, closing her eyes to control her breathing.

The cries of battle approached gradually, first a hundred paces away, then fifty paces, and finally thirty paces.

Mu Lan abruptly opened her eyes, and at that moment, her gaze was sharp as a knife.

Mu Lan slowly rose, decisively removing her broken arm armor, leaving only her inner red garment. Her jet black hair danced wildly in the blood wind, with strands sticking to her forehead, making her face even paler than paper.

Yet she stood tall and straight.

She raised her hand, and the Red Phoenix Bow drew open like a full moon.

The ghost-faced daoist’s expression drastically changed, "Impossible, can she shoot a third arrow?! Stop her quickly!"

Divine Skill — Red Heart Arrow!

There was no arrow on the bowstring.

Mu Lan’s essence blood condensed between the bowstring, forming into a blood arrow.

In the next moment, Mu Lan exhaled and shouted, the bowstring vibrated like the clang of a phoenix!

With nearly all her heart’s blood as the guide, a fearless, inevitable arrow!

A crimson divine light pierced through the formation wall and then rapidly spread. More than three hundred cultivators presiding over the array at the array eye screamed in unison, most bleeding from their seven orifices, dying on the spot!

The momentum of the arrow light did not diminish, directly shooting at the ghost-faced daoist.

The ghost-faced daoist spat blood and flew backward, his chest burst with a large bloodhole. Although he was a Nascent Soul level cultivator, his full protection under the Red Heart Arrow was as fragile as paper.

But the shock in his heart was not entirely from this divine skill, but rather—

"You... you’ve already cracked the weak point of the formation?!"

Mu Lan shouted coldly and fiercely, "Red Flower Camp, attack with me!"

The formation was broken, the enemy army suffered severe backlash, it was the perfect opportunity.

The morale of the Red Flower Camp soared like a rainbow, launching a full counterattack.

The entire army erupted!

Blood Tide Crossbow firepower fully opened, the enemy army without the formation’s protection fell in swathes.

Mu Lan charged forward with her spear.

The Blood-tassel Spear transformed into a bright red lightning, with each thrust of the spear tip, an enemy cultivator’s throat would spray blood. Her spear technique was unadorned but terrifyingly precise.

With her as the spearhead, she directly attacked the weak points of the enemy, tearing through key nodes of the enemy army.

"Stop her!" The ghost-faced daoist struggled to get up, blood oozing from his mouth and nose, disregarding everything. His mana surged as he infused it into the array plate in his hand, trying to reorganize the formation.

Mu Lan was already charging over!

The spear light cut through three Core Formation cultivators, its momentum smashing the array plate in the ghost-faced daoist’s hand.

The enemy formation was completely broken!

"Retreat... retreat!" The ghost-faced daoist gritted his teeth and ordered, turning into blood light to flee.

"Where do you think you’re going?!" Above Mu Lan’s head appeared the Red Flower Dharma Image.

The dharma image, seemingly slow yet swift, descended onto the ghost-faced daoist, capturing and sealing him on the spot!

The surrounding sounds of battle gradually subsided.

The overall situation was settled.

Mu Lan stood in place, no longer making any moves.

"This ambush definitely has insiders involved."

"Even though Prince Qiming gifted me army units, there are undoubtedly still people in the court yet to relent against my General Mu Mansion."

The sealed enemy leader was encapsulated within the flying red flower, slowly landing in Mu Lan’s hand.

She immediately sensed it, not just the one before her but also two others afar.

"Flying Cloud Country, husband, it’s not easy to see you..." In Mu Lan’s Divine Sea, the image of a big-headed boy appeared, seemingly smiling and sighing.

At the Wanxiang Sect’s Main Mountain Gate.

Bluestone Cave Mansion.

"Alas." Ning Zhuo furrowed his brows tightly, holding a red flower in his hand, and sighed softly.

The once red flower had transformed into an ominous purple-black, with Wu Hen inside no longer human-shaped, resembling a tumor.

The petals grew thinner, while the tumor inside stubbornly continued to grow.

"Why does it feel like Monster Path principles are rapidly proliferating?"

This couldn’t be casually disposed of.

Ning Zhuo knew well: this was a clear clue. If mishandled and known to outsiders, it would surely lead back to him swiftly.

Currently, he’s in the limelight but holds many advantages due to unclear intelligence.

Once his origins, background, and history are thoroughly investigated, Ning Zhuo would fall into a passive situation.

Therefore, Chef came to the Myriad Images Sect under a hidden identity. Qing Chi stayed by Ning Zhuo’s side, seldom showing himself, occasionally appearing as a foreshadowing for Ning Zhuo’s future identity reveal in White Paper Immortal City.

Everything was planned.

"The Red Flower like Thunder Pearl, who knows when it might self-destruct."

"What a pity for Wu Hen."

Originally, Ning Zhuo’s plan was to put him on trial, to inquire about how he cultivated the Monster Path.

But given the current situation, it is evident that Wu Hen is done for, and this valuable clue is also wasted.

Ning Zhuo pondered for a moment, deciding to place it in the cave mansion, under the strict surveillance of Chef, Gongsun Yan, and Qing Chi.

"The priority is still the fifth round in the Confucian Cultivator trial."

"Thanks to Zhong Dao lending me the White Rainbow Righteousness Festival, my current situation is much better than anticipated."

"Striving to grasp the manufacturing method of the Inheriting Dao Jade Page in the fifth round, that’s my main goal!"

Ning Zhuo never forgot his initial resolve.

In his view, Gu Qing is merely an obstacle, a nuisance, not the true target.

"Conclusion of the fifth round also marks the end of the third Confucian Cultivator trial."

"Regardless of whether I win or lose ultimately, it doesn’t matter. Gu Qing is a Confucian Cultivator, and the overseeing cultivator is his mentor, winning is normal. On the contrary, losing to an outsider like me would be embarrassing."

"In fact, having gone through the first four rounds, his reputation and stature have been significantly undermined by me."

Currently, Ning Zhuo places importance not only on the manufacturing method of the Inheriting Dao Jade Page but also on the second Xingyun Trial at the Evil Slaying Hall.

He was greatly elevated by Zhong Dao, not without cost.

When publicly returning the White Rainbow Righteousness Festival, Ning Zhuo reached a political consensus with Zhong Dao.

In the second Xingyun Trial at the Evil Slaying Hall, he needs to participate most actively, both to show his stance and support.

Victory in attaining the Inheriting Dao Jade Page is of no matter. But in the second trial at the Evil Slaying Hall, Ning Zhuo knows he must strive for first place!
