Chapter 998: Chapter 362: I’m a Good Young Man Who Heals the Sick! (Part 3)

He was dispatched by the Lin Family to assist Lin Jinglong, not precisely as a combatant, but to provide technical support.

However, even so, Gongsun Yan, due to his innate talent and equipment, still ranked among the top in combat power among Lin Jinglong’s subordinates.

Ning Zhuo, aware yet speechless, reached out his hand and directly grabbed Gongsun Yan’s head, like holding a ball.

"Damn it, this Demon Cultivator might be trying to use Soul Search on me!" Gongsun Yan’s heart leapt to his throat immediately.

Yet Ning Zhuo simply employed mana and divine sense to inspect Gongsun Yan’s internal condition.

Gongsun Yan had no power to resist and could only let Ning Zhuo’s divine sense sweep through his body, revealing everything inside.

"You’re indeed seriously ill. Don’t be afraid, I can cure you." Ning Zhuo showed him a friendly smile.

He then took out his Lifebound Mechanism Fan.

Gongsun Yan’s eyes widened, his upper body instinctively leaned back, trying to distance himself from Ning Zhuo.

Unsuccessfully.

Gongsun Yan threatened through gritted teeth, "Wait, wait! You should know, I’ve already joined the Lin Family. If you act against me, consider Young Master Lin Jinglong, consider the Lin Family of Mo Gao Peak!"

Ning Zhuo remained deaf to his words, merely waving the Five Elements Mechanism Fan.

His Lifebound Techniques, the Five Elements Life and Death Technique, instantly took effect!

Gongsun Yan’s five elements within were unbalanced, causing various illnesses, but under the influence of the Five Elements Life and Death Technique, there was a dramatic change.

Gongsun Yan’s body shook, eyes widened, initially showing a bewildered expression.

Yet the bodily response was genuine.

It was like a dry desert suddenly encountering a torrential rain.

In the rain, the fire-attribute receded significantly, the Water Element irrigated, Earth Element nourished, Wood Element soared, Gold Element flourished, finally achieving the balance of the Five Elements.

His bronze skin noticeably dulled, and the dark golden hues of his muscles and bones showed common luster.

The power of spring thunder is about destruction followed by rebirth, healing him. Ning Zhuo’s Five Elements Fan rebuilt the Five Elements balance from the root. The former addresses symptoms but not the root, while the latter directly solves the fundamental issue!

These are completely two levels of solutions.

Gongsun Yan looked at Ning Zhuo in disbelief, "How did you do that?"

How could his internal five-element balance be rebuilt in an instant?

Gongsun Yan had long been troubled by this, having tried countless methods. Ultimately, he reluctantly chose to join the Lin Family precisely because implementing the treatment plan to rebuild the five-element balance was too difficult.

Ning Zhuo smiled, "It’s quite simple, just a breeze from the fan."

"You’re lucky, I happen to have a Five Elements Fan like this."

Gongsun Yan’s gaze turned fervent, staring intently at Ning Zhuo’s Lifebound Magical Treasure, momentarily stunned.

Ning Zhuo continued to introduce himself, "My name is Ning Zhuo, I believe Daoist Gongsun Yan might have heard of me."

Gongsun Yan was dazed for a moment before reacting.

"Ning Zhuo? You are Ning Zhuo?!" He was no longer that fearful, but instead questioned out loud, "Do you know that acting like this against me violates sect rules!"

"We agreed to resolve conflicts in the Martial Arts Hall."

"And yet you secretly infiltrated my Cave Mansion and attacked me..."

Ning Zhuo interrupted, "No, I came to heal you."

Gongsun Yan sneered, "Sophistry..."

Ning Zhuo spread his hands, interrupting again, "Just say, does it work well or not?"

Gongsun Yan’s expression froze, then he gritted his teeth, "Do you think it’s that easy to heal me? I can fix my physical ailments, but how do I dispel the various restlessness, anger, and distractions in my Divine Sea? I still need to use Thunderstruck Wood!"

Ning Zhuo was obviously prepared, without hesitation, he waved the fan again.

This time, the wind directly affected Gongsun Yan’s Upper Dantian, consolidating all the restlessness, anger, and so forth in his Divine Sea, completely sweeping them away.

Ning Zhuo smiled, "How is it?"

Gongsun Yan opened his mouth wide, utterly stunned, staring blankly at Ning Zhuo... and the Mechanical Treasure Fan in his hand.
