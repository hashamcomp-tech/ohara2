Chapter 609: Chapter 171: I am your boss, you know_2

The Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat’s hull was not just trembling wildly, this time it was shaking violently, as if it wanted to break free from the five-colored radiant light.

But Ning Zhuo’s hands were firmly pressing down on the hull, preventing it from shaking loose.

The magic prohibition of the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat expanded directly to three times its previous size, filling the cabin and making it blindingly bright. To the average person, all that could be seen was a vast expanse of white!

Ning Zhuo’s vision was hindered, but his Divine Sense constantly monitored the boat and the people’s condition.

“The Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat has reached its limit!” Ning Zhuo noticed that many cracks had appeared on the body of the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat.

The spatial rifts almost came to a standstill.

Almost!

Ning Zhuo couldn’t help but clench his teeth in frustration.

Suddenly, he thought of a certain Demon God.

As the thought emerged, he scattered his Divine Sense and controlled the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon.

Cabin doors flew open, a beaded bracelet was lifted by an invisible force, and was sent straight to Ning Zhuo’s side.

As it turned out, ever since the Dice Bone Demon God was subdued by Ning Zhuo, it had transformed into a dice bracelet.

Before fully confirming its loyalty while pretending to be the Old Monster in Stone, Ning Zhuo had placed this bracelet in an empty cabin of the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon in advance.

“Dice Bone Demon God, hastily deploy your dojo and accept us all,” Ning Zhuo commanded.

The bracelet immediately changed, reverting to the Dice Bone Demon God.

It prostrated on the ground: “In front of the master, this old servant dares not call himself a Demon God. Master rest assured, this old servant will definitely spare no effort!”

In the next moment, the dice dojo unfolded and enveloped Sun Ning, both persons, the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat, and the Stone Ball within it.

A dice appeared above Sun Lingtong’s head, which after spinning for a while, turned into a six.

The Dice Bone Demon God’s eyes, also dice, rolled out two ones.

“What?!” The Demon God was utterly baffled.

How could such a thing happen after swearing allegiance to Ning Zhuo?

But fortunately, the dice above Ning Zhuo’s head was also a six.

Thus, six plus one plus one equals eight, which is greater than Sun Lingtong’s single six.

The spatial rifts on Sun Lingtong’s body were suppressed, and the Stone Skin continued to thicken, its speed rising by a few strands.

While it was just a trivial increase, it genuinely created a qualitative change!

The spatial rifts finally stopped eroding and expanding, completely motionless.

Ning Zhuo was finally satisfied and stopped harming the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat.

Crack, crack.

The Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat was covered in cracks all over.

The Dice Bone Demon God was also half-kneeling on the ground, with cracks appearing on its skeletal body. Even the dice that served as its eyeballs had shattered one.

Its power was greatly damaged!

“Old Dice, you did well,” Ning Zhuo reassured it.

The Dice Bone Demon God immediately expressed its willingness to undertake any risks for Ning Zhuo, even if it meant walking through fire or boiling water!

“Come back.”

At Ning Zhuo’s command, the Dice Bone Demon God transformed again, turning into a bracelet and willingly placed itself on Ning Zhuo’s wrist.

Ning Zhuo enveloped Sun Lingtong with his Divine Sense, reconfirming that the latter’s injuries had been stabilized.

All of the spatial rifts were suppressed, no longer spreading or proliferating.

“The boss absorbed that marrow and needs time to digest it.”

“In this regard, I cannot offer assistance.”

“But as long as his body doesn’t collapse, there is still hope!”

After confirming that Sun Lingtong’s injuries were stable, Ning Zhuo sat down on the ground with a flop.

His whole body was drained, his clothes drenched with sweat, feeling weak and feeble all over.

For a time, he sat on the ground, not wanting to move at all.

Mulu Town.

Shuang Ling also collapsed onto the Magic Platform, disheveled and glaring furiously: “Not dead!”

“The Old Monster in Stone was hit by my Divine Power and he still isn’t dead!!”

“He’s still alive.”

“Impossible!”

“This time, in pursuit of a sure kill, I used at least ten National Power Bricks!”

Shuang Ling’s connection to the Storage Bag remained, still faintly present.

But the location wasn’t stable, just chaos. As long as she carefully sensed it, her sense of direction would still get confused.

Shuang Ling was not unfamiliar with this situation: “The Old Monster in Stone is located in the void. Within the void, there are neither concepts of direction such as front, back, left, right, nor southeast, northwest, nor the concept of distance. Hence, such an illusion arises.”

“The Old Monster in Stone can withstand my Star Falling Heavenly Punishment and cross through the void with his flesh body…”

“Does this mean he is… at the Divinity Transformation Level?!”

Shuang Ling’s expression revealed a trace of shock.

If the Thousand Peak Forest side acquired a second individual at the Divinity Transformation Level, it would inevitably have a strong impact on the entire war situation.

Such significant military intelligence naturally could not be neglected by Shuang Ling.

She suppressed the indignation and anger in her heart and, after descending from the Magic Platform, immediately rushed to the Central Army’s tent to see Commander Du Tiechuan.

Upon hearing Shuang Ling’s report, Du Tiechuan’s face was full of solemnity as he said in a deep voice: “Divinity Transformation Level!”

“With such a performance from the Old Monster in Stone, he is undoubtedly at the Divinity Transformation Level.”

“Princess, your contribution this time is not small. To have uncovered such military intelligence is extremely important for our army.”

Shuang Ling’s face was pale, without much pride: “The Old Monster in Stone has taken my platform and treasures, most of my possessions are in his hands now.”

“I sensed his final stopping point, right here!”

As she said this, Shuang Ling, using the map of Thousand Peak Forest in the tent, pointed to a certain location with her finger.

Commander Du Tiechuan followed her gesture: “Bikong Mountain?”

“The Golden Spear Army is about to arrive at Bikong Mountain now. I’ll immediately send them this latest intelligence, telling them to be extremely vigilant against the Old Monster in Stone!”

As he spoke, Commander Du Tiechuan fixed his gaze upon Shuang Ling: “Princess, are you sure that as long as the Old Monster in Stone leaves the void, you can sense it?”

Shuang Ling: “As long as I conduct the ritual on the Magic Platform, as long as the Old Monster in Stone hasn’t broken my methods, as long as he hasn’t left Liangzhu Country, I can sense him.”

“To be precise, it’s the sensing of my many platforms!”

Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon.

Sun Lingtong used Divine Sense to contact Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo joyfully hastened to the presence of the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat.

It was uncertain if it was a misperception, but upon “sighting” Ning Zhuo, the shattered Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat trembled slightly.

Ning Zhuo transmitted thoughts through Divine Sense: “Boss, are you awake? How is your recovery? How do you feel?”

Sun Lingtong had already used Divine Sense to check his body and, with a quick turn of thought, guessed that he must have been saved by Ning Zhuo.

But he did not utter any words of gratitude.

Given the relationship between Ning Zhuo and him, there was simply no need for such words.

Sun Lingtong said: “I’m feeling quite bored right now!”

“Hurry up and let me out, Little Zhuo.”

“But Boss, your injuries…” Ning Zhuo scanned Sun Lingtong’s entire body with Divine Sense and was surprised to find that the latter’s injuries were completely healed!

“Hahaha!” Sun Lingtong transmitted thoughts, “Who do I think I am?! I am Sun Lingtong, your boss’ tooth! These minor injuries are nothing at all.”

Ning Zhuo nodded repeatedly, giving a thumbs up: “Boss, you truly deserve to be my boss!”

About the earlier incident of Sun Lingtong vomiting blood and lapsing into unconsciousness, Ning Zhuo didn’t mention a word.

That Sun Lingtong recovered so well was beyond Ning Zhuo’s expectations.

“This shows that Boss’s comprehension in the realm of void is incredibly astonishing.”

“After all, he only cultivated Youthful Emperor’s Technique, yet he possesses the Empty Seal.”

“No wonder he was valued by his master!”

While contemplating, Ning Zhuo took action, removing the heavy Stone Skin from Sun Lingtong and restoring his freedom.

Sun Lingtong stood on the floor tiles, moving his limbs, standing on his tiptoes to jump, and then giggled with laughter.

“Little Zhuo, watch me!”

He extended his hand and aimed his palm at the Storage Bag tied around Ning Zhuo’s waist.

The Storage Bag had been emitting a faint starlight.

But the next moment, as Sun Lingtong performed his Stealing Technique, the Storage Bag became dull and lightless, the starlight completely dissipating.

“Boss!” Ning Zhuo was overjoyed.

Sun Lingtong placed his hands on his hips and laughed heartily: “See that? See that!”

“I have absorbed the marrow of the void demon, oh no, more accurately, the Empty Seal in my Divine Sea has absorbed it.”

“This seal has undergone a transformation, a qualitative change, enabling me to use many methods.”

“Stealing Technique, Spells, Martial Arts or anything related to space have all seen an astonishing improvement! I am terrifyingly powerful now!!”

In the end, Sun Lingtong splayed his five fingers, then clenched his fist tightly, pursing his lips, and on his flushed little face, he tried to make a “ferocious” look.
