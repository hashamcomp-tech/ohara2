Chapter 1065: Chapter 394: Ning Zhuo: Shut Up!

"Someone can produce nearly twenty strands of Void Spider Silk every day, and the quality reaches Golden Core level? This output is quite exaggerated." Even though Ning Zhuo didn’t have much understanding of the Void Spider species, he couldn’t help but be amazed just evaluating it from a market value perspective.

"Could it be that he possesses some kind of innate ability to accelerate production?"

Ning Zhuo was just guessing and felt it wasn’t appropriate to ask in detail.

This was his first meeting with Che Zhuzi, and asking directly would be considered offensive.

Ning Zhuo chose to trust. Because there’s no need for deception, and someone as astute as Che Zhuzi, having lived in Myriad Images Sect for so long, must value business reputation.

"In that case, I’ll order sixty strands first," Ning Zhuo said.

Che Zhuzi was still unaware of the scale of this business, still smiling smugly, and asked, "What quality?"

In his view, Ning Zhuo was a Foundation Establishment Cultivator and should use Foundation Establishment level treasures. Of course, to pursue quantity, it’s normal for a Foundation Establishment Cultivator to use Qi Refining level materials.

Ning Zhuo said calmly, "Of course, Golden Core quality. If the senior can produce Nascent Soul level, and the price is feasible, I will also purchase."

For those who can use high-level items, naturally, they use high-level items.

Che Zhuzi was secretly taken aback, his face slightly changed, and he stared at Ning Zhuo to confirm, "Sixty strands of Golden Core level Void Spider Silk, are you sure?"

Ning Zhuo nodded, "Of course. Here’s the deposit."

As he spoke, he took out a Ghost Dao Golden Core and handed it directly to Che Zhuzi.

"A, a Golden Core?!" Che Zhuzi’s eyes widened.

Ning Zhuo demonstrated both financial power and strength!

A Golden Core...

Che Zhuzi himself was only a Golden Core.

Which Foundation Establishment Cultivator transaction directly uses a Golden Core as a deposit?

For a moment, Che Zhuzi wanted to ask Ning Zhuo, "Who are you exactly? Where is your family from?"

His estimation of Ning Zhuo quickly rose.

He tried to calm his mind and, with a calm demeanor, said, "Just sixty Golden Core level Void Spider Silk strands, this Golden Core is enough for the full payment, more than enough."

Ning Zhuo was slightly surprised, then quickly apologized, "Oh, senior, it was my fault for not explaining clearly!"

"What I meant was, sixty strands per month."

Che Zhuzi couldn’t keep calm, staring at Ning Zhuo in astonishment, "Sixty strands per month, are you sure? For how long?"

Ning Zhuo shrugged slightly, "First, order for a year, how about it?"

Che Zhuzi frowned, contemplating, "Even though my output has soared seventeen times, maintaining continuous production is not feasible."

"Producing seventeen Golden Core level strands every day would be extremely exhausting. Over time, it would deplete my essence blood, origin, and could even shorten my lifespan."

"Producing sixty strands of Void Spider Silk per month, giving it my all, would take at least four days. But realistically, I definitely can’t give it my all."

"Under normal circumstances, it would take six days."

"How many sets of six days are there in a month?"

Che Zhuzi felt somewhat troubled.

He didn’t have only Ning Zhuo as a client; in fact, Ning Zhuo was a newcomer.

Che Zhuzi maintained a stable business relationship with other buyers, covering their demands with his production.

But Ning Zhuo had now come in strongly as a later entrant...

Seeing Che Zhuzi deep in thought, Ning Zhuo decisively increased his offer, "Senior Che Zhuzi, do you feel the deposit is insufficient?"

"No problem, I can add more if needed."

As he spoke, he took out another Ghost Dao Golden Core.

Che Zhuzi’s heart shook once more.

Ning Zhuo took out the Golden Core as if he was taking out an ordinary elixir pill, it was just too easygoing.

"With such financial strength, he’ll certainly be a major client in the future!" Che Zhuzi’s heartbeat accelerated.

Interrupted by Ning Zhuo, he no longer felt any anger as before; instead, his face was full of smiles, hastily saying, "Two Golden Cores as a deposit, more than sufficient, they are more than enough!"

Ning Zhuo was even more straightforward, urging directly, "Then let’s first sign the contract."

Under the gaze of Silk Lady, the two smoothly concluded the contract.

"I’ll be leaving now, Senior Che Zhuzi," Ning Zhuo turned and left.

This is not a small business, and its prospects are vast.

Che Zhuzi quickly spoke, "Ning Zhuo, no need to hurry. We’ve gotten along well, why don’t you let me prepare a little, and we can enjoy some fine wine together?"

Ning Zhuo shook his head, "I have other matters. Senior Che Zhuzi, we can meet again when time allows."

Che Zhuzi was refused but smiled even brighter.

He personally saw Ning Zhuo out of the cave mansion, "Don’t call me senior anymore, we can speak as equals."

"That works," Ning Zhuo nodded, naturally accepting.

This casual attitude caught Che Zhuzi’s attention, leading him to speculate: It seems Ning Zhuo’s identity indeed isn’t simple, and a Golden Core Cultivator might not be anything special to him.

Che Zhuzi increasingly felt that befriending Ning Zhuo was a correct decision.

As Ning Zhuo was departing, he suddenly said to Che Zhuzi, "Fellow Daoist Che Zhuzi, I have a proposal for you to consider."

"If Ban Ji seeks to purchase Void Spider Silk from you, refuse him completely."

"In return, I will purchase the corresponding quantity at a fifty percent premium to make up for your loss."

"What do you think?"

Che Zhuzi’s eyes suddenly flashed with sharpness.

He understood Ning Zhuo’s deeper intention—it was to make him, Che Zhuzi, take a side.

"This indeed requires consideration," Che Zhuzi didn’t immediately refuse.

Within a short time, Ning Zhuo had shown his strength, already changing his initial thoughts during their exchange.

Ning Zhuo returned to Bluestone Cave Mansion and first interrogated Silk Lady.

Silk Lady lay weakly on the ground, crying and pleading: first praising Ning Zhuo as a paragon of the Righteous Path, a moral gentleman, questioning how he could force a weak woman into servitude. If he let her go, she promised to reform and repay him in the future.
