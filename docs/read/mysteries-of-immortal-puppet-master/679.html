Chapter 679

This world has already been reconstructed by the fire-attribute!

The earth is scorched and barren, with fierce flames burning everywhere.

The temperature keeps rising, and everyone within is constantly being roasted and burned by the blazing flames.

Yet, the most striking thing is that any living being within the dojo will be crowned with a fire crown.

The fire crown swiftly consumes everyone's foundations, forcing each person to maintain their peak state.

Fire Crown Dojo!

A restless commotion surged through the Three Generals Camp, and everyone looked around with heavy expressions.

This is an unexpected situation beyond the intelligence report.

The situation is dire!

The Divine Pine of Fire Crown actually ascended to become a god without a sound!

Though its level is not high, only Nascent Soul Level, but this is its home turf.

On its home turf, the power of the Divine Pine of Fire Crown is immensely magnified, becoming almost invincible!

"Retreating first is the safest bet!" Liu Er, unwilling to fight head-on, transmitted thoughts to urgently discuss with the other three.

"Difficult!" Guan Hong stroked his beard, eyeing the Divine Pine of Fire Crown with a dangerous gaze, "To retreat safely from a god's dojo is nearly impossible. At least a significant blow must be dealt to find a good opportunity for retreat."

Even the hot-tempered Zhang Hei had a solemn expression and did not oppose the suggestion of retreat.

He also knew that fighting within the dojo would greatly disadvantage the Three Generals Camp!

Liu Er gritted his teeth, "At this moment, we can only act according to circumstances."

As he spoke, he secretly mobilized his innate Spirit Sound Pulse Detection to quickly understand the Fire Crown Dojo and find its flaws.

Once the dojo is spread out, it alters the environment within a certain range, fully exhibiting the understanding of the truth externally.

The master's power within the dojo reaches its peak.

Meanwhile, the enemies within face extreme threats to their lives. But also, by personally experiencing and contacting the truth, they rapidly increase their understanding of the truths elucidated by the dojo!

Liu Er's innate Spirit Sound Pulse Detection is not adept at reconnaissance but perfectly suited for understanding in this context.

Sharing similar thoughts was Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo's idea was to leverage his own fire-attribute realm to rapidly increase understanding in hopes of spotting weaknesses.

"The fire crown on my head allows my thinking and comprehension abilities to maintain peak state."

"This is an unexpected delight!"

Luo Chen's emotions compared to Ning Zhuo were far worse.

To be precise, utterly terrible!

He had intended to retreat but was intercepted and crowned with a new fire crown.

At this moment, Luo Chen greatly envied the captured Yan Jin.

Yan Jin's identity and constraints were far less severe than Luo Chen's.

But Luo Chen had no choice. Appointed as the Forest Master of Ten Mile Scorched Forest, he was restricted by the Divine Decree of the Ginseng Whisker Dragon King.

Thus, even though he greatly resented the attitude and actions of the Divine Pine of Fire Crown towards him, he could only stand with the Divine Pine of Fire Crown and clash once again with the Three Generals Camp!

Boom boom boom…

Luo Chen's offensive was several times more fierce than before!

The Three Generals Camp also received the fire crown's blessing, escalating their combat prowess.

The two sides exchanged blows for nearly ten rounds, fiercely contending.

All three generals were injured, and their subordinates' casualties were increasing.

Luo Chen, drenched in sweat, lost any semblance of previous poise.

Each of their attacks maintained peak intensity, and the presence of the fire crown forced them to exert their utmost in offense and defense, quickly draining both sides.

"At this rate, I'll be drained dry before defeating the enemy."

"Divine Pine, remove my fire crown first!"

Luo Chen, fighting bitterly for a long time, transmitted thoughts again.

Only to receive a cold snort and refusal from the Divine Pine of Fire Crown: "Luo Chen, perform well. Do you think I don't know your intentions?"

"Only if you exert yourself well will I refrain from reporting against you to the Dragon King!"

The Divine Pine of Fire Crown was perceptive and well-versed in human nature.

Luo Chen gritted his teeth in anger but could do nothing to the Divine Pine of Fire Crown.

Hidden in the dark, Gao Cuo also wore a worried look: "Sword Elder, we are now trapped within the Fire Crown Dojo."

"The Divine Pine of Fire Crown had already discovered us, yet chose not to expose us."

"Do you think if I act under your cover, my identity would be revealed?"

Sword Elder pondered, "Hard to say."

"Although the Divine Pine of Fire Crown merely stands in one place, it contacts numerous characters daily, all of varied kinds."

"These cultivators come to the Ten Mile Scorched Forest and pray under the tree before refining artifacts, seeking a fire crown. This incense makes the god quite knowledgeable of worldly affairs."

Gao Cuo clenched his fists, "So we just watch helplessly?"

He felt deeply unwilling but helpless.

His situation was actually similar to Luo Chen's. Gao Cuo had been the Mountain Master of Bikong Mountain, and naturally, bore the constraints of the Divine Decree from the Ginseng Whisker Dragon King.

With such constraints, once his identity was exposed, extreme drawbacks were inevitable.

As for why he had stealthily come to the Ten Mile Scorched Forest, he couldn't explain it publicly.

Meanwhile, Lin Bufan wasn't encompassed within the Fire Crown Dojo.

The moment the Divine Pine of Fire Crown appeared, he immediately ascended on clouds, avoiding the dojo's coverage.

Lin Bufan now looked down on the dojo, feeling anxious.

"Ning Zhuo…"

"You brat, it's always me cleaning up your mess."

Lin Bufan directed his gaze towards Ning Zhuo, continually calculating whether exerting his full strength would affect the situation.

Lin Bufan kept evaluating the true combat power of the Divine Pine of Fire Crown, while refraining from hastily intervening.

"Damn it, damn it! Why won't you just die?!" Luo Chen berated the Three Generals Camp.
