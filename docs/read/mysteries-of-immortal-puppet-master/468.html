Chapter 468: Chapter 92: Treating Dao Injury_3

This is far from easy.

Anyone who has reached the Grandmaster Realm can start their own sect.

Zhang Zhongyi shook his head, “Even if an Earth Element Grandmaster were to be invited, they would still have to follow my diagnostic methods to design the spell.”

Thinking that the other party might have a significant identity, he added an explanation, “Dao injury resides within the human body, and many spells can target Dao injury, but can also harm the body.”

“From what I can tell so far, the child has hardly any signs of Body Refinement, and if the Dao injury is forcibly removed, the small physique is likely to have difficulty bearing it.”

...

Ning Zhuo nodded, signaling understanding.

He continued to inquire, “I’m worried that if I were to invite an expert of the Earth Element Grandmaster Realm, Lord Zhang would have gone to the front line with the military camp by then. May I ask, if I find an Earth Element Grandmaster and seek the assistance of a common Doctor, is there a high certainty of safely removing the Dao injury without harm?”

Zhang Zhongyi stroked his beard, arrogantly smiling, “Even Nascent Soul Level Doctors mostly don’t have such certainty.”

“My case is special, my past experience as an Imperial Physician has built my vision and talent.”

“At least within the entire Liangzhu Country, I do not believe there is a second Doctor who can cure this type of patient.”

Ning Zhuo understood and quickly praised Zhang Zhongyi, “From this perspective, we are truly honored to have Lord Zhang take action!”

“Lord, there is also a matter of Dao injury on my side that I would like you to take a look at.”

After hesitating for a while, Ning Zhuo still took out Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin.

“Golden Core Level Mechanical Puppet?” Zhang Zhongyi’s eyelids twitched slightly upon seeing Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin, and he reassessed Ning Zhuo.

Without showing his emotions, he inspected it carefully and spotted the purple traces of Spiritual Nature on it.

He finally could not hide his emotions any longer and became very moved.

“This Mechanical Puppet possesses Spiritual Nature!”

“Such profound Spiritual Nature, to suffer such a Dao injury and yet be able to withstand it, even supporting it for a considerable period of time.”

“Friend Ning, is this the protector of the Dao given to you by your elders?”

Ning Zhuo nodded and couldn’t help but swallow hard, fixated on Zhang Zhongyi, “May I ask if Lord Zhang can heal this type of Dao injury?”

Zhang Zhongyi first frowned deeply, then slowly shook his head, “Too severe, its Dao injury is far too severe, I’m afraid I am incapable of helping.”

“Ah?” Ning Zhuo’s face turned slightly pale.

Zhang Zhongyi explained, “This Dao mark is related to the Night Rain Dynasty. The Night Rain Dynasty has two main Monster Paths, one is the Ghost Dao, and the other is the Weird Dao.”

“Because of the presence of the Ghost Dao, the Netherworld is extremely prosperous, filled with countless ghosts. After death, there remains a significant destination.”

“The Weird Dao is the most absurd and bizarre, extremely difficult to comprehend.”

“Unfortunately, this Dao injury originates from the Weird Dao.”

“It is not something I can solve. Even if a master of Weird Dao were found, I could not work with him because I am unable to use normal logic to understand its principles.”

Ning Zhuo took a deep breath, remained silent for a long moment, and then spoke with difficulty, “So that’s how it is.”

Zhang Zhongyi revealed a slight smile, “Where does Friend Ning come from?”

Ning Zhuo replied, “The junior comes from the Ning Family of Fire Persimmon Immortal City. In the past, our clan migrated south from North Wind Country, and were betrayed by the Six Cave Sect. The latter acted treacherously, nearly putting my entire clan to death.”

“This time, while I am traveling abroad and visiting various places, I saw the grand army’s campaign against Thousand Peak Forest. Happening upon it, I wished to join in and contribute to the military efforts.”

“However, currently my friend has suffered a strange injury that is urgent. I must heal him first before considering anything else.”

Upon hearing this, Zhang Zhongyi’s eyes lit up slightly, “I see.”

“If Friend Ning invites an Earth Element Grandmaster, feel free to come find me. As for the medical fee, I have a good suggestion.”

“Why not join the army and become part of Red Flower Camp, replacing the medical fee with military merits? Not only can you treat your friend sooner, but you can also take revenge for your family.”

“Now is the best opportunity for vengeance.”

“Under the iron hooves of our great army, even the Six Cave Sect, as local tyrants, will be crushed.”

Ning Zhuo smiled slightly, shaking his head inwardly while appearing contemplative on the surface, “Senior Zhang, my mind is preoccupied with my friend’s trouble, and I cannot consider the further future. Let me find a Grandmaster first and think it over.”

Zhang Zhongyi stroked his beard, “That’s fine, that’s fine.”

The conversation ended, he gave Ning Zhuo a Token and called over a boy, bidding him to escort Sun Ning and his companion out of the tent.

Concerning his own life and death, Sun Lingtong did not say a word, cooperating tacitly with Ning Zhuo and watching how he handled the situation.

Before leaving the tent, he tucked himself back into the Mechanical Dragon.

Only after they had completely left the Red Flower Camp did Sun Lingtong contact Ning Zhuo to ask about his plans.

Ning Zhuo replied, “To heal your injury, old pal, we must make use of Zhang Zhongyi’s power.”

“But Red Flower Camp is not a good place to go.”

“Following Zhang Zhongyi’s approach makes us too passive. We need to turn passivity into initiative.”

Sun Lingtong pressed, “How do we turn passivity into the initiative?”

Ning Zhuo smiled slightly, “We still have to join the army!”
