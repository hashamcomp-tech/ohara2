Chapter 700 -215: Ning Zhuo Achieved a Great Feat!_3

“Can you shoulder this heavy responsibility?”

Liu Er looked directly at Master Lieyang, puffed out his chest, and said confidently, “We are all very certain. It’s because, after this person revealed his identity, Advisor Ning woke up and personally confirmed it.”

Master Lieyang’s eyelids twitched. He bit his lip, swallowed his regret, and forced a smile, “That’s good, that’s good!”

Zhao Xi sighed. Without getting Ning Zhuo, he couldn’t grasp the means to continue balancing the Twin Ghosts of Heaven and Earth.

His heart was full of regret, but he could only smile at Liu Guanzhang, “You have all worked hard. This desperate battle has shown the courage of the Three Generals’ Camp and displayed the might and fighting spirit of our Liangzhu Country.”

“Follow me back to the main camp. Rewards must be granted to you all!”

Liu Er quickly expressed his thanks loudly and was happy for Ning Zhuo—he had avoided this calamity.

Originally, when Tian Shuang chased after Earth Ash and the battle concluded, Zhu Xuanji, who had been observing all along, frowned deeply.

He had a huge doubt in his mind—whether Ning Zhuo had truly mastered the Life Hanging Divine Power?!

The Nandou Royal Family knew many secrets about this divine power. One of them was that unless one possessed profound methods, most people would find it difficult to detect the Life Hanging Thread.

A cultivator who had mastered the Divine Power Life Thread could discover the Life Hanging Threads.

Otherwise, the Nandou Royal Family wouldn’t have gone to such great lengths to send Shen Lingshu to indirectly probe for this answer.

“Ning Zhuo’s state at the time, reinforced by military art, was at the peak level of the Golden Core. If he had this divine power, he could have connected with the Tian Shuang mask.”

“But divine power does not require physical contact. Yet Ning Zhuo placed both palms on the back of the ghost mask. This action negates the possibility.”

“Can the Buddha Heart Demon Seal alone be enough to fill the ghost mask with Buddha Thoughts and then affect the body?”

“In any case, Ning Zhuo’s situation is not safe. It’s better to rescue him first.”

Zhu Xuanji had Lin Bufan come forward to bring Ning Zhuo back.

After Ning Zhuo fell unconscious, Sun Lingtong maneuvered the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon to fly out from Ning Zhuo’s waist.

Although he didn’t leave the Mechanical Dragon, he still leaked his aura and voice, revealing his identity to the Three Generals.

Guan Hong stroked his beard, “During the fierce battle earlier, I did feel someone pulling out the ice cones from the advisor’s body. So it was you after all.”

Zhang Hei “guessed” Sun Lingtong’s identity and said knowingly, “Oh, I get it. You are the advisor’s little assistant.”

Liu Er then looked at the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon. He had long noticed this mechanical artifact but pretended to see it for the first time now, sincerely exclaiming, “The advisor is truly loyal and righteous, inspiring respect. With such an artifact, he could have traversed the void to retreat early. Yet he stayed with us through the fight, never retreating. How fortunate I am to have such a comrade!”

With these words, Guan and Zhang also changed their expressions, revealing emotions of being moved.

Sun Lingtong rolled his eyes and thought, “Do you really think we never considered retreating?”

Of course, he said nothing and, seeing the Three Generals’ expressions, thought, “Let this beautiful misunderstanding continue.”

At this moment, Lin Bufan revealed himself, stirring up the vigilance of the Three Generals’ Camp soldiers.

Of course, Liu Guanzhang did not trust Lin Bufan immediately.

But Sun Lingtong vouched for Lin Bufan at this time.

Lin Bufan discreetly showed the Fire in Wood to the Three Generals.

The Three Generals finally began to trust him, but they still did not let Ning Zhuo leave until Ning Zhuo himself woke up and confirmed Lin Bufan’s identity.

Lin Bufan suggested to Ning Zhuo that he part ways with the Three Generals’ Camp and not return to Liangzhu Country’s main camp.

Ning Zhuo had the same idea, so after distributing the spoils of war, he temporarily said goodbye to the Three Generals.
