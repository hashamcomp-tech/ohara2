Chapter 916: Chapter 321: Breaking into the Altar Battlefield (Part 2)

Sure enough, amidst the pervasive cold air, Bai Shuang drifted, beginning to affect the surrounding space.

Ning Zhuo dared not be careless, decisively controlling the Light Goose to dive straight into the thickly ominous barrier of foul air.

The power of the Light Goose decomposed the barrier of foul air, while the burning of the Returning Silence Path Candle formed a temporary passage on its foundation.

This was a pitch-black, winding path. On either side of the path, countless ghostly shadows overlapped, resembling a hundred ghosts carrying a coffin.

A lone goose carried a candle, breaking through the heavenly curtain, it succeeded!

For a moment, the Qing Jiao Army erupted in cheers, the sound resonating to the heavens.

"Indeed! He is the savior of the White Paper Immortal City, the wise leader of my life," Luo Si’s heart trembled fiercely, though his face showed no change, while Jiao Ma’s father felt so invigorated he almost went numb.

Qing Chi leaped with excitement, shouting, "Amazing, Xiao Ma, you are incredible!"

Ning Zhuo turned to look at her, flashing a brilliant smile, "Qingqing, you really helped me a lot."

Recognized by the one she liked, Qing Chi placed her hands on her hips, letting out a hearty laugh.

Qing Yan wanted to speak but hesitated, his gaze complicated.

The Earth Evil God was bewildered!

What, what is this?

He never expected that choosing the safest defensive strategy would end up as lifting a rock only to drop it on his own foot.

If he had known, he might as well have protected himself with the Great Array’s formation boundary!

"Qingqing, we need to incorporate Luo Si into our army," Ning Zhuo immediately commanded.

Qing Chi nodded, responding with a heavy "hmm".

"Luo Si, you will command us," Ning Zhuo employed without doubt.

Luo Si felt a wave of trust and emotion, yet remained outwardly calm, swiftly ordering the entire army to move out.

The Qing Jiao Army charged down the narrow winding path, rushing towards the altar’s core.

"That is... the altar!"

"This is the core area!"

"Hahaha, follow young master Jiao Ma to final victory!!"

The Qing Jiao Army spotted the gigantic altar at once, their morale soaring high.

"Damn it." The Earth Evil God nearly ground his teeth to pieces.

Pressured by the circumstances, he could only halt eroding the Ice Coffin temporarily to launch a head-on assault on the Qing Jiao Army.

Transforming Spirit Level!

The powerful aura of the Earth Evil God was unmasked, causing the Qing Jiao Army to shudder in its entirety.

No one expected such a colossal figure to remain!

How could they battle this?

Even with a Cultivation Army, the opponent was Transforming Spirit Level!

The gap in cultivation levels was too vast.

The Qing Jiao Army alone could not bridge this gap.

"Stay in the passage; all military power, supply it to me!" Luo Si, seeing this, dared not take any chances, gritted his teeth, and faced the Earth Evil God.

Ning Zhuo, Qing Chi, and the others were far too distant from the Transforming Spirit Level in cultivation strength.

Even with military power and such levers, it was insurmountable. The Qing Jiao Army was, after all, a new army!

In reality, even if there was an army of the world’s strongest, with enough military power to leverage to the Transforming Spirit Level, Ning Zhuo and Qing Chi could not bear such an immense external force.

Their physical bodies, souls, dantians, and other endowments were still limited by their cultivation levels and couldn’t withstand such vast external force.

Luo Si shot forth like a bolt, engaging directly with the Earth Evil God in the air!

Boom.

With a thunderous explosion, the Earth Evil God remained in his original position, only stepping back slightly, while Luo Si flew backward.

Luo Si’s entire body was numbed to the point of losing sensation, and the fist he had used in the clash was completely devoid of feeling.

The Earth Evil God stood nearly three feet tall, entirely pitch-black, seemingly carved directly from the most ancient of iron mountains.

His enormous muscular texture resembled a parched, cracked ancient riverbed, each ravine remarkably deep, exuding an unmatched weighty impression.

Thick, dark red, fervent veins, like lava streams bound deep underground, were faintly visible.

Just the form alone exuded a formidable aura!

"Divine Separation!"

Ning Zhuo, Qing Chi, and others widened their eyes.

Even geniuses like them, it was their first time witnessing a presence at the Transforming Spirit Level with their own eyes.

Luo Si’s expression grew grave as never before; a single confrontation made him keenly aware of the vast gap between them.

"Young Master, I can’t hold on for long." As he conveyed through Divine Sense Transmission to Ning Zhuo, he also retrieved a Bronze War Chariot.

Luo Si stood atop the chariot, charging towards the Earth Evil God.

Boom boom boom...

Their fierce clash, each collision akin to a meteor striking the earth, created terrifying sound waves resonating across the surrounding space.

With the help of a war chariot of Magic Treasure Level, Luo Si compensated for the difference in physical strength.

However, the Qing Jiao Army all looked grim, their breath weakening.

This war chariot consumed Military Power, being a weapon; when regular Mana is used for consumption, it often yields twice the results with half the effort. Military Power is the opposite.

Fighting at the Transforming Spirit Level, the consumption of Military Power is truly terrifying.

The Qing Jiao Army had already rested and was in excellent condition, but after a few rounds of clashes, they could no longer hold on.

This forced Qing Chi to reveal his trump card early, using the stored military supplies.

The military supplies were already few, and now they were rapidly consumed to make up for the huge deficit in Military Power, preventing the entire army from collapsing immediately.

Luo Si maneuvered the Bronze War Chariot, constantly circling around the Earth Evil God.

After another collision, the Bronze War Chariot retreated a dozen steps, with numerous cracks appearing on its surface.

Standing on the chariot, Luo Si found it hard to maintain balance, almost falling.

The Earth Evil God seized the opportunity and suddenly raised its giant iron arm.

Martial Arts—Mountain Shattering Fist!

No flashy spell glows, just the simplest and most direct punch.

The foul air in front was directly compressed into a wall of air.

The space moaned under the unbearable pressure.

The surface of the fist revealed dark red molten blood veins, suddenly accelerating, giving the punch frighteningly high heat!

Luo Si’s heart chilled, with his rich combat experience, he knew he must not be hit.

Military Art—Darkness Covers Chen Cang!

Outwardly, he turned the chariot towards allies, but in fact, he abruptly withdrew.

Bam.

Though the Earth Evil God’s punch was terrifying, it ultimately hit empty space.

Luo Si narrowly escaped disaster, his brow furrowed, showing a heavy expression.

The accumulated damage to the Bronze War Chariot resulted in decreased speed and defense, making it even harder for him to grapple with the Earth Evil God.

In his busy moment, Luo Si glanced at the passage, noticing the Qing Jiao Army’s weak state.

"This new army is still too weak; the Military Power provided struggles to support this war chariot."

If he were commanding a complete Forbidden Army, he would be confident in leveraging his Military Power to fight multiple rounds against the Earth Evil God.

But now, he knew he could only rely on himself.

Luo Si shouted sternly, pouring a large amount of Nascent Soul Mana into the chariot beneath him.

Buzz!

The chariot emitted a bronze glow, with halos flowing over the numerous runes on the chariot, and carrying Luo Si it suddenly drifted sideways to narrowly avoid another punch from the Earth Evil God.

Boom!

The Mountain Shattering Fist scraped the rear of the chariot and crashed down.

Crack.

Just the pressure of the punch brushed past, and the rear of the chariot issued a chilling cracking sound!

The bronze glow instantly dimmed a bit.

Luo Si felt as if struck by a heavy hammer, his chest tightened.

"In such a battle situation, I can only attack instead of defend, striving with all my might to buy time." He endured the shock, a fierce look flashed in his eyes, knowing that passive avoidance only led to a dead end!

He cast a fishing rod, tied with the River of Oblivion Conveyance Line, and the Hook of Three Lifetimes hung from the line.

He employed Fishing Skill; the hook moved with exceptional Spirit, carrying the line, flashed like lightning around the Earth Evil God’s physique.

The next moment, the Earth Evil God was tied up tightly by the fishing line.

Luo Si poured all his strength and Mana, the line sharply contracted, embedding deeply into the Earth Evil God’s body.

The hook soared to the sky, descending vertically towards the Earth Evil God’s forehead.

The Earth Evil God struggled in place, thick black smoke spurting from the holes in its head.

The black smoke piled up into clouds, blocking the hook.

Crack, crack.

Luo Si’s expression slightly changed.

The hook and line were okay, but the rod began to show dense cracks...

"Luo Si is no match for this Divine Separation!"

"I must race against time to reach the altar, and utterly disrupt the Lord of Wanchuan’s century-long plot."

Ning Zhuo had already started to act.

Yet, even with the altar right in front of him, he had to halt his steps.

The river water surged endlessly.

He was blocked by a Wangchuan Tributary.

The tributary, like a jade belt, encircled the altar. Ning Zhuo tried to break through from any direction, but it flowed toward there, creating obstacles.

"This is the river water of the River of Oblivion; how can I cross it?" Ning Zhuo had no choice but to stop, deeply contemplating a way to break through.
