Chapter 753 -241: Star Burning Pit

“Speaking of which, should I sneak near the Liangzhu Country’s army, or inside the Canglin Immortal City, to secretly meet one last time with Liu Guanzhang and General Mu Lan?”

Ning Zhuo hesitated for a moment, in the Dragon Head Cabin.

Resolving the matter of separation is not worth considering. This requires going through official channels, which will surely take a long time and expose me.

For Ning Zhuo, it’s harmful with no benefits, too risky.

Apart from the formal farewell, Ning Zhuo mainly wants to utilize the Three Generals Camp and Red Flower Camp to exchange some of his battle achievements, making it easier for him to take what he needs.

It’s worth noting that he performed extraordinarily in the second great battle, with remarkable achievements, according to the rules, he should receive huge rewards.

“Last time, the Three Original Cross Treasures Talisman exchanged with battle achievements was really useful.”

“Even though Liangzhu Country is small, it’s still a Cultivation Kingdom. To me personally, even for the entire Ning Family, it’s a gigantic entity, mastering resources like mountains and seas. I can certainly use the battle achievements to exchange for treasures suitable for my use.”

“With these cultivation funds, when I set off again, it will definitely greatly benefit my future cultivation.”

Just as Ning Zhuo thought this, the Mechanical Ring in his hand suddenly contracted, with considerable force.

It must be noted that Ning Zhuo’s physical level is now vastly improved, yet the ring’s contraction still made him wince in pain.

“Lingering here is tantamount to seeking death?” Ning Zhuo’s eyes suddenly narrowed.

“Have Heaven and Earth Ghosts returned for me?”

“Or has Du Tiechuan been lying in wait?”

“Or perhaps this time the Ginseng Whisker Dragon King caught a fake Old Monster in Stone, indirectly found some clues, and has come looking?”

For a time, various speculations swirled in Ning Zhuo’s heart.

What’s the point of a secret meeting then?

Time to flee!

“I’m just a mere Foundation Establishment Stage cultivator with small arms and legs; there’s no need to flit around in front of these Divinity Transformation and Nascent Soul-level powerhouses.”

“Although I made some impressions on the battlefields before, it was during their life-or-death struggles when they couldn’t care for other things.”

Anyway, Lin You and Wu Hen, the two captives, have already been directly handed over by Mu Lan to Ning Zhuo.

The main purpose has been achieved. Ning Zhuo chooses to quit while ahead.

“As the son-in-law of Mu Shang General, the battle merits I established in the Liangzhu Country expedition should also be taken care of.”

“Firstly due to national systems and reputation, secondly for the close relationship with Mu Shang General Mansion, if they don’t even help their son-in-law, how can they talk of revitalization?”

Ning Zhuo again perceives the benefits of his marital relationship with Mu Lan.

“Return to Liangzhu Country in the future, then receive the results of the battle, and handle this relationship.”

With these thoughts, Ning Zhuo has no more attachment and activates the Mechanical Dragon, speeding ahead.

The Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon traverses through the void, serving as an excellent long-distance travel tool.

Three or four days later, spatial ripples emerge, and a Mechanical Little Dragon appears out of thin air.

It’s the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon.

The Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon deliberately stops at this location; Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong gather in the Dragon Head Cabin, observing the magnificent scene outside.

This is a giant ring-shaped mountain, with a diameter of over eight hundred li!

Viewed from high above, the mountains encircle each other in a ring, and within is a gigantic deep pit.

The pit walls are completely glazed, covered with spiderweb-like cracks. The cracks occasionally emit a bluish-silver glow, the rhythm of the glow resembling a giant’s natural breath.

Whoosh!

Suddenly, the ground forces surge, and a large plume of blue silver fire erupts from cracks everywhere, forming a raging flame across the ring-shaped mountain.

The fiery flames soar over hundreds of meters, tongues of fire straight into the sky, temperatures wildly escalating, creating deadly extreme heat around.

Sun Ning and the others were prepared, positioning the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon far away. Yet when the blue silver flames shoot up into the sky, they still hastily retreat the dragon.

The Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon shows signs of melting all over its body, fortunately quickly retreating, suffering only slight damage.

Sun Lingtong breaks out in a fine layer of cold sweat: “This is the Star Burning Pit! Even though there are records in books, the power of this Pure World Heavenly Fire is still underestimated.”

The blue flame towering into the sky is reflected in Ning Zhuo’s eyes, remaining silent for a long time.

This is one of the wonders of the Cultivation World.

According to legends, a star had fallen from the sky, crashing into this mountainous region’s depths, creating a massive meteor crater.

The erupting Pure World Heavenly Fire delves deeply into the earth veins, entwining and coexisting with this region, burning continuously from ancient times to this day.

Many legends swirl around this place.

For instance, during the medieval era, excellent Spirit Roots emerged endlessly. At that time, the seven great Super Sects united for exploration, deploying a total of thirty thousand cultivators, resulting in only seven survivors.

These seven greatly benefited from their exploration, collectively known as the Seven Sons of Heaven Tour.

Coming to modern times, Spiritual Energy is abundant, innate talents appear. The Star Burning Pit thus benefits, the Spiritual Nature accumulated over long years ignites qualitative change through quantitative growth, forming an exceptionally powerful Fire Charm, cultivating itself, proclaiming as Pure Fire Heavenly Girl.

The Pure Fire Heavenly Girl left the Star Burning Pit, causing a calamity to the entire Cultivation World, burning everything, most forces perished because of Her.

The blue silver flames were too magnificent, indeed forming a giant mountain!

Centered on the flames, a mysterious force spreads outward, instantly transforming surroundings into a distinct blue universe space.

Space is completely open, allowing free entry and exit. Beneath countless bright stars, lines of star lights paint intersecting straights and curves in the azure universe.
