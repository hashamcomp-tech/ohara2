Chapter 1067: Chapter 395: What Do You Know About the Soul Searching Technique?

"Boss, help me!" Ning Zhuo uses Life Hanging Thread to send a message.

"I’m coming." Sun Lingtong, who had been hiding inside the Shuyu Pavilion, diligently researched the Water Vortex Array, exerting all his efforts, wanting to quickly obtain the Luoshu Book Pages.

Facing Ning Zhuo’s request for help, he responded immediately without any hesitation, using his Spiritual Eye Talent to pierce through the array and see the essence of the Luoshu Book Pages.

Sun Lingtong, influenced by the Taoist Artifact, despite his numerous encounters, couldn’t help but marvel in his heart, "What a powerful Taoist Artifact this is!"

Through the Life Hanging Thread, Ning Zhuo also slightly changed his expression, feeling himself being affected by the Luoshu Book Pages.

Bitter Ink was already prepared.

The Jade Slip, which recorded the Boat Burning Soul Crossing Technique, was placed horizontally on the desk.

Beneath the Jade Slip, a piece of Xuan Paper was spread out.

Ning Zhuo held the Ideograph Brush in his right hand and a Wooden Cone in his left.

Beside him, a bundle of Hemp Rope was also placed.

Ning Zhuo mobilized his Divine Sense, channeling Mana, causing the Hemp Rope to react immediately, rising vertically, one end tying around his hair, the other end tying to an infinite beam.

Meanwhile, the Wooden Cone in Ning Zhuo’s hand slowly pierced into the inside of his thigh.

Head Hanging from Beam, Awl-Thorn Vigilance Art!

The Luoshu Book Pages, Ideograph Brush, Bitter Ink, along with Head Hanging from Beam, Awl-Thorn Vigilance Art, and finally absorbing the surrounding Literary Style, Ning Zhuo officially entered into a state of learning.

He began to imitate the Boat Burning Soul Crossing Technique.

This spell was obtained from Tian Shuang, the Jade Slip’s style is ancient, not something of this era.

The opening is not the main text, but rather admonitions left by former sages.

"Burn one’s soul, cross the sea of desire, acquire thousands of skills, steal creation to reach profoundness. But what the soul boat carries is not just treasures, but other people’s lives in downfall. When the boat is fully burnt and the fire extinguished, the kindler is no longer I, nor the one who picks up the remnants in the ashes. The Boat Burning Soul Crossing is truly to invite fire to oneself, using oneself as a sacrifice. Caution! Fear it! This taboo is no touch for those who are driven to madness by obsession or seeking to live through death!"

Ning Zhuo tried to imitate this passage, resulting in a sudden enlightenment.

Traces of intent gathered from the tip of the brush into his Divine Sea.

"This Jade Slip is truly an ancient artifact, possibly from the creator of this Cultivation Technique?"

Since Soul Devouring Sect is a Super Sect with a long history?

In any case, the intent contained within this Jade Slip is quite abundant. Unlike the Cultivation Technique Jade Slip made by Ning Zhuo himself, which almost had no imitative value.

Begin to learn the actual content of the Boat Burning Soul Crossing Technique.

This spell is divided into four parts.

The first part is the Root of Karmic Fire.

The opening sentence poses a question: "Offering soul-fat to the Karmic Fire, using wisdom to cross the Bitter Sea, can those who abandon the raft for the shore realize the raft is bone?"

The second part is the Ghost Crossing Bitter Sea.

Ning Zhuo imitated throughout, the phrase that left the deepest impression was: "A thousand years of sorrow and joy are merely transient, ten thousand fathoms of red dust are all Karmic Waves. When the Primordial Spirit enters the boat, it falls into the limitless illusion..."

Then came the third part named Return to Truth from Ash Sea.

After imitating, Ning Zhuo’s expression grew solemn, his gaze focused on one line: "Beyond the last ship, not the other shore, only the ashes of trials bury the star raft"

The fourth part is the Five Perils Tangling Truth.

It mentions five kinds of perils, whichever cultivator practices or uses this Boat Burning Soul Crossing Technique may bring about five severe repercussions.

The first is Name Oblivion, the second is Knowledge Ashes, the third is Boat Scar, the fourth is Karmic Burn, and the fifth is Lost Voyage.

Name Oblivion means losing memory of one’s own name, then forgetting others’, eventually losing all interpersonal connections.

Knowledge Ashes is when Divine Sense is generated within the Upper Dantian of the Divine Sea, it spontaneously combusts without fire, burning to ashes.

Boat Scar is the manifestation of the Boat Burning Soul Crossing Technique eroding the flesh, some scorched marks appear on the forehead, others on the chest, back, etc. Once the scorched marks cross each other, forming the shape of a solitary boat, the cultivator’s soul will ignite, seemingly affected by the Boat Burning Soul Crossing Technique, finally burning to nothing.

Karmic Burn raises Red Lotus Karma Fire, scorching cultivators. At its mildest, it sends green flames, burning meridians; at its height, the internal organs blaze, cooking oneself to pulp!

This demonstrates the spell’s offending taboo, greatly against heavenly harmony.

The last peril, Lost Voyage, is the cultivator completely drowning in another’s memories, unable to extricate themselves, severely confusing self-awareness without regularity, constantly shifting.

Ning Zhuo finished learning it once, the Xuan Paper was filled completely with words.

Each stroke was like carving, Ning Zhuo exerted all his strength, chiseling open mysteries.

His one thigh was already bloodied, scalp tightened, spirit draining rapidly under the Luoshu.

Ning Zhuo achieved initial mastery, fully perceiving the sharpness of this technique.

"Burn souls as boats, forcefully seize creation. Risking falling into an abyss, drawing on others’ lifetimes of cultivation!"

"Wondrous! Wondrous!"

The power of this spell is truly immense, seeking extreme ends, making Ning Zhuo eager.

After taking a breath, Ning Zhuo began studying it a second time.

Still starting from the beginning to imitate.

The Hemp Rope was stretched straight, creaking, many strands of hair scattered on the ground.

A shallow layer of blood had already formed on the floor. The Wooden Cone had changed position, frequently stabbing into the other thigh.

After learning it the second time, Ning Zhuo’s understanding of this Boat Burning Soul Crossing Technique deepened significantly.

His expression grew solemn, beginning to focus on the fourth part’s Five Perils Tangling Truth.

"The Boat Burning Soul Crossing Technique is too extreme."

"I have Heavenly Capital Self, can resist Name Oblivion, Lost Voyage. But how to deal with the remaining Knowledge Ashes, Boat Scar, Karmic Burn?"

"This still needs careful contemplation."

Having researched the technique to this point, he increasingly regarded the Five Perils as tremendous. Underestimating will inevitably lead to a heavy price.

"Can Buddha Heart Demon Seal counter Knowledge Ashes? If Divine Sense cannot be produced within a certain time, that would be very bad."

Divine Sense’s auxiliary function is extremely significant to cultivators!
