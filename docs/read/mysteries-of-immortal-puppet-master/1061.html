Chapter 1061: Chapter 392: Cheating, He’s Cheating!

So, the allure of Silk Lady towards Che Zhuzi had never taken effect!

The Void Spider is actually a eunuch!

Silk Lady looked at the Demon Cultivator in front of her, still unable to believe it.

Looking at his dense beard, and his immensely strong physique, so masculine, how could it be?

Che Zhuzi, following her gaze, touched his bushy beard and puffed out his chest proudly, "I know what you want to ask. I have absorbed a large amount of Yang Energy from the outside world, and it cost a lot of money. However, all these investments are completely worth it. Compared to a seventeen-fold increase in production, the investment in purchasing Yang Energy is insignificant!"

Silk Lady: ...

Che Zhuzi took out a contract: "Come, sign it."

He infused it with Mana, allowing the contract to fully unfold in front of Silk Lady.

Once she discerned it clearly, she began to struggle once more, filled with mixed rage and shock: "This is, this is a Slave Contract?!"

Yet no matter how hard she tried with all her might, she was still unable to budge.

If it were a normal fight, she might not be able to defeat Che Zhuzi, who was at a Golden Core Level, but she would have a good chance to escape.

Now, having fallen into a trap, the consequences were too humiliating for her.

"I was too overconfident! I treated Che Zhuzi as prey, not realizing it was me, and that I walked into the trap myself."

Silk Lady’s expression turned bleak.

Seeing she couldn’t break free, she began to plead: "Master Che Zhuzi, do you really want to be this ruthless?"

"To be honest with you, this time I got close to you to deal with Ning Zhuo, which the Ban Family benefits from."

"You surely know the Ban Family, what kind of existence it really is!"

Silk Lady did not mention Ban Ji, but more cautiously, directly brought up the Ban Family to exert pressure on Che Zhuzi.

Che Zhuzi remained unmoved: "The Ban Family is of the Righteous Path, would it employ a Demon Cultivator like you?"

"You may have misunderstood something."

"I don’t want to get involved in this trouble. Whatever secret information there is, go and speak to Ning Zhuo yourself."

Silk Lady froze, her expression changing again: "Wha-what do you mean? Could it be you want to..."

At this moment, a strong feeling of dread surged in her heart.

Che Zhuzi smiled with ill intent: "Oh yes, I’m just an outsider, innocently dragged into this. What I want most is to make money, who has the patience to let your petty issues drain my energy? I’m a businessman, remaining neutral is enough."

The identity of a disciple of the Myriad Images Sect gave him such confidence.

"Your matters, solve them on your own!" Che Zhuzi finally emphasized.

"Am I, do I have to become a dog for Ning Zhuo? No, no!" A surge of absurdity and shame overwhelmed Silk Lady, making her lose her composure.

Sensing something, Che Zhuzi stopped communicating and turned his gaze, piercing through to a certain spot within the Great Array.

Ning Zhuo was battling as he retreated!

There truly was no choice, the enemy outnumbered him two to one, the numerical advantage too obvious, Ning Zhuo’s fists couldn’t handle four hands.

If the two opponents were novices or lacked control, there would still be hope. But the reality was, these two blocking Ning Zhuo had no weaknesses and were even more adept at manipulating Mechanical Artifacts than Ning Zhuo!

In fact, Ning Zhuo’s opponents were indeed two elderly cultivators.

They had known each other from earlier years outside the field and had reached an agreement to first eliminate the "outsider" Ning Zhuo before settling the score between them.

They pursued Ning Zhuo relentlessly.

In this situation, the outcome of the battle was pretty much set.

Che Zhuzi suddenly took action.

Rumble...

The labyrinth underwent yet another transformation, with various walls shifting and traps springing into action, intercepting the two pursuers chasing Ning Zhuo.

One after another, walls sliced through the space, allowing Ning Zhuo to quickly escape the pursuers’ field of vision, providing him with a precious moment to catch his breath.

Then, under Silk Lady’s wide-eyed gaze, Che Zhuzi lightly tapped with his finger, immediately transmitting the only brand new Mystic Iron Puppet in the Great Array to Ning Zhuo.

"Cheating, someone is cheating here!" Silk Lady wanted to shout.

"What is going on here?!" Ning Zhuo’s joy knew no bounds at the sight of a brand new Mystic Iron Puppet.

This wasn’t just a moment to breathe, but also gave Ning Zhuo a huge opportunity to turn the tables!

Without any hesitation, Ning Zhuo immediately switched his puppet.

The one he had been envious of had long been on the verge of collapse, and now he was finally free of it.

"Continue to rest?" Ning Zhuo shook his head, decisively dismissing his previous plan.

"What I need to do is seize this battle opportunity and immediately turn back to eliminate the enemy!"

In Ning Zhuo’s eyes, flames of intense fighting spirit burned.

In his view, with the labyrinth’s new alteration, surely many new Mystic Iron Puppets were also brought in.

"It couldn’t be just the one I replaced!"

"The other two enemies might also encounter new puppets and replace their old ones."

"I must seize this moment to take advantage of their weakest state and fully utilize my side’s advantage!"

In a situation where only the Mystic Iron Puppet and Void Spider Silk could be used, Ning Zhuo, facing a two-on-one, did not hold the upper hand.

"The longer the time drags on, the less advantage I have."

"Where are you?"

"Here I come!"

Ning Zhuo could barely contain the urge to teleport in front of his opponents right now.

Che Zhuzi and Silk Lady watched Ning Zhuo’s latest actions intently. They both knew the truth—the only brand new Mystic Iron Puppet in the entire field was now in Ning Zhuo’s possession.

The two did not communicate, silently observing.

The distance between Ning Zhuo and his two opponents grew closer and closer.

Che Zhuzi, beyond the sight of both parties, was also manipulating the Great Array, altering the labyrinth’s layout, several times reducing the distance between both sides significantly.
