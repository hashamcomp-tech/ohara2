Chapter 1253: Chapter 485: New Insights from Review (2)

"At least three levels higher."

After reviewing, Ning Zhuo began his daily cultivation.

Upper Dantian Mirror Platform Spirit Communication Technique and Middle Dantian Five Elements Qi Regulation Technique were not the focus.

The profound depth of the Demon-infused Bloodline Technique far surpassed the other two cultivation techniques, becoming Ning Zhuo’s main target for insight.

Because of previous accumulation, Ning Zhuo gained insights every time he comprehended the demon skill, always maintaining good progress.

Today, as he delved once more, he naturally thought of weaving techniques.

A spirit light flashed across his Upper Dantian Divine Sea.

Ning Zhuo followed the inspiration, manipulating his internal energy to transform into a fascia.

He used weaving memories to craft more structures from the fascia—starting with plain weave, then changing to twill weave, finally forming a satin weave.

With these three weaving methods, the efficiency of blood qi flowing among them differed distinctly.

"Plain weave is dense, best for defense; twill weave is smooth, good for explosive speed; satin weave is flexible, best at dispersing force..."

"Interesting!"

Ning Zhuo looked again at the spiral, layer, and spiderweb structures within the Demon-infused Bloodline Technique and gained new insights.

"Isn’t this the same as weaving methods, just with different end products?"

"Spiral weaving is like twisted rope, best for power transmission, when blood qi erupts, it drills through, making penetration extremely strong."

"Layered weaving is like thousand-layer armor, layer upon layer, unrivaled in defense."

"Spiderweb weaving can spread in all directions, catching wind and shadows, best at imprisoning and infecting."

"Additionally, the palindrome weave can be considered a blend of forward and reverse, continuously regenerating, possessing a magical self-healing property."

"When I use demon tendon to infect others’ blood qi, it is essentially using my own ’weaving method’ to forcibly overlay and rewrite others’ blood qi structure—this is ’demon infecting’!"

Ning Zhuo felt a surge of joy in his heart.

He peeled away another layer of mist, seeing more of the truth.

Connecting the dots, integrating knowledge—this is one of the joys of cultivation.

After completing his daily lessons, Ning Zhuo communicated with Sun Lingtong through Life Hanging Thread.

"Boss, I have quite a few insights into my cultivation today." He explained his progress to Sun Lingtong.

Sun Lingtong chuckled, also sharing his recent insights into strategizing the water array.

The two exchanged ideas for a while, both realizing they gained much from each other, making great progress, feeling happy for one another.

"I have comprehended the water array around Luoshu thoroughly, and I’ve found a way to resolve the array."

"But I can’t do it alone."

"Little Zhuo, we need to collaborate to securely dismantle it."

Since Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong secretly explored Shuyu Pavilion and discovered a hidden page of Taoist Artifact Luoshu, Sun Lingtong had been delving into the water array.

Finally, the end was in sight.

Ning Zhuo was thrilled, "That’s great. Boss, what should I do? I’ll follow your lead."

Sun Lingtong, "Hehe. In fact, it’s you who are the main force, you need to face the array itself directly."

"Little Zhuo, your Five Elements Realm is much more profound than mine, you need to counter water with water."

Ning Zhuo didn’t quite understand, "Oh."

Sun Lingtong added, "But how to counter water depends on testing your array abilities."

Ning Zhuo frowned, "In this area, can’t you help, Boss?"

Sun Lingtong slightly shook his head in Shuyu Pavilion, "Difficult."

"The water array can change at any moment."

"When Luoshu moves, the array’s changes become even more drastic. But we’re attempting to take Luoshu out from the center of the water array."

"I can guide you, but there’s simply no time."

"Little Zhuo, you are the main force, needing to calculate the water array’s changes and counter them within a breath."

"This requires your array abilities to be at least on par with mine."

Ning Zhuo, "Boss, I understand."

"I’ll allocate more time to learn about arrays."

"But I don’t have time right now. I need to immediately prepare to participate in the second round of the Xingyun Trial at Evil Slaying Hall, striving to seize the top position!"

Sun Lingtong, "Hehe. I’ve thought of two ways we can advance together."

"First, rely on your learning. I can help you acquire some Demon Cultivator’s Souls to accumulate relevant array foundation."

"Second, rely on the Inheriting Dao Jade Page. We already have the refining method for the Inheriting Dao Jade Page, you refine some, and I infuse my understanding of Array Dao into them. When you absorb it, your Array Dao Realm will increase."

This method would cause Sun Lingtong’s Array Dao Realm to drop, needing to be regained through learning and insights.

Ning Zhuo was unreserved and even eager, "Using the Inheriting Dao Jade Page might be more convenient."

The relationship between him and Sun Lingtong was so close, almost inseparable.

In fact, he plotted around the Inheriting Dao Jade Page previously, wanting to use his formidable learning ability to improve his realm, then help Sun Lingtong advance.

It’s about learning first and leading later.

Now, Sun Lingtong was merely giving his Array Dao Realm to Ning Zhuo first.

Ning Zhuo, "But refining the Inheriting Dao Jade Page takes quite an effort. Acquiring the needed materials also takes time."

Sun Lingtong, "For cultivators who have reached the ninth stage, it’s not just you, Little Zhuo. There are others."

"Let Chef get in touch with them and see if we can purchase at a high price."

Ning Zhuo nodded, finding this approach agreeable.

He wasn’t short on money now but lacked time. Solving this problem with money would be ideal.

"However, during the Xingyun Trial period, when opportunities are everywhere, would there be cultivators willing to make paper for others?" Ning Zhuo was unsure.

Sun Lingtong shrugged, indifferent, "Let’s try."

"Hmm." Ning Zhuo nodded.

The two discussed and agreed.

Taoist Artifact Luoshu!

Even one page is extraordinarily valuable.

Those who used it all praised it!

Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong used it repeatedly, always giving a thumbs-up each time.

Actually extracting Luoshu would likely greatly enhance Sun Ning’s computational abilities, also securing it safely.

Leaving it in Shuyu Pavilion was not safe!

Ning Zhuo continued to recuperate, while Sun Lingtong disguised himself and returned to the black market in the cave.

The gray-robed cultivator, upon seeing Sun Lingtong, trembled all over, nervously greeted, "Elder Sun, you’re, you’re here."

Sun Lingtong laughed secretly.

He and Ning Zhuo had discussed earlier, knowing the inside story about Qin De, clearly understanding that the gray-robed cultivator couldn’t fulfill the previous agreement.

But Sun Lingtong pretended not to know, eagerly said, "I’m here! I’ve given you so much time, you should have succeeded, right? Where’s the goods? Let me see!"

"About that, that..." The gray-robed cultivator was extremely embarrassed, hesitating.

Sun Lingtong’s expression changed, his gaze and tone becoming sharp, "Friend, you didn’t succeed again, did you? Or are you trying to raise the price last minute?"

"You must remember, we’ve signed a Divine Contract."

Divine Contract!

The mere thought of this made the gray-robed cultivator feel numb. He regretted his decision greatly.

He originally thought he could get Qin De’s soul but found no opportunity despite his full effort.

He messed up!

This business was thoroughly messed up!

Now with the goods owner confronting him, the gray-robed cultivator could only helplessly smile, forced to explain.

Sun Lingtong listened halfway and simply waved, "These are all excuses! I don’t care, I just want the goods!"

"Are you trying to play me?"

"I just want the goods!"

"Just deliver them!!"

"If not, heh."

Sun Lingtong’s cold laugh sent shivers through the gray-robed cultivator, sweat already gathering on his forehead.

"How was I supposed to know it would turn out like this!"

"Now I know, the waters here are very deep."

"Elder Sun, you wouldn’t knowingly set me up, would you?"

Sun Lingtong immediately responded with a cold laugh, "You broke the promise first, yet you’re blaming me? Fine, fine! This deal’s off, have you thought about how to handle the Divine Contract backlash?"

"Please, please, let’s discuss this, Elder Sun!" The gray-robed cultivator instantly relented, begging mercy.

If Sun Lingtong agreed to terminate the contract, he wouldn’t suffer the Divine Contract backlash.

Sun Lingtong glared, his stance resolute.

Terminate the contract?

Impossible!

The gray-robed cultivator felt despair, but he was prepared, forcing himself to continue despite the pain, "Elder Sun, don’t be quick to reject, I have good compensation for you!"
