Chapter 474: Chapter 95: Humbly speaking, I am the Earth Element Grandmaster!

The Guerrilla General’s seal was issued the next day.

It was made of bronze, with a perfectly square imprint surface, each side about three inches long, and very thick.

Carved on the seal was a swallow with outspread wings, its tail as sharp as scissors, and the wings unfolded, creating the impression of a bird shooting into flight.

The characters on the seal read “Guerrilla General”, with sharp and forceful strokes, symbolizing the mobility and expertise in surprise attacks often exhibited by the subordinates of the Guerrilla General.

Zhang Hei ran his thick fingers over the Guerrilla General’s seal and pressed it firmly a few times.

He then tucked the seal into his chest.

...

Guan Hong didn’t even glance at it and simply took it.

Liu Er, on the other hand, scrutinized the seal in his hand and thought to himself, “The Guerrilla General’s seal is carved with a swallow, I have heard that General Yingyang’s General Seal displays a magnificent eagle plunging down with its wings outspread.”

Liu Er, being half-human and half-demon, had four ears to prove it. In other Cultivation Kingdoms, it was quite rare for someone of his mixed heritage to be granted such an esteemed military rank.

To put it nicely, he was a relative of the Blood Slaughter Dynasty, but to be frank, there were too many such relatives, and the influence of the Blood Slaughter Dynasty had long since fallen into a quagmire.

Ning Zhuo was also examining the seal in his hand.

This was his Military Adviser’s seal.

The seal had a round face, two inches in diameter, made of jade, and felt smooth and gentle to the touch.

The front of the seal depicted the bright moon and clouds, with the characters for “Military Adviser” inscribed in a dignified and elegant manner.

Holding the seal and channeling Mana, Ning Zhuo simply made the seal glow slightly.

Seeing this, Zhang Hei laughed and said, “It’s just a minor token, what’s there to test? Only higher-ranking General Seals and Official Seals contain Military Art and Official Skill. Our Guerrilla General Seal doesn’t have it, let alone your Military Adviser’s seal.”

Liu Er gathered his thoughts and carefully put away his seal. Seeing Ning Zhuo’s studious look, he couldn’t help but chuckle, “Brother Ning, if you want a better seal, you should earn some merits. I can report them for you, and getting you two or three ranks higher wouldn’t be an issue.”

Ning Zhuo quickly declined, “I’m just studying it because it’s my first time receiving such a national treasure.”

“I’m not particularly interested in the Official Seal of Liangzhu Country.”

“It is because my family is located in Southern Bean Country, and if I wish to govern or command an army in the future, I should seek it from Southern Bean Country.”

“Not to conceal from the three Generals, I am great friends with Zhu Xuanji, a member of the Nandou Royal Family. He once intended to offer me a position.”

“I am aware of my limitations, being only at the Foundation Establishment Early Stage with limited experience and not yet capable of significant responsibility.”

“Now by following the three Generals, I hope to use this opportunity to join you in battle and see if there’s a chance to avenge my family and the Six Cave Sect!”

“After that, I need to carry treasures and conclude trade with a major power to fulfill my family’s mission.”

“And then, I need to travel even more to broaden my horizons.”

“I see,” said Guan Hong, stroking his beard.

Zhang Hei glared, “Zhu Xuanji of Southern Bean Country? He’s a renowned Divine Hunter, famous throughout the nation. I’ve heard of him.”

Once again, Zhang Hei looked at Ning Zhuo with new respect. He hadn’t expected the Foundation Establishment Cultivator before him to have such close ties with a celebrity.

Hearing this, a renewed sense of regret swelled in Liu Er’s heart.

Ning Zhuo, with his treasure materials and abundant wealth, not to mention his masterful Wood Element Magic, displayed extraordinary combat prowess despite being at the Foundation Establishment Early Stage.

Now, hearing about Ning Zhuo’s powerful connections, with even Zhu Xuanji favoring him, Liu Er realized why he had inquired so much. He wanted to win over Ning Zhuo and the Ning Family for his own benefit. However, Ning Zhuo’s words made it clear that this young Cultivator should not be underestimated; he was a son of the Ning Family and had deep ties with the Nandou Royal Family. His background was significant, and he was beyond the reach of Liu Er’s influence.

“At least, not right now!” Liu Er clenched his teeth inwardly, his yearning for fame and fortune growing ever stronger in that moment.

In the evening.

A military force slowly set out, entering Canglin Immortal City.

The setting sun’s light reflected off their golden armor, making them appear even more noble and majestic.

The Golden Spear Army!

One of Liangzhu Country’s Forbidden Armies had officially arrived at Canglin Immortal City.

Although this news had already spread throughout the city, when the citizens actually witnessed such a disciplined military force, they couldn’t help but become solemn and marvel in their hearts at such a strong army!

The army flags soared high, rustling in the evening breeze.

The entire army rode horses, their hoofbeats powerful and resonant.

Each soldier held a golden spear that pointed straight to the sky, forming a forest of spears and emitting an endless sharp aura.

Sun Gan, as the Commander of the Golden Spear Army, rode at the very front of the formation.

His expression was stern, his gaze as sharp as a sword, sweeping over the surroundings. The city’s residents unconsciously avoided his stare, not daring to meet his eyes.

In stark contrast to him was an official from Liangzhu Country.

Though he also rode a majestic horse with straight posture, his demeanor did not fit in with the Golden Spear Army—it was obvious he was an outsider.

But no one dared to underestimate this official.

For the official uniform he wore and the staff he held denoted his status—an imperial envoy!

Unlike other military forces, the Golden Spear Army, being the least in number and the most loyal and reliable, did not set camp outside the city but was accommodated directly inside Canglin Immortal City.

The Immortal City Lord had already arranged their lodgings, taking the initiative to welcome Sun Gan and the imperial envoy and cordially invited them to join the banquet that night.

Sun Gan, with a face of iron, indicated that he would consider attending the banquet only after the troops were properly settled.

The imperial envoy laughed heartily, requesting this evening’s banquet include the leaders of several military forces, so he could get to know his colleagues and bond with them.
