Chapter 521: Chapter 118: Irreconcilable Differences

Shuangjing continued to use talismans, several in a row without any repetition, casting them onto himself.

Hoo hoo hoo!

A series of Nascent Soul Level talismans were consumed in quick succession, his combat power soaring to more than three times what it was before.

Lord Long was beating the Golden Core Deputy Generals into a wretched state, and he noticed Shuangjing amplifying his own strength. At first, he didn’t care much, but soon his complexion changed, and his gaze turned extremely cold.

Shuangjing’s aura had risen too much, almost reaching the peak of the Nascent Soul!

As for the treasures he used, most of them were unrecognizable to Lord Long. A few he did recognize were so valuable that they startled him deeply.

Lord Long let out a long howl to the sky, at first the sound of a human voice, which soon turned into a majestic dragon’s roar.

Divine Gifted Technique—Vitality Collapse!

The life force he absorbed from others was all transformed into a violent surge of life energy, erupting in that moment, boosting his state in every aspect.

His height shot up again, reaching a full sixteen feet. His frame was already burly, but now even more majestic. His upper body was like a city wall, and his robust lower half in combat trousers was stretched by the bulging leg muscles, looking like they were going to tear.

“Can this old geezer get even stronger?!”

“Damn it, he has absorbed our life force, his aura has become mixed, and the battle formation’s weakening of him has drastically reduced.”

“Not just that, even the national power is hesitating, feeling like he’s one of our own!”

The Golden Core Deputy Generals realized the subtlety and were all struck with terror.

They were able to hold their ground in direct combat solely relying on the support of the battle formation, military power, and national power, etc. In contrast, Lord Long was subjected to multiple reductions and suppressions.

But now, Lord Long’s own combat power had surged by a large margin. Having reduced the weakening of the national power, he immediately broke the deadlock that White Jade Camp had been barely maintaining!

Which of the Golden Core Deputy Generals didn’t have injuries on them?

Seeing Lord Long’s frenzied stance, they all hesitated, only daring to surround him, not daring to attack!

Realizing this, Shuangjing was terrified.

If the generals show signs of falling back in the midst of battle, it would undoubtedly be a massive blow to the morale of the troops.

He hastily bellowed, “Change formation!”

The General’s Seal was upon him, and the Golden Core Deputy Generals immediately received the instructions, all letting out a huge sigh of relief, quickly retreating.

The soldiers of White Jade Camp hurriedly shifted from the Seven Stars Gathering Formation to the Moon Surrounding Stars Formation.

Starlight twinkled, and this time instead of converging upon the seven Deputy Generals, it formed a huge pillar of moonlight influx, all pouring onto Shuangjing.

Shuangjing’s aura rose again, his combat power skyrocketing, surging wildly!

Shuangjing opened his mouth and exhaled a breath of sword energy.

The sword energy at first was just a mass of air, emitting a platinum color.

Then in an instant, it shot out, turning into a rainbow of light, its brilliance so intense, it was blinding.

White Tiger Sky-tearing Sword Qi!

This was one of the famed Four Symbols Sword Qi, filled with military and slaughter intent, complementing the battle formation and the battlefield.

This was Shuangjing’s ultimate trump card.

The platinum sword qi reached its target in a blink, not giving Lord Long any time or chance to dodge.

Boom!

The intense aura was like a hurricane sweeping through the surroundings, the blinding platinum brilliance shooting up to the sky, forming a massive column of light, piercing straight through the clouds above.

Shuangjing laughed heartily, “The Overlord’s Desperation Pill I’ve taken grants me a dominating strength for 30 minutes, worth thirty-eight thousand Mid Grade Spirit Stones on the market.”

“My Ant-Shaking Powder, with the effect of the Demon’s Dismemberment Technique but with no repercussions, one bottle is worth forty-two thousand Mid Grade Spirit Stones.”

“My Spirit Sound Talisman, Fierce Dragon Talisman, Fierce Talisman, Dragon and Tiger Empowerment Talisman, and so on, total up to over one million six hundred thousand Mid Grade Spirit Stones.”

“In order to master a single breath of White Tiger Sky-tearing Sword Qi, I used the bone marrow of a Nascent Soul Level White Tiger, drew the Sword Spirit from a Nascent Soul Level Magic Sword, and expended Cangtian Tears. Over sixty years, I’ve killed thirteen White Tigers, destroyed seven Nascent Soul Level Magic Swords, and used sixty-four drops of Cangtian Tears. Just the material costs alone reached seventy-six million Mid Grade Spirit Stones.”

“Old geezer, can you withstand this? Can you bear it? Hahaha!”

“Hmm?!”

Shuangjing’s expression changed slightly.

The platinum brilliance was rapidly weakening, and a giant black figure became so clear within the column of light.

The brilliance quickly dissipated, and Lord Long stood in a deep pit with a radius of one mu, his figure mountainous, standing unyielding.

The translucent Dragon Scales on his body were in tatters, blood visible in many places, but his aura remained overwhelmingly strong.

On his broad, solid shoulder, a small boy with dragon horns sat.

The child had golden pupils and large red coral-like dragon horns, stuffing his thumb into his mouth, making a sucking sound.

Seeing Shuangjing staring at him intently, a hint of blush appeared on the child’s cheeks, instantly transforming into its true form—a right shoulder armor.

The right shoulder armor was shaped like a dragon head with an open mouth, enveloping Lord Long’s right shoulder.

It was gold and red, smooth as a mirror on the surface, scattering a metallic luster. The dragon’s eyes were like diamonds, as brilliant as stars.

The shoulder armor also had detailed cloud patterns. These cloud patterns were slowly moving, layer upon layer, endlessly changing.

“Spiritual Nature manifested!” Shuangjing and others were dumbfounded.

Lord Long smirked proudly, “That move was indeed powerful.”

“However, if what you said is true and you’ve spent so much on Spirit Stones to forge such an assault.”

“Then how much should this Spiritual Treasure of mine be valued?”

Shuangjing’s Adam’s apple moved, unable to speak for a moment.

Lord Long looked down, his figure suddenly vanished from the spot.

In the next instant, he almost teleported in front of Shuangjing, pushing fiercely with a dragon palm.

Boom!

“Damn it!”

Shuangjing couldn’t dodge in time, and braced quickly, only to be slammed three zhang into the ground.
