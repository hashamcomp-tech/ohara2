Chapter 600 -167: Hongmen Wine

Due to the surrender of the Dice Bone Demon God, the intense battle abruptly came to an end.

The dojo quickly dissipated, revealing the original appearance of the Giant Tree Mountain.

The soldiers still kept their gaze on Ning Zhuo, more precisely, on the string of beads on his wrist.

Ning Zhuo’s gaze was no exception.

At the same time, he communicated using his Divine Sense: “Dice Bone Demon God, are you truly willing to submit to me?”

The Dice Bone Demon God sighed: “Having lost six times in a row, what can I do?”

“Master, you need not doubt.”

“I am a Demon transformed from a Bone Dice, receiving offerings and incense. Although my strength has increased, it has also brought drawbacks, that is adhering to related principles.”

“One of the principles is losing six times in a row, means completely losing all.”

Ning Zhuo naturally wouldn’t take his word for it and continued to investigate: “How can you prove this?”

“This is simple. Soon, your servant will reopen the dojo. You, Master, enter the dojo to comprehend the Tao. With enough time, you will be able to understand the related principles. Then you will naturally see that your servant’s words are true.”

“Dojo…” Ning Zhuo’s eyes twinkled with a sharp light.

The meaning of cultivation, one explanation is to cultivate the true principles of the world.

Cultivators who grasp the principles can spread their own paths, forming a domain. This domain often becomes a place in itself, like the Dice Bone Demon God’s dojo, which can cover the original mountains and forests.

In the dojo, wise beings can closely and maximally interact with the “Tao,” and those with strong comprehension can gain enlightenment.

Once a cultivator deploys the dojo, their strength reaches its peak state.

However, the drawback that comes with it is that the wise beings inside the dojo, by touching the Tao, would achieve various enlightenments, quickly understand the cultivator’s foundation.

Just now, Liu Er knew the rule of losing all because of this.

“But Liu Er’s speed in grasping this layer seems a bit too much.” Thinking this, Ning Zhuo asked the Dice Bone Demon God.

The Dice Bone Demon God nodded: “This is one of the deepest secrets of my dojo, yet he uncovered it. He must have some method or innate ability to quickly understand the Tao I mastered.”

Having heard this, Ning Zhuo was quite certain that the words of the Dice Bone Demon God were true.

Since according to his suggested method, it would be easy to verify.

Ning Zhuo then asked about matters concerning lifespan: “What Cultivation Technique do you practice? It seems related to lifespan?”

The Dice Bone Demon God said: “As for Divination and Qi Fortune, those skills belong to the hidden aspects of the Cultivation Hundred Skills. Unfortunately, your servant did not have such luck to obtain such a true inheritance.”

“The reason I know such principles is purely due to my origin.”

“However, master, if you ask about the principles of lifespan, your servant does have some understanding and experience.”

“For instance, lifespan and Qi Fortune are different.”

“Lifespan refers to a number, a base, a foundation; fortune involves movement, operation, functioning.”

“Lifespan is static, fortune is dynamic.”

“Lifespan is already a result, while fortune is a trend.”

“Master, your lifespan is not bad, and your Qi Fortune is even incredibly fierce, and that’s why your servant lost six times in a row.”

“Rolling six points each time is not particularly rare. As long as the foundation is higher than mine, in this dojo, one often can roll higher points. If it’s a being at the Transforming Spirit Level, whose Cultivation Level far exceeds mine, definitely every roll would be six.”

“But even if it’s at the Transforming Spirit Level, every roll being six, your servant might not necessarily lose every time.”

“After all, your servant is the master of this dojo. Others have one dice, but your servant has two, and the advantage is very significant.”

Ning Zhuo suddenly realized: “You mean, every roll being six is not strange, what’s strange is that you continously rolled six times, and the sum of the two dice each time was lower than six?”

Dice Bone Demon God: “Exactly!”

“This shows, master, that your strong Qi Fortune has significantly influenced my lifespan, making the points I roll each time lesser than yours.”

“Your servant dares to ask, master, what exactly is this technique?”

Ning Zhuo was baffled: “I don’t know!”

He paused: “However, I do have a treasure on me that suppresses Qi Fortune.”

The Dice Bone Demon God denied: “That won’t work. Suppressing Qi Fortune means fending off negative external influences, safeguarding one’s own lifespan, there would be a lower limit.”

“Master, I am sure that something has happened, without you knowing.”

“And it has influenced your Qi Fortune.”

Ning Zhuo felt extremely puzzled: “Who would be so kind?”

He communicated with the Dice Bone Demon God, while Liu Guanzhang began to take control of the situation, starting to clean up the battlefield.

Seeing Ning Zhuo standing in place, staring intently at the dice bracelet on his wrist, Liu Er’s heart stirred with a familiar feeling once again.

He felt empty, as if someone had stolen a very important treasure from him.

The first time he felt this way was when Ning Zhuo and Mu Lan publicly signed a marriage contract.

Consequently, his resentment towards Ning Zhuo deepened even further.

“Caw caw caw!”

A series of harsh bird calls came from the sky, attracting the attention of all the soldiers.

Subsequently, an Iron Feathered Eagle, beating its broad wings, descended from the sky.

Someone wanted to take a shot at it, but was quickly stopped by Liu Guanzhang.

Because the three Golden Core Cultivators saw the Token on the Iron Feathered Eagle’s body, their General Seals responded to the Token.

The Iron Feathered Eagle hovered midair, looking down at the crowd of soldiers, and said in a hoarse voice: “I am Commander Du’s messenger. Three Generals Camp, you have fought well, and even subdued a local Demon God.”

“Be aware, this local Demon God is very familiar with the enemy’s secrets, and these pieces of intelligence are extremely important for our country to clear the Thousand Peak Forest.”

“Moreover, just by spreading the news that the Dice Bone Demon God has submitted to our country, it would lay a massive blow on the morale of the Thousand Peak Forest.”
