Chapter 887: Chapter 307: Madam Ning Assembles Her Army (Part 2)

She changed her stance, no longer standing straight, but crouched down slightly.

She flipped her wrist, holding the Big Snake Scythe horizontally across her chest, with the long handle parallel to the ground.

Her face was calm, with no sorrow or joy in her eyes. Then, under everyone’s watchful gaze, she made an extremely light and gentle motion—like dusting away the dust from a scripture table—by slicing horizontally with that strange scythe.

Mysterious Path—Compassionate Cut.

Silent and unperceived.

Where the scythe’s blade passed, space was not torn apart, and there was no sound, light, or airwaves.

Yet still charging, the nearest group of Ghost Riders, both man and horse, suddenly froze. In the next instant, both the human and horse ghost bodies collapsed, turning into powder, fluttering in the air, and being carried away by the wind.

Then, this phenomenon spread rapidly, covering the entire Ghost Cavalry.

No matter how dispersed they were, where they ran to, or what protective measures they used, they all had the same ending.

The whole scene was deathly silent!

The pupils of the Bone Tomb Taoist shrank to needlepoint size.

"Wh-Where did this peerless fiend come from?!"

"Why wasn’t there any intelligence about this?!"

"With an existence like this, what talk is there of conquering White Paper Immortal City?!"

Run, run, run!

The Bone Tomb Taoist was the farthest away. Originally seeing his side with the upper hand, he followed behind, approaching Ning Zhuo and the others, wanting to rely on allies to pick up advantages.

But the situation changed too quickly!

Just a light cut, and the massive cavalry regiment was gone. The previously imposing Ghost Generals, leaving only the Ghost Path Golden Core floating in the air.

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin, with just her strength, completely overturned the battle situation!

The Bone Tomb Taoist already had injuries and turned to run immediately.

In fact, even if he were in peak condition, he wouldn’t dare to oppose Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin.

"So strong, so strong! Mother’s combat strength is definitely at the Nascent Soul Level, even ordinary Nascent Soul Cultivators can’t stop her."

"Mysterious Path..."

Ning Zhuo was immensely shocked.

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin stood upright again, holding the Big Snake Scythe vertically, her gaze distantly fixed on the Bone Tomb Taoist.

The Bone Tomb Taoist’s speed was incredibly fast!

In the eyes of Ning Zhuo and Qing Chi, he had almost shrunk to a small dot on the horizon.

It was truly impressive that, with an injured body, he could escape so fast!

In fact, even the Bone Tomb Taoist himself was surprised—so he could escape so decisively!

Just when everyone thought Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin was out of reach, and the Bone Tomb Taoist’s heart slightly settled, Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin raised her arm slightly, lifting the Big Snake Scythe higher, then tapped the end of the handle on the ground.

Silent and unperceived.

But the Bone Tomb Taoist, sprinting at high speed, seemed to be hit on the back of his head with a heavy blow, his eyes rolled back, and he collapsed unconscious on the spot.

His speed was so fast that he skidded hundreds of steps over the terrain of the Netherworld, rolling non-stop, his body tumbling like a broken doll, finally coming to a halt after a violent struggle.

Everyone was speechless: ...

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin spoke gently: "Alright, go bring that Taoist back."

"Un-Understood." Qing Chi stammered, giving a signal, and a subordinate spurred on a dark horse, rushing out.

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin used Divine Sense Transmission, her tone gentle: "Little Zhuo, I deliberately spared this Taoist’s life, it’s for you to use."

"You have some tribulation on you, connected to the Soul Devouring Sect, there’s a cause and effect involved."

"This is a Super Sect, with magic treasures to suppress Lifespan, so be cautious, the sooner it’s resolved, the better."

"Otherwise, the collision and exhaustion of Lifespan will be astonishingly damaging, and very hidden."

Ning Zhuo quickly responded: "Mother, did you calculate this? The Heavenly and Earthly Ghosts of the Soul Devouring Sect indeed have a great feud with me."

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin slightly nodded, continuing to communicate with Ning Zhuo in secret: "Use this captive to negotiate with the Soul Devouring Sect. Say that if this matter isn’t settled, you’ll send this Taoist to the Myriad Images Sect."

"This time, the Soul Devouring Sect and Lord of Wanchuan joined forces to plot against the White Paper Immortal City and the Myriad Images Sect, which is a significant friction between Super Sects."

"The Soul Devouring Sect is not afraid of you, but they will be wary of the Myriad Images Sect. And indeed, they were out of line this time."

"If the Bone Tomb Taoist falls into the hands of the Myriad Images Sect, they will have testimony and evidence, extremely disadvantageous to the Soul Devouring Sect."

"Therefore, this is very important to the Soul Devouring Sect."

"Use this bargaining chip to fully negotiate with the Soul Devouring Sect. As for what you can gain, how much you benefit, it all depends on yourself."

Ning Zhuo was invigorated: "Understood!"

Immediately, he pondered, stroking his chin: "So, there are hidden exchanges between Lifespan."

"The reason Super Sects last so long is because they have treasures that suppress Lifespan."

"Then in the future, I’ll try to avoid entanglements with them."

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin slightly shook her head: "This is the important significance of being kind-hearted with others."

"However, you need not be overly concerned. The Buddha Heart Demon Seal on you also has a miraculous use for suppressing Lifespan."

"Take this."

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin conveyed through Divine Sense, handing various miscellaneous treasures to Ning Zhuo.

They were precious items, but quite chaotic.

They were things scattered by the Taixu Disintegration Ruler earlier.

The Paperman Giant Statue picked them up before jumping into the black hole path.

"Before leaving, my intuition touched me, realizing there are objects among these crucial for you."

"But precisely what, even I can’t fully grasp." Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin advised.

Ning Zhuo hurriedly received them, nodding earnestly, looking like a well-behaved child: "Mother, I’ll take good care of them."

Their communication ceased, Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin raised her head, her voice gentle, yet resonating through the entire scene: "Alright, next, everyone will listen to my command."

No one raised any objections.

The whole scene was silent.

Didn’t you see Ning Zhuo obediently nodding?

The main reason was that Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin’s displayed combat strength was too powerful.

Everyone listened quietly to Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin’s instructions.

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin lowered her head to look at her left hand.

She continuously calculated with her fingers, quickly making arrangements.

Everyone present would be divided into two routes.

One route, led by her commanding the Paperman Army, taking Sun Lingtong, toward the Wangchuan Immortal City.

The other route, led by the Qing Jiao Army and Ning Zhuo, heading to the altar.

"This setup has been brewing for many years, leveraging the power of the Mysterious Plain Scholar." Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin glanced at the Paperman Army behind her.

She sighed: "If we fail this time, then Lord of Wanchuan’s power will certainly surge, inevitably invading White Paper Immortal City again."

"When that time comes, the Mortal World Immortal City will surely suffer great devastation."

"I’ve calculated: Although the altar is far from Wangchuan Immortal City, the secret connection is very close."

"To disrupt Lord of Wanchuan’s great plan, we must strike with both hands! And in timely coordination."

She transmitted thoughts through Divine Sense, only speaking to Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong.

Ning Zhuo worried: "Mother, according to your arrangements, do we have to split up?"

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin nodded: "Trust me, this is the best arrangement."

Ning Zhuo gritted his teeth, looking at Sun Lingtong: "Boss, please take care of my mother!"

Sun Lingtong rolled his eyes: "Take care of Auntie? With me?"

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin comforted: "There’s no need to worry. I absorbed the spiritual nature in the Paperman Giant Statue, and also gathered a lot of military evil energy, enough to ensure this major battle."

"I just calculated, there is a secret connection method between you two, what is it?"

Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong exchanged glances. The former said: "Mother, are you referring to the Divine Power Life Thread?"

After his introduction, Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin said contentedly: "Very good, Little Zhuo’er, you’ve mastered this divine skill."

"Moreover, you have deliberately restrained it, not abusing it. This is especially outstanding, showing your excellent character."

She calculated again, suddenly becoming serious: "You must maintain this good habit!"

"This habit will provide you with immensely important assistance in the future."

Ning Zhuo felt genuinely happy being praised by his mother: "Mother, I use the Life Hanging Divine Power as swiftly as I can withdraw it."

Buddhist Doctor Meng Yaoyin glanced at Qing Chi: "Especially with her, you must uphold this principle."
