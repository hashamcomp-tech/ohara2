Chapter 735 -233: Array Slash Demon Heart!

“The mechanical carriage has finally fallen into my hands!” Ning Zhuo’s heart surged with excitement.

Previously, he recklessly intruded into the Six Cave Sect’s territory, which made Ning Zhuo reluctant to use the Mechanical Dragon to further advance.

A direct confrontation became his first choice.

In the end, he caught Lin You off guard, greatly underestimating Ning Zhuo’s enmity.

Ning Zhuo was skilled in endurance.

He had been enduring since he was two years old, enduring for over ten years, and achieved extraordinary success at the Lava Fairy Palace.

This time, in Liangzhu Country, he endured once again and achieved his goal!

“My son!” Suddenly, in the midst of intense battle at the National Front Line Camp, the Demon Heart Cave Master’s expression changed dramatically.

He clutched his chest, feeling that a warning planted on Lin You had been triggered.

This Six Cave Sect Demon Dao Cave Master immediately realized that his most cherished disciple was heavily damaged!

“You keep fighting!” Demon Heart Cave Master threw this remark to Poison Scorpion Cave Master and Blood Shadow Cave Master before instantly leaving the core battlefield.

“What?!” Poison Scorpion and Blood Shadow Cave Masters were left stunned.

Demons are selfish by nature, and Demon Heart Cave Master didn’t care about them; he was furious and followed the sensing straight to the culprit.

“Dare to lay a hand on my son! All of you shall die!” Demon Heart Cave Master shot into the sky, attacking Ning Zhuo’s group.

He flew high in the air, immediately facing numerous military weapon strikes.

Magic – Qiu Jin Demonic Scales!

He maintained this Demon Dao Technique, his entire body covered in a thick and resilient Blood Vein Armor.

This protective technique allowed Demon Heart Cave Master to withstand numerous attacks, quickly crossing the battlefield to appear above his enemies.

Magic – Nine Abyss Heart Devour!

Thud.

It was as if a drum was struck at their chests, causing the hearts of soldiers from Three Generals Camp and Red Flower Camp to race, skipping a beat.

Thud.

Another drum-like vibration, hearts racing even faster.

Thud.

The third drum-like heartbeat left many soldiers feeling palpitations.

Thud thud thud…

After three more consecutive drumbeats, many soldiers clutched their chests, half-kneeling or falling over.

The battle formations of Three Generals Camp and Red Flower Camp faced a severe test; nearly half the soldiers lost their ability to move.

Hearts raced again.

“Ugh…” Some soldiers turned white-eyed, bleeding from their seven orifices, instantly dying from heart explosion.

Fighting with all his might, Demon Heart Cave Master demonstrated his terrifying prowess as the second Cave Master of Six Cave Sect.

As casualties threatened Three Generals Camp and Red Flower Camp, Zhang Zhongyi quickly intervened, casting numerous Elixir Pills which instantly turned into Elixir Qi, protecting everyone’s hearts.

Despite this, after the seventh heartbeat, more than fifty souls were lost.

Bear in mind, this army was bolstered by military arts and cultivation.

“Such an attack, how vicious and bizarre!” Liu Er commented, blood trickling from his mouth.

“We can’t let him continue attacking!” Mu Lan frowned deeply, drew her bow, and nocked an arrow.

Military Art – Bursting Bluff!

Yet, Demon Heart Cave Master forged ahead relentlessly.

Thud.

The eighth drumbeat saw sixty more soldiers perish instantly.

Mu Lan’s bluff proved fruitless, her brow furrowed even tighter. It was clear she wouldn’t foolishly believe Demon Heart Cave Master had lost his mind; the obvious answer was he had seen through her bluff.

Liu Guanzhang attacked in succession, but with Three Generals Camp’s morale in disarray and diminished military power, their attacks appeared feeble. Even those hits that landed on Demon Heart Cave Master were easily withstood.

This revealed the flaws of the Military Family.

Combining the power of many, targeting the army can shake its foundation.

Of course, not just anyone can achieve this.

Demon Heart Cave Master’s techniques directly affected opponents’ hearts, largely circumventing conventional military defenses, achieving startling results.

“Dare lay hands on my son, who gave you the courage?”

“All of you shall die!!!” he screamed.

Clang.

A golden light, like a bolt of lightning, struck Demon Heart Cave Master’s chest, halting his furious momentum.

In the next moment, a downpour of Five Elements Magic rained upon him, forcing him to the ground.

Demon Heart Cave Master fell, covered in dirt, his Qiu Jin Demonic Scales showing cracks, injuring him.

Crucially, external attacks forcibly interrupted his Nine Abyss Heart Devour magic, causing backlash and making him spit blood.

“Impossible! Why weren’t you suppressed?” Demon Heart Cave Master questioned, staring at the culprit who injured him – Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo stood tall, his body straight as a spear.

His recent blood spitting, frowning, pain was all an act.

He was completely unscathed!

“Husband,” Mu Lan’s eyes sparkled.

“Strategist, you’re incredible!” Zhang Hei rejoiced, giving a difficult thumbs-up.

Just as Jin Tun River countered Ning Zhuo’s Heroic Return Skill, Ning Zhuo’s Fire Element Heart Temple countered Demon Heart Cave Master’s ace!

The white-clothed youth’s heart had long been modified, although affected, the impact was too minimal.

“Struck down the weak, now comes the strong.” Ning Zhuo shouted, “Perfect! You and your son committed many evils; now is my time to purge demons and defend the Tao, for the benefit of all!”

Demon Heart Cave Master’s eyes twitched with rage: “Brat, I will grind your bones to dust.”

Boom boom boom…

Both sides launched into furious combat.

Demonic Qi surged from Demon Heart Cave Master, his physical strength formidable, his vicious heart-targeting attacks nearly unmanageable.

Ning Zhuo focused on stability, relying on Hundred-Armed Arhat Armor and Fire Element Heart Temple’s protection, using various Five Elements Magic, striking down the once-dominant Demon Heart Cave Master.
