Chapter 580 -157: Liu Er Borrows a Treasure

Divine Skills——Spirit Sound Pulse Detection!

From the moment Ning Zhuo expressed his intention to find Mu Lan and left the camp in front of Liu Er, Liu Er used his innate ability.

His innate ability, as always, did not disappoint, allowing him to overhear the details of the conversation between Ning Zhuo, Mu Lan, and Zhang Zhongyi.

“The ‘Xuanjin Armor Breaking Technique’ passed down through your family? Didn’t the strategist say he wasn’t interested in this Cultivation Technique?”

“It’s because I didn’t promise him military provisions, so he went to seek help at the Red Flower Camp. This is merely a trade of interests!”

When he heard that Ning Zhuo had only looked at the “Xuanjin Armor Breaking Technique” for a while and then discussed and designed a healing plan with Zhang Zhongyi on the spot, Liu Er fell into a momentary silence.

“Truly born from a prestigious family.”

“No, even for someone born from a great family, the talent and perception of Strategist Ning Zhuo are extraordinarily rare.”

Despite the growing hatred in Liu Er’s heart, he had to admit that Ning Zhuo was outstanding.

Later, when Zhang Zhongyi’s misplaced acupuncture needle caused Mu Lan to spit out a bit of fresh blood, Liu Er’s expression slightly brightened.

“Good, there are mistakes in the treatment, Ning Zhuo is also responsible!”

But the next moment, he heard Zhang Zhongyi shouting, “Quick, administer palace massage and blood transfusion for the General!”

“Palace massage and blood transfusion?!” Liu Er’s ears perked up, his expression drastically changed.

Then, he heard Zhang Zhongyi sigh and leave the tent.

Liu Er’s expression changed again, his heart squeezed tightly: “This old wretch, this old wretch!”

He realized Zhang Zhongyi was trying to pair up Ning Zhuo and Mu Lan.

Liu Er was furious, gnashing his teeth.

He could no longer sit still, stood up, and paced back and forth in the tent, his expression agitated, while furiously spurring his Innate ability to listen intently.

“No, it won’t happen.”

“Ning Zhuo comes from a great family, a gentleman of integrity, extremely trustworthy. He said he wouldn’t engage in Dual Cultivation with Mu Lan, so he will surely hold to it!”

Liu Er consoled himself, continually persuading himself to calm down.

Then, in the next moment, he heard Ning Zhuo speaking— “There’s one last blockage, I need to break through with full force, just endure a bit.”

Liu Er felt as if struck by lightning, suddenly stopping in his tracks.

“Blockage? Where in the body is there a blockage?!”

“Break through where?!”

“Endure?!”

Whether Mu Lan could endure or not, Liu Er could no longer hold back.

“No, no!”

“A young man and a young woman alone together, it’s too dangerous, far too dangerous!”

“Ning Zhuo is a young man, vigorous and full of youthful energy. As for Mu Lan, my investigations show though she’s a Golden Core and has been hard training in the military for years, she hasn’t been intimate with any man and remains a virgin!”

“It’s too dangerous, they can’t continue to stay together like this.”

Liu Er hurried to the tent flap.

Just as he lifted the tent flap, he overheard Mu Lan’s soft moan.

In an instant, Liu Er’s eyes widened, his pupils sharply contracting.

“What are you two up to?! What on earth are you two up to?!”

“No, absolutely not!”

Liu Er could no longer remain composed, leaping into the air and flying towards the Red Flower Camp.

Red Flower Camp.

Ning Zhuo, hearing Mu Lan’s voice, abruptly looked up: “What’s wrong?”

Instantly, the romantic atmosphere was completely shattered.

Mu Lan, annoyed, felt the impulse to reach out and smack Ning Zhuo’s head hard.

But facing Ning Zhuo’s clear, innocent eyes, she couldn’t bring herself to do it.

Finally, she scoffed coldly, suppressing the shame in her heart, then forcibly changed the subject: “Your healing techniques still need refinement.”

She assumed Ning Zhuo would retort with something like “I’m not a doctor” or similar.

Instead, Ning Zhuo immediately accepted her criticism, nodding continuously, sincerely responding, “Yes, indeed.”

“The Medical Family possesses profound knowledge; I am merely an outsider, with inherently weak healing abilities.”

“Compared to the esteemed Doctor Zhang Zhongyi, I am no match, and even other ordinary doctors have much more experience and skilled techniques than I do. If it were them, they definitely wouldn’t have caused General Mu Lan pain.”

Mu Lan: …

She paused briefly, then said, “Young Master Ning Zhuo, Dual Cultivation between us is mutually beneficial.”

“From your massive acquisitions of Mechanical Puppets and assembling an army, it’s clear: you don’t just want to participate in the war; you also aim to shine in the upcoming battles and earn military achievements.”

“War is perilous. You’ve experienced an ambush before; you should understand the weight of this word.”

“In such a scenario, Dual Cultivation with me will surely increase your Cultivation Level, helping you to improve your survival chances on the battlefield. Why would you hesitate?”

Ning Zhuo remained silent.

Mid-air, Liu Er, hearing this conversation, became even more anxious.

A gust of wind hit him in the face, and he shouted inwardly: “No, don’t engage in Dual Cultivation, no!”

Red Flower Camp.

Mu Lan, her gaze fixed on the young man in white clothes close to her, continued persuading: “I know your concerns are just about Origin Yang.”

“But is Origin Yang really that important?”
