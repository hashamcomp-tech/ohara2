And now, this former nightmare, the culprit that tormented Ning Xiaohui, became the subject of a repair mission, assigned to Ning Xiaohui.

Not long after, a teleportation ring appeared in front of Ning Xiaohui, and after a burst, delivered the Golden Blood Battle Ape to Ning Xiaohui.

She saw the Golden Blood Battle Ape on the table, her expression exceedingly complex.

Immediately, a vicious look appeared on her face.

Ning Xiaohui looked at the Golden Blood Battle Ape, imagining Ning Zhuo in her mind. She gritted her teeth and said, "Good, this is really good!"

"I will fully repair you, restore you to your best state."

"Then, I will make you the biggest obstacle for those people, and the pain they gave me, I will inflict on them a thousand times over."

The more Ning Xiaohui spoke, the more excited she became, her eyes shining brightly.

From the Golden Blood Battle Ape, she saw the hope of revenge.

Ning Xiaohui focused all her attention, starting to examine the condition of the Golden Blood Battle Ape.

The Golden Blood Battle Ape was mostly injured, some places were quite serious.

Not long ago, Ning Zhuo had just used it to break through the last checkpoint of the Buddhist Sect. This left it almost unrecognizable, with some massive wounds revealing the mechanical parts inside.

After observing for a moment, Ning Xiaohui’s brows furrowed deeply.

"This Golden Blood Battle Ape is so severely damaged that its true strength is becoming increasingly difficult to exert."

"Ning Zhuo must have taken advantage of this, no wonder he was able to drive back this mechanical artifact."

Ning Xiaohui did not detect much of the operational mysteries of the Golden Blood Battle Ape.

In fact, even Ning Zhuo did not know much about this mechanical artifact of his.

"Though I do not understand its structure or have its mechanical diagrams, none of this hinders me from treating it, repairing it."

"My talent, Ice-jade Hands, is just that unreasonable, hahaha..."

Ning Xiaohui took out a medicine bottle, opened the stopper, and poured dozens of elixir pills into her mouth.

Then, she sat cross-legged, frantically refining the medicinal power on the spot, adjusting her state to the best.

She fully activated her talent, encasing the Golden Blood Battle Ape in a giant block of ice.

Ning Xiaohui exhausted all her strength, pushing her talent to the limit, causing blood to slowly flow from her seven orifices.

Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit: "..."

It muttered inwardly, "No, Ning Xiaohui, do you have to be so desperate?"

Although the Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit could fully understand the reasons behind Ning Xiaohui’s actions,

as an observer, the Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit knew very well: the Golden Blood Battle Ape was Ning Zhuo’s mechanical artifact, his most crucial fighter in the Magma Fairy Palace assessment, bar none.

The Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit could not help but wonder, "If Ning Xiaohui finds out in the future that the Golden Blood Battle Ape she painstakingly repaired was the mechanism of the person she hated the most, what would her reaction be?"

The Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit could not imagine such a scenario, but couldn’t help feeling a sense of anticipation.

Just then, its expression changed.

Utilizing the Magma Fairy Palace, its gaze penetrated through layers of space, locking onto Zhu Xuanji.

Zhu Xuanji, ever since obtaining the Mechanical Demon General, had been working on its repair.

Currently, the severed left arm of the Mechanical Demon General had been reattached.

For this, Zhu Xuanji had been repairing it day and night, working tirelessly.

When the reply from the Royal Family reached his hands, he was momentarily dazed before reacting.

Back then, Zhu Xuanji had been searching for the True Scripture to investigate the little monkey.

So, he specifically wrote to the Royal Family seeking help—he needed a portrait of the little monkey grown up.

Now, the Royal Family’s reply had arrived.

Zhu Xuanji unfolded it and saw the Royal Family’s unique encrypted jumbled text.

This didn’t matter.

In his mind, Zhu Xuanji quickly translated these encrypted texts.

Thus, the images of Yuan Dasheng as an adult and in old age surfaced in his mind.

"Yuan Dasheng?!" Zhu Xuanji was stunned.

For a moment, he stood still, like a statue.

But in his mind, thoughts were swirling like a storm, turning everything upside down!

The Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit saw this scene, excitedly standing up, its four claws grounding firmly.

Although it couldn’t understand the content of the Royal Family’s reply, just seeing Zhu Xuanji’s dramatic change in expression, it could guess a thing or two.

"Find Ning Zhuo, find Ning Zhuo!"

"He is the real culprit, the mastermind."

"Arrest him, hurry and arrest him."

"Hahaha."

Anticipating Zhu Xuanji’s imminent capture of Ning Zhuo, the Dragon Turtle Fire Spirit was extremely agitated, its face full of excitement and delight.

It was incredibly impatient, eager to see this moment happen in the next instant!
