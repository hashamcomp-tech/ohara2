Chapter 648 -191: Ginseng Whisker Dragon King vs Du Tiechuan

Dragon King Giant Wood Mountain!

This is the tallest and most towering mountain peak among the whole Thousand Peak Forest.

It stands a thousand zhang high, rising between heaven and earth, weathered by winds and rains, scoured by time.

When it began to tilt, the entire world seemed to lose its balance in a flash.

The mountain’s spine was as straight as a knife cut, cliffs steep, precipices rugged, and every rock and crevice on the mountain accumulated years of buildup.

Besides the rocks, there were tens of thousands of temples on the mountain, large and small, the small ones merely niches, the big ones covering a hundred acres, clustered around the middle of the mountainside like a giant belt.

However, all this splendor could not withstand the sudden forces of fate and the savage ravages of war.

Boom——!

The foot of the mountain was directly breached by Iron Stream Flat Country, the entire mountain collapsing with a force of billions of pounds smashing onto the ground.

The loud thunderous sound was deafening, filling heaven and earth.

The dust raised by the fierce winds soared hundreds of zhang high, like a thick layer of black fog, almost engulfing the entire sky.

The dust in the air spread for miles, covering everywhere, the space between heaven and earth turned a dull yellow, even the sun’s brilliance obscured at this moment.

The mountain’s collapse dragged down countless rocks and clumps of dirt, the ground was overwhelmed with flying dust and debris, followed by a raging landslide; massive amounts of dirt, sand, and vegetation rolled down like floodwaters, producing numerous dull thuds in the valleys.

The surrounding mountains and vegetation, originally lush, turned to nothing in the face of this sky-shattering disaster. Groups of trees were knocked down, torn apart; they fell like straws under the fierce impact of the mountain collapsing.

The cracking boulders, numbering millions, rained down from the sky like a storm, each impact chilling to the bone.

Each falling stone kicked up a storm, lifting dust, thousands of fragments splattering everywhere.

The nearby mountains were violently shaking, some linked peaks also collapsing, or smashed in half.

Boom, boom, boom!

Another series of thunderous roars shook the earth within a hundred miles intensely.

The mountain’s profile no longer intact, rocks piled up, completely covering the valleys and forests. The air was thick with the smell of dirt and dust, unbearably choking.

Yet amidst this dust and debris, the Heavy Armor Main Army was still marching proudly forward, their charge unceasing for a moment!

“Crush Dragon King Giant Wood Mountain!” Du Tiechuan ordered coldly.

Although Dragon King Giant Wood Mountain had collapsed, countless Dragon King Temples on it burst into divine light, covering the mountain with layers of protection.

Thus, the surrounding peaks suffered greatly, but Dragon King Giant Wood Mountain itself was still relatively intact. At least on the several facets that didn’t collide with the ground, it was mostly undamaged.

Under the commander’s order, the morale of countless Heavy Armor soldiers boiled, united as one, charging towards their goal.

If they were really allowed to rush over, Dragon King Giant Wood Mountain would absolutely not be able to withstand it.

At this critical moment, Ginseng Whisker Dragon King had no choice but to make a move.

Countless ginseng whiskers burst through the earth, disregarding sacrifice, continuously carrying out a surround attack.

Though the ginseng whiskers were powerful, Iron Stream Flat Country was like a whole entity, showing no signs of collapse in the short term.

Seeing that Iron Stream Flat Country was about to sweep through Dragon King Giant Wood Mountain…

At this moment, an immensely powerful pressure suddenly spread out, darkening the sky slightly, the air torn by a tremendous force, and all Cultivators nearly skipped a heartbeat, feeling an inexplicable fear and sense of crisis.

The ground suddenly bulged!

A few moments later, the surface suddenly exploded, Ginseng Whisker Dragon King’s huge body breaking through the earth!

Its dragon head, robust as a mountain, was like a fortress, dragon eyes golden orange in color, emitting a terrifying oppressive force, felt by anyone as its towering rage roared!

The thick dragon body, lacking metallic Dragon Scales and not as agile and slim as normal members of the Dragon Clan, instead appeared bloated and bulky.

Thousands of dragon whiskers, tearing through the air, wildly danced mid-air.

A surging wave of air billowed around it, sweeping in all directions.

Ginseng Whisker Dragon King’s body continued to rise, proudly standing in the midst of the battlefield, its huge dragon body imposing an immense pressure, as if it could obscure the heavens and earth.

Roar——!

It opened its mouth and exhaled a yellowish-brown Dragon Breath.

Great Earth Breath!

Dragon Breath of the Transforming Spirit Level, carrying a terrifying Earth Element power, hit Iron Stream Flat Country squarely.

Including Ning Zhuo, countless soldiers groaned at once, feeling an unrivaled heavy force abruptly strike them.

The dark iron torrent thus came to a halt, causing thousands of ripples, spreading quickly around those parts hit by the Dragon Breath.

And those Heavy Armor soldiers directly hit by the Dragon Breath all turned to stone statues, then crumbled into the finest grains of soil.

Du Tiechuan and others had already retreated from the front line, seeing this scene, their pupils constricted.

The strength of Iron Stream Flat Country had already reached the Transforming Spirit Level. Now their morale was like a rainbow, boosting their combat power to high-end Transforming Spirit Level.

Even so, it was still struck down by 30% of its forces in one full blow by Ginseng Whisker Dragon King!

Du Tiechuan did not act to maintain the main army, instead he brewed Military Art.

He held a sharp sword, waving it to produce a brilliant sword light.

Military Art—Draw the Bow and Load the Crossbow.

swordsmanship—Ten Thousand Zhang Edge.

swordsmanship—One Sword to the Throat!

A move that merged Military Art and swordsmanship, this was Du Tiechuan’s full powered strike.
