Chapter 612: Chapter 173: Enemy Attack

Two days later.

Ning Zhuo was hiding inside the Mechanical Dragon.

At this moment, the surface of the Mechanical Dragon had undergone a drastic transformation. Its body was covered with thick new plating, resembling layers of scabs.

This was Ning Zhuo, using Python Pattern Blood Fir and Dragon Scale Stone as main materials, sparing no expense, reliving the past, and restoring the Mechanical Dragon.

In fact, Ning Zhuo only took a day and a half to repair the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon.

Next, he devoted himself wholeheartedly to the refining of Mechanical Puppets.

The Xuanyuan Nine Palace Platform, Mystical Gear Platform, and the Immortal of Colossal Spirit Shaping Platform were set up in three separate compartments.

An Immortal of Colossal Spirit was summoned, sitting cross-legged on the floor tiles, surrounding the Shaping Platform, bowing his head and bending his waist, shaping the materials.

He took a Python Pattern Blood Fir, extended his large hand, and started peeling from the root to the crown. The immense strength, combined with the mysterious functions of the Shaping Platform, allowed the Immortal of Colossal Spirit to peel just once and obtain a straight and smooth trunk.

The Immortal of Colossal Spirit also handled metals.

First, He collected a large amount of metal, holding it in the palm of His hand.

Then, His arm muscles bulged, and with a forceful grip in both hands, He compressed the metal fiercely into a ball. His palms constantly applied pressure from all directions, compressing the metal ball smaller and smaller.

During this process, a large amount of impurities turned into metallic liquid, flowing out, becoming waste and set aside.

After a series of compressions, the Immortal of Colossal Spirit would break open the metal ball again, crumble it, and then compress it into a ball once more.

After several rounds like this, He obtained a bright and pure alloy ball.

He held His palm slightly closed, forming a circle with His thumb and index finger. Then, pressing hard with His middle, ring, and little fingers.

The metal ball was immediately squeezed out into a small clump, which was then taken out by the Immortal of Colossal Spirit using His right thumb and index finger.

He placed the small metal clump on the surface of the Immortal of Colossal Spirit Shaping Platform and rubbed it with the palm of His hand until it was perfectly round.

In this methodical process, a large number of metal spheres were produced.

A large amount of coarsely processed materials were then sent to the Mystical Gear Platform.

This platform, fashioned like a palace, turned slowly, containing countless intricate gears and mechanisms that also turned minutely, in close coordination.

Mechanical Techniques—Gear Regulation Art!

Ning Zhuo activated this Mechanical Technique, controlling with divine sense, processing all the materials.

Accompanying Ning Zhuo’s full operation of the platform, the gears and other mechanisms inside emitted a subtle humming sound, as if some mysterious power was awakening.

Various materials quickly assembled, and a multitude of talismans were inscribed into them. With the addition of numerous gears, these Mechanical Puppet embryos acquired the initial ability to move.

Ning Zhuo exhaled a murky breath, opened the Mystical Gear Platform. A series of Mechanical Puppets flew out consecutively, totaling forty-eight!

These forty-eight Mechanical Puppet embryos were finally sent to the third compartment of the Mechanical Dragon.

The Xuanyuan Nine Work Platform was responsible for the final processing.

One after another, the Mechanical Puppets rested on the simple and smooth platform surface.

Underneath the platform, numerous talismans constantly changed, appearing and disappearing, intercombining, forming various symbolic arrays to create different manufacturing processes.

Heavenly Fire Melting and Casting!

Within and outside the bodies of Mechanical Puppets, many flaws were refined and burned away by countless points of heavenly fire. Some of the heavenly fires were as large as flames, others were like sparks, or even mere dots.

Each point of heavenly fire had the right intensity.

Stacked Forging!

Invisible forging forces, like water waves, surged toward the Mechanical Puppets repeatedly.

The Mechanical Puppets floated in mid-air, three feet above the platform surface below them.

The forces of forging struck the Mechanical Puppets from all directions, creating layer upon layer of forged patterns on their surfaces, exceptionally pleasing to the eye.

Fine Carving!

The rough surfaces of the Mechanical Puppets became as smooth as flowing water. Any impurities left from the Heavenly Fire Melting were precisely removed.

The gears, channels, and other structural components within the Mechanical Puppets were sculpted smoother and more precisely fitted to each other.

In this way, the three Mechanical Platforms coordinated with each other, complementing one another’s processes.

A large number of Mechanical Puppets were produced, saving a massive amount of time and energy for Ning Zhuo!

In previous battles, Ning Zhuo had used up over two hundred Mechanical Puppets. Now he had replenished them, with even thirty more than before.

Clang Clang Clang!

Suddenly, an alarm sounded throughout the camp.

“Enemy attack!”

“There is an enemy assault!!”

Ning Zhuo’s heart tightened, and he immediately left the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon to return to the outside world.

Alarm bells echoed throughout the entire military camp.

Ning Zhuo activated a spell, empowering himself, and then immediately set multiple Magic Artifacts to protect his body as he ran out of the military strategist’s tent.

The Three Generals, Liu Guanzhang, had already taken their positions, directing soldiers to fortify the defenses.

This battle was different from the previous ones; it was a defensive battle for the camp.

“Where is the enemy?” Ning Zhuo climbed the watchtower to Liu Er’s side and cast a spell to look into the distance.

Soon, he saw a vast expanse of purplish-black poison fog slowly rising at the foothills of Giant Tree Mountain.

The interior of the poison fog was too deep to see, unknown what lurked within.

The Three Generals Camp was currently stationed midway up the mountain. Although there was some distance from the poison fog, given the situation, it was only a matter of time before they would be engulfed by the fog.

Liu Er’s brows were tightly knitted, his face full of worry: “Strategist, you’ve arrived!”

“You are aware of our strengths.”

“If the enemy attacks directly, even if we cannot defeat them, we can still make the enemy pay a price.”

“But now, the enemy has only used poison fog, and our battle formations are not suited for such a situation.”
