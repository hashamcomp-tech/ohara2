Chapter 1085: Chapter 403: Then Shine Bright! (Part 3)

Of course, Ning Zhuo couldn’t be certain.

After all, the matter of the White Paper Immortal City involved too vast interests.

"Would Zhong Dao compromise in the face of the sect’s immense interests?" Facing this crucial question, Ning Zhuo had no way to answer.

His understanding of Zhong Dao was still based on information purchasable on the market, and he hadn’t personally interacted with Zhong Dao, so it was hard to be sure.

The idea of borrowing strength from Zhong Dao came to Ning Zhuo after he first reviewed the information.

It wasn’t until moments ago on the stone platform, when facing the third question, that Ning Zhuo made his decision.

Once decided, Ning Zhuo no longer hesitated.

After leaving the Evil Slaying Hall, he immediately found the nearest registration point.

Even after all these days, the line of cultivators registering here was still long, with a large number of people.

Ning Zhuo was familiar with the process and directly walked up to the cultivators.

"Hey, you with the big head, go line up in the back!" someone shouted when they saw Ning Zhuo walking to the front.

The cultivator in charge of registration was drawn to him, noticing Ning Zhuo wasn’t someone he recognized. Just as he was about to ask, Ning Zhuo took out the proof of three first prizes.

"I’m here to exchange for the Stone Token," Ning Zhuo said.

The responsible cultivator’s face instantly changed. At a time like this, holding three first prize proofs was enough to prove Ning Zhuo’s excellence.

And this level of excellence was among the best at this year’s Feiyun Conference.

Realizing Ning Zhuo’s vast potential, the responsible cultivator immediately stood up, plastering a smile on his face: "Daoist Fellow, may I ask your name?"

Ning Zhuo replied.

The responsible cultivator’s smile grew wider: "Daoist Fellow Ning Zhuo, please hold on a moment, allow us to first ensure the authenticity of the proof."

"Let me, let me." A companion by his side couldn’t wait to reach out, taking the proof to test it.

Several companions reached out at once, almost causing a fight.

Seeing this, the cultivators lined up behind Ning Zhuo fell silent.

The whole scene was silent.

The three cultivators responsible for the test quickly responded almost simultaneously: "It’s real."

The cultivator facing Ning Zhuo burst into laughter, took out the Stone Token, and exchanged Ning Zhuo’s wooden token: "Daoist Fellow Ning Zhuo, congratulations to you, but there’s one last trivial matter. After receiving the Stone Token, the sect needs to record some information about you."

"Oh, I know, what do you need to ask?" Ning Zhuo inquired.

The responsible cultivator quickly answered: "We need to record the cultivation technique you practice and your current cultivation level."

Ning Zhuo was already prepared and immediately replied: ", currently in Foundation Establishment Mid-Stage, at the Abnormal Phenomenon Seed Talisman phase."

The responsible cultivator quickly recorded this in a Jade Slip: "I see, thank you, Daoist Fellow, I have recorded it."

"Daoist Fellow Ning Zhuo, this is my magic power aura. Should you wish to exchange for a token next, you can come to me. I will surely handle these trivial matters for you with utmost speed."

His companions immediately cast envious glances.

Cultivators assigned such tasks were basically at the grassroots level of the Myriad Images Sect. To get acquainted with someone like Ning Zhuo was quite an honor for them, potentially altering their own path.

Ning Zhuo smiled, taking the magic power aura handed over by the cultivator, but did not hand over his own magic power aura.

And just this motion alone drew even more envious, if not jealous, glances towards the responsible cultivator.

The person concerned beamed with joy, unable to contain his happiness!

"Daoist Fellow Ning Zhuo, take care." Finally, under the fraternal farewell of the responsible cultivator, Ning Zhuo leisurely left this nameless small mountain peak where the registration took place.

On the way back to the Bluestone Cave Mansion, Ning Zhuo maintained a calm expression, his eyes glistening.

He knew that his actions would inevitably bring about many repercussions.

Information about him would be highly valued and actively investigated by many interested parties.

"Then let them come."
