Chapter 608 -171: I am your boss, you know

“Boss!” Ning Zhuo was horrified and dashed to Sun Lingtong’s side.

He hastily cast a spell to check on Sun Lingtong’s condition.

Soon, Ning Zhuo’s eyebrows furrowed tightly, and his expression became anxious.

Sun Lingtong’s body surface gradually developed countless fine cracks. These cracks crisscrossed on Sun Lingtong’s body, revealing specks of starlight within the gaps.

Ning Zhuo immediately performed Healing Magic, but all were ineffective.

He then took out a large amount of Elixir Pills, fed them into Sun Lingtong’s mouth, and poured Mana into his body to help him immediately metabolize the medicinal power of the elixirs, so it would take effect throughout his body.

Still ineffective!

Ning Zhuo was so anxious that he broke out in sweat, his face pale.

Although his Five Elements Realm was profound, facing the void, he felt helpless!

“Boss, boss!”

Ning Zhuo began to use various methods such as Life Hanging Thread, Stone Cast Earth Treasure Divine Power, and so on, but none were effective.

Ning Zhuo’s body trembled, helplessly watching Sun Lingtong’s body cracks growing larger.

He gasped for air, his body covered in sweat, seeing Sun Lingtong fall into unconsciousness, life hanging by a thread, no longer having his previous calmness!

“Boss, boss…”

Ning Zhuo continuously took out items from his Storage Waist Belt, frantically trying them.

Suddenly, his movements paused, his bloodshot eyes fixed on the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat in his hands.

Ning Zhuo took a deep breath, a faint glimmer of hope sparkling in his eyes.

He immediately infused Mana, restoring the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat to its normal size.

Afterwards, he placed Sun Lingtong inside the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat.

Ning Zhuo then placed the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat within the corpse of the Stone Monster, making the scene of the past reappear.

The Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat was once again forced out of Magic Prohibition, the entire “Heavenly Born Earth-Raised Fetal Breathing Technique” became evident.

Stone Clothes began to cover Sun Lingtong’s body.

The spread rate of the Stone Clothes was much slower than before.

Ning Zhuo watched with great anxiety, his eyes focused on the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat, forcibly injecting Mana into it.

As a result, the Magic Prohibition of the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat disappeared, and the Stone Clothes on Sun Lingtong’s body began to recede!

Ning Zhuo’s heart was shocked, he slapped his own forehead angrily.

The reason why the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat could stimulate Magic Prohibition was for self-protection, hence it mobilized all abilities, borrowing the power of heaven and earth to create the Fetal Breathing State.

Now that it received Ning Zhuo’s strong support, naturally it would not exert itself and was unable to reach the trigger point for Magic Prohibition.

Sun Lingtong, without the enhancement of the Fetal Breathing state, and the essence of the Stone Clothes originated from the “logic” of the Old Monster in Stone, no longer had a good environment to spread.

Ning Zhuo took a deep breath, his eyebrows tightly furrowed, his eyes shimmering, trying to stabilize his mind.

This turmoil made him realize his own state — he could hardly think!

Ning Zhuo gritted his teeth, immediately withdrew his Mana. The body of the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat trembled slightly, falling back into an isolated state. The newly born Spiritual Nature inside again started working at full power, trying to save itself.

Sun Lingtong once again received the Fetal Breathing state, and Stone Clothes gradually spread.

“It’s getting slower!” Ning Zhuo noticed that the speed of the Stone Clothes growth was slowing down, and based on this trend, it wouldn’t cover Sun Lingtong’s head and would stop midway.

Ning Zhuo fiercely looked at the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat.

With determination, he performed a Gold Element Technique, a swirl of golden brilliance lingering in the palm of his hand, sharp as a sword and needle.

Ning Zhuo placed his palm on the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat, continuously rubbing it.

Sizzle sizzle…

The golden light was extremely sharp, piercing the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat, sparking countless sparks flying everywhere.

The Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat, under attack, trembled slightly, bursting with potential, making the Magic Prohibition shine several times brighter.

Sun Lingtong’s Fetal Breathing state deepened!

Ning Zhuo joyfully saw that the Stone Clothes on him regained their previous speed, firmly spreading towards his head until his whole body was enveloped.

“It works!”

Ning Zhuo’s Divine Sense constantly stayed on Sun Lingtong, noticing that his injuries were suppressed by the Stone Clothes.

However, the good scenery did not last long.

Although the Stone Clothes enveloped Sun Lingtong, the void cracks gradually appeared on the Stone Clothes.

The Stone Clothes also began to crack.

“Based on the previous performance, the Stone Clothes should rapidly grow and thicken, becoming Stone Skin.”

“But being suppressed by the force of the void, the Stone Clothes remained just a thin layer, and now it is penetrated by the void cracks!”

Ning Zhuo clenched his teeth, stretched out both hands, pressing them on the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat.

Sizzle sizzle…

His Gold Element Technique continuously struck the boat body, forcefully prompting the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat to burst out more potential.

The Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat trembled greatly, the entire Magic Prohibition cast a blinding bright light.

The Fetal Breathing state became even stronger, enhancing Sun Lingtong’s body.

Stone Skin began to slowly grow all over Sun Lingtong’s body.

The Stone Skin became thicker and thicker, but it stopped growing after reaching three inches.

“Hmm?” Ning Zhuo’s brows had just relaxed, but they furrowed again now.

He kept a close eye on the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat: “Hold on, little boat!”

He spewed Mana, performed more spells, surrounding the boat body in constant rotation.

The Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat, suffering from many spells, trembled violently, the entire Magic Prohibition illuminating the surroundings, lighting the hold as if it were day.

The Stone Skin on Sun Lingtong became thicker, and he “grew” from a small figure into a stone fat man.

“Still not enough, still not enough!”

As Ning Zhuo infused his Divine Sense, he found that although the void cracks were greatly suppressed, upon careful observation, they could still be seen strengthening bit by bit.

Ning Zhuo once again turned his gaze to the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat.

“Little boat.” He reached out, stroking the boat body, “I believe you can do it! Keep going!”

Ning Zhuo shouted lightly, his Mana soared, turning into a swirl of five-colored brilliance.

Within the brilliance, various Five Elements Magic performed in rotation, wrapping around the Fetal Breathing Spirit Boat from the outside.
