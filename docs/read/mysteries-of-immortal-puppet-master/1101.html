Chapter 1101: Chapter 411: Gotta Hold Tight to Ning Zhuo’s Coattails

Qingzhu Peak.

Inside the cave mansion, Qinghuangzi wore a serious expression, explaining the general situation of his family’s heritage to Ning Zhuo before the divine statue.

Empty Valley Sound Green Mechanism Tube!

This is the name of this mechanism inheritance.

The original source was the Bamboo Mountain Sect.

This sect no longer exists; at the time, the main mountain gate was attacked, the enemy’s methods were extremely clever, inciting internal strife within the sect, avoiding engagement and fleeing, abandoning a few cultivators loyal to the sect who preferred to die fighting.

The number of cultivators was too small, and the defense line was broken all the way, ultimately retreating into the bamboo forest in the valley.

At the critical moment, among the surviving cultivators, there were two individuals, one adept in sound rhythm and the other skilled at mechanisms, who cooperated to quickly renovate the bamboo forest, transforming a section of bamboo into a sound whistle.

When the enemy made a strong incursion, the cultivators activated the array, summoned a great wind to sweep through the bamboo forest, causing the bamboo whistles to erupt with sudden sound, countless sound blades emerging, circling around the bamboo poles, slaughtering the enemy with blood spilled everywhere.

The enemy’s several assaults were all unable to conquer, causing heavy losses.

One solution would have been to uproot the bamboo forest, depriving the adversary of its support. But the leader considered the bamboo, all spirit plants of great value, and preferred to endure patiently and plot slowly.

The main mountain gate of the Bamboo Mountain Sect was completely breached, leaving only this valley behind.

At the time, war was the predominant tone throughout the cultivation world, and after the leader occupied this domain, faced with various external pressures, increasingly lacked the energy to seize the remaining valley bamboo forest.

Since the sect scattered due to internal strife, it was essentially already extinct.

The leader, at regular intervals, sent several experts to the valley bamboo forest to attempt persuasion. During these negotiations, it inevitably involved exchanges and competitions.

Both sides, utilizing the windy bamboo forest, conducted numerous defensive and offensive battles, some lightly skimming, stopping when appropriate, others escalating to fatalities and fervent combat.

Through continuous honing, the elders in the valley worked together, incessantly innovating, advancing from the initial clever idea of the windy bamboo forest to developing a comprehensive system.

This system was then passed down through generations, progressing innovatively, reaching Qinghuangzi, it was the twenty-eighth generation.

Qinghuangzi looked solemnly into Ning Zhuo’s eyes: "Ning Zhuo, you are now the twenty-ninth generation inheritor of the Empty Valley Sound Green Mechanism Tube!"

"Our heritage isn’t part of Myriad Images Sect. Quite the opposite, Myriad Images Sect annexed the sect of the previous generation’s inheritor, resulting in the current situation."

"However, being within Myriad Images Sect isn’t necessarily a bad thing. Many times, it was only with Myriad Images Sect’s support that the heritage was preserved."

"Throughout this long tradition, we’ve seen Transforming Spirit Level entities emerge. Though, they pursued another main cultivation technique."

"The earliest sages who fully cultivated this heritage reached Nascent Soul Level. By today’s standards, this is a Nascent Soul Level inheritance."

At this point, Qinghuangzi sighed: "I see you’ve achieved Foundation Establishment, with another main cultivation method. That’s not a problem as long as you successfully swear under the divine statue, first to safeguard this heritage to the best of your ability, and if possible, strive to perfect, improve, and promote this heritage."

"Ning Zhuo, your cultivation talent is far superior to mine. Youthful ambition, like the vitality of dawn. It’s good to be young."

"I’m conscious of my own limitations, so rest assured. You needn’t worry about inheriting this heritage requiring you to take me as master."

The relationship between master and disciple is extraordinary!

This social bond sometimes surpasses blood lineage.

In a super sect like Myriad Images Sect, with numerous factions and countless people, entangled in interests and conflicts, creating immense internal complexity.

Ning Zhuo truly taking a master in the future marks genuine integration into a particular faction.

"My acquisition of the stone token revealed such talent that even Qinghuangzi hesitated to accept me as a disciple."

"This might be counted as one of the benefits of my choice to be high-profile."

Had Ning Zhuo not sufficiently demonstrated excellence earlier, Qinghuangzi might have required the inheritor to pledge as a disciple.

Pledging early as a disciple would be a significant loss for Ning Zhuo!

Given Qinghuangzi’s personal combat strength and connections are too weak, he himself isn’t even a Golden Core True Transmission. From this perspective, he remains at the fringe of Myriad Images Sect’s power structure.

Ning Zhuo faced the divine statue.

The statue’s posture was peculiar, different from most statues.

It kneeled on one knee, wearing a dragon-patterned hair crown, three strands of bronze hair drooping over the forehead, ends sharply cut like blades. Its left hand held half a frosty blade across the chest, shorn hair entwined around the sword’s spine.

Ning Zhuo could recognize, it was indeed The Shorn-Hair Sword Lord.

In life, his deeds of shorn hair pledging were famous throughout the land, having passed a thousand years, still widely acclaimed, with his name enduring in history.

Ning Zhuo raised his left hand, forming a sword finger, mimicking the blade, resting against his throat.

Upon reciting the divine name, he immediately activated the statue’s power.

White light radiated from the statue, gathering in mid-air to form a circular mirror. The mirror’s edge embedded with nine hundred and ninety-nine copper nails, reflecting the clear image of the oath-taker Ning Zhuo.

Facing the divine statue, Ning Zhuo completed the oath.

The divine light mirror divided into two streams, one entering Ning Zhuo’s body, the other merging into the statue.

The light then dissipated.

Having witnessed the entire process, Qinghuangzi exhaled heavily, exuding joy, and said, "Good, very good."

Afterwards, he led Ning Zhuo into the inner chamber, handing over more than a dozen jade slips and three storage bags to Ning Zhuo.
