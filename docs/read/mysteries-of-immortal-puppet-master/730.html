Chapter 730 -231: Counterattack Wave

Indeed, the next moment, Du Tiechuan’s voice echoed across the battlefield: “All soldiers, listen to orders, counterattack with full force, victory will be rewarded!”

Instantly, all the soldiers within the Liangzhu Country army shouted in unison.

On the contrary, Thousand Peak Forest’s morale significantly dwindled.

The enemy’s Divinity Transformation level cultivators have made a move, where are Thousand Peak Forest’s own?

Countless people eagerly awaited the appearance of the Ginseng Whisker Dragon King, but not even a single ginseng whisker was seen.

Lu Hongtu shouted: “Do not fear! This is the enemy bluffing, Du Tiechuan himself has not personally emerged!!”

The expressions of the crowd were filled with suspicion.

After waiting for a few breaths, all combat slowed down, everyone was in waiting.

Du Tiechuan’s face was gloomy, inevitably speculating: “Is there some kind of trap, forcing me to reveal myself first?”

“Ginseng Whisker Dragon King…”

“Where exactly are you hiding?”

Du Tiechuan was injured, being able to imprison Xiang Yue already made his breath unstable and left him feeling strained.

No matter how he searched, he could not find the trace of the Ginseng Whisker Dragon King, making him increasingly apprehensive.

Under such circumstances, he dared not take risks and reveal himself first.

Thus, on the whole battlefield, neither side awaited Du Tiechuan’s appearance.

The morale on Thousand Peak Forest’s side stabilized!

“Six Cave Sect’s Great Cave Master, his mind works quickly.” Lord Long gratefully glanced at Lu Hongtu, worthy of being the chief strategist of the Thousand Peak Forest allies.

In just a few breaths, Lu Hongtu thought of a countermeasure.

Even though Lord Long’s combat power was superior to Lu Hongtu, he couldn’t think of such a strategy.

This is the power of wisdom!

“Hahahaha!” Shuang Ling suddenly laughed up, her sharp laughter echoed across the battlefield.

She pointed at Lord Long: “Your Ginseng Whisker Dragon King has yet to appear, could it be that he has already died from severe injuries?”

She truly lived up to the name Mechanical Princess, immediately countering their strategy with their own tactics.

Lord Long’s expression changed abruptly, he roared sharply: “Wretch, sharp-tongued and audacious, dare to insult my god! Today, I will tear your soul apart, peel your skin, and annihilate you!”

Lord Long, furious, launched a wild attack on Shuang Ling.

The verbal exchange between the two severely impacted the morale of both armies.

Shuang Ling, controlling the Flexible Bone Dark Concubine, actively resisted Lord Long, who attacked fiercely, driving Shuang Ling to retreat continuously.

Even so, Shuang Ling felt enormously invigorated, her mental pressure reduced by at least half.

Before, she needed to multitask, both resisting Lord Long and suppressing Xiang Yue, leaving her mentally and physically exhausted.

Although she represented her country’s Nascent Soul power, resisting Lord Long and Xiang Yue simultaneously was still too challenging.

But now it was different.

Xiang Yue was personally suppressed by Du Tiechuan, losing his influence over the battlefield. This allowed Shuang Ling to focus entirely on dealing with Lord Long.

In fact, it wasn’t just Shuang Ling; the morale of the entire Liangzhu Country army was rising swiftly!

The reason was simple.

Their Divinity Transformation level Du Tiechuan at least made a move once, and with that one move, he directly suppressed Xiang Yue.

Previously, Xiang Yue had inflicted massive damage and pressure on the Liangzhu Country army, and now with this immediate suppression, the soldiers’ Heart Qi Strength rapidly revived.

Hence, a wave of full-out counterattack officially surged!

“Aaaargh!” Xu Dali, bathed in blood, roared.

He charged forward fiercely, relying on his Body Cultivation foundation, employing Military Art, directly knocking away all Barbarian Clan cultivators in his path, advancing unstoppably!

Ding Pi’s kitchen knife danced, in an instant, the blade flashed like snow, a Pangolin Demon Cultivator was dismembered on the spot.

Its meat slices flew into the pot, cooking quickly.

Ding Pi’s apprentice Jia Wan danced around the big pot, continually adjusting the cooking fire.

The cooked meat slices emitted rich aroma, being controlled by Ding Pi’s Divine Sense, they flew into his mouth, quickly chewed and swallowed, transforming into an immense mana, greatly replenishing Ding Pi.

Mo Yechen took a deep breath, activated the formation on his robe, staining the sky, transforming it into a piece of night.

The night descended like ink, countless black chains fell from the heavens, locking down numerous enemy troops, rendering them immobile, then easily slaughtered by the soldiers before them.

Such signs appeared almost simultaneously throughout the battlefield.

The rising morale made the Liangzhu Country soldiers, who were fighting separate battles, unanimously choose to attack.

And though there were parts of the offensive that failed, most managed to temporarily suppress Thousand Peak Forest.

If it were before, individual choices were inconsistent, though the soldiers of Thousand Peak Forest would retreat and fall behind, they often quickly counterattacked, reclaiming the upper hand or bringing the battle back to equilibrium.

But now it was different.

The drop in Thousand Peak Forest’s morale made them instinctively apprehensive, adopting a defensive stance.

One person’s or a sector’s defensive stance would not affect the overall situation. But the collective choice instantly altered the entire battlefield’s dynamics.

In almost an instant, the vast battlefield saw a clear change in the wind direction — Liangzhu Country took the upper hand, Thousand Peak Forest fell into a disadvantage.

Such change further intensified the psychological transformations of soldiers on both sides.

The Liangzhu Country soldiers, initially fueled by sheer spirit, discovered with surprise that their counterattack worked and the enemy seemed weaker, further boosting their morale.

On the contrary, Thousand Peak Forest’s soldiers, being fearful and defensive, suddenly found the enemy much stronger. Their common thought was — indeed! My choice to defend and evade was correct.

Hence, this further confirmed Liangzhu Country’s strength and Thousand Peak Forest’s weakness.
