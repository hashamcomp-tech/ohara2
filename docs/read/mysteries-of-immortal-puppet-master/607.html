Chapter 607 -170: A Narrow Escape from Death_2

Meanwhile, far away in Mulu Town.

Shuang Ling stood atop the Magic Platform, her eyes suddenly widening with a fierce and excited expression: “Finally, I have found you!”

“Old Monster in Stone!!!”

“Dare to take my things, I will make you die!”

Divine Power Execution——Star Falling Heavenly Punishment.

The next moment, starlight condensed like droplets high in the sky, then aimed at Ning Zhuo wrapped with the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon, descending rapidly.

A straight long line was pulled through the sky by the trace of a star.

Ning Zhuo instantly felt a great disaster looming!

“Quick, hide inside the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon, we will navigate through the void,” Sun Lingtong urgently shouted.

Ning Zhuo wanted to do so too, but just as he was about to act, his Mechanical Ring suddenly tightened.

Warning!

Ning Zhuo was immediately perplexed: “Could it be that even if I hide inside the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon, concealing myself in the void, I would still be shot by this starlight?”

For a moment, he was totally uncertain.

Seeing Ning Zhuo in a daze, Sun Lingtong rushed hurriedly to his side, grabbed his arm, and was about to drag him into the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon.

But in the next moment, Ning Zhuo transmitted thoughts through his Divine Sense, informing Sun Lingtong about the ring’s warning and his guess.

Sun Lingtong froze in place.

On a distant mountain peak.

The team of Cultivators originally stationed in Bikong Mountain all showed shocked expressions, watching the starlight fall and pulling out a straight thin line.

“What kind of spell is this?”

“Who exactly is making a move?!”

“Thank goodness, we left here early. Otherwise, we would have been the ones under attack.”

The Cultivators, amidst their astonishment, were greatly relieved.

Gao Cuo felt an empty and ominous sensation within, and he immediately communicated this feeling to Elder Jian: “I want to go back and see!”

Elder Jian immediately vetoed: “You cannot go!”

“The aura of this starlight has been restrained to the extreme, you have greatly underestimated its power.”

“Even a hundred of you could not withstand its power.”

Gao Cuo’s heart skipped a beat: “What?”

Elder Jian snorted coldly: “Young man, you’re still too inexperienced to see what’s happening.”

“This attack contains the essence of reasoning, but it is still a spell, it should be a Divine Power Execution.”

“Divine Power Execution is one thing, but the key is that it contains an extremely enormous National Power of Liangzhu Country!”

Upon hearing this, Gao Cuo suddenly paled: “What?”

The scope of Military Power is that it operates near the military force.

While the reach of National Power extends over the entire country.

Thousand Peak Forest is, by legal definition, the territory of Liangzhu Country, and thus could be ravaged by the National Power of Liangzhu Country!

At this moment, the threat of death loomed over Ning Zhuo’s head.

Sun Lingtong suddenly let go, no longer caring for Ning Zhuo. He directly spurred the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon, and escaped into the void.

“Boss!” Ning Zhuo was alarmed.

He knew that Sun Lingtong was driving the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon himself, drawing away the attack to allow Ning Zhuo to escape.

However, although the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon was disembodied, the starlight had already contaminated Ning Zhuo’s entire body, causing him to emit beams of starlight as well.

“It’s useless.” In an instant, Sun Lingtong drove the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon back to its original position.

His control skills were much stronger than Ning Zhuo’s.

Sun Lingtong’s face turned deathly pale, transmitting thoughts through Divine Sense: “I rushed into the void, yet starlight still leaked from the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon. It’s still locked in!”

“I have an incredibly strong premonition that this beam of starlight can also traverse the void!”

The void is not safe.

Ning Zhuo almost forgot to breathe, under immense pressure, his thinking remained swift and as cool as ice.

A Spirit Light suddenly flashed in his Divine Sea.

He jerked his head towards the bottom of Bikong Pond. More precisely, to that place where only Sun Lingtong could see the mature Void Crystal.

“The only chance for survival!” a strong premonition rose in his heart.

And as he attempted to move towards the bottom of the pond, his Mechanical Ring suddenly stopped tightening and calmed down.

This tangible change served as a strong confirmation, instantly boosting Ning Zhuo’s spirit!

“Boss, let’s hide over there!” Ning Zhuo immediately acted, diving into the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon.

The unspoken understanding over the years made Sun Lingtong follow Ning Zhuo’s suggestion without a second thought.

They dived into the bottom of the pond, just hiding beneath the mature Void Crystal.

Almost at the same moment, the Star Falling Heavenly Punishment struck the surface of the pond, piercing directly through the water to the bottom.

The mature Void Crystal clashed with the Star Falling Heavenly Punishment!

There was no explosion, no dazzling bright light, and no overwhelming blast of air.

Star lines focused on the surface of the Void Crystal, scattering a spherical ball of light that was not brilliant, its brightness merely like that of a candle.

But Sun Ning and his companion’s faces changed drastically.

The alarm in the Dragon Head Cabin blared frantically! The alarm sound was unprecedentedly sharp!!

During the confrontation between spear and shield, Sun Ning and his companion observed through the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon the terrifying power contained within the star lines.

“Water Element Power, this familiar scent, it’s the National Power of Liangzhu Country!” Ning Zhuo cried out in disbelief.

His mouth was dry, his scalp was numb, his heart tightly clenched, making breathing difficult.

Click, click.

Sun Ning and his companion’s hearts skipped a beat, seeing cracks appearing on the surface of this piece of Void Crystal.

The cracks rapidly spread across the entire Void Crystal!

If the Void Crystal couldn’t hold up, Sun Ning and his companion were facing imminent death.

But in the next moment, a bone emerged within the Void Crystal.

The gray bone was covered in menacing barbs, exuding malice.

“This is an Evil God Bone!” Sun Lingtong exclaimed, realizing suddenly, “So that’s how it is! This is a piece of a Void Evil God’s bone, drifting in the void, one day it fell into Bikong Pond and eventually hid within this piece of Void Crystal, causing the marvellous change.”

Even with Sun Lingtong’s innate abilities, he couldn’t have observed the Evil God Bone.

But at this moment, forced to materialize, the bone revealed its overwhelming power!

The Void Crystal in which it resided began to show layers of overlapping images, as though suddenly a large cluster of crystal clusters had grown.

The space violently fluctuated!

“Not good.” Sun Ning and his companion quickly maneuvered the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon, shuttling through the void.

Space changed unpredictably, whipping up tumultuous waves. Countless fragments of space pelted the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon all around, like a cruel torturous dissection!

The National Power contained in the starfall fully erupted.

Starlight invaded the space, piercing through the crystal-clear outer shell and then striking the Evil God Bone.

The surface of the Evil God Bone revealed countless talismans, twisting and entangling like snakes or earthworms.

Ning Zhuo only glanced at them once and felt a strong dizziness. He quickly averted his gaze.

But Sun Lingtong stared wide-eyed, captivated by these talismans.

Although the Evil God Bone was mysterious and odd, it was just a piece of relic bone, infiltrated more and more by the starlight, deeper and deeper.

Crack.

A crack formed in the Evil God Bone.

Space suddenly underwent violent distortion, and a burst of star-like brilliant bone marrow flowed out through the crack.

The starlight surged, erupting in a terrifying brightness in an instant.

The full force of the National Power struck the bone marrow, but was contained and accepted by countless layers of space.

It was only a few breaths of time, but to Sun Ning and his companion, it felt as long as years!

All of the Void Crystals vanished, completely dissolved by the starlight.

The Evil God Bone too melted in the starlight, like snow dissolving under sunlight.

Only the bone marrow, as if containing countless diamonds, remained dazzling as before, not overshadowed even by the starlight.

The Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon suffered erosion from countless star flares, forming thousands of tiny holes.

Space was still violently shaking; Sun Ning and his companion felt as if they were in a tsunami! The Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon had become a broken vessel, perilously close to falling apart.

Once the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon collapsed, Sun Ning and his companion would be instantly killed by the terrifying spatial fluctuations or annihilated by the starlight, without even a chance to scream.

The Mechanical Ring contracted to the extreme, as if it would sink into Ning Zhuo’s finger bones.

Sun Lingtong glanced back at Ning Zhuo and clenched his teeth: “It doesn’t matter if I die, Little Zhuo must not!”

“The Bone Marrow of the Void Evil God, that’s my only chance.”

He had no certainty, and could only treat a dead horse as if it were alive.

Sun Lingtong extended his arm, activating a certain seal within his Divine Sea.

It was the Empty Seal!

A miracle occurred.

Guided by the Empty Seal, the Evil God Bone Marrow, like swifts returning to their nest, swiftly burrowed into the Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon, entering into Sun Lingtong’s Divine Sea, and merged with the Empty Seal mark.

In an instant, Sun Lingtong’s eyes widened, his head swelling as if to burst!

His Stealing Technique power soared explosively!

Blessed by a stroke of insight, he used his Stealing Technique to enhance the entire Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon, then personally controlled it.

The Ten-Thousand-Li Travelling Dragon seemed to come alive, nimbly shuttling through the endlessly fluctuating void, quickly escaping from the starlight, fleeing far away.

“Boss, boss!” Ning Zhuo was overjoyed and incredulous.

“You’re too incredible!”

“How did you do it?!”

Sun Lingtong placed his hands on his hips, about to burst into laughter, but as soon as he opened his mouth, a large amount of fresh blood spurted out.

Amidst the spray of blood, his eyes rolled back, and he passed out on the spot.
