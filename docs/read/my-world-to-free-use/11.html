Unfortunately, there were only four E rank rats in the group, but it was all good. After all, it was still a good amount of experience points I obtained from all of them.

[EXP: 492\500]

It was a pity I didn’t level up from that group. But it was still better than killing hundreds of rats to obtain only one level. If I faced a few more groups like that, I would easily be able to level up to level ten before going home today.

And I wasn’t disappointed.

Just as I turned a new corner, I saw hundreds of rats running towards me. While a little bad for me, I was sure I wouldn’t be harmed to the point of death.

Besides, with my level increasing every time, I would be able to survive for longer.

~~

I severely overestimated myself.

I ran to the entrance of the sewer as a swarm of rats ran after me.

[You have killed a mutated infected mouse x191]

[You have gained 1910 EXP]

[You have gained an F rank mana crystal x191]

You have killed a mutated infected mouse x36]

[You have gained 3600 EXP]

[You have gained an E rank mana crystal x36]

[You have leveled up x7]

[EXP 402\1200]

[You have received 35 APs]

[You have reached level 10]

[A full heal will be granted as a reward from the system]

So far, everything was good news. Then why was I running? A very good question.

Well, that would be because of that big rat behind all those smaller ones.

[Mutated rat leader C]

Not a D rank but a C rank. Impossible for me to defeat, even if I were to invest all the stat points I gained from seven level-ups.

The only option left was to run. It was difficult for me to defeat a D rank monster right now. If a C rank one caught me, I would be sliced in half without even a little resistance.

Luckily, thanks to the system’s full heal, not only was my health restored, but my stamina was also fully restored, making it very easy for me to run.

Being a C rank monster, of course, the boss rat was faster than I, but even then, I was able to escape. Because as soon as I saw it appear from the other end, I didn’t hesitate and ran away.

I wanted to invest in agility to run faster, but I didn’t want to waste stat points on that yet. I had mana to focus on for now. The rest was after that.

By the time I arrived at the end of the sewer, the exit was in my sight. Just as I entered it, the monsters behind me stopped. They didn’t cross a certain boundary.

Correction. They couldn’t cross a certain boundary.

So that was how this condition was being contained. By casting a shield of magic at the exit of the sewer, which would hold these rats back.

The magic used was at least B rank, and it was hidden well, so that explained why I couldn’t sense it earlier.

I sighed and exited the sewer.

Just like yesterday, I knocked on the nice lady’s door.

Just like yesterday, she opened the door.

Just like yesterday, I knocked her out.

After cleaning myself and taking another piece of clothing from the woman’s husband, probably, I sat down on the floor, naked.

I didn't wear the clothes yet.

The reason?

I stunk.

Even though I washed myself, it wasn’t of any help. I still smelled just as bad, or maybe a little less, but stunk either way. I didn’t want to ruin the clothes as well.

[AP(s): 35]

[Intelligence: 20]

[MP: 200]

First of all, I added eighteen points to the intelligence, which in turn raised my mana.

That left me with seventeen points. I wanted to save these points at first, but after facing that monster down there, I was having second thoughts.

This was a world of the awakened. Just having a body at the level of an optimal human wasn’t enough. I needed to have more.

[Strength: 15]

[Agility: 15]

[Stamina: 15]

So I added five each to the rest. As I felt my body becoming warm due to the mana increment and full of power due to the stat increase at the same time, I calmed myself with deep breaths.

[Cleanse]

Cleanse. A utility spell at F rank. It used only twenty points of mana if used by the system, but if I cast it without the system, it took a whopping 100 points. Hundred points I couldn’t afford.

So instead of casting the skill, I moved on to the matter at hand.

After all, the good news hadn’t ended yet.

[You have reached level 10]

[Shop has been unlocked]

[Inventory has been unlocked]

[Alchemy has been unlocked]

Three functions of the system came online at once.

I opened the inventory first. Although I couldn’t use the inventory before and had to waste the bodies of the rats, the system-extracted mana crystals were still stored in the inventory.

[F rank mana crystals x301]

[E rank mana crystals x40]

It was a bummer I couldn’t use these crystals to raise my mana, but that was okay. I could still use them to restore my mana by consuming them straight from the inventory like a potion.

And the best part about them was that, unlike mana potions, there was no cooldown time for these, so as long as I had these, I had never-ending mana.

But even that wouldn’t have been a useful aspect against that monster down in the sewers.

Anyway, with everything else out of the way, I looked into the shop to search for a skill. Using spells with my limited mana wasn’t a good idea, even if I had mana crystals. Mana crystals were a resource that wasn’t available in the shop, and unlike mana potions, they didn’t have a cooldown, so I couldn’t senselessly use them.

[Skill: Cleanse]

[Rank: F]

[Price: 50 SC]

[Effect: Cleans everything the user wills]

[Additional Effect: After usage, the user is marked by fragrance]

[Cost: 50 MP]

Damn. Fifty MP was a lot for such a low-level skill, but when considering it was pretty useful in cases of bed, it was amazing.

And in my current condition as well, because I smelled like a squirrel who had been dead for a month.

Other than that, it was quite useful to clean myself as well.

I mean, the other kind of cleaning.

If there was one thing I learned when I was transferred back to the more primitive worlds, it was how unsanitary everyone was when it came to certain aspects.

The number of diseases in common folks without mana or Ki was innumerable. And in certain cases, even those who possessed a great deal of power fell to the diseases of the genitals.

Cleanse helped a great deal to clean myself of any bacteria that were left on me or could be associated with any such disease.

I sighed.

[Shop coins (SC): 50]

Upon the opening of the shop, I get 100 coins. After that, I have to earn more by killing monsters or selling things to the system shop.

With just 50 coins, I wouldn’t even be able to get a low-level attack skill, much less a skill that could upgrade as well.

I needed at least ten thousand coins for an upgradeable skill. And if I wanted just a one-time skill that would be useless after some time, even for that, I needed at least one thousand shop coins.

No doubt if I were to kill the rats in the sewer, I would easily be able to do it, but I wouldn’t be able to get past all of them without an attack skill, and my mana wasn’t enough to even cast a [Flame ball].

Haa…

I sighed once again and turned to the woman who was sleeping peacefully on the floor. I also had to pay her for her troubles, and something told me that just pleasuring her wouldn’t cut it.

I pulled a few F rank mana crystals. Since they were in the inventory and extracted by the system, they had no trace of filth on them.

[Cleanse]

Speaking of filth, I was filthy, so I used ‘Cleanse’ on myself to get rid of that horrible stench and then moved on to put the crystals beside the woman. I didn’t know what their worth was, so I left ten F rank mana crystals, whatever they were worth to her.

I then left the house and made my way to my house, Jake’s house.

After all, I now had enough mana to know what was wrong with the anatomy of this body. And knowing that I was around Rayla and her daughters, whose names I still didn’t know, I couldn’t hold myself back anymore.

I needed to solve my number one problem right now.

I needed to awaken the son.
