Chapter 1455: Do Not Doubt If It Is Lost (II)

Li Ximing stared at him blankly and his expression turned strange. A mix of surprise and awkwardness appeared on his face as he asked, "So that means... you and I are now at the same level?"

"Not so." Li Zhouwei shook his head.

The Daoist Master in the white golden Daoist robe immediately understood, slapping his palm in frustration as he replied, "That is true... my injuries are still far from light. How could I be considered at the same level as you?"

Li Zhouwei looked slightly awkward. After a brief moment, he replied, "It is not about the dharma body... I obtained a green talisman, which elevated one divine ability. The remaining energy was not used up, so it condensed again into pure cultivation and was sealed within Shenyang acupoint. Once I cultivate the next divine ability, I can draw it out again with a thought..."

"

Oh!

" Li Ximing turned and sat down. After thinking for a moment, he let out a light laugh, "After all, it is a green talisman!"

But he quickly reacted, exclaiming in surprise, "If it can raise your cultivation, would that not instantly bring all six cycles to completion? Have you cultivated [Radiance Under Heaven]? Have you decided on that?"

Li Ximing had heard Tinglan explain that Radiance Under Heaven was best used for the final divine ability. He himself had no particular thoughts, but Li Zhouwei was different.

When he asked, Li Zhouwei shook his head and said with a smile, "Of course I remember the advice..."

He then turned serious, "I cultivated [Sovereign In Peril], but I have not used the green radiance within the Shenyang Mansion. I was just about to discuss this matter with you, senior."

"First, my [Heaven-Venerating Gate] is already complete by relying on talisman qi. Refining a divine ability is subtle, and each person's mastery differs. It is a personal matter and hard to measure, so it is not a major concern. But if I continue to use it, I might rapidly condense a second divine ability. That would be entirely different. It would be impossible to conceal in battle."

His expression grew solemn, "I may have some destiny on my side, but no matter what, I could never achieve a second divine ability in just a few days. If others saw that, they might suspect I am the reincarnation of the Wei's Emperor. Moreover, all sides are already watching me. If something so far beyond their expectations happens, it will surely bring disaster."

Li Ximing nodded heavily as Li Zhouwei continued, "Therefore, in my view, I should use this time to cultivate on my own. Once I forge the second divine ability, I can then use the talisman qi to bring it swiftly to completion. That would be the safest approach and the best way to make full use of the green talisman."

"Good..." Li Ximing stroked his beard and replied, "It is best if you arrange it yourself."

His tone grew more complex as he asked, "Since you mentioned the arrangements of all parties, our family is becoming increasingly entangled in the matter of the Wei's Emperor. What is your view?"

Li Zhouwei seemed to have already thought about it. His gaze darkened slightly as he said softly, "The struggle at that level is beyond both me and the family. We have no right to question it. In the end, whether we follow the arrangements of the Dragon Clan and cultivate through reincarnation, or are forced into a corner and compelled to break through, none of it is decided yet. For now, there is only one thing to do, strengthen ourselves and build up our power."

Li Ximing nodded silently. After thinking for a long while, he took out a piece of dark jade from his sleeve and said, "You are still using a dharma artifact, which is hardly fitting. The Viewing Pavilion has long been completed. Take this portion of spiritual essence and use it, so you can obtain a proper weapon sooner."

Li Zhouwei had naturally considered this already. He replied after a moment of thought, "In my earlier fight with Helian Wumeng, this dharma artifact of mine was damaged. I have long wanted to repair it, but our lake region has always lacked in the art of refining. We do not have top-level talent, so I can only nurture it slowly myself. Although Jiangzong has studied it for years and achieved some results, he still cannot produce something at the level of a spirit embryo. We will need to find someone."

"That is not difficult. Ning Wan has yet to be found, and it is inconvenient to rely on Chu Minglian, but Purple Smoke Gate or Xiukui Dao Gate can both be approached now."

Li Ximing chuckled, then suddenly remembered something. He quickly took out a small jade bottle, which had streams of pale blue-white qi swirling within. He showed it to Li Zhouwei and said, "Take a look at this. It also came from within heaven and earth."

There were five bottles in total. There were actually a few more left behind in between heaven and earth, but Li Zhouwei had been focused on cultivation and had not examined them closely. Now that he looked at it, he judged and said, "It seems to be some kind of spiritual qi... related to the Supreme Yin. If one were to ask about it, I suspect only Chunyi Dao in the world would know."

Li Ximing shook his head with a hint of frustration and replied, "I am aware of that. In fact, I have even refined a major pill of the Supreme Yin Dao. It would be extremely valuable to Chunyi Dao. But whether it is the spiritual qi or the spirit pill, Supreme Yin has vanished from the world. If such things appear in our hands without reason, it will only arouse suspicion."

Li Zhouwei nodded and said, "... Did we not also obtain news of a cave dwelling within heaven and earth? There is no need to rush this. Once you have recovered, and the northern situation stabilizes, you can make a trip. It would be best to find a suitable excuse and settle the matter."

Li Ximing understood what he meant. This was a sensitive time. It was not suitable for Li Zhouwei to travel, nor could he inexplicably head out to the outer-sea and pinpoint a hidden cave dwelling in some strait. Only Li Ximing could quietly go and retrieve the items.

"Alright!"

Li Ximing nodded inwardly and took out a pill furnace from his sleeve that shone in gold and ivory hues. It was the [Treasure-Elephant Furnace]. He immediately smiled and said, "I still owe Master Dingyang a batch of [Serpent Water Suspension Powder]. This is a good chance to refine it!"

The Realm of Sun and Moon Radiance had far denser spiritual energy, making it a better place for alchemy. Unfortunately, [Serpent Water Suspension Powder] was a Pit Water suspension-type pill, and its nature did not align with balanced harmony. So he immediately took out the jade box, given to him by Master Dingyang.

When he opened the jade box, he found a palm-sized scale, black-blue in color and faintly glowing. It was the main ingredient, the [Cangzhou Serpent Scale]. The other auxiliary ingredients had also been prepared by Master Dingyang, so he could begin refining immediately.

At a glance, the quality of this spirit material was excellent, prompting Li Ximing to think to himself,

Master Dingyang wants six pills per batch. I can certainly achieve that. But any extra pills would also be Pit Water pills. They are rather useless to my family. Their applications are too narrow and tied too specifically to certain Dao lineages. Unless there is a specific need, their value is not high...

Having spent so many years on this, he valued Purple Mansion Realm spirit materials greatly. Taking a closer look, this scale was not only of exceptional quality, it also seemed to have been taken recently!

Scales were not like other materials. Even if Master Dingyang did not have a stable source, this at least suggested he possessed more than one. Li Ximing's mind turned, and an idea formed.

If I can make this work, I might as well maintain long-term dealings with him. If I refine enough of these pills over time, I can gather the surplus into a set of six. That would effectively give me one Cangzhou serpent scale in return. Then it would no longer be useless. It would be a proper spirit material, something I could trade with other cultivators!

With that thought, his interest surged. He immediately began refining on the spot. Flames shot upward, True Fire blazed fiercely, and shifting light filled the hall.

Meanwhile, Li Zhouwei took his leave from the hall, his expression slightly heavy as he thought,

It is time I paid a visit to the demon den...
