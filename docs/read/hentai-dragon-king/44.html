"From this moment on, the Magic Attack Power test will begin. Those who cannot use magic will be lent a magic staff. Please chant the designated spell. In our country’s language, chanting ’Fireball, burst forth!’ will activate the elementary fire-type magic *Fire*."

I see.

Even someone like me who can’t use magic can have their magic power measured with a magic tool.

"Those who can use magic without magic tools may use whatever spell best showcases their true ability. However, those who can only use support magic should use *Fire* for measurement. If you wish to appeal with the effects of a special magic, please do so during the skill measurement."

Understood.

So Magic Attack Power was strictly for measuring offensive power.

"Then, test number 1. New workshop foreman Ban from the blacksmith workshop, step forward!"

"O-okay..."

"Fireball, burst forth!"

The spectators’ reaction was lukewarm. There was only a faint scattering of polite applause, like they were clapping out of courtesy for the first examinee.
