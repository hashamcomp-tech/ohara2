Not just Physical Attack Power, but Magic Attack Power as well — did that make me the strongest overall in the country?

Hm?

What about announcing my score?

Hey, why is he suddenly bragging?

What the hell is this about?

I have zero interest in suddenly listening to some random old man’s life story.

However, unlike me who had just arrived in this city, the residents were watching the priest with serious eyes. It seemed Priest Griess was deeply trusted by the citizens.

And people in medieval-like worlds really love listening to sermons and long stories from church officials.

I see.

Isn’t this completely off-topic?

Don’t get the wrong idea, okay?

Lucia... you should’ve warned me!

Just like the Physical Defense test, it was about how much magic attack I could withstand — but I was too tough, and no one could damage me. It seemed they would need to bring out magical weapons used in war to measure my Magic Defense Power.

Given the situation, I didn’t want to stand out too much, but since I wanted to free as many beastmen slaves as possible, I needed the money. I couldn’t afford to hold back.

Just the two of us.
