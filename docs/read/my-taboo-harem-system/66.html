Chapter 66: Upgrade [3]

The translucent system notification still hovered before him as a light chuckle escaped his lips.

’Then this skill is actually quite impressive.’

The explanation of the skill wasn’t entirely clear. It wasn’t that Denzel was restricted to a single ability, nor that he had been granted all abilities. Rather, he possessed the power to bend abilities that were formed through mana. That is, abilities created or controlled by mana.

He couldn’t bend natural, unenhanced elements.

In other words, he couldn’t manipulate something like ordinary water. He could only bend it if it had been infused with mana or was actively controlled by it.

’Hmm, if I think about it, this isn’t as exciting as I first thought. It has clear limitations. This flaw is a bit much.’

He ran a hand through his face.

’I was expecting something overwhelmingly OP, but I suppose it still has potential. It depends entirely on skill level. And knowing how the system works, progression should still follow the same pattern the higher my rank, the stronger I become.’

’All I need to do is train harder, grow stronger, and I’ll eventually reach where I need to be. Maybe even refine how efficiently I use it.’

’Doesn’t sound too bad now, does it?’

He paused, stretching lightly.

’Still, I can’t deny it. I’m kind of pumped. Even a small ability like this closes the gap. It’s a starting point closing the distance between me and Patrick and everyone else in this world wielding elemental powers.’

’With this ability, and the system that lets me grow stronger through women I could definitely carve my way to the top of this world.’

"Heh."

’Though... I wonder what comes with that kind of attention.’

He let the thought linger for a moment.

’But the world’s boring as it is. I might as well use everything I have to make it more interesting.’

’Oh well.’

With that thought, he rose from the bed.

’Since I have this ability now, I should test it properly. I need to understand exactly how far I can push it.’

’But the flaw says I can’t influence normal elements not at this stage. Not while the ability is still F-rank.’

’So that means’

’I need to test it against someone else’s ability. See how much control I actually have over another person’s mana-based attacks.’

’But who would I even test it on?’

’The only ability users I know aren’t even from this house.’

’There’s Patrick, and’

His eyes suddenly sharpened.

’Oya. Antonia.’

’Aisha mentioned yesterday that Antonia has an element that’s the only reason she’s still working at the massage parlor.’

’So maybe it wouldn’t be a bad idea to test it with her.’

’It’s a chance to understand this ability better, to see exactly how far I can push it.’

A faint grin formed on his lips.

’And besides it might also be useful to use this chance to fuck that fat ass or hers, maybe fuck some sense outta her while at it.’

’Hmm, well, since that’s all said and done, I do have to ask, where’s mom? She’s been gone for a few minutes. Wasn’t she supposed to bring me some breakfast or what? Is she trying to say there are things more important than feeding her king?’

’Hmm perhaps I should punish her, just like I did Aisha.’

He thought this as a soft smile formed on his lips.

’Well, since it’s been a while, I guess I’ll have to go looking for her.’

So he stood up and stepped out of the room.

He walked down the corridor toward the kitchen.

When he turned into the kitchen, his eyes landed on Maria and beside her stood none other than Sophia.

Sophia’s gaze met his for a brief moment, then she quickly looked away, pretending she hadn’t seen him at all.

Maria immediately spoke up.

"Denzel, I told you I would bring the food."

’I just got a bit hungry. I couldn’t just sit around.’

"Oh, I just got a bit hungry. I couldn’t just sit around," he replied.

Maria offered an apologetic smile.

"Oh, I’m so sorry, my dear. I just needed to warm it up for you. Just give me a few minutes and I’ll be done. And for the time I wasted, I promise I’ll...I’ll make it up to you."

Denzel looked at her quietly.

’I think we both know what she means by make it up to me.’

He nodded slightly.

’Okay.’

Maria continued speaking, and his eyes shifted to Sophia.

"Sophia, good morning," he said.

Sophia gave a small nod.

"Good morning, Denzel," she replied.

After that, she said nothing else.

Denzel kept watching her for a moment.

’This woman almost ruined my night almost cost me from my pleasure with mum last night. And ever since I found out about her little job expedition, she’s been giving me this attitude.’

’But she has no idea. She just made a mistake showing up here. She thinks someone like her is enough to stop me? She’s out of her mind.’

Without changing his expression, he turned back to Maria.

"Hey Maria, can you fetch me some orange? Just something from outside. Something sweet to put in my mouth since I’m already a bit starved."

Maria’s cheeks flushed slightly.

’My son is hungry I need to feed him as soon as I can. Damn, why isn’t the food done already.’

"I’m, I’m on it, but the food will be done soon," she said.

"I know, but I really want the orange," he insisted.

Maria nodded.

"Understood."

Then she left, walking toward the storage area where the fruits were kept.

Once she was gone, Denzel made sure she was out of sight. And as she disappeared into the corridor, a faint Mischievous smile drew on his face as he turned his attention back to Sophia, who had her back turned to him.

Now that they were alone, his expression hardened slightly.

’Finally, I’ve got you exactly where I want you. Now it’s time to find out what her secret is.’
