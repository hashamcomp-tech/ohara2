Chapter 82: Liar

Denzel remained silent, a look of surprise lingering on his face as he gazed at Sophia.

Sophia paused.

To her own surprise, she didn’t feel the need to avert her eyes this time. Instead, she held his gaze, staring directly into his eyes.

Then, slowly, a faint smile formed on her lips, and the uncertainty in her eyes seemed to fade, replaced by a newfound clarity.

"It’s really nothing..." she said softly.

"Truth is I just went out a few days ago and saw something that I thought you’d really like."

Denzel blinked.

"And what exactly do you think I’d like?"

"Well..." Sophia hesitated, her smile turning slightly awkward.

"It’s a little silly."

She glanced away for a moment before continuing.

"There was this really beautiful necklace."

A nostalgic look crossed her face.

"It was expensive. Really expensive. I saw it while passing by one of the local market and honestly I thought it would look perfect on you."

She let out a small laugh before shaking her head.

"I wanted to buy it for you. I wanted to keep it as a surprise."

Her gaze lowered toward the floor.

"But for that, I needed money. Quite a lot of it. So I figured I’d work and slowly save up enough."

She paused before continuing.

"And since you helped me back then when you got me new clothes"

A warm smile appeared on her face.

"I thought this could be my way of repaying my lovely brother."

"What?"

"It’s nothing."

"Maybe."

Sophia smiled.

"But I insist"

Before Denzel could respond, she stepped forward and gently placed a hand beneath his chin. Her eyes met his as she smiled warmly.

"I just want to make my big bro happy."

For a moment, the world seemed to fall silent. Then she added quietly,

"Is that really so strange?"

A hint of embarrassment flashed across her face.

"I would’ve preferred to keep it a surprise, but I could tell I was making you worry."

Her fingers slowly slipped away from his chin.

"So I just wanted to take that worry away."

Silence followed.

Denzel’s gaze remained fixated at her, instantly, he didn’t even need anyone to tell him.

’This bitch is lying to me.’

’But I do wonder why. Why, of all things, would she choose to start lying to me now?’

"Okay. I guess I was getting worked up over nothing." he said.

Sophia shook her head lightly.

"It’s not like that. Just calm down, bro."

As she spoke, she gently cupped his chin.

"Everything is going to be fine. I’ll get you what you need, I promise. Everything will work out."

Her cheerful smile never faltered.

A moment later, she leaned forward and gave him a kiss.

When she finally pulled away, her eyes remained fixed lovingly on his.

"You should get some rest...don’t worry, you can keep your own secret till you’re ready to talk to me....till then, I’ll leave you be. But if you need anything, just call me. I’ll be there."

Denzel smiled and nodded.

She leaned in, locking her lips onto his so shamelessly as though he wasn’t truly her brother.

After a short while, she finally pulled out from their deep smooch and then she headed out.

Just before leaving, she paused.

Turning back, she cast him one last glance over her shoulder, a warm smile on her face.

Then the door clicked shut behind her.

Silence filled the room.

’Now I’m absolutely sure something isn’t right.’

His expression darkened.

’First, there’s the missing memory, now Sophia is acting strangely... and then the woman who’s purposely ran from me...’

’One problem at a time. Step by step.’
