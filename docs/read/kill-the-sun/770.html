The two of them looked at Nick with furrowed brows.

"What way?" the Left Arm asked.

The Champion threw a quick glance at the Left Arm.

"One of my abilities," Nick said.

"You're a Late Demon," the Champion said. "I don't think your ability is enough to be useful against the Envious Researcher."

'He immediately wants to dismiss me. As expected, he doesn't want to fight the Envious Researcher. Even if I give him a way and prove that it works, he will make the mission fail due to another random reason.'

'I'm risking a lot here by exposing part of my ability. I just hope the Left Arm has a good plan.'

"It works," Nick said. "It is the reason why Lust died. When Lust died, I was only an Early Demon, and it still worked perfectly. Now, I'm a Late Demon. I'm 70% sure it works on the Envious Researcher, but if I become a Peak Demon, I'm 95% sure it will work."

"I'm fine with these odds," the Left Arm said. "It worked on Lust while you were much weaker."

"Lust was only a Peak Fallen," the Champion said. "The Envious Researcher is a Mid Adversary, at least. There is a gigantic gap in power. Nobody has ever even seen the Envious Researcher."

"Julian," the Left Arm said, "why don't you tell us how this ability works?"

'I'm risking a lot here,' Nick thought with some anxiety. 'I hope she knows what she's doing.'

"I can sense Specters," Nick said.

The Champion's brows furrowed. "Explain in more detail."

"More precisely, I can sense Specter Zephyx. I can't sense Specters directly, but I can sense when they are producing Zephyx. I can see the Zephyx traveling through the air and can pinpoint the location of a Specter due to that."

"While the Envious Researcher is most likely the penultimate Specter when it comes to staying hidden, I don't think it can affect its surroundings to keep its Zephyx generation secret. As long as the Envious Researcher is producing Zephyx, I'm certain that I can pinpoint its location."

The Champion furrowed his brows, and Nick could tell that he was troubled.

While the Champion was the strongest human, he was probably not much older than Nick. He might even be younger.

'If I am already able to tell what he is thinking to such a degree, he's probably an open book to the Left Arm.'

"Can you prove that it will work on the Envious Researcher?" the Champion asked.

"No, but I can tell you how I sensed Lust," Nick said. "Lust didn't come out from hiding before the Right Arm attacked. If it did that, I would be dead. I am only alive because the Right Arm struck it before it could attack me. "I transmitted the approximate location to the Right Arm, and the Right Arm would have hit Lust if it hadn't seen my Barrier activate. Luckily, the Right Arm was much more powerful than Lust and managed to kill it in a couple more strikes."

The Champion didn't say anything for a while and just looked down with furrowed brows.

"We can try, but sensing it is not the only issue," he said.

Then, a feeling of longing appeared in the Left Arm's eyes.

"He was so powerful."

"Alas…"
