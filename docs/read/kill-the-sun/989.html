Chapter 989

The thin General immediately went onto the offense again and attacked the Nightmen.

The Nightmen all used their energy to defend against the attacks by deflecting them.

But that wasn't easy.

The General couldn't pick them off, but its attacks were still devastatingly powerful.

The Nightmen were using up more energy than the General, and soon, they would run out of energy.

"How much longer?!" one of them transmitted to the Duskbringer.

"I'm done," the Duskbringer transmitted.

Then, he teleported and appeared in front of the thin General, who immediately struck back.

Two of its claws pierced through the Duskbringer's arms to block his attack, while the other claws attacked his head and torso.

DING! DING!

But then, all the blades were blocked by identical-looking blades!

The Nightmen watched in shock as eight additional arms shot out of the Duskbringer's body.

Even more, these arms were identical to the General's arms!

'Having a purely biological body has its advantages,' the Duskbringer thought as he struck forward with his remaining arms.

The Nightmen had several mechanical parts in their bodies, but that was not true for the Duskbringer.

He was completely biological.

Naturally, Nick had also given the Duskbringer immense control over its own biology.

There were disadvantages and advantages to having a certain body.

A normal human body could use all its power with two arms. After all, power didn't only come from the arms but also from the back, chest, waist, and legs.

A human who launched a proper punch used all the muscles in their body.

Adding more arms would not increase one's power since only one or two arms could use all the muscles of the body at the same time.

However, only having two arms reduced adaptability.

When one needed to block many attacks, having more arms would be better than only having two.

But one couldn't just grow new arms when they wanted, right?

Wrong!

The only way to have the perfect body during every moment in combat was to make the body changeable.

Because of that, Nick had developed a Technique that allowed the Duskbringer to consume biological matter, which would store the absorbed Pure Energy and distribute it amongst more appendages.

Since this was undigested Pure Energy, this change in biology was only temporary.

With time, the new appendages would lose all Pure Energy and die.

But for these moments in which the new appendages existed, they would be extremely useful.

The Duskbringer attacked the arms that attacked it, cutting two more off.

He needed to use several arms to cut off one arm since the absorbed Pure Energy from that one arm was spread across eight of them, making them weaker.

The General immediately moved back, and the Duskbringer absorbed the two arms he had cut off, adding their Pure Energy to his added arms.

At that moment, the Nightmen attacked again.

This time, they actually charged forward to surround the thin General.

With far fewer arms, the thin General had issues defending itself from the attacks, and some of the attacks hit its weak armor.

Its armor exploded off its body, releasing purple blood.

The General noticed that this was a bad fight.

Most likely, it wasn't going to win.

So, it started to retreat.

At that moment, ten more Duskbringers appeared, and all of them created portals.

Countless portals appeared around the fleeing General, and an avalanche of attacks shot through them.

It avoided almost all of them, but then, another portal appeared in front of it.

One of the Duskbringer's new arms shot through.

The General could immediately tell that this one was real, and it launched a storm of blades at it.

But then, the arm suddenly swelled.

BOOOOOM!

And exploded!

The stream of blades was pushed to the side, and the thin General was pushed back by the explosion.

The Duskbringer had transformed the foreign Pure Energy into a volatile substance, which was similar to antimatter.

Another portal appeared in front of the General, and another volatile arm came through it.

BOOOOM!

This repeated three more times.

The explosions didn't injure the General a lot, but they stopped it from fleeing.

Then, one last portal opened, and the Duskbringer charged through it.

It ran straight into the thin General.

BANG!

The two of them collided, and they were chest to chest.

Naturally, the thin General immediately attacked the Duskbringer's back.

BANG!

But the Duskbringer used his original arms to keep the remaining arms at bay.

At the same time, the remaining new arms wrapped around the thin General.

The blades at the end of the fake arms swelled behind the thin General.

BOOOOOM!

And exploded!

Several of these explosions from point-blank range were not easy to resist, and a huge chunk of the General's back exploded off of it.

The Duskbringer had been protected from the explosion by the General's body.

However, the General didn't stop attacking.

It immediately struck the Duskbringer again.

But then, its blades just phased through the body in front of it.

An illusion!

When had he changed locations with an illusion?!

At that moment, the General realized what was going on.

The explosion was actually not meant to kill or injure it!

It was meant to distort its perception!

When a huge amount of energy detonated, spacetime distorted, and sensing things became much more difficult.

Just now, the General had noticed a tiny speck entering its body through the open wound on its back.

Sadly, the General had noticed it too late.

The tiny speck cut through the flesh inside the thin General and reached its cortex in an instant.

BANG!

The tiny speck enlarged, turning into the Duskbringer.

Biological bodies couldn't be changed into stuff like mist, but they could be shrunk or expanded.

The Duskbringer had swapped places with an illusion, entered the thin General's body, and destroyed it from the inside.

The thin General's head exploded, and just to make sure, the Duskbringer completely hacked the body into pieces.

After some seconds, the entire body was turned into fine powder, and the Duskbringer used a burst of energy to turn the powder into nothing.

It was done.

The last member of the Amorphous Race in this solar system was eliminated.

They had done it!

They had freed humanity!
