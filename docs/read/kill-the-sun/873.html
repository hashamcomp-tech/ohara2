Prephyx was useless.

It was toxic.

It was waste.

Prephyx was like ash.

Getting any kind of energy out of it was almost impossible since it had already given off all of its energy when it had turned to ash.

Yet, compared to ash, Prephyx couldn't easily be used to raise plants.

Nothing consumed Prephyx since it was just horrible.

On other planets, Prephyx represented contamination.

It spread across the worlds like toxic dirt.

Being in an area with a high density of Prephyx would damage lifeforms using Star Energy or Pure Energy.

The worst part was that it didn't gather in one spot.

It just spread into individual particles that mixed with everything else.

The Enlightened Ones had never seen any other worlds, but they could make educated guesses as to how the Prephyx would affect the living beings there.

One shocking fact was how the evolution of biological lifeforms in relation to Pure Energy would endanger the survival of the entire planet.

Prephyx was produced when Star Energy lost all of its energy.

If there were a kind of plant that grew incredibly powerful by consuming Star Energy, it would also give off Prephyx.

Over many years, this would increase the density of Prephyx on the planet, making survival for many kinds of life impossible.

Only the strongest and oldest plants would be able to resist the Prephyx.

The younger plants would wither and die.

This would leave a planet with only a couple of mighty plants, while the remainder would be devoid of life.

But eventually, the Prephyx would become so dense that not even these mighty plants would be able to gather any Star Energy anymore.

And finally, these plants would also die, leaving behind a dead world.

It would take thousands of years for the Star Energy to wash the Prephyx out from the planet, and new life would appear.

This time, it had to hope that nothing developed an affinity for Star Energy.

The Enlightened Ones believed that the aliens gathered a gigantic amount of Prephyx and sent it into the solar system.

The forcefield kept the Prephyx from reaching Earth, but it still surrounded it from a distance.

This disturbed their sensors, making it harder to aim their weapons.

That was when the aliens used their powerful warriors in a devastating attack.

The Enlightened Ones believed that many, many warriors of the aliens died, but in the end, at least one of them managed to reach Earth.

And that was the end of it.

Humanity couldn't use their weaponry to target things on Earth.

It killed all but a very few genetically altered humans.

The other colonized planets were eradicated shortly after.

That was how Earth was turned into a Zephyx mine.

At least, that was the best guess the Enlightened Ones had.

The existence of Prephyx was a decisive factor in the war.

If humanity had learned to deal with it, it would have most likely won the war.

Prephyx was contamination, but it could also be used as a weapon.

After talking about Prephyx for a long time, the Ice Maiden talked about Zephyx.

Zephyx was amazing.

It had an incredible density of energy and was also stable.

Over time, they also managed to make use of Star Energy and discover Pure Energy.

As for the Prephyx…

They just dumped it into outer space.

The Prephyx couldn't gather on Earth due to an artificial repellant.

Over the many years, a lot of the Prephyx gathered on the other planets in the solar system.
