Chapter 952

Nick looked at the aggressive Null for a while longer before focusing on his abilities again.

The next abilities he focused on came from the Fog.

He tried the first ability and vanished.

He was gone.

There was no mist.

He was just gone.

Yet, if someone with a powerful mind and perception looked at him, they would see blind spots.

'I have the ultimate Force Specter body that supports my primary ability.'

'The Null's body.'

Yes, Nick had essentially turned into the Null.

Nick moved through the underground without interacting with any solid matter.

He moved through the underground city.

Then, he moved through the Containment Units.

Later, he entered the outside world.

Not even the Sun's light interacted with him.

He didn't exist.

A moment later, he moved to Aegis' headquarters.

He easily passed through the walls.

He entered the Champion's office, and the Champion didn't notice Nick at all.

After that, Nick moved into Nurse Alice's Containment Unit.

Entering the Containment Unit was the first difficult thing, and Nick needed to use a bit of Zephyx to pass through the walls.

But as soon as he entered the Containment Unit, his ability deactivated.

Nurse Alice had a terrifying array of abilities, and one of them could sense Nick, apparently.

"Am I being executed?" she asked.

Of course, Nurse Alice didn't think this was Nick.

She thought he was the Null.

Without saying anything, Nick just left the Containment Unit again.

He could still see Nurse Alice with his primary ability.

Since she was a Specter, she didn't show any emotions on her face.

She also wouldn't ask the Sun about this since that might unveil her terrifying knowledge of Energy manipulation.

It was better to act like she had never seen the Null.

Nick returned to an isolated location and tried the new ability from the Fog.

A moment later, his body expanded until it was over 1,000 kilometers wide.

And yet, nobody noticed him.

After all, he was still the Null.

He was imperceivable and nowhere, but his body was also so big that it covered a huge chunk of Earth.

Nick expanded more and more, but at some point, he started to use more Zephyx than he was regenerating.

That moment occurred when he was ten thousand kilometers wide.

'My theoretical limit of expansion is my perception, but the bigger I get, the more Zephyx I use.'

At first glance, this didn't seem very powerful.

Yet, one had to remember something.

Nick could materialize in any location he was.

This meant his body could appear and disappear anywhere within this ten-thousand-kilometer-wide area.

He could attack and kill anyone within his range.

At this moment, five Shields were within Nick's range.

If he wanted to kill them, his body could strike one of them after the other with speeds that might as well make the attacks simultaneous.

The only one who could see that the attacks didn't happen simultaneously would be the Champion.

And yet, even if he could see them, all the attacks would have finished before he could stand up from his throne.

Nick returned to his earlier location and focused on the abilities he had received from the Blinding Light.

A sphere of light appeared in his hand.

It turned into a blade.

It turned into a laser.

It turned into anything he wanted, except Pure Energy or Star Energy.

'I was thinking about increasing my offensive capabilities, but that was unnecessary.'

Nick used a lot of Zephyx and summoned a tiny needle of light.

His Zephyx was draining rapidly as he held the needle in his hand.

The Containment Unit around him immediately turned into gas.

There was an unimaginable concentration of light in this tiny needle.

'Without my primary ability, this would only be as strong as the weapons the Empire made.'

'But with my primary ability, the power is so much higher.'

If Nick let the light in his hand detonate…

'It would be as powerful as a hydrogen bomb.'

This thing could turn the surrounding twenty kilometers into gas if it detonated.

However, Nick could also launch it without wanting it to detonate.

In that case, it would be the explosion of a hydrogen bomb focused onto a tiny spot.

The only things that could possibly survive this were extremely powerful Pure Energy materials or warriors at the Mid or Late Ninth Level.

As Nick looked at the needle, he realized something.

'I can kill the Sun.'

The Sun would be able to survive a couple of these needles, but Nick would be able to launch enough before the failsafe kicked in that would kill him.

'But the Sun is just the first step. It is not the greatest threat.'

Next, Nick looked at the new ability he had received by becoming an Eternal.

The new ability the Blinding Light gave him.

As Nick used it, the energy around him gathered.

All the Zephyx, Prephyx, and light in the surroundings gathered around Nick.

'I can manipulate all forms of conventional energy and energies related to Zephyx and Prephyx.'

Nick absorbed all of the energy he gathered.

'This increases my Zephyx recovery speed severalfold.'

'And yet…'

Nick summoned a Star Energy generator.

As it released Star Energy into the surroundings, Nick used the ability again.

The Star Energy swirled around Nick.

'I can control the movement of Star Energy, but that's it.'

'I can't turn it into Zephyx, Prephyx, or Pure Energy.'

'However, I can use this to move through a place covered in Star Energy.'

'I can create a tunnel in the Star Energy, allowing me to pass through it without interacting with it.'

After that, Nick looked at the two abilities he had received from the Nightmare.

Nick closed his eyes and used the first one.

Then, Nick saw everything on Earth that was covered in darkness.

And since the majority of the underground was also dark, he could also see the majority of the underground.

That was when Nick used the other part of the same ability.

There was a magma lake deep underground where Nick used the ability.

An instant later, all the light in the magma lake was snuffed out.

The magma cooled, and the underground lake was no longer illuminated.

Nick hadn't known that the magma lake existed.

He just knew that there was a spot he couldn't see underground, which meant that there was light there.

'I can also create darkness in exchange for Zephyx,' Nick thought as he could now see the extinguished magma lake.

After that, Nick used the other ability from the Nightmare.
