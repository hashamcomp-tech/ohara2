Chapter 806

The team of Agents turned the entire city upside down.

The Governor and five of his most powerful employees were executed.

Over 20 corrupt rich people indirectly supporting Wrath were executed as well.

Several gangs outside of the tower were executed.

Five Specters were captured and fed to the Crimson Sea.

However, compared to how Aegis usually did things, one servant of Pestilence was spared.

The Merchant, the Specter who created the medicine used all over the world.

The Merchant was confiscated by Aegis and transported to their headquarters.

This Specter was simply too valuable.

The entire world needed its medicine.

Of course, during all of this, Aegis also interrogated Nick in his new disguise.

But Nick had already expected that to happen, which was why he had assumed the identity of this specific person.

This person had been a magnanimous philanthropist on the outside.

He had always advocated for people with a lower status than him and did a lot of charity.

But all of that was just a disguise to hide his true intentions.

He was building an empire that profited from the guilt of the same people it saved.

Forced labor.

Almost no pay.

The people were almost worked to death.

However, this person was so good at hiding all of this that nobody knew about these dark secrets.

Well, except for Nick, who had uncovered them very quickly.

On the surface, Nick's disguise was that of a good person who just wanted to help humanity, and that was enough for Aegis.

Aegis didn't have the time to conduct a thorough investigation of the thousands of rich people in the city.

Naturally, Aegis also tested Nick's identity.

They checked his Barrier and its records. Those couldn't be falsified.

Well, except for the fact that they could.

Nick's Barrier was special, and the Technician gave it the ability to essentially copy any Barrier.

Then, they put Nick through a couple of tests to uncover Specters.Those also couldn't be falsified.

At least, they thought so.

Nick passed all of the tests.

Due to the outstanding performance and image of Nick's disguise, Aegis gave him a high-ranking job in the new government.

He wasn't powerful enough to be a Governor, but he would have a huge part in developing the new city.

Within the next couple of months, the prices of the medicine fell drastically, and the Specter spreading the diseases also vanished.

The security inside the tower was intensified, and assassinations became much more difficult.

Two servants of Sloth and Wrath appeared within these months to regain control, but Nick found them quickly and executed them.

Six months after the city changed, Nick went to the next phase of his plan.

He entered a secret room inside the tower.

In that room was a Greater Relay.

Nick walked over to the Greater Relay and activated it.

He waited for a couple of seconds as it tried to establish a connection.

And then, the other side picked up.
