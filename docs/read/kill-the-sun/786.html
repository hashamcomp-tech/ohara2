Chapter 786

Ghosty remained still for several seconds.

"Are you serious?" he spoke out loud.

The others looked at Ghosty.

"Yes, I am," Nick answered. "I saw it die myself. I am 99% certain it is dead."

"How?" Ghosty asked.

"I'm good at sensing Specters, as you know. The Champion of Light is very powerful. I told the Champion where it was, and he killed it. Simple as that."

The Champion!

Yes, if the Champion could see the Envious Researcher, he could probably kill it!

Yet, it was still difficult for Ghosty to believe it.

Nick could see Ghosty's doubts.

"How do you think I got this huge file?" Nick transmitted. "The Technician knows the danger of the Envious Researcher, and he wouldn't distribute all of his knowledge if he weren't sure that it's dead."

"It is dead."

"You may share all you know with each other. You don't need to work in separate teams anymore."

"However, it is still important that you stay out of any source of light."

Ghosty took a deep breath.

He looked at his saved files and deleted almost all of his own notes.

"Send it to me," Ghosty told Michael.

Michael sent the file, and Ghosty looked at it.

It was so difficult to believe.

But here it was.

All of the Technician's knowledge.

It wasn't curated yet since the Technician was still in the process of trimming it down, but it was there.

It was too good to be true.

"You have to hurry up now," Nick transmitted. "Humanity will be hot on your heels in terms of technological progress. You need to make the Star Energy Generator as small as possible so that we can access the Seeds and research Pure Energy." ṝ
