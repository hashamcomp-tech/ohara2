Chapter 812

Nick talked a bit more with the Technician before he went to the Left Arm's office.

As he entered the office, he looked at the spot where the Left Arm usually sat.

It was strange to see that spot empty.

With time, the Politician would most likely take over this office.

Nick walked past the office and opened the door in the back, entering the Champion of Light's office.

The Champion had his eyes closed as he sat on his throne.

"What is it?" the Champion asked without opening his eyes.

"The Left Arm isn't here anymore. I am unsure who my superior is now," Nick said.

"The Politician will take over the Left Arm's job," the Champion said. "Until then, you can do whatever you want."

The Champion sounded a bit disheartened.

Nick wasn't sure why he was disheartened.

Was it Aegis' current situation?

Was it the Left Arm's death?

"My other job is progressing smoothly," Nick said.

"Good," the Champion answered curtly.

"I managed to lead the Protectors to kill Gluttony," Nick said. "I believe I deserve a reward for that. I want the next 30 years to focus on some things before I want to receive Aegis' help to become an Early Fallen."

"Early Fallen?" he asked.

Nick nodded. "My indirect impact on the world will push me into the realm of the Fallen within these 30 years."
