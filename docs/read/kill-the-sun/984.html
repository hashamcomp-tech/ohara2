Chapter 984

The final battle arrived.

Daniel, ten more Nightmen in the Tenth Realm, and 350 Nightmen in the Ninth Realm.

All of them were battling the two Generals.

Ten thousand years ago, such a force would have been unimaginable on Earth.

Even the weakest of the Nightmen joining the battle could fight evenly with Nick.

And yet, the results of the battle were still up in the air.

Considering this incredible force and how much trouble it had with the Generals, the Empire was most likely eradicated without any opposition.

The two Generals assumed a formation in which the bigger one was in the front while the thin and long one was behind its back.

As soon as the smaller General reached the bigger General's back, it vanished.

It seemed like only the big General was left, but everyone knew that this was not the case.

Daniel's sword was covered in dark energy, and he slashed forward.

A wave of terrifying energy approached the big General.

But then, Daniel's eyes widened when he saw the General moving its weapons around in a strange manner.

The black energy shooting towards it split into pieces, flying past it.

It was almost like the black energy was a stream, and the big General was a massive boulder in its middle.

But Daniel wasn't alone.

The other Nightmen in the Tenth Realm arrived as well, and they all launched their own attacks.

They launched energy attacks, physical attacks, force attacks, mind attacks, gravity attacks, and so on.

The General's weapons moved with terrifying speed and finesse.

The energy attacks washed over it.

The physical attacks hit its massive shell but bounced off.

The force attacks pushed it back, but its insides were so incredibly stable that it didn't receive any injuries.

The mind attacks didn't work at all. The General had entered some form of fortified mindset, which made learning new things impossible. Since it couldn't learn new things, it was impossible to alter its state of mind or manipulate it.

The only thing that seemed to work was the gravity attack. It locked the big General in place, stopping it from moving.

The gravity made it more difficult for the General to move its weapons to disperse the energy attacks, and the energy attacks landing after the gravity attack started to show effects.

The chitin of the burly General started to heat up massively.

The chitin was burning so brightly that it outshone the sun.

Meanwhile,the Nightmen in the Ninth Realm all gathered together to combine their powers.

They created a gigantic ball of chaotic energies, and they aimed it at the burly General.

These Nightmen were not as strong as the primary fighters, but when combined, their attack reached levels of power much higher than those of the primary fighters.

But then, a storm of chitinous sickles appeared beside them, and in the middle of the storm was the thin General.

The thin General charged forward with unbelievable speed.

It was the fastest thing any of the Nightmen had ever seen!

BANG!

The thin General appeared behind the Nightmen in the Ninth Realm.

Or, more precisely, the bloody chunks that remained.

In a single instance, the thin General had killed all 350 Nightmen in the Ninth Realm.

Naturally, the remaining eleven Nightmen noticed what had happened, and a cold shower ran down their backs.

This speed was absurd!

It was just as absurd as the defensive capabilities of the other General!

"Gather!" Daniel transmitted, and all the Nightmen gathered.

If the thin General managed to isolate one of them, it could kill them in an instant, but as long as they were close to each other, they could defend.

The thin General's defenses couldn't be as powerful as the big General's defenses.

If they managed to hit the thin General, it might die.

The thin General vanished again a moment later while the Nightmen rushed towards the big General in a tight formation.

They jumped onto it like flies jumped onto a rotting carcass.

The man with the Gravity Techniques used them to slow the General down while Daniel focused on defending him.

His attacks were crucial in winning the fight, and Daniel had to make sure that he didn't die.

The big General kept defending against the attacks, and its shell became brighter and brighter.

CRK!

A spear managed to penetrate the shining shell of the General, and for the first time, the Nightmen saw the deep purple blood of the General.

The shell had been heated to such a degree that it had become unstable.

They injured it!

"Attack!" Daniel transmitted, keeping an eye out for the thin General.

The Nightmen attacked with renewed fervor.

CRACK!

A staff broke off a part of the glowing chitin, revealing the dense muscle beneath.

They were getting somewhere!

But then, the big General held its hand out as it kept being attacked.

In an instant,its entire body stopped glowing as all the energy around its body gathered on its hand.

Then, it moved its hand forward.

BOOOOOOM!

A gigantic storm of plasma shot out of it, covering three of the Nightmen, which were immediately annihilated.

The storm of plasma shot into the distance and reached Earth.

It hit Earth at an angle.

BOOOOM!

The remaining Nightmen saw the attack hit Earth, and they became terrified.

The attack left a gigantic crevice!

This thing had cut across all of Asia, creating a crevice that was over 1,000 kilometers deep and almost 10,000 kilometers long.

Earth was no longer a sphere.

Now, it looked like a half-moon!

The structural integrity of Earth was already compromised, and storms of fire ravaged its surface.

Stones were collapsing and scattering as Earth's gravity tried to recreate a spherical shape.

SHING!

At that moment, the head of the Nightman, using the Gravity Techniques, was harvested by the thin General.

There had been 361 Nightmen at the beginning of the assault.

Now, there were seven.

Daniel looked at the Generals with terror.

This did not look good!

And then, everything suddenly exploded.
