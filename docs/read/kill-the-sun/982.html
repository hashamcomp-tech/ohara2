Chapter 982

"Everyone ready?" Daniel asked while standing beside Kiran.

Several hundred Nightmen were standing in front of them.

Naturally, the Nightmen didn't want to die for some greater cause, but they didn't have any other choice.

If they wanted to achieve something, they needed to risk their lives in the final battle.

And if they managed to survive, the entire universe would be free for the taking!

The Nightmen all nodded in affirmation.

"As discussed, anyone who is not giving their all will be killed," Daniel added with a slight smile.

"Anyone who gives their all and survives gets dibs on the universe."

"And anyone who gives their all and dies… well…"

An arrogant smile appeared on Daniel's face. "Sucks to be you!"

Some of the Nightmen chuckled.

"We are not fighting for glory," Daniel said. "We are not fighting for some greater cause."

"We are not fighting to free our dear weak relatives."

"No, that's not what we're about, and we all know it."

"Riches! Power! Eternity! Status!" Daniel shouted.

"We have milked Starbreaker Base for all it's worth, and if we ever want to have more, we need to get out of this stupid cage!"

"Look at us! We have several people in the Tenth Realm! One simple planet is not enough! We need more!"

Then, Daniel glanced at Kiran with a devious smile.

"And as soon as we are out of this Prephyx-filled shithole, we also do not need to rely on our weaker siblings anymore."

"The universe is filled with Star Energy, and as long as we are surrounded by Star Energy, we can survive! Sure, growing will still be hard, but we don't need to wear these dumb generators anymore just to survive!"

"Ten thousand years! That was how long our dear creator has worked for this day!"

"And today is exactly that day!"

The Nightmen were getting excited.

They were less like a resistance army and more like a band of robbers readying for an assault.

"Any last questions?" Daniel asked.

"Who gets the alien bitches?" one of them asked.

"My guy," Daniel said with some disgust. "Have you seen the Amorphous Race? If you want to go to town on that, you're free to. I don't think you have a lot of competition here."

Some of the Nightmen chuckled.

"What about their Zephyx storages?" one of them asked.

"We'll distribute those according to contributions," Daniel said casually.

"Other planets?" another one asked.

"We can't hand those out," Daniel said. "We need those for our kids and to make Zephyx.We're going to deal with these administrative matters as soon as everything is over."

A couple more stupid questions were asked, and Daniel entertained all of them.

"Are we done with the questions now?" he asked.

No more questions.

"Alright, then, let's get to it!"

Daniel looked at Kiran. "Stay here with your people. You won't be useful. Try not to die to the flood of Star Energy."

Kiran just nodded. "I hope you will all survive."

Daniel just chuckled a bit.

"Mission start!" he shouted.

The next moment, 99% of the Nightmen gathered on the ceiling of the base, while only a single one stopped near its bottom.

"Three," Daniel said.

"Two."

"One."

BANG!

Daniel used his sword and attacked the ceiling, while another Nightman in the Tenth Realm attacked the bottom.

An instant later, all of the Nightmen shot through the holes.

As soon as the two holes were created, something happened on the moon.

The Illusionists watched as gigantic blue spots appeared on their spheres.

These blue spots symbolized Pure Energy.

The readings of the accumulated Pure Energy were unreasonably high!

These readings were much stronger than the last time humanity had tried to escape!

The Illusionists immediately sent distress signals to the Chief Illusionists, and the Chief Illusionists immediately sent their signals to the Strategists.

All of this took place in about ten milliseconds.

The Strategists immediately declared crisis mode, which allowed them to make decisions without having to ask the Representatives for permission.

Orders were immediately spread across the Amorphous Race, and everyone started to move.

The two Strategists charged out of their room and entered the room with the galactic communicator.

The Mother had to be informed about this immediately!

They reached the communicator and sent a message to the Mother immediately.

The message was sent, but they couldn't wait for an answer.

The transmission itself would take a couple of seconds, and these seconds were crucial.

While the Strategists were sending a message to the Mother, one of the two Chief Engineers charged toward the antimatter laser.

The two Chief Illusionists immediately accessed the Sun.

Lastly, the two Generals shot towards Earth.

They would get there in a bit more than two seconds.

However, for beings at this level of power, two seconds was a very long time.

After all,everyone was moving at speeds that had to be measured in thousands of kilometers per second.

While the moon was thrown into a state of orderly chaos, many things happened on Earth as well.

One of the Nightmen in the Tenth Realm reached the core of the Earth.

He easily dug into it, passing through the dense metal like it was air.

Inside the Earth's core, the man's perception picked up another sphere.

It was camouflaged very well.

People of the Ninth Realm might not notice.

But to him, the sphere was quite obvious.

This sphere was over ten kilometers wide and surrounded by several biological weapons.

The weapons noticed the man and started shooting.

They unleashed powerful lasers filled with Pure Energy, turning all the metal into gas.

The man smirked and stopped.

All the lasers hit him in the chest, burning his clothing away.

'Aw, that's cute. They're trying to kill me,' the man thought with a chuckle.

The next moment, the man pulled out a gigantic hammer.

In an instant, he arrived in front of the sphere.

Then, he struck it.

The entire sphere cracked into pieces.

That was when the man gathered his Pure Energy.

Since the walls were broken, his perception now stretched through the entire sphere.

He saw the Null, the Nightmare, and all the different biological machines.

'Thanks for the good genes!' the man thought with a chuckle as he saw the Nightmare.

A moment later, a purple ball appeared above the Nightmare.

This was a Gravity Technique the Nightman had developed.

"And bye," he said before snapping his fingers.

CRACK!

The entire sphere was pulled to the purple ball.

The ten-kilometer-wide sphere turned into a compressed sphere that was barely a couple of centimeters wide.

The gravitational forces were comparable to those of a neutron star.

The machines were destroyed.

The portal was destroyed.

The Nightmare was destroyed.

Even the Null was destroyed.

Nothing on Earth could resist the power of a Pure Energy being in the Tenth Realm.
