-If something is bothering us, he asked us to think as one… If we’re going through hard times, we should put our heads together… It’s unfamiliar to me, but he said he’ll be with us. I don’t fit in a place like this and even feel uncomfortable, but I’d like to ask you guys for help. I hope that the Blue, the Holy See, and the State will merge their powers to protect Kiyoung.

It was impossible to attack the Blue dungeon. However, it might be possible to attack the State dungeon. Oftentimes, the larger the size of any group and the more humans gathered…

‘The gap is bound to get wider.’

If the Song Villain weren’t an idiot, he would realize that.
