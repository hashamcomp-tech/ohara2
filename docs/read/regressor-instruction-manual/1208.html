Chapter 1208. The Demon King of Sunset’s Castle, the Final Story (1)

Everything felt surreal. I was sure others felt the same way at the sudden turn of events.

Kim Hyun-Sung raised his head quietly while sporting sunset wings. Amidst the collapsing ceiling bathed in the rays of sunset, he spread his wings and looked around.

Overall, it was an unrealistic sight.

I couldn’t really see him because of the messy, long hair covering his face, but when he spread his wings, I got a glimpse of his face. There was a frown beneath the billowing hair. The sunset feathers flying everywhere descended slowly to the ground.

This wasn’t the best entrance because he simply descended from the sky.

However, the feathers flying around Kim Hyun-Sung, his demeanor, his face, and everything else were supporting his sudden appearance. He was setting the mood, and it was like he was saying that this was how the final boss ought to appear.

If I had to point out something I disliked...

‘Why... is he in such bad shape?’

Aside from his long hair that resembled a mane, his eyes glimmered in a light that made him look as if he had been cornered. His eyes were bloodshot, and his pupils were trembling anxiously. He was biting his lips, and his hands were trembling. Even though he always maintained the same weight, he looked as if he had lost some weight.

If he were chained up, he would look like a psychopathic patient in a psychiatric ward, and the sight left me speechless.

‘I can’t help it.’

After seeing that, who would question if the guild master was really mad? Even if I hadn't planted the seed, there was a chance that they had been talking about it among themselves.

A transcendent being couldn't change that drastically in such a short period of time, which made this scene even more shocking.

For some reason, I felt oddly pleased at the sight, but I also found him pitiful. Of course, I immediately suppressed those feelings.

And that was when Kim Hyun-Sung’s eyes landed on me...

‘How should I react?’

I decided to act surprised for now. I grabbed my head, showing the same reaction I would have upon seeing something I missed deeply. I was indirectly telling him that I wasn’t sure why I was crying.

Although I had used this method a few times before, it was always effective, so I had no other choice but to use it again. At the very least, he would surely feel confused.

I wasn't sure how he ended up here, but he definitely fought with the guild members. He looked like he was trying his best to hide his feelings, but he had always been an overthinker.

He wasn’t the type who could suppress the questions popping up in his head.

Of course...

‘He’s not the type to stay unmoving, either.’

Once he had made up his mind on something, he would make a move without hesitation. He had come out here to act, not to think.

It was the most natural reaction lowly mortals could show upon staring at a transcendent being. It was natural for mortals to think that they were dreaming at the sight of the Monarch of Sunset.

The hearts of everyone present were pervaded by the rawest fear and terror, along with an incomprehensible sense of helplessness and awe.
