They probably have never actually seen me in person. If they had been lucky enough, they could have seen me during a prayer meeting, but I felt like everything they knew about me was through the media.

“Raise your heads. I didn’t come to punish you,” I said to them.

When I spoke, I saw them cautiously lift their heads. Carlton and Yoojin seemed only a bit shaken, but Big Boy looked as if all the bad things he had ever done in his life had come rushing back; he was crying and sniffling, looking like he was truly repenting.
