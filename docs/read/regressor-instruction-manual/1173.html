Since the system got involved unofficially, it could be a sign telling us that Rohen’s forces weren't enough, but thanks to its involvement, we got a buff that decreased our MP consumption and increased our recovery rate.

The buff was valuable and worth the effort.

William and Emilia seemed like they just wanted to enjoy the victory right now rather than ask about that incident. To be exact, they created an atmosphere that helped the soldiers celebrate.

‘Yeah, yeah. I am the Rank One player, after all.’
