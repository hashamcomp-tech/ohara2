What was really baffling was that my statement wasn’t appealing to Jin’s subordinates at all. The old man who had been calling me a snake since earlier, the brat who looked so young that it seemed like he had yet to hit puberty, and the quiet Yongwol stared at me with even more hostility than before.

They looked like they were thinking, ‘And why the hell are you going to keep them?’ No, more than that, their expressions looked like they were asking just how much longer I intended to mock them before I would finally be satisfied.

The old man’s face turned red with fury. Judging from how he was trembling all over, it looked like he was about to unleash the Twenty-Four Plum Blossom Sword Technique.
