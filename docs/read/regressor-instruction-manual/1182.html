‘It’s well made.’

Its scenery blended with the Ashen Sunset, and if I were to exaggerate a bit, I'd say the entrance looked like it was welcoming its guests to hell.

‘Actually, it’s more like they remodeled the castle. Didn’t they say that it was originally some king’s castle?’

It was natural for some people, including Marybell, the fifth princess and the survivor of this kingdom, to be upset. Perhaps she thought the demons had damaged the pride of the royal family.

I also saw a sense of duty and responsibility in her eyes.

Overall, everyone looked nervous. They were all probably thinking the same thing. They didn’t want to enter the castle, and if I had come here without knowing anything, I would have withdrawn the troops.

“What should we do, Mr. Lee Ki-Young? Should we make a safe zone...”

“No, I think we should enter right away before we get surrounded by the demons of the 21st Legion. We worked hard to get here after shaking off the pursuers, and I don’t want to let that go to waste,” I answered.

“I see.”

“We’ll create the safe zone inside the Demon King’s Castle,” I added.

The path that Kim Ye-Ri and the members of the Blue Guild had made was really helpful to us, but publicly, it was a surprise attack on the enemy.

The demons probably hadn't expected all of us to enter the Demon King of Sunset’s Castle. The command center probably thought that I was showing off my abilities as the Genius Commander, but we couldn’t delay any longer.

‘It’s not like we didn’t get into any battles before coming here.’

We intentionally avoided battles.

The demons were actually maintaining the defensive line of the Ashen Sunset Region. The Blue Guild said that they would open a path, but the 21st Legion actually belonged to Kim Hyun-Sung, and not the Blue Guild.

They had to have witnessed their master getting beaten to death in real-time, so if they didn’t want to get purged, they had to stop us with everything they had. This applied to the demons protecting the outer walls of the castle, and they were trying to stop us from entering.

Unfortunately for them, we had a way to counter that.

“I see.” She nodded and bit her lips with a determined face.

Honestly, it didn’t really matter if the fifth princess of a fallen kingdom didn’t know the way very well because the blueprint of the Demon King’s Castle that I got from Kim Ye-Ri in advance was already in my head.

The only reason the princess was here was so that I could answer questions when asked about how I knew the way.
