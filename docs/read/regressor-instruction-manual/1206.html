It was to the point that I questioned if he was still the same guild master whom I was familiar with. To be honest, I was skeptical, but I had no choice but to admit that Miss Sun Hee-Young could be right.

Either the guild master had gone crazy, or...

‘He might not be the guild master whom we're familiar with.’

We had yet to reach a conclusion. To be honest, I didn’t know what was going on because it was up to my seniors to make the decision. It would be nice if I knew their conversations and meetings, but for now, I had to follow the orders they gave me.

I left the trembling Shiro and hurried to the door.

“...”

“...”

Ah...

Woof!

In some ways, it was pretty obvious.

If there was something actually wrong with the guild master, then they wouldn’t have left me here all alone.
