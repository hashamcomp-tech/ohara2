The fact that her stamina was legendary-grade also showed that she could be used as a blacksmith.

Of course, this was not because Yoo Ahyoung would achieve great success as a warrior in the future. Kim Hyunsung seemed not to know Yoo Ahyoung well.

Because I had my Mind’s Eye.
