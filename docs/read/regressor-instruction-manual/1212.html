Chapter 1212. The Demon King of Sunset’s Castle, the Final Story (5)

The thought that abruptly came to me lingered in my mind.

‘I have to go back.’

It was like an obsession—no, it felt more like a binding or a curse. I was already aware that the thought wasn’t logical or wise. There was no valid or logical reason for why I had to return to that hellish place.

However, my desire to go back to that place felt absolute.

‘I have to go back. I have to go back. To the place where everyone is waiting for me.’

At this point, I kept repeating the same thing like a parrot, as if I were casting a spell. I immediately got up without realizing it and then sat back down.

It was because I knew that moving now wouldn’t be helpful.

Completing my contract with Lee Ki-Young was the most urgent task, and even if my goal had changed, the task that needed to be done remained the same. In any case, clearing the Demon King’s Castle was the top priority.

Everything else would come afterward.

Naturally, I thought about Woo Hyo-Yeol and William. In fact, those two were the most important cards and had the most important roles in this event. The direction of this event depended on how those two handled things and how far they could go. By now, they were probably fighting the Queen of Succubi or the Gatekeeper of the Dark Shield.

‘The battle won’t last long.’

Kim Ye-Ri and Park Deok-Gu knew the importance of time. Since Kim Hyun-Sung had injured them, the raid would probably be pretty short. I was confident that, somehow, we would clear this dungeon. The stats earned through the raid were important, but the experience points they would obtain as a bonus were really important, so it would probably feel like a training session.

William was capable of piercing the armor of the Blood-Crazed Holy Knight and the Gatekeeper of the Dark Shield. Woo Hyo-Yeol was fast enough to chase after the Queen of Succubi—no, he at least needed to know how to fight against someone who was faster than he was.

Since I could give commands during battle, I was internally debating whether they really needed those experience points, but having bought some time, it seemed logical to take everything they could.

‘I wonder how things are going.’

They executed things much faster than I thought. I didn’t have my telescope, and Jung Ha-Yan seemed too busy to set up Anemone’s Eye, but I was already connected to Woo Hyo-Yeol.

‘I wonder if William can handle them.’

I couldn't see why not. I was managing a fake private server, so I didn't need as many features and complex functions as the official one.

‘I won’t be able to call it the Regressor Instruction Manual...’

It wasn’t Mythic, and it wasn't extravagant. It wasn't soul-bound, and everything couldn't be shared like what was made for Kim Hyun-Sung.

As long as I could communicate with him one-way and see what was happening, I wouldn't need a two-way communication between us. Getting to feel his five senses was obviously a luxury, but if possible, I’d at least like to be able to have the sense of touch.

‘Skills can be improved.’

The skills embedded in Lee Ki-Young’s body were likely the upgraded versions of what was in me. The Regressor Instruction Manual, the Telescope, and the Mythic alchemy skills were all unique traits imprinted on Lee Ki-Young's soul rather than his body, so it was impossible to implant them into me.

Anything Mythic and Semi-Mythic was capable of disrupting the continent’s order, so deploying them required the system’s approval, but everything else could be bypassed pretty easily.

Just like whenever GENIUSES made a skill or a job, I was sure he knew what I was talking about.

“That’s why I need it...” I mumbled.

“...”

“Let’s make another deal. I have a lot of coins,” I suggested.

[The Boy Adorned With Earrings of Truth says that he wants to become your GENIUS.]

[Would you like to change your GENIUS?]

[Level 6 Mimic is now unlocked.]

Unlike the time when I chose the Goddess of Flower and Fertility to become a GENIUS, there weren’t any flashy visual effects. The holy power coursing through me was disappearing, but at this point, my skills as the Saint of Flower and Fertility were useless.

I couldn’t say that this was any better, but I saw a massive chessboard before me.

What was the point of his five hundred deaths? He overcame his fear of death, so what was he so scared of that he was acting like a fool?
