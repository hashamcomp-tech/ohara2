Jung Hayan only stayed for ten minutes every time she came, as if she tried her best to finish her business with me very quickly. Cha Hee-ra stayed for about an hour. Even Lee Jihye was a regular. It seemed that organically adjusting the time and coming by seemed to express her personality well.

As I looked at her quietly, I saw her clearing her throat.

Although other people were coming by, most of the visitors for the past three days were those four. Since I didn’t know how much they thought of me up until now, I couldn’t help but shed tears of gratitude.

I wondered if they had done something to me…

‘No. There’s no way they would.’

No matter how weird those people were, they wouldn’t have behaved impulsively around me as a patient.

I shook my head.

After I cleared my mind, I began to gulp the contents of a potion silently. Once again, I felt something like the water of life going down my throat.

‘Did I choose to be an alchemist for this? I’m dying in shame.’
