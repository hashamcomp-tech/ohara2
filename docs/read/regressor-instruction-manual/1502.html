It was quite a dramatic and sad situation. It was particularly sad because he was swallowing everything alone. The fact that I was no longer throwing a complete fit at the guy was unexpectedly effective.

At the beginning of the incident, he insisted that I needed to calm down and regain my composure, but now that I wasn’t showing any emotions and was tightly suppressing everything inside, he became more worried than ever.

Since the situation was something that could never be called ordinary, it was only natural for him to react that way. The next morning, the morning after that, and the morning after that morning, he behaved as if nothing had happened.

‘No matter how many times I keep doing it, it does seem to work.’

Being steadfast would always work. There was a reason steadfastness was included among the traits of past heroines. If pain was hidden along with it, then the possibility of defeat wouldn't exist, even by a single percent. There was a reason the royal road was called the royal road.

I acted bright and hopeful when we were together, but I'd cry until I was completely exhausted whenever I was alone.

I continued to maintain a stance that looked like I could shatter at any moment. I appealed to him that I was barely holding on. Of course, despite all this pain, I didn’t show any of it outwardly.

It was a method that Hedgehog Ki-Young could use because Mikael was no longer an object of resentment to him. He finally understood that this wasn’t Mikael’s fault alone.

Of course, Hedgehog Ki-Young couldn’t honestly forgive him completely, but wasn’t the human heart quite complicated? He could forgive him, and yet he couldn't forgive him at the same time. He resented him a little, and yet he sympathized with his pain.

He felt anger toward him, and yet he pitied him. Even while thinking he would absolutely never let him inside his heart, there was already an individual called "Mikael" somewhere in a corner of his heart.

‘Hedgehog Ki-Young... really is soft-hearted.’

“But... Mr. Mikael... there’s no need to go this far... You don’t have to fight on the front lines yourself... right...? Especially when...” I trailed off.

‘He’s even feeling sorry. In a situation where he should be angry, he’s instead feeling even more sorry.’

A vague sense of guilt made Mikael stand on the battlefield, and it was all for the sake of letting Hedgehog Ki-Young live comfortably.

“You don’t need to worry so much, Mr. Lee Ki-Young. It was an inevitable choice. Since it’s impossible to know exactly how long we should remain here, I simply needed a place where it would be easier to gather information and act independently.

"It will limit my freedom of movement more than blending in with the refugees would, but it'd make things much easier overall. If the opportunity arises, I might even be able to go outside. Of course, that doesn’t mean I would be away for a long time...” Mikael said, reassuring me.

“But...”

“It’s really not because of you, Mr. Lee Ki-Young. Besides... in my case, I will not be directly standing on the battlefield,” he said.

“...”

“I told them that I specialized in barriers and protective spells, and it seemed I'm exactly the kind of personnel Castle Rock needs the most right now. Thanks to that, I have been able to receive some special treatment,” he added.

‘It’s way more than just "some."'

“Then... that’s a relief...” I mumbled.

Of course, he hadn’t revealed all of his original abilities, but receiving treatment like this simply because he was a necessary personnel...

‘That makes no sense. Even you think it’s ridiculous, right? You’re treating me like an idiot, don’t you agree?’

It looked like he hadn't lied to me about how he told them that he specialized in barriers and protective spells, but he had to have shown abilities impressive enough to make people drool even at this point in time.

‘They’re treating him well.’

Three warm and delicious meals a day, a warm, comfortable, and spacious room as well as soldiers who could be summoned at any time. Seriously, someone even appeared to attend to his bath. Of course, in the case of the last one, he politely refused, but it also made me realize that Mikael really was a man from an older time.

In the end, it wasn’t like he was happy with everything, but at this point in Castle Rock, this was already more than he could ask for. The commanders themselves were eating pig slop to conserve supplies, and the fact that only I could eat proper meals was already...

‘This bastard really did put thought into it.’
