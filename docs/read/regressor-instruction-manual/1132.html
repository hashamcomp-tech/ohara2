Chapter 1132: Impact (1)

Like our continent, the Rohen Continent's waters were muddy and deep; there were many vested interests to consider, such as businesses and dungeon expeditions.

Small familias need not worry about many things, but major familias were different.

The small familias only had to worry about obtaining supplies and some specific items, but the major familias had to spend a lot of money and manpower for a single expedition.

With that in mind, they had to be deeply involved in businesses to obtain some funds.

In addition, most familias had created strategic alliances to maintain and protect their businesses.

Sending reinforces to isolated familias and watching the familias leave for an expedition was something we often did on our continent as well. The Blue Guild was in an alliance with the Red Mercenary Guild and the Black Swan Guild.

The only difference between us and them was that they had another reason behind why they had decided to create an alliance. The familias had formed an alliance because their GENIUSES were close.

Even if the familias didn’t get along or had different plans, they were forced to enter an alliance because that was what their GENIUSES had chosen to do.

They were in that kind of situation.

The five major familias were allied with the Flower and Fertility Familia, and they gathered in Scandia, their base.

It wasn’t just the major familias. I didn’t know their names, but I saw representatives of mid-sized familias as well. The leaders of the major familias were on a level slightly lower than the old Eight Seats, and although they appeared to be weaker than Cheon Kwan-Wi and Yu Ran, they looked fancier than them.

‘They spent a lot of money.’

The room was too fancy to call it a simple meeting room.

It was like I was looking at a prayer room in the Vatican. Every face in this room looked proud, and it was probably because they were the top players on the Rohen Continent.

The adjutants who had come here with the leaders of the familias were standing behind their respective leaders while exuding a menacing aura. Thanks to their demeanor, the meeting was pretty tense.

‘They all think they’re the best.’

It was pretty different from the meetings held in Lindel, which were always filled with laughter.
