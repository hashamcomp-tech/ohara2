There was a saying about how the early bird would get the worm, and it applied here.

They could claim hunting grounds, accept quests first, and carry out missions first. It would also increase their chance of encountering a monster. Basically, they could be one step ahead of their competitors, and the expedition members here were all one step ahead of everyone out there.
