Strax leaned back in the chair, running his fingers through the System notifications, absorbing the information as if it were a strong drink, full of power and promises. He focused on one specific ability, which seemed to have evolved into something grander.

"Incubus evolved into... King of Lust Asmodeus..." He murmured softly, staring at the description of the ability.

Strax looked at the screen, his eyes reflecting a mix of fascination and amusement. The ability was not only powerful, but also offered subtle psychological control over others, something he knew he could wield with mastery, especially in the political and social world of vampires.

"So, now I'm the King of Lust, the master of desires..." He smiled, imagining the possibilities. This ability would be a powerful card up his sleeve, especially when dealing with allies, enemies, or... his wives. He wondered just how far he could push this power without losing control.

On an impulse, he closed his eyes and released the aura. Instantly, a soft, almost hypnotic sensation took over the room around him. He could feel the very room respond to his presence, the furniture and even the light seeming to react to his will.

"This will be interesting," Strax thought, feeling a growing satisfaction as the power flowed in his direction. He knew that, with this ability, he could easily manipulate hearts and minds, but he also felt the responsibility that came with this control.

"But I need to be careful." He forced himself to focus. The temptation to abuse this ability was strong, but he knew that if he lost control, the consequences would be disastrous. Even he, with all his strength and power, knew he wasn't immune to his own desires.

He deactivated the ability, letting the aura dissipate, but the heat of his power still burned in his veins, amplifying the feeling of supremacy that crawled through his mind.

Strax slowly rose, the chair creaking under the movement. He made his way to the window, observing the night outside. The Vampire Kingdom was peaceful now, but he knew that wouldn't last. He needed more power, more control, and with the mission ahead of him—Elizabeth Tepes, the Empress of Vampires—he would have to use all his abilities to the fullest.

"I know what to do now..." Strax murmured to himself, a cruel smile forming on his lips. He felt more prepared than ever, and he knew that by taking control of Elizabeth, his position in the vampire kingdom would be solidified.

But something in the back of his mind made him hesitate. He looked at the System message screen again, thinking of the battles ahead, the challenges that awaited him. "Yeah, now everything is really out of control..." But instead of fear, a soft laugh escaped his lips. "Perfect."
