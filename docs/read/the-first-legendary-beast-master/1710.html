Chapter 1710: Break Room

Having heard Dana’s thoughts, Opal came right over after she finished setting everything up for their new guests and having a short nap.

These things never happened on time with her sleep schedule.

The bunnies were startled when the small Fae appeared inside the warehouse compound, which was normally protected by barriers that would prevent such a thing.

You couldn’t just have anyone able to come and go as they pleased within the warehouse. That would defeat the purpose of trying to keep things safe and regulate access for everyone to have a fair shot at getting what they needed.

It would also require the Elders to be on constant guard to prevent theft.

"So, what is the option for the day? A whole city setup for the bunnies in this empty warehouse?" She asked eagerly.

"It’s actually still in use. First, do up something nice for them in the break room. I was thinking of doing a villa stone for them, but you can likely do better. I know that you’ve got the creativity if you aim it in the right direction."

Opal gave her two thumbs up.

"I’ve got this. Employee break room with sleeping quarters, so that they don’t have to set up makeshift bunks in empty shelving units.

Though, some of those look quite cozy, so they might want to keep them anyhow.

Alright, you fix the building’s structure and I will make them the perfect break and relaxation room, so that the New Home’s bunnies are running at peak efficiency."

Dana was more than a little concerned about the phrasing of that.

But Opal was helping, so she let it slide.

The Forest Trickster was planning to live up to the reputation of her species this time, and she was going to make a special space that was just for the bunnies, and the followers of Karl, naturally.

The front room was perfectly normal, exactly what you’d think that it should be.

A break room lounge with a long table, and a kitchen that she had to work very hard to make perfect, as she was a bit clumsy at converting the illusion of food into real food with runes.

On the far side of what appeared to be the whole break room were a bunch of comfortable chairs and sofas.

Then, there were two doors. One that led to a room full of bunk beds for naps, one marked utility closet for storing cleaning materials, and one unmarked one. That one was only visible to the bunny species and anyone aligned with Karl.

That was where the real break room started.

She had the whole warehouse worth of space to work with, as there were no other overlapping separate spaces to get in the way. So, she created an entire spa, complete with salon, massage stations, hot tubs, pools, a steam sauna, and an obstacle course.

She surrounded it with gardens, planted with flowers and vegetables, then created stairs up to a second level, where there would be nearly a hundred luxury apartments.

They all had huge beds, as Opal knew that the bunnies like to sleep in groups for the sense of community and safety. With that in mind, she also made the option for the spiral staircases to be folded into the wall, invisible to those who didn’t know they existed.

When that was done and the hatch was closed, the upper level would be hidden from nearly all intruders who made it past the first door.

The sense of safety would encourage the bunnies to stay in here, which was the goal that Dana had in mind when she asked Opal to come and help. But the hiding stairs weren’t the only safety measure.

The spa wouldn’t be a spa without workers.

The [Gargoyle] and [Epic Golem] spells were perfect for creating staff for the spa.

A few Elven High Priestesses, for healing and recovery, as well as protecting the reception area. And then a whole lot of male bunnies

.

There were virtually none in the Clan that she had noticed, and the ladies deserved some big buff eye candy. It took her some time, but her research efforts had given her a solid foundation to base the male bunnies on, going by the Clan members that the bunnies subtly watched as they passed.

Once everything was made, all that Opal had to do was layer a second illusion with the necessary runes to make the effects real and use [Reality Warp] to turn that illusion into a solid object, bypassing the writing part of the Runecrafting process.

Satisfied that her work was complete, Opal flew back out of the break room to find the bunnies waiting eagerly to see their new space.

"Ladies and... ladies. Welcome to your new break room. Please come in and explore to your heart’s content. If I have missed anything, please let me know. But I think that I’ve got all the essentials. I even added the extra fluffy towels." Opal announced.

The bunnies looked confused as they stepped into the kitchen, and then faintly scandalized as a buff male with bunny ears and a very visible six-pack of abs visible over the black slacks that made up his entire outfit.

"Oh hello! Are you new here? It’s so good to meet you," the closest of the bunnies announced as she rushed forward to greet him.

The big bunny man smiled at her and reached into the oven to take out a tray of cinnamon buns that he had been warming for their arrival.

"Indeed, you could say that I am new here. I am part of the break room enchantment. Miss Opal thought that it was only right that those who serve others so diligently should have somewhere that they could be served and waited on like the fluffy eared goddess they are."

Outside the break room, Dana sighed. That break room had a one hundred percent chance of turning into the scene of an erotic novel, and they hadn’t even made it past the kitchen yet.

Opal had the bunnies addicted with just one man and a tray of sweet pastries.
