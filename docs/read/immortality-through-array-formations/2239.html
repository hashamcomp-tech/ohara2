Chapter 2239: Chapter 1039: Five Elements Source Armor (2)

"Thirty against over a hundred, it’s simply unwinnable."

"Mo Hua’s formation methods seem flashy and intimidating, but somehow... useless?"

"Perhaps it’s a helpless move under urgent circumstances."

"Indeed..."

This discussion reached the ears of the Elders of the Ten Thousand Formations Sect.

The elders said nothing, but had some doubts in their hearts.

On one hand, they felt Mo Hua’s formation was indeed somewhat inappropriate.

On the other hand, they always felt that Mo Hua’s formations seemed to have another purpose, far from being as simple as they appeared.

Though Mo Hua was young, he exuded an unfathomable aura.

What exactly was his intention?

Even though they were Elders of Formation Arts, having studied formations for many years, they were at a loss.

...

In the sword discussion arena, Shi Tiangang, Qin Cangliu, and others also felt uneasy.

Their dread towards Mo Hua was earnest.

Therefore, even though Mo Hua had only set up a Trapping Formation, confining them within the valley,

Even though their numbers were three times that of Taixu Gate, within Mo Hua’s formation, they dared not act recklessly.

Mo Hua, the Formation Leader, was not an ordinary one.

No one could imagine what he could accomplish with formations.

Thus, everyone spread their Divine Sense, scanning the valley repeatedly until they found no other formations besides the Trapping Formation sealing the mountain, then they relaxed.

Mo Hua excelled in formations, Taixu Gate also excelled in formations.

With formations, they were strong.

Without formations, Taixu Gate was like a toothless tiger, appearing strong but weak inside, posing no threat.

Since there was only a Trapping Formation sealing the mountain, with no other formations lying in ambush, one hundred against thirty, Taixu Gate had no chance of winning.

"Can we kill?"

Everyone’s thoughts ran wild.

At this moment, a person stepped out, filled with resentment, staring directly at Mo Hua. It was Ye Zhiyuan of the Daluo Sect.

As a sword control prodigy of the Daluo Sect, he was killed by Mo Hua’s Flying Sword, becoming a complete laughingstock. Now, facing his enemy, his eyes were especially bloodshot.

Ye Zhiyuan, pointing at Mo Hua, said with hatred:

"Mo Hua, since I was defeated last time, I have endured bitter cultivation of the Daluo Sword Control. Today I will wash away my shame in one battle, and take your head with a single sword!"

These words also stirred the hatred in everyone’s hearts.

Everyone had prestigious titles of Qianxue’s Genius; who hadn’t been "humiliated" by Mo Hua’s fireball, water prison, concealment, movement technique, formation...?

Suddenly, emotions boiled over, swords clashed, all directed at Mo Hua.

"Kill Mo Hua."

"Whoever slays Mo Hua can become the Alliance Hierarch!"

Amidst the roaring crowd, a Flying Sword shot up into the air, transforming into a Sword Qi that aimed directly for Mo Hua’s head.

The Daluo Flying Sword Control was Ye Zhiyuan’s doing.

Just as the Flying Sword was about to reach Mo Hua, five burly men emerged, their Giant Swords sweeping in unison, intercepting the Flying Sword with surging Strength.

The Flying Sword failed to kill Mo Hua, but the battle was ignited immediately.

Geniuses from Four Sects and Seven Gates either unsheathed swords, cast spells, or relied on their Vajra bodies, all charged at Taixu Gate.

Over a hundred geniuses attacked simultaneously, Spiritual Power surged like waves, a terrifying momentum.

The Taixu Gate side, solemn and prepared.

Mo Hua, with an indifferent look, gazed at the surging crowd, his eyes revealing a hint of icy sharpness.

Even facing the geniuses of Four Sects and Seven Gates, his goal was never defense.

Defense does not lead to being the first.

He wanted to kill everyone.

"Kill!"

Mo Hua said calmly and indifferently.

This single word "kill," like a valve, opened the Ultimate Formation acquired from the Five Elements Sect:

Five Elements Source Formation, activate!

The disciples of Taixu Gate’s Armor and Taoist Robes simultaneously lit up with five-colored, Five Elements Radiance, dazzling and stabbing.

The formation controlled by Mo Hua infused with Divine Sense, merged with Taixu disciples’ Blood Qi and Spiritual Power.

Using Divine Sense as a guide, amplifying Five Elements.

Taixu disciples felt their Blood Qi and Spiritual Power boiling.

An unbelievably powerful force descended upon them, stimulating their meridians, scorching their Spiritual Power, as if with "divine" aid.

"Kill!"

In an instant, Taixu Gate’s battle intent boiled over, murderous aura like a rainbow.

The Tai’a Five Brothers as the vanguard, Cheng Mo, Ouyang Xuan, Situ Jian and others closing in for the kill.

Yang Qianjun and others formed Taoist Soldiers for support.

Linghu Xiao stabilized the formation, preparing the Chongxu Sword True Art.

According to predetermined tactics, everyone formed a battle line, facing the fierce onslaught of the Four Sects and Seven Gates’ over a hundred geniuses.

The frontal attack between the two sides was like heavenly thunder stirring earthly fire, erupting with astonishing fluctuations.

But this frontal confrontation only lasted a moment.

What’s more astonishing is the scene that followed.

The geniuses of Four Sects and Seven Gates, with numerical advantage, collapsed instantly under the frontal assault with Taixu Gate.

The Taixu disciples, surrounded by the shimmer of Five Elements Radiance, obliterated the attack from the geniuses like decaying wood.

The originally robust and strong Tai’a Five Brothers, bolstered by the power of Five Elements, had their auras soar, like Demon Gods, wielding their Giant Swords like Purgatory’s butchers’ knives. In their swings, geniuses of the Four Sects and Seven Gates, if slightly careless, would meet their end in the Underworld.

Linghu Xiao’s Taoist Robe flickered with crystalline, boiling water-like hues.

This was Mo Hua, after acquiring the Chongxu Sword True Art from Mount Chongxu, who improved and specially tailored the Five Elements Water Moon Source Robe according to the sword technique’s essence.

This Taoist Robe was custom-made, amplifying Linghu Xiao’s Sword Qi to a more terrifying extent.

The might of Chongxu Sword True Art was unleashed to its fullest.

Under Linghu Xiao’s sword, the Sword Qi was ethereal like Water Shadow, like the long moon aloft, sweeping away, unstoppable, directly slaying more than twenty geniuses, instilling fear.
