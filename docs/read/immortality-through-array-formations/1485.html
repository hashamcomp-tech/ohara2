Chapter 1485: Chapter 803 Ghost_3

Mo Hua felt heavy-hearted, and thought indeed, the situation had become somewhat serious.

These signs could already be the harbinger of transforming into a Fierce Ghost.

Hua Qianqian glanced at Mo Hua and couldn’t help but ask:

"Junior Brother Mo, has something happened to Junior Sister Jin?"

"It’s still hard to say..."

Mo Hua sighed.

There were still relatively few clues, and he could only speculate.

As for the truth, without seeing it firsthand, he found it hard to make a judgment.

After hesitating for a long time, Mo Hua finally spoke: "Sister Qianqian, can you... take me into the Hundred Flower Valley?"

Hua Qianqian was stunned, shook her head, and sighed:

"The rules of the Hundred Flower Valley are very strict; all male cultivators are forbidden to enter."

"Is there really no exception?"

"None..." Hua Qianqian replied.

"Alright then."

Mo Hua felt a bit disappointed.

It seemed he could only think of other ways.

For instance, could he call out all the Hundred Flower Valley disciples who were familiar with Senior Sister Ye Jin to meet?

Especially those who had nightmares about Senior Sister Ye Jin...

While Mo Hua was deep in thought, Hua Qianqian secretly observed him, her gaze flickering, and her heartbeat couldn’t help but quicken.

"Actually... there’s a way..."

Hua Qianqian whispered.

"What?" Mo Hua was somewhat surprised.

Hua Qianqian’s glistening eyes roamed over Mo Hua’s face, and ultimately, unable to suppress her inner flutter, she whispered:

"Junior Brother, if you... change into a Hundred Flower Valley Taoist robe, I can take you in."

Mo Hua was taken aback, his expression shocked, and immediately refused:

"No, no!"

Absolutely not!

As a dignified man and the Junior Brother of Taixu Gate, how could he wear the Taoist robe of the Hundred Flower Valley?

Hua Qianqian was surprised herself when she said this.

Then she immediately realized that this might be the only chance in her life, a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity.

If she missed it this time, fulfilling her wish later might be forever impossible.

"Just wear it for a moment to sneak into the valley." Hua Qianqian said.

At least let herself see the appearance of Junior Brother Mo wearing the Hundred Flower Valley Taoist robe.

Mo Hua was resolute in his refusal, firmly stating:

"No means no."

"Alright..."

Seeing Mo Hua’s determined attitude, Hua Qianqian felt regretful but had no choice but to give up, "Then how do we investigate Junior Sister Jin’s matter?"

Mo Hua thought for a while and said: "Can you call out the senior sisters who’ve had nightmares to let me meet them?"

Hua Qianqian looked troubled but still said: "I’ll try, but time is tight, and the sisters all have things to do. They might not all be able to come during this rest period."

"Alright," Mo Hua nodded, "Thank you, Senior Sister."

After that, Hua Qianqian returned to the Hundred Flower Valley.

Mo Hua was still considering the matter of Ye Jin, but as he thought about it, he suddenly realized a problem:

Calling out these senior sisters seemed to be of no use.

A person’s Divine Sense is stored in the Sea of Consciousness.

Ghosts invade, invading the Sea of Consciousness as well.

If he couldn’t achieve ’Primordial Spirit Emergence,’ his Divine Sense couldn’t leave his body. Unless the ghosts sucked him in, he could only watch helplessly with no solution.

As for the Sword Bone...

In this situation, the Sword Bone was also of no use.

Moreover, it was a Sword Demon, best not revealed in front of people.

The trickiest part was that Senior Sister Ye Jin, who died in Hundred Flower Valley, perished in the place where grievances were heaviest.

Not resolving the ’source,’ even if he helped some senior sisters dispel ghostly dreams, it was merely treating the symptoms but not the cause, unable to solve the problem.

Mo Hua felt a headache.

During the rest period, he pondered for an entire day and still had no solution.

Nearing evening, after much internal struggle, Mo Hua finally sighed deeply and resignedly sent Hua Qianqian a message:

"Senior Sister, could wearing the Hundred Flower Valley Taoist robe really allow me to sneak into the Hundred Flower Valley..."
