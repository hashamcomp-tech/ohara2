Chapter 1692: Chapter 869: Sword Tomb

Because the Fushi Soul among the Seven Souls of the Life Soul was parasitized by the Evil Embryo, Mo Hua did not dare to be too arrogant. Therefore, he manifested various Five Elements Spells and numerous Bagua Formation Methods to obliterate these Demon Monsters one by one.

Wave after wave of Demon Monsters died at his hands, instantly refined by him, transformed into pure Telekinesis, and absorbed into his mouth.

Outside his Sea of Consciousness, Big White Dog was also "eating" with glee.

The Demon Monsters that escaped from the gap guarded by Mo Hua naturally ended up in front of Big White Dog, following the blockade formed by the Divine Barrier Array and Divine Lock Array.

Big White Dog widened its eyes, a majestic virtual image manifested in its eyes, intimidating a group of Demon Monsters.

Then, taking advantage of this gap, it immediately pounced forward, crushing them all in one bite before swallowing them into its stomach.

It appeared to be biting randomly at the air, not actually "eating" any flesh and blood.

But Big White Dog ate very joyfully.

This kind of Demon Monster Telekinesis, for it, was a rare supplement.

This "two-legged beast" Mo Hua didn’t trick it; this truly was a "feast."

And Big White Dog, being an Exotic Beast rather than a Cultivator, with no complex human motivations, coupled with the Divine Beast bloodline in its body, was not at all afraid of Evil Thoughts polluting its Taoist Heart. Therefore, the Demon Monsters it swallowed whole were absorbed, leaving not even a trace of Evil Thoughts.

The night deepened, and Yin Qi permeated the room, with Demon Monsters dancing wildly.

Wenren Wei stood outside the house, perceiving the eerie atmosphere inside and felt a chill in his heart, anxious and uneasy.

But he remembered Mo Hua’s instructions, patiently kept his composure, and firmly stood guard outside the door.

The battle inside continued.

Mo Hua stood at the forefront, using Spells and Formations to kill wave after wave, simultaneously recovering the consumed Divine Thought through battle.

Big White Dog was extremely excited, running around wildly, slaughtering the Demon Monsters that Mo Hua missed.

After an unknown amount of time, the torrent of Demon Monsters gradually receded.

In the end, the Divine Authority Tree began to wither, the Black Water Embryo dried up, and fewer Demon Monsters hatched.

These Demon Monsters were numerous, but not individually strong.

The truly strong ones were not a match for Mo Hua.

Those Sheep-Horned Enforcers and elite Demon Monsters like Goat-Headed Generals were already considered "small fry" in front of Mo Hua, who had reached the Realm of Nineteen Patterns and Divine Sense Sublimation.

After all, in some sense, he was already an existence comparable to the Evil Embryo of the Master of the Great Wilderness.

Such minor Demon Monsters could be defeated in one move; if not, then two moves.

When the last Demon Monster General was crushed by Mo Hua with his bare hands, refined by Formation, and absorbed into his stomach, this fierce battle finally came to an end.

Meanwhile, in the Back Hill Forbidden Area, the long-bearded elder still released his Divine Sense, searching quietly.

But this time, he was full of concerns, searching slowly.

Moreover, because he did not dare to fully release his Divine Sense, fearing it might cause a disturbance and attract his junior brother, his perception was very vague.

Coupled with the Divine Mist Array laid by Mo Hua, the long-bearded elder searched for a long time but found nothing.

And just now, he noticed that the Cause and Effect of the raging, rampant Demon Monsters was already gradually dissipating.

It was unclear whether the source of these Demon Monster torrents was cut off.

Or if these Demon Monsters were all exterminated.

But in any case, with the dissipation of the Cause and Effect, the traces would also gradually disappear...

Patriarch Dugu frowned, slightly angry.

He was still not resigned, continuing to release his Divine Sense, scanning back and forth on Taixu Mountain.

But search as he might, he found only vague traces...

...

Inside the Disciple’s Residence.

With the slaying of the last Demon Monster General, the eerie and sinister Qi in the room was swept away.

Amidst the curling smoke, Yu Er was still sleeping soundly, and, dreaming of something, began sucking her thumb.

The atmosphere was tranquil and peaceful.

Mo Hua nodded, taking Big White Dog and leaving the inner room.

Upon seeing Mo Hua, Wenren Wei’s eyes lit up, and he asked, "Young Master Mo..."

Mo Hua nodded and said, "It’s all resolved. Yu Er is sleeping very well."

Wenren Wei clasped his hands gratefully and said, "Thank you, Young Master."

"Uncle Wei, there’s no need to be so polite."

After exchanging a few more pleasantries, Mo Hua was about to return to his room.

However, he thought for a moment and decided to leave Big White Dog behind, saying, "This Big White Dog should stay here to keep watch, just in case anything unanticipated happens tonight."

Wenren Wei thanked Mo Hua again, feeling moved in his heart by the young master’s meticulous and thoughtful actions.

And Big White Dog didn’t refuse.

It had eaten its fill, with a round belly that needed digesting.

At this time, it was too lazy to move, so upon hearing Mo Hua’s words, it lay down and closed its eyes, starting to refine the Demon Monsters it had just swallowed.

Mo Hua then returned to his own room.

The battle, overall, was within his expectations; there were no fluctuations or unexpected incidents.

The Demon Monsters were killed, the Evil Embryo didn’t act up, and Yu Er was unharmed.

He had also consumed many Evil Spirits and now needed to thoroughly refine them, cleanse away the evil filth, and absorb their pure Telekinesis to further advance his Divine Sense.

In the simple and tranquil room,

Mo Hua held his breath, concentrated, and sat down in meditation.

Strands of Telekinesis were being refined.

His Divine Sense gradually strengthened, the Realm of Divine Thought began to rise from Nineteen Patterns.

The Sea of Consciousness expanded, the Divine Thought became robust, and his perception of the world became increasingly clear.

A large amount of Telekinesis was refined, and Mo Hua’s Divine Sense initially grew rapidly.

But soon, he encountered a bottleneck.

It seemed his Divine Sense had reached the limit of a Foundation Building Cultivator, an almost insurmountable Heavenly Chasm blocking his path.
