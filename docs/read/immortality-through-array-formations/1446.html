Chapter 1446: Chapter 790: Escape Quickly_3

He really wanted to know what was hidden in this empty wall mural before, to have such "rich and sweet" divine essence.

Mo Hua inspected the wall mural inch by inch again.

He even used Divine Sense to carefully "scan" the other things in the temple.

Yet, still found nothing.

"Pretty clean work..."

Mo Hua shook his head, seeing that there were no more clues around, he could only sigh inwardly:

"Forget it, it’s getting late, let’s head back first."

"Anyway, I’ll be dealing with the Great Wilderness Evil God in the future, when there are clues, I’ll unravel them slowly."

"Moreover, this time the demon wraiths ate too much, if I don’t return early to meditate and eliminate distractions, there will be issues with my Taoist Heart..."

Mo Hua nodded slightly.

The air still carried the remnants of Mr. Tu’s Demon Thought after his death.

Mo Hua, somewhat disdainful of these demon thoughts, naturally wouldn’t consume them.

He formed a few strands of Sword Qi to eradicate the remaining demon thoughts completely, eliminating all future troubles, and then left the hall.

After leaving, Mo Hua found the Sword Bone hiding in a corner, "minding its own business."

Since the demon wraiths surged forth, the Sword Bone had been "playing dead" here.

Of course, Mo Hua never expected it to help him.

Amidst the demon wraith rebellion in the great prison, it was fortunate enough to survive.

"Let’s go back..." Mo Hua said.

"Yes, yes..." The Sword Bone nodded repeatedly.

Its waist, reduced to just bones, bent even more.

Seeing countless waves of demon wraiths in the great prison, completely subdued and devoured by Mo Hua, the Sword Bone was as silent as a cicada in winter, not daring to raise its head to look at Mo Hua.

Since becoming a Sword Demon, it had never seen a deity.

But thinking a "deity" was no more than this.

Thinking about the powerful Divine Thought of this little ancestor before me, comparable to a deity, the Sword Bone couldn’t help but bend its waist even lower.

All the way back, the little Mo Hua walked tall, while the tall Sword Demon bowed low.

Thus, one man and one demon returned the way they came, leaving the Demon Refining Diagram, back to the Blood Pool of the Sword Bone.

In the Blood Pool, Ling Huxiao and the other two were still unconscious.

Mo Hua checked their condition and found nothing unusual, then said to the Sword Bone:

"Let me out first, then when I tell you to let someone out, do it. If you dare to play tricks..."

The Sword Bone hurriedly said:

"I wouldn’t dare, wouldn’t dare..."

How could it dare to play tricks now?

Is that playing tricks? That’s playing with its own little life.

Mo Hua nodded in satisfaction, then reached out his small hand, saying to the Sword Bone: "I’m going out."

The Sword Bone trembled instinctively at Mo Hua’s small hand, as the broken rib it cracked with these hands throbbed again.

But it managed to control itself.

The Sword Bone reverted to its true form, becoming a bone sword.

This bone sword was the key to entering and exiting the Blood Pool.

Mo Hua grasped the bone sword, focused his mind, and then blood mist spread, the scenery before him changed, when he opened his eyes again, he found himself inside the Valley of Ten Thousand Demons.

Right in front of him was the Demon Refining Diagram, like a wall mural.

And in his hand, he still held a White Bone Broken Sword, which was inserted on the wall mural.

At the moment Mo Hua opened his eyes, the three Golden Core Elders, Xun Ziyou and the others, sensed it.

Xun Ziyou and Xun Zixian both breathed a sigh of relief, their expressions slightly joyful.

Only Elder Shangguan Xuanjian of the Rushing Void Sect had a nervous look, his tone betraying a trace of hope, but mixed with a touch of despair:

"Mo Hua, Xiaoer, he..."

Mo Hua smiled brightly, saying: "No worries, I brought him back."

Seeing this, Shangguan Xuanjian felt, for a moment, that there was no face more handsome than Mo Hua’s in this world.

Even a heavenly immortal’s smile was not as attractive as Mo Hua’s smile right now.

Shangguan Xuanjian deeply sighed in relief, like someone who had been drowning for a long time, finally struggled ashore after great effort, feeling both "exhausted" and "liberated."

"Xiaoer, he’s..."

Shangguan Xuanjian glanced at Ling Huxiao, still unconscious, pale-faced and bloodless, his expression still worried.

Mo Hua said: "Wait a moment."

He pulled out the White Bone Broken Sword and walked to Ling Huxiao, placing the bone sword on Ling Huxiao’s forehead, whispering:

"Sword Bone, release them."

The Sword Bone inside dared not slack off in the least.

After a while, Mo Hua sensed a trace of Divine Soul aura on Ling Huxiao, some color returning to his face, and faint breathing at his mouth and nose.

Feeling this change, Shangguan Xuanjian couldn’t help but feel delighted.

Afterwards, Mo Hua similarly restored the Divine Souls of Ouyang Mu and Song Jian to their bodies.

Thus, all three were considered rescued.

However, they had been "sacrificed" into the diagram, their Divine Souls forced out of their bodies, now with the souls returned, they needed time to adjust and rest before waking up.

Shangguan Xuanjian clasped his fists towards Mo Hua:

"Little brother, there’s no need for thanks for this great favor. If you ever need anything in the future, as long as it doesn’t violate the cultivator’s code, I will give my utmost effort!"

Rescuing Ling Huxiao, this Sword Dao genius, in a sense, also saves the future of the Rushing Void Sect.

Mo Hua waved his hand saying: "Elder Shangguan is too polite, I only did a trivial ’little thing’."

A trivial little thing...

The three elders, Xun Ziyou and the others, heard this and their expressions were a bit subtle.

Concerning the path of Divine Thought, this "trivial little thing," at least for them, these three late-stage Golden Core Elders, wasn’t something they could do...

Xun Ziyou couldn’t help but pat Mo Hua on the shoulder, gritting his teeth saying:

"Alright, a person should know humility, but excessive humility is arrogance."

"Elder Shangguan is distinguished and reputable, he means what he says, if he tells you to seek him out when needed, don’t be too polite."

The subtext being, accept it quickly, lest they regret it.

A promise from a late-stage Golden Core Elder is something others would dream of but have no chance to get.

Mo Hua understood and returned the courtesy immediately, smiling:

"Thank you, Elder Shangguan."

Shangguan Xuanjian nodded.

Since he said it, naturally there’d be no regret.

Besides, compared to Ling Huxiao’s safety, this promise wasn’t really significant.

"Alright," Xun Ziyou said, "now that all three children are saved, the Valley of Ten Thousand Demons breached, let’s return, the sect will handle the subsequent matters, you all..."

But Mo Hua interjected: "Elder Xun, wait."

Xun Ziyou was stunned, "What’s wrong?"

Mo Hua pondered briefly, then slowly said in a deep voice:

"Nearby... there should be another door..."
