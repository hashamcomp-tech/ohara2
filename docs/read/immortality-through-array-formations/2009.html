Chapter 2009: Chapter 966: Mine

The four Golden Core experts, in mutual slaughter, were temporarily crippled.

Young Master Xuan reaped the benefits like a fisherman, obtaining the Dragon Vein.

Then, quite casually and naturally, he handed the Dragon Vein to Mo Hua.

Mo Hua hugged the Dragon Vein and ran away.

For a moment, everyone was stunned.

Young Master Xuan was even more infuriated, his chest tight with rage almost spitting blood, he screamed hoarsely, "You brat! I’ll kill you!"

Mo Hua ran even faster.

"Stop!" Young Master Xuan roared.

Mo Hua’s figure had already begun to leave afterimages.

Young Master Xuan’s eyes nearly burst with anger, turning into a streak of blood-light, like a grotesquely stretched human skin, he dashed at Mo Hua with a very bizarre movement technique.

Mo Hua’s Water Passing Step excels at dodging and shifting within inches, not very good at straight-line sprints.

Moreover, he was only at the Foundation Establishment Late Stage, suffering in terms of cultivation.

Thus, within a few breaths, Young Master Xuan caught up with him.

A foul wind rose abruptly, Young Master Xuan’s gaze fierce, reaching out with a pale hand to grasp Mo Hua. Just as he was about to grasp him, in the next moment, water flashed and Mo Hua dissolved into a mist of water and vanished, reappearing several meters away.

Young Master Xuan cursed in rage.

He could only try to grab again, but just at this moment, a furious wind roared behind him, a pair of large hammers with immense strength swung towards his head.

Young Master Xuan dodged with a duck, catching a glimpse of a sword tip scraping towards his face.

Young Master Xuan’s blood sword swung to deflect the sword, then he retreated a few steps, dissipating the remaining force, only then stabilizing himself.

Looking up again, two burly men were already guarding Mo Hua.

It was Fan Jin and Master Gu.

Their inheritance was insufficient, their own Magical Treasure at a disadvantage, in the battle of Late Golden Core and peak stages, they dared not intervene, thus could only keep their focus on Mo Hua.

The process of Mo Hua "seizing" the Dragon Vein was too dramatic, it stunned both men too, leaving them in a daze for a moment without reacting immediately.

Seeing Young Master Xuan persistently pursuing Mo Hua, they hurriedly intervened, joining forces to repel Young Master Xuan.

Their Tao Cultivation inheritance, though not matching Young Master Xuan, they were seasoned cultivators of the cultivation world, experienced, not easy to handle.

Both now stood like two gates, blocking in front of Mo Hua and Young Master Xuan.

In terms of "hard strength", the Foundation Establishment Realm Mo Hua was naturally no match for the Golden Core Realm Young Master Xuan. But now that there was someone backing him, he had no need to run.

Mo Hua hugged the Dragon Vein, looking at Young Master Xuan, his brows couldn’t hide the smile.

Young Master Xuan was livid with fury, his heart held a tint of disbelief, he gritted his teeth:

"You didn’t fall for my Planting Devil in Taoist Heart?"

Mo Hua shook his head inwardly.

"This Young Master Xuan is really too green, now hasn’t caught on, it’s not about whether I fell for Planting Devil in the Taoist Heart or not, it’s about him falling for mine instead, affecting his perception and judgment."

At this level, learning Planting Devil in Taoist Heart is absurd.

Meeting an Uncle, fearing in an instant, he wouldn’t recognize himself...

But truth is hurtful.

And it easily provokes him, turning him irrational, into a mad dog.

Mo Hua then put on a look of not quite understanding, "I don’t know, I just muddled and woke up, then you gave me the Dragon Vein."

He emphasized the word "gave" heavily.

Not stealing, not robbing, things were given to him.

Looking at Mo Hua’s innocent, shameless appearance, Young Master Xuan was almost grinding his teeth to dust.

"Give me the Dragon Vein."

"No," Mo Hua stuffed the Dragon Vein into his chest, "You gave it to me, thus it’s mine now."

Young Master Xuan was fiercely hateful, but with Master Gu and Fan Jin guarding Mo Hua, he had no way for the moment, he could only suppress his anger:

"This Dragon Vein, keeping it is useless to you, give it to me..."

"Why is it useless?" Mo Hua asked.

Young Master Xuan held his temper, patiently explained:

"This is a dragon, dragon nature is arrogant, not something ordinary people can control."

"The Great Wilderness Dragon Vein, only the Great Wilderness royal descendants can possess it. The inheritance in the Dragon Vein, without royal bloodline, cannot be comprehended."

"You have ordinary cultivator’s bloodline, the Dragon Vein won’t recognize you, and you can’t learn the Great Wilderness inheritance."

Mo Hua was displeased, "You look down on me?"

"Not looking down on you..." Young Master Xuan said, "It’s a matter of bloodline, no matter how strong your ability is, how high your insight, how good your talent, if the bloodline is lacking, it just won’t do."

"It’s all predestined, you cannot change your birth, cannot change your parents, thus cannot change your bloodline."

"Oh..." Mo Hua said.

"Therefore," Young Master Xuan softened his tone, "Give me the Dragon Vein, this great karmic thing, is of no use to you..."

"No," Mo Hua still shook his head, "Even if it’s useless I’ll keep it. At least it’s a Dragon Vein, keeping it as a collectors’ item, a family heirloom is good."

Someday when he returns home, he can even bring the Dragon Vein back, let his parents see.

Let his parents know, their son is the one who grabbed the Dragon Vein.

Besides, how could the Dragon Vein actually be useless...

A person who can’t even properly learn Planting Devil in Taoist Heart still wants to fool me?

Young Master Xuan was almost dead with rage, this lad, looks like a naive pretty boy, but actually is a shameless tough nut, no matter what he said, couldn’t get through to him.

Since that’s the case, he could only try to snatch it.

Young Master Xuan looked around, surveying the situation, then spoke to Elder Xiong:

"Elder Xiong, assist me. Once accomplished, when I cultivate the Mystery Demon Foetus, gain the Dragon Vein, I will pass you the Great Wilderness Dragon Diagram."

These few were in Late Golden Core Stage, all heavily injured.

The Golden Core Peak Shen Shouxing, was even backfired by the Dragon Vein, crippled an arm, and was fully suppressing the rampant dragon energy within.
