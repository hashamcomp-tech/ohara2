Chapter 1486: Chapter 804 Heaven and Man

"Yes! Yes!"

Almost instantly, Hua Qianqian responded with lightning speed.

Mo Hua: "..."

Is she really that eager...

Hua Qianqian saw Mo Hua remain silent and immediately said: "As long as you wear the Taoist Robe of Hundred Flower Valley, I’ll do whatever it takes to get you into the valley!"

"I can handle any accident that comes up!"

"I’ll take full responsibility!"

Hua Qianqian vowed confidently.

Mo Hua: "..."

"How about it? How about it?" Without waiting for Mo Hua’s reply, Hua Qianqian immediately said, "Then it’s settled! This kind of thing should be done sooner rather than later. Come find me first thing tomorrow morning!"

Hua Qianqian decided unilaterally.

Since Junior Brother Mo has agreed, we should act quickly.

We must move fast. Delays can lead to changes.

If I drag it out for a few days and Junior Brother Mo suddenly changes his mind, my wish will be ruined!

Seize the moment, don’t give him a chance to regret!

Mo Hua didn’t know what to say.

He thought about Senior Sister Ye Jin, and considered the saying from "The Taboos of Cultivation" about "dying a miserable death, the resentment heavy, turning into a Fierce Ghost," and sighed helplessly:

"Alright..."

"Great!"

Inside Hundred Flower Valley, due to the incident with Ye Jin, Hua Qianqian hadn’t been able to eat or drink properly for several days, and had been feeling very down. Suddenly, she felt that life was full of hope again.

"Let me see which kind of Taoist Robe best suits the adorable Junior Brother Mo..."

Hua Qianqian was wide awake, got up and rummaged through boxes and cabinets, selecting the perfect Taoist Robe for Mo Hua from an array of colorful garments.

Early the next morning.

Mo Hua made a trip to Hua Qianqian’s cave residence.

The location of the cave residence was told to him by Hua Qianqian.

This cave residence is situated in Hundred Flowers City outside the valley, and it’s an property of the Hua family.

Hua Qianqian is a direct lineage of the Hua family and is well-loved by her family, so this cave residence was assigned to her by the Hua family for her to live, cultivate, rest, practice Alchemy or entertain Taoist Friends during her leisure periods.

At the front of the cave, a plaque with "Shallow Fragrance Courtyard" was hanging.

Judging by the handwriting, it seems Senior Sister Qianqian inscribed it herself.

Mo Hua stood at the entrance of the cave residence, looking at the plaque, and immediately regretted, feeling he shouldn’t have impulsively agreed to wearing the Taoist Robe of Hundred Flower Valley.

"Should I sneak away?"

The affairs of Hundred Flower Valley have nothing to do with me.

The business with the Rouge Boat isn’t actually so urgent either.

Regarding Senior Sister Ye, all I can do is express regret.

Mo Hua thought anxiously.

He slowly retreated a few steps, turned, and was about to sneak away, but before he could, the door of the cave residence suddenly opened.

"Junior Brother Mo, you’re here!"

Hua Qianqian’s eyes sparkled.

She had gotten up early and waited at the door, fearing Mo Hua might think of sneaking away. So as soon as she saw him, she immediately opened the door.

"Hello, Senior Sister, I..." Mo Hua hesitated, "I suddenly remembered, there are some affairs in the Sect, I... still have unfinished Formation drawings, maybe we can talk about entering Hundred Flower Valley next time..."

Hua Qianqian wouldn’t listen to his excuses, she immediately grabbed Mo Hua.

Mo Hua was momentarily careless, didn’t dodge, and was tightly held by Hua Qianqian, unable to break free, and helplessly was forcibly dragged into the cave residence by Hua Qianqian.

This cave residence had been decorated by Hua Qianqian to be cozy and exquisite, with an abundance of colorful flowers everywhere.

The fragrance of pills and flowers mixed together, refreshing.

Hua Qianqian pulled Mo Hua into the inner chamber, sat him down at the table, then promptly closed the door, sealing it with Formation.

She decided that regardless of whether Junior Brother Mo enters Hundred Flower Valley today, he must wear the Taoist Robe she had meticulously prepared.

Mo Hua only saw a flash before his eyes; suddenly, a large, gold-patterned Storage Box was placed before him.

Hua Qianqian opened the box, in which countless robes were neatly arranged.

The styles were either glamorous, elegant, dignified, or cute, all different.

On them, sewn with precious silk threads and exquisite stitches, were gorgeous patterns.

Some had intricate floral designs, some depicted grand landscapes, some bore luxurious phoenix patterns, with radiant light flowing, stunningly beautiful.

"I didn’t sleep all night, spent the whole night picking..." Hua Qianqian eagerly said to Mo Hua, "Junior Brother Mo, have a look at which style you like."

Mo Hua looked at the excited-faced Hua Qianqian and suddenly felt like a "lamb entering a tiger’s den."

He turned his head, glanced at the clothes in the box, meekly said:

"These seem...not like the Hundred Flower Valley’s Taoist Robes..."

Hundred Flower Valley’s Taoist Robe, although embroidered with flowers, was actually a standard robe, it wouldn’t have such a variety of peculiar styles.

Hua Qianqian paused.

She almost forgot, Mo Hua had asked for the Taoist Robe of Hundred Flower Valley.

In her excitement, she had chosen only the clothes she liked.

"Such a small matter shouldn’t impact it...," Hua Qianqian whispered, "As long as it’s women’s Taoist Robe, it should...maybe be able to enter Hundred Flower Valley too."

Mo Hua silently looked at Hua Qianqian.

Hua Qianqian felt a bit disappointed, but suddenly her eyes lit up, "Just wear it for a moment, let me see! Just a look is enough!"

"Senior Sister, we have serious business in Hundred Flower Valley." Mo Hua said reluctantly.

Hua Qianqian sighed, "Alright..."

She had to turn around, and selected a few somewhat plain, not as much to her liking styles of the Hundred Flower Valley’s Taoist Robes, and handed them to Mo Hua.

"Junior Brother, try it on first."

Mo Hua was still somewhat resistant in his heart.

But Hua Qianqian kept urging him on, so he couldn’t refuse, and reluctantly put on a set of gorgeous Taoist Robes from Hundred Flower Valley.

Hua Qianqian took one look, paused for a moment, then couldn’t help but cover her mouth.

Mo Hua was already handsome with defined eyebrows and an elegant demeanor, and now with the dazzling robe, he showed a striking beauty with quiet refinement, exhibiting a clear charm with a gender-neutral allure.
