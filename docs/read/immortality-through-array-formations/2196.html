Chapter 2196: Chapter 1026: Duanmu Qing (Part 3)

Moreover, he hails from a Monster Hunter background, making him extremely familiar with monster beasts.

Thus, the moment he released his Divine Sense, coupled with his instincts, he almost instantly located the bear demon that would decide the outcome of the sword discussion.

The group from Taixu Gate immediately charged towards the bear demon.

With their movement technique fully activated, Mo Hua and the others were near the bear demon in just fifteen minutes.

What followed was the monster hunting segment that everyone enjoyed seeing, and it went incredibly smoothly.

The process was something Mo Hua and the others were very familiar with.

Moreover, their coordination was seamless.

In Refining Demon Mountain, they had hunted countless times.

Set bait, lay traps, create formations, lure monster beasts.

Then, the traps would ensnare the monsters, the formation would explode, leaving the monster beasts heavily injured.

Linghu Xiao gathered Sword Qi, further injuring the monster beast.

The others cooperated, restricting from the side, and adding additional damage.

Everything was orderly and exceptionally skilled; the monster hunting training from Refining Demon Mountain was fully displayed.

This "Taoist Soldier transformation" and "process-oriented" monster hunting left the watching cultivators dumbfounded.

This powerful late Second Grade bear demon collapsed with a roar.

Linghu Xiao sliced open the bear demon’s chest and abdomen with one sword.

Cheng Mo stepped forward, extracted the monster beast’s inner core with his hand, and crushed it directly.

The sword discussion ended.

At this very moment, Ao Zheng, with his strong cultivation, and the disciples from Longding Sect were still on their way, cautiously observing their surroundings, wary of Mo Hua’s formations, and battling wits with "air."

Mo Hua wanted to kill him.

It was certain that Mo Hua would first use formations to kill him.

Ao Zheng’s eyes were sharp, scanning the surroundings suspiciously.

Where would the killing opportunity be?

Could the ground ahead be where Mo Hua had buried the Killing Formation?

Could the forest ahead be where Mo Hua had arranged ambushes?

If they encounter monster beasts, should they engage?

Could this be Mo Hua’s "bait?"

Mo Hua attacked the city, resulting in an explosion, a scene that shockingly flashed in Ao Zheng’s mind from time to time.

As a result, when Ao Zheng looked at the entire forest, he felt somewhat anxious.

He even once suspected that this crazy Mo Hua might blow up the whole mountain to bury them.

But along the way, there was not a trace of anyone.

Gradually, Ao Zheng finally realized something was off.

But it was too late.

Before Ao Zheng and the others could take any action, the bell at Dao Mountain rang.

They were forcibly transported out of the monster hunting grounds.

The sword discussion was over, Longding Sect had lost.

Ao Zheng was dazed for a long time.

Later, some disciples from outside recorded the process of the sword discussion using "Sculpting Shadow Map" and showed it to Ao Zheng.

Ao Zheng felt a rush of anger rising from the bottom of his heart, burning through his body, causing his entire being to hurt with a faint ache.

He wished he could crush Mo Hua, that nasty kid, with his fists, turning him into minced meat.

Before the battle, with a cold face, he said, "This debt must be settled."

He said, "All five of you must die."

But once the battle started, he shamelessly went off to kill the monster beast.

He didn’t even seem to plan to confront Longding Sect.

For the first time in his life, Ao Zheng deeply realized Mo Hua’s "shamelessness."

Moreover, the things Mo Hua said and did were clearly premeditated strategies.

He deceived him with words.

He intimidated him with glances.

He used formations to make him wary.

He even exploited his own imagination to make him hesitate.

"Mo Hua..."

Ao Zheng’s face reddened, wishing he could crush his teeth.

...

Among those inside the sword discussion, only Ao Zheng and the disciples realized Mo Hua’s "sinister and despicable" schemes.

The spectators outside had a simpler view.

In their eyes, Taixu Gate had a clear target, followed orders strictly, seized every moment, and hunted the monster beasts.

Longding Sect paused every three steps, looked left and right hesitantly, missing their chance.

They looked like a group of "big idiots."

The reputation of Longding Sect was harmed, unsurprisingly adding five names to Mo Hua’s "enemy" list.

Five more "Tumo Orders" found new owners.

The power of the Demon Slaying Alliance expanded further within the Qianxue state boundary.

...

Taixu Gate on the other hand, felt much more at ease.

They won another round, and their ranking was slightly stabilized.

The sect as a whole, relieved, also breathed a sigh of relief.

But they hadn’t been at ease for long before they became worried again.

Two days later, the next "card" issued by the Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument arrived.

This time, their luck was extremely bad.

Mo Hua’s opponent was Wanxiao Sect, Duanmu Qing.

Wanxiao Sect was the strongest spell sect among the Four Great Sects.

Duanmu Qing was also one of the Qianxue Four Talents, who could be mentioned in the same breath as Xiao Wuchen, Ao Zhan, and Shen Linsu.

One of the truly top talents among the Four Great Sects.

Moreover, she was the only female disciple among the top talents.

And that’s not all; it was said this Duanmu Qing was breathtakingly beautiful, with a cool demeanor, one of the top beauties in Qianxue state boundary.

Throughout Qianxue state boundary, the male disciples who admired Wanxiao Sect’s Duanmu Qing, much like those who admired Purple Mist Sect’s top beauty Lu Zhenlong, were numerous enough to form a queue from Dao Mountain all the way to Mistwater River...

But unlike Lu Zhenlong, Duanmu Qing’s aptitude was too excellent, appearance too stunning, demeanor too cold, often making people feel ashamed of themselves.

The male disciples who admired Duanmu Qing dared only to keep this admiration hidden deeply in their hearts.

They didn’t dare to express the slightest hint of their feelings before Duanmu Qing’s breathtakingly beautiful face.

Some of the men didn’t even dare to say a word to Duanmu Qing.

These tidbits were specifically learned by Mo Hua from Hao Xuan.

Last time, he accidentally used Fireball Technique to blast the face of Purple Mist Sect’s most beautiful woman, earning resentment for a long time.

Even Uncle Zhang Lan, who came from afar, questioned him about this.

Afterward, Mo Hua learned his lesson the hard way.

Before this sword discussion, he had already learned about this "Duanmu Qing" in advance to avoid repeating past mistakes and causing unnecessary trouble.

Women are very troublesome.

The more beautiful the woman, the bigger the trouble.

But speaking of which, gathering information didn’t seem to be of much help.

Duanmu Qing wasn’t like Lu Zhenlong; she was genuinely one of the "Qianxue Four Talents."

In a direct duel, Mo Hua might not even be able to beat her, let alone use Fireball Technique to blast her face.

Moreover, the strength of Wanxiao Sect’s team was equally absurdly strong.

Mo Hua thought for a long time but still was at a loss.

With the current limited conditions, their chances of winning were very, very small.

Qianxue Four Talents, four top-rarest talents, including Duanmu Qing, were almost insurmountable obstacles...

Mo Hua sighed.

But given the situation, there was no escape; they could only try their best to fight a match.

Fight, perhaps there’s still a chance.

Not fighting means there’s truly no chance.

Afterward, Mo Hua followed usual preparations, orchestrating their tactics.

On the day of the sword discussion, Mo Hua, as usual, led Linghu Xiao and the others to the Inner Mountain of Dao Mountain.

But just at the mountain gate of the Inner Mountain, Mo Hua paused and looked up.

In the distance, another team of cultivators was approaching just in time.

Two men and three women, each displaying extraordinary demeanor.

The lead was a woman with jade-like features, exquisitely crafted, wearing a beautiful and noble Wanxiao Taoist Robe of five colors and Five Elements, with a cool demeanor and eyes unfazed by the world.

This was precisely the opponent for today’s sword discussion.

Also the only female in the Qianxue Four top talents.

"Duanmu Qing..."

Even Mo Hua was slightly taken aback upon first encounter.

Linghu Xiao and several others were also somewhat lost.

Duanmu Qing was indeed extremely beautiful, with a kind of outstanding arrogant and cold demeanor.

Simple beauty wasn’t noteworthy, but when beauty combined with strength, it inevitably drew attention.
