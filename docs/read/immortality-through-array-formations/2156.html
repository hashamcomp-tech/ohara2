Chapter 2156: Chapter 1014: Above Me

The sword discussion system of the Xuan Character Bureau has various formats.

But whether Mo Hua is lucky or not is uncertain, as so far the formats he has participated in are either beheading matches or skirmishes.

Other formats, such as offense and defense, monster hunting, and escorting missions, he has yet to play a single round.

This sword discussion with the Carefree Sect is just a basic skirmish.

You must "kill" all of the opponent’s disciples in order to win.

Of course, at this point, none of the cultivators watching cares about "format"; they only care about one thing:

Who exactly is capable of killing Mo Hua?

Who can allow this despicable and shameless Mo Hua to die once in the sword discussion conference?

Who can wash away the disgrace of Qianxue’s prodigies with Mo Hua’s "blood"?

Just being able to kill Mo Hua is enough.

They don’t care about winning or losing.

Or rather, as long as Mo Hua can be killed, that person is the "winner" in their hearts.

This simple yet intense desire fills the hearts of the cultivators on the Mount of Tao.

The atmosphere of the spectators is as if it were a furnace, igniting passionate blood.

And in this sword discussion, the one placed with the highest hope is the prodigy of the Carefree Sect, Feng Zichen, handsome and elegant, dressed in white, with sword qi like the wind.

Not only do the elders of the Feng family have such expectations.

The majority of the observing cultivators are expecting the same.

They anticipate Feng Zichen slaying Mo Hua with a single sword.

And soon, the sword discussion begins.

The disciples from both the Carefree Sect and Taixu Gate, after some simple scouting and maneuvering, meet each other, erupting instantly like thunderclaps striking the earth, sword light dazzling, entangling in a kill.

There are no pretentious tactics, only pure slaughter.

And of course, Mo Hua bears the brunt of the attack.

Until now in the sword discussion, even an untrained eye can see that Taixu Gate has two people who must not be allowed freedom.

One is Linghu Xiao.

Allowing Linghu Xiao to roam, facing Chongxu Sword’s True Qi onslaught would inevitably lead to a certain loss.

The other is Mo Hua.

Allowing Mo Hua to act will disgust you to death, similarly unwinnable.

Initially, Mo Hua’s importance was beneath Linghu Xiao’s.

But sadly, he has accumulated too many enmities, and his handsome face naturally invites ridicule.

Thus, more people want to kill him than those wanting to kill Linghu Xiao.

Moreover, this sword discussion is a culmination of previous sword discussions amongst the sects, crystalizing the wisdom of various sects’ Elder of Daoist Law, the Carefree Sect having both the means and resolve to "definitely kill" Mo Hua, making their offensive even more aggressive.

With movement as fast as wind, sword qi like shadows, coming and going freely, fierce attacks cornered Taixu Gate.

Mo Hua had no choice but to flee in desperation.

The audience watched with surging emotions.

Elder Feng was also deeply comforted.

"The Carefree Sect’s sect-protecting legacy is the Carefree Wind Shadow Skill, with movement technique as Wind Stepping Technique, while practicing the Carefree Sword Control Skill; hence, the disciples focus on ’fast swords’..."

"Not only is the movement technique superb, but their swords are also swift. In the blink of an eye, the sword qi moves with the wind, delivering a deadly strike, severing throats, taking lives."

"When wind-based movement technique and sword skill combine, the faster the movement, the stronger the swordsmanship."

"Because of these ultimate techniques, the Carefree Sect was able to overshadow several Sword Dao Sects of the Twelve Streams, becoming one of the Eight Great Gates."

Elder Feng eloquently conveyed to the Great Elder Zhang, and then happily added:

"And our family’s prodigy, Feng Zichen, is the elite of this generation in the Carefree Sect. His skills and swordsmanship originate from the Carefree Sect, but his movement technique, based on the Carefree Sect’s Wind Stepping Technique, also integrates our family’s unique Trigram Wind Wandering Step."

"Carefree Wind Stepping Technique, combined with Trigram Wind Wandering Step, these two ultimate technique movement skills are merged into one."

"In terms of swordsmanship, Zichen may not be the strongest. But when it comes to movement skills, throughout the entire sword discussion conference, few can compare to him."

"And the swordsmanship of the Carefree Sect heavily relies on movement skills. The stronger the movement, the stronger the swordsmanship; hence, when Zichen executes the Wind Sword Skill, it is unstoppable..."

Elder Feng was exceedingly proud.

The Great Elder Zhang looked towards the image on the curtain and indeed saw that the five disciples of the Carefree Sect each had swift movements, their sword moves like gusts of wind.

The one leading them had even faster movement skills.

Underneath fluttering white robes, the unity of person and sword moved about like the wind, elegant yet fierce.

Especially his movement technique, embodying the swiftness of the Wind Stepping Technique and the agility of the Wind Wandering Step, was exceptionally marvelous.

"Good!"

The Great Elder Zhang nodded in praise but couldn’t help but be envious.

Their Zhang family also excels in movement techniques.

The family’s Falling Flower Steps and Water Passing Step were remarkable, but compared to the Carefree Step and Trigram Wind Wandering Step before him, they seemed inferior by a margin.

Moreover, none of the disciples among the Zhang family could cultivate these movement techniques to perfection.

Let alone compete with the Feng family’s prodigy on the sword discussion stage.

Comparisons are aggravating.

The Great Elder Zhang did not voice it, nor did he show it on his face, but he couldn’t help feeling somewhat troubled inside.

And on the stage, Feng Zichen led the Carefree Sect disciples, unharnessing great power.

Sword qi swept across like a raging storm.

In just a few dozen rounds, Taixu Gate’s formation was severed by the Carefree Sect’s swift swords.

Once the formation was disrupted, Mo Hua lost his protections.

The members of the Carefree Sect immediately sent sword qi like the wind, all attacking Mo Hua.

Mo Hua truly couldn’t withstand it and could only trigger concealment, vanishing before everyone.

Feng Zichen was prepared in advance, immediately taking out a Five Elements Breaking Concealment Spirit Mirror, shining it above, revealing Mo Hua’s just-disappeared figure, crystal-clear and obvious.

An uproar arose among the crowd, and people praised:

"It’s broken!"

"Wonderful! Truly marvelous!"

"That kid’s Concealment Technique is finally broken! I wonder where he’ll hide now?"
