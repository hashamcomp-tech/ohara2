Chapter 1642: Chapter 853: Bloody Battle (Part 4)

Of course, Mo Hua and the others were fine, as were Master Xiao and his group.

However, Xie Liu was already gone.

With cold eyes, Xia Canonical Official looked at Master Xiao, thinking about the bizarre Water Prison Skill from earlier, his expression heavy and his tone indifferent:

"I did not know that Master Xiao was hiding such powerful Taoist Skill."

Master Xiao also smiled, "It’s just a small trick, my apologies for displaying it."

Gu Changhuai sneered, "Where exactly did you acquire this Taoist Skill? Neither the Water Prison Sect nor the Gui Water Sect would possibly have taught you such a skill, right?"

Master Xiao replied, "The inheritance of Taoist Skills is a secret, so it’s not convenient to disclose it to Supervisor Gu. Perhaps we should consider what to do next?"

Gu Changhuai said coldly, "What do you intend?"

Master Xiao said, "Now, the Fish Bone Token is in your hands, and the Water Prison Forbidden Box is in mine. We’ve both achieved our goals, so we can naturally call a temporary truce. The only problem is this White Bone Hall in front of us..."

"That Mr. Witch acts strangely, and who knows how much danger is hidden in this White Bone Hall. Fighting among ourselves at this time would be unwise, and it might lead to mutual destruction, giving others an opportunity to take advantage..."

Gu Changhuai raised an eyebrow, "Do you want to form an alliance?"

Master Xiao shamelessly said, "Exactly."

"There is no other way," Master Xiao continued.

Gu Changhuai glanced at Xia Canonical Official, who frowned and then nodded, "Agreed."

She did not trust Master Xiao.

But given the current situation, she had no choice but to temporarily agree to team up with Master Xiao.

Master Xiao was slightly pleased and cupped his hands, "Xia Canonical Official truly understands the greater good."

"Let’s go, time is tight, we should first see if there’s a chance to get out..." Xia Canonical Official said.

"Alright." Master Xiao nodded.

Then everyone gathered together again and continued to search for an escape route.

But there was some distance between both sides, as they were wary of each other.

After walking a while, everything seemed fine.

Mo Hua also relaxed a little, following behind Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official, observing the surrounding bone pillars, inferring the formation traces, and was somewhat distracted.

A moment later, as Mo Hua was pondering the formation, he suddenly sensed a familiar killing intent coming towards him.

He found it hard to believe for a moment.

Again?

This scoundrel, intends to make a move on him again?!

This is truly shameless...

Mo Hua was speechless for a moment, turned his head slightly, and indeed saw that Master Xiao had found an opportunity, executed Water Shadow Step, and was already within three yards of him.

Mo Hua sighed and could only continue to dodge.

And at the moment Master Xiao made his move, Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official also noticed, their expressions turning cold as they protected Mo Hua, and jointly attacked Master Xiao.

But almost at the same time, the Water Yama standing by the side, with eyes turning red, emitted evil qi and quietly executed the Blood Pupil Skill.

No good!

Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official, being battle-hardened, instinctively reacted to this Pupil Skill.

They quickly turned their eyes aside, narrowly avoiding Water Yama’s Pupil Skill.

But when they turned their eyes back, they still saw a pair of bloody eyes.

It was Master Xiao!

Master Xiao’s eyes were blood-red, with blood seas churning and evil qi surging between his eyes, and wraiths howling.

Water Yama was an illusion, Master Xiao was real.

Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official dodged one danger but not another, and ultimately fell into the trap.

And with the cooperation of Water Yama, Master Xiao aimed for a quick victory, using all his power to execute the Blood Pupil Skill almost without hesitation.

His Blood Pupil Skill far surpassed Water Yama’s.

Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official only felt the evil qi invade their minds, causing excruciating pain in their Sea of Consciousness, their faces turning pale, and their expressions filled with pain, standing in a daze.

"Uncle Gu!"

Mo Hua was startled, just about to run away, but as he looked up he saw Master Xiao’s bloody eyes.

Mo Hua was dumbstruck for a moment, but his gaze remained clear.

Seeing Mo Hua’s reaction, Master Xiao also hesitated momentarily.

Then in a flash, Mo Hua immediately reacted, letting out a miserable scream, and fell to the ground, clutching his eyes, "in pain" and rolling around.

"Ah, my eyes..."

Mo Hua secretly rubbed his eyes to make them red.
