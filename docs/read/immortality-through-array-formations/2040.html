Chapter 2040: Chapter 976: Auspicious Omen

"It’s hard to say, the heavenly secret is vast, can’t calculate it quickly, but it’s certainly auspicious." Elder Master Xun asserted.

"What you said is no different from saying nothing?" Linghu Ancestor remarked.

Elder Master Xun lamented: "The heavenly secret is just like this, elusive and ambiguous, you need to comprehend it yourself. Once you understand it, you get it; if not, no matter how much is said, it won’t be clear to you."

"You this..."

Tai’a Ancestor found no words to say.

Elder Master Xun’s words seemed vague, as if spoken yet nothing was said.

It appeared nothing was said, yet what he said had some validity.

Because the heavenly mechanism causality is indeed such profound and mysterious, hard to grasp, being able to derive ’auspicious’ is already good.

"Can there be auspiciousness out of nowhere?"

"Not out of nowhere..." Elder Master Xun’s gaze settled.

Elder Ouyang paused, "How do you explain?"

Elder Master Xun said deeply: "All this is due to causality, in the unseen, there is a causality driving our Three Sects Unification, letting our sects, separated for millennia, reunite as one lineage."

"This is no coincidence."

"And now, as our three sects unite, Taixu Mountain reveals an anomaly, night as day, golden trees bursting forth, doesn’t this precisely indicate our three sects unification is the right choice?"

"Someday, we might truly revive the glory of the Ancient Sect, inherit the Qian Path’s will, and become the number one Great Sect in the Qian Learning State Boundary!"

Elder Master Xun’s tone was enthusiastic, painting a vision for them.

The three were indeed shaken for a moment.

"So this is truly... the Taixu Gate’s revival among the one sect and three veins... the omen of auspiciousness?"

Elder Master Xun nodded, "Precisely."

Elder Ouyang fell silent.

Having spoken to this extent, Elder Master Xun wasn’t easy to question further.

Mainly because that golden brilliant anomaly with law circulation, saying it’s auspicious isn’t too much.

Elder Master Xun seemed somewhat pale, as if the calculations earlier consumed significant Divine Sense, now somewhat exhausted, couldn’t help but sigh.

"Age has caught up, spirit wanes, the calculation took a toll..."

Everyone knew Elder Master Xun was issuing a ’guest expulsion order’.

They ought not to dig further impolitely.

Because Elder Master Xun was truly old, and deducing the heavenly secret indeed consumes Divine Thought significantly.

"Brother Xun, thank you..."

"We won’t disturb you further, take care."

The three, Elder Ouyang among them, exchanged some pleasantries and opened void rifts to leave one after another.

Murong Ancestor walked last, glanced at Elder Master Xun, hesitated a moment, and still asked: "Brother, is everything really alright?"

Elder Master Xun’s gaze was cautious, nodded slightly, "Rest assured."

"Alright."

Murong Ancestor nodded, then left using the Void Escape Skill.

As the void rift faded, peace returned to the residence instantly.

Elder Master Xun also exhaled, after a silent moment, couldn’t help but murmur internally:

"Foundation Establishment Realm, Divine Sense Core Formation, and unknown whether these cosmic Divine Thought anomalies are caused by this child..."

Elder Master Xun shook his head.

"I initially thought that kid named Zhuang was already an incredible anomaly, yet unexpectedly, his disciple, seemingly unremarkable, but quietly became so ’frightening’..."

"This sect lineage indeed only produces ’evil monsters’..."

"Moreover, by generational rank, Mo Hua this child is the treasure at the bottom, the last ’evil monster’..."

...

Tai’a Sect.

After leaving, Elder Ouyang and Linghu Ancestor gathered again.

"Auspiciousness... Did he really deduce this?"

"Why do I feel he made it up?"

"Uncertain, the heavenly mechanism causality, we haven’t studied it, so it’s up to him to say whatever."

"Then what’s to be done?"

Linghu Ancestor of the Rushing Void Sect sighed lightly: "Let it be."

Elder Ouyang frowned, "Not investigating further?"

Linghu Ancestor shook his head, "Taia Refining Spirit Sword, Rushing Void Proliferating Sword Qi, Tai Xu Sword Intent, since ancient times, the good and bad omens on heavenly secrets have been warned and observed by the Tai Xu lineage, and severed."

"Neither you nor I specialize in the Divine Thought’s Dao."

"He says it’s auspicious, let it be then."

"Besides, Three Sects Unification prospers together, suffers together, if there’s benefit we don’t lose out, if there’s trouble, they can’t escape either."

Elder Ouyang contemplated briefly, murmured: "It can only be like this..."

The two ancestors fell silent, each occupied with thoughts, after a moment Elder Ouyang couldn’t help but speak gravely:

"To be honest with you Brother Linghu, lately I have felt rather uneasy, sensing that soon... something significant might happen."

"The current situation is already tumultuous, if there’s another major event, it surely will stir up a storm, that time the sect’s ability to protect itself might be in question..."

Linghu Ancestor also frowned deeply.

With cultivation at their realm, occasionally there are premonitions of ’knowing the heavenly mandate’.

He also frequently had similar forebodings.

Thus seeing anomalies in the sky stirred strong unease.

But the exact meaning of this anomaly was indeed perplexing, beyond their understanding, and Elder Master Xun was reticent.

Linghu Ancestor pondered momentarily, sighed helplessly: "Take it one step at a time... let’s hope this anomaly is truly auspicious..."

Elder Ouyang nodded, also sighed: "Let’s hope so."

Only after pondering for a long time, the two ancestors still couldn’t figure out what ’auspicious’ truly lay within Tai Xu Gate...
