Chapter 2390: Chapter 1088: Earth-Shattering Massacre (Part 2)

As long as the foundation of the Formation is there, or rather, as long as these disciples of the Demon Sect are there, there will always be a way.

Mr. Tu’s expression was cold, and he was pondering in his mind how to reconstruct the Large Formation and how to reverse the unfavorable situation before him.

But just as he was thinking for a few moments, Mr. Tu’s face changed.

"It’s still not right..."

Previously, he had sensed a chilling cold intent.

Although the Formation Pivot was blown up, and the loss was severe, it still had not reached the point of making him feel this cold all over.

This meant that all of this... wasn’t over yet?

Mr. Tu’s pupils slightly widened.

That eerie smiling face appeared again in his mind, and besides innocence and weirdness, there was an additional trace of "cruelty."

It was as if a Heavenly Demon from another realm was smiling at him.

Just then, another change occurred.

Beneath the ruins, a trace of reversed power revived, like a "relay," initiating a second round of disintegration.

And the target of this round of disintegration was...

Mr. Tu was horrified: "Formation eye?!"

First collapse the Formation Pivot, then collapse the Formation eye?

And the Formation eye of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation was precisely that heart of the ancient demon of the Great Wilderness!

Mr. Tu’s face immediately turned pale, and then his body deformed, turning into a mass of flesh, diving into the bottom of the ruins, trying to protect the Formation eye.

But one step slow, step by step slow, everything was already too late.

The power of the Reversed Spirit had already spread widely.

The Formation eye of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation started to reverse, and cracks covered the heart of the ancient demon of the Great Wilderness.

Then the heart shattered, and the overwhelming blood-colored evil power contained within it surged out like a vast wave, rushing along the predetermined formation paths, spreading outward.

Within the Large Formation, many demon cultivators, and even many flesh and demon monsters, were inundated and engulfed by these tidal-like evil powers.

Evil power is the root of the cultivation and dao of the demon cultivators.

The overwhelming evil power was something they originally desired greatly.

But the problem was, the evil power contained within the entire Blood Sacrifice Formation eye, the entire heart of the ancient demon of the Great Wilderness, was simply too overwhelming, so overwhelming that it exceeded the carrying capacity of the demon cultivators.

Many Foundation Establishment demon cultivators began to overflow with evil power, their bodies exploding to death.

Even some flesh and demon monsters couldn’t bear it, their bodies started distorting, then limbs deforming, exploding to death...

Using the ancient demon Formation eye as the core, a thick evil power instantly exploded, spreading to the sky.

The evil power of the heart combined with the self-exploding flesh, forming a crimson blood color, smearing over the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation.

From a distance, it looked like a volcanic eruption.

It’s just that this "fire" was wrought by flesh, cruel yet beautiful.

On the Sword Observation Tower, a group of Heaven Void Ancestors were all voiceless.

Within the Blood Sacrifice Formation, Mr. Tu and others immersed in evil power were equally terrified beyond words.

Then, before they could think further, what followed was the third round of disintegration.

With the Formation eye collapsed, evil power and blood erupted like a "volcano," leaving only a trace of blood-red at the sky’s edge.

But in the next moment, amidst the blood-red, a layer of crystal-clear blue emerged.

This is the color of Spiritual Power.

The Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation was constructed by massive slaughter, obtaining a large amount of cultivator skin, flesh, bones, and blood as formation material.

But due to various incidents at the Sword Discussion Conference because of Mo Hua, Mr. Tu had to hastily activate the Blood Sacrifice Formation.

Therefore, due to insufficient preparation in the early stage, later suppressed by the various forces of Qianxue, the slaughter of demon cultivators was curbed, and the formation materials became scarce, and many Formation Masters perished.

Under such manifold difficulties, Mr. Tu had no choice but to use Mo Hua as the Formation Master, employing the Righteous Dao’s Formation System to assist in building the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation.

Hence, this Blood Sacrifice Formation is a formation system where righteousness and evil are aligned.

Within the Large Formation, spiritual power and evil power run parallel.

The evil power part is managed by Mr. Tu.

And the spiritual power part is completely controlled by Mo Hua.

Before this, spiritual power and evil power coexisted, without interfering with each other.

But Mo Hua broke this balance, first collapsing the Formation Pivot, then the Formation eye, and then intertwining the evil power and spiritual power together.

In the Cultivation World, all forces of heaven and earth, generate and counteract, integrate and repel, opposites but unified.

Both evil power and spiritual power are forms of cultivation power of heaven and earth.

Once blue spiritual power and red evil power came into contact, they tangled and intertwined together. The forces of righteousness and evil strangled each other, repelled each other, yet transformed and infiltrated each other...

The first two disintegrations were merely preludes.

And this, was what Mo Hua aimed to "disintegrate" in the end.

The Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, with the momentum of united strength, covered the vicinity of Qianxue, encompassing a total of nine small state boundaries and medium state boundaries, with an overall scale that was too large.

The first collapse of the Formation Pivot, core collapsed, but only affected a very small area.

The second collapse of the Formation eye, evil power spread, yet occupied only a fifth of the Large Formation.

But the third, and Mo Hua’s final prepared disintegration, leveraging the reverse weaving of spiritual power and evil power, spread like a spring breeze igniting a wildfire, almost in the blink of an eye, reaching the entirety of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation.

Amidst the ruins, after the previous two rounds of reverses, sufficient "disintegration" power had accumulated, like a mass of fire seeds.

At this moment, the vast intertwined spiritual power and evil power were like a giant "dynamite" at a critical point.

Fire seeds encountering dynamite, ignited almost instantaneously.

The power of disintegration ignited the ocean-like spiritual power and evil power.

At that moment, the entire world darkened.

Daylight was swallowed, darkness engulfing heaven and earth.

The overarching blood color, the crystal blue, were all shrouded in the pitch-black shade of disintegration.

Whether it was the blood-colored evil power or the blue spiritual power, during their interweaving and entangling, they all took on a terrifying black edge.
