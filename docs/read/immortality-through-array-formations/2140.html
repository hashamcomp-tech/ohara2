Chapter 2140: Chapter 1008: Myriad Spells (Part 3)

This is the Sword Discussion.

With so many eyes watching, many moves can only rely on surprise, used once or twice to gain an advantage.

Any revealed trump card, within one or two rounds, will certainly be countered.

Both the Fireball Technique and Water Prison Technique were restricted.

Mo Hua couldn’t hold back any longer.

He no longer suppressed himself and began to use the vast array of low-level spells he knew.

These spells were diverse and came from various sources.

Most of them were acquired while capturing Sin Cultivators during bounty missions.

When Mo Hua first entered the Qian Learning State Boundary and joined the Taixu Gate, he followed his senior brothers and sisters on bounty missions, capturing many Sin Cultivators.

A large portion of these Sin Cultivators were rebellious disciples from different sects within the Qian Learning State Boundary.

They carried with them a lot of sect inheritances.

Especially various weird, eclectic, and low-level spell secret manuals, which were almost all confiscated by Mo Hua through "intense interrogation."

Apart from that, there was also the Taoist Repository of Taixu Gate.

And other small spell manuals acquired through various accidental means.

Altogether, they numbered no less than hundreds of types.

These spells were of inferior grade, as long as the Spiritual Root matched, they were generally not difficult to learn.

Mo Hua’s Divine Sense was strong, his comprehension high, and he could usually use the spells proficiently after just a few glances at the spell diagrams.

This was easily done.

The more he learned and used, the deeper his understanding of the spells became, and his proficiency in spells naturally improved.

Unfortunately, Mo Hua had not used these low-level spells much before.

Because since the early stage of Foundation Establishment, his opponents were cultivators of the level of the "Fire Buddha" and "Water Yama."

Later in his involvement, like "Xiao Zhenhai," "elders of the Demon Sect," "leaders of the Demon Sect," "Young Master Xuan," they were all Golden Core cultivators.

No matter how well he used low-grade second-class spells, they were of no use.

But now it’s different.

This is in the Sword Discussion Conference, where his opponents are sect disciples at realms similar to his. This "path of all-encompassing spells" is starting to show its power.

Hence, the five disciples of Qian Taoist Sect became the first batch of Sword Discussion disciples to receive Mo Hua’s "spell baptism."

Mo Hua focused his Qi and spirit, then his hands formed an invisible grip, spiritual power flowed with Divine Sense, and spells streamed from his fingertips, attacking the disciples of Qian Taoist Sect.

In an instant, lights shone continuously, and spells kept coming.

Golden Blade Technique, Wooden Binding Skill, Water Jet Skill, Fire Brightness Skill, Earth Prison Skill, Quicksand Skill, Earth Burying Skill, Wood Poison Art, Golden Sound Skill...

Five Elements rotating, emerging in an endless stream.

The diverse array of spells was available at his fingertips.

Though their power was somewhat lacking, Mo Hua’s Divine Sense converted quickly, and he knew so many spells; they were fast, accurate, and unpredictable.

It was dazzling and overwhelming.

This was the effect of merging the concepts taught by Old Kui, "all spells in the world, only speed is unbeatable," with Elder Yi’s saying, "Five Elements rotation, all encompassing spells."

Mo Hua stood still, fingers moving rapidly, casting Five Elements spells one after another, like a human "spell machine gun," bombarding the Qian Taoist Sect disciples so they were at a loss, overwhelmed, and extremely embarrassed.

The watching cultivators, including the elders from Qian Taoist Sect, fell silent.

The previously vocal audience also immediately "shut up."

Whereas Wenren Wan, Yu Er, Murong Caiyun, Hua Qianqian, and the disciples of Taixu Gate, including the elders of Taixu Gate, watched with surging emotions.

Especially Elder Yi, whose expression was extremely excited.

He was an Elder of Daoist Law, devoted his life to the study of spells.

This scene of "all spells in the world" had been lingering in his mind.

Mo Hua was the first disciple to present this "Five Elements rotation" scene in reality.

Moreover, this was in the Sword Discussion Conference.

Against the disciples of Qian Taoist Sect.

Using basic Five Elements spells to suppress the genius disciples of the Four Great Sects so that they couldn’t lift their heads, was enough to prove the powerful capacity of this "spell style" to transform decay into magic.

But after the excitement, Elder Yi also felt regret:

"This child Mo Hua, after all, has too poor a Spiritual Root and too low spiritual power..."

Even if they were only low-level Five Elements spells, the rapid rotation still consumed a lot.

Mo Hua couldn’t possibly maintain this casting state for a long time.

His Qi Sea, his spiritual power, couldn’t support such "extravagance."

Otherwise, relying on himself alone, with his swift, vast, and massive Five Elements harmony, mutually reinforcing and counteracting, a "spell chain" with no weaknesses, he could overwhelm the five disciples of Qian Taoist Sect to death.

"What a pity..."

Elder Yi sighed once more.

However, nothing is perfect in everything.

Even though Mo Hua’s "Five Elements rotation, all encompassing spells" had foundational flaws, it was enough to handle the current situation.

Furthermore, this is the Sword Discussion, and Mo Hua is not alone.

With the help of Five Elements spells, he temporarily suppressed the disciples of Qian Taoist Sect, allowing Linghu Xiao to catch his breath and have room to counterattack.

Additionally, there were Ouyang Xuan, Cheng Mo, and Situ Jian cooperating.

The disciples of Qian Taoist Sect quickly fell into a disadvantage.

With Mo Hua’s Fire Brightness Skill scorching the eyes of one Qian Taoist Sect disciple, then Quicksand Skill trapping their feet, a Water Jet Skill dissolving their Fire Talisman, and a Golden Blade Technique cutting a small opening...

The tide of battle completely shifted.

Although these spells were low-level, their effects were very subtle.

However, in a battle of masters, life and death are on the line.

These subtle changes, accumulated, were already enough to turn the tide of battle.

Situ’s Li Fire Sword stabbed through the small opening Mo Hua created, into the chest of the Qian Taoist Sect disciple.

Cheng Mo swung an axe overhead, Ouyang Xuan followed with a sword, and Linghu Xiao swept Sword Qi, resulting in the Qian Taoist Sect disciple’s Dao Discussion Jade shattering, and their life ending instantly.

With one dead, four disciples remained from Qian Taoist Sect, and the situation took a drastic turn for the worse.

And with one less from Qian Taoist Sect, there was no longer anything to hold back Linghu Xiao’s Sword Qi, leaving no one to stop him.

The battle continued for over a hundred more rounds, with the remaining four disciples of Qian Taoist Sect being "slain" one by one by the cooperation of the five from Taixu Gate.

This round, Taixu Gate won.

In the vast spectator seats, there was silence.

The elders of Qian Taoist Sect looked grim.

Even the elders from other sects had somber expressions.

They finally realized Mo Hua, this Formation Leader at the Sword Discussion Conference, was "not positioned" as before.

They all underestimated him.

This Qianxue Formation Leader’s identity at the Sword Discussion Conference was not that of the previous "Formation Master," but of an "underdog" with remarkable spell proficiency and formidable potential.

He was a practitioner unable to cultivate Superior Daoist Magic but was proficient in a variety of low-level spells, embodying the prototype of "all-encompassing spells" as a "Spell Master."
