Chapter 2382: Chapter 1086: Miracle (Part 2)

Just like that, Mo Hua was surrounded by countless Demon Monsters, constantly rising, until he reached the edge of the abyss cliff.

Mo Hua, holding Yu Er, left the Demon Monster steps, stepped onto the cliff, glanced back, softly gave the final command, and then left without looking back.

Behind him, countless Demon Monsters gnawed at the earth and stones, causing the cliff to collapse inch by inch.

The entrance of Yanluo Mountain was completely sealed by the collapsed Mountain Stone...

Leaving Yanluo Mountain, the world opened up.

At this point, Mo Hua finally rescued Yu Er, who was a Divine Fetus, from the nightmare of the Evil God, from the deepest part of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, and from the hands of four Feathered Immortal cultivators, hundreds of Demon Golden Core cultivators, and the myriad Sect Demon Cultivators, and ultimately escaped alive.

Having been confined for so many days, he finally saw the light of day again.

Mo Hua looked up at the sky.

However, at this moment, the sky was still red, Evil Qi like clouds, blood sea covering the sky, without a trace of sunlight.

Mo Hua pinched his fingers, calculated the time, and silently thought to himself:

"Soon..."

After Yu Er fell into the Abyss, she temporarily lost consciousness.

Mo Hua hesitated no more and carried the temporarily unconscious Yu Er on his back after falling into the Abyss, and then step by step, he left Yanluo Mountain, left the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, heading towards the Qian Learning State Boundary.

...

Yanluo Mountain, within the Abyss.

Demon Monsters rioted, Demonic Qi surged.

After Mo Hua left, it was unclear how much time had passed before the riots slightly calmed.

Suddenly, a dark golden light emerged, a Golden Corpse appeared, slaughtering all surrounding Demon Monsters.

Afterwards came the Evil Qi of the Demon sword, as well as the astonishing Blood Qi.

The Feather Transformation of Yin Corpse Valley, the elder from Demon Sword Sect, Shangguan Wang, and Mr. Tu, these four Feather Transformation entities, climbed out from the Abyss one after another.

Being submerged by the tide of Demon Monsters, having their flesh gnawed by blood and flesh mouths, could not truly take the lives of these four.

But the four were covered in scars, flesh mottled, clearly they suffered significant injuries.

Feather Transformation is the Realm of True Man, not so easily slain.

In ordinary state boundaries, Feather Transformation is already a towering figure.

In some mid-level Great Families and Sects, Feather Transformation could be the patriarch.

In some small state boundaries, Feather Transformation Realm cultivators are enough to establish a sect.

But now, these four Feather Transformation cultivators were in a sorry state, anger boiling in their hearts.

They wanted Yu Er.

To wield the Divine Fetus to command the Demon Path was something they all desired.

But as the clam and the snipe fight, the fisher benefits, ultimately capsizing in the ditch, unexpectedly intercepted by a mysterious and unpredictable Foundation Establishment Realm youngster.

However, aside from anger, everyone felt a tinge of chill in their hearts.

Especially, the final scene, when Mo Hua held the Evil Embryo and commanded the Demon Monsters, left a deep impression on all present Feather Transformation cultivators.

The elder from Demon Sword Sect, with sharp eyes, looked at Mr. Tu, questioning:

"Mr. Tu, are you not telling the truth about this kid’s background?"

"Usually, a cultivator of this age, no matter how talented, no matter how great the gift, absolutely cannot possess such means."

"To hold deities hostage, to see through human hearts, even to command thousands of Demon Monsters..."

"Such deeds are nothing short of ’miracles’."

"This kid... Who is he really?"

Mr. Tu remained silent for a moment, slowly saying: "He... is not human."

The elder from Demon Sword Sect’s eyes twitched, "What do you mean?"

Mr. Tu no longer concealed, indifferently said:

"This boy called ’Mo Hua’, he is also a deity, no, to be precise, there is a terrifying ’Fierce God’ residing within him, he is a puppet flesh fetus of the Fierce God."

The face of the elder from Demon Sword Sect changed.

The Feather Transformation of Yin Corpse Valley revealed a gleam in his muddled eyes.

Shangguan Wang was similarly shocked.

He was an elder of the Shangguan Family, had known about Mo Hua early on, initially he thought Mo Hua was a lucky Loose Cultivator, an eccentric genius specializing in Formation, yet now Mo Hua turned out to be a "Divine Fetus" too.

A fetus of the Fierce God.

No wonder Mr. Tu feared him so much, no wonder the Divine Lord trusted him so much.

No wonder his every word and action seemed like ’miracles’...

The Feather Transformation of Yin Corpse Valley coldly said: "Why didn’t Mr. Tu inform us of such an important matter earlier?"

Had he known earlier, he would certainly have done whatever it takes, by any means necessary, to possess this Mo Hua.

Mr. Tu could obviously see his thoughts, but only sneered coldly.

The atmosphere was somewhat heavy, and after a moment, the elder from Demon Sword Sect slowly said:

"We must first find a way to seize the Divine Lord back. We absolutely cannot let him fall into the hands of Qianxue’s forces, and even, if he stays with the Divine Fetus Mo Hua, it’s very dangerous..."

"At this time, we should abandon past grievances, our Demon Path, united as one."

Everyone had calm expressions, different thoughts in mind, but none disputed.

Those present, whether Feather Transformation Elder Demons, or once powerful elders of the Great Family, had eyes only for ’profit’, not bothered by momentary grievances or disputes.

Even though a moment ago they were fighting tooth and nail for the Divine Lord, now with the situation changed, naturally they would join forces without caring for past grudges.

"Alright." Mr. Tu nodded.

The Feather Transformation of Yin Corpse Valley asked: "Mr. Tu, what are your plans next?"

Mr. Tu indifferently said: "We are the Demon Path; in the ways of the Demon Path, there is only one word... kill!"

"Although there were some unexpected incidents, causing deviations in our plan, the Divine Lord indeed has descended, this matter is undoubted."

"We only need to continue advancing the original plan..."

Mr. Tu’s gaze turned cold, "Expand the Blood Sacrifice Array with all efforts, unite strategically, complete the blockade, and then assault the Qianxue State Boundary, creating mass slaughter."

"The more people killed, the heavier the sins of killing, the stronger the formation."
