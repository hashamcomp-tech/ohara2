Chapter 1442: Chapter 789 19_2

They became even more reckless, rushing towards Mo Hua to kill.

The Demon Wraiths arrived like tides again.

Mo Hua used the same technique as before, first using the Formation to kill on a large scale, then using the Fireball Technique to kill individually, thus killing yet another wave.

Afterward, Demon Wraiths continued to rush forward.

But Mo Hua noticed that his Divine Thought seemed to be somewhat lacking.

After all, employing Heavenly secret and counteracting the Formation in such a large range consumes an enormous amount of Divine Thought Power.

Mo Hua had no choice but to temporarily consume a few bites to replenish Divine Thought before killing again.

This temporarily consumed Divine Thought, without refining, contained the Evil Thoughts of the Demon Wraiths, and directly absorbing it would gradually pollute the cultivator’s Divine Thought over time.

However, Mo Hua’s Divine Thought was special, and he was accustomed to Evil Thought invading his mind due to his extensive battle experiences, so the temporary impact wasn’t significant.

Another wave was killed, and the surrounding Evil Qi grew stronger.

The brutality of the Demon Wraiths intensified.

Mo Hua felt it was about time, so he set up the Li Mountain Fire Cremation Formation again to "clear" the group of Demon Wraiths around him. Then, taking advantage of the gap when the old demons were dead and the new ones hadn’t yet arrived, his eyes flashed with Golden Light. Utilizing his understanding of the Heavenly Dao Seal, he operated his Divine Thought, manifesting several purer golden formations a few feet around him.

Divine Lock Array!

This is the core formation of the Great Wilderness lineage, a Divine Formation heritage.

It can seal the Demon Wraiths and even use the "devouring" of their Evil Power to form a Telekinesis cycle, maintaining the stability of its own Formation.

The Divine Lock Array encompassed Mo Hua like a golden cage.

Mo Hua "sealed" himself with this and also "protected" himself with it.

As expected, the Demon Wraiths rushing over were blocked outside the "Divine Lock Array," unable to get near Mo Hua for the time being.

They bared their teeth and clawed, continuously tearing at the Divine Formation Patterns.

In the Divine Formation Patterns, the "Sealing" Principle understood by Mo Hua was infiltrated. While these Demon Wraiths attacked, a part of the Evil Power was also devoured and converted into the power of the Formation.

However, the "Sealing" Principle that Mo Hua comprehended was still quite rudimentary.

Although the Divine Formation blocked these Demon Wraiths, it still darkened and deteriorated bit by bit, only buying Mo Hua some time.

But the time gained was enough.

Mo Hua had long planned this in his mind.

Individually, Demon Wraiths might not be very strong, but when gathered in the thousands, they were not to be underestimated.

His slender arms and legs couldn’t withstand their attrition.

Therefore, in group battles, it was crucial to prepare room for him to "breathe."

In this way, he would kill while "eating."

After eating to replenish Telekinesis, he would continue to kill.

Just like the "Sealing" Principle contained in the Divine Formation, which converts devoured Evil Power for personal use.

Fighting to sustain fighting, eating to sustain killing, in a continuous cycle.

In this way, he wasn’t afraid of being "exhausted."

And the "Divine Lock Array" for sealing was just right to buy himself room to breathe.

Of course, other defensive formations could be used, but none were as powerful as this Divine Formation like the Divine Lock Array.

This was a method that Mo Hua had been pondering in his heart since he learned the Divine Formation to cope with "Demon Wraith group attacks" and avoid being "exhausted to death."

The nearby Demon Wraiths were sealed by the Divine Lock Array.

The distant Demon Wraiths, unable to charge through, trampled on each other, even tearing and devouring each other.

The scene instantly became chaotic.

Relying on the shelter of the Divine Lock Array, Mo Hua sat calmly amid the raging "Demon Tide," beginning to sit cross-legged in meditation, taking advantage of this time to absorb the Telekinesis of the Demon Wraiths.

Once refined, the Telekinesis was integrated into him to replenish his Divine Thought.

The remaining few bloodthirsty desires would gradually corrode his Taoist Heart, temporarily suppressed by Mo Hua deep in his heart, to be gradually refined and discarded later through meditation.

Many Demon Wraiths had perished.

The vast Telekinesis converged into Mo Hua’s Divine Thought body.

Not only did it replenish the Divine Thought just consumed, but it also slightly enhanced Mo Hua’s Divine Sense.

But unexpectedly, the slight enhancement of Divine Sense disappeared in an instant.

Mo Hua closed his eyes and could perceive a void fissure streaking across his Sea of Consciousness.

In the fissure, the Law flowed, swallowing all of Mo Hua’s increased Divine Sense.

"Divine Thought leaves the body, and the Heavenly Dao Seal is still present..."

"In other words, even if the Divine Thought leaves the body, it still maintains a certain connection with one’s Sea of Consciousness, not completely isolated."

Mo Hua thought silently in his heart.

In this way, unless he truly feeds this "Heavenly Dao Laws," it would be impossible to resolve the "Seal" of the Heavenly Dao.

"Not leaving any loopholes at all..."

But it didn’t matter either.

Mo Hua opened his eyes and looked at the grand and drooling Demon Wraiths with fierce claws and teeth ahead.

"With so many Demon Wraiths, I refuse to believe I can’t feed you!"

As long as he fed this Heavenly Dao Seal, breaking the constraints of the Heavenly Dao, his Divine Sense would surge forward like a dragon into the sea, advancing further towards the Foundation Establishment Late Stage of Divine Thought Realm!

Mo Hua’s gaze was sharp, and once his Divine Thought was abundant, with a wave of his hand, he unlocked the seal of the Divine Lock Array.

Without the restrictions of the Divine Tao Array, the surrounding Demon Wraiths roared and rushed towards Mo Hua.

Unyielding, Mo Hua stood still with deep-set eyes.

The Separation Mountain Cremation Formation appeared once more, incinerating all the nearby Demon Wraiths.

The remaining few Demon Wraiths were also killed one by one by Mo Hua.

After that, like waves of the sea, another wave of Demon Wraiths attacked...

Then another wave...

Mo Hua continued like this, killing each wave as they came.

After a while, Mo Hua felt his Divine Thought a bit exhausted, so he set up the Divine Formation, rested for a while, ate a wave of Demon Wraiths, and replenished his Telekinesis.

After replenishing Divine Thought, the excess Telekinesis was refined and absorbed by Mo Hua, used to enhance his Divine Sense.
