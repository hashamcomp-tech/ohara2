Chapter 1608: Chapter 843: All Present_2

"It’s you, that remnant of the Water Prison Sect, that damn dog thief!"

Water Yama said through gritted teeth.

Ouyang Feng and Hua Qianqian were both stunned.

Wasn’t Mo Hua the Junior Brother of Taixu Gate?

How did he become a remnant of the Water Prison Sect?

Moreover, this sinister and powerful one-armed cultivator looked at Mo Hua with bloodshot eyes and seemed ready to burst with rage, as if there was a deep vengeance between him and Junior Brother Mo.

Saying it’s a vendetta for killing his father or taking his wife doesn’t seem too much...

What exactly did Junior Brother Mo do?

The two were a bit confused.

"Alright, I never thought you would dare appear in front of me..."

At this time, Water Yama’s eyes were fierce as he questioned Mo Hua, "Where are my things? Where?"

Mo Hua played dumb, "What things?"

Water Yama was so furious at his attitude that he laughed in his rage:

"You damned little brat, I’ll break your limbs right now and torture you bit by bit, let’s see if your mouth stays sharp..."

Mo Hua glanced at his empty sleeve and said crisply:

"I don’t know if my limbs will break, but one of your limbs is already broken, and it probably can’t be reattached, can it? No wonder you’re always thinking of breaking others’ limbs. Turns out, your arm was chopped off by someone, but you’re too cowardly and incompetent to get revenge, so you just take it out on others..."

Water Yama felt a surge of blood qi, rushing straight to his forehead, and the evil qi around him almost couldn’t be suppressed.

He didn’t dare talk to Mo Hua anymore.

Water Yama directly drew out the bloody Water Punishment Whip and attacked Mo Hua with vicious water force.

Mo Hua’s movement was like water, dodging with ease.

Seeing Water Yama attacking Mo Hua, Ouyang Feng immediately invoked the sword qi and came forward.

Hua Qianqian, having experienced many changes, was shaken in spirit, but seeing the enemy in front of them, with Ouyang Feng striving to fight and the much younger Junior Brother Mo also maneuvering against the fearsome demon, courage welled up within her as she gathered her spiritual power to restrain Water Yama with Hundred Flower Valley’s embroidered spirit needles.

While Mo Hua dodged, he used various spells to assist from the side.

His Water Prison Technique couldn’t trap Water Yama, but its limiting effect was still there.

Occasionally, he’d use the Concealment Technique along with the Water Passing Step, appearing and disappearing around Water Yama with his form stained with blood mist, not launching any killing move but still putting tremendous pressure on Water Yama.

Water Yama couldn’t figure out Mo Hua’s abilities and didn’t know his true power.

But he clearly remembered that on Water Island, in that secret room, there was a strange glow with a chilling murderous intent that made his heart tremble, so he dared not act carelessly.

Whenever he acted, he had to make sure of Mo Hua’s position and never expose a flaw before him.

Water Yama, distracted by three fronts, felt the pressure from Ouyang Feng lessen sharply.

Ouyang Feng, inherently smart with profound cultivation and high talent in the Sword Dao, only lacked experience compared to Water Yama.

In a previous accidental encounter, he suffered a great loss.

But now the pace slowed, and after exchanging dozens of moves with Water Yama, he gradually became familiar with Water Yama’s various techniques, responding more and more skillfully.

More importantly, he had Mo Hua, who understood Water Yama thoroughly.

Water Yama’s eyes turned red, and Mo Hua reminded, "Don’t look at his eyes."

When Water Yama used the Water Shadow Step, Mo Hua directly cast a fireball to his true body, breaking his Water Shadow Step.

Without these two special moves, with all the restrictions, head-on with Ouyang Feng, Water Yama couldn’t gain any advantage.

Water Yama’s heart was filled with hatred, especially towards Mo Hua.

"This damn brat, when on earth did he see through all the skills I painstakingly cultivated my whole life?"

This brat absolutely cannot be spared!

Otherwise, how can I continue in the Dao world in the future?

I must kill him...

A sinister look flickered in Water Yama’s eyes.

At the same time, Mo Hua thought the same.

"The time is almost ripe, I feel like I can kill..."

A person like Water Yama should be dealt with early for peace.

Right now, his evil qi was in disorder, his divine soul was violent, and although he appeared ferocious and terrifying, in Mo Hua’s eyes, he was just bluffing and full of weaknesses.

A single Soul-Shocking Sword should be able to resolve this.

But Mo Hua was a bit uncertain...

Especially after fighting the Sect Leader’s watchdog, Big White, in Taixu Gate, Mo Hua suddenly lost a lot of confidence in the power of his Soul-Shocking Sword.

One should never underestimate any opponent.

Moreover, Water Yama had been cultivating this pupil skill for at least a hundred years.

Although he took shortcuts using demon evilness, he must not be underestimated.

Mo Hua thought for a moment and decided to try it out first.

Use Water Yama for a sword test.

As a cultivator who proved the Dao with divine sense, the painstakingly cultivated Soul-Shocking Sword of his should at least be better than Water Yama’s...

Even if it can’t achieve complete victory, in this three-on-one mixed battle, even a small disturbance could determine the outcome and decide life and death.

Mo Hua made up his mind.

But before that, there was one thing he had to confirm.

Mo Hua cast a fireball to distract Water Yama and then retreated to widen the distance, asking Water Yama in a deep voice:

"Was it you who harmed Senior Sister Ye Jin from Hundred Flower Valley?"

Ouyang Feng and Hua Qianqian were both stunned.

"Ye Jin?" Water Yama was perplexed, then grinned, "The number of women I’ve played with, I can’t even count. Those I killed after getting bored with, I can’t even remember which one you’re talking about!"

Mo Hua’s gaze turned cold.

Hua Qianqian was even more shocked and angry, gritting her teeth, "So it was you... You killed Jin Er, and I... I want you to pay with your life!"
