Chapter 2536: Chapter 32: Young Master Shi (Part 2)

Before tonight, he was a "highly respected" Golden Core Elder of a family in Little Post City, benevolent and treating the Foundation Building Cultivators with the warmth of spring.

Until tonight, he tore off his disguise and reverted to the Great Golden Core Demon of Yin Corpse Valley, murdering and refining corpses, full of evil deeds.

However, in front of Young Master Shi, this Golden Core Corpse Cultivator, who looked like a "wealthy man," was rather courteous.

Young Master Shi nodded in greeting and said:

"Elder Fu..."

Young Master Shi did not know the real name of this "Elder Fu" but only knew that before he fell into the Demon Path, he was quite wealthy and had a portly stature, always dressed in sumptuous rich man’s attire, hence earning the title "Elder Fu."

Corpse refinement is an occupation that consumes a lot of resources and riches.

Without some wealth and a number of slaves, where’s the qualification to "refine corpses."

The affluent refine corpses, and those who refine corpses are affluent; thus it makes sense.

Elder Fu smiled, his demeanor kind, and after a brief glance around, he asked with some confusion, "Young Master Shi, whom did you have a duel with?"

Young Master Shi’s gaze turned slightly dark, "A boy of unclear origin..."

Elder Fu was even more puzzled, "Foundation Building?"

Young Master Shi remained displeased and did not answer.

Elder Fu was also secretly amazed.

In this little Little Post City, to encounter someone capable of forcing Young Master Shi’s "Corpse Transformation" out and still escape safely in front of him, a Foundation Building Cultivator?

It seemed even that Young Master Shi suffered a bit?

Truly bizarre...

But he didn’t ask further.

Anyone eligible to vie for the title of Demon Path Saint Child possesses exceptional talent, audacious and proud; speaking too much could easily earn their resentment.

Elder Fu said, "I’ll send people to pursue and capture this Foundation Building cultivator for you to deal with."

Young Master Shi coldly said, "No need, sealing the city is the priority. Just seal the city, kill everyone, refine them into zombies one by one, and capturing this kid won’t be a problem."

Elder Fu nodded.

Not competing for temporary advantage and understanding priority, such temperament surely accomplishes great deeds.

He turned his head, glanced at the decadent and bloody boudoir, and said, "These..."

Young Master Shi replied indifferently, "I’ve arranged with Mei Fairy, they extract essence and blood, we refine corpses, each takes what they need."

"Good," Fu nodded, contemplated for a moment, and then reported the progress:

"The Corpse Formation is opened, the city is sealed, zombies are feeding on humans, estimating in two or three hours, they’ll have eaten enough..."

"Then, we’ll clean up, kill all the survivors, and ensure no one knows what happened in Little Post City."

"Afterward, we’ll use the Yin Corpse Blood Formation to refine the city. If everything goes smoothly without disturbance, in about a month, this Little Post City will become a thorough ’Corpse City,’ also the only Third Grade ’Corpse City’ within a thousand miles."

"Using this Little Post Corpse City as a foothold, our Yin Corpse Valley can expand amid the chaotic situation, continually promote the Demon Gate Corpse Path, develop and grow stronger..."

"This Little Post City will become one of the most important Corpse Path bases of our Yin Corpse Valley in Li State."

"If this succeeds, you’ll be more favored by the ancestors, Young Master..."

Young Master Shi was warmed inside but maintained a calm exterior, nodding and said, "Proceed quickly, do not delay, as delays bring change."

Elder Fu with a benign face replied, "Alright."

...

The corpse cultivators of Yin Corpse Valley accelerated the pace of city slaughter.

The tragedy in Little Post City continued to intensify.

One cultivator after another died miserably at the mouths of zombies, and then were infected with Corpse Poison by the sinister corpse cultivators, awakened by copper bells.

Many cultivators resisted, but they were only at Qi Refinement, at most Foundation Building.

Most were from small families and sects, with limited inheritance, their Spiritual Artifacts and Taoist Skills were inferior, no match at all for the formal Demon Path corpse cultivators of Yin Corpse Valley with systemized inheritance,

Let alone the seven or eight Yin Corpse Valley Golden Core Demons of higher cultivation, along with their Third Grade Iron Corpses and Copper Corpses.

And the surrounding city walls of Little Post City were entirely sealed by the Yin Corpse Valley cultivators using Evil Path Formations.

Such powerful formations were impossible to break.

Golden Core guarded, corpse cultivators slaughtered the city, and formations sealed it.

No one could escape.

The crimson spread.

The corpse plague rampaged.

Despair gradually enveloped Little Post City under the night...

...

Northwest corner, a high building.

Mo Hua hid himself, sitting on the rooftop, silently watching everything before him, eyes filled with pity.

After a moment, he sighed, stretched out his fair palm, pinched his fingers, and silently calculated for a moment, muttering:

"Almost there..."

...

At the center of Little Post City, inside a luxurious grand hall.

The surrounding cultivators were all dead.

Elder Fu was in the center, spreading out a Map of Little Post City, observing the movements across the entire city.

Beside him, several Formation Masters of the Corpse Path were controlling the sealing formation.

Young Master Shi stood on a high platform, looking down at the nightmare descending upon Little Post City, his face expressionless.

Everything proceeded according to plan.

The operation of the formation was entirely within the plan.

This compound formation is Yin Corpse Valley’s frequently used formation for "sealing" and "slaughtering" a city, handed down by ancestors, and basically error-free.

This operation was also the first city slaughter by Yin Corpse Valley after many years, thus taken very seriously, deploying as many as seven or eight Golden Core Demons.

Including three at Golden Core Middle Stage and one at Late Golden Core Stage, truly giving sufficient face.

With this force, slaughtering an Immortal City without even a "Golden Core" is truly using a sledgehammer to kill a chicken.

Such disparity in cultivation and Realm can essentially eliminate all unexpected occurrences.
