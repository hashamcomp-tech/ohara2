Chapter 1611: Chapter 844: Grand Fireworks (Part 2)

Mo Hua let out a cold snort, his face full of arrogance as he said:

"I only need to give the order, and this Rouge Boat will be reduced to ashes! You’d better behave yourselves."

Xie Liu mocked, "Alright, today I want to see how a little Foundation Establishment brat like you will turn my Rouge Boat into ashes?"

Hua Ruyu also had a smile on her face.

Only Water Yama suddenly felt a tinge of unease in his heart.

He was different from the other two.

Both Xie Liu and Hua Ruyu, although they knew Mo Hua, hadn’t really crossed paths with him.

In Xie Liu’s eyes, Mo Hua was just a "little dog" of the Taoist Court.

In Hua Ruyu’s view, Mo Hua was just a "little junior sister" from the Hundred Flower Valley.

But Water Yama was different, he had suffered numerous hidden losses at Mo Hua’s hands.

He knew well that this little brat Mo Hua seemed ordinary, but his skills were peculiarly strange.

No one knew what hidden cards he had.

Water Yama said in a deep voice, "Don’t be careless, this brat is not simple. Keep a close eye on him, and never give him any opportunity."

Seeing Water Yama acting so wary of the little cultivator Mo Hua, Xie Liu and Hua Ruyu were somewhat surprised, but then they also put away their contempt.

Mo Hua raised an eyebrow.

This Water Yama was truly sharp and cautious, but it was already too late.

Mo Hua smiled lightly.

Seeing the expression on Mo Hua’s face, Water Yama immediately felt a chill in his heart and said,

"Not good."

He lashed his long whip, drawing a bloody water light, the iron chain like a venomous snake, attacking directly at Mo Hua.

Mo Hua used the Water Passing Step, barely dodging it, and before he had even stabilized, Xie Liu’s Gui Water Sword Qi and Hua Ruyu’s Hundred Flowers Spirit Needle had already struck, leaving Mo Hua no time to breathe.

Water Yama also seized the opportunity to use the Water Shadow Step, trying to get close to Mo Hua.

But just at this moment, the ground suddenly flashed.

Several light cyan beams materialized into a Wooden Prison, momentarily blocking the Sword Qi and Spirit Needle, also holding back Water Yama.

"Second Grade Wood Prison Formation? When was it set up?"

The expressions of Water Yama and the others slightly changed.

But this formation, although it was a Second Grade High-Level Formation, couldn’t hold against a Golden Core and two at Foundation Establishment Peak.

In just a moment, the Wood Prison Formation was broken.

However, this brief delay gave Mo Hua the time needed; he had already reached Ouyang Feng and Hua Qianqian, taking out a Storage Bag and tossing it to Ouyang Feng.

"Senior Brother Feng!"

Ouyang Feng caught the Storage Bag, found inside an exquisitely forged iron Shield, and then without any hesitation, took out the Shield, setting it on the ground, standing before them, also shielding Mo Hua and Hua Qianqian.

Water Yama and the others, seeing this, were a bit puzzled.

"Just one iron Shield to stop the three of us? That’s wishful thinking, isn’t it?" Xie Liu sneered.

But before his voice fell, he heard Mo Hua softly say,

"Explode!"

As soon as the word was spoken, the formation responded.

Outside the boat, a dazzling light suddenly lit up, red and green intertwining, magnificently splendid, streams of fireworks shot into the sky.

The three of Xie Liu were stunned, and then they all understood.

Fireworks!

This brat was setting off fireworks, sending a signal!

The people of the Taoist Court must be nearby!

Xie Liu was mildly annoyed.

He knew that once the people from the Taoist Court arrived, the matter of the Rouge Boat would definitely not end well.

When the Taoists came to investigate before, he had guessed that this day might come.

But he hadn’t expected it to come so quickly and hastily, catching him without any preparation.

In his urgency, Xie Liu could only say: "Capture these three as hostages, deal with the rest later..."

At this moment, he also didn’t know how many people from the Taoist Court had come, or where they were ambushed.

He could only make the worst assessment.

If the situation couldn’t be salvaged, then keeping these three as hostages would be his bargaining chip.

With bargaining chips, there might be a slim chance for a turnaround.

Hua Ruyu and Water Yama thought so too.

However, Water Yama, knowing a bit more about Mo Hua’s disposition, thought one step further.

"Why set up a Shield?"

"To guard against fireworks?"

"Impossible..."

"So what’s it guarding against?"

The deck beneath their feet began to tremble, Water Yama recalled Mo Hua’s words about making them "turn to ashes," his pupils shrank, instinctively stepping back.

But Xie Liu and Hua Ruyu took a step forward.

Just at this moment, a loud rumble abruptly sounded.

A formation aura, more intense and overwhelming than before, emerged.

This time it wasn’t fireworks. It was truly Second Grade High-Level Li Fire, containing immense destructive power and filled with slaughtering energy!

Flames surged instantly.

The fierce flames, like a flood dragon, engulfed the entire boat.

Moreover, the Spirit Boat itself had numerous formations inscribed during its construction.

The explosion of the Fire Formation was quite powerful, also obliterating these formations inside the Spirit Boat, causing spiritual power to go chaotic, uncontrolledly spreading outwards, crossing and clashing with one another, further augmenting the explosion’s power.

The outsides of the Rouge Boat were blasted into pieces.

A genuine bright red flame, rose to the sky, spreading outwards, in the night sky, resembling a fire dragon.

And the Rouge Boat itself was linked with iron chains.

The range of Mo Hua’s formation setup wasn’t that large initially.

However, once the formation exploded and the Spirit Boat burned, the Li Fire rapidly spread, igniting all the Spirit Boats.

The Rouge Boat, from the front to the back, caught fire.

Through the dense mist, one could see the astonishing firelight, as if it were a sea of fire.

In the distance, on the ship of the Taoist Court.

Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official, who were tracking the Rouge Boat but couldn’t find its whereabouts, were staring blankly at the scene before them.

Even from so far away, they could feel the intense fluctuations of spiritual power.

Fireworks rose, followed by a beam of fire into the sky.

The overwhelming surge of spiritual power swept through, and the sound of explosions rang out continuously.
