Chapter 2151: Chapter 1012: Visitor (Part 2)

"Especially in the beheading situation, this Mo Hua, as long as he can stay invisible, he is invincible. Whoever encounters him is unlucky."

"In the end, it doesn’t matter whether he can win or not, anyway, he won’t let you win."

"Even when faced with the top talents of the Four Sects, even if he can’t defeat them, as long as he stays hidden, no one can do anything about it, and ultimately if it’s a draw, Taixu Gate can accept it, can you?"

A draw, however, doesn’t carry victory points, equivalent to a double loss.

The Four Sects also compete with one another.

Anyone who ends up in a ’draw’ with Mo Hua is equivalent to conceding a game, falling behind in the competition among the Four Great Sects.

If it comes to this, all one can do is pray that they don’t encounter this ’disaster’ Mo Hua.

Thinking of this, everyone started to feel a headache.

They didn’t expect that one day even the Elders of Daoist Law from their Great Sect would be so flustered by a Concealment Technique.

After a moment of silence in the hall, a respected elder spoke:

"Then let’s do it this way..."

"Sacrifice some of the Xuan Character Bureau to break Mo Hua’s concealment. As for compensation, we’ll cover it from the Four Great Sects."

"The spiritual artifacts for breaking shadows will also be arranged by us."

"We can’t let Taixu Gate continue to develop like this."

"And this boy named Mo Hua, the Qianxue Array Dao Leader, absolutely cannot be allowed. Whatever skills he has, dig them out as early as possible, find a way to counter them..."

Other sect elders nodded upon hearing this.

The plan was thus decided.

For the time being, the sects joined forces and shared information, using ’deciphering’ Mo Hua’s concealment technique as the first strategic priority in subsequent Xuan Character Sword discussions.

In the first match later, Xuan Qi Men went up against Mo Hua.

Each disciple of Xuan Qi Men held a spiritual mirror of different shapes to break concealments based on different principles.

Moreover, they were equipped with special hidden-breaking formations.

All tactics revolved around ’Mo Hua’.

This tactic clearly had many drawbacks.

Focusing solely on Mo Hua and ignoring Linghu Xiao, the already not very strong Xuan Qi Men, couldn’t possibly win.

And this wasn’t a beheading match, where defeating Mo Hua would automatically mean victory.

This left Mo Hua a bit confused.

He couldn’t quite understand what Xuan Qi Men meant.

Were they... giving away points?

Until the sword discussion, Mo Hua was surrounded and had to use concealment.

The five disciples of Xuan Qi Men didn’t hesitate, activating their spiritual artifacts immediately.

Five mirrors of various principles emitted different colored lights, all shining towards Mo Hua.

Gushan Refining Workshop was revitalized by Mo Hua.

He had participated in designing many refining diagrams.

Therefore, although his actual refining skill was low, his theoretical understanding was quite impressive.

With just one glance, Mo Hua understood.

"Xuan Qi Men never intended to win. Their goal in this match is to use different spiritual artifacts to break my concealment..."

Mo Hua’s mind clicked, and he immediately canceled his concealment, openly allowing the light from the five mirrors to shine on him.

The breaking concealment spiritual artifacts are meant to break concealment.

If he’s not concealed, naturally, nothing can be broken.

Xuan Qi Men disciples were furious and attempted to kill Mo Hua.

Mo Hua used a flowing water step to distance himself and immediately called out to Linghu Xiao and the others:

"Xiaoxiao, come protect me."

Linghu Xiao and the four others hesitated no longer and gathered around Mo Hua, shifting the focus of their tactics to protecting him.

Thus, the pressure on the Xuan Qi Men disciples increased.

Their strength was already relatively weak, and their physical defenses were far inferior to those of Vrara Sect and Longding Sect disciples, making them unable to withstand Linghu Xiao’s sword qi to forcefully kill Mo Hua.

And Mo Hua was appearing and disappearing intermittently.

Xuan Qi Men used mirrors to expose him, and he revealed; no mirrors, he concealed.

The most infuriating was Mo Hua’s speed of switching between concealment and visibility, which was faster than the Xuan Qi Men disciples could retrieve their mirrors and activate the mirror lights.

Causing the Xuan Qi Men disciples to be at their wit’s end.

Outside, elders of sects and Taoist fields secretly cursed Mo Hua as a "little trickster," despicable, shameless, as sly as a ghost.

Xuan Qi Men thus lost.

Next was Sever Gold Sect.

Sever Gold Sect was stronger than Xuan Qi Men and learned from Xuan Qi Men’s lessons, optimizing their strategies against Mo Hua.

Sever Gold Sword Jue was a superior sword technique with strong power of killing.

Although Mo Hua, based on the core inheritance of Sever Gold Sect, designed the "Gold-Cutting Armor," using their shield to defend against their spear, it could largely immunize against Sever Gold Sword Qi damage.

But Mo Hua couldn’t wear this armor.

Because his physique was too weak.

This "Gold-Cutting Armor" even after reducing, the residual Sever Gold Sword Qi would still be lethal to him.

And with new grudges and old grievances, Sever Gold Sect’s disciples genuinely wished to kill Mo Hua.

Thus, in this sword discussion match, driven by hatred, they almost exerted all their strength.

Sword light danced across the sky; blossoming sword flowers flew toward Mo Hua like snowflakes.

Mo Hua couldn’t withstand it, relying only on concealment to save his life.

Moreover, he had to be completely invisible, unlike when toying with Xuan Qi Men, switching between visible and invisible.

Because once visible, even for a moment, it would mean five chaotic blades hacking forward.

Thus, Sever Gold Sect had ample time to use various breaking concealment spiritual mirrors to test Mo Hua’s concealment technique.

But upon testing, issues still arose.

The five disciples of Sever Gold Sect quickly discovered their spiritual mirrors were working intermittently.

Sometimes they could expose Mo Hua.

But then, the figure on the mirrors disappeared, suddenly unable to be reflected.
