Chapter 1612: Chapter 844: Grand Fireworks (Part 3)

The vibrations of the Rouge Boat chain explosions even transmitted through the water surface of Mistwater River to the vicinity of their Spirit Boat.

Ripples spread across the water, causing even the cabin beneath their feet to tremble slightly.

Xia Canonical Official was somewhat lost, opening his mouth, unable to find the right words, finally exclaiming in disbelief:

"Is this... the commotion caused by Mo Hua?"

"Probably..." Gu Changhuai was similarly stunned, "He said he would give us a ’hint’..."

Xia Canonical Official took a shallow breath.

"He’s just a Foundation Building Middle Stage cultivator, how on earth did he manage such a big disturbance, it’s too outrageous..."

Gu Changhuai was silent for a moment and sighed helplessly: "That’s how it is when he doesn’t restrain himself..."

Xia Canonical Official: "..."

With the fire as a clue, the position of the Rouge Boat was exposed.

Time was of the essence, and the two did not delay any further.

Xia Canonical Official ordered all the Spirit Boats of the Taoist Court to surround the Rouge Boat from all directions, determined to catch all the cultivators on the Spirit Boat.

If there is any resistance, no matter who they are, they should be killed without mercy!

On Mistwater River, the Spirit Boats of the Taoist Court surged through the dense fog, surrounding the burning Rouge Boat...

At this point, the cultivators on the Rouge Boat were already in a complete state of chaos.

Mo Hua had detonated his own array formation, causing the Spirit Boat to explode.

Those with bad luck, who were near the center of the explosion while engaged in activities, were suddenly blown away by the spiritual power fluctuations.

Those who were farther away were also affected by the aftermath of the explosion, fleeing in panic.

Many men and women, either unclothed or wrapped in sheets, ran around on the boat.

In an instant, the Rouge Boat turned into a sea of fire, and the "gentle boat" became a "nightmare."

Meanwhile, Mo Hua, the mastermind, had already grabbed a small boat and was paddling towards the shore with Ouyang Feng and Hua Qianqian.

Everything was calculated.

Their location was the "Life-gate" of the formation explosion, pre-planned by Mo Hua.

Being in the killing formation he set, it’s fundamental for a Formation Master to leave a "Life-gate" for oneself, a line of survival.

As the formation exploded, he had Senior Brother Feng raise a large shield.

This large shield was designed by Mo Hua for Taixu Sect Disciples, particularly for a big guy like Cheng Mo, used for hunting Fire-series Monster Beasts.

The shield was covered with various Fire Formation arrays, protecting against spiritual power impacts and fire-series burns.

During Monster Hunting, a Body Cultivator lifting the shield could withstand many attacks from fire-series Monster Beasts directly.

Mo Hua had kept one spare, just right for the occasion.

Occupying the Life-gate, plus the fire-shield defense.

Thus, even though the Rouge Boat exploded with a huge commotion, none of Mo Hua’s group were harmed.

And at this moment, with the Rouge Boat in chaos, Mo Hua took advantage of the situation to escape.

The formation exploded, the Spirit Boat destroyed, appearing mighty, yet such killing power certainly couldn’t kill that Golden Core of Gui Water Sect named Xie Liu.

There was no time to waste; it was time to run.

Ouyang Feng was also aware of the urgency, hastening the Spirit Boat away from the Rouge Boat.

Along the way, some cultivators who had fallen into the water tried to seize their boat but were repelled by Mo Hua and Hua Qianqian using Fireball Technique and Spirit Needles.

After escaping a certain distance and almost getting away, suddenly a deadly wave arose, and a water-blue sword light struck.

Mo Hua quickly used his movement technique to dodge away.

But the Spirit Boat wasn’t a cultivator; it couldn’t use movement techniques, couldn’t turn, and was split open by the water-blue sword light.

Water began flooding into the boat.

The small boat wouldn’t last long before sinking, and there wasn’t enough time to reach the shore.

Mo Hua had no choice but to abandon the small boat with Ouyang Feng and board another Rouge Boat nearby.

This Rouge Boat was farther from the central Flower Boat, lightly impacted, receiving minor damage, but because it was locked together, it was dragged along and caught fire everywhere.

The moment Mo Hua and the others steadied themselves on deck, they saw someone standing opposite them.

It was Xie Liu.

Not only that, suddenly the sound of water surged, and a white figure emerged from the river, it was the extremely aquatic Water Yama.

The two blocked Mo Hua’s group.

But at this moment, their expressions were unspeakably grim.

Xie Liu’s body was bleeding, his water-blue Taoist robe blown to tatters, his skin charred and red in patches.

The second-grade high-level Killing Array explosion, along with the Spirit Boat detonation, had hit him directly.

If not for his Golden Core foundation, he would probably have half his life gone.

Water Yama was also injured but fared much better.

He was sharp-witted and wary of Mo Hua. Thus, when Mo Hua activated the formation, he almost instinctively stepped back a few paces.

Those few steps made all the difference.

Otherwise, he wouldn’t possibly be standing here intact.

Regarding Hua Ruyu, Mo Hua didn’t see her.

But she, at Foundation Establishment Peak, attacking Mo Hua together with the Golden Core Xie Liu, might have been blasted half-dead if not killed outright.

Three enemies became two, but the situation remained dire.

Senior Brother Feng was injured, and Senior Sister Qianqian’s cultivation and strength were far below Water Yama.

The pre-setup formation had been used.

Xie Liu and Water Yama wouldn’t give them time to set up a new formation.

Mo Hua could conceal, use the Water Passing Step, and knew some Water Avoiding Demon Extermination Skills, possibly escaping.

But Senior Brother Feng and Senior Sister Qianqian wouldn’t be able to get away.

In the distance, shadows of boats loomed; people of the Taoist Court were rushing in.

But distance couldn’t save the immediate burning crisis...

Right now, there was no one to help.
