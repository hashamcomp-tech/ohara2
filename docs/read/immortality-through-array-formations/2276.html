Chapter 2276: Chapter 1051: Massacre (Part 2)

Inside the cave, the atmosphere was heavy.

Cheng Mo and a group of Taixu Gate disciples were anxiously waiting, afraid that Mo Hua might encounter any mishaps.

Even the other sect disciples were somewhat worried about Mo Hua’s safety at this moment.

Of course, they were not concerned for Mo Hua, but worried that without him, they might not walk out of the large formation alive.

Amidst this anxious atmosphere, a figure flashed in the void, and Mo Hua appeared.

Everyone secretly breathed a sigh of relief.

Situ Jian asked, "Junior Brother, how is it?"

Mo Hua nodded, "I’ve roughly figured out the situation..."

He took out a piece of paper, drew a simple map, and traced a route, "Inside here, lies the formation core passage. At the end of the passage is a hall, inside which are seventy or eighty Evil Formation Masters, drawing an evil formation..."

"Later, I will lead you in," Mo Hua’s eyes gleamed with fierceness, "you will all strike together, and slaughter all these seventy or eighty Evil Formation Masters!"

Everyone was stunned, "Slaughter all of them?"

Mo Hua nodded, "Kill them all, not a single one left!"

Mo Hua still remembered the teachings from the deceased "Mr. Pi" in the Solitary Mountain tomb:

"No matter what you’re doing, it’s best that there’s only one Formation Master among a group."

"And the best thing is if that Formation Master is you."

Among the disciples of the Four Sects and Eight Gates Twelve Streams, there were also Formation Masters.

But these Formation Masters were ordinary disciples, who did not understand large formations and posed no threat to him.

However, Mr. Tu’s Evil Formation Masters were different and would certainly be a hidden danger to him.

If these Evil Formation Masters had not encountered him, it would have been fine.

But now that they had, it meant they were out of luck for eight generations.

It would be best to kill them all, but if not, then as many as possible.

The fewer the Evil Formation Masters, the smaller the threat to him.

Righteousness and evil do not coexist, and practitioners in the same field are enemies.

With these two causes and effects in place, these Evil Formation Masters are doomed to their demise.

"Be covert in action, quick, ruthless, and ensure a fatal strike," Mo Hua said solemnly, "These Evil Formation Masters are deeply sinful and supporting the tyrant, they must die."

"Whether or not we can kill these Evil Formation Masters is crucial to whether we can suppress the Evil Path Great Formation, and whether we can escape alive."

Mo Hua’s expression was solemn.

Most of the Heavenly Pride disciples, knowing the severity of the matter, nodded in agreement.

Only a few Heavenly Pride disciples, used to being proud, didn’t take it seriously.

Mo Hua knew well that people’s personalities differ, some need to be cajoled, others need to be managed inversely.

Mo Hua coldly said again:

"You are all Heavenly Prides from various Great Sects, surely seventy or eighty Evil Formation Masters wouldn’t be unstoppable by you?"

"Don’t make me look down on you."

This time, all the Heavenly Prides focused their gaze, their expressions became serious.

No one wanted to lose face, nor could they allow Mo Hua to look down on them.

They were already "defeated subjects" of Mo Hua, being looked down upon by him was a humiliation they couldn’t bear.

"Very good."

Mo Hua nodded, then took out the map to begin planning and arranging personnel.

Once arranged, Mo Hua first entered the cave, "cleaning" the lurking evil spirits again to ensure nothing was missed.

Then Mo Hua broke the formation patterns in the formation core passage, dissolved the stone walls, opened several passageways, and set silencing formations and concealment formations nearby to cover their tracks.

Concealment and stealth, probing reality, then luring the "wolf" into the den.

Such matters came easily to Mo Hua, who had done it countless times from a young age, never making a mistake even with eyes closed.

Finally, Mo Hua used Divine Sense to control ink, quietly painting a "Divine Mist Formation" at the entrance of the hall, which isolated sound, spirits, and even divine sense probing.

This way, the entire hall was "isolated from the world."

The four Golden Core Evil Formation Masters were also shut out.

In a short time, no one would know what transpired inside the hall.

When everything was ready, Mo Hua issued the "slaughter order."

Hundreds of Heavenly Pride disciples from the Four Sects and Eight Gates Twelve Streams followed the hidden paths set by Mo Hua, directly entering the core hall, stationing themselves stealthily at the corners, sealing the hall tightly.

Then, with Mo Hua’s command, the slaughter commenced.

The previously ordinary surroundings of the hall suddenly turned as if "divine soldiers descended," and in an instant, the figures of a wave of Qianxue’s Genius disciples reappeared.

What followed were superior Water and Fire Five Elements Skills, exquisite Four Sects and Eight Gates swordsmanship, and various spiritual artifact assassinations like Hundred Flowers Needle, Money Dart, flying knives, and sleeve arrows, plus body cultivation attacks like Vajra, mountain stone, fierce tiger, or Azure Dragon, accompanied by powerful strikes of punches and kicks...

The disciples of the Four Sects and Eight Gates Twelve Streams bore high-level legacies.

The Heavenly Prides present, top figures from each sect, displayed their diverse, exquisite methods, killing resolutely with astonishing power.

Protected by the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, those busy constructing evil formations inside the "secure" formation core hall never anticipated that the Evil Path’s outer defenses would remain intact, yet enemies would invade their headquarters.

Caught off guard, one Evil Formation Master after another was slaughtered by spells, swordsmanship, and body cultivation, either split open by sword qi, burned by raging flames, or suffered a chest-piercing iron fist, dying tragically on the spot...

Good and evil reap their recompense.

Now, these heinous Evil Formation Masters met their "retribution."

Some Evil Formation Masters, keen-minded, reacted instantly when the slaughter began, immediately severing fingers, biting tongues, gouging eyes, or disemboweling themselves... attempting self-harm to trigger causes, stimulate blood qi, and activate life-saving evil formations.
