Chapter 2329: Chapter 1068: The Earth Veins’ Lament

But... is this method really feasible?

Mo Hua frowned, calculating the subsequent "consequence" in his mind based on the established "cause"...

Once the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation is fully formed, all nearby second and third-grade state boundaries will connect, forming a wall of flesh and blood, completely sealing off the Fifth-grade Qianxue State Boundary.

This is a bloody and tight "lockdown."

The Blood Sacrifice Array is wicked.

And within the formation, numerous Demon Path sects’ Demon Heads gather.

Various Walking Corpses, Iron Corpses, Copper Corpses, Blood Slaves, Sword Slaves, Lust Slaves raised by the Demon Path... also surge like tides.

And within the vein of blood, there is an endless army of demon monsters.

With the large formation’s blessing, this is an exceedingly powerful Demon Path force.

The cultivators outside can hardly bypass the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation to enter the Qianxue State Boundary.

The cultivators inside the Qianxue State Boundary cannot get out either.

Cultivators below the Golden Core, once entering the Blood Sacrifice Formation, surrounded by Demon Heads, Demon Cultivators, various Demon Path slaves, and demon monster armies, will undoubtedly die.

There is a Heavenly Dao restriction in second and third-grade state boundaries.

Even those who have undergone Feather Transformation, if unable to fully exert their cultivation, cannot fly, once surrounded by endless demon monsters, they might also fall.

Those with Heaven Void cultivation are too strong, may comprehend Laws, and perhaps break through the encirclement.

But the premise is that the Great Wilderness Evil God hasn’t awakened.

If the Great Wilderness Evil God wakes up, and Evil Power spreads, then this Blood Sacrifice Array would become a "forbidden" territory entirely.

Even those with Heaven Void might not dare to traverse this Blood Sacrifice Array.

If polluted, parasitized, or turned into a puppet or plaything of the Evil God, the consequences are truly unimaginable.

Heaven Void is restricted by the Evil God.

Feather Transformation may perish if slightly careless.

Below Golden Core, there is no hope of survival.

The vast Qianxue State Boundary turns into stagnant water, only to be slowly consumed by the forces of the Evil God.

And it’s not just that...

Mo Hua’s pupils tightened.

The Qianxue Sword Discussion Conference is a grand event in the Nine State.

At this moment, within the Qianxue State Boundary, cultivators from various parts of the Nine State Cultivation World are gathered.

The number is several times more than before, and all are elite cultivators of various state boundaries.

Once the Qianxue State Boundary falls, these people will either become Demon Cultivators, Evil God’s minions, Walking Corpses, or Blood Slaves.

Or they will be directly killed, their blood skin and flesh reduced to materials for Demon Path Artifact Refining and Drawing Formation.

The strength of the Demon Path will also swell instantaneously.

Moreover, Mo Hua has not forgotten, near the Fifth-grade Qianxue State Boundary, there is an entire Spirit Mountain of the Mount Qianlong spiritual mine.

This is a large Fifth-grade Spirit Mountain, producing countless Spirit Stones, supporting the cultivation and operations of various sects in Qianxue.

Once Qianxue falls, this Mount Qianlong spiritual mine will naturally become the possession of the Evil God.

Spirit Stone is the most important resource for Tao cultivation.

To achieve great deeds, a sea of Spirit Stones is necessary.

Not to mention, considering solely from the aspect of large formation supply.

Once Mount Qianlong spiritual mine is polluted, these countless Spirit Stones will transform into endless Evil Power, endlessly supplying the formation eye of the Blood Sacrifice.

The Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation will also receive endless supply and continue to operate indefinitely.

With Spirit Stones, resources, people, territory.

Evil God’s awakening, blood sacrifice spreading, formation unceasing.

The entire Fifth-grade Qianxue State Boundary will also become a self-sufficient, deeply rooted, and exceedingly difficult to eradicate Demon Path "stronghold."

Then, using the Qianxue State Boundary as a foundation, continuously expand outward, spreading the Evil God’s power, outward contamination and spreading, probably the entire Qian State will also gradually fall step by step...

The more Mo Hua thinks, the more terrifying it feels.

This Mr. Tu, who plotted for a thousand years, his scheme is truly horrifying.

His formation arrangements are indeed magnificent to the extreme.

Although the process may not be smooth, full of variables and challenges.

But according to his plan, it is genuinely possible, with a Quasi-Third Grade Blood Sacrifice Array, to refine a Fifth-grade Qianxue State Boundary, turning the entire Qianxue prosperous land into Asura Purgatory, transforming into Evil God Dojo, bringing catastrophe upon the living, countless casualties...

"Could the ancestors of the Qianxue State Boundary see it?"

Mo Hua frowned in thought, trying to place himself in the position of the "ancestors" of various aristocratic families and sects, and felt perhaps not.

The matter is shrouded in mystery, with subtle details, even Heaven Void may not easily perceive to this extent.

At least before it happens, they are unlikely to fully understand.

Moreover, Qianxue has been peaceful for too long.

The Demon Path has been dormant for too long too.

The Evil God was long forgotten by people.

The flourishing Qianxue State Boundary, ringing bells and dining lavishly.

All thoughts of aristocratic families and sects are focused on the Sword Discussion Conference, on vying for fame and profit, on pursuits of interest.

Given such circumstances, they cannot avoid competing, and must compete.

But once embroiled in disputes of fame and wealth, blinded by the immediate leaf, naturally they cannot see the more dangerous causality.

This precisely fulfills the saying:

Its rise is intense, and its demise sudden.

Prosperity dismantles in moments, life and death descend in an instant.

Mo Hua frowned, while he followed Mr. Tu’s "instructions," continued using the Great Wilderness Bone Carving Skill to carve the Formation Pivot of the Blood Sacrifice Array, while pondering this matter.

But he thought for a long time, still had no leads.

After drawing the formation for a while, when his Divine Sense was depleted, Mo Hua lay down on a huge skeleton, resting while contemplating.

The current situation is actually more severe than he anticipated.

Not only the present "cause" but the more terrifying is the subsequent "effect."

He doesn’t wish to witness the scene of Qianxue State Boundary in disaster.

He is a disciple of Taixu Gate in the Qianxue State Boundary, in this land, cultivating for nine years.

He knows many people, and has received much kindness and care from them.
