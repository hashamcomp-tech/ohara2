Chapter 2350: Chapter 1074: Awakening (Part 4)

"From the beginning to the end, I have been the servant of the Divine Lord."

The ancestor of the Daluo Sect cursed inwardly, knowing that the ancestor of the Xiao family was beyond saving.

"Since we’ve known each other, let me send you off." After saying that, he raised his heavy sword and slashed towards the ancestor of the Xiao family again.

The ancestors of the Little Spirit Sect, the Spirit Talisman Sect, and the one from the Kun State’s Heaven Void, all joined forces to subdue and slay the ancestor of the Xiao family.

The Soul Suppressing Talisman suppressed the evil energy of the Xiao family’s ancestor.

The Daluo Unification Sword tangled directly with the ancestor of the Xiao family.

The Divine Thought Skill of the ancestor of the Little Spirit Sect assisted from the side.

Meanwhile, the faint Exorcising Thunder Light on the Thunderwood Sword also gradually weakened the power of the Xiao family’s ancestor.

The Primordial Spirit of the Xiao family’s ancestor was suppressed, and the black aura around him was gradually peeled away, revealing his Primordial Spirit’s condition to everyone.

His Primordial Spirit was already rotten!

It was covered with bite marks, gnawed beyond recognition, and his chest was hollowed out, forming a large hole.

Clearly, the foundation of the Primordial Spirit was damaged, yet the ancestor of the Xiao family was excited and frantically insane. With the aid of Evil Power, he was even stronger, like a "final burst of brilliance."

The battle in Heaven Void was fierce, with waves of Divine Thought rippling in all directions.

True Man Situ couldn’t intervene, nor did he intend to; his involvement would not make much of a difference in this situation.

He hadn’t forgotten his main goal, which was to eliminate the Evil God True Embryo and resolve the great crisis threatening the Qian Learning State Boundary and the entire Qian State.

This was the most important matter.

Seizing this moment, True Man Situ’s Divine Thought rapidly circulated, quickly sorting through all the causes and effects of the situation in his mind.

Their goal for this journey was to "slay the god."

The Evil Demons, on the other hand, surely wanted to stop them.

It must be Mr. Tu, through some kind of ritual, who called this corrupted ancestor of the Xiao family into the Divine Lord’s nightmare.

The ancestor of the Xiao family also prematurely broke a Fetal Demon Egg, using it to stall their group.

The focus is... why stall? Why not just slaughter them?

Because... the Evil God cannot kill us?

True Man Situ’s heart had always been shrouded in fear of the Evil God, but he overlooked one thing: they might fear the Evil God, but the Evil God was actually wary of them too.

This group, with four Heaven Voids and ten Feather Transformations, was indeed a formidable force.

The Evil God was strong, but not at this moment.

The Demon Monster Eggs hadn’t hatched either.

Their group was enough to make Mr. Tu, the ancestor of the Xiao family, and even the Evil God wary.

Therefore, Mr. Tu and the ancestor of the Xiao family, along with the Evil God’s minions, could only stall for time.

Once the Evil God awakens, they would all perish.

But if the Evil God doesn’t awaken, it means their group is actually the stronger side.

In other words, right now, at this moment, is when the Evil God and other demon monsters are at their most "vulnerable."

Realizing this, True Man Situ’s heart instantly cleared, and his sense of urgency increased.

They must race against time and kill the Evil God as soon as possible!

Right now, they are the "stronger" side, but once the Evil God awakens, the balance of power will be instantly reversed!

The situation would then worsen to an irreversible disaster.

Murderous intent surged in True Man Situ’s eyes.

In his original plan, he wanted to save Yu Er if possible.

Save Yu Er, then kill the Evil God True Embryo.

But now, he had no idea what to do to save Yu Er. His understanding of the Evil God was too limited, and he didn’t even know if the Yu Er he saved would still be Yu Er.

And now, the situation didn’t give him the time to ponder.

He could only...

Kill the Evil Embryo along with Yu Er...

True Man Situ felt deeply conflicted, with a wave of painful murderous intent in his eyes.

As if a bond between mother and child, Wenren Wan sensed Yu Er’s danger immediately and slowly realized True Man Situ’s intention.

True Man Situ... was going to kill Yu Er.

The person who originally intended to save her child now had to kill her child.

Wenren Wan was suddenly overwhelmed with grief.

Her voice was hoarse, wishing to plead with True Man Situ not to kill her Yu Er, but she didn’t know how to speak.

This was the great catastrophe of the Qian Learning State Boundary.

True Man Situ himself was willing to face death.

Could she really ignore the lives of countless people in Qian State for the sake of her child? Moreover, this also concerned the Wenren family, the Shangguan family, her father, her husband... so many lives.

"But... Yu Er..."

Wenren Wan didn’t know how to choose, her eyes filled with tears and her chest aching as if it would suffocate.

True Man Situ didn’t dare to look at Wenren Wan, forced to endure the guilt and pain in his conscience, as he took each step towards Yu Er.

Feeling his murderous intent, the suppressed red powder Fetal Demon started to shriek sharply.

The ancestor of the Xiao family was also frenzied, desperately trying to protect his Divine Lord, but was relentlessly blocked by the four Heaven Voids.

In just a few breaths, True Man Situ was before Yu Er.

He took out a sword, engraved with the Beidou Seven Stars, the starlight dazzling.

This was the Formation Pivot of the Mystic Sky Beidou Seven Star Formation. By stabbing this sword into the Evil Embryo, a powerful force of the Stars could be invoked to pierce the body of the Evil God, using the starlight of heaven and earth to obliterate the Evil God True Embryo. Even if it couldn’t kill, it could severely damage the unborn Evil God.

The fate of Qianxue and the life and death of all living beings hinged on this moment.

True Man Situ hesitated no more; his eyes cold, he raised the Seven Stars Sword, about to pierce it into the Evil God True Embryo. But as he lifted his head, he saw a pair of innocent, clear eyes, with a touch of eeriness, staring blankly at him.

It was... Yu Er.

Yu Er was silently watching him.

True Man Situ’s pupils suddenly widened, his whole body turning cold.

The Evil God... had already awakened?!
