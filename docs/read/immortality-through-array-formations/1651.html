Chapter 1651: Chapter 856: Worse Than a Dog (Part 2)

But Mr. Witch clearly held the upper hand, especially with the assistance of numerous blood zombies, allowing him to attack and defend with ease.

If this continued, the defeat of Gu Changhuai and the other two was just a matter of time.

Mo Hua observed the situation and made a decision in his heart, then handed the incense burner to Ouyang Feng, "Senior Brother Feng, you all stay back, I’ll be right back."

Ouyang Feng didn’t know what Mo Hua was planning to do, so he could only caution: "Junior Brother Mo, be careful."

"Mm, rest assured, Senior Brother Feng."

After saying this, Mo Hua silently employed the Minor Five Elements Stealth Skill. His figure dimmed and vanished once more.

While invisible, Mo Hua took advantage of the frenzy of the battle when no one was paying attention to quietly circle around Mr. Witch and his group, avoiding the blood zombies as he made his way to the main gate.

He remembered clearly that the blood zombies had crawled out from near the main gate.

Mo Hua searched for a moment at the main gate and indeed found the blood-red Evil Formation carved under the white bone stone seat.

These Evil Formations were used in the Blood Sacrifice method to refine the blood zombies.

Mo Hua could not fully understand them, but breaking them was not difficult.

Around the Evil Formation, he painted some Gold and Fire Formations, using gold to repel evil, fire to burn evil, and formation to break formation.

In fact, the best repelling evil formation is the Thunder Formation.

But the Thunder Formation inheritance in the Cultivation World was extremely rare, not even possessed by the Taixu Gate, and Mo Hua simply did not know it.

However, these Blood Sacrifice Formations were not high-level, and just destroying them violently didn’t require such profound complexity.

Mo Hua glanced up, seeing the ongoing intense battle with no one paying attention to him, then used Divine Sense Control Ink, his fingers sketching and guiding the Spiritual Ink to form a formation.

Soon, the formation materialized.

Without hesitation, Mo Hua immediately detonated the formation.

In an instant, flames burst forth, thunderous sounds erupted, and the white bones on the ground were blasted apart, destroying the blood formation at the same time.

The moment the blood formation was destroyed, the surrounding blood zombies were instantly out of control, twitched a few times, then fell to the ground, motionless.

Even Xie Liu no longer had the strength to resist, collapsing with a thud, offering his neck to be slaughtered.

Gu Changhuai gathered spiritual power and condensed it into an extremely powerful Wind Blade, severing Xie Liu’s head, then turned around to join Master Xia and Master Xiao in surrounding Mr. Witch.

The blood zombies lay prostrate, and Xie Liu was dead.

In the blink of an eye, Mr. Witch, who had been so imposing surrounded by a tide of blood zombies, was now standing alone.

Mr. Witch was greatly shocked, then said furiously: "What scoundrel broke my formation?!"

Mo Hua naturally did not make a sound.

At such a time, he had no need to step out and attract Mr. Witch’s hatred.

Master Xiao and the other two knew well that this was a rare opportunity, so they all made their moves, Wind and Sword Qi bursting forth, beginning to surround and kill Mr. Witch.

Mo Hua watched in hiding for a while, thought for a moment, and began secretly setting up formations, preparing for the next phase of the plan.

Mr. Witch transformed into a yaksha-like monster, covered in dragon scales, impervious to blades and spears, unaffected by spells, and very powerful.

But he was facing three Golden Cores.

Moreover, regarding how to tackle Mr. Witch, Mo Hua had previously devised a plan, and Gu Changhuai and the others were already experienced.

At this point, it was simply repeating old tactics, implementing Mo Hua’s plan again.

Indeed, Mr. Witch could not withstand the wear and tear.

In less than an hour, the Blood Qi around him nearly thinned out, and the scales on his body became clearly visible.

Without the swirling Blood Qi and the energy supply of blood, Mr. Witch finally panicked.

He didn’t want to repeat past mistakes, so he charged left and right, trying to find an opening to escape, hoping to absorb more Blood Qi and make a comeback.

But the three Golden Cores present were not easy to deal with.

Mr. Witch had no chance at all.

It took just a short while for him to be captured.

Master Xiao originally intended to kill Mr. Witch, but found that Mr. Witch, upon transforming into a yaksha monster, had undergone some unknown change. He had not only retained his intelligence but his scale armor had become even harder than before.

His jaws were tightly clenched, and blades couldn’t penetrate, unable to shred his internal organs and take his life.

"Keep him trapped, we’ll figure a way out first."

Mo Hua now revealed himself, and then personally secured Mr. Witch with chains and trapping formations, one after another, binding him tightly.

Master Xiao gave Mo Hua a deep look and said coldly: "The young master’s formation skills are indeed extraordinary."

Mo Hua waved his hand modestly, "Just so-so, enough to make a living."

Master Xiao’s gaze darkened slightly, his expression inadvertently becoming more serious.

With Mr. Witch and the blood zombies resolved, Ouyang Feng, Hua Qianqian, and Xiao Tianquan from afar also gathered.

As everyone gathered, Master Xiao suddenly paused, frowned, and asked:

"Where is Water Yama?"

Everyone looked at each other in confusion.

Mo Hua said: "Dead..."

"Dead?!" Master Xiao’s pupils constricted.

Mo Hua sighed: "For some unknown reason, he suddenly lost his sanity, his eyes turned red, and he went mad trying to kill me. I, along with Senior Brother Feng and Senior Sister Qianqian, were not his match. Luckily, Brother Xiao arrived in time and killed Water Yama..."

Master Xiao’s sharp gaze suddenly turned to Xiao Tianquan.

Xiao Tianquan only felt his scalp tingle, but he had no choice but to brace himself and say, "Yes... I killed him..."

Master Xiao’s gaze grew dark as he glanced at Xiao Tianquan, remaining silent, then turned around and said: "Let’s figure out how to get out first."

Everyone had their thoughts, but said nothing.

Reaching the main gate, they found the doors tightly closed, the ram’s bones grim, with not a crack in sight, nor any formation to open the gate.

But fortunately, everyone discovered a demon copper statue.

This copper statue was identical to the one at the Dragon King Temple gate, used to insert the Fish Bone Token to open the temple door.
