Chapter 2214: Chapter 1031: Shock (Part 3)

"I don’t know, never heard of it, maybe just someone trying to ’fish for fame and reputation’?"

"Could it be ignorance? Even Ye Zhiyuan hasn’t heard of it."

"Speaking of which, was there someone in the alliance yesterday, claiming to be ’Ye Zhiyuan’, saying he wanted to ’take Mo Hua’s head with one sword’?"

"It seems that was the case..."

"During the sword discussion today, it seems there was still someone, who had his head taken with one sword by Mo Hua... This person happened to be also called ’Ye Zhiyuan’?"

"No way?"

"Could it be, could it really be... these two Ye Zhiyuan’s are the same person?"

"Could it be such a thing?!"

"Absolutely impossible! Beware the Daluo Sect accusing you of slander... "

"Indeed, absolutely impossible. Who is Ye Zhiyuan? The renowned Heavenly Pride Disciple of the Daluo Sect’s Sword Dao, reputed to be the foremost in Qianxue Sword Control."

"Reputed to be the foremost in Qianxue Sword Control, then killed by someone else’s Sword Control, with one sword?"

"Tsk tsk, I won’t say anything..."

"It seems the one who killed him was a Formation Master?"

"A Sword Dao Heavenly Pride, killed by a Formation Master, with one sword?"

"Can such a person truly be a Sword Dao Heavenly Pride?"

"Indeed, ’If he can’t even kill Mo Hua, how dare he claim to be a Heavenly Pride?’ Who said that?"

"Alright, alright, these are minor issues, in my opinion, choosing a capable Alliance Hierarch, is the major issue."

"Indeed."

"By the way, our Alliance Hierarch was supposed to be crowned king today, right?"

"Yes."

"Where is our Alliance Hierarch?"

"Killed with one sword by someone, perhaps..."

...

The once boastful words, each and every one, became ’a historical record of time’.

Like a swirling flying sword, with one sword after another, pierced back into Ye Zhiyuan himself.

The Heavenly Pride Disciples of the Demon Slaying Alliance seized the opportunity, repeatedly flashing Ye Zhiyuan’s words on screen, with sarcastic ridicule, publicly executing him.

Ye Zhiyuan was resentful and furious, but could only pretend to be dead, not daring to utter a word.

In his cultivation room, he set up a straw man.

On the straw man, he pasted a crooked image of Mo Hua, drawn by his own hand.

He no longer said things like, ’The Heavenly Pride of the Four Sects and Eight Gates are completely blinded by hatred, only then they would consider ’killing Mo Hua’ a matter...’

’Those driven by hatred would have no grand vision...’ and the like.

He was wrong.

Hatred is the greatest driving force for people to move forward.

Endurance is actually the greatest vision.

Ye Zhiyuan began to use Mo Hua’s straw man to practice the Daluo Flying Sword Skill.

He planned to make ’Slaying Mo Hua’ his lifelong pursuit.

Revenge became his ’ambition and pursuit’ for this life.

Several flying swords soared in the air, piercing one by one into Mo Hua’s crooked face in the distance.

Piercing several playful faces, with a bit of an eerie smile.

...

Meanwhile.

Taixu Gate.

A group of Junior Brothers were taking turns to toast Mo Hua, their eyes full of admiration.

After this battle, their reverence for Mo Hua was as ceaseless as the flowing river.

With this victory in the sword discussion, Taixu Gate, at least for now, steadily secured the position of ’Leader of the Eight Great Gates’.

Once upon a time, Taixu Gate was at the bottom of the Eight Gates.

Tai’a and Chongxu had also suffered a great loss in vital energy, their rankings plummeting, almost to the point of falling out of the Eight Great Gates.

But now, the Three Sects Unification, turned around the fortune, and they rose to the first among the Eight Great Gates.

Not just the ordinary disciples, the elders of the Taixu Three Mountains were equally grateful to Mo Hua.

If not for the restrictions of identity and face, they all wished to raise a cup in toast to Mo Hua.

And the good news doesn’t end here.

Afterwards, Mo Hua used ’Sword Control’ to win two more Earth-ranked sword discussions.

The situation unknowingly changed.

The Sect Leaders of the Three Mountains, after pondering, unexpectedly found Taixu Gate with another, albeit slim but truly existent opportunity:

An opportunity to be listed among the Four Great Sects.

In the Qianxue Debate, not only Taixu Gate was in a heated battle, the other Four Great Sects were fighting fiercely as well.

The Four Sects were not a unity in essence, there was competition among them, each striving to be first.

Externally, they may be united.

But when it comes to real competition of fame and profit, the Four Sects slaughtered each other mercilessly, none would concede an inch.

Between the Heavenly Pride Disciples, no one accepted the others, the killings were inconclusive.

Therefore, after fierce Earth-ranked sword discussions and mutual killings among the Heavenly Pride Disciples, the rankings of the Four Great Sects were actually not too different.

And because of the ’internal strife’ of the Four Sects, mutual consumption, and absorbing each other’s points, this led to their advantage over Taixu Gate not being significant.

Now with only two Earth-ranked sword discussions remaining.

As long as Taixu Gate can win two consecutive matches, and any one of the Four Great Sects, except the Heavenly Sword Sect in first place, loses two matches, then Taixu Gate can ascend directly.

This condition is a bit stringent.

Moreover, it not only depends on luck but also on ’the face’ of others to advance.

But this is, for Taixu Gate, the only hope at present.

To be honest, just having this hope is already not bad.

Other sects, wishing for this opportunity, do not have it.

As for the following Heaven-ranked sword discussions, the situation is too variable to consider momentarily.

As of now, with two more consecutive wins, Taixu Gate reasonably holds the possibility of advancing to the Four Great Sects...

This possibility leaves all the Sect Leaders of the Taixu Three Mountains burning with anxiety.

At this point, they hope for nothing but for ’luck’ to be a bit better.

They hope Taixu Mountain, their ancestors and forebears would bless, to give Taixu Gate an opportunity to advance to the Four Great Sects.

Let Taixu Gate meet two ’soft tomatoes’ in the next two rounds of sword discussions.

Or, even if they aren’t ’soft tomatoes’.

Mo Hua and his group are already strong enough; as long as they do not meet top Heavenly Pride Disciples, they can fight, and the win rate is not low.

The possibility of winning two consecutive matches is still there.

Under such anticipation, the list of opponents for Mo Hua’s next round of sword discussion was confirmed through the Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument.

After the Taixu Gate top to bottom saw this list, their faces all changed, and a chill went through their hearts:

"Qian Taoist Sect, Shen Linsu."
