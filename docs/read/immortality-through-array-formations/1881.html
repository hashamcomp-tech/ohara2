Chapter 1881: Chapter 925: Killing Intent

After confirming this thought, the killing desire in Mo Hua’s heart slightly subsided.

Once the killing desire subsided, Mo Hua suddenly realized:

"As a Foundation Establishment, how can I wipe out a Demon Sect full of Third-grade Golden Core Demons?"

Just a moment ago, he had made up his mind in a moment of impulse, realizing that he might have been overly arrogant now that he was calmer.

But the killing desire in his heart still surged restlessly.

The thought of "eliminating demons and slaying evil" was eager to move, as if the Demon Sect wasn’t eradicated, as if blood didn’t flow like a river and there weren’t extreme casualties, it would be difficult to suppress this hunger in his heart.

But he truly didn’t have that ability.

Mo Hua thought for a moment and took out the Reverse Spirit Array Map, "Thinking of a way to set up a Reverse Spirit Formation, decompose a Second-grade Array, and obliterate the entire Demon Sect?"

It seemed that if he really wanted to massacre demon cultivators on a large scale, this was the only way.

Under the decompose of a Large Formation, all beings are equal.

Even the Taoist Demons would be executed, let alone the mortal demon cultivators?

"However, it’s not certain that a Large Formation would be established inside the Demon Sect... The destructive power of an ordinary Second-grade Array decomposition might not be that great."

Mo Hua looked at the Reverse Spirit Array Map again and sighed.

"Moreover, I can’t learn this Reverse Spirit Formation right now..."

The Reverse Spirit Formation is a Second-grade Twenty Patterns Formation, which requires a Divine Sense of Twenty Patterns to master.

He could try to learn and practice it now, but to truly master it and personally set up the Reverse Spirit Formation to decompose an array, the Divine Sense must reach the Golden Core Realm.

Cultivation Foundation Establishment, Divine Sense Golden Core.

The Divine Sense surpasses a Great Realm.

This is almost impossible according to the knowledge of normal cultivators.

But Mo Hua is different, he must take this path.

However, Mo Hua had a premonition; although his Divine Sense was only a line away from Core Formation, this gap was not easy to cross in a short period.

After all, this is Core Formation.

Cultivation Core Formation is already not easy, let alone achieving Divine Sense Core Formation in advance.

"Since this is the case, how can the demon cultivators be exterminated?"

Mo Hua pondered repeatedly and felt that he had to resort to "killing with a borrowed sword."

A noble person’s nature is not different, but good at using things. What you can’t accomplish yourself should be pushed towards the desired direction through networks and strategies.

He couldn’t exterminate the demon cultivators himself, but someone could.

Ultimately, as long as these demon cultivators are wiped out, it doesn’t matter who did it, the "killing desire" in his heart should also be reduced.

Mo Hua thought for a moment and gradually made up his mind.

If the Demon Sect is not destroyed, who knows how many innocent cultivators will suffer tragically, and how many loose cultivators will become their blood slaves.

Mo Hua no longer hesitated and immediately sent a transmission to Gu Changhuai.

...

A few days later, during the ten-day break.

Inside the Secret Chamber of the Fifth Grade Taoist Court of the Qian Learning State Boundary.

Even the always aloof Gu Changhuai couldn’t help but whisper sneakily to Mo Hua:

"Be very careful, don’t make any noise, and don’t let anyone know."

Gu Changhuai warned again, "This is the Secret Chamber of the Taoist Court, strictly prohibiting any outsiders from entering. I sneaked you in, already breaking the rules; if others know, my job as a Supervisor would be over."

"Does Sister Xia know?" Mo Hua asked softly.

"Nonsense," Gu Changhuai said, "The key is in her hands, how could I bring you in without her opening the door?"

"Oh..." Mo Hua’s expression was subtle.

Gu Changhuai frowned, "Check what you need to check quickly, don’t waste time."

"Hmm hmm," Mo Hua nodded.

Although the Taoist Court is bloated, with excessive staff, corruption, and inefficiency... despite these shortcomings, it doesn’t mean the Taoist Court is truly useless.

On the contrary, the information system of the Taoist Court is deeply rooted and has always been powerful.

Previously, Mo Hua had stolen secrets from the Demon Sect through the Original Magnet Compound Formation, designing the assassination of Demon Sect’s Golden Core.

But he didn’t think he was the only one who could acquire intelligence.

After all, large-scale, targeted campaigns against the Demon Sect have always been conducted by the Supervisors and Enforcement Leaders of the Taoist Court.

There must also be channels for obtaining information within the Taoist Court.

Listening to both sides enables clarity.

Mo Hua needed to integrate this intelligence, combining it with the secrets he had infiltrated from the Demon Sect, to find out the location of the Demon Sect, understand their foundation, and "prescribe the right medicine," seeking ways to exterminate this Demon Sect.

This was also the reason why Gu Changhuai, including Xia Canonical Official, was willing to illegally let him into the Secret Chamber.

They also didn’t want this battle between the righteous and demonic to continue, to prevent further casualties and more innocent cultivators from dying tragically.

The Secret Chamber was filled with various and miscellaneous intelligence, including dossiers, records, jade slips, and some portraits.

Mo Hua’s fair fingers gently flipped through them, like a startled swan skimming across the water, going through all the dossiers and jade slips one by one.

Due to his strong Divine Sense and sharp mind, he almost never forgot any information he saw.

Moreover, he frequently monitored and had a deep understanding of the Demon Sect.

Therefore, in his eyes, this mix of information was distinctly categorized.

Issues not noticed by the Taoist Court, he could pay attention to.

Clues undetected by the Taoist Court, he could detect.

The information that seemed mundane to the Taoist Court, Mo Hua knew the specific meaning hidden within...

Mo Hua’s expression was focused and serious, his fingers flying swiftly, no piece of intelligence escaped his grasp.

Gu Changhuai watched in a daze, unable to help thinking:

"This child, if he can enter the Taoist Court in the future, will surely achieve great things..."

After reviewing for a while, Mo Hua found a jade slip, transcribed some information, handed it to Gu Changhuai, and also circled several locations on a map:

"Yanluo Mountain!"
