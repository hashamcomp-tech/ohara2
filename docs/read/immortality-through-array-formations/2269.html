Chapter 2269: Chapter 1049: The Leading Big Brother

The "power" of Mo Hua is too great, with thirty disciples from Taixu Gate under his command.

The prodigies from Gui Water Sect, Sever Gold Sect, and Vajra Sect also seem to be under his orders.

Fighting is certainly out of the question.

And in this withered forest of blood swamps, everywhere is sinister and eerie, with no place to escape to.

If fighting is impossible and escaping is futile, Feng Zichen has no choice but to join.

On the way, Feng Zichen observed the disciples of Gui Water Sect, Sever Gold Sect, and Vajra Sect. They appeared normal, showing no signs of being brainwashed by Mo Hua, the "Evil Demon," and turned into puppets, which gave him a sigh of relief.

As for why they would associate with Mo Hua, their "great enemy," Feng Zichen couldn’t understand and dared not ask.

Mo Hua was indeed terrifying.

The scenes of the Shura battle were not long past, and Feng Zichen remembered them vividly.

Harnessing Divine Sense Control Ink, drawing ground into formation, the formation was like a demon skill, leading Taixu Gate to slaughter the prodigies from his seven sects.

It then self-destructed with a bizarre formation, killing the prodigies of the Four Great Sects.

Furthermore, using some terrifying Taoist skill, he slain five of Shen Linsu’s strongest Qianxue prodigies, even shattering their special Eternal Life Rune.

Five Heaven Void Dharma Phase appeared simultaneously, contending with the Mount of Tao’s Fifth-grade Large Formation, shaking the heavens and earth.

Even with all that, Mo Hua managed to survive.

Given the current situation, opposing Mo Hua is even more unlikely, and Feng Zichen could only bury old grudges deep in his heart, quietly following behind Mo Hua.

The group continued forward, later encountering a group of disciples from Lingxiao Sect.

The enmity between Lingxiao Sect and Mo Hua wasn’t significant, and understanding the principle that those who know the times are wise, they had no choice but to go with the flow and submit to Mo Hua.

Afterwards was the Purple Mist Sect.

The Purple Mist Sect posed more of a problem.

Because within the Purple Mist Sect, there was Lu Zhenlong, the most beautiful woman in the sect, whose beauty was renowned throughout the city.

Common people might assess the situation sensibly, but women often won’t, especially those who are beautiful and favored.

Mo Hua had used the Fireball Technique to scar her face, something Lu Zhenlong would remember for a lifetime and refuse to ever ally with Mo Hua.

And all the disciples of Purple Mist Sect acted according to Lu Zhenlong’s wishes.

They weren’t bewitched by her looks, merely trying to please Lu Zhenlong.

It was because Lu Zhenlong was the legitimate daughter of the Lu Family, a prominent family in Kun State, which symbolized wealth.

The prestigious Lu Family had donated large quantities of Spirit Stones and Spiritual Objects to the Purple Mist Sect.

Meanwhile, many talented disciples with mediocre backgrounds were also supported by Lu Zhenlong, and once they graduated in Core Formation, they would go to Kun State to work for the Lu Family.

Talented disciples had the backing of the Lu Family, and the Lu Family had amassed numerous Tao Cultivation talents for themselves, a mutual benefit.

Hence, Lu Zhenlong’s status was extraordinary within the Purple Mist Sect.

Lu Zhenlong’s Spiritual Root was excellent, but due to her privileged upbringing, she wasn’t diligent in cultivation, and thus her true strength was rather ordinary.

Yet despite this, by virtue of her prestige and the resources she could provide, she was still the "core" disciple of the Purple Mist Sect, truly the one "admired by all."

At this moment, upon seeing Mo Hua, Lu Zhenlong, protected by the disciples of the Purple Mist Sect, showed an angry face, pointing her fair finger at Mo Hua and furiously said:

"Whoever kills Mo Hua, I will let my father spend money and effort to nurture him to Feather Transformation!"

Among Lu Zhenlong’s entourage, seventeen Purple Mist Sect disciples immediately showed difficult expressions.

They weren’t worried that Miss Lu would go back on her word.

This young lady of the Lu Family, although a bit conceited and willful, has always been true to her word, and what she promises she delivers, giving money as promised.

Regarding some Heavenly Materials and Earthly Treasures, no matter how precious, once she made a promise, she was generous without hesitation.

So far, she had dispensed a great amount of wealth within the Purple Mist Sect, which allowed her to gather hearts.

The notion of the Lu Family spending money to nurture them to Feather Transformation sounded outrageous.

After all, achieving Feather Transformation is as difficult as scaling the heavens, and not something that can be done solely with Spirit Stones or by consuming Heavenly Materials and Earthly Treasures.

But having the Lu Family supply Spirit Stones and Spiritual Objects would surely make things easier, lowering many barriers.

Presently, none of the Purple Mist Sect disciples could remain unmoved.

"But...killing Mo Hua..."

They glanced at Mo Hua.

Attempting to kill Mo Hua, first of all, one must contend with eighteen indestructible Vajra warriors.

The eighteen prodigies from Vajra Sect stood in front of Mo Hua, like a fortress of steel.

And among these eighteen Vajra warriors was the Big Senior Brother from Vajra Sect, Shi Tiangang.

Forget about killing Mo Hua, even breaking through this "Vajra Iron Wall" and getting close to Mo Hua was a problem.

Additionally, around Mo Hua were disciples from Sever Gold Sect, Gui Water Sect, Carefree Sect, and Taixu Gate.

Out of these people, the most terrifying perhaps was Mo Hua himself...

The Purple Mist Sect disciples felt bitterness in their hearts.

The young lady can act irrationally, but they cannot.

Someone then softly advised Lu Zhenlong:

"Miss Lu, Mo Hua is simply too fierce for us to handle. We should retreat and reconsider our options..."

Lu Zhenlong looked angry.

Though she was arrogant and headstrong, she wasn’t truly foolish.

She also understood that the Purple Mist Sect members were no match for Mo Hua, the "big bully," nor would they recklessly oppose Mo Hua to the death.

Her previous impulsiveness upon seeing Mo Hua had been reflexiveness, but cooling off now, Lu Zhenlong still couldn’t overlook the urgency of the situation, and felt indignant.

Particularly towards the people from Vajra Sect, Carefree Sect, Sever Gold Sect, and Gui Water Sect.

What about the promised Demon Slaying Alliance?

What about the promise to eliminate "Mo" for the sake of Dao?
