Chapter 2326: Chapter 1067: Ambition

Draw the Blood Sacrifice Formation to summon the rebirth of the Evil God.

This demand is something that a typical Formation Master would never agree to.

Mo Hua considered pretending to refuse again, but upon seeing Mr. Tu’s cold and ruthless eyes, which clearly held little patience, Mo Hua decided not to refuse anymore and nodded honestly.

Mr. Tu flicked his sleeve, revealing bloody Formation Patterns, released the flesh prison, and said, "Follow me," before walking ahead.

Mo Hua followed closely behind Mr. Tu.

Together, they walked deeper into the Blood and Flesh Maze.

Along the way, the flesh was gruesome, the white bones eerie, the maze overlapping, the atmosphere oppressive and suffocating.

Though Mo Hua was already familiar beyond belief, he still seriously pretended to be unfamiliar, displaying a tense and fearful appearance.

Meanwhile, he silently memorized Mr. Tu’s route.

Mr. Tu was deep in thought, unaware that this secret and solemn maze forbidden area had long been thoroughly explored by Mo Hua.

After walking for an unknown length of time, the atmosphere gradually changed, and the environment became unfamiliar.

The blood fog around them grew denser, the oppression stronger.

Mo Hua could even feel a sinister heartbeat, hovering between life and death, affecting his pulse, blocking his blood flow, suffocating his chest.

After walking for another quarter of an hour, a large white bone door appeared in front of them.

Passing through the door, a strong breath of the Evil God rushed towards them.

A magnificent and eerie formation structure towered before Mo Hua.

Mo Hua’s pupils contracted as he held his breath.

After so many twists and turns, he finally followed Mr. Tu to the deepest part of the flesh maze.

And he also finally saw the Formation eye of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Large Formation and its most central pivot.

His previous premonition was correct.

There was indeed a heartbeat sound.

Because the Formation eye at the core of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Large Formation was a gigantic "heart" soaked in the blood earth veins, resembling a great mountain.

The blood in the earth veins flowed into the heart, injecting life into it.

As the Blood Sacrifice Array operated, the evil power condensed by the formation merged into the blood through the heart that served as the "Formation eye," and then surged back into the earth veins, polluting them into the foul river of blood.

Using the giant heart as the Formation eye to integrate blood and evil power.

Then using blood and evil power to pollute the earth veins, transforming the vast endless earth veins into the blood vessels of the Blood Sacrifice heart.

The evil power flowed through the blood vessels, gradually spreading, and polluting the entire land of Qianxue.

"This is the Formation eye of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Array..."

Mo Hua was momentarily stunned, then looked up at the colossal skeleton above the Formation eye heart.

The skeleton soared, towering over the earth veins, covered densely with Blood Sacrifice Patterns, grand beyond words.

This is the general pivot of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Large Formation.

With the heart as the Formation eye, the spine bone as the general pivot.

And this heart and this skeleton echoed each other, their breath united, as if from the same ancient monstrous beast.

"Mr. Tu... killed a gigantic Monster Beast, then peeled open the beast’s heart to use as the Formation eye, refined the beast’s skeleton to use as the general pivot, in order to construct the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Large Formation?"

Mo Hua’s emotions fluctuated, and at the same time, he couldn’t help but be confused.

Is this really a Monster Beast?

Can a Monster Beast be this large?

If not a Monster Beast, then what kind of beast?

Mo Hua couldn’t resist asking Mr. Tu, "Mr. Tu, is this..."

Mr. Tu instinctively wanted to answer, but at the tip of his tongue, he resisted, forcing himself to stop.

"I can’t answer this kid’s question..."

Mr. Tu thought to himself, then remained indifferent and silent.

Mo Hua felt quite regretful, he turned his head again to glance at the endless river of blood, the crimson heart, and the grand skeletal pivot above it, the shock in his heart was still hard to calm.

This was unlike anything he had ever seen, even based on his knowledge of formations, he couldn’t have imagined such a magnificent formation structure.

The Formation is vast and profound.

Its profoundness lies in the subtle significance of its Patterns.

Its vastness lies in its majestic grandeur.

He had seen even more magnificent Large Formations.

The Fifth Grade Dao Discussion Formation on the Mount of Tao was even more grand.

But to use such a massive heart as the Formation eye, such a huge skeleton as the Formation media to construct the Formation pivot, the two blending completely as if a huge Demonic Creature, it wasn’t just a unique concept—it actually was built and displayed right before his eyes.

Mo Hua sighed slightly, his chest filled with a sense of incredulity, indeed feeling the vastness of the Cultivation World and the limitless possibilities of formations.

Mr. Tu handed Mo Hua a piece of parchment, whose material was unknown, the paper had a grand and barbaric general pivot diagram.

"I’ve taught you the Blood Sacrifice Formation pivot."

"Now, according to this pivot diagram, draw the pivot I taught you seamlessly onto this... skeleton..."

Mo Hua glanced at the general pivot diagram.

On the diagram, many Third Grade pivots were crossed out and replaced with Second Grade pivots, obviously modified by Mr. Tu for Mo Hua’s sake, temporarily, to allow Mo Hua to complete the construction of the Blood Sacrifice Ritual Formation.

Although lowering grades and Patterns would reduce the array’s power, at this moment, Mr. Tu was hindered by other matters, unable to attend to many details.

"Alright."

Mo Hua nodded, prepared to start drawing the formation, but Mr. Tu stopped him.

Mr. Tu said, "You don’t draw it like that."

Mo Hua didn’t understand.

Mr. Tu seemed reluctant to teach Mo Hua, but given the situation, he had no choice, hesitated for a while, and then slowly said:

"You know Drawing Ground into Formation, don’t you."
