Chapter 2194: Chapter 1026: Duanmu Qing

The situation at Taixu Gate is quite severe.

Most cultivators in the sects, including the three Sect Leaders, feel uneasy.

Because they don’t know who they will face in the upcoming sword discussion, nor the outcome.

The opponents in the sword discussion are determined by the Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument.

After each sword duel, the list for the next duel is deduced on the spot by the Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument.

Before the Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument finalizes its deductions, everything is unknown.

Mo Hua secretly calculated the Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument.

Even, if possible, he considered using the art of heavenly secret to alter the deduced list of the Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument.

Manipulate a bit to have some "greener" teams lined up on his side.

Let Xiao Wuchen, Ao Zhan, Duanmu Qing, Shen Linsu, these top prodigies, fight and kill each other, competing for victory until their brains spill out.

This way, Taixu Gate can easily advance.

Shen Linsu and the others competing internally, at least half of the prodigies would fall in the sword discussion of Earth.

Control the heavenly secret, manipulate clouds and rain.

Manipulate causality, borrow a knife to kill.

This is the true usage of heavenly mechanism causality.

Unfortunately, Mo Hua currently does not have that ability.

The Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument is the heavenly treasure left by the ancestor of the Mount of Tao.

The ancestor of the Mount of Tao is a legendary figure within the Qian Learning State Boundary.

Whether this ancestor is an ordinary Hollow Void Cultivator, a powerhouse of the late stage of Void, or an unknown cultivator above Void, no one knows...

Such an unfathomable ancestor, an ancient treasure, tightly woven heavenly mechanism causality.

It’s fundamentally beyond the perception and alteration by ordinary cultivators.

Even Void might not be able to do it.

Let alone Mo Hua, a small Foundation Establishment cultivator.

Therefore, the upcoming sword discussion largely depends on the "face" of the Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument.

Whatever card it deals, Mo Hua must take it.

In extreme cases, if luck is really against him, facing prodigies like "Xiao Wuchen" level.

Mo Hua would likely just accept defeat and continue losing.

A day later, the Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument operates, deducing causality.

The opponent for Mo Hua’s next sword discussion has been revealed.

It’s one of the Four Great Sects, the Longding Sect.

This time, the luck is slightly better; the opponent isn’t the top prodigy "Ao Zhan."

But the luck isn’t entirely good, as the opponent is almost the strongest disciple under Ao Zhan of Longding Sect:

Ao Zheng.

Ao Zheng’s skills are almost the same as Ao Zhan. It’s just that he’s slightly inferior to Ao Zhan in spiritual root, cultivation technique, and Taoist skill.

That tiny bit isn’t much.

But in the Four Great Sects, where prodigies are numerous, it’s the difference between first and second place, a gap that can’t be crossed.

But that’s for Longding Sect.

In the sword discussion conference, as an opponent, Ao Zhan is strong, Ao Zheng is also very strong.

Mo Hua calculated with his fingers, frowning, feeling it’s not easy to win.

If fought normally, it’s likely to end up being defeated by Longding Sect like the previous round.

And coincidentally, the sword discussion is in monster hunting competition.

In the monster hunting competition, first defeated by Ao Zhan, then defeated by Ao Zheng.

Mo Hua sighed slightly.

The sword discussion of Earth has few rounds left.

It’s truly at the point where every victory must be fought for.

Otherwise, if the Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument doesn’t give face next time and deals the "card" of a top prodigy, then all is lost.

But how to defeat this "Ao Zheng"?

Mo Hua, while pinching his fingers, looking at the map of sword discussion terrain, pondering over his plan, brightened up his eyes slightly once he had a plan.

However, Mo Hua sighed:

"Such a righteous person like me doesn’t want to do this, but for the sake of winning, there’s no choice..."

Afterwards, Mo Hua arranged the tactical process.

On the day of the sword discussion.

The outside is still crowded with people.

The Inner Mountain where disciples duel is much quieter.

Mo Hua went to the Inner Mountain of the Mount of Tao in advance, bringing Linghu Xiao and the others, guarding at the entrance of the Inner Mountain.

All disciples participating in the sword discussion will enter the Inner Mountain from the entrance.

Taixu Gate is no exception, nor is Longding Sect.

Linghu Xiao and the others, looking serious, lined up, standing guard by the mountain path.

Mo Hua, meanwhile, casually played with a blade of grass, practicing formation patterns on the ground leisurely.

As a Formation Master, to make progress in formations, one should practice formation patterns anytime, anywhere, without wasting any time or opportunity.

This is the self-cultivation of a Formation Master.

After practicing for a bit, Mo Hua suddenly discarded the grass root in hand, looked up, and said: "They’re here."

Linghu Xiao and several others immediately tensed up.

After a moment, indeed from the other end of the mountain path came a team of cultivators, each imposing, eyes proud, wearing the luxurious Taoist robes of Longding Sect.

The leader, with a square face, an awe-inspiring presence, every move, blood and qi condensed as if substantial, body refinement surging.

This person is Ao Zheng.

Today’s sword discussion is between Longding Sect and Taixu Gate.

The two sides are openly opposing each other.

Ao Zheng leading the team, upon meeting Mo Hua and the others, his gaze immediately sharpened, deep battle intent revealed.

And, of course, a hint of concealed killing intent.

Mo Hua, as he stood up, instantly switched his expression from friendly and relaxed to indifferent and arrogant.

Standing on the steps, looking "down" at Ao Zheng.

Otherwise, with his height, he could only reach Ao Zheng’s shoulders, unable to intimidate Ao Zheng.

Ao Zheng indeed was "shocked" for a moment.

Mo Hua, the "Formation Leader" of Taixu Gate, has always been quite mysterious, his actions tinged with cunning and unpredictability.

Additionally, Mo Hua’s expression was cold at this moment.
