Chapter 2215: Chapter 1032: Qilin Blood

At the same time, various cultivators who learned the list of sword discussions had mixed feelings.

Some were relieved, some gloating, some sighed in dismay, lamenting Taixu Gate’s unfortunate run, misfortune on luck, even the only chance couldn’t be grasped.

Taixu Gate, Sect Master’s chamber.

"Why is it precisely Shen Linsu..."

The sect leaders of Three Mountains gathered together, their faces not looking good.

For these last two rounds, a bit of good luck, avoiding those few strong enemies, would give a great chance of victory.

But unexpectedly, what you fear will come.

The opponent, so happens to be the most tricky one.

"Our ancestors aren’t blessing us..."

Chongxu Sect Leader sighed lightly.

Taia Sect Leader pondered, "Do you think there’s a chance to win?"

Chongxu Sect Leader glanced at him, "You’re a sect leader, don’t you know the details of Shen Linsu?"

Taia Sect Leader’s gaze was startled, silent and speechless.

Chongxu Sect Leader said softly: "Qianxue Four Talents, logically speaking, are not on the same level as ordinary disciples."

"Superior-grade spiritual root, superior-grade cultivation method, extreme spiritual power cycle."

"The talent for Tao cultivation, heritage and resources, the gap is too vast."

"These are already outstanding as they are."

"Yet this generation’s Four Talents, compared to the past, are actually more extraordinary..."

Chongxu Sect Leader frowned slightly, slowly said: "They each have flowing, secretive bloodlines..."

As soon as the word bloodline was mentioned, the atmosphere turned a bit heavy.

Chongxu Sect Leader paused, sighed, continued:

"The Duanmu family’s Colorful Phoenix Bloodline, the Ao family’s Half-step Dragon Vein, the Xiao family’s Dustless Sword Bloodline, the Shen family’s Kirin Bloodline..."

"In previous Four Talents, no, even in the entire Qianxue state boundary, having one disciple awaken a gifted bloodline was already remarkable."

"But this generation, there are four of them... previously, this was unimaginable."

"This is also why the Four Great Sects have such confidence."

"Bloodlines, this kind of thing, the Taoist Court is reluctant to show, aristocratic families are veiled in secrecy, yet it truly exists."

"There are people in this world, born superior."

"They are born, blessed by the Heavenly Dao, with extraordinary bloodlines, gradually awakened through cultivation..."

"In a sense, these people might not even be considered ’human’."

"These individuals carrying bloodlines, if cultivating spells, they break through the firmament; if cultivating body techniques, their flesh remains intact; if cultivating swordsmanship, their sword heart reaches immortality..."

"These bloodline talents, their flesh and spiritual power, compared to ordinary cultivators, truly differ like clouds and mud, separated by a Heavenly Chasm."

"More importantly..."

"Every single one of them has been implanted with special Eternal Life Runes by their clan’s Heaven Void Ancestor."

"A special Eternal Life Rune, is that an ordinary thing?"

"Even some great families, great sects’ direct lineage, only have this ’Eternal Life Rune’ allocation once every several hundred years."

"To protect their fate, ensure longevity, demonstrating the importance placed on these exceptionally talented individuals."

"Ordinary people watching sword discussions, bustling with excitement, are merely watching the spectacle, with few understanding the intricacies."

"They may not understand, but how could we sect leaders not know?"

"Wanting to defeat such top talents, how easy is that to say?"

The three sect leaders remained silent.

The higher the status, the more secrets known, the heavier the understanding of the words "bloodline."

Still, despite that, there was some reluctance felt.

Especially Taia Sect Leader, he said:

"This is a defense battle, Taixu Gate defends the city, Mo Hua from that kid, his formation skills are excellent, perhaps..."

Chongxu Sect Leader shook his head, "Can only say there’s a sliver of chance, but don’t hold too much hope."

"Frankly speaking, when you and I were at Foundation Establishment, facing such talents as Shen Linsu, we’d hardly win."

"Indeed, it’s too difficult for these children..." Taixu Sect Leader lamented, "Let these children do their best."

Taia Sect Leader also nodded, "Winning would be good, losing... just accept it..."

"The ancestors don’t bless us, it’s not that the disciples don’t try..."

...

Taixu Gate, Disciple’s Residence.

Mo Hua looked at the list of sword discussions, after contemplating, also sighed lightly:

"Too unfortunate..."

In the Earth division sword discussions, Qianxue Four Talents, Heavenly Sword Sect Xiao Wuchen, Longding Sect Ao Zhan, Wanxiao Sect Duanmu Qing, he’s fought them all, not winning a single round.

Now came Shen Linsu from Qian Taoist Sect.

This time he’s truly come across all of Qianxue Four Talents.

Luck isn’t just bad.

Sword discussions are inherently challenging, yet his difficulty level is "hellish."

In Heaven division sword discussions, such treatment isn’t strange.

But in Earth division sword discussions, these opponents "assigned" to him, it’s indeed absurd.

"That Dao Discussion Heaven Instrument, could it have been manipulated, specifically targeting me?"

Mo Hua muttered with a frown.

Of course, considering these things now is meaningless.

The key is tomorrow’s sword discussion.

How to fight?

Really able to defeat Shen Linsu?

Mo Hua frowned, pondering deeply.

Shen Linsu, he’s only met once, purely judging from intuition, he knows his qualifications and cultivation are extraordinary.

To battle Shen Linsu will certainly be extremely difficult.

But this still isn’t the biggest problem.

The biggest problem is, even if he defeats Shen Linsu, what next?

Defeating Shen Linsu, then winning another round, then with good luck, one of the Four Great Sects loses two consecutive rounds.

Taixu Gate, thus barely enters the top four in sword discussions.

And then what?

What to do with the Heaven division sword discussions?

Judging by current advancement rates, within Taixu Gate, there are few disciples who can advance to the Heaven division rounds.
