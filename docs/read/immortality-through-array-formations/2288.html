Chapter 2288: Chapter 1055: Mr. Tu

Outside Yanluo Mountain.

A group of Heavenly Pride Disciples survived the ordeal and prepared to withdraw.

Cheng Mo looked left and right, suddenly asked, "Where is Junior Brother?"

Situ Jian said, "Junior Brother told me to let us go ahead, he will come later."

Linghu Xiao frowned, "Is Junior Brother alright?"

Situ Jian said, "Junior Brother said we should rest assured, he will be fine."

Linghu Xiao nodded, "Let’s wait for a while for Junior Brother."

"Alright."

"When Junior Brother comes out, we’ll return to the Sect together."

A group of Taixu Gate disciples gathered outside the encampment of Taoist Court, waiting to join Mo Hua, but left and right, they couldn’t see that familiar figure.

Just then, a rumbling sounded in the distance, the mountain peak collapsed, evil qi spread, and the entrance to Yanluo Mountain’s Evil Path Great Formation was sealed again.

And they still didn’t see their Junior Brother.

Everyone’s face changed drastically.

"Junior Brother didn’t come out!"

"Is he still inside that evil demon mountain?"

Everyone exchanged looks, their expressions extremely heavy.

Situ Jian frowned and pondered, just when he lifted his eyes, he saw Gu Changhuai in the distance, looking solemn, and immediately stepped forward and asked:

"Supervisor Gu, where is our Junior Brother?"

Linghu Xiao, Cheng Mo, and other Taixu Gate disciples all looked at Gu Changhuai, eyes bright with hope.

Gu Changhuai suddenly deeply realized that Mo Hua, this child, truly held an extraordinary "status" in the hearts of Taixu Gate disciples.

Gu Changhuai sighed, "Mo Hua... he stayed inside the formation."

Everyone expressed shock, "Supervisor Gu..."

"Mo Hua... he wants to save someone."

"Save whom?"

Gu Changhuai shook his head.

Situ Jian and the others vaguely guessed that this was a secret of the Taoist Court, not convenient to disclose, and they weren’t in a position to ask more.

But their Junior Brother disappeared...

"Why don’t we... also go back and help Junior Brother?"

Situ Jian shook his head, calmly said, "The entrance has collapsed, the formation is sealed, we cannot go back."

"There are so many demon monsters as well..."

"Also, going back won’t help Junior Brother, we’re unfamiliar with the matters of the formation, we’d only be a burden..."

The Taixu Gate disciples all had somewhat downcast expressions.

Gu Changhuai then said, "You... go back to the Sect first, Mo Hua’s safety is our responsibility at the Taoist Court."

They had a hard time rescuing these Heavenly Pride Disciples and couldn’t let anything happen to them again.

Linghu Xiao resolutely shook his head, "I will wait here, whenever Mo Hua comes out, we will return to the Sect together."

Other Taixu Gate disciples also solemnly nodded:

"If Junior Brother doesn’t come out, we won’t leave!"

"If we return to the Sect, we’ll return together!"

Gu Changhuai’s expression was moved, no longer insisted.

Meanwhile, disciples from other major sects started leaving one after another, but halfway, someone suddenly asked:

"Why isn’t Taixu Gate leaving?"

"It seems they are waiting for Mo Hua..."

"Where is Mo Hua?"

"It seems he is still inside the formation?"

Everyone’s expression changed, "Still inside the formation?"

"I just overheard them talking with the Taoist Court Supervisor, saying Mo Hua is staying to save someone, so he hasn’t come out yet..."

All Sect Heavenly Pride disciples stopped in their tracks.

Mo Hua had already saved them.

Now that they were out, but Mo Hua remained in the ominous and dreadful Evil Path Great Formation, to save lives...

The impression of Mo Hua in their hearts suddenly became somewhat blurred.

Although his mouth was a bit vicious, the attitude was arrogant, the means were sly, his actions were quite infuriating, but he indeed single-handedly united the disciples of various sects, successfully allowing everyone to escape the Demon’s Cave, now even staying alone in the cave, "giving up oneself for others," to save more people...

This Mo Hua, doesn’t seem to be a "villain"?

Perhaps, quite the opposite?

Everyone’s expression was silent.

Remaining anger, hatred, gratitude, admiration, shame, and various emotions intertwined, echoing in their hearts, indescribably.

The Longding Sect’s Ao Zheng was the first not to leave, said in a deep voice, "I will also stay."

Shen Cangfeng furrowed his brow, but said nothing.

Xiao Ruohan stood coldly, unmoving, evidently unwilling to leave alone.

As the Heavenly Pride of the Four Great Sects, their sense of self-respect was stronger.

The Mo Hua who they resented helped them, yet they turned their back and left, this would make them despise themselves from the heart.

Therefore, they stayed, not for Mo Hua, but for their own "Taoist Heart," for their belief in Tao cultivation, for their open-hearted honor.

They didn’t want to be "cowards who flee the battle."

Among the Eight Great Gates, the Vajra Sect’s Shi Tiangang slowly said:

"Mo Hua has done a favor to our Vajra Sect, we also will wait."

The Vajra Sect’s group of Heavenly Pride nodded.

In other sects, Qin Cangliu from Sever Gold Sect, Lu Zhenlong from the Purple Mist Sect, Feng Zichen from the Carefree Sect all stayed.

Song Jian from Sever Gold Sect genuinely worried about Mo Hua, but superficially carried a trace of cold smile, pretending to reluctantly "follow the crowd."

Ye Zhiyuan from Daluo Sect, ever since being ridiculed by Mo Hua for "flying sword can’t fly far," went into depression all the way.

He also didn’t want to leave at this moment.

Mo Hua was the opponent he considered, sooner or later he planned to personally ride the Daluo Flying Sword to defeat Mo Hua, a way to clear his disgrace.

Before that, he didn’t want Mo Hua to die first.

The leading Heavenly Pride didn’t leave, naturally, other sect disciples stayed as well.

At this time, the Heavenly Pride of the Four Sects and Eight Gates Twelve Streams, after escaping the Blood Sacrifice Array, all gathered outside Yanluo Mountain, silently waiting.

They harbored different intentions, some for the deep camaraderie of fellow disciples, some for the pride of being Heavenly Pride, some with gratitude, some harbored resentment, some just following the masses...

But no matter the reason, at this moment, the celestial geniuses of each sect within Qian Learning State Boundary, all looked towards Yanluo Mountain, silently waiting for that figure...
