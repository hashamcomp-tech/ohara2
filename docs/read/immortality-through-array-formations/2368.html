Chapter 2368: Chapter 1081: Gui Tao_3

In the corner of the altar, there is also a massive Demon Monster egg.

This is the fourth Fetal Demon Egg.

Born alongside the Evil God True Embryo, there are four powerful Fetal Demon Eggs.

Three of them have already hatched, experienced a Divine Thought battle, and were then crushed by Mo Hua, leaving only the one before him.

Strangely, the Evil God has emerged, yet this Fetal Demon is still incubating its egg.

Moreover, after the intense battle just now, where Divine Thought surged like a storm, sweeping in all directions, this Fetal Demon Egg was not annihilated.

True Man Situ, Aunt Wan, and Yu Er were unscathed because Mo Hua deliberately controlled the scope of the slaughter.

The Evil God himself needed to keep "food" and his "mother" and "brothers," so he wouldn’t attack True Man Situ and Aunt Wan.

But this Fetal Demon Egg received no special attention from Mo Hua.

Nor did the Evil God True Embryo care for it.

Yet it survived?

Mo Hua waved his hand, and a Sword Array appeared, condensing into a Sword of Divine Thought, ready to crush both the demon and the egg together.

But as he was about to strike, Mo Hua’s expression slightly changed, and a warning arose in his heart.

It seemed that some dreadful karma was hidden inside.

If he truly acted and broke the egg, it would bring about an unforeseeable and terrifying consequence.

Mo Hua’s pupils slightly contracted.

"Who left behind this dark seed?"

"Who is it?"

"The Great Wilderness Evil God?"

"Other deities?"

"Or some... unfathomable cultivator with great ability?"

Not good...

Mo Hua’s gaze slightly focused, observing this blood-red interwoven, almost broken Fetal Demon Egg, and after a moment, he silently withdrew his gaze, keeping the Sword of Divine Thought in his hand.

"Forget it, I can’t afford to provoke it."

The karma within is indistinct to him, so it’s best not to get involved.

Anyway, his immediate objectives have been achieved. The best strategy now is to retreat, as lingering might provoke an unexpected turn of events.

Mo Hua pointed a finger, and under golden light, first sent Yu Er’s Divine Soul through the fissure, to leave the nightmare and return to her own body.

Then, Mo Hua also left the nightmare, returning to his own body.

The nightmare of the Evil God began to collapse bit by bit.

And that last Fetal Demon Egg, in the absence of anyone, slowly cracked open, revealing a black, obscure... Deceit Pattern.

But all of this was unknown to anyone...

...

Leaving the nightmare, Divine Thought returned, going back to his Sea of Consciousness, Mo Hua opened his eyes.

But in the next instant, his face changed, and a thick black Evil Qi surged from his Divine Soul, mottled evil patterns covered Mo Hua’s cheeks and spread step by step, crawling over his entire body.

Unable to suppress the Evil Qi any longer, Mo Hua opened his mouth and spat out viscous black blood.

Not only that, but black blood also started flowing from his seven orifices.

All the black blood pooled on the ground and, after reconsolidating, revealed the evil face of a child, followed by ram horns, a human body, and scale armor... finally forming the likeness of the Great Wilderness Evil God True Embryo.

The Evil God True Embryo sneered at Mo Hua, saying:

"The Evil God is undying and indestructible, I told you, you can’t kill me."

Mo Hua coughed several times, completely coughing out the black blood inside him, then slowly got up, looked at the Evil God, and nodded:

"I told you, I know."

The Evil God True Embryo’s face changed, and in the next moment, only felt a flash of golden light around him, and with Divine Lock Formation, like chains, securely locked him in place.

"Divine Formation?"

The Evil God True Embryo stared incredulously at Mo Hua, unable to comprehend how, in the mere twenty-odd years since his birth, Mo Hua could have learned so many Dao of the Divine spells.

But Mo Hua paid him no mind, solely focused on forming more Divine Arrays, surrounding the Great Wilderness Evil God True Embryo, locking him tightly.

This Evil God True Embryo, having undergone premature birth through a Caesarean section, was inherently weak.

Not long after birth, he underwent a calamity of fierce battles.

Moreover, he was severely wounded by Mo Hua, using the Taixu Divine Thought Slash God Sword technique, making it impossible to break free of Mo Hua’s Divine Tao Formation.

At least not for the time being.

While binding the Evil God True Embryo, Mo Hua pondered internally about how to handle this fledgling Evil God.

The first question is whether he is truly the personification of the Great Wilderness Evil God?

According to reason, Mr. Tu spent so much effort, constructed such a huge Blood Sacrifice Formation, and what was hatched shouldn’t be fake.

Moreover, this Evil God True Embryo is stronger than any similar Evil God entity Mo Hua has previously encountered.

He indeed possesses a thread of True God’s will.

But to assert he is equivalent to the Great Wilderness Evil God, Mo Hua feels a bit of discord.

Mo Hua cupped his chin, pondered for a long time, and suddenly realized that his understanding of an "Evil God" might have some misconceptions.

"The Evil God divides into thousands, is undying and indestructible..."

That means every split Evil God Embryo bears part of the Evil God’s origin will but also has its own consciousness.

The evil embryo at the Dragon King Temple was such.

The evil embryo at the bottom of the Solitary Mountain tomb also the same.

And here, this Evil God True Embryo born through the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Array, using Yu Er’s Karma Dragon vein as the host, might also follow the same Evil God Law.

It’s just that the origin will it carries is stronger.

In comparison to other evil embryos, its lineage is more legitimate.

It’s just like...

Mo Hua furrowed his brow, then had an epiphany.

"The Evil God proper is like the emperor, while the evil embryos are like ’Princes’.

"There is only one emperor, but there can be many princes."

"Every prince has the potential to become the ’emperor’. Likewise, all evil embryos have the potential to transform into ’Evil Gods’."

"Ultimately, which prince ascends the throne, becomes emperor, which evil embryo attains the Divine Position, it’s all uncertain."

The struggle among princes, the victor becomes the king; the struggle among evil embryos, the victor becomes a deity.

This is akin to "raising Gu", where the most resilient survivor after all the changes and karma, becomes the emperor, the true Evil God.

"Is this... the Law of the Evil God’s division into thousands?"

"And indeed the reason for the Evil God’s undying nature?"

At this thought, Mo Hua’s heart skipped a beat, suddenly recalling his fearsome Uncle:

"Uncle, in his wisdom, cultivates Gui Tao, with Divine Thought dividing into thousands, isn’t it... this very path, I suspect..."
