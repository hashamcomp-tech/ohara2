Chapter 2286: Chapter 1054: Fierce Battle (Part 3)

"Indeed, got taken advantage by these little brats, damn it..."

They don’t know how these sect brats found the flaw to infiltrate this hall, but the Taoist Court is attacking tightly, the situation is extremely urgent, and there’s no chance to delve into it.

"Capture these heavenly talents, force back those Taoist Court dogs!"

"If the Taoist Court doesn’t retreat, we’ll kill these little punks one by one!"

Ao Zheng, Shen Cangfeng, Xiao Ruohan, and others were furious upon hearing this.

They are all favored children of heaven, highly esteemed since childhood, when have they ever been called "little punks"?

Each of them was filled with anger at this moment, wanting to rush forward and fight, but Mo Hua rebuked them:

"Hold the formation, don’t forget what I just said, focus on defense, don’t think about killing!"

"These demon path bastards are destined to die sooner or later, not worth dirtying our hands..."

Ao Zheng and the others halted.

The Demon Golden Core, upon hearing this, looked at Mo Hua with venomous eyes and sneered:

"Little brat, such big talk."

He then opened his mouth wide, revealing fangs, spewed foul breath, summoned a White Bone Lantern magic treasure, protected himself, and transformed into a gust of Yin wind, attacking Mo Hua.

But Mo Hua sat at the deepest part of the hall, and before him were heavenly talents from the Four Sects and Eight Gates Twelve Streams.

Ao Zheng let out a Dragon Roar, a punch carrying Dragon Qi, hit the body of the Demon Golden Core.

It was only a moment, but it interrupted the Yin Wind Escape Skill of the demon cultivator.

Afterward, Xiao Ruohan’s sword Qi, One Sword from Heaven, incredibly sharp, struck the back of the Demon Golden Core.

Then came Shen Cangfeng, with the powerful Cangfeng Sword Qi, and the secret skill of Wanxiao Sect’s Five Elements circulation.

Further ahead were Sever Gold Sword, Gui Water Sword, Lingxiao Sword, Carefree Sword, Purple Mist Skill, Daluo Flying Sword Control of the Eight Great Gates, and eighteen Vajra Sect heavenly talents like bronze and iron casts.

The five brothers of Tai’a, like five fierce door gods, guarded Mo Hua.

Linghu Xiao’s Chongxu Sword True Art was also ready.

The Four Sects and Eight Gates, hundreds of talented disciples, each sect’s superior Daoist magic, intertwined as a heavenly talents "Great Wall", all guarding before Mo Hua, even this Demon Golden Core, with unpredictable cultivation and terrifying Taoist skill, was unable to move an inch for the moment.

Getting close to Mo Hua was impossible, let alone killing Mo Hua.

This Demon Golden Core immediately realized something was wrong.

If these heavenly talents were separate formations, no matter how high their talent or good inheritance, in front of his ruthless Golden Core great demon cultivator, they’d be fish on the chopping block, easy to slaughter.

But strangely, these heavenly talents from the Four Sects and Eight Gates Twelve Streams could abandon their sect views and unite as one "iron board", advancing and retreating together, which was incredibly troublesome.

Who has such a background and means to gather these unruly geniuses together?

The Demon Golden Core looked at Mo Hua in the depths of the crowd, a hint of dread in his bloodthirsty eyes.

"Kill!"

He dared not delay any longer and ordered all evil cultivators and demon cultivators to start attacking Mo Hua and the others together.

But Mo Hua had already arranged the formation, and the ground was full of formations drawn.

These heavenly talents trapped and killed by Mo Hua using formations in the Shura Sword Discussion, were now under Mo Hua’s arrangements, with the "assistance" of his formation, engaged in fierce combat with numerous demon cultivators.

Everyone felt both complex and relieved.

Mo Hua’s formation was strong, and under the formation’s support, the already exceptionally talented Qianxue’s genius had even greater strength.

Linghu Xiao and others even donned Five Elements Source Armor, borrowing Five Elements boost, unleashed powerful spiritual power, killing demon cultivators in a rout.

Only the Demon Golden Core, relying on major realm suppression, could directly contend against the joint efforts of these heavenly talents.

But that’s all.

Even though he is a Golden Core, for the moment, he couldn’t find a breakthrough from these "iron board" heavenly talents and bite off a piece.

Mo Hua would never give him this chance.

Defend without killing, this was the rule he set beforehand.

Thinking about killing leads to rashness, rashness leads to exposure to flaws, once a flaw is revealed, someone will die.

On the contrary, as long as they defend well, wait for Taoist Court support, none of them would die, none of these demon cultivators would escape either.

And everything indeed went as Mo Hua expected.

No matter how madly the demon cultivators attacked, they couldn’t breach the defense set by Mo Hua.

In fact, because of reckless attacks, quite a few demon cultivators died under Linghu Xiao’s sword Qi.

As time passed, the Taoist Court reinforcements finally arrived.

Yanluo Mountain’s Evil Formation faced "internal strife and external threat".

And these demon cultivators faced "attack from both front and rear".

Dozens of Taoist Court Golden Cores took the lead, charged into the demon path ranks, and started a mass slaughter.

And that Demon Golden Core, although with deep cultivation, surging evil power, and strong Taoist skill, could not withstand the siege from dozens of Righteous Golden Core, and soon was sealed by Ice Sword at the throat, Wind Blade opened the chest, stabbed to death by chaotic swords.

The Golden Core Evil Formation Masters all perished.

Thus, the demon cultivators within Yanluo Mountain were completely eradicated.

Gu Changhuai and numerous Golden Core Supervisors, seeing Mo Hua intact, along with a crowd of sect heavenly talents, all felt a sense of relief, the massive stone pressing on their hearts, suffocating them, slowly settled down.

"Uncle Gu." Mo Hua called.

Gu Changhuai gave Mo Hua a deep look, feeling both gratified and fortunate, as well as thankful, asking: "Are you okay?"

"Mm." Mo Hua nodded.

Gu Changhuai didn’t bother with pleasantries, spoke gravely: "This is not the place to talk, we should leave first."
