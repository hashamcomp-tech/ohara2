Chapter 1817: Chapter 904: There’s a Mole_4

If I were to seek out the Taoist Court or Master Xia, they would surely ask about the source of my information.

With Uncle Gu, I could muddle through somehow—not because Uncle Gu is easily fooled, but due to our friendship, Uncle Gu trusts me, so he wouldn’t question much.

But Master Xia is different.

If he were to notice anything amiss, it would be a huge problem.

Moreover, there’s simply no time now.

Mo Hua felt increasingly anxious.

Soon, in the Yuan Magnetic Sea of the Demon Sect Token, there was a sudden moment of silence. After a brief moment, it quickly boiled over again.

It seemed that the time for action had arrived, and there was no need to hide anymore.

Orders full of killing intent were sent out one after another. Magnetic Patterns appeared in waves, and densely packed messages were transmitted in an orderly manner.

...

"Everything is ready..."

"The dogs have arrived, they’ve entered the ambush..."

"I’ve got people watching ’the Cat.’ If he moves, we’ll kill him immediately."

"How many people?"

"The one named Gu, that woman, and a Golden Core from outside Qianxue State."

"The other Foundation Establishment Realm cultivators of the Taoist Court, estimated at a hundred."

"Not enough to eat..."

"Almost enough, don’t be greedy."

"Do they have an ambush?"

"No..."

"The information fed back by their own painstakingly cultivated ’spy,’ surely they would believe it."

"Seal off the retreat..."

"Don’t worry, the Leader has spoken, it’s already sealed..."

"The lookout is still watching, they’re fast, they’ve entered the city, already reached the East Gate..."

"Pull the lookout back, that one named Gu is very vigilant, he might notice."

"Pass it down, that one named Gu must not escape, the Leader specifically wants him dead."

"Order the below to start the massacre and begin the Blood Banquet..."

"Once the Blood Banquet begins and the stench of blood spreads, the Taoist Court will catch wind of it and quickly come over. By then, the trap can be closed..."

"Understood."

...

This was a highly significant operation by the Demon Cultivators.

The planning was very secretive, and the plan very meticulous, involving a large number of demon cultivators, with great strength.

Even within the Demon Sect, it was taboo, not daring to be openly discussed.

Yet, until now, this plan revealed its "true face."

Mo Hua felt a chill in his heart.

It’s over, really out of time...

The event happened suddenly, almost without warning, by the time the Taoist Court truly became aware, it was already too late.

And with the Demon Sect mobilizing a large number of demon cultivators, the Taoist Court simply couldn’t be a match.

Not only might Uncle Gu not escape alive, but even the ordinary Enforcement Leaders of the Taoist Court, along with a large number of cultivators from the Gu Family, would likely perish.

Moreover, not just any death.

It was being made into sacrifices for the Blood Banquet by the demon cultivators, dying tragically drained of blood.

But with things as they were, he had no time to set up an arrangement, nor time to warn anyone, with his capability, he could do nothing.

It seemed he could only watch, as Uncle Gu and the others marched to their deaths.

In the Demon Sect Token, Magnetic Patterns continued to surface one by one.

The encirclement by the Demon Sect also tightened step by step.

The Taoist Court’s cultivators, moving step by step into the ambush, heading towards death...

The faces of Uncle Gu, Sister Xia, along with Gu An, Gu Quan, and other familiar cultivators of the Gu Family, flashed in his mind like a Zouma Lantern.

Mo Hua felt a slight pain in his heart, worried and burning with anxiety, then, in a flash of lightning and fire, he suddenly recalled something.

After a moment’s pause, Mo Hua’s eyes became determined, and he hesitated no more.

His eyes suddenly became deep, golden light surged from their depths, as he pushed his Divine Sense to the limit, using the Heavenly secret Tricky Calculation, aiding it with the Heavenly secret Calculation, with strong Divine Sense Calculation Power, he manipulated the Thunder Magnetic Miniature of Returning to the Source, and in an instant, sent a "anonymous" message to all demon cultivators involved in the ambush:

"There’s a traitor, abort the plan."
