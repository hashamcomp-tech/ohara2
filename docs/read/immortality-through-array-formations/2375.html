Chapter 2375: Chapter 1084: Holding the Emperor Hostage

"Four Feather Transformations... What should we do?"

Mo Hua’s mind turned rapidly.

If it were just Mr. Tu, he could still use the time he had spent to understand Mr. Tu, leveraging Mr. Tu’s loyalty to the "Master of the Great Wilderness," Mr. Tu’s confusion about the situation, and a bit of Planting Devil in the Taoist Heart to "deceive" him a little.

But now, three more Feather Transformations arrived.

A traitor from the Shangguan Family, once a Powerful Elder, a Corpse Demon from Yin Corpse Valley that raises Golden Corpses, and a Featherization Old Demon from the Demon Sword Sect, the situation suddenly became much more complex and dangerous...

Mo Hua’s gaze slightly narrowed. Using his Divine Sense to control his facial expression, he put on a calm facade while racking his brain to come up with words of "persuasion."

On the other side, as Shangguan Wang and the others entered the grand hall, their pupils shrank instantly, and their expressions turned somber.

Inside the hall, blood was splattered like ink.

The Golden Core Demons from various sects were all dead or injured, and looking around, it was all mutual slaughter, devouring each other’s flesh and dying, in an extremely miserable state.

It was obviously caused by a ruthless "Great Demon."

The Feather Transformation from Yin Corpse Valley immediately showed a cold expression, suppressing his anger, with eyes filled with a dead silent killing intent, glaring at Mr. Tu:

"Mr. Tu... it’s only been a few hours, why have my Golden Core disciples from Yin Corpse Valley all died tragically?"

"Do you... owe me an explanation?"

His anger and killing intent influenced the gold coffin beside him, from which a strong Corpse Qi emanated, accompanied by the grating sound of the Golden Corpse scratching the coffin.

On the other side, the Featherization Old Demon from the Demon Sword Sect also activated the Demon sword, murderous intent locked onto Mr. Tu, and sneered:

"My Demon Sword Sect, it’s rather difficult to raise some Sword Slave disciples, especially Golden Cores. Now, not one is left. Mr. Tu, don’t you also owe me an explanation?"

Shangguan Wang’s expression was indifferent, watching coldly.

Being a traitor now, with no Demon Gate and no disciples, it was none of his business, so he didn’t want to get involved.

Mr. Tu’s face also looked grim.

Clearly, he was also plotted against, the ritual disrupted, the Soul Lamp extinguished, and his body nearly devoured, yet suddenly, these troubles were being attributed to him.

"These Demon Cultivators’ deaths have nothing to do with me."

Mr. Tu coldly declared.

The Yin Corpse Valley’s Feather Transformation sneered, obviously unwilling to believe Mr. Tu’s words alone.

The Golden Corpse within the gold coffin began to howl excitedly, about to break free from the coffin.

The Featherization Old Demon from the Demon Sword Sect also opened the Sword Box, from which black eyeballs sprouted on the Demon sword.

They were ready to kill Mr. Tu.

Mr. Tu furrowed his brows tightly.

If it were usual times, he wouldn’t fear these two Old Demons, but now his Divine Thought was countered, his body devoured, his Vital Energy greatly damaged, truly not necessarily a match for these two Featherization Old Demons.

Moreover, the most troublesome person now was not these two Demon Path Feather Transformations, but...

Mr. Tu’s tone eased slightly, patiently explaining:

"The ritual had a flaw, I was also ambushed. These fellow Demon Cultivators’ deaths were not by my hand; the real culprit is someone else..."

"Who?"

Mr. Tu looked up towards the top of the altar, where Mo Hua was holding Yu Er by the neck on the White Bone Throne.

Shangguan Wang, the Corpse Demon from Yin Corpse Valley, and the Old Demon from the Demon Sword Sect, the three Feather Transformations, three pairs of gloomy eyes, all looked towards Mo Hua, their pupils constricted, expressions varied.

Shangguan Wang’s gaze was sinister, with a hint of greed when looking at Yu Er.

The Featherization Old Demons from Yin Corpse Valley and the Demon Sword Sect frowned, full of doubts internally.

Matters concerning the Evil God, mysterious and inscrutable. The specifics of this, though they are Feather Transformations, they do not fully understand.

And the scene before them was even more puzzling.

This kid named "Mo Hua," they had seen before.

They knew his identity too; he was the Formation Leader of Taixu Gate, someone with a low Spiritual Root but freakishly strong Formations, an exceedingly peculiar "genius."

But the problem lies here.

Wasn’t this kid subdued by Mr. Tu?

Why is he appearing here?

Mr. Tu, a respectable Big Demon Feather Transformation, Great Witch of the Evil God, couldn’t subdue a little brat in the Foundation Establishment Stage?

Furthermore, how did he manage to climb onto the altar?

Isn’t this altar the Evil God’s forbidden ground? For mere mortals, merely touching this forbidden zone would at the very least strip them of their Divine Soul, at worst their body would explode and perish; these scenes they have witnessed personally.

Most absurdly, how is he able to clutch the neck of that kid from the Shangguan Family?

The core of this Wild Heaven Blood Sacrifice, they do not fully grasp, but nor are they entirely in the dark.

The kid from the Shangguan Family was the "Divine Fetus" for the Great Wilderness God’s descent.

A few hours ago, they also sensed a tremendously strong and evil Divine Thought fluctuation, terrifying even to these Feather Transformations, instilling a sinister belief.

Evidently, after meticulous orchestration and bloodshed, the Great Wilderness God Master had already descended upon the world.

Therefore, this kid from the Shangguan Family was the physical vessel for the Great Wilderness God Master’s descent, making him the "Divine Lord" in flesh right now.

Yet now, the "Divine Lord" of the Great Wilderness is being choked by a Foundation Establishment Realm kid.

What does... this mean?

The Divine Lord of the Great Wilderness, being held hostage?

Is someone using the Divine Lord of the Great Wilderness to threaten Mr. Tu?

This thought arose, and the two Feather Transformation Old Demons from Yin Corpse Valley and the Demon Sword Sect found it unbelievable.

The situation became instantly tense and complex.

The Old Demon from Yin Corpse Valley’s gaze deepened, questioning Mo Hua: "Did you cause the death of my Yin Corpse Valley and all these Golden Core Demon Cultivators in attendance?"

Mo Hua smiled faintly, "That you should ask Mr. Tu..."
