Chapter 1874: Chapter 922: Dao and Grade_3

Fortunately, this Formation itself was designed according to specifications by others, and he only needed to simply activate it, so the difficulty wasn’t too high.

And the Thunder Magnetic Array Plate did not let Mo Hua’s Spirit Stones down.

After 30 minutes, it trembled slightly, emitting a pale blue thunder magnetic glow. A wisp of Secondary Thunder Flow with clear "instructions" seeped into the Guest Elder Token along the Array Plate, connecting to a small patch of Fixed Magnetic Patterns.

With Mo Hua’s strength, he could not fully activate this Thunder Magnetic Array Plate. This plate could only achieve this much.

But this was enough.

At the bottom layer of the Sect Token, the structure of the "Merit Vault" was the most tightly secured, with densely packed Magnetic Patterns forming a "brick-like" city, protecting the secrets within.

This was a strong Original Magnet City.

Mo Hua was not trying to break open this "city."

Of course, with his abilities, he couldn’t break it open.

His Thunder Magnetic Miniature could only "alter," without possessing "attack and defense" capabilities.

Therefore, he needed to use the "Quasi-Third Grade" Thunder Magnetic Array Plate to generate a Thunder Magnetic Flow with a "disintegration" command to open a small gap on this "city."

This way, the Thunder Magnetic Miniature could pass through this "gap," bypass the Magnetic Pattern defenses, and infiltrate the core "Merit Vault" of the Demon Sect.

The Thunder Magnetic Array for attack and defense, and the Thunder Magnetic Miniature for infiltration made a perfect combination.

The Thunder Magnetic Array Plate, under Mo Hua’s manipulation, produced a subtle Secondary Thunder Flow like an arrow, whistling along the predetermined Yuan Magnetic Patterns and striking against the "city wall" crafted from Magnetic Patterns at the bottom of the Demon Sect.

In an instant, the electric light and Original Magnet surged.

But the entire process occurred within the micro world of thunder magnetism, and besides Mo Hua, who silently orchestrated everything, no one could notice this miraculous and delicate scene.

Soon, on the Magnetic Pattern city wall solid as gold soup, a small breach was "blasted" open by the arrow-like Secondary Thunder Flow produced by the Thunder Magnetic Array Plate.

The Thunder Magnetic Miniature immediately slipped through this breach with a "zip," infiltrating the Demon’s Merit Original Magnet Library, leaving behind only a faint electric light pattern.

"Success!"

Mo Hua’s eyes lit up.

This was his first successful activation of the Thunder Magnetic Array Plate, marking the first step towards becoming a "legitimate" Thunder Magnetic Array Master.

This attempt also laid the foundations in his mind for combining the Thunder Magnetic Array and the Thunder Magnetic Miniature, complementing each other for thunder magnetic attack, defense, and infiltration.

With the Thunder Magnetic Miniature successfully infiltrating the "Magnetic Pattern City," Mo Hua began the actual work.

Generally speaking, such obvious attacks and infiltrations would be detected by other Thunder Magnetic Array Masters.

But Mr. Yuan was already dead, the Demon Sect’s Thunder Magnetic Array was unmanned, and even the Guest Elder Token serving as the interface for the Original Magnetic Compound Formation was in Mo Hua’s possession.

Playing both thief and bodyguard, Mo Hua naturally had no worries.

With the Thunder Magnetic Miniature infiltrating the "Merit Vault," Mo Hua could perceive in front of him the densely packed Magnetic Patterns, as neat and tight as "tofu blocks."

Mo Hua was now quite proficient in annotating Magnetic Patterns.

These densely packed Fixed Magnetic Patterns, in his eyes, converted into text in mere moments:

"Blood Lotus Skill, three layers, entry-level cultivation technique, absorbs the blood of others, merging into one’s own Blood Qi, capable of running up to twelve Circulations... needs Demon Points: 100."

"Blood Lotus Fruit, an Evil Object needed for cultivating Blood Lotus Skill, tempered with human blood, a cycle every three years. Demon Points: 20..."

"Ten-year Blood Lotus Fruit, nourished with human blood, ten-year-old Blood Lotus Fruit... Demon Points: 200."

"Blood Sucking Blade, Second Grade Evil Artifact, kills and absorbs blood, the more it absorbs, the stronger it gets. This is a standardized Evil Artifact derived from the Demonic Path Treasure Heaven-Swallowing Blood Blade... Demon Points: 400."

"..."

Beneath this were numerous Demon Sect cultivation methods and items of various categories.

"As expected, it’s the Demon Sect’s Merit Vault."

"Could the Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map be hidden inside?"

Mo Hua thought.

If the Four Symbols top-level Formation possessing Divine Beasts’ power—the Green Dragon Array Map—was truly hidden in this Demon Sect’s treasure vault, it would save him a lot of trouble.

Then his own Magical Treasure would have a lead.

Mo Hua silently chanted in his heart:

"Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map, Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map... it must be there, it must be there..."

After chanting several times, Mo Hua began to "search" through one Magnetic Pattern after another.

One Magnetic Pattern after another was "deciphered" into text by Mo Hua, and then recorded onto a Jade Slip.

The Demon Sect was completely unaware that their most precious "Merit Vault" was being "peeked" at bit by bit by Mo Hua.

And this blood-sucking Demon Sect, whose lineage they followed was unknown, contained numerous merit items, leaving Mo Hua "peeking" one at a time, "deciphering" one at a time, unable to scan through quickly, thus being time-consuming.

In this manner, after a full half month, Mo Hua finally "deciphered" most of the Demon Sect’s "Merit Vault," transcribing many entries.

Most of the inheritances or items were related to Demon Cultivators, unusable for Mo Hua.

A few belonged to the Righteous Dao, perhaps robbed through murder, of low grade and not rare, completely overshadowed by the profound Merit Scrolls of the Fifth Grade Taixu Gate, despised by Mo Hua.

Among them, there was no sign of the Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map.

Seeing the remaining items dwindling, Mo Hua couldn’t help but feel a bit anxious.

If the Green Dragon Array Map couldn’t be found in this Merit Vault, then the matter of his Magical Treasure would become extremely troublesome.

He would only have the option to come up with a way to kill the sect’s late Golden Core leader, who bore the Green Dragon Formation, with strength unfathomably deep.

That way, the difficulty would increase several times over.

In the Third-Grade State Boundary, killing such a Golden Core Realm Great Demon Head, while Mo Hua only had Late-stage Foundation Establishment Cultivation, truly felt like there was not the slightest clue.

"Let me take it easy... save me some trouble..."

"Give me the Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map..."

Mo Hua hoped in his heart.

But in the Demon Sect’s Merit Vault, the Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map still did not appear.

Mo Hua had no choice but to patiently keep searching and deciphering.

And so, he deciphered until the final item.

This item had the highest encryption level, and judging by its appearance, it was a "Formation," causing Mo Hua’s heart to skip a beat and making him somewhat expectant.

"Green Dragon Array Map, Green Dragon Array Map..."

Mo Hua silently chanted a couple more times in his mind, then began "decrypting."

Decrypting took some time.

Ultimately, the item name presented before Mo Hua was three characters long.

As expected, it was not the Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map.

Mo Hua’s suspended heart finally sank.

"Indeed, there’s no such thing as free fortunes falling from the sky..."

The Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map, being so precious, although Mr. Tu used the lives of so many Monster cultivators from the Valley of Ten Thousand Demons to research it, couldn’t simply fall into his hands...

Mo Hua sighed, disappointedly deciphered the last three characters, then glanced at it, suddenly freezing in place.

For a moment, he thought he had seen it wrong.

This is...

"Reversed Spirit Formation?!"

Below the Formation name, there was an annotation:

"Second Grade Twenty Patterns, an unusual Formation Diagram, containing some Power of Spiritual Power Law, extremely difficult to comprehend, effect unknown, usage unknown. Demon Points: 3000..."

Mo Hua slowly took a deep breath.

He never expected that the last item hidden in the Demon Sect’s Merit Vault would turn out to be...

The Second Grade Twenty Patterns Reversed Spirit Ultimate Formation?!
