Chapter 2311: Chapter 1062: Mystery Heaven_2

Besides, spending extra effort to meticulously study one or two Tao Cultivation skills within the Dan Formation Talisman is even more valuable.

Specialization in one’s craft.

Most of the esoteric and obscure Tao Cultivation knowledge they only have a superficial understanding of, not being able to truly master it.

Especially in the accomplishments and spell points of Divine Thought.

Divine Thought, intangible and insubstantial, elusive and ever-changing, covering a multifaceted range, encompassing heavenly secret, causality, blessings, evading disaster, seeking fortune, avoiding misfortune, divination, exorcism, slaying demons, revering the divine, and so much more, profound and obscure, vast as the stars.

Such heritage is incredibly difficult to learn, requiring painstaking dedication from youth, absorption in study; even after mastering it, it’s hard to gain renown, often criticized by laymen as superstitious ramblings to confuse people’s minds.

Nowadays in the Cultivation World, Tao Cultivation lacks heart cultivation; people only focus on Spiritual Roots, muscles and bones, family heritage, fame and power, and other external things.

Thus the Divine Way dwindles, and much of the knowledge and spell points related to Divine Thought are on the verge of being lost, to date, few forces possess complete heritage.

One such force is the Mystery Valley.

The Mystery Valley Elder Situ, although only at Feather Transformation cultivation level, due to his lifelong rigorous cultivation, holds deep Tao experience in heavenly secret and causality Divine Thought, and is quite respected by various Great Families and Sects during his travels, even esteemed by some Heaven Void Ancestors as a honored guest.

Currently, deep within the Large Formation, causality trembles, birthing massive Evil Thoughts, only the experienced Mystery Valley Chief Elder—Elder Situ can resolve the situation.

At least, able to penetrate through the evil mists, uncover the truth, and find ways to resolve it.

Naturally, within the Qian Learning State Boundary, there are some Heaven Void Ancestors who have cultivated the heavenly secret causality method.

But cultivating heavenly secret causality might allow one to seek fortune and avoid misfortune, yet doesn’t necessarily mean they can slay demons or exorcise spirits.

In the Divine Thought, one is "calculating," the other is "killing."

These are vastly different.

In fact, the Divine Sense that kills people differs from killing ghosts, demons, and spirits in each category.

The Divine Thought is extremely troublesome and harbors hidden dangers.

Therefore, these Heaven Void Ancestors proceed with caution, hesitant to act rashly.

Of course, that doesn’t mean they do nothing, just that they need an "expert" to provide guidance to avoid violating taboos.

After a while, a Heaven Void Ancestor with a clear and lean face suddenly recalled something and asked, "By the way, where’s the Xiao family Ancestral? Why isn’t he here?"

Currently, those who cultivated heavenly secret causality from all sides have gathered at the Sword Observation Tower, waiting for Elder Situ, yet the Xiao family Ancestral is absent.

The Xiao family Ancestral also cultivated the heavenly secret method.

Having more people to help is always beneficial.

"The Xiao family Ancestral seems to still be in seclusion, his cultivation seems to have encountered some issues," an Ancestor in the know said.

"Issues with cultivation..."

The clear-faced Ancestor’s brows furrowed slightly.

Not far away, Elder Master Xun’s pupils also contracted slightly, sensing something was amiss...

...

Inside the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation.

Within the flesh-blood city.

Mr. Tu was still leading Mo Hua, heading towards the interior of the city, all the way to the front of a grand and eerie hall constructed of White Bone.

Mr. Tu bowed down.

Mo Hua, after contemplating, felt that ideally, he should also "play along" and kneel like Mr. Tu to show his "devoutness" to the Master of the Great Wilderness.

But unfortunately, his bones were somewhat rigid, even if he forced himself, he couldn’t kneel down, so he decided to let it be.

Thus Mr. Tu knelt, while Mo Hua stood.

Moreover, Mr. Tu, indeed someone suited for grand tasks, did not concern himself with these minor formalities of Mo Hua, nor did he mind it.

Mr. Tu devoutly knelt on the ground, and shortly after, seemed to receive some grace, then rose to push open the hall’s door made of White Bone.

A heavy sound emanated.

The friction sound between the White Bone was somewhat unsettling, interspersed with either shrill, lamenting, or sad, fearful ear-piercing sounds.

The White Bone door slowly opened.

Everything behind the door presented itself before Mo Hua.

What met his eyes was a blood and flesh maze, filled with magnificent ancientness, profound complexity, yet laden with sinister Formation Patterns.

This was an ancient Formation.

Mr. Tu’s expression started turning fervent, his gaze filled with yearning, excitement, anticipation, veneration, and various emotions.

"This is the core of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, the flesh transformation into divinity Formation!"

Mo Hua’s expression froze, once he discerned all before him, his eyes gradually widened, showing incredulous shock.

What he was shocked by was not the blood and flesh maze.

Not the profound mysteries of this Formation.

But the core Formation of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation before his eyes... he actually knew it, moreover, had studied it, and was very familiar with it.

It was... the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation!

Stripping away the flesh exterior, the Desolate Heaven Evil God’s rebirth core of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation was... the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation!

And the bottleneck of his Heaven Yan Jue was also the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation!

Mo Hua took a sharp breath.

A wave of disbelief surged through Mo Hua’s heart, causing his pupils to tremble greatly, with an expression of uncontrollable astonishment on his face.

Heaven Yan Jue, Mysterious Heaven Great Formation, Evil God rebirth...

All of these interlinked, rendering Mo Hua’s thoughts momentarily chaotic, even faintly apprehensive deep within.

Mo Hua forced himself to calm down.

All before him had causality.

Moreover, tracing back to origins, it was all traceable.

From the start, what was engraved on the Bronze maze in the Bi Mountain Demon Cavern was part of the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation’s Patterns.
