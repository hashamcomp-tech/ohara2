Chapter 2137: Chapter 1007: Support (Part 3)

With other disciples from the sect "covering the base," his mindset was relaxed, coupled with Mo Hua’s "guidance" on swordsmanship, Song Jian had an epiphany, and performed exceptionally, winning quite a few matches at the Xuan-level sword discussion.

Although his results weren’t the best in Sever Gold Sect, he was able to rank in the top three among the disciples of his generation.

In adversity, being able to withstand pressure and perform exceptionally.

Song Jian’s performance made the Sect Leader nod in approval, brought comfort to the Elder, and made his parents smile with joy.

No one expected that the usually overlooked Song Jian could have such an impressive performance at a critical moment.

Song Jian is a member of the Song family, inherently "righteous and upright," and with the foundation of his sword discussion results, naturally caught the attention of the higher-ups and was remembered.

Song Jian became increasingly favored, gradually gaining fame within Sever Gold Sect, becoming a "genius" among his peers.

However, no one knew that this "genius" of Sever Gold Sect was supported by a "hidden hand" from their enemies — Taixu Gate...

...

On the other side, with points gifted by Sever Gold Sect, Taixu Gate suffered a setback losing substantial points, but though they couldn’t reclaim the first rank, they gradually secured the second position.

The sect breathed a sigh of relief.

Mo Hua had done all he could, in the upcoming sword discussions, there wouldn’t be another "Sever Gold Sect" giving out points.

They could only rely on the junior brothers of the sect to perform by themselves, winning as much as they could.

He also needed to focus entirely on his own sword discussion.

There were many spectators in the sword discussion, many were laymen.

But among them, there were certainly those who understood, especially the Elders from various sects.

Mo Hua knew clearly that as long as he demonstrated considerable strength, he would certainly attract attention.

The matches ahead would become increasingly difficult.

...

A few days later came another round of sword discussion.

Mo Hua faced Hundred Refinement Sect from Twelve Streams.

This is an artifact refining sect, and the first sect to truly make arrangements targeting Mo Hua.

Each of them wore a set of armor.

This is a "Fire Ward Armor."

Moreover, it’s a meticulously optimized armor to counter Mo Hua’s "Fireball Technique," combining shock resistance, interruption resistance, and burn resistance into one set of Fire Ward Armor.

There was also a helmet to prevent head explosion from flames.

This is inside the venue.

Outside the venue, beside Taixu Gate’s seating.

Murong Caiyun looked around, her gaze slightly solemn.

Having participated in the Sword Discussion Conference, and being the previous "genius" of Taixu Gate’s sword discussion, she quickly identified the abnormality at a glance.

At this moment, there were many special cultivators around the audience seats.

Most of these cultivators were above Golden Core Realm, with long-lasting aura and fast spiritual power circulation, likely Elders specializing in Taoist Skills teaching from various sects.

These Elders held a Jade Slip, their eyes focused and coldly stared at Mo Hua on the platform of Fantian Hua Ying, occasionally entering some text into the Jade Slip.

Some held a "Shadow Sketch," recording Mo Hua’s every move and performance on the spot.

Prior to this, Mo Hua did not receive this kind of treatment.

But with his strength showcase, especially after using Fireball Technique to end the Sword Dao Genius Qin Cangliu, clearly all sects started paying attention to him.

There were even people specifically researching him.

The intricacies of his Fireball Technique may not be recognized by the general public.

But its subtlety couldn’t escape the eyes of those experienced Elders of Taoist Skills from major sects.

Seemingly just casting a Fireball Technique.

But when to cast it, where to aim, speed, precision, intensity, decisiveness... the duel experience encompassed and the Taoist Skills mastery in it made a world of difference.

The simpler the thing, the harder it is to do it well and to the extreme.

Mo Hua’s Fireball Technique was far from simple as it looks, it’s something that cannot be overlooked...

The wheel of fate turns.

In this match, Mo Hua for the first time tasted the bitterness of being "targeted."

His Fireball Technique hit the Fire Ward Armor on Hundred Refinement Sect disciples, akin to "tickling."

Not only was the fireball damage reduced by half, but even the explosive impact and flame burn were absorbed by the fire armor.

Moreover, embedded within the Fire Armor was a "Fire Transfer Formation."

Hit by the Fireball Technique, part of the fire damage would be absorbed by the armor and converted into spiritual power, thereby charging the fire armor to drive its operation.

Mo Hua couldn’t help but sigh.

Indeed, an eye for an eye.

He had just used "Gold-Cutting Armor" to sanction Sever Gold Sect.

Then was sanctioned by others using "Fire Ward Armor."

Mo Hua somewhat sympathized with the bitterness in the hearts of Sever Gold Sect disciples.

Despite being arduous till the end, Taixu Gate still won.

Mainly due to Linghu Xiao.

Linghu Xiao is a "growth-type" genius, defeated by Qin Cangliu’s hand taught him a painful lesson.

After Mo Hua’s guidance, he abandoned the mind for victory and stored the will for tempering, undistracted, his swordsmanship instead advanced more.

Almost all the damage was inflicted by him.

As for Mo Hua...

Not exactly without accomplishment, at least his Fireball Technique charged a bit of spiritual power for Hundred Refinement Sect disciples’ armor.

Unfortunately, Linghu Xiao was too strong.

This bit of spiritual power didn’t help Hundred Refinement Sect much.

After this sword discussion ended, Mo Hua realized his Fireball Technique was basically wasted.

Though, not entirely wasted.

At least not very useful.

Thus, in the subsequent match against the Danqing Sect disciples from Twelve Streams, Mo Hua used Water Prison Technique.

The formation remained as before.

Cheng Mofang, Situ Jian guarded, Linghu Xiao was the main attacker, Ouyang Xuan was the vanguard, Mo Hua orchestrating the overall situation with spells for support.

Just that the support spell was changed from the singular Fireball Technique to "Water Prison Technique."

The parties clashed, charging at each other.

Linghu Xiao said nothing, directly deployed Golden Body Talisman, charged Chongxu Sword True Art.

Danqing Sect disciples were stunned, somewhat at a loss.

Is there even a sudden move and go all out at once?

They weren’t foolish and chose to flee of course.

Burning the Golden Body Talisman, dodging the Sword Qi finishing move, for a short time, Linghu Xiao wasn’t a threat.

Without Linghu Xiao as a major threat, they were almost sure of winning.

Danqing Sect disciples turned to escape.

Mo Hua’s pale hand stretched out, grabbing from the void, water light condensed into chains one after another, forming a cage, binding the disciples of Danqing Sect, one by one.

Mo Hua’s strike was exceptionally swift and precise, coupled with strong Divine Sense locks, making it impossible to defend against.

The disciples of Danqing Sect were locked by Mo Hua, with an expression of shock.

Linghu Xiao’s killing move swept through, "sending" them all off.

Outside the sword discussion arena, the audience was in an uproar.

Within Gui Water Sect, some aging, seasoned Elders with inside knowledge were visibly shaken:

"Water Prison Technique?!"

"Something from the Water Prison Sect back then... how does this kid know?!"
