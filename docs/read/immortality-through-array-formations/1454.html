Chapter 1454: Chapter 793 Tai Xu Diagram_2

Above the mountains and forests, white clouds and mist float.

Immortal cranes fly across, exotic beasts vanish into hiding, adding a touch of vivacious immortal aura.

...

"Is this... Taixu Gate?"

Mo Hua stared at the Contemplation Map for a while, feeling it’s somewhat similar, but not quite.

It indeed seemed to depict a sect.

But this sect was obviously larger than Taixu Gate; the style of the halls and pavilions seemed similar to Taixu Gate, yet appeared older and more ancient.

Especially the sky above the sect, reaching higher, with a clearer and purer aura.

"Is it the Taixu Gate of the past?"

Mo Hua pondered for a while, shook his head, and stopped fixating on it.

"Better to focus on contemplation first..."

He released his Divine Sense, sensed the Contemplation Map, and truly found no trace of evil spirits or ghosts, just as Elder Master Xun had said, it was a "clean" Contemplation Map.

"Rare..."

Mo Hua gathered his thoughts and began to contemplate calmly.

The mountain scenery, floating mist and pavilions; the ancient and elegant style of the Ancient Mountain Daoist Court; each unique element instilled serenity within.

A clear and lofty desire to seek the Dao, like gentle rain, nourished his heart.

In just a moment, Mo Hua felt that all malicious thoughts, hostile energy, fleeting thoughts, greed, and other distractions within him had been cleansed.

His Divine Thought barely grew.

But his Taoist Heart became much clearer.

"This is..."

Mo Hua’s heart was startled.

Could this be the true "Contemplation Map"?

Sitting in contemplation, comprehending the essence within the map, purifying the Divine Thought, cleansing the Taoist Heart?

Were all the ones I encountered before merely corrupt and demonic counterfeits?

Mo Hua slowly nodded.

No wonder the master previously told him, the Contemplation Map is the treasure of cultivators, a secret of large families and even the Taoist Court.

Yet also warned that the Contemplation Map harbors dangers, difficult to distinguish between real and fake, extremely perilous...

The true Contemplation Map contains a righteous essence, can enhance Divine Thought, refine the Taoist Heart, indeed a peerless treasure.

The fake Contemplation Map harbors evil spirits and corpse ghosts, can devour Divine Sense, stain the Taoist Heart, truly extremely perilous.

Hard to distinguish truth from falsehood, fortune and misfortune coexist.

Ordinary cultivators truly find it difficult to differentiate.

"However..." Mo Hua frowned slightly, "After contemplation, my Divine Sense barely increased?"

"Am I using the wrong method, or is it that our Taixu Gate’s Contemplation Map only refines the Taoist Heart, with tiny benefits to Divine Sense growth?"

Mo Hua raised his head again, glanced at the Contemplation Map, found it hard to comprehend, then said:

"Forget it, be content with what you have. It’s enough to cleanse the Taoist Heart and rid oneself of evil thoughts, one must not be too greedy..."

Mo Hua calmed his mind and continued to contemplate.

Contemplating for two hours like this, Divine Thought was clear, without obstacles, and devoid of evil and distracting desires.

Mo Hua felt it was adequate, then respectfully and carefully stored the Contemplation Map away.

Elder Master Xun had instructed him, every day for two hours, neither less nor more, just right is the best.

Listening to advice brings food on the table.

After storing the Contemplation Map, Mo Hua took out his Storage Bag, and from within retrieved a broken sword.

This was a White Bone Broken Sword, entirely white, appearing somewhat eerie.

It was precisely the broken sword housing the Sword Bone.

Such an eerie bone sword, under normal circumstances, should not be allowed into the sect.

Mo Hua pleaded with Elder Master Xun, stating that keeping this bone sword could be greatly useful, also ensuring it wouldn’t cause any issues.

Elder Master Xun pondered for a moment, then nodded, allowing Mo Hua to bring the bone sword into the sect.

The other elders could only turn a blind eye.

However, some rules still needed to be adhered to.

This sword was "registered" at the mountain entry elder’s location.

Entering the mountain might not be checked, and it could be brought into the Disciple’s Residence.

But should anything evil or bizarre happen within the sect, the first suspect would naturally be Mo Hua.

Mo Hua did not want to take the blame.

Therefore, to be safe, he added a Divine Lock Array on the White Bone Broken Sword, to seal evil thoughts, and added a Divine Mist Array, to conceal its aura.

Not giving the Sword Bone a single chance to act up.

After ensuring there were no risks, Mo Hua gently unsealed a tiny part of the formation, subtly moved his Divine Thought, sensed the aura inside the broken sword, and then softly said:

"Hello..."

There was no response.

"Sword Bone, are you dead yet?"

Sword Bone: "..."

"Speak up!"

"Oh oh," Sword Bone immediately responded, "Thanks to the little ancestor, still alive..."

Mo Hua frowned, "Stop calling me ’little ancestor’, I’m not old, nor an evil spirit, why are you calling me ’ancestor’?"

You might not be an "evil spirit", but you eat "evil spirits"...

Sword Bone silently retorted in its heart.

"Then... how should I address you?" Sword Bone whispered.

"Call me what you see fit." Mo Hua said.

Sword Bone instantly understood, feeling bitter inside.

This was a test of its awareness, seeing if it recognized the situation, had any sense of timing, and could discern the circumstances.

The current circumstances were crystal clear.

Sword Bone respectfully said: "Then, may I call you... ’Master’?"

Mo Hua shook his head, "I don’t like those two words... In this world, no one should be anyone else’s master."

Sword Bone was stunned.

What does he mean?

It was a bit confused.

Then it continued: "Then may I call you... ’Sir’?"

Mo Hua’s expression turned stern, "Are you mocking me for not growing up?"

In reality, he could grow, although not evidently, but his height indeed increased year by year.

Yet his Divine Thought’s incarnation seemed to remain at the Foundation Establishment stage, before the "Divine Sense Transformation" state.

He wasn’t sure if he could grow in the future.

Therefore, Sword Bone’s calling him "Sir" made Mo Hua very unhappy.
