Chapter 2279: Chapter 1052: Demon Valley (Part 2)

The handwriting was messy, the structure complex, and the patterns obscure, extremely abstract.

They could understand a single formation pattern, at least knowing it was a Second Grade pattern, but when put together, it was completely like a Heavenly Book, with no understanding of which line or pattern had any meaning...

"Is this the world of a Formation strongman..."

Everyone looked at Mo Hua, secretly shocked.

From the outside, everyone should be human, but surely the brains did not belong to the same "species."

At least, normal people should not, and could not understand these "abstract" formation patterns.

Even Ten Thousand Formations Sect disciples, established by formations and having been assessed as Second Grade Formation Masters, when looking closely at these patterns and lines casually drawn by Mo Hua, felt their brains tremble and their hearts suffocate.

For a moment, they even experienced intense self-doubt.

Doubting whether they had really studied formations before...

Doubting whether they had really been assessed as Second Grade Formation Masters...

Why did the formation diagram in front of them feel so unfamiliar and hard to understand even though it was obviously a Second Grade one?

"Are formations drawn like this?"

"Were the formations we learned before all fake?"

During the previous sword discussion, their feelings were not so profound.

But now, right beside Mo Hua, watching Mo Hua study formations, especially the principles of Large Formations, the impact was too strong, too brain-burning.

Many people were shocked, unable to calm down for a long time...

Like this, time slowly passed.

Mo Hua remained fully focused, studying the Evil Path Great Formation.

The evil formation of the Large Formation was omitted by him, and the structure of the formation pivot was refined little by little by him.

The overall outline, much like a tree’s branches, was restored by Mo Hua on the Formation Paper with a messy yet profound brushwork.

The main structure of the entire Large Formation also became clearer in Mo Hua’s Sea of Consciousness.

But as he calculated, Mo Hua’s brow gradually furrowed.

"Can’t calculate it all?"

The formation derived, with his current location as the pivot, and extending outward, encompassing most of the Yanluo Mountain area’s large formation structure.

But this part of the structure could not form a formation pivot cycle.

This meant that the Yanluo Mountain’s Large Formation was not the entirety of the Evil Path Great Formation.

This entire Evil Path Great Formation was larger than Mo Hua had previously imagined.

Even the Yanluo Mountain area was merely a "compound formation block" within the whole Evil Path Great Formation.

This somewhat exceeded Mo Hua’s existing experience with large formations.

It was at least completely different from the structure of the First Grade Five Elements Slaughter Demon Great Formation he once constructed.

"No wonder it’s a near Third Grade Large Formation..."

Mo Hua sighed slightly.

Even with his current gift in Divine Sense and formation knowledge, comprehension was exceptionally arduous.

Mo Hua flicked through the Evil Formation Master’s "remnants" and found a few words on an ancient formation diagram fragment:

"Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice."

Mo Hua’s heart trembled slightly.

"Is this the name of the large formation? Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation..."

These words indeed carried an ancient and immense evil aura, sounded very bloody, very ferocious.

Desolate Heaven Blood "Sacrifice"...

The focus might be on the word "Sacrifice," and such a sacrificial formation would definitely differ from other large formations, beyond what he had learned in the realm of formations...

Mo Hua’s brow furrowed tighter and tighter.

He realized he still underestimated this Evil Path Great Formation.

This large formation was larger and more complex than he imagined.

Moreover, it must hide deeper formation mysteries that could not truly be comprehended through a surface-level study, like walking through it once or reverse deducing the formation pivot structure based on the patterns...

Mo Hua patiently continued researching for a while.

The outline of the large formation became clearer in Mo Hua’s mind.

But that was as far as it went.

The existing Evil Formation Diagram collection scrapped from the Evil Formation Master was no longer enough to support him in continuing deeper research.

Unconsciously, Mo Hua thought of the four Golden Core Evil Formation Masters in the secret formation pivot room, drinking with deformed eyes.

These four Golden Core Evil Formation Masters controlled more core formation pivots and certainly had more secrets related to the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation.

"Kill them, search them, and then study the more confidential formation pivots myself?"

Mo Hua felt tempted inside, but after thinking for a moment, he restrained his murderous intent.

The Third Grade Golden Core, this was another large realm, another domain of Tao cultivation.

Moreover, Yanluo Mountain being a Third Grade territory, in this territory, Golden Core Realm Masters, especially Evil Formation Masters, are not suppressed by the Heavenly Dao. They can fully exert their cultivation and fully mobilize the evil Third Grade formation, which is rather insidious and terrifying.

Especially, since this was also the stronghold of the Evil Formation Masters.

The Golden Core Evil Formation Masters, there were as many as four...

Mo Hua dared not take the risk.

Although he currently had about several hundred Qianxue’s Heavenly Pride disciples under his command, plus his Divine Thought into a sword, he should have a chance to kill these four Golden Core Evil Formation Masters if it really came to a fight.

But the price would surely be that someone had to die.

As for exactly how many would die, it would depend on specific planning, luck, and the cultivation and means of those four Golden Core Evil Formation Masters.

The most crucial point, Mo Hua previously did not dare to boldly spy on the four Evil Formation Masters for prudence, thus not knowing their exact cultivation realm.

If those four Evil Formation Masters were only at the Initial Golden Core stage, it would not be bad.

But if one or two of them were at the mid-to-late stage of Golden Core, the outcome would definitely be worse.

If it really came to a fight, many Heavenly Pride disciples from all sects might die.
