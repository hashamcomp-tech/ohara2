Chapter 2363: Chapter 1080: Madness and Atrocity

Witnessing Mo Hua, impervious to all evil, slaughtering the dreamlike demon monsters like chopping vegetables, True Man Situ fell into deep self-doubt.

And seeing his demon monster "offspring" being massacred by Mo Hua, the Evil God True Embryo was also overcome with rage.

His figure grew taller by several inches.

The horns on his head became even more sinister.

Ancient patterns turned into pitch-black scales, covering his skin like an evil armor.

This Evil God True Embryo was growing at a speed visible to the naked eye, while simultaneously, his Innate Divine Ability was gradually awakening; black fog, blood claws, evil roar, scale armor... Numerous tactics emerged continuously, all striking towards Mo Hua.

But before the battle-hardened, experienced Mo Hua, these newly awakened minor divine skills posed no threat to him.

The black fog was dissolved by fireballs, blood claws were blocked by the Golden Body, and the evil roar was no match for Mo Hua’s Dragon Roar.

In terms of Divine Thought, the Evil God’s True Embryo had just been born, still just a "fledgling."

Whereas Mo Hua, seasoned in formations, possessed a golden body transcended by Taoism and had now surpassed the Great Dao, breaking through to the twenty-pattern third-grade Golden Core Realm; his Divine Thought form was unrivaled, with his techniques being profound and boundless.

The skills formed by Divine Thought were sharp and swift, every punch and kick radiated divine might, further complemented by formations formed in an instant.

The Great Wilderness Evil God’s True Embryo was being controlled, suppressed, bound, burned by fire, soaked by water... then further restrained by Mo Hua’s powerful punches and kicks, beaten back repeatedly until it was in a miserable state.

And even the law of his divine authority seemed ineffective against Mo Hua; even emitting the prestige of a deity could not instill fear or submission in Mo Hua.

This was a combat at a deity-level.

A newborn evil god was being beaten down, such an unbelievable scene truly manifested before our eyes one day.

True Man Situ, mouth agape, truly thought he was dreaming for a moment.

Yet...

Watching the fierce battle unfolding before him, True Man Situ frowned slightly.

It seemed that Mo Hua indeed dominated.

But his Divine Thought attacks, no matter how fierce, could only "humiliate" the Evil God, unable to truly harm the Evil God’s Origin.

This newborn Great Wilderness Evil God True Embryo merely appeared pathetic; seemingly beaten by Mo Hua without any temper, covered with scars, but wasn’t under any "fatal" threat. In fact, his strength continued to slightly recover, and the awakened divine skills grew ever stronger.

It seemed that Mo Hua, this powerful "enemy," exerted extreme pressure, forcing him into rapid awakening, evolving step by step.

All of Mo Hua’s attacks turned into a "whetstone" for the Evil God’s transformation.

If it continued like this, the situation could indeed become troublesome...

True Man Situ looked serious.

Sensing the Evil God’s Embryo growing stronger under his "humiliation," Mo Hua’s expression changed slightly, silently sighing in his heart:

"Truly worthy of the Great Wilderness Evil God... Evil Embryo is difficult to kill, True Embryo even harder."

Even though his Divine Thought Taoism had already broken through to the Golden Core Realm, the power of various Divine Thought methods nearly doubled in strength, by virtue of his own power, he could only suppress the newly born Evil God True Embryo, making it unable to retaliate, but unable to really "tear" it apart.

Moreover, this Great Wilderness Evil God True Embryo, under his "tempering," continued to grow stronger, and divine skills and techniques were gradually mastering.

If this kept going, once he grew to a certain stage and awakened even stronger divine skills, it would indeed become uncontrollable.

"There’s still quite a disparity..."

Mo Hua sighed silently in his heart.

Suddenly he trembled, hearing a faint, strange sound beside his ear.

This sound was obscure and ancient, like an archaic divine language.

Mo Hua paused, looking up, noticing the Evil Embryo using various divine skills to deal with his Divine Thought techniques while opening and closing his mouth, uttering ancient divine language, seemingly conversing:

"On you... is the aura of the True Self..."

"Who... are you?"

Mo Hua vaguely understood his meaning but didn’t speak the divine language, so could only remain silent.

The Evil God saw Mo Hua not responding, suddenly paused, ancient voice speaking, "You... are not a deity?"

Mo Hua still did not reply.

The Evil God True Embryo’s gaze suddenly turned cold, voice shocked, even carrying a trace of inexplicable excitement and fear:

"You... have surpassed the deity’s taboo, you are... a Heavenly Dao heretic!"

Mo Hua paused, then frowned heavily.

Heavenly Dao Heretic?

Me?

"Heretic!" The Evil God True Embryo roared.

Seemingly stimulated by "heretic," the Great Wilderness Evil God True Embryo’s demeanor began to distort, his innate memory rapidly awakened, the evil thoughts of his body began to enhance, an ancient and powerful aura also gradually waking...

Mo Hua’s expression changed, thinking: Not good!

In his perception, the Evil God’s infant form was growing at an unbelievable speed, evolving step by step into the true Great Wilderness Evil God.

It was growing way too fast.

Mo Hua no longer held back, immediately threw a punch, carrying majestic golden light, towards the Evil God’s head.

But in the next moment, a pitch-black hand grabbed Mo Hua’s fist.

The overbearing evil telekinesis suppressed Mo Hua’s golden Divine Thought.

The Evil God’s True Embryo had grown a head taller than Mo Hua, his hand had doubled in size, covered with ancient scale armor, fingertips sharp as claws, tightly grasping Mo Hua’s fist.

Mo Hua frowned, a trace of coldness emerging in his eyes.
