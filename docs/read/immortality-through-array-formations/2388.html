Chapter 2388: Chapter 1087: Eerie Smiling Face_4

The average Formation genius can’t even touch the edges of these wonders.

For the first time in his life, Mr. Tu felt "fear" towards a Foundation Building Cultivator.

This monster-like talent imposed an indescribable immense pressure on Mr. Tu, prompting the instinctive thought, "This child must not be left alive, or my life will end."

If such a monster were truly allowed to grow, with his cultivation rising and formations advancing by leaps and bounds, who else in this world could compete with him?

Who deserves to play with him?

Who can afford to play?

Mr. Tu gritted his teeth, cold sweat pouring.

And now, before Mr. Tu was just such a monster’s remaining "exam question."

But Mr. Tu had no way out.

Under immense pressure, under the "fear" of Mo Hua’s terrifying talent, he exerted all his efforts to remain calm, tackling one difficult problem after another left by Mo Hua.

But time was running out.

Mo Hua, this insidious "Instructor," was truly despicable, truly shameless. He had hidden the questions too deeply, and the "answering" time left for Mr. Tu was absolutely insufficient.

The danger of the Reversed Spirit Formation.

The obscurity of the Mystery Sky Formation.

The complexity of the Large Formation structure.

Mo Hua, Formation Leader, Divine Thought Formation, Ultimate Formation Master, Formation Demon, Large Formation Main Formation Master... this series of terrifying titles.

Especially, the constantly spinning, ticking, ever-counting-down Sun Dial Formation...

All made Mr. Tu feel a suffocating sense of drowning in deep seas.

His pressure multiplied exponentially.

His mental defense was also on the verge of collapse.

And soon, the countdown of the Sun Dial Formation would end. That pointer, after turning circle after circle, notch after notch, was approaching the final death line.

Time was about to be up.

But before him lay vast areas of the Reversed Spirit Formation untouched.

Mr. Tu’s "exam paper" was barely one-third answered.

"It’s over!"

Infuriated by despair, Mr. Tu’s mental state finally fell out of balance.

Seeing the Sun Dial about to tick to the end, he directly punched, shattering the Sun Dial Formation.

The Sun Dial Formation came to a halt.

But nothing happened.

Mr. Tu stood dazed for a moment, his mental state gradually calming, then he furrowed his brows.

"Nothing?"

Was this Sun Dial Formation fake?

But in the next instant, a sudden change occurred.

The shattered circular Sun Dial Formation began changing, lines shifting, forming a small, eerie smiling face.

The smile seemed to mock Mr. Tu.

And within the smile also hid a Reversed Spirit Formation Pattern.

Mr. Tu’s pupils enlarged, suddenly realizing.

This Sun Dial Formation was fake!

From the beginning, this Sun Dial Formation was just idling!

Its countdown had no actual significance!

Even if the countdown reached the end, it wouldn’t explode.

Because Mo Hua couldn’t precisely time his own departure, if he misjudged the timing or any mishap occurred midway, he himself would also be blown up.

Therefore, he wouldn’t leave a countdown; this Sun Dial Formation was merely "form."

However, absolutely do not touch this Sun Dial Formation. Because this Sun Dial Formation was actually the fuse for the Reversed Spirit Formation’s disintegration.

Once the Sun Dial Formation is destroyed, it’s tantamount to proactively "lighting the fire" for the Reversed Spirit Formation!

So, this "exam" essentially had no real "exam questions," the true "question" was only this mindset-wrecking Sun Dial Formation.

Do not touch it!

Otherwise, it will "tear the paper," forcibly triggering self-destruction!

Mr. Tu’s face turned pale instantly, his heart ashen.

Played...

Everything was Mo Hua’s scheme.

Everything was under his bizarre calculations.

From start to finish, Mr. Tu was mocked fiercely by this despicable, shameless, manipulative brat...

The strange little face on the Sun Dial Formation laughed at Mr. Tu.

And then a wisp of pitch-black, reversed Spiritual Power emanated from the eerie smile, infiltrating the Blood Sacrifice Array.

The Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation began to disintegrate...
