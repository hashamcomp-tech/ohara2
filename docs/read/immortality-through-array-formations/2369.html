Chapter 2369: Chapter 1082: Life Formation

Every Gui Tao’s person is not him.

Yet every Gui Tao’s person is him.

As long as a wily mind remains, he can be eternal and immortal.

Mo Hua shivered just thinking about it.

He hadn’t forgotten that there were deep karma and grudges between him and Uncle.

He himself was magnanimous and didn’t care much about Uncle’s difficulties for him.

But Uncle was a narrow-minded person who, if given the chance, would surely not let Mo Hua go, and if he saw or sensed Mo Hua’s presence, he would be in great danger.

Previously, he deliberately avoided Uncle, but as his cultivation realm improved, he became increasingly entangled in various events, making karma more apparent. Mo Hua always felt that somehow, the distance between him and Uncle was getting closer.

There might even be another possibility...

"I’ve actually encountered Uncle at some times or places, but I wasn’t aware of it?"

This thought made Mo Hua involuntarily gasp.

It was definitely possible, or even quite likely.

Mo Hua was somewhat fearful.

Then he shook his head repeatedly, trying to strip these thoughts from his mind.

"No, can’t keep thinking about this..."

After all, Uncle wasn’t someone he could handle.

Thinking too much is useless; not only that but it might even backfire and cause trouble.

If he keeps thinking, he might accidentally summon Uncle, and then he’d be doomed...

Mo Hua discarded these distracting thoughts, turning his attention back to the Evil God True Embryo bound by the divine formation.

Since it is a "True Embryo," it certainly differs from other evil embryos in some sense.

Other evil embryos might be a "Prince," but this true embryo in his hand is evidently the "Crown Prince," most closely connected to the Evil God Origin and most eligible for upgrading to the Great Wilderness Evil God as the Evil God Crown Prince.

At his current strength, he still cannot kill this "Prince."

Even the reason he could win, bully, and beat it was all because it had just been born, was innately weak, and hadn’t had much time to grow or awaken its divine skills and deity memories.

If he drags it out, the tables might turn drastically.

Even with Divine Thought into sword and Divine Tao Formation as armor, he wouldn’t be a match for it.

Perhaps... use robbery thunder to eradicate it?

Mo Hua frowned, his gaze revealing a hint of dangerous gleam, but then he shook his head slightly.

By rights, robbery thunder could annihilate all things.

The Evil God True Embryo should be able to be annihilated too.

But what then?

A True God Embryo contains enormous divine marrow, vast telekinesis, and infinite evil thoughts, which he currently can’t digest...

Moreover, erasing the origin will of a True God might create some terrible changes; Mo Hua was uncertain about that.

Mo Hua didn’t forget that he was still inside the Evil God’s base camp, the core of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, surrounded by danger, making it impossible for him to take risks.

And also...

"Yu Er might still hold the Evil God’s karma..."

Mo Hua’s pupils contracted as he pondered for a moment, getting a measure in his heart. Then, grabbing the Evil God True Embryo, he deftly broke open a sealed path, and followed the densely packed Divine Lock Formation to its end, entering a Divine Temple.

Inside the temple awaited his new "good friend."

A repelling evil divine beast - a Pixiu.

At this moment, the large-headed Pixiu was napping on the ground, idly whipping its tail against the floor. It looked bored until Mo Hua entered the temple, startling it. It raised its head, eyes glowing golden, instantly spotting the black evil object in Mo Hua’s hand, and appeared contemplative.

The aura of this evil object was extremely strong, its evil qi overwhelmingly dense, seemingly not a regular evil spirit, but rather like a newly born...

Evil God?!

The Pixiu froze, its large head unmoving, staring at Mo Hua in disbelief.

Moments later, its golden fur bristled, excitedly jumping up and circling Mo Hua several times, affectionately licking him with its large tongue.

The large-headed Pixiu hadn’t anticipated that while it had napped briefly, Mo Hua fulfilled his agreement with it.

Moreover, he had overfulfilled the agreement.

Mo Hua had caught a young Evil God for it!

The Pixiu was so happy, acting vividly like an "adoring companion."

Mo Hua couldn’t handle its enthusiasm, quickly throwing the Evil God True Embryo to divert its attention. Only then did the Pixiu settle down.

It placed the Evil God True Embryo in front of its chest, using repelling evil golden light, transforming the light into chains, and chained the Evil Embryo’s Life Soul.

The Evil God True Embryo roared in terror, trying to struggle.

The Pixiu extended its large paw and pressed it down firmly.

The Evil God, oppressed by the Pixiu, could not move, not even speak, only gazing at Mo Hua with indignant anger.

But this anger posed no threat whatsoever to Mo Hua.

Mo Hua was merely surprised.

He knew the Pixiu’s power to repel evil was strong, but hadn’t anticipated such power to be so strong, effortlessly suppressing the Evil God True Embryo while maintaining no detectable evil qi.

The Evil God could essentially not grow.

Is this the divine beast grown by heaven and earth, nearly akin to a deity?

Repelling evil is also an inherent divine skill law?

Mo Hua secretly marveled.

Meanwhile, a Pixiu horn suddenly manifested on Mo Hua’s forehead.

On the dragon-shaped Pixiu horn, the golden light became increasingly intense, as if the Pixiu was extremely satisfied with his fulfilling the contract, hence it offered him a generous "blessing."
