Chapter 1995: Chapter 961: Dragon Coffin (Part 2)

As expected!

Inside the Solitary Mountain, there’s an evil fetus hidden, and this evil fetus is surprisingly sealed within the dragon coffin of the Great Wilderness royal family.

But...

How does this black-robed elder know all this?

Mo Hua was puzzled in his heart.

At this moment, Young Master Xuan also asked, "Is this true?"

The black-robed elder slowly nodded.

Young Master Xuan frowned, his eyes flashing, "Second Elder, don’t just try to scare me with an illusory ’evil fetus’ title. If you want our cooperation to go smoothly, I ask Second Elder to explain this matter clearly."

"What is this evil fetus, and where did it come from?"

The black-robed elder remained silent for a moment, sighed, and a cold glint appeared in his eyes, "This matter needs to start from a once highly respected ’Shaman’ in my tribe..."

"Shaman?" Young Master Xuan was slightly taken aback.

The black-robed elder said, "A Shaman is one who serves Ghost Gods, draws Four Symbols, controls totems, commands beasts, foresees fortune and misfortune... the wizard of my Great Wilderness tribe."

"The Taoist Court killed our Great Wilderness royals, severed our Great Wilderness bloodline, and destroyed our Great Wilderness foundation. After so many years of turmoil and wandering, the Shamans of the Great Wilderness, barely one in ten remain."

"Even so, the surviving Shamans still bide their time, concealing their light to revive our Great Wilderness royal family."

"But heaven did not bless us, there was a bloody ’betrayal’ among the Shamans!"

The black-robed elder was both angry and somewhat fearful.

Young Master Xuan’s expression turned slightly gravely.

The black-robed elder trembled and said, "One Shaman, during a ritual to serve Ghost Gods, violated a taboo and awakened the ancient Evil God of the Great Wilderness."

"Under the bewitchment of the Evil God, he betrayed us."

"On the surface, he was still a highly respected Shaman, but secretly, he strayed from his original intention, betrayed the Great Wilderness, and devoted his body and mind to the Evil God, causing a catastrophe, stealing the Great Wilderness’s inheritance, seizing the Great Wilderness’s bloodline, and nurtured an evil fetus in this Divine Temple!"

"This is the first evil fetus he raised."

"He wanted to use the evil fetus to bring the Evil God descending."

"But for some reason, he failed. The Evil God did not descend, and the evil fetus went out of control."

"The traitor could only seal this evil fetus in the Divine Temple at the bottom of Solitary Mountain."

"And right now..." the black-robed elder looked at the golden dragon coffin before him, with fear in his eyes, "that evil fetus is lying dormant in this coffin."

Young Master Xuan’s face changed upon hearing this, but after a moment, he frowned, still somewhat skeptical.

However, Mo Hua, who heard this, couldn’t help but feel a surge of emotion.

Young Master Xuan was unaware, but he was different; he was deeply involved in the matters of the Evil God, and naturally understood better than anyone else.

The Second Elder’s words revealed too many secrets.

And answered many of Mo Hua’s questions.

Mo Hua’s mind was quickly turning, pondering over the Second Elder’s words:

"The traitor, who the Second Elder spoke of, the one who betrayed his tribe and devoted himself to the Evil God... is ’Mr. Tu’?"

"Mr. Tu was actually a ’Shaman’ of the Great Wilderness tribe?"

"Serving Ghost Gods, drawing Four Symbols, controlling totems, commanding beasts, foreseeing fortune and misfortune... no wonder he could master the Four Symbols Formation, the Divine Formation, raise monster cultivators in the Valley of Ten Thousand Demons, conceal the Heavenly secret for the Rouge Boat, and could foresee blessing and curse..."

"He nourished an evil fetus in Solitary Mountain, which was also the first evil fetus."

"He had even attempted for hundreds of years to use this evil fetus to bring the Evil God descending..."

Mo Hua thought of the ten thousand pits, those densely packed mining cultivator corpses of over a hundred thousand, and his heart felt as heavy as water.

On the other side, Young Master Xuan pondered for a moment, then said with a faint smile, "Senior, do evil gods truly exist in this world?"

"You better pray you never encounter one in your lifetime..."

The black-robed elder glanced at him, "Such beings as Evil Gods, if you don’t encounter them, they don’t exist. If you do encounter them, whether they exist or not no longer matters, because by that time, you won’t be you anymore."

"You’ve studied the Planting Devil in Taoist Heart, you should understand." The black-robed elder said.

Young Master Xuan felt a slight shiver in his heart, and after a moment, he added, "If it’s really as the senior said, that a dragon coffin holds an evil fetus, aren’t we supposed to awaken it?"

"It must not be awakened." The black-robed elder said.

Young Master Xuan frowned, "Why?"

"You don’t know how terrifying the evil fetus is," the black-robed elder said, "Once it’s awakened, all of us will die."

Young Master Xuan was slightly unbelieving.

The black-robed elder saw his thoughts and said solemnly:

"Don’t think just because you’ve mastered the Planting Devil in Taoist Heart, you’re proficient in the Divine Thought. You’ve never truly experienced the bizarreness within the Divine Thought, nor do you understand the immense horror within it."

Young Master Xuan maintained a somewhat cold expression, "This evil fetus is, at most, only of the Third Grade, right?"

"Only Third Grade..." the black-robed elder sneered, "Do you know what a Third Grade Divine Fetus implies?"

"This is the fetus of a Third Grade Evil God that failed to descend into the current world!"

"Once it awakens, not to mention you, even if your family elder arrives, he might not escape this Solitary Mountain alive. Even if the body leaves, his soul will be trapped here."

"What’s more, if I’m not mistaken..." the black-robed elder’s eyes filled with deep fear, "This evil fetus is likely at the peak of Third Grade, nearing the Fourth-grade realm..."

Upon hearing this, while Young Master Xuan was indifferent, Mo Hua felt his scalp tingle.

A Third Grade peak, nearing Fourth-grade evil fetus?

This is impossible!

Completely unbeatable, absolutely unbeatable.

Mo Hua’s heart was racing.

Slaying a Second Grade evil fetus already took a great deal from him, let alone facing a Third Grade peak evil fetus.

They are on entirely different levels of existence, incomparable.

Moreover, this seems to be a failed descent, and a "lost control" evil fetus, raising the danger level even higher.
