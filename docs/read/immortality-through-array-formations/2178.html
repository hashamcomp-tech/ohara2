Everyone in Taixu Gate, in their hearts, were both hopeful and tense.

Yet this was not the most troublesome part.

Heavenly Sword Sect attacked, while Taixu Gate defended.

Five Heavenly Sword Sect geniuses, with strong sword qi and astonishing combat power, attacked; Taixu Gate defended.

Anyone who had watched the sword discussion matches understood.

This was almost a certain death scenario.

No chance of victory at all.

In a certain sense, the severity of this situation was no less than the match against Xiao Wuchen.

The Four Sects and Eight Gates, and many other cultivators paying attention to the Sword Discussion Conference, took one look and shook their heads, silently noting Taixu Gate's misfortune.

The cultivators concerned about Taixu Gate truly felt a bit heartbroken this time.
