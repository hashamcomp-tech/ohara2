Chapter 2248: Chapter 1042: Collapse!

Mo Hua softly whispered the word "explode."

In that instant, the entire valley, filled densely with formations, seemed to respond to its master’s "summon," trembling and lighting up with vibrant and piercing brilliance.

These formations were all explosives.

It was Mo Hua who had continuously painted formations from the sword discussion until now, one after another, constantly piling them up.

A trace of reversed light faintly emerged.

This was the half-step Reversed Spirit Formation that Mo Hua had previously buried.

This half-step Reversed Spirit Formation was like a flame, igniting all the formations between the entire valley that were ready to explode.

A thread of spiritual power began to reverse, decomposing the Five Elements Spiritual Power into the black power of collapse.

And this thread of collapse power was like the source of a "plague," rapidly spreading along the spiritual power paths calculated by Mo Hua, with the speed of a swift whirlwind, spreading throughout the valley, like the black "poppy" flowers blossoming in succession within the valley.

One blossom after another, one patch after another, one formation after another collapsed, covering the entire mountain landscape, assembling into a terrifying force, instantly triggering large-scale formation collapse and explosion.

On top of Fang Tianhua Shadow, full of reversed spiritual power and collapsing formations, creating a terrifying black "poppy" flower.

The entire Mount of Tao was trembling.

The world was shrouded in complete darkness.

Yet there wasn’t the slightest sound, as though these collapses had even swallowed the sound itself...

All cultivators in the area were shockingly speechless.

Fear was evident on the faces of many cultivators.

Even some Golden Core cultivators were filled with horror.

This was a scene most cultivators had never witnessed, containing large-scale spiritual power reversal, formation collapse, and the annihilation of all things...

Even the sect leaders of various great sects were momentarily lost in shock.

In the Sword Observation Tower, some nobles sipping tea on the upper floors also stood up in shock, staring out the window with disbelief in their eyes.

And within the Dongxu Hall.

The normally immovable Heaven Void Ancestors, who had closed their eyes in meditation, also had to open their eyes and look at the terrifying black scene of spiritual power collapse on Fang Tianhua Shadow.

A sense of shock resonated in the hearts of these placid ancestors.

Finally, all Heaven Voids’ gazes simultaneously turned to Elder Master Xun of Taixu Gate.

These gazes were mixed with puzzlement, doubt, shock, inquiry, and coldness.

Elder Master Xun felt immense pressure, instinctively feeling a chill down his spine.

The palm hidden within his sleeve was trembling slightly.

This scene in front of him deeply shocked him as well.

At this moment, he truly believed the horrifying "feats" he had once heard of, about Mo Hua setting up large formations, collapsing them, and killing Big Demons back home, might indeed be true...

But under the silent scrutiny of so many Heaven Void Ancestors, Elder Master Xun could not afford to show fear.

He chose to ignore everything, pretending to remain calm, sipping his tea.

As if all this was just a small spectacle.

As if they hadn’t seen the real grand display yet, which wasn’t worth making a fuss over...

...

And the formation collapse, with Fang Tianhua Shadow engulfed in reversed blackness.

Between heaven and earth, all was silent for a long time.

After a while, the shocking feeling in everyone’s chest gradually dissipated.

The observing cultivators slowly came back to their senses.

The crowd, who were shocked to the point of forgetting to breathe, finally took a deep breath and murmured:

"What is... this thing..."

"Formation explosion?"

"Is this... Mo Hua’s handiwork?"

"Does this mean... that Mo Hua, using formation setups, triggered such a terrifying explosion, killing all the Qianxue Four Talents, including the top fifty-plus prodigal sons of the Four Great Sects and Seven Great Gates, all at once?!"

"Did he blow them all up?!"

"Is this something humans can actually do?"

"Utterly defying logic... horribly terrifying."

The sound of cold breaths being sucked in echoed through the venue.

Someone furrowed their brows in shock and asked, "What formation did he use? How could it be so powerful?"

This question was a bit high-level.

Most cultivators exchanged confused glances.

Within Ten Thousand Formations Sect, most disciples and elders also frowned.

Only some seasoned, knowledgeable elders whispered in near disbelief:

"This is spiritual power reversal... it’s a Reversed Spirit Formation."

"Reversed Spirit Formation?"

"It’s a Second Grade Twenty Patterns... Ultimate Formation."

Upon hearing this, the entire hall was struck with astonishment, and all the elders’ expressions changed dramatically as they trembled and said:

"Twenty Patterns?!"

"Has Mo Hua formed his divine sense core?"

"What are you thinking?" An elderly elder with white hair shook his head, "Foundation Building Cultivator, how can divine thought form a core?"

"Then this is..."

"It’s not a complete Reversed Spirit Formation," the white-haired elder furrowed his brow and said, "You might have forgotten, the theme that topped the last Formation Competition was actually a half-step Reversed Spirit Formation above Second Grade Nineteen Patterns containing spiritual power reversal..."

"If I’m not mistaken, Mo Hua learned and painted this formation at that time, integrated and memorized it..."

"So he acquired this formation from the Formation Competition and used it in the Sword Discussion Conference. In a way, it’s a form of cause and effect..."

All the elders nodded upon hearing this.

After finishing, the white-haired elder couldn’t help but sigh.

Whether it’s learning a half-step Reversed Spirit Formation above Nineteen Patterns.

Or using this Reversed Spirit Formation to trigger formation collapse, annihilating fifty-plus of Qianxue’s strongest talents.

These are truly extraordinary feats.

He had lived most of his life, teaching formations for countless years, witnessing countless formation talents, experiencing numerous Mount of Tao conferences, and had personally witnessed the birth of many Formation Leaders.
