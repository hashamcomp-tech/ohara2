Chapter 1870: Chapter 921: Four Symbols Azure Dragon (Part 2)

It’s not just the Dragon Bone, there are other issues as well.

The Four Symbols Monster Pattern of the Valley of Ten Thousand Demons, all use methods of the Evil Formation, and the Azure Dragon Divine Beast Pattern they raise is probably not a proper "Azure Dragon."

If so, putting this Evil Formation on oneself, wouldn’t that be akin to entering the Demon Path?

Moreover, this Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation, according to the Demon Cultivators, is a Divine Beast Pattern that only the true Imperial Family of the Great Wilderness is qualified to refine.

If that is the case, then to refine this Azure Dragon Formation and inherit the power of the Four Symbols Divine Beast, there should also be a bloodline requirement?

In my own body, there is no blood of the Great Wilderness Imperial Family flowing, do I have the qualifications to refine the Green Dragon Pattern?

These are just the issues I can think of at the moment...

This powerful, ancient, almost lost, unprecedented Azure Dragon Formation that even Mr. Tu needs countless Monster cultivator lives to research, if I truly want to refine it into my own Magical Treasure, I’m afraid there would be many more difficulties...

Mo Hua’s frown deepened.

"It’s too difficult, there are too many problems..."

Mo Hua was entangled in his heart, but after carefully considering for a long time, hesitated several times, he finally made up his mind:

No matter, just mark a dragon for myself!

Choosing an ordinary Core Formation Magical Treasure is relatively safe and carries no risk. But no risk means only "mediocrity."

To refine the Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation, it’s obvious without thinking that it would inevitably be fraught with obstacles and extremely difficult. But precisely because of this, it holds the value of pursuit.

"Own Magical Treasure" is cultivated in conjunction with one’s life and spirit, accompanying a cultivator throughout their life, laying the foundation for Core Formation, and is one of the biggest "watersheds" for Golden Core Cultivators.

Many noble families’ direct lineage talents begin nurturing their Magic Treasure Embryos from the age of three.

Mo Hua is already significantly behind these noble family’s sons in terms of Spiritual Root, physique, and heritage, if he doesn’t choose a better own Magical Treasure, the gap in apparent cultivation in the future will only widen.

This Green Dragon Formation, if he didn’t know about it, it would have been the end of it, he wouldn’t have any thoughts.

But since fate and coincidence made him aware, that is another matter.

A once-in-a-lifetime opportunity is in front of him, no matter how difficult, he must try it.

If he misses this opportunity, it will be too late for future regrets.

What’s more, what he plans to do in the future, whether rescuing his master or seeking all techniques and attaining immortality, will be even more difficult.

If he shrinks back now, the road in the future will only become harder and harder.

Mo Hua nodded, heart resolved:

He was going to give himself a dragon!

Find a way to refine the "Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation" and make it his own Magical Treasure!

One should do their best and leave the rest to fate, to get anything good, one must put in the corresponding effort. Effort does not necessarily result in outcomes, but without effort, blessings certainly will not fall from the sky.

Since he decided, Mo Hua no longer hesitated.

"If I want to refine the Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation, the issue of materials can be considered later, the methods of the Evil Formation can also be scrutinized again, but before that, the first thing to do is to find a way to get the ’Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map’ in hand..."

Mo Hua contemplated in his heart.

The Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map is the premise of everything.

Without the Array Map, everything else is empty talk.

And this Array Map is one of the most prestigious Formation inheritances among the Four Symbols Formations, bearing the name of the Divine Beast "Azure Dragon."

Taking a step back, even if he doesn’t refine it into an own Magical Treasure, he must find a way to obtain this Array Map.

The Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map, just from its name, is extraordinary.

"But again, how to get the Array Map..."

Mo Hua sat at the table, propped his chin, his thoughts continually swirling, "The leader of the Demon Sect, he gave himself the Green Dragon Pattern, that means he definitely has the Green Dragon Array Map on him."

"But he is the leader, mysterious and unpredictable, and is at the late Golden Core stage, coupled with the Green Dragon Pattern—even if it is incomplete, his strength must be extremely formidable, thinking about going after him is a bit much..."

"Then... Mr. Tu?"

"Mr. Tu must also have it, but he is very likely in the Feather Transformation stage, even stronger than the leader of the Demon Sect, making it even more impossible to snatch from such a powerful tiger’s mouth."

"Inside the Demon Sect, perhaps no one else knows about this Formation, even the ’think tank’ Elder You may not know any clues about this Array Map..."

Mo Hua thought it over and over, seemingly unable to find any breakthrough...

His grand plan to mark a dragon as his own Magical Treasure had already "collapsed" before it even started...

In the following days, Mo Hua was mulling over this matter.

Finally, a few days later, he thought of a possibility:

"The Merit Vault of the Demon Sect!"

The Merit Vault is a place to store Sect treasures and inheritances, just as in the Righteous Dao, so it is in the Demon Path.

The Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Map is definitely a Sect treasure, it might be stored in the Merit Vault of the Demon Sect.

But the problem is, this Array Map is so valuable, so valuable that not even the Merit Vault might qualify to store it.

Both are possible, but at least it’s a direction.

Having a direction is worth a try.

After finishing his contemplation, Mo Hua formally planned to target the Demon Sect’s "Merit Vault"—this was also an idea he had long ago when he first learned the Yuan Magnetic Formation principle:

Use the Yuan Magnetic Formation principle to alter Formation permissions and steal the resources and inheritances from other Sects’ vaults!

Mo Hua gave it a try, and unsurprisingly failed.

Stealing inheritance is not that simple.

He had overestimated his Yuan Magnetic Formation skill.

The "Merit Scroll" is a magnetic core of all Sects, any Sect, when constructing the Yuan Magnetic Formation, administers the "Merit Vault" with utmost rigor.
