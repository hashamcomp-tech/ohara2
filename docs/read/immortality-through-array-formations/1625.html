Chapter 1625: Chapter 848: Dragon Blood (Part 2)

Fortunately, he has a bunch of "henchmen" now.

Mo Hua jumped down from the beam, and Master Xiao feigned a smile and said, "What’s up there that has young master looking for so long?"

Because he and Gu Changhuai were on guard against each other, he didn’t go up to check in person.

Mo Hua said, "There’s a lair; a monster once resided here. It’s probably not far away, we can keep searching."

He kept the matter of the Dragon Blood secret.

Master Xiao couldn’t possibly know, and just nodded slightly, "Alright."

Afterwards, Mo Hua continued to lead the way.

But after circling the area once, he found plenty of traces of the monster, yet still couldn’t see the monster’s figure at all.

Just as Mo Hua was frowning in thought, his heart suddenly throbbed, as if something was pulsating.

"Dragon Blood..."

The dragon blood he had licked earlier seemed to be taking effect now.

Mo Hua felt the blood flow within him accelerating.

The sound of dragon cries echoed in his ears, his heart beat faster, his meridians were swelling painfully, and his limbs were a bit paralyzed, as if the dragon blood was gradually waking within him, devouring his bloodline.

Even though it’s just a trace of dragon blood.

Moreover, it might not even be True Dragon Blood.

But with his frail physique, he couldn’t bear it at all.

Mo Hua’s physical body was too weak, and his bloodline too feeble, this trace of "dragon" power was far beyond what his body could handle.

"I’m finished. If I’d known beforehand, I wouldn’t have eaten randomly..."

Mo Hua regretted deeply in his heart.

Fortunately, Dragon Blood is not something he can eat at will.

Earlier he only licked a tiny bit, not even a blood star, so the chaos within his bloodline only lasted for a moment and then gradually calmed.

Cold sweat formed on Mo Hua’s forehead as he breathed a long sigh of relief.

Beside him, Xia Canonical Official saw this, and his expression changed slightly. He reached out with his pale hand to lightly wipe Mo Hua’s forehead, frowning:

"Mo Hua? What’s wrong with you?"

"Nothing," Mo Hua sighed, "I ate something bad."

Xia Canonical Official was a bit confused, not knowing what he was talking about.

How could one eat something bad all of a sudden?

"Sister Xia, I’m fine now, let’s continue looking for that monster, the monster is urgent..." Mo Hua quickly shifted the topic.

Seeing Mo Hua was alright, Xia Canonical Official didn’t dwell on it, but nodded and asked:

"That monster seems to have disappeared; how do we find it now?"

Mo Hua frowned again, he expanded his Divine Sense and perceived around, yet still found nothing.

The monster seemed to have vanished.

Suddenly, Mo Hua’s heart trembled again.

Mo Hua was startled.

Is it not over?

But when he carefully sensed, he found that it was not his own heart that was beating, but another heart...

The "heart" of that monster?

Mo Hua closed his eyes, held his breath and concentrated, after a moment, opened his eyes, his expression had a hint of realization.

It wasn’t the beating of a heart.

But... a kind of sensing between Divine Souls.

Mo Hua slightly furrowed his brow.

Earlier he had consumed a trace of Dragon Blood.

This trace of Dragon Blood seemed to contain the "Divine Soul" of an ancient divine beast.

And through this trace of "Dragon Blood," Mo Hua seemed to have formed a subtle sense with the "Divine Soul" of this ancient divine beast.

He could sense the flow of other Dragon Blood within the Dragon King Temple.

In other words, he could sense... the location of that monster.

Mo Hua found it somewhat incredible.

Is there a "Divine Soul" contained within Dragon Blood?

Why?

The reason dragons are called divine beasts, could it be because their "Divine Souls" can merge with flesh?

Mo Hua was secretly shocked, but now wasn’t the time to think about those things, so he turned his head to the group and said:

"I know where the monster is, follow me..."

Then Mo Hua confidently proceeded down the corridor on his right.

The group behind him exchanged looks, unsure how Mo Hua knew.

However, Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official always trusted Mo Hua, so hesitated little and followed him.

Water Yama knew Mo Hua was mysterious, his skills were strange, so he didn’t doubt it.

Xie Liu wanted to get the Water Prison Forbidden Box from Mo Hua, so he would follow at any cost.

Xiao Tianquan hesitated for a moment, then followed the crowd.

Only Master Xiao quietly watched Mo Hua’s back, his gaze growing more surprised, feeling this young master increasingly difficult to fathom.

He was silent for a moment, blood flashing in his eyes, then quietly followed Mo Hua.

The group followed Mo Hua all the way to a small square where they were met with scattered corpses.

On the ground paved with bluestone, blood splattered like ink, like wild cursive script, spilled on the ground.

The cultivators in the scene, whether Supervisor or Evil Cultivitors, were experienced, and immediately saw a massacre had occurred here.

Moreover, it was a "massacre" that caught them off guard.

It seemed that not long ago, these temple guards gathered here for unknown reasons, then suddenly fell victim, slaughtered en masse.

The killer didn’t use spells or spiritual artifacts, relying only on sheer physical strength.

Most likely, it was the monster.

Mo Hua surveyed the surroundings, using Divine Soul sensing, then pointed at a plaque in the distance.

"Over there."

It was a plaque hanging high up in the hall, broad and thick, with a blue lacquer background, etched with ancient golden words. On the plaque were three large characters:

"Yaksha Hall."

The group followed the direction Mo Hua pointed, and frowned.

Water Yama said, "Is there a monster there?"

"Just based on feeling, it’s in that direction..."

Mo Hua said it was "based on feeling," but his tone was very certain, "It should be behind the Yaksha Hall plaque, where it’s dug another hole for hiding."
