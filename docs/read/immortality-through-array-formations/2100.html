Chapter 2100: Chapter 996: Supreme Goodness Like Water (Part 2)

The process went smoothly, and the atmosphere was quite normal.

Taia Sect Leader breathed a sigh of relief, thinking ’This child Xuan Er, although he appears rebellious usually, he shows good judgment at critical moments, not causing trouble and executing the strategy well.’

Indeed, after parting for a few days, he deserves a fresh look.

You can’t always look at people with "prejudices."

People can change and grow.

This "clan brother," who is of the same generation as me, now gets along quite well with disciples from other mountains.

Taia Sect Leader felt deeply gratified.

He picked up his teacup, about to drink, when he suddenly heard a "thump," signaling an error occurred.

Ouyang Xuan and that big guy had a tactical position mix-up, suddenly colliding front and back, each taking a fall.

The big guy got up, grumbling about something.

The expression on Ouyang Xuan’s face turned cold.

The tension rose suddenly.

Taia Sect Leader paused, thinking something was wrong, before he could react, a crisp voice rang out:

"Little Xuan, feint attack from the right wing, be careful not to collide with Cheng Mo..."

Being in Feather Transformation, his cultivation was profound, and despite the distance, he still heard it.

This sentence directly puzzled the Sect Leader.

"Little Xuan"... Who is being referred to?

Could it be... Ouyang Xuan?

With that young lad’s stubbornness, would he tolerate it?

Taia Sect Leader looked closely, sure enough, seeing the displeasure on Ouyang Xuan’s face, seeming highly resistant to this "nickname," eyes showing anger.

Clearly, he couldn’t stand it.

The next moment, with an arrogant demeanor and disdain, he snorted coldly, directly heading to the right.

Clearly, he tolerated it.

Taia Sect Leader was stunned, almost thinking he had seen wrong.

This person is Ouyang Xuan?

He can tolerate this?

In Taia Sect, even if I lectured him a little, he would be impatient; if a True Transmission Elder said something, he could directly show displeasure.

This "stubborn mule", upon arriving at Taixu Mountain, has changed character?

Taia Sect Leader pondered momentarily, deeply looking at Mo Hua, with turbulent emotions.

Linghu Xiao from Mount Chongxu wants to team up with him.

Now even their own Ouyang Xuan from Taia Mountain listens to his commands, even being called "Little Xuan" without losing his temper.

This kid might be... a "demon" who can enthrall people?

He glanced at Mo Hua’s handsome looks, bright eyes and white teeth, becoming increasingly suspicious...

This matter lingered in Taia Sect Leader’s thoughts.

Even when he bid farewell to Taixu Sect Master, left the training field, returned to Taia Mountain, and sat in the Sect Leader Residence, he remained deeply concerned.

After a while, he made up his mind, summoned an Elder, and instructed:

"Have the disciples in Taia Mountain go find Mo Hua, ask Mo Hua to help team up, train together, and participate in the Debate."

The Elder was slightly startled, "Sect Leader, this..."

Taia Sect Leader waved his hand, "Enough, I’ve thought it through, this child called ’Mo Hua’, if he can ’tame’ even Xuan Er, arranging teams for Taia Mountain disciples shouldn’t be a problem."

"Besides, I’ve seen Taixu Mountain’s training; it’s very well organized. Winning is uncertain, but it’s surely better than my Taia Mountain’s."

"Since the Three Sects Unification, there’s no distinction between us."

"We should follow along..."

As Sect Leader, he has always been pragmatic.

If someone is good, learn from them; if someone is strong, follow them.

This is called being pragmatic.

Precisely because of this, Taia Sect once "mixed" to the top position among the Eight Great Gates.

As the Sect Leader spoke, other Elders generally dared not oppose.

"Then..." The Elder deliberated, "Shall I go make arrangements?"

"Yes." Taia Sect Leader nodded, "The sooner the better, Qianxue Debate is approaching, time waits for no one."

"Understood."

The Elder withdrew, following the Sect Leader’s orders, passing the instructions.

The command spread among the disciples.

In Taia Mountain, inside the Disciple’s Residence.

A group of disciples gathered together, discussing animatedly.

"The Elder instructed us to find ’Junior Brother’, to let Junior Brother lead us to the Debate."

"What ’Junior Brother’?"

"Mo Hua, who else could be Junior Brother?"

"I know, I’m saying that..." A disciple lowered his voice, somewhat dissatisfied, "He is the Junior Brother of Taixu Mountain lineage, what’s it to do with our Taia Mountain?"

"Didn’t he also teach you Formation?"

"Taught or not, at most greet him, behind his back calling ’Junior Brother’, he hasn’t earned that title."

"Suit yourself, you don’t call, I will."

"No backbone."

The disciple retorted, "He taught me Formation, and even graded my work ’A’, saying I have great potential... If I’m not calling him Junior Brother, should I call you?"

"People should have some conscience..."

"Alright, alright, let’s talk business."

"Are we sure to go mix with Mo Hua for the Debate?"

"What else? It’s the Elder’s orders, it’s the Sect Leader’s intent..."

"Speaking of which, Mo... Junior Brother, he’s just a Formation Master, is he adept at Debate?"

"Not sure..."

"But the Debate at Taixu Mountain seems quite different. A few Taia Mountain disciples have teamed up with Taixu Mountain disciples, I asked around, what they’re practicing is entirely different from ours..."

"So, are we really going to Taixu Mountain?"

The disciples from Taia Mountain hesitated.

After a while, someone with bright eyes asked a slightly dark-skinned, somewhat petite disciple among the crowd:

"Mu Tou, you’re close with Junior Brother, is Junior Brother really this impressive?"

Ouyang Mu was buried in designing artifact refining diagrams.

These were all given by Mo Hua, asking for help in correcting and improving Formation and Artifact Refining compatibility.

Upon hearing this, Ouyang Mu raised his head.

Although still somewhat petite, having been with Mo Hua for long and having gone through the ordeal at the Valley of Ten Thousand Demons, he matured a lot, with a determined confidence between his brows.
