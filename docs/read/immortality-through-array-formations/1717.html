Chapter 1717: Chapter 876: Self-Severance (Part 2)

The divine marrow slowly seeped into the body, like an all-powerful spiritual medicine, repairing the wounds on Mo Hua’s divine soul.

And just at this moment, as if sensing the aura of the divine marrow, the evil embryo showed some disturbance, with a hint of greed.

But ultimately it didn’t dare to show its head and snatch Mo Hua’s divine marrow.

"Consider yourself lucky..."

Mo Hua snorted coldly.

However, afterwards, his heart was not at ease.

This time he gave this little lamb a lesson, but the end result was somewhat a "lose-lose" situation.

Of course, the evil embryo had lost more.

Compared to the evil embryo, he was a "small loss."

But continuing like this is not a solution either...

Although he suppressed the arrogance of the evil embryo, his own divine soul had also suffered a certain degree of damage.

With the sword tempering the soul, divine soul self-slashing, just as Patriarch Dugu said, is an extremely dangerous cultivation method.

Even he couldn’t endure it, let alone others.

Mo Hua sighed in his heart.

Those predecessors of Taixu Gate who did not walk the path of divine sense proving the Dao, nor had their divine thought transformed into Taoism, and managed to successful cultivate the "God Slaying Technique" through their own efforts, their talent was strong, their mind was firm, and their comprehension was high, all of which were admirable.

Slaying one’s own life soul is truly too difficult...

"Then should I continue to learn this God Slaying Technique?"

Mo Hua frowned slightly.

He sat cross-legged on the ground, pondered deeply for a while, and silently firmed his resolve.

I still have to persist in learning it.

Even if the divine soul is injured, I must learn it.

If this were usual times, it would be fine. He could learn it a bit later, or follow the teachings of Patriarch Dugu, proceed step by step, learn the God Slaying Technique steadily, learn it slower, without too much impact.

But now it is different.

The evil embryo is parasitic in his life soul, nourished by his sea of consciousness, still slowly awakening divine skills, regaining memory, from whichever angle one looks, it is a tremendous hidden danger.

This hidden danger must be eliminated.

This evil embryo is also forcing him to learn the God Slaying Technique.

If he doesn’t learn it, cannot slay this evil baby, in the future with just a bit of accident, letting this evil baby seize the opportunity, he could very likely be completely polluted, becoming a puppet of the Great Wilderness Evil God.

Therefore, the Godslaying Sword must be learned!

His opponent is the evil embryo, and even in the future could be the evil god.

The Taoist Stele and Robbery Thunder, although they are in his sea of consciousness, are considered "external objects."

Whereas the "Godslaying Sword," is the only technique he can actively control and can counter-kill the evil god.

His own life must be held in his own hands.

Mo Hua’s gaze was determined.

And now the only issue is the injury left behind after the life soul self-slashing.

The divine marrow can repair the injuries of the divine soul, but after these few damage experiences of the divine soul, the divine marrow in Mo Hua’s possession is not much left.

With less divine marrow, Mo Hua’s current divine thought taoism is also somewhat weaker.

The divine thought incarnation is far from being as golden and brilliant as before.

"This is indeed a problem..."

And currently, he has no means to replenish the divine marrow, and combined with being constrained by the evil embryo, needs to spend a lot of time learning formation and swordsmanship, it is even less possible to seek out altars everywhere.

Mo Hua frowned tightly.

Just then, he noticed his hands were dirty and sticky, lowered his head to see, only to find black sticky liquid on his palm.

It seemed to be the blood skin shed off forcibly by the evil embryo during its escape.

These black fluids were too dirty, Mo Hua felt a bit disgusted.

Moments later, he was suddenly taken aback, his eyes slightly lit up, and threw these filthy "blood skins" onto the Taoist Stele, the Robbery Thunder killed them.

A faint, terrifying howl that seemed like a demon monster rang out.

Afterwards the black gas was evaporated, evil thoughts were purified, and the originally filthy blood skin oozed out pure golden marrow liquid.

Moreover, this golden color was deeper and purer than any divine marrow he had acquired before, with a trace of luster.

Mo Hua wiped these divine marrow with his finger, licked it into his mouth, his eyes immediately brightened.

"So pure!"

Only a pity, the quantity was too small, he sucked it up a few times, and it was gone.

Mo Hua, still yearning, yet his mind was enlightened:

"Almost forgot, the evil embryo is the embryo of the Great Wilderness Evil God, it naturally carries extremely rich divine marrow..."

The evil embryo is parasitic in his life soul.

In other words, it’s like a "divine marrow treasure trove," hidden within his divine soul.

It’s just that he needs to find a way to extract the divine marrow from this treasure trove...

Mo Hua’s eyes immediately sparkled.

This Godslaying Sword must be learned!

Slay the evil embryo, and there will be good things to savor!

But how to slay?

Mo Hua pondered for a moment, gradually forming a plan in his heart.

The Godslaying Sword, to some extent, is also an "act of self-harm," hence it must not be rushed, slay one sword a day to avoid too much burden on the divine soul and too severe injury.

Just slay this one sword a day.

Use this "one sword to temper the divine, self-slash life soul," to understand Godslaying Sword Intent, cultivate Godslaying Sword Forms.

Day after day, water drops stone wear.

Sooner or later, he can completely kill this evil embryo!

And if he can, relying on his own strength, with his divine thought, honor Taixu’s sword intent, and slay this evil embryo, it would in some sense mean...

His Godslaying Sword, if not fully accomplished, has at least achieved minor success!

This evil embryo is both his "punching bag" for practicing Godslaying Sword and his "touchstone" for testing the fire control of Godslaying Sword.

And once the evil embryo is completely slain, he can obtain a large amount of divine marrow.

With such pure, such rich divine marrow, it is enough to repair the accumulated damage from years of self-slashing life soul.

With disaster comes an opportunity.
