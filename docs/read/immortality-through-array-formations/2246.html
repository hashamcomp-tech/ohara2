Chapter 2246: Chapter 1041: Explosion! (Part 3)

The Sword Qi of Linghu Xiao and his companions began to attack from afar.

Ye Qingfeng said, "Don’t disperse, forge ahead along a single path."

There are too many Formations set up by Mo Hua; it’s impossible to break them all.

Breaking them separately is time-consuming and laborious.

It’s better for everyone to gather together and break through one path, aiming at Mo Hua.

To injure ten fingers, it is better to sever one finger.

To damage ten Formations, it is better to break one Formation.

Ye Qingfeng led the way, using the Daluo Unification Sword as a guide, gathering everyone’s strength, and forcibly carved out a path from Mo Hua’s weak spot in the Formation.

Everyone followed this path, focusing on the Sword Qi of Linghu Xiao and his companions from Taixu Gate, breaking formations step by step and advancing into the depths step by step.

Meanwhile, Shen Linsu and the other three did not act rashly.

They still stood on the periphery, watching everything before them indifferently, especially the densely packed Formations.

They wanted to know what tricks Mo Hua was playing.

Is there some mystery hidden in Mo Hua’s Formations?

A lion uses its full strength to catch a rabbit.

They may disregard Mo Hua, but they must respect Mo Hua’s Formations sufficiently.

As Mo Hua’s Formation was cracked open, revealing a path, the appearance of the entire Formation "city" gradually became clear.

These Formations, strong as they are, seem to be the ordinary Second Grade High-Level Formation.

Moreover, they are all solid defensive and trapping Formations, with no Killing Formation.

Mo Hua’s goal seems to be just "defending."

Holding out as long as possible.

"But is it really... just this?"

Shen Linsu looked up at Mo Hua in the distance, surrounded by more than a hundred gifted cultivators, still unmoving, focused solely on Formation Painting, frowning gradually.

With Ye Qingfeng leading the team and forging a bloody path, they attacked deeper into the Formation.

The disciples from both sides met, and the fighting became increasingly fierce.

Under the amplification of the Five Elements Source Armor, Linghu Xiao wielded the Chongxu Sword True Technique to confront Ye Qingfeng’s Daluo Unification Sword Skill.

The Giant Swords of the Five Tai’a Brothers barred the way for Dragon Ding Sect’s Ao Zheng and other top-tier gifted cultivators.

Furthermore, Shen Cangfeng of Qian Taoist Sect and Xiao Ruohan of Heavenly Sword Sect also fought against the people from Taixu Gate.

And now, it truly became a life-and-death battle.

No one dared to be careless, and no one held back.

Taixu Gate was at a disadvantage in number, but with the amplification of Source Armor, within the small area delineated by Mo Hua’s Formation, their explosive power was extremely strong.

At the same time, black-and-red mixed Spiritual Ink threads wandered around, controlled by the Formation, assisting in killing.

Everyone from both sides fought as if their lives depended on it, unleashing their full Spiritual Power, engaging in a deadly struggle.

The place of confrontation was truly like a "meat grinder."

Blow by blow, sword by sword, mingled together.

Fierce killing intent merged, impenetrable.

One Dao Discussion Jade after another shattered.

Cultivator after cultivator died.

Indeed, it was extremely tragic.

As they watched, the gifted cultivators of the Four Sects and Seven Gates, one after another, died or retreated, while Taixu Gate fought desperately and grew ever more fierce, Shen Linsu and the other three could not hold back in the end.

They couldn’t let more die...

Nor could they let Mo Hua’s people continue killing...

Despite previous apprehension about Mo Hua’s Formations, unsure of what tricks Mo Hua was playing.

But after this killing, Mo Hua had exhausted his tricks.

The process of him setting up the Formation had been scrutinized countless times by Shen Linsu and the others.

Divine Sense Control Ink, Drawing Ground into Formation.

Using Formations for defense, using Formations for restraint, using Formations for trapping, using Formations for assistance...

This routine of Formations has also been seen through.

Taixu Gate, its fate is sealed...

Xiao Wuchen was the first to step into the "city" of Mo Hua’s Formation.

Then followed Duanmu Qing and Ao Zhan.

Finally, it was Shen Linsu.

Shen Linsu was the last to enter the range of Mo Hua’s Formation.

Upon entering, Shen Linsu suddenly felt an inexplicable dread, then he gave a disdainful and self-deprecating smile:

It’s just Mo Hua, yet I find myself apprehensive because of him, truly laughable...

With the entry of the Four Great Gifted Cultivators, the situation was thoroughly rewritten.

Xiao Wuchen, Ao Zhan, Duanmu Qing, Shen Linsu, each one possesses absolutely strong cultivation, wielding superior Cultivation Techniques and top-level Taoist Skills, their Spiritual Power Circulation Times nearing perfection.

Each person’s every punch, every sword, every spell, contains powerful combat strength, requiring great effort to resolve.

The people of Taixu Gate fought with all their strength.

But the disparity in strength is indeed too great.

No matter how hard the Taixu Gate disciples fought, no matter how strong Mo Hua’s Formation was, they fought until exhausted and spiritually depleted, yet ultimately couldn’t change the tide.

Taixu Gate disciples began to fall one by one.

Hao Xuan died by Xiao Wuchen’s Heavenly Sword Technique;

Yang Qianjun was slain by Shen Linsu’s Purple Gold Sword Qi;

Cheng Mo faced Ao Zhan for several rounds, then was killed with a punch;

Situ Jian died under Duanmu Qing’s indiscriminate spell bombardment;

Among the Five Tai’a Brothers, Ouyang Lu, Ouyang Xi, and Ouyang Cai, exhausted after a long battle, died surrounded by Ao Zheng, Shen Cangfeng, and others...

...

Eventually, of Taixu Gate only five remained dead: including Mo Hua, only Linghu Xiao, Ouyang Xuan, Ouyang Fu, and Ouyang Shou were left.

And there were still over fifty gifted cultivators from the Four Sects remaining.

A ten-fold difference in numbers.

Among these fifty-plus people, those who remained are all the cream of the crop from the Four Great Sects.

In addition, there’s Daluo Sect led by Ye Qingfeng, along with several elites.

And, the four top Bloodline Gifted Cultivators from Qianxue.

Now, Mo Hua’s Divine Sense is close to depletion, and Spiritual Ink is also exhausted; due to repeated overdraft and exhaustion of Divine Sense, after painting a large number of Formations, his complexion turned pale and his breath weak.
