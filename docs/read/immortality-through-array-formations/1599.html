Chapter 1599: Chapter 840: Madam Miao (Part 2)

"What do you think."

The two exchanged glances, unable to resist the urge to kiss. Another round of entanglement ensued.

Mo Hua found it offensive to look at.

Thought to himself, these shameless people, truly revolting, not doing anything proper, just dawdling here.

"Before leaving the Rouge Boat, why not trick these shameless ones to their death together..."

This Water Yama, full of evil deeds, has long been seeking his own doom.

As for Hua Ruyu, on the surface an instructor, but secretly a shameless "procuress," also no good.

Almost put a hat on Uncle Gu.

Let them be "locked" on this Rouge Boat, so they won’t harm others anymore.

Mo Hua patiently watched for a while, hoping they’d mention some important matters.

But after a while, the two seemed about to attend to "proper business."

Water Yama undid his belt, his only hand roamed over Hua Ruyu’s body, undressing her.

Mo Hua couldn’t bear to watch.

Kept watching, might as well grow a stye.

Such filthy scenes would taint his pure Taoist Heart.

Mo Hua sneered contemptuously, intending to leave.

At this moment, a voice sounded in the room:

"No..."

Halfway undressed, Hua Ruyu pressed down Water Yama’s restless hand, flushed face, panting, said:

"Master Xie, now is not the time."

Water Yama’s hand didn’t stop, urgently said, "It’s been so long since we’ve met, I’ve endured hard."

"Really can’t," Hua Ruyu said, "Rouge Boat is a significant matter, can’t afford any negligence."

Water Yama’s rage couldn’t be quenched, unwilling to relent.

Hua Ruyu softly said: "Afterward, I’ll definitely let you enjoy a good time."

Water Yama looked at Hua Ruyu’s delicate face, gaze burning hot, voice slightly hoarse said, "One time is not enough..."

Hua Ruyu blushed, whispered: "It’s all up to you."

Only then was Water Yama satisfied, no longer pestering.

Afterward, the two lowered their voices, discussing matters of the Rouge Boat.

Mo Hua nodded slightly, feeling a little satisfied, and continued to listen.

"Are all the people here..."

"Yes."

"This time, at the young master’s command, all the disciples from the Sect who need special attention are invited up."

"Except for Taixu Gate, the young master says Taixu Gate has some issues, best not provoke them for now, avoid trouble..."

"Aside from Taixu Gate, other Eight Great Gates, Twelve Streams, have core disciples on board. Especially Tai’a Sect and Rushing Void Sect, need special hospitality."

"The young master said..." Hua Ruyu whispered, "Have them take the pills, lose themselves for a while, so that they relish the taste, never forgetting, forever unable to leave Rouge Boat..."

Mo Hua heard this, gradually his gaze became intense.

Afterward, the two inside the room said something, Hua Ruyu then pushed Water Yama, said:

"You better go back, tonight is crucial, don’t be careless, and you’re seriously injured, need proper recuperation, can’t exhaust yourself."

Water Yama didn’t move, only sighed:

"Been so long, do you really want me to leave?"

Hua Ruyu blushed, "I’m afraid if you don’t leave... I won’t be able to resist."

Water Yama paused, kissed Hua Ruyu’s cheek again, "Alright, I’ll see to proper business, then come to service you."

They entwined for a while again.

Water Yama then got up to leave, but unable to resist his desires, reaching the door, suddenly remembered something, his gaze flickered, asked:

"By the way, did you bring a ’chick’ on board this time?"

Outside the window, Mo Hua heard this, paused.

Brought a "chick"?

Inside, Hua Ruyu also hesitated for a moment, her tender expression receding, her demeanor cooling a bit, warily said: "How did you know?"

"I heard."

Hua Ruyu displeased, "Such sharp senses... So what?"

Water Yama just smiled.

Hua Ruyu coldly laughed: "Don’t blame me for not warning you, this person is prepared for the ’young master’ above, you can’t touch her."

Water Yama coaxed her: "Miaoer, what are you thinking."

"Enough," Hua Ruyu said with a straight face, "I won’t hide from you, this girl is direct lineage from the Sect, her status is not ordinary, originally not to be touched. But the trouble is, she thought she was clever, secretly investigating our affairs all along, unfortunately, she really uncovered the inside story, knew some things she shouldn’t know."

Hua Ruyu coldly laughed, "Since it’s like this, then don’t blame me for being ruthless. Can only find a way to drag her into the mess, make her soak herself, let her learn a lesson, realize the world’s harshness..."

A direct lineage of bloodline, status is not ordinary, still a chick, appearance also excellent.

Water Yama coveted greatly in his heart, but seeing Hua Ruyu’s cold gaze, outwardly only smiled and said:

"What are you thinking, I told you, my heart has only you, other women, even if they look like Heavenly Immortals, I wouldn’t give them a glance."

Then he vowed and proclaimed sweet nothings to Hua Ruyu, only then did Hua Ruyu’s expression slightly ease.

The two intertwined for a while again, then Water Yama left.

After sending off Water Yama, Hua Ruyu turned back to her room, just sat down, frowned again, constantly feeling uneasy.

"All these rotten men under heaven, all have the same vile nature."

"Saying their heart holds only you, yet actually loving one wherever they see one."

"There exist damnable mice, yet no cats abstaining from fish."

Hua Ruyu thought more and more uneasy.

"Can’t let this dead man think of sneaking around, ruin my affairs, offend the young master..."

Hua Ruyu frowned, then immediately got up, left the room.

Mo Hua’s gaze shifted slightly, then also moved to leave, climbed inside the ship along the ventilated window.

But having delayed a bit, after entering the Flower Boat, Hua Ruyu’s figure was already nowhere to be seen.
