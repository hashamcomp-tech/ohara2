Chapter 2132: Chapter 1006: Victory or Defeat

In the sword discussion arena.

Sword Qi surged, and sword light radiated far and wide.

The mountain stones on the ground were cut by Sword Qi into web-like cracks, and the surrounding trees were reduced to powder amidst the powerful collision of Sword Qi.

Qin Cangliu activated the Qin family’s secret Canghai Hengliu Swordplay, while Linghu Xiao practiced the Chongxu Sword True Technique, the sect protecting art of Mount Chongxu.

One was a pure Water Series Swordsmanship.

One was a Sword Technique of water and moon transformation.

Both were Superior Taoist Skills, one vast as the sea, the other clear as the water moon, two distinctly beautiful shades of blue interwoven, containing the terrifying power of killing that would change the color of any Foundation-building Cultivator who saw it.

The momentum of the duel between two prodigies was truly too strong.

Under the flow of Sword Qi, other disciples from Taixu Gate and Gui Water Sect had to avoid their sharpness and find another venue to fight.

Mo Hua thought for a moment and decided not to use the Fireball Technique on Qin Cangliu, giving them the space for one-on-one combat.

The rivalry among prodigies was also a good opportunity for Xiaoxiao.

Xiaoxiao had excellent talent and high comprehension, but was still young and lacked battle experience, so for now, he was only a good sword "embryo."

Mo Hua aimed to use the Sword Discussion Conference to temper Linghu Xiao, allowing this precious sword to slowly "sharpen" and become stronger.

A sharp sword is forged through grinding.

Only in this way could they go farther in the Sword Discussion Conference.

Only then could Xiaoxiao progress unhindered in the Sword Dao.

Mo Hua avoided Qin Cangliu and began to support Cheng Mo and others with the Fireball Technique.

Meanwhile, Linghu Xiao and Qin Cangliu, two top sword dao geniuses, began to fight with single-minded focus.

In their eyes, there was only each other.

For geniuses, the more they face geniuses of the same level, the more they can spark each other’s battle intent.

Once they defeat prodigies, their Taoist Heart, and their entire Dao are confirmed, they can then advance further.

This was not just for sword discussion but also for their mutual Sword Dao.

Therefore, both began to go all-out, confronting each other fiercely, in a life-or-death struggle.

Exquisite sword moves appeared like antelope hanging horns, emerging endlessly, and powerful Sword Qi, like lotus blossoms, shone brightly.

This match instantly became the focus on the canvas of the power of killing.

Everyone’s gaze was drawn to this splendidly bright yet murderous prodigy sword discussion.

Their hearts were filled with amazement:

"This is the real sword discussion!"

"This is the true prodigy!"

"Such exquisite superior swordsmanship and explosive clashes of prodigy killing is befitting of the top sword discussion event."

"What’s the Fireball Technique even worth..."

They traveled far and wide to Qian Learning State Boundary to witness scenes like this.

Not to see someone hiding in the forest, shooting at people with Fireball Technique.

The battle became increasingly fierce.

The tangled fight of sword Qi reached a fever pitch.

Every cultivator watching was entirely focused, their spirits tense.

A suspense also rose in everyone’s hearts:

Who would win?

Qin Cangliu, although not famous, showed through the recent exchanges that his Sword Dao prowess was extraordinarily refined, merely hindered by his background and family origins, which led him to join Gui Water Sect of the Twelve Streams.

In terms of innate ability, even if he wasn’t part of the Four Great Sects, he would still be more than eligible to join the Sword Dao Sect of the Eight Great Gates.

Given time, he is sure to become an exceptional sword cultivator.

As for Linghu Xiao, there’s even less need to say.

A sword dao genius of Chongxu Sect unseen in five hundred years, his comprehension astounding, talent visible to the naked eye, although now still slightly immature, just like a guarded sword embryo, once refined, will certainly bloom with astonishing sharpness.

Such a peak duel is rare even in the Xuan Character Bureau.

Anyone could win, but equally, anyone could lose.

Everyone watched intently, witnessing the outcome of this prodigy sword discussion.

And within the sword discussion arena, time passed bit by bit.

The sword Qi clash never ceased, and the battle intensified gradually.

Unknowingly, almost everyone had fought to the point of ’running out of oil and burning out of the lamp,’ their spiritual power nearly depleted.

But Qin Cangliu’s fighting spirit was ever more invigorated.

His temperament is calm, tending to the cold, almost seldom showing emotions, but after battling Linghu Xiao, his expression turned excited and bloodthirsty.

This is a true opponent!

This is true killing!

Only an opponent with such sharpness can be his "grindstone," allowing him to endure grinding and make further advancement in the Sword Dao, climbing higher peaks.

At this moment, he was completely immersed, seeing only the sword.

A golden light enveloped his body.

Qin Cangliu activated the Golden Body Talisman, using the talisman power for protection, holding his longsword horizontally, his gaze fluctuating with eyes like rivers.

His spiritual power surged like rivers, Sword Qi emitting brilliant light, with mighty power gathering in the longsword.

The Canghai Hengliu Sword Form.

The final form of the Canghai Hengliu Sword Technique.

The ultimate killing move of superior swordsmanship.

Qin Cangliu wished to decide victory or defeat with this sword technique imbued with his life’s efforts.

This was an invitation to a duel between prodigies.

Linghu Xiao did not dodge, opening his Golden Body Talisman.

Under the shelter of golden light, his gaze was clear, the longsword seemingly lit with ice-blue moonlight, his entire aura seemingly integrated with the sword.

He was like the sword, also like the moon.

Cold moonlight, also like sword light, carried ethereal killing intent.

A duel of major sword killing techniques.

This was the key point for deciding victory of the sword discussion, also meaning a substantial opening for vulnerability.

Ouyang Xuan swung his sword to disperse the enemy, leading the charge, longsword like a peak, thrusting toward Qin Cangliu.

Cheng Mo and Situ Jian understood immediately, also abandoning the enemy at once and went all-out toward Qin Cangliu.
