Chapter 2336: Chapter 1070: Descent_2

"Even if he is a Formation Demon, in the end, he is just a Foundation Establishment, a brat who has been alive for just over twenty years."

"Such apprehension seems to overestimate him too much..."

Mr. Tu sneered, then ceased to have any concerns.

He had lived for over a thousand years. During these many years, he wore a human disguise, interacted with various cultivators, and had met all sorts of people.

These people, either deceived, used, or killed by him, ultimately became "stepping stones" for his grand plans.

People are meant to be "used."

Once used, they are discarded or killed, perishing with time, without leaving any trace of karma.

This brat named "Mo Hua" was no exception.

His "mission" was complete, and thus his "destiny" had come to an end.

And with the completion of plans spanning a millennium, the Divine Lord was about to descend, marking a transformative change in his fate.

In Mr. Tu’s eyes, a blood-red light flickered, burning with raging ambition.

"Time to prepare... for the ’God Invitation’..."

...

Qianxue State Boundary.

The day that was to come arrived without surprise, as it ultimately had to.

True Man Situ’s predictions were spot on. From the time he borrowed the Beidou Seven Stars Formation to perceive the heavenly secret, to the unfolding of all these events, indeed, it had been precisely fourteen days.

In the first seven days, the progress of the blood sacrifice was very slow, even slower than before.

But in the last seven days, it suddenly accelerated several times over, as if someone was secretly urging the progression of the situation.

True Man Situ’s expression was as calm as water.

He realized that the obscure and indiscernible "puppet master" behind this game might be more numerous than he had imagined.

And from the bloody sky above, he truly sensed that terrifying aura.

This is his...

"Death Tribulation."

True Man Situ sighed long and deeply.

Those who practice heavenly secrets, having peered into the worldly truths unseen by ordinary people, understand how to pursue fortune and avoid misfortune, turning danger into safety.

However, by doing so, they inevitably often reveal heavenly secrets and alter karmic causality.

Over time, they would suffer the "backlash" of heavenly secrets, facing "divine retribution," encountering numerous karmic trials.

Among all these trials, the most difficult to overcome is the "Death Tribulation" concerning life and death on the Great Dao.

Throughout his life, True Man Situ had encountered numerous trials, but most were turned into safety. He even survived an encounter with Gui Tao’s people without losing his life or Taoist Heart, which speaks of his good fortune.

And now, the Death Tribulation had finally arrived.

Before this, True Man Situ had also considered what his "Death Tribulation" might be.

He had various strange and unusual speculations.

But he never expected that his Death Tribulation would be an emerging "Evil God."

He, a Heavenly Mechanism Cultivator, to fight against an Evil God?

Under normal circumstances, True Man Situ would choose to end it himself, to die by his own hand.

He had lived a life, now old and frail, only wishing to live peacefully, and die peacefully.

If fallen into an Evil God’s clutches, everything becomes "terrifying."

He might truly experience the feeling that life is worse than death.

But there was no other way, the Evil God’s birth ground is the Fifth Grade Qianxue State Boundary, involving the lives of millions of cultivators, the stakes were too high.

Since this matter came upon him, True Man Situ had to brace himself and take it on.

He had woven karma, and relying on peering into heavenly secrets, a lifetime of turning dangers into blessings, had taken advantages.

Now that heavenly secrets placed a "heavy burden" on him, he had no space to refuse.

Even if it cost him his life, that would be the best choice.

If selfish and cowardly, going against heavenly secrets by refusing to take this responsibility, the outcome would surely be more frightening than "death."

The Heavenly Dao gives, and must also take.

Every gift comes with a price.

This is heavenly secrets, it is karma.

True Man Situ began preparing for the upcoming "Great Dream Heavenly Lead" ceremony and discussed with a group of Heaven Void Ancestors, saying:

"Tonight at 22 p.m, I will use the Mystic Sky Beidou Seven Stars Formation to empower and activate the Mystery Valley’s treasured item, to connect mother and son, and enter the Evil God’s nightmare."

"Those tasked with entering the dream to ’kill the god’ have also been arranged."

"But this is far from enough. Once the main ritual of ’God Invitation’ begins, and we enter the dream, we will inevitably face the obstruction of the Evil God’s minions, so at 22 p.m., I urge all the Heaven Void Ancestors to command the Sect cultivators to launch a full-scale assault on the Blood Sacrifice Array."

"Not only to pressure the Evil Demon but also to maximize the destruction of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, delaying the Evil God’s descent."

"The success or failure hinges on this..."

True Man Situ said in a deep voice.

There was one more thing he did not say out loud:

"Life and death also hinge on this, the safety of the entire Qianxue State Boundary, and even the entire Qian State, might also depend on this."

If this "God-slaying" battle is lost, the fate of countless cultivators in the entire Qian State would be uncertain.

True Man Situ’s expression was grim.

The seated Heaven Void Ancestors had equally solemn gazes.

They somewhat understood Mr. Tu’s conspiracy. This is a general’s move in chess, but they, these Fifth Grade Heaven Void Ancestors, indeed could neither evade nor act.

Their aristocratic family and Sect foundations were here.

These foundations are the sacrifices of these generations of ancestors, spanning hundreds, thousands, and even tens of thousands of years.

They could not abandon these, nor could they.

Moreover, most of Heaven Void’s cultivation requires vast forces to provide deep-rooted inheritance and resources and Spirit Stones to support.

Abandoning the foundation is equivalent to severing their path in Dao.

Mr. Tu’s Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation is an "open plot."

These Heaven Void Ancestors have no choice but to face Mr. Tu’s "ruse" head-on.
