Chapter 1650: Chapter 856: Worse Than a Dog

"You are no longer of use."

In those eyes, there was a hint of indifferent majesty, a golden, awe-inspiring sword light contained within.

Then a flash of golden light.

Water Yama’s expression turned dazed, as if trapped in a cage woven of sword light, a thousand strands of golden sword light slicing through his divine soul.

"Pupil Skill?!"

At the critical moment, Water Yama mustered all his strength, channeling evil qi into his eyes, trying to resist the golden pupil skill with the Transforming Evil Blood Pupil Skill.

A sea of blood emerged, wrathful spirits snarling.

But the golden light swept through, and in an instant, the entire sea of blood was annihilated.

Water Yama’s divine soul seemed to have been slashed by a sword, intimidated by the sword intent, and immense fear arose within his heart.

He still wanted to struggle, but he discovered that the evil qi he had suppressed for so long was on the verge of losing control, gradually eroding his sea of consciousness.

The pupil skill duel failed, and there was a backlash of evil qi.

Once the sea of consciousness was flooded by evil qi, he would surely die!

Water Yama’s expression twisted, using the last bit of his rationality to desperately suppress the evil qi.

But his eye sockets were still gradually filling with blood, and at the same time, he saw Mo Hua.

At some unknown time, the spirit-binding lock on Mo Hua’s body had already been unlocked.

He approached Water Yama, taking advantage of his divine sense being out of control, and right before Water Yama’s eyes, took his Water Prison Forbidden Box.

The one he had spent so much effort, cultivated spiritual power, sacrificed half of his blood essence, and had just "opened the box" not long ago...

The Water Prison Forbidden Box!

Water Yama felt a surge of fury attack his heart, the evil qi in his sea of consciousness about to break through his restraint.

At this moment, he heard Mo Hua murmur softly:

"Sigh, I really overestimated you. You’re far worse compared to that dog in my Taixu Gate..."

Hearing this, Water Yama’s anger surged, spitting blood, unable to suppress the evil qi within him any longer.

His sea of consciousness suffered a backlash from the evil qi, and his divine soul began to fracture.

And his eyes, from deep red, slowly turned to black-gray, leaving only some instinctive parts, furiously biting around.

Mo Hua saw this and quickly retreated.

Ouyang Feng and Hua Qianqian saw Water Yama, for some reason, suddenly lose his mind, spiritual power in chaos, aura gray and defeated, quickly taking the opportunity to attack.

Ouyang Feng stabbed directly at Water Yama’s heart vein with a longsword.

Hua Qianqian used the Hundred Flowers Spirit Needle to pierce into Water Yama’s forehead.

Both of these were lethal wounds.

Water Yama, severely injured, had his evil qi gradually retreating, experiencing a slight revival before his death, seemingly about to say something.

At this moment, another shadow turned around from the corner of the wall.

This person, wearing a Taoist Court robe, with handsome features and fair skin, was Xiao Tianquan.

Xiao Tianquan, quick-eyed and agile, immediately flashed over and pierced Water Yama’s throat with a sword.

Water Yama’s pupils shrank, dazedly looking at Xiao Tianquan, and then his gaze gradually dissipated, losing his breath altogether.

This Water Yama, who had committed countless atrocities on the Mistwater River, was thoroughly dead.

Mo Hua glanced at Xiao Tianquan, then patted his chest and let out a long breath, saying with lingering fear:

"Brother Xiao, it’s lucky you arrived in time, or else I would’ve fallen victim to that Water Yama."

Xiao Tianquan nodded, "This person committed countless sins, deserved to die."

But...

Xiao Tianquan looked at the black-gray dead eyes of Water Yama, frowned, and said:

"How did he suddenly lose his senses?"

Mo Hua shook his head, "I don’t know... Probably did too many bad things and got punished by the heavens, faced his retribution."

He executed his pupil skill discreetly earlier, so no one present saw the golden sword intent in his eyes.

Xiao Tianquan furrowed his brows tightly, stared at Water Yama for a while, then relaxed slightly, and said in a low voice:

"Probably couldn’t suppress the evil qi and got a backlash..."

Mo Hua raised an eyebrow.

This Howling Celestial Dog knows quite a lot.

With Water Yama dead, the matter was settled here, Ouyang Feng pondered for a moment, then asked:

"Supervisor Gu and Xia Canonical Official are entangled with that Mr. Witch. Should we go see if we can help?"

At this moment, even if they wanted to escape, they couldn’t.

And in this White Bone Maze, with dangers lurking at every turn, there were the scaled monsters "Mr. Witch", countless blood zombies, and the treacherous Master Xiao.

Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official in the Golden Core Realm were their reliance.

If Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official remained safe, there might be a chance.

Once they met with misfortune, then the few of them, including Mo Hua, might be in desperate straits.

Mo Hua pondered for a moment, then nodded.

"Let’s go, let’s check it out."

He held the incense burner left by Water Yama, and that segment of red incense to drive away the blood zombies, and then stepped towards the big door.

Ouyang Feng and Hua Qianqian followed behind Mo Hua.

Xiao Tianquan was at the back, but he deliberately hesitated for a moment, looked down at Water Yama, and pulled out the sword stuck in Water Yama’s throat, still not at ease, he reversed grip on the sword and stabbed a few more times at Water Yama’s heart vein after already dead, then stealthily searched Water Yama’s body and his waist storage bag, but found nothing.

"Nothing?"

Xiao Tianquan’s expression was slightly tense as he looked up at Mo Hua, his gaze obscure, and after pondering for a moment, he silently followed them.

Mo Hua led the group, driving the blood zombies away, retracing their steps, arriving once more in front of the White Bone Gate.

Before the gate, several Golden Cores were still fighting to the death.

Blood zombies were everywhere, blood nearly soaking every inch of the ground red.

Gu Changhuai, alone, held off Xie Liu turned into a blood zombie, while Xia Canonical Official and Master Xiao teamed up against Mr. Witch, clad in dragon scale armor.

Everyone bore more or less injuries, their auras significantly weakened.

Even that monster Mr. Witch, his blood qi had nearly been consumed half.
