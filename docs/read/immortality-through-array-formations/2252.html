Chapter 2252: Chapter 1043: Sever! (Part 2)

Not right!

Kill Mo Hua first!

The five had no time to think. Almost instinctively, they wanted to strike first to kill Mo Hua, or at least interrupt his sword moves.

But it was already too late. They had already seen Mo Hua’s eyes.

Those were eyes, inhuman eyes.

Brilliant with golden light, filled with pure divinity.

Within the divinity was compassion for all beings.

Within the humanity was a demonic nature that slaughtered all.

A blood-colored sword dao purgatory was imprinted in Mo Hua’s eyes, intimidating Shen Linsu’s five people’s divine souls, making them afraid due to guilt, not daring to make any moves.

Seizing this opportunity, Mo Hua calmed his mind, abandoning all distractions, and mobilized all his divine sense within his sea of consciousness.

The mysteries of the four sword arrays, Gold-breaking, Mountain-opening, Gui Water, and Li Fire, circulated endlessly in his eyes.

The ancient and vast Tai Xu Sword Intent was also integrated within.

A sword intent, far surpassing the Foundation Establishment Realm, sharp and astonishingly powerful, suddenly burst forth from Mo Hua’s thin body.

Mighty and vast, like an astounding storm of divine thought, sweeping invisibly around.

Mo Hua’s eyes grew even more brilliant.

The sword intent catalyzed by pure golden divine thought rose to its extreme, becoming incredibly concentrated and sharp.

Mo Hua pointed to the space between his eyebrows.

At his fingertips, a spiritual power lingered, crossing the space between his eyebrows, communicating the boundary between reality and illusion. By the "Out-of-body Divine Thought" method he explored for himself, he gradually drew out the powerful and profound Tai Xu Sword Intent, integrated into the Five Elements Sword Formation.

The massive God-slaying style, its astonishing sharpness, once again unveiled to the world.

Then Mo Hua, with an icy gaze, pointed forward, drawing a surging sword intent, and the Divine Thought Giant Sword swept across the air.

Slash!

This was purely the Sword of Divine Thought.

It was an existence fused with divine marrow, transformed into pure gold, transcending ordinary cultivators’ cognition.

Countless Foundation Building Cultivators present could see almost nothing; they could only see Mo Hua pointing, seemingly invisible yet faintly gold light in the air.

Golden Core Cultivators could see a vague sword shadow, with golden strands of sword intent occasionally mixed in.

Those in Feather Transformation could see a dazzling, glass-like giant golden sword body.

Only those in the Heaven Void could see the complete God-slaying Giant Sword and the flowing Five Elements Laws within the sword intent, many sword arrays, and the ancient, profound Tai Xu Sword Dao.

The higher the realm, the clearer they could see, the more terrifying they felt.

And this terrifying sword, right in front of all the cultivators, like the morning sun breaking, the golden light breaking the heavens and earth, slashed towards the five top Qianxue’s geniuses present.

Ignoring flesh, ignoring spiritual power, ignoring all armor and Taoist robes.

Ignoring the Dao Discussion Jade, even surpassing the Fifth-grade Mount of Tao’s formation defense laws, it directly penetrated the eyes, slashing at their sea of consciousness, erasing their divine thought.

Shen Linsu and the others, their flesh, spiritual power, cultivation, Taoist Skills, were all more powerful than Mo Hua by countless times.

But their divine thought, their Taoist heart, compared to this "deity" Mo Hua, was utterly vulnerable.

At the instant of being slashed by the God-slaying Sword, signs of their sea of consciousness collapsing, and ominous signs of divine sense annihilation, appeared among the five.

Then a layer of golden light suddenly descended, protecting them.

On the foreheads of Shen Linsu and the others, a bright and sacred rune immediately appeared.

This layer of golden light was not unfamiliar to Mo Hua.

He had also seen this rune before.

The special Eternal Life Rune!

It was a rune formed by an ancestor above Heaven Void, near the end of their lifespan, with no hope of the Great Dao, condensed with their life essence true essence, planted into the life charts of direct lineage descendants. In moments of life and death, it self-activates, capable of killing all external enemies, preserving the descendants’ lives.

This slash of Mo Hua’s God-slaying Sword indeed possessed the power to annihilate Shen Linsu’s five divine thoughts and indeed slashed into their divine souls.

Naturally, it also shattered the five... special Eternal Life Runes embedded in them!

Slashing out five... Heaven Void Dharma Phases!

At that moment, a deafening rumble echoed to the heavens and earth.

The sky and earth changed color, the ground shook and mountains swayed.

A massive golden light soared into the sky, on Dao Discussion Mountain, the wind and clouds churned, five powerful Heaven Void Dharma Phases appeared simultaneously, spiritual power vast and boundless, covering the sky and earth, their terrifying might enough to make everyone’s soul scatter in fright.

The residual might shook the heavens and earth.

Not only Dao Discussion Mountain but the entire Qianxue State Boundary was shaken by it.

The combined might of five Heaven Void Dharma Phases was incredibly frightening. The golden light spread from Dao Discussion Mountain outward, extending to almost the entire state boundary.

In various places of the Qianxue State Boundary, and even in all the surrounding small and medium state boundaries, all cultivators could see this earth-shaking spectacle.

Even in Solitary Mountain City, Mistwater River, Loose Cultivators in a small fishing village were all shocked, looking up at the sky, at the golden light like the scorching sun, and within the golden light, the faintly visible five Heaven Void shadows.

Five shattered Eternal Life Runes, five Heaven Void Dharma Phases emerged.

This was a grand scene that all cultivators, in all their lifetimes, might never witness.

And at the same time, in the depths of the forbidden area of the Four Great Sects and the Daluo Sect.

Five Heaven Void Ancestors simultaneously spat out a mouthful of blood.

...

And the aftermath had not yet subsided.

In the Sword Discussion Field, Mo Hua swung out this single sword.

A single sword shattering five Eternal Life Runes.

A single sword slashing out five Heaven Void Dharma Phases.

A single sword making the heaven and earth change colors.

A single sword making five Heaven Void Ancestors spit blood.

This single sword from Mo Hua, was too astounding.

Following it, came a vast, boundless killing intent like the surge of heavens and earth.

The five Heaven Void Dharma Phases, their immense and terrifying Heaven Void killing intent, simultaneously locked onto Mo Hua.
