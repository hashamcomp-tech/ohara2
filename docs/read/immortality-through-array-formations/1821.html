Chapter 1821: Chapter 905: Who Is It? (Part 4)

This matter, Mo Hua knew in advance, and sent out a message.

At present, it seems that only Mo Hua knew in advance.

Subsequently, the Demon Sect set up an ambush, surrounded halfway, and then suddenly withdrew...

Could it be...

Is it that Mo Hua did something secretly, causing so many Demon Cultivators to have to cease their plan and retreat reluctantly?

This speculation is too absurd.

Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official initially just thought it was impossible.

But thinking about Mo Hua’s always unexpected and incredible actions, waves of shock gradually surged in both of their hearts...

...

Within the Taixu Gate.

At this moment, Mo Hua was incredibly busy.

After the message "there’s a mole" was sent out, he began to erase Magnetic Patterns overnight, preparing to "flee".

Of course, it’s not really "fleeing", but rather erasing all traces he left in the Demon Sect’s Yuan Magnetic Formation base to avoid detection by these Demon Cultivators.

First, he restored all the Fixed Magnetic Patterns he had modified through the Thunder Magnetic Miniature during this period.

Afterwards, he decisively blocked the entrance to the Yuan Magnetic Compound Formation, cutting off the connection to the Yuan Magnetic Sea.

Then, he changed the identity of the Demon Sect disciple "Chi Fei" back to "ordinary disciple".

All the permissions he had altered through Magnetic Patterns for him were all blocked.

The Demon Sect Token was also changed to a "closed" state by him.

After erasing all traces, Mo Hua retracted the Thunder Magnetic Miniature and threw the Demon Sect Token back into the Storage Ring, acting as if nothing had happened, attending classes, cultivating, and practicing swordsmanship as usual.

As for what happens next, he no longer cared.

But in his heart, there was a bit of anxiety.

What if he got discovered and was sought out by these Demon Cultivators...

Mo Hua was momentarily stunned, suddenly realizing he might be overthinking it.

He is now an honorable disciple of the Taixu Gate, ranked third among the Eight Great Gates, and currently situated in Taixu Mountain.

In Taixu Mountain, there’s the Heaven Void Ancestor overseeing, and the Sect Leader is Feather Transformation.

There are countless Golden Core Realm Elders who are familiar with him.

What is there to fear?

Just a few, no, just a dozen or so Golden Core Demons.

If it were outside, he might still give them some face, try to avoid them.

But this is the Taixu Gate, if they dare to come, even without the Ancestor acting, those Feather Transformation Realm Chief Elders could chop them all down with one sword each.

Mo Hua’s back straightened suddenly; he realized he truly had "backing" now.

Thus, two days later, he couldn’t resist the loneliness and quietly accessed the Demon Sect Token again.

Then he discovered he was "deactivated".

It seemed the Demon Sect noticed the anomaly and "deactivated" all such lost and untraceable Demon Sect Tokens.

Mo Hua’s heart sank.

It’s over, this Token, he wasn’t going to play with it just once and then have it "deactivated", was he?

He hadn’t had enough fun yet.

"I have to think of a solution..."

The situation was urgent, Mo Hua couldn’t be bothered with too much, so he once again summoned the Returning to the Source Thunder Magnetic Miniature to infiltrate the Demon Sect Token.

He wasn’t afraid of being discovered by Demon Cultivators.

If by chance he was discovered, he would just destroy the Demon Sect Token and throw it off the cliff of Taixu Mountain.

After the Thunder Magnetic Miniature infiltrated the Demon Sect Token, Mo Hua indeed saw the Magnetic Patterns of the Demon Sect Token starting to turn gray, and no information could be transmitted.

The permission status was also changed to "destroyed".

This Demon Sect Token seemed to be invalidated.

Mo Hua furrowed his brows.

This Demon Sect was so petty; saying they would destroy the Demon Sect Token, just like that, couldn’t play the game...

They didn’t want to play, but he hadn’t had enough.

"What to do?"

Mo Hua pondered for a while, then based on his Thunder Magnetic experience and the principles of the Thunder Magnetic Array taught by Elder Zheng, he started devising a plan and began attempting.

And after several attempts, Mo Hua gradually had an idea.

The Demon Sect Token was in his hands; the Demon Sect couldn’t "deactivate" it starting from this token.

They could only sever the connection from the Yuan Magnetic Compound Formation end.

And each Demon Sect Token had a Magnetic Pattern Sequence to identify "identity" and connect with the Yuan Magnetic Compound Formation.

This principle was the same as the Soul Pivot Ultimate Formation with First Grade Twelve Patterns he had learned before.

The Demon Sect "destroyed" the Sequence of his Demon Sect Token, so it became invalidated, even if the permission was changed to "Skill Transmission Disciple," it still couldn’t access the Yuan Magnetic Sea of the Compound Formation.

"In other words, as long as the Magnetic Pattern Sequence can be altered, it can still be accessed..."

"But... where is the Sequence Magnetic Pattern, and how to change it?"

Mo Hua patiently, focusing all his attention, combed through the Fixed Magnetic Patterns in the Demon Sect Token from beginning to end.

Finally, after combing through it a full five times, Mo Hua found a hidden spot with a complex pattern, unclear meaning, but very intricate special Yuan Magnetic Formation Pattern.

"Found it!"

Sequence Magnetic Pattern!

Mo Hua’s eyes lit up, and then he immediately started thinking, how does this Magnetic Pattern need to be changed? Into what form?

Sequence Magnetic Patterns themselves have no special meaning, purely for identifying token identity.

Hence, the texture itself would be very complex.

Mo Hua didn’t have other Sequence Magnetic Patterns with Demon Sect permissions to use temporarily.

So he could only try random modifications himself.

See if a blind cat could stumble upon a dead mouse.

Mo Hua, having studied the Spiritual Pivot Formation, knew that Sequential Formation Patterns, for ease of management, were also derived according to a certain pattern.

If he just "forged" one, it should be usable.

But this "forging" also required effort.

Afterwards, Mo Hua, relying on his profound Formation knowledge and keen Dao intuition, began attempting to forge a new "Magnetic Pattern" based on the original sequence.

Each time he forged a Sequence Magnetic Pattern, he attempted to communicate with the Thunder Magnetic Miniature, to see if he could leverage Thunder Magnetic Power to re-open the interface and engage with the Yuan Magnetic Compound Formation of the Demon Sect.

In this manner, trying one by one...

Attempting from various angles, directions, structures, trying over fifty pieces, Mo Hua finally succeeded in creating a truly useful "Sequence Magnetic Pattern".

Mo Hua used the "forged" Sequence Magnetic Pattern to start interfacing with the Yuan Magnetic Formation of the Demon Sect.

In an instant, Yuan Magnetic vibrated, and Secondary Thunder Flow surged.

The gray Magnetic Patterns began lighting up one by one.

A Thunder Magnetic interface opened, with the intricate yet orderly Yuan Magnetic Flow, carrying a vast amount of information, rushing like a river.

A multitude of Demon Cultivator information appeared in Mo Hua’s mind once again.

Seeing the familiar scene before his eyes, Mo Hua couldn’t help but have his eyes light up.

"Success!"

"I’m back again!"

Even under "deactivation", he managed to break through multiple obstacles, infiltrating this Yuan Magnetic Sea once more with his wit and wisdom.

"This Token can still be played with..."

Mo Hua felt anticipation in his heart.

Meanwhile, staring at the myriad of complex Yuan Magnetic Flow before him, a thought couldn’t help but surface in his mind:

If in secret, leveraging the Thunder Magnetic Miniature, mastering the Yuan Magnetic Formation, it seems like the Golden Core game isn’t one he can’t play...
