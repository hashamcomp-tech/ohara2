﻿Chapter 2175 Chapter 1020: Ao Zhan

The sword discussion match with Dragon Empire Sect's Ao Zhan was monster hunting.

Disciples from both sides competed with each other, going out to hunt a

Second Grade High-Rank Monster Beast.

Whoever can kill the Monster Beast and carve out its inner core, will be considered the winner.

During the sword discussion, cultivators must not only hunt monsters but also deal with people.

They can utilize the Monster Beasts to weaken their opponent's strength.

It's also possible to be trapped by opponents and fall victim to the Monster Beast's poison.

Competition revolving around Monster Beasts can lead to different tactics, inclusive of endless situational changes, making the battle more complex and the ending more difficult to predict.

However, the disciples of the Dragon Empire Sect adopted a more direct approach.

They did not hunt the monsters, but directly hunted the "people."

Ao Zhan led the team, abandoning the Monster Beasts, and directly rushed at the five from Taixu Gate, planning to kill the people first, then slay the

monsters.

This was an extremely confident strategy.

This meant that they had sufficient self-confidence to crush and kill the five from Taixu Gate in a short time.

And after killing the five people from Taixu Gate, they would still have enough strength left to continue hunting a Second Grade High-Rank Monster Beast.

Clearly, they didn't take Mo Hua and the others seriously.

Some might even say, there was a sense of "humiliation" in it.

But facts proved, they indeed had such strength and were indeed qualified to do so.

At the start of the battle, the five from Dragon Empire Sect roared with Dragon Power, accompanied by terrifying whistling sounds, like fierce tigers released from cages, charging straight at Mo Hua and his team.

Mo Hua had previously encountered the second-rate disciples of Dragon Empire Sect, experiencing the power of Dragon Tiger Body Refining Skill.

But those disciples were completely incomparable to the five in front of him, especially the leading Ao Zhan.

Ao Zhan, at the Foundation Establishment Peak, was vigorous, exuding heavy dignity and formidable presence.

A phantom Green Tripod Dragon Shadow coiled around his body.

Blood Qi surged with faintly heard dragon chants.

In a trance, there seemed to be a resemblance to the majesty of Shen Tuao, leader of the Demon Sect, bearing the Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation, who perished in Gushan Temple, and was also the Third Prince of the Great Wilderness.

The two sides met, the strong clashed first.

Ao Zhan roared furiously, his fist wind like a dragon, then engaged in deadly combat with Linghu Xiao.

Xiao Wuchen was the first person of Heavenly Sword Sect.

And Ao Zhan was the first person of Dragon Empire Sect, their fame and strength were equally matched.

But Ao Zhan was a powerful body cultivator with integrated offense and defense, with surging Blood Qi and rampant Dragon Power; once he got close, his fists and feet would open wide in thunderous force, making it difficult to resist.

In this aspect, he restrained Linghu Xiao better than Xiao Wuchen.

Ao Zhan's close combat technique was fierce, fast, and aggressive, with unmatched power, dragon shadows flying, splitting mountains and rocks.

Linghu Xiao was continuously suppressed, without even a chance to proactively brandish his sword, only able to retreat step by step.

From the surface, it seemed even more thorough in defeat compared to the battle with Xiao Wuchen, with no power to retaliate.

Spectating cultivators shook their heads.

Some elders from Mount Chongxu even showed their worried expressions, constantly sighing.

With Linghu Xiao at a disadvantage.

The remaining few from Taixu Gate were pressed so hard they could hardly breathe.

Not to mention assisting Linghu Xiao or creating a sword opportunity for him, in the face of Dragon Empire Sect disciples' smiting strike, they could barely protect themselves and that was extremely difficult.

Mo Hua fared no better.

A tall disciple from Dragon Empire Sect was pursuing him to kill.

Mo Hua could only use his movement technique to evade and maneuver.

The Dragon Empire Sect disciple couldn't break through the Water Passing Step, and kill Mo Hua.

Mo Hua similarly had no way to use movement technique to kill him.

This was Mo Hua's second field sword discussion.

The field, the competition format, and the opponent were all different from the first one.

But the development of the battle situation had no real difference.

Quickly, Linghu Xiao exhausted his strength and was killed by Ao Zhan's punch, thus marking his defeat.

The situation swiftly deteriorated.

Situ Jian, Ouyang Xuan, and Cheng Mo also fell in quick succession.

Once more leaving only Mo Hua alone to weakly maneuver using movement technique.

Five against one.

But this time, the five from Dragon Empire Sect were not as courteous as

Heavenly Sword Sect disciples.

The five showed no martial ethics, attacked simultaneously, two pursued Mo Hua with fists and feet, while two blocked his position.

Ao Zhan's eyes were sharp, fixed on Mo Hua, with ferocious Dragon Blood Qi between his fists, preparing to unleash a killer move.

Xiao Wuchen was arrogantly aloof, not cutting down a fallen dog.

But he was different.

A lion hunts rabbit with all its strength.

He wanted to personally kill Mo Hua!

The five prodigies of Dragon Empire Sect surrounded, with killing intent locked in.

Mo Hua's heart gave a jolt, secretly thinking: "Not good."

If he doesn't run now, he might truly "die" at Ao Zhan's hands. Mo Hua immediately took a Water Passing Step to pull away, pointed his hand at his forehead, again intending to self-fragmenting the Dao Discussion Jade. Ao Zhan sneered coldly, seemingly anticipating this, immediately channeling his Dantian, resonating through his chest, opened his mouth wide, and roared suddenly.

This roar indeed rang out with a dragon's cry.

Like cracking stones and piercing the sky, a white sound wave charged with the impact of a dragon chant directly surged into Mo Hua's eardrum.

The dragon cry pierced his eardrum, went straight through his Sea of Consciousness, shaking Mo Hua's Divine Sense.

And then there was nothing after...

Mo Hua's Divine Thought and Golden Body were flawless, immovable like a

mountain.

The dragon roar, which amazed ears and made hearts quake, entered Mo Hua's ear like whispers in the breeze, without a hint of intimidation.

He had consumed the Dragon Soul.

How could he be afraid of this human-roaring dragon chant?

This dragon roar couldn't intimidate him at all.

Mo Hua merely blinked, then without any delay, vibrated the Spiritual Power at his fingertips, shattered the Dao Discussion Jade, and disappeared.

At the instant Mo Hua disappeared, a dragon shadow fist wind howled and arrived, shattering the ground and mountain stone where he stood into fragments.

Strength extended outward, the whole ground cracked inch by inch like a spider web.
