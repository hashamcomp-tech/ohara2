Chapter 1079 -673 Divine Tao Array_3

“The Evil God preaches, and it is similar to how other deities preach, but the means employed are bloody and eerie.”

“Evil Gods divide and preach.”

“Because deities are also restricted by the Heavenly Dao, a great Evil God often divides its own Divine Body into many First Grade, Second Grade, or Third Grade ‘Divine Remains’, spreading them throughout the lower-grade state boundaries of Nine State, to disseminate the Evil Path to the greatest extent, feeding on the degenerate desires of humans, nourishing itself…”

“Preaching divine statues, parasitizing flesh, nurturing desires, devouring human hearts…”

“Evil Gods also require ‘ritual offerings’, using human flesh and Divine Sense as offerings.”

“Especially the distorted desires rooted deep in human hearts, the filthy sins within cause and effect, and the bloody and fervent beliefs are considered premium offerings for Evil Gods…”

Lord Yellow Mountain pondered, then summed up:

“Hence, if you ever encounter bizarre divine statues, weird cultivators, Demon Monsters’ altars, flesh as offerings, and distorted sins… They are most likely related to Evil Gods.”

“…Do not hesitate; run as far as you can.”

“Right!” Mo Hua vigorously nodded, then blinked and humbly asked, “What if I can’t run away?”

Lord Yellow Mountain shook his head, “Then I have no solution.”

“You are a Mountain God, and you have no solution either?”

Lord Yellow Mountain felt a bit aggrieved:

“How can a Mountain God fight against an Evil God…”

Let alone an Evil God, I probably can’t even beat you, a little cultivator kid…

Lord Yellow Mountain silently grumbled internally.

“Really not a single solution?” Mo Hua asked again.

Lord Yellow Mountain glanced at Mo Hua, pondered for a moment, and then slowly said:

“If you’re fortunate in the future and learn a Formation that can restrain deities, maybe you can fight…”

Mo Hua was stunned, then his spirits lifted, his eyes sparkling.

“A Formation that can restrain deities?!”

“Yes…” Lord Yellow Mountain nodded, then suddenly became alert, “Just don’t use it against me…”

“Don’t worry, you’re not an Evil God!” Mo Hua reassured Lord Yellow Mountain, “Besides, we’re good friends now.”

Although Lord Yellow Mountain didn’t fully agree with the term “good friends,” he didn’t dare to object.

Friends are always better than enemies.

“What’s different about a Formation that can restrain deities, compared to ordinary Formations?” Mo Hua eagerly inquired.

He never expected that inquiring about deities would lead to new secret Formation insights from Lord Yellow Mountain.

Lord Yellow Mountain nodded, “Naturally…”

He sighed, looked up through the broken temple roof, and lamented:

“Heaven and earth have the Great Dao, the Dao transforms myriad things, and all are Law.”

“Formations are one of those myriad transformations of the Great Dao, except they are closer to the origin of the Great Dao.”

“Great Dao is myriad, thus Formations are also myriad, with diverse categories and differing rules.”

Lord Yellow Mountain turned to Mo Hua, “The Formations commonly used by your Human Race are mostly based on natural forces like the Five Elements and Eight Trigrams; however, their root is using Divine Sense, comprehending the Heavenly Dao, employing Laws, and manifesting through natural spiritual powers like the Five Elements and Eight Trigrams…”

“In Formations, both Divine Thought Power and the power of the Five Elements and Eight Trigrams are incorporated.”

“Different Formations emphasize natural spiritual power and Divine Thought Power differently.”

“For dealing with humans, natural spiritual power based on the Five Elements and Eight Trigrams is primarily used.”

“But to confront deities…”

Lord Yellow Mountain paused, corrected himself, “To confront ‘Evil Gods’…”

He emphasized the words “Evil Gods” heavily, implying I’m not an Evil God, don’t come after me.

Mo Hua nodded understandingly.

Lord Yellow Mountain then continued, “To confront Evil Gods, emphasis in the Formation must be on ‘Divine Thought Power’.”

“The laws of heaven and earth harm the tangible with tangibles and the intangible with intangibles.”

“Bodily spiritual power is ‘tangible’.”

“Blood Qi turning into Strength, spiritual power morphing into Spells, can harm ‘tangible’ flesh, meridians, and Qi Sea.”

“Divine Thought is ‘intangible’.”

“A deity’s essence is a certain type of Divine Thought condensed from all spirits between heaven and earth. Without knowing the Lifebound Divine Statue, only Divine Thought Power can harm the ‘intangible’ deity.”

“Therefore, in ancient times, some powerful Ancient Cultivators, in order to restrain Evil Gods, manifested a part of the cosmic rules to create Formations that could maximize the use of Divine Thought Power.”

“These kinds of Formations focus on the ‘intangible’ divine and lessen the ‘tangible’ force, possessing strong restraining effects against secretive deities but having weak killing power against ordinary cultivators.”

“Some Ancient Cultivators collectively referred to these Formations as…”

Lord Yellow Mountain’s gaze intensified, showing deep reverence.

“Divine Tao Arrays!”

Divine Tao Arrays…

Mo Hua was deeply shocked, holding his breath.

This name sounded both cool and powerful!

Wanted to learn!

Mo Hua’s eyes glowed with excitement, unable to resist asking:

“How do I learn the Divine Tao Array…”

Lord Yellow Mountain shook his head, “I am a Minor Mountain God, neither a human nor a Formation Master, how would I know…”

“Moreover, through the many years of the Cultivation World, much of the ancient Formations established by Ancient Cultivators have been lost; even if you want to find them, it’s not easy.”

“Even if you find them, they’re tough to learn.”

“If they are sealed within some massive Tao Cultivation powers and considered a ‘taboo,’ it is even more impossible to let you learn.”

Mo Hua felt a bit disappointed, “Such important things shouldn’t they be passed down through generations, to be further glorified?”

Lord Yellow Mountain profoundly said, “In ancient times, the Human Race was vulnerable, and the greatest killers were Demon Monsters, Heavenly Demons, and Evil Gods.”
