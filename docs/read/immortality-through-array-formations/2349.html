Chapter 2349: Chapter 1074: Awakening_3

True Man Situ said in a deep voice, "Find this wicked creature!"

This Red Powder Demon can hide its form, stirring human desires imperceptibly. If not eradicated soon, it will surely bring about great havoc.

Afterwards, everyone began to use their Divine Thought Techniques or Divine Thought Treasures to search and kill, yet none could find the trace of this Red Powder Demon.

True Man Situ frowned, his mind stirred, and he immediately joined his fingers together, casting a flash of red-gold light towards the altar where Yu Er, the true embryo of the Evil God, lay.

Sure enough, as the red-gold light floated in the air, the Red Powder Demon appeared, spewing pink Evil Qi to block the light.

It is an accompanying demon of the Evil God True Embryo, existing solely to protect the Evil Embryo.

The others quickly understood and began using their Divine Thought Techniques to attack the Evil Embryo.

Attack its lord, the demon monster will surely save.

Sure enough, the Red Powder Demon roared in fury but had no choice but to withstand True Man Situ’s assault for its "master."

Though being a Fetal Demon, it possesses strong powers; its strength lies in its elusive nature, stirring human desire and corrupting the Primordial Spirit.

Besides this, its true power is only at the Peak of Third Grade, and in direct combat, it naturally cannot match True Man Situ and the others.

Before long, the Red Powder Demon was struck by the Peach Wood Sword, burned by the Six Yang Red Gold Lamp, and subdued by the Soul Suppressing Talisman from the Spirit Talisman Sect’s elder.

The Red Powder Demon was completely subdued.

True Man Situ breathed a sigh of relief, intending to "eliminate evil completely," and to completely kill this Red Powder Demon. Just then, images of Fetal Demon Eggs cracking flashed in his mind once more.

True Man Situ pondered briefly and then, with a shock in his heart, said:

"Something’s not right!"

Accompanying Demon Eggs do not hatch before the Evil God awakens.

This is true for the Red Powder Demon as well; if it hatched prematurely, it must have been deliberately released.

And indeed, there were "scratches" on the Demon Egg.

This means that before them, something had already been to the altar and released a Fetal Demon Egg in advance to "ambush" them.

"There’s something else!"

As soon as this thought emerged, True Man Situ’s heart went cold.

Almost simultaneously, a black shadow appeared in the void, and a massive inky hand, imbued with powerful Divine Thought Power, suddenly attacked True Man Situ, intending to kill him.

A warning alarm rang in True Man Situ’s mind as he quickly summoned the Six Yang Red Gold Lamp for protection and hastily retreated.

The three brothers of the Xuanmen sect immediately blocked in front of True Man Situ.

But the black shadow’s Divine Thought Power was incredibly strong; with just one palm, it repelled the three brothers, its sinister telekinesis entering their bodies, making them spew blood.

The black shadow continued its assault towards Mr. Tu.

A tattooed man stepped forward to block but was still severely injured by a single hit.

Seeing that this black shadow’s hands, like a Devil’s Claw, were about to reach True Man Situ,

the elder of the Daluo Sect brandished a Heavy Sword, using the profound Divine Thought of the Heaven Void Realm to block the black shadow.

One palm, one sword clashed violently, sending powerful tremors through the Divine Thought Power.

The elder of the Daluo Sect backed off three steps.

But the black shadow only retreated one step.

This showed that the black shadow’s Divine Thought Power was even greater than that of the elder of the Daluo Sect.

Strangely enough, the black shadow was shrouded in mists of evil energy, heavy with Evil Thoughts, yet its silhouette was more human than monster.

The elder of the Daluo Sect frowned and asked:

"Are you... human?"

The black shadow, enveloped in sinister fog, remained silent.

"You scoundrel, hiding your head while showing your tail! I have loathed vile folks like you all my life!"

The elder of the Daluo Sect rebuked, then, lifting his Heavy Sword, invigorated with the Daluo Unification Sword Skill, struck heavily towards the black shadow.

His Daluo Unification Divine Thought Sword Technique was something he had been envious of and tried to imitate the True Jue from the Taixu Gate, something he came up with on his own, barely qualifying as a Divine Thought Sword Skill, but it was rather crude, not intricate at all.

Being in the Heaven Void Realm and a Sword Cultivator himself, as long as his Divine Thought could cut, the power remained formidable.

The elder of the Daluo Sect relied on this crude Daluo Unification Divine Thought Sword Skill to entangle with the black shadow.

The other three Heaven Void Realms also joined in to assist.

The black shadow had profound Divine Thought, seemingly proficient in Divine Thought Techniques, and its Divine Thought was mixed with sinister energy, stronger than any Primordial Spirit present.

But no matter how powerful he was, he couldn’t prevail against the combined might of four Heaven Void Realms, and after dozens of rounds, he found himself at a disadvantage.

After a while longer, the elder of the Daluo Sect found this black shadow’s aura peculiar and vaguely familiar, focusing his eyes, he used all his Divine Thought, his Sword Qi surged, striking the black shadow’s head with greater speed.

The black shadow had no choice but to dodge.

Seizing the opportunity, the elder of the Daluo Sect suddenly threw away his sword, instead, using his massive fists, powered by telekinesis, punched directly at the black shadow’s face.

The black shadow’s head was deformed by the intense punch.

A moment later, sinister energy reconvened, reforming a head, but the obscuring black fog had already been scattered by that punch.

The elder of the Daluo Sect, now having seen the black shadow’s face, gritted his teeth and shouted:

"Xiao Zhengming!"

Xiao Zhengming, the name of the Xiao family elder.

The Xiao family and the Daluo Sect had many interactions, therefore the elders of both were personal acquaintances.

But, precisely because of this, the elder of the Daluo Sect grew even angrier.

Xiao Zhengming, his face full of wickedness, seemed indifferent.

The elder of the Daluo Sect suppressed his anger and coldly asked, "When exactly did you become a dog for the Evil Demon?"

This question seemed to provoke the Xiao family elder as well. His face twisted with a hint of devoutness:
