Chapter 2270: Chapter 1049: The Leading Big Brother (Part 2)

What about Mo Hua’s promised death?

How did they suddenly become allies with Mo Hua?

Indeed, a man’s words are as unreliable as a ghost’s lies...

It’s a pity I considered giving them Spirit Stones to help deal with Mo Hua.

Lu Zhenlong’s pretty face frosted over, and she glared at Mo Hua before letting out a cold snort, "Let’s go!"

"Wait." Mo Hua called out to them.

Lu Zhenlong turned back, looking coldly at Mo Hua.

Mo Hua then said, "Don’t blame me for not warning you. If you leave, you might not make it out alive."

Lu Zhenlong sneered, "Whether I live or die is none of your concern."

Mo Hua shook his head, sighed softly, and said, "You think it’s simple, but it’s not as easy as dying."

Lu Zhenlong frowned, "What do you mean?"

Mo Hua said, "Did you see the sights along the way?"

Lu Zhenlong’s face turned pale, and she nodded slightly.

Mo Hua sighed, "Staying in this Large Formation for too long, one’s mind gets tainted by evil thoughts. Gradually losing reason, diminishing humanity, ending up killing each other, becoming greedy, bloodthirsty, neither human nor ghost..."

"And you all won’t be an exception."

Mo Hua’s expression was regretful, but the corner of his mouth carried a strange smile, "Your Purple Mist Sect’s dozen people, they look fine now, but soon someone will begin to turn evil, starting to slaughter each other."

"Once the killing is complete, human nature extinguished, you’ll become a Demon Monster."

"And Demon Monsters devour people."

"Your flesh will be nibbled away by your fellow disciples, piece by piece..."

"You’ll be gutted, your intestines spilling on the ground..."

"Your innards will be devoured clean..."

"Your face will be torn apart, covered in bite marks, bloody and indistinguishable, grotesque beyond recognition..."

Mo Hua’s small mouth, as if poisoned, with each word, made Lu Zhenlong’s face turn a shade paler.

Finally, Lu Zhenlong’s elegant face turned ashen, and she screamed in terror, covering her ears:

"Enough! Stop speaking!"

Mo Hua had much more bloody and cruel words left unsaid, seeing Lu Zhenlong couldn’t bear it any longer, he somewhat reluctantly stopped and asked her:

"Do you want to come with us?"

Lu Zhenlong’s face was ghastly pale, covering her ears, but saying nothing.

Mo Hua continued, "In the sword discussion arena, everyone is an enemy. But outside it, the Eight Great Gates should form the same alliance."

"We are looking for the way out. If you wish to join, then bring the disciples of Purple Mist Sect along."

"If not, it’s your choice. Either way, in the end, you and the people of Purple Mist Sect will die."

"Just your death might be a bit miserable, so prepare yourself mentally..."

After speaking, Mo Hua led everyone away.

After walking for a while, they indeed noticed Lu Zhenlong, bringing along the disciples of Purple Mist Sect, slowly followed up.

This Lu Zhenlong was unwilling in words, but her body still responded honestly.

Mo Hua nodded slightly.

Although he had some minor grudge with Lu Zhenlong, he wouldn’t stand by and watch her die.

Furthermore, Lu Zhenlong was the legitimate daughter of the prominent Lu Family in Kun State.

And the mighty Earth Sect is located in Kun State.

The First Grade Eleven Pattern Thick Earth Ultimate Formation is the Earth Sect’s legacy.

In the Solitary Mountain City tomb, that tomb robber called "Mr. Pi" whom I killed also seemed to bear the Earth Sect Formation.

The "Mojin Talisman" he carried was a token of the Earth Sect’s Secret Department.

And that centuries-old Contemplation Map of immense heritage—the "Emperor Heaven and Earth Diagram," is also hidden within the Earth Sect, regarded as its supreme treasure.

This Contemplation Map, named "Emperor Heaven and Earth," cannot be tolerated by the Taoist Court, hence it must be split into two. The "Emperor Heaven Diagram" was absorbed into the Taoist Court, leaving only the other half, the "Thick Earth Diagram," within the Earth Sect.

Even so, this "Thick Earth Diagram" remains a pivotal treasure of the Sect Protecting Faction, evidencing the object’s extraordinary nature.

Mo Hua always felt that he had a lot of unfinished ties with the Earth Sect.

At the very least, he truly wanted to take a look at this "Thick Earth Diagram."

Yet in Kun State, he knew no one.

So helping Lu Zhenlong now could be considered forming a small bond.

One day, when he goes to Kun State, this eldest lady of the Lu Family, Lu Zhenlong, would, at the very least, be a "familiar face."

Gaining more support leads to success, and fewer allies lead to failure; having more acquaintances makes things easier.

"But I wonder, would this woman hold a grudge and always remember our feud..."

Mo Hua pondered.

Yet being a mere incidental act, Mo Hua didn’t dwell too much on it.

Having brought Lu Zhenlong of Purple Mist Sect in too, agreeing on three terms, the team further expanded.

By doing this, beyond the Daluo Sect, Mo Hua had gathered almost all the geniuses of the Eight Great Gates together.

And soon enough, Mo Hua followed the threads of cause and found the Daluo Sect’s genius.

The once first Sword Control genius of the Daluo Sect, Ye Zhiyuan.

Ye Zhiyuan was the one most deeply "hurt" by Mo Hua.

At this moment, seeing Mo Hua, he truly was furious as enemies meeting again, red-eyed with rage.

But he didn’t act rashly; instead, he looked towards the other geniuses of the seven great gates "under" Mo Hua, shaking with shock and anger.

"These traitors!"

One by one, their slogans loud enough to shake the heavens, swearing to kill Mo Hua, only to turn around and follow him.

It was honestly ridiculous.

Yet they still called themselves Qianxue’s Geniuses.

Ye Zhiyuan sneered inwardly.

But when Mo Hua asked him, "Die here or leave with me," Ye Zhiyuan fell silent.

He battled inwardly for a long time, finally making a painful decision:

A true man knows when to bend and to stand tall.

To not bend is not truly courageous.

To stay beside the wicked tiger is genuine heroism.

First preserve one’s life, leverage Mo Hua’s power to escape danger, and when stronger, seek to avenge past humiliation.

"It’s not Mo Hua oppressing me to follow his orders, but me taking advantage of him to achieve my own goals!"
