Chapter 2364: Chapter 1080: Insane Beyond Redemption (2)

"It’s growing stronger again, we can’t delay any longer..."

Seeing Mo Hua being suppressed instead, the Evil God True Embryo’s face broke into a sinister smile.

He began to dominate the battle, launching attacks with punches and claws, pressing close to Mo Hua, surrounded by black mist and blood claws, along with various terrifying divine skills, which made Mo Hua unable to lift his head for the moment.

True Man Situ witnessed this, his heart skipped a beat, secretly thinking this was bad.

The Evil God of the Great Wilderness had a tremendous origin, with an ancient divinity, and an immensely deep foundation; its growth was simply too rapid!

It’s becoming uncontrollable!

Elsewhere, the Evil God True Embryo continued to grow in strength, and at the same time, he relentlessly suppressed Mo Hua with increasingly fierce and dangerous moves.

His consciousness was also continually leveling up.

And because he had devoured too many blood sacrifices from cultivators’ resentment before death, combined with hatching from a human divine fetus, and being still in his infancy, his divinity hadn’t fully awakened, thus his character was mixed with too much human evil.

Compared to a terrifying Evil God, the Master of the Great Wilderness now seemed more like a powerful malignant Evil Spirit, representing "Human Nature’s innate evil."

His eyes mocking and despising when looking at Mo Hua.

He even began to ridicule Mo Hua with increasingly fluent and sharp voice:

"Is this all your strength?"

"With only this level of divine way, you vainly attempt to obstruct my descent, it’s truly laughable!"

He clawed out fiercely, blood claws appeared, slashed into Mo Hua’s arm, creating several cuts, while simultaneously pushing Mo Hua back a few steps, proceeding swiftly, wielding fierce moves to suppress Mo Hua further while using ancient divine language to ridicule Mo Hua:

"You are an anomaly to the Heavenly Dao, daring to steal the divine way, overstepping the Great Dao’s taboo, yet not contemplating further advancement, devouring the ’Straw Dog’ of the heavens and earth, ascending above all beings, yet in your young immaturity, you unwisely make enemies with me, truly, a... fool whose mortal heart hasn’t died!"

The profundity of these words was extensive, revealing too much information, leaving Mo Hua momentarily stunned, furrowing his brows:

"Devouring Straw Dogs?"

He didn’t understand divine language, but speaking in human tongue, it seemed this Evil God True Embryo could also understand.

The Evil God saw Mo Hua’s "ignorant" appearance and felt disdain, subsequently speaking in a hoarse voice, in a lofty manner:

"Heaven and earth regard all things as Straw Dogs, divinity regards all living beings as Straw Dogs."

"Straw Dogs are merely offerings."

"Only by nurturing and devouring vast amounts of offerings can one consolidate and expand the divine way, becoming the supreme Celestial God, transcending all laws and merging with the Dao, achieving eternal life..."

"And now you willingly fall with such base Straw Dogs, destined to be forever disconnected from the Heavenly Dao. What you know, practice, and seek in the Dao is too inferior..."

Mo Hua’s gaze remained icy, indifferent.

The Evil God sneered, seemingly mocking Mo Hua’s stubborn ignorance.

"An anomaly is just an anomaly, crude and undeveloped, unable to comprehend the Heavenly Dao."

However, delaying here for so long is sufficient...

The Evil God’s eyes suddenly turned red, issuing a roar, a strong evil thought law spread around, causing disarray among all the demon monsters and primordial spirits present, rendering their minds unclear.

This seemed to be a newly awakened divine skill.

True Man Situ felt only the painful tumult of his primordial spirit and telekinesis; under such oppressive evil thought, the world fell silent, devoid of any sound.

True Man Situ was stunned, quickly realizing something, his expression changed dramatically.

He turned to look at Wenren Wan, and indeed saw Wenren Wan’s lips moving, yet uttering no sound.

"Stripped of the Law!"

"Within this nightmare, sound can’t be transmitted!"

"This is bad!"

True Man Situ immediately turned to the Evil God True Embryo, and indeed saw the Evil God True Embryo’s form suddenly grow, his body enveloped by armor of evil thoughts, strong and mighty, while the two horns atop his head became pitch-black and sinister, between which a stronger evil power gathered into a point, emitting terrifying ripples.

Awakening divine skill, stripping the Law, temporarily silencing Wenren Wan.

Such a maternal law can no longer restrain him.

And with the innate divine ability of the Evil God, he could form a killing blow, concentrate all evil power within an instant, completely erase the greatest threat, Mo Hua...

The Evil God’s innate divine ability is on par with top-level Divine Thought Skills.

And the potency of such divine skill killing moves is truly terrifying.

Even deities couldn’t possibly withstand it.

True Man Situ’s face turned pale, his body trembling in distress. He struggled to assist Mo Hua, but his divine thoughts were exhausted, his treasures all shattered, leaving him helpless, forced to watch it all, watching the earthshaking evil thoughts coalesce.

As True Man Situ was engulfed in despair, his gaze caught sight of a golden light.

A sharp golden light.

True Man Situ turned to see Mo Hua, facing off against the Evil God, now calmly composed, his hands held up above, his eyes sparkling like translucent glass, bursting with an intense radiance.

At the same time, Opening Mountain, Severing Gold, Li Fire, Gui Water Sword Formation successively manifested, ancient Tai Xu Sword Intent surged.

A palpitation-inducing Sword Dao aura gathered in Mo Hua’s palm.

"This is..."

True Man Situ’s pupils contracted, his face filled with astonishment.

A name he could hardly imagine appearing, one he never expected to encounter again in this lifetime, resurfaced in his mind.

"Tai Xu Divine Thought... Sword Transformation True Jue?!"

True Man Situ shuddered inside, his pupils shook:

"No, this is not any ordinary Sword Transformation True Jue, it is the ultimate form of Sword Transformation... Godslaying Sword?!"
