Chapter 2195: Chapter 1026: Duanmu Qing_2

Ao Zheng really didn’t dare to underestimate.

But Ao Zheng is also a prodigy, a powerful body cultivator, inherently confident and arrogant. He only snorted coldly and walked towards the mountain gate.

Ao Zheng didn’t say anything.

He wanted to annihilate this team from the Taixu Gate, to kill Mo Hua.

But he disdained to say such words before the battle.

Everything would be said after it was done.

After annihilating the team from Taixu Gate and killing Mo Hua, he would then speak mockingly.

This was a display of strength.

If one boasted without achieving, it was shallow and incompetent.

Everything would be resolved on the sword discussion platform, with real weapons and combat.

Ignoring Mo Hua, Ao Zheng continued towards the Inner Mountain, but as he passed by Mo Hua, he suddenly heard a clear yet cold voice by his ear:

"Your Longding Sect’s Ao Zhan offended me last time..."

Ao Zheng hesitated, turned his head, and looked at Mo Hua.

Mo Hua raised his chin slightly, cold and arrogant, "In this Monster Hunting competition, I will settle this score exactly as it was."

"Our Taixu Gate is not to be trifled with."

"All five of you must die!"

Hearing this, Ao Zheng was filled with rage, his face contorted, and he glared fiercely at Mo Hua.

But the moment he met Mo Hua’s deep and mysterious gaze, a shudder rose up from within him, causing the words of mockery to get stuck in his throat.

Mo Hua finished speaking, snorted coldly, and swaggered off like the "Evil Bully" of the Taixu Gate, with Linghu Xiao and others in tow.

Other disciples of the Longding Sect were momentarily overwhelmed by Mo Hua’s presence and momentarily forgot to speak any "harsh words".

After Mo Hua left, they finally came to their senses, feeling anger inside.

Ao Zheng’s gaze was icy, his brows furrowed, but ultimately, his mind calmed a bit.

Mo Hua’s words just now still echoed in his ears.

The look in the eyes, filled with killing intent and Evil Qi, was also deeply imprinted in his mind.

The killing intent in those eyes was real.

The Evil Qi was extremely intense.

Without experiencing real slaughter, it would be difficult to have such a gaze.

This Mo Hua was definitely a "ruthless" and "cold-hearted" person.

From that brief exchange of glances, Ao Zheng had a first-hand understanding.

Moreover, he said...

To settle a score?

Ao Zheng frowned in contemplation, slowly recalling.

Last time, during the sword discussion between Taixu Gate and Longding Sect, Mo Hua and his team were first killed by Ao Zhan and then hunted the monsters.

Taixu Gate had no power to resist and lost completely.

"Is this the debt that Mo Hua wants to settle?"

"Does he want to do like Ao Zhan did, first kill the five of us, then hunt the monsters, to save face and seek revenge?"

Ao Zheng slowly nodded, then sneered internally:

"Just a mere Taixu Gate, what do they rely on?"

"On Linghu Xiao, perhaps?"

Ao Zheng’s heart suddenly skipped a beat.

Formations?!

The recent siege warfare between Taixu Gate and Qian Taoist Sect, where the entire city was leveled by the wildly exploding formations, was still vivid in his mind.

Scenes like that made even prodigies like Ao Zheng feel uneasy.

Although on the surface, the Elders of Mount of Tao said that the explosion of the formations was an "accidental mistake" unrelated to Mo Hua.

But is that really the truth?

Mo Hua is the Formation Leader after all.

What kind of terrifying Killing Formation does he hold in his hand, no one knows.

If he uses these Killing Formations in this Monster Hunting competition...

Ao Zheng’s heart grew serious, and his expression turned grave as he instructed:

"Today’s Monster Hunting competition, be wary of formations."

"Mo Hua, this sinister and vicious child, is skilled in using formations, likes to scheme and ambush, and is vengeful. You must be on guard."

"Avoid the formations, and Linghu Xiao alone won’t hold the line. Taixu Gate is not to be feared."

"Yes!" the other Longding Sect disciples responded.

...

Inside the Inner Mountain of Dao Mountain.

The "Evil Bully" Mo Hua walked in front.

Cheng Mo and others walked behind.

After a while, seeing no one around, Situ Jian finally couldn’t help but ask in a low voice:

"Junior Brother, is it not wise for us to provoke Longding Sect like this? Are we really going to kill them later?"

"We’re not going to fight them." Mo Hua said.

Situ Jian was taken aback, and the others were also a bit confused.

Mo Hua lowered his voice and said:

"I’m tricking that big fool..."

"The Longding Sect is too strong, we can’t beat them. If we can’t beat them, why fight them directly?"

"I said some harsh words to make them suspicious. Then we’ll quickly push through."

"This is a Monster Hunting competition, fight quickly to kill the Monster Beasts, and we’ll win, who cares about them?"

Mo Hua looked as if it was only natural.

Linghu Xiao and the others looked at Mo Hua, their expressions became complicated.

For a moment, they didn’t know whether to praise Mo Hua’s wisdom or his cunning.

Mo Hua sighed slightly.

He knew it, these Junior Brothers were indeed still too "green".

"Acting skills" also needed honing.

That’s why he hadn’t explained it before.

The fear was that Linghu Xiao and the others, with poor acting skills, would give it away.

Mo Hua whispered again:

"Remember, once we enter the field later, I’ll signal the location of the Monster Beasts, and you all rush over with me to take them down. Every second counts, the faster the better!"

Linghu Xiao and the others solemnly nodded.

...

Thereafter, the sword discussion began.

As soon as Mo Hua entered the field, he looked around, released his formidable Divine Sense, and within an instant, he found the trace of the Monster Beasts.

It was a late Second Grade Bear Demon.

Mo Hua pointed, "Charge!"

Linghu Xiao and the others wasted no time and used their movement techniques to dash towards the direction Mo Hua indicated.

Generally speaking, finding Monster Beasts is one of the challenges in the Monster Hunting competition.

How to find the position of the Monster Beasts in the complex terrain of mountains and forests, based on their habits and hunting tracks, also takes some time.

But unfortunately, Mo Hua’s Divine Sense was simply too abnormal.
