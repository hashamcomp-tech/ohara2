Chapter 2243: Chapter 1040: The Sinister Formation Master (3)

They knew that Junior Brother wanted them to finish the battle quickly.

Kill!

The Tai’a five brothers shouted in unison, their battle spirits soaring, Spiritual Power boiling, resembling five Demon Gods, wielding their Giant Swords for the final slaughter.

Adorned in the Water Element Source Robe, empowered by the force of the Water Moon, Linghu Xiao began to charge his sword, further elevating the power of the Chongxu Sword.

The other disciples of Taixu Gate, their momentum unstoppable, began to encircle for the kill.

On top of that, Mo Hua’s Divine Sense Control Ink, seamlessly coordinating with the Formation.

Disciples of the Four Sects and Seven Gates began to fall one by one.

Song Kui of the Sever Gold Sect was stabbed to death by Yang Qianjun’s spear;

Qin Cangliu of the Gui Water Sect died under Linghu Xiao’s Sword Qi;

Feng Zichen of the Carefree Sect, exhausted from prolonged battle, was blocked by the joint forces of Situ Jian and Cheng Mo, finally dying under Ouyang Xuan’s sword;

Shi Tiangang of the Vajra Sect was killed by the chaos of the Tai’a five brothers’ swords.

...

With the death of these top talents from the Eight Great Gates, the remaining disciples were even more powerless, slaughtered swiftly and completely.

Thus, every disciple of the Four Sects and Seven Gates within the valley was annihilated.

The valley was completely cleared.

This was the first time Mo Hua’s Five Elements Source Armor was displayed in public.

And this first display, this first slaughter, helped Taixu Gate achieve victory against the odds, annihilating over a hundred sect talents from Qianxue.

Outside the Sword Discussion arena, there was dead silence.

All cultivators watching were stunned and frightened.

"They’re all killed..."

"Taixu Gate, with just thirty people, slew over a hundred talents from the Four Sects and Seven Gates, including top-tier talents from the Eight Great Gates?!"

Is this Taixu Gate?

Is this Formation Leader Mo Hua?!

Damn ruthless...

Gasps of disbelief intermittently arose in the crowd.

Only now did they realize, they had misunderstood this Sword Discussion from the start.

This was never the Four Sects and Seven Gates "besieging" Taixu Gate.

It was Taixu Gate’s "counter-siege" against the Four Sects and Seven Gates!

Taixu Gate’s goal was not defense, but slaughter!

And Taixu Gate displayed this power.

In this bloody battle, Taixu Gate, supported by the Five Elements Source Armor and the Formation, united fiercely, the damage they dealt being terrifying, even comparable to the Four Great Sects.

All cultivators watching were deeply shocked.

Some who previously underestimated Taixu Gate couldn’t help but feel respect now.

Some who had mocked Mo Hua felt their faces burning with shame.

The entire Taixu Gate, whether Elders or Disciples, witnessing Mo Hua’s slaughter, were ecstatic, their blood boiling.

Even the Sect Leaders of the Three Mountains felt exhilarated.

This battle won them prestige and display, truly giving them great honor as Sect Leaders.

In stark contrast, the other Seven Gates’ Sect Leaders had faces as dark as a cauldron.

Even some Sect Ancestors started to frown, showing displeasure...

...

Inside the arena, the Sword Discussion continued.

Taixu Gate, leveraging the Five Elements Source Armor and Mo Hua’s Formation, counter-sieged over a hundred sect talents from the Four Sects and Seven Gates.

This intense battle seemed complex with back-and-forth exchanges.

However, the entire process took less than an hour.

During this period, the talents within the valley were besieged by Taixu Gate.

The talents outside the valley continuously attacked the Sealing Mountain Formation set by Mo Hua.

But the terrain was narrow.

This was a layout reinforced with a Third Grade Array by the Mount of Tao Elder fearing Mo Hua’s "mischief," difficult to alter.

Hence, at the same time, it couldn’t accommodate many people breaking the Formation.

Furthermore, Mo Hua’s Compound Formation was a top-tier Second Grade Formation, integrated with the mountains, resonating with the earth.

Under the condition that Shen Linsu and the other Qianxue Four Talents didn’t make a move, breaching it was extremely difficult.

Thus, disciples from the Four Sects and Seven Gates outside the valley, isolated by Mo Hua’s Compound Formation, could only watch helplessly as their companions were slaughtered by Taixu Gate.

This scene was equally tragic and shocking for them.

To the extent that a chill rose in their hearts, none dared to breach the Formation.

Break the Formation, and then what?

Should they charge in, only to be slaughtered by Mo Hua and them down to the last man?

They saw clearly the slaughter unfolding within the valley.

The current strength of Taixu Gate was terrifying.

The Tai’a five brothers’ Giant Swords killed countless, instilling fear from just watching.

Not to mention the Sword Dao talent Linghu Xiao, and Formation Leader Mo Hua.

Even renowned figures like Shi Tiangang, Qin Cangliu, and Feng Zichen from the Seven Gates were slain one by one, let alone them.

At this moment, the reason they hadn’t died wasn’t because of their strength.

It was purely luck, not having stepped into this valley, thus isolated by Mo Hua’s Compound Formation.

If they had set foot into the valley, they would have been the ones to die.

Thinking of this, everyone instinctively stepped back.

The valley entrance seemed like "the gate of hell," anyone entering was destined to die.

In their eyes, Mo Hua was the "Little Yama" presiding over the Nine Nether.

Amid the eerie calm, it all quieted down.

The talents from the Four Sects and Seven Gates outside the valley dared not act recklessly.

Inside the valley, Mo Hua and his allies finally got a moment’s respite.

Yet Mo Hua didn’t forget the most crucial task:

"Collect all the Storage Bags and bring them to me."

This was the very reason he annihilated these hundred talents.

He wanted to seize their Storage Bags.

At the Sword Discussion Conference, a talent’s Storage Bag contained Spiritual Artifacts, Formation media, Pills, Spirit Stones, Spiritual Ink, and such items.

Among them, Spirit Stones and Spiritual Ink were vitally important to Mo Hua.

Due to strict control over "resources" at the Sword Discussion Conference.

Taixu Gate’s disciples had insufficient Spirit Stones and Spiritual Ink for Mo Hua’s needs.

To set up more Formations, he required more Spiritual Ink.

And to activate more Formations, he similarly needed more Spirit Stones.

Acquiring these two items could only be achieved through "slaughtering" a considerable number of talents and obtaining a bounty of Storage Bags.

Only with sufficient Spiritual Ink and Spirit Stones, could he continue implementing his plan to construct Formations.

This is called sustaining battle through battle.

Now, his plan was halfway successful: adequate Spirit Stones and Spiritual Ink were acquired.

Mo Hua also was ready to commence the next step of his plan.

What he needed to do was simple: draw formations, continuously draw formations, draw more and more formations, covering every part of the valley with formations.

Mo Hua meditated for a while, restoring his Divine Sense, then resumed drawing formations.

Various Spiritual Ink transformed into threads, guided by him, winding within the valley, condensing into dense Formation Patterns, forming Formation after Formation...

And outside the valley.

Shen Linsu, indifferent and unmoved by everything, suddenly felt a twinge of unease.

He slowly furrowed his brow, his gaze growing colder.

He sensed a subtle, inexplicable feeling of apprehension...
