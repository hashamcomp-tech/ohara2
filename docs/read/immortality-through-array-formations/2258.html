Chapter 2258: Chapter 1045: Shocking Upheaval (Part 2)

But now, it’s different.

Without any triggering condition, the Fifth Grade Large Formation suddenly began to operate at full capacity...

"Someone tampered with the Fifth Grade Formation?!"

"What is the purpose?"

Many Heaven Void Ancestors turned to look at Elder Master Xun.

Elder Master Xun of the Taixu Gate is one of the few Fifth Grade Formation Ancestors in the Qianxue State Boundary.

Elder Master Xun frowned, watching the light of the formation on the horizon, sensing the flow of formation patterns, and as he reverse-engineered the operation of the formation and its pivot’s structure in his mind, he judged the law structures involved in the reoperation of the large formation...

Not for killing, not for defense, not to protect disciples, but...

Elder Master Xun’s heart trembled:

"Teleportation?!"

He immediately realized something, feeling a chill in his heart, and urgently shouted:

"Hurry! Notify the elders of Mount of Tao, forcibly seal the formation eye, and cut off the spiritual power supply to the large formation!"

The Fifth Grade Formation of Mount of Tao, he could not interfere.

The purpose of building this large formation was to protect the sword discussion disciples from all external interference.

These interferences, primarily, are from the Heaven Void Ancestors of various sects and aristocratic families.

After all, if Heaven Void Ancestors really interfere with the competition, no one can stop them.

This also means, the primary precaution of this large formation is against these Heaven Void Ancestors present.

Everything within the large formation they cannot interfere with and are not allowed to.

Therefore, forcibly sealing the formation eye and cutting off the formation is something that only the elders of Mount of Tao can handle, he cannot intervene.

But such a matter is not trivial, Mount of Tao cannot easily make a decision.

And at this moment, it’s all too late.

The death of "Elder Shen" was the key, activating the large formation.

The large formation operated as planned, and one by one, blue lights of "teleportation" began to flicker.

All disciples participating in the Shura battle, who were defeated and withdrew, were reverse teleported, and began a "re-entry".

In a desolate Shura field.

One by one, figures who had once "died" began to reappear.

Linghu Xiao, Ouyang Xuan, Cheng Mo, Situ Jian, the five brothers of Tai’a, Hao Xuan...

These disciples of Taixu Gate, with the flickering of blue light, gathered again around Mo Hua.

Besides Taixu Gate, the Four Great Sects, Eight Great Gates, and even some Heavenly Pride Disciples of the Twelve Streams also appeared one by one...

Shen Cangfeng, Ao Zheng, Xiao Ruohan, Shi Tiangang, Ye Zhiyuan, Qin Cangliu, Feng Zichen, Song Kui...

Looking around, densely packed, all of them were Mo Hua’s "enemies".

But at this moment, they had no time to "seek revenge" on Mo Hua, just looking at each other in confusion, not understanding what happened, why they were defeated and withdrawn, yet were teleported back again.

No one answered them.

And the teleportation continued.

Blue light began to flicker again, and these disciples gathered in the Shura field disappeared one by one.

When they came to their senses, calmed down, and looked around, they saw towering mountains and low clouds, surrounded by mist, a sense of climbing the peak, reaching for the sky, and a feeling like an immortal.

Soon, some disciples reacted:

"Xuantian Peak!"

"Is this Xuantian Peak?!"

The highest point of Mount of Tao, one peak, one tower.

The peak is Xuantian Peak, the tower is Sword Observation Tower.

The Sword Observation Tower is a place for the ancestors of aristocratic families, the direct lineage of Taoist State, and the nobles of the court pavilion to watch the sword, hidden in the clouds, invisible to ordinary people.

The highest point of Mount of Tao visible to everyone is the towering, sky-pointing Xuantian Peak.

Xuantian Peak is also the final battleground for the "Heaven" level sword discussion at the Sword Discussion Conference.

After the fierce battles at the Earth level sword discussion, the successful Heavenly Pride Disciples from various sects gather at the top of Xuantian Peak, to have a final decisive battle with the strongest Heavenly Pride of Qianxue.

But at this moment, these Heavenly Pride Disciples unexpectedly found that all of them, were teleported to the top of Xuantian Peak.

"What happened?"

"What does this mean?"

The crowd was shocked and bewildered.

But even at this point, the teleportation didn’t stop.

An extremely dazzling light suddenly erupted over the Fifth Grade Formation.

An even sharper Spirit Roar sounded.

A massive amount of Spirit Stone was devoured in an instant, converted to spiritual power supplying the large formation.

Powerful spiritual power fluctuations, like a hurricane, swept in all directions.

Vast as an ocean, the blue light swept straight into the sky, engulfing the entire Xuantian Peak.

The operation of the Fifth Grade Formation of Mount of Tao was nearly at its limit.

Then, under the incredible gazes of countless cultivators, the towering, sky-reaching Xuantian Peak was cut off by Void Power from its roots, and then directly...

Disappeared.

Everyone was astonished.

The Heaven Void Ancestors were also greatly shaken.

Xuantian Peak... was teleported away?!

Right before their eyes, the Xuantian Peak, which had stood for over ten thousand years in the Qianxue State Boundary, was teleported away by the Fifth Grade Formation of Mount of Tao?

And on Xuantian Peak, there were hundreds of top disciples from the Four Sects and Seven Gates!

These are the real treasures, the marrow and blood, of the major sects.

"Quick!" A Heaven Void Ancestor shouted in shock and anger, "Find out where Xuantian Peak was teleported to!"

The teleportation involved Fifth Grade Heaven Void Power.

The teleportation conditions are harsh, and restrictions are numerous, let alone such a huge Xuantian Peak, even if teleported, it couldn’t be too far, at most it’s near the Fifth Grade Qianxue State Boundary.

One by one, the Heaven Void Ancestors unfurled their Divine Sense.

Astonishing Heaven Void Power, unrestrained, swept across the entire Qianxue State Boundary.

There were also Heaven Void Ancestors who took out the treasured Heaven Peeping Mirror, overseeing the entire state boundary.
