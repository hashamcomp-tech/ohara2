Chapter 2207: Chapter 1029: Grand Luo Flying Sword Control (Part

"With this Sword Array, even facing the sword prodigies of the Daluo Sect, it will be invincible and unmatched in its killing prowess..."

Mo Hua did not hold back on words of praise.

The Taia Sect Leader felt utterly relaxed at hearing this.

But deep down, he was well aware.

Sword Array and Swordsmanship are not the same thing.

How can you use a Sword Array meant for forging swords to counter Sword Control techniques?

Mo Hua, this child, is definitely just humoring him.

But after all, he is the Sect Leader, and this child is a Sect Disciple. Saying something false in his presence seems unlikely...

The Taia Sect Leader hesitated a bit and asked Mo Hua:

"Really?"

"Mm!" Mo Hua nodded repeatedly, his gaze clear.

If nothing else, just his face matched with that gaze makes one feel like this child couldn’t possibly be lying.

The Taia Sect Leader was momentarily at a loss.

A Sword Array is not an ordinary thing; it’s a core heritage, not to be disclosed lightly.

However, the upcoming Sword Discussion Conference is equally of utmost importance. Losing this event would bring irrevocable regret.

The Taia Sect Leader frowned, pondered for a moment, steeled his heart, and waved his hand:

"Alright! I’ll give it to you!"

You can’t catch wolf cubs without risking the kids.

No matter how precious the Sword Array is, it is not as important as the position of the leader of the Eight Great Gates.

Moreover, this trip also aims to "befriend" Mo Hua.

If one is to befriend Mo Hua, one shouldn’t be stingy with the Sword Array.

What a genius Mo Hua is, being the Qianxue Array Dao Leader, the "treasured gem" of the Taixu Gate’s ancestor. Without some real benefits, how could one build a connection?

In these times, who would open their heart without dining together?

Empty promises and hollow talk provide no benefits and fool no one.

Mo Hua was equally delighted.

He did not expect the Taia Sect Leader to be so generous.

Even gifting the Sword Array to him.

"Thank you, Sect Leader!" Mo Hua said with a smile.

"I can show you the Sword Array, but..." The Taia Sect Leader was still a bit hesitant, "Will this Sword Array really be useful for tomorrow’s discussion?"

"Mm!" Mo Hua nodded, "With this Sword Array, tomorrow’s discussion will be ’simpler’."

The Taia Sect Leader was half convinced and half doubtful.

"But the discussion is tomorrow; if you look at it now, is it still in time?"

"No problem," Mo Hua assured, "I learn Formations very quickly."

No, even if you learn Formations quickly, it can’t possibly be that quick...

The Taia Sect Leader silently thought to himself.

But since he promised, it was meaningless to dwell on these concerns.

The Taia Sect Leader, known for his swift and decisive actions, immediately said, "Wait, I’ll have someone deliver it to you shortly."

Mo Hua, delighted, said, "Sect Leader, you are so kind!"

The Taia Mountain Sect Leader was quickly categorized as a "great person" in his heart.

...

Meanwhile.

In the Tumo Order.

A person named "A Sword’s Distance" arrogantly said:

"What is the Taixu Gate worth?"

"If not for the Three Sects Unification, they don’t even have a decent sword technique."

"At the Sword Discussion Conference, without a Sword Dao Heritage, what sword are they discussing? It’s truly laughable."

"Among their entire sect, only Linghu Xiao is worth a look."

"Mo Hua?"

"Merely a Formation Master seeking fame and acclaim. Can’t even lift a sword yet wants to discuss swords with others?"

"Even Linghu Xiao is just my defeated opponent. To be equated with me, is he worthy?"

...

This reckless and verbose tone was very familiar, prompting someone to say:

"Ye Zhiyuan?"

"So what if I am?"

Someone questioned, "How did you get in here? You don’t have a grudge with Mo Hua, do you?"

"Don’t worry about that..."

"A Sword’s Distance:" "I only ask you this, is it true that killing Mo Hua would make me the Alliance Hierarch?"

The group was silent.

They disapproved of Ye Zhiyuan’s arrogance, but didn’t want to break their promise either.

"That’s right."

"Good!" A Sword’s Distance said, "Tomorrow, with a single sword, I will take Mo Hua’s head, and become your Alliance Hierarch!"

"If you can’t even kill Mo Hua, what’s the point of calling yourself a prodigy?"

"A bunch of useless individuals. All of you just wait and see."

"Tomorrow, your Alliance Hierarch will be crowned king!"

The group listened to his words, irritated but helpless.
