Chapter 1765: Chapter 889: Taixu (Part 3)

"Take a ’Tai’ character from Tai’a Sect and take a ’Xu’ character from Rushing Void Sect to make up..."

Taia Sect Leader turned back to the future, immediately widened his eyes, and slammed the table, saying:

"Isn’t this Taixu?! Well, I wondered why you kept silent, you’ve been waiting for this moment, haven’t you?"

He was deceived by this seemingly calm demeanor.

He thought, regarding the sect’s name, how could anyone truly be indifferent?

After all this time, this seemingly Zen-like guy was the most insidious!

Taixu Sect Master smiled slightly and said:

"You agreed to take characters from your two sects’ names, it’s you who agreed; it’s you who agreed that I choose."

"Since you’ve all agreed, how about this: after the Three Sects Unification, it will be called ’Taixu Gate’."

"All of us are Sect Leaders, leading a sect, our words carry the weight of Mount Tai, surely we can’t go back on our words..."

Taia Sect Leader’s face turned red.

Chongxu Sect Leader was also speechless.

But the words were already spoken, the initiative was given by them, to backtrack now would be akin to slapping their own faces.

Chongxu Sect Leader sighed: "Then let it be."

Taia Sect Leader also said unwillingly: "Then use ’Taixu’ for the name..."

Taixu Sect Master smiled calmly.

Of course, all three of them knew in their hearts.

Though they debated for a while, the name of the new sect would ultimately be decided by Taixu Gate.

The power is greater than people, and the power dynamics between sects ultimately depend on strength.

Currently, Taixu Gate ranks third among the Eight Great Gates, its reputation the most prominent.

In terms of heritage, the Three Sects share the same lineage, complementing each other, making it hard to truly distinguish superiority.

But in sword-casting or Sword Qi, no matter how strong, they aren’t unique.

The most unique is indeed Taixu Gate’s Divine Thought Sword Skill.

Even if the Divine Thought Sword Skill can’t be transmitted, improving upon it to iterate the Sword Intent Technique would be a profound Sword Dao heritage.

Sword Intent, while weak during Foundation Building, appearing insignificant, once cultivated to Golden Core, when Divine Sense reaches a certain depth, Sword Intent reveals its sharpness.

Feather Transformation is even stronger, and thereafter, the higher the cultivation, the more terrifying the Sword Intent.

Such profound material only exists in sects with deep foundations.

Calculating this way, in terms of heritage, Taixu Gate’s foundation is actually the deepest.

And if it’s about disciples, it’s even more apparent.

Taia Sect has the most disciples, but after this ordeal, few excel.

Rushing Void Sect indeed has a Sword Dao Genius, but after all, that’s for the future, hasn’t participated in Debating Sword, actual combat power unknown.

But even if really strong, at most, they can just rival top geniuses of the Four Great Sects.

But Taixu Gate is different.

Taixu Gate has a "monster."

Foundation Building Middle Stage, first in Array Competition, Array Leader, this is almost crushing, cliff-like genius, virtually "pre-appointed" as next Array Leader.

This is reality, an unprecedented genius.

Thus counting all factors, Taixu Gate is the strongest.

Sect’s name carries great significance, they are the Sect Leaders of their sects, no matter what, must strive for it, exert the utmost effort.

But if can’t achieve, there’s nothing to be done.

In this matter, Taixu Sect Master seemed to toy with small schemes, but in reality, gave everyone a way to step down.

Manipulated by Taixu Gate, only then lost the sect’s name.

Better than admitting that they truly aren’t as good as Taixu Gate, hence compelled to compromise, using "Taixu Gate" as the name is preferred.

Moreover, Taixu Sect Master’s reasoning is actually correct.

Taixu does indeed contain Taia Sect’s "Tai" and Rushing Void Sect’s "Xu," truly suitable as the name for the newly merged sect.

Thus, this matter was settled.

Taixu Sect Master changed from his leisurely manner, gave a formal salute to the other two Sect Leaders, solemnly said:

"Rest assured, the Three Sects Unification won’t be biased. The names of each sect won’t be discarded."

"From now on, the Three Gates merge into one, collectively known as ’Taixu Gate,’ each sect’s disciples will be referred to by their Mountain Gate."

"Taia Mountain, Mount Chongxu, Taixu Mountain, three mountain disciples, merge into one lineage."

"Three mountains one lineage, same alliance, sharing a boat, united heart!"

Taia Sect Leader and Chongxu Sect Leader also gave salutes and said:

"Three mountains one lineage, united heart!"

The voices of the three Sect Leaders echoed within Taixu Mountain.

From this point, the greatest sect at the Qian Learning State Boundary, after establishing their own sect, enduring a thousand years of trials and tribulations, have come together again.

...

The decision of Three Sects Unification immediately caused an uproar within the Qian Learning State Boundary.

Especially among the Four Great Sects, from top to bottom, there was a great disturbance.

"Such... shamelessness!"

"Too thick-skinned!"

"How could they do this? Don’t they want their sect foundation anymore? To agree just like this?"

"What a joke! Established their own sect for so many years, why play ’Long-unity will divide, long-division will unite’?"

Some were puzzled: "Why was there no rumor earlier?"

"Cause and Effect was concealed..."

"It’s the handiwork of Taixu Gate Ancestor..."

"No, it’s not that simple... it’s because of Taixu Gate’s monster."

"We’ve been distracted by Taixu Gate’s Array Demon, consuming too much time and Heavenly Mechanism Calculation Power, everyone calculating that shocking monster, resulting in a lack of awareness of Taixu Gate’s schemes."

"Using the sudden emergence of the Array ’monster’ to confuse, conceal Cause and Effect, thus secretly advancing the Three Sects Unification..."

"With thunderous means, a blitz, by the time we realized, it was too late..."
