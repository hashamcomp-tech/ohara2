Chapter 1647: Chapter 855: Roll Call_2

No wonder the Taoist Court wiped them out...

It’s just that he didn’t really understand the intricacies inside, he could only watch the spectacle, and after a while, he felt bored.

Mo Hua yawned, his eyelids drooped, and he pretended to fall asleep.

Master Xiao glanced at him and did not ask, but concentrated, staring intently at the Water Yama.

Mo Hua was actually not asleep.

Thanks to the mystical Taoist Stele, he generally doesn’t need to sleep.

He was just resting with his eyes closed, contemplating some things.

The altar is hidden deep within the Dragon King Temple.

But this altar is different from the one in Bi Mountain Demon Cavern and even the small fishing boat’s altar; it may have existed for a long time.

With the desire between men and women on the Rouge Boat as bait, and such a vast Dragon King Temple as protection,

This must be a very crucial altar.

With his ability alone, attempting to sneak into the Dragon King Temple is quite difficult, and approaching the altar is almost impossible.

The Dragon King Temple is too large, the halls interconnecting.

The temple guards are all dead, Mr. Witch is a monster.

The main door is locked by Mr. Witch, the inner hall is a sea of blood, and beneath the blood sea lies a maze.

Within the maze, there are countless blood zombies.

This layer upon layer of obstacles and dangers make it impossible for him to enter alone.

Therefore, he can only find people to work and take risks for him.

Such a thing is very dangerous.

Senior Brother Feng and Sister Qianqian are not suitable, their cultivation is not enough.

Uncle Gu and Xia Canonical Official do have enough cultivation, but they count as "one of us."

As everyone knows, exploring secret realms generally costs "teammates."

Mo Hua is somewhat unwilling to let Uncle Gu and the others take risks for him.

Therefore, Master Xiao and several others are the best candidates.

And on this journey, without Xie Liu paving the way, Shui Yan opening the door, and Master Xiao dealing with Mr. Witch turned monster, he really might not have made it into this inner hall.

However, although the situation is generally developing according to his calculations, there are still some surprises.

The biggest surprise was Master Xiao.

Mo Hua truly didn’t expect that Master Xiao would be this strong.

He possesses the orthodox core direct lineage inheritance of the Water Prison Sect, not to mention cultivation technique and movement technique, and with the Water Prison Skill and Blood Pupil Skill, it’s even more terrifying.

Such skills, strictly speaking, are only eligible for the sect leader of Water Prison Sect to learn.

To have learned these skills, previously, Master Xiao was almost supposed to be the designated sect leader of the Twelve Streams Water Prison Sect.

Looking across the entire Qian Learning State Boundary, there are not many Golden Cores with such treatment.

Saying this, it’s no wonder Uncle Gu and Xia Canonical Official couldn’t beat Master Xiao.

Uncle Gu is of the direct lineage of the Gu Family, but is not favored, though he learned the core spell points of the Gu Family, it’s definitely inferior compared to the true sect leader’s inheritance.

Xia Canonical Official is the same.

She is from the direct lineage, but there are quite a few in the Xia family’s direct lineage, and her inheritance is definitely not the most core.

No matter how deep the Xia family’s wealth, the inheritance of a direct lineage disciple cannot be more powerful than the sect leader inheritance of the Twelve Sects.

What’s more, the smiling tiger is an old hidden rogue.

He had always remained unremarkable, all his skills were suddenly displayed at critical moments, catching Uncle Gu and Sister Xia off guard, falling into his trap is not surprising.

Really can’t blame Uncle Gu and Sister Xia...

However, Master Xiao’s actions have reminded Mo Hua.

Killer moves and trump cards should be kept as tight as possible and not used until the last moment; then they are deadly.

Indeed, there is definitely someone to learn from.

There are many things on Master Xiao worth learning for himself.

Thinking of this, although Mo Hua kept his eyes closed pretending to sleep, he couldn’t help but nod.

Beside him, Master Xiao was puzzled.

Seeing Mo Hua nodding even while asleep, he was a bit uneasy.

That kid...what did he suddenly realize again?

Surely he has a keen mind; just wonder where this little devil learned his sly and cunning techniques...

...

This place is not suitable for a long stay, after the group rested for a while, recovering from any injuries, they set off again.

Mo Hua had "woken up," so he didn’t need Shui Yan to carry him anymore; instead, he wore the Spirit-binding Lock, following behind Master Xiao.

Xia Canonical Official, Gu Changhuai, and Ouyang Feng, and Hua Qianqian all sighed in relief upon seeing Mo Hua.

Hua Qianqian asked: "Junior Brother Mo, are you alright?"

"Mm-hmm." Mo Hua nodded, "Don’t worry, Sister, I’m alright."

Hua Qianqian finally put her mind at ease.

"Alright, no more reminiscing, better think of a way to leave early." Master Xiao said.

Everyone understood and didn’t say much, instead continuing to wander in the maze, trying to avoid the endless blood zombies and find the way out.

Arriving at an intersection, Master Xiao looked around, frowned, thought for a moment, and prepared to go right.

Mo Hua suddenly said: "Go left."

Master Xiao was slightly stunned, his gaze deepened, looking at Mo Hua.

Mo Hua said candidly: "The exit is on the left."

Master Xiao’s expression tightened, he asked in a deep voice: "How do you know?"

Mo Hua decided not to hide his skills.

The time is almost ripe, having seen the altar and entered the maze, the gatekeeper "Mr. Witch’s" various means have mostly been forced out, there’s no need to delay anymore.

It’s time to close the net.

Mo Hua said: "Ancestor taught me a Formation, related to Mystery Formation, containing mystery formation layout, directional path, formation changes, and so on..."

"This White Bone Maze secretly aligns with the Mystery Formation, embedded with some blood sacrifice corpse raising formations. As long as you follow the Formation’s direction, you can determine the maze’s location and safely leave here..."
