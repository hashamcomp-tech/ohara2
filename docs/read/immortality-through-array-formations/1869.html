Chapter 1869: Chapter 921: Four Symbols Azure Dragon

"Don’t forget, why the Valley of Ten Thousand Demons was established..."

"So many cultivators were used for experiments, integrating various monster patterns into their bodies, causing them to degenerate into monster cultivators. The purpose was to keep making mistakes, iterating, and integrating, ultimately to nurture this ’Dragon’."

"What does the Dragon signify?"

"The Four Symbols Dragon Pattern is a genuine divine beast pattern, only qualified to be refined by the Great Wilderness Imperial Family. Once refined, the bearer possesses the Green Dragon Power, their skin like dragon scales, indestructible, their roar like that of a dragon, full of majesty, all Ten Thousand Demons surrender, not daring to disobey..."

"You follow the path of the Bear Demon, though strong, don’t think of such a bold move against Big Brother, otherwise, you won’t even know how you died..."

"But, I see Big Brother he’s..."

"Shut up, don’t mention this matter again!"

...

Mo Hua held the Guest Elder Order, staring at the words deciphered from the Magnetic Patterns, his expression blank, but his heart was surging with turbulence.

Dragon Pattern!

Divine Beast Pattern!

Four Symbols Green Dragon Array Pattern?!

The real purpose of nurturing so many monster cultivators in the Valley of Ten Thousand Demons was to use a large number of monster patterns, to continuously iterate, refine, and eventually produce the Azure Dragon Pattern?!

No wonder...

No wonder, in the Valley of Ten Thousand Demons, Mr. Tu, realizing the unfavorable situation, didn’t hesitate for a moment to "destroy the evidence."

The monster cultivators were all ordered by Mr. Tu to go to their deaths.

The Four Symbols Monster Patterns on the monster cultivators also all self-destructed, exploding into a cloud of blood mist.

Furthermore, all Formation Diagrams, books, and jade slips were either transferred or destroyed, leaving nothing behind.

He didn’t dare to leave any trace.

Fearing that any clue left behind might allow someone, through the remaining formations and following the principles of formation, to deduce his plot.

Mr. Tu’s true intention was to restore the authentic, possessing divine beast power, and the near-extinct Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation!

"Such great ambition..."

If it weren’t for Mo Hua, using the prototype of a Source Pattern from Returning to the Source, accidentally infiltrating and spying on these details within the Demon Sect, he wouldn’t have known Mr. Tu had such ambition.

So it comes to this...

"The Valley of Ten Thousand Demons is used for research and experimentation on the Four Symbols Green Dragon Pattern..."

"The Dragon King Temple on the Mistwater River, it should also be related to this..."

"Dragon King Temple, Dragon Blood, Dragon Bone..."

"The Valley of Ten Thousand Demons studies formations, the Dragon King Temple provides materials."

Mo Hua remembered, in the Dragon King Temple, that Mr. Witch who could transform into dragon scales, turning into a monster, invulnerable to swords and spears.

It seems Mr. Witch was also an "experiment" of the Green Dragon Formation?

Or, he also utilized the research of the "Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation," to some extent gaining indestructible Green Dragon Power?

Mo Hua gradually linked everything together.

At the same time, a bold thought gradually arose in his mind:

The Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation...

"What if my own Magical Treasure..."

He restrained his excitement, forced himself to calm down, and then slowly pondered:

"Copper Skin Iron Bone," is just a general term for a type of Magical Treasure, its core is using certain special spiritual materials, with secretive alchemy doctors, artifact refining, and formation methods, attached to the skin, or implanted in the bones, to strengthen the physical body.

But its essence is still an external "layer of armor," or internal "armor" within the bones.

For body cultivation, this "Copper Skin Iron Bone" is naturally an all-in-one offense and defense, incomparably strong.

But for me, it still doesn’t quite fit, it’s still missing some "soul."

And now, Mr. Tu’s plotting offered me a direction:

Formation!

Copper Skin Iron Bone is just a form, my Divine Sense Proving the Dao walks the path of formations, my own Magical Treasure should naturally relate to "formation."

And before me, there seems to be the most suitable formation:

Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation.

Shedding the bloodthirsty monster pattern method of the Demon Sect, using the method to forge "Copper Skin Iron Bone," this "Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation," is to be my own Magical Treasure, integrated into my flesh, skin transforming into dragon scales, commanding the divine beast power!

Mo Hua was temporarily elated, then calmed himself and thought carefully:

"But this Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation, is something Mr. Tu schemed for so long, consumed so much effort, and researched meticulously, surely it’s not that simple..."

"First is the materials..."

"Dragon Blood... I don’t have any..."

"The Mr. Witch at the Dragon King Temple, who could transform into dragon scales, surely consumed Dragon Blood, but he died cleanly, leaving no clues, then could it be... should I have a chance to find it at the Dragon King Temple?"

"Or, look within the Demon Sect, to the ’Big Brother’ in the mouth of the Golden Core Demon... which is the leader of the Demon Sect, since he cultivated the Four Symbols Dragon Pattern and nurtured the Green Dragon Power, he surely has Dragon Blood."

"Dragon Meat... not sure if it’s needed."

"Dragon Bone..."

Mo Hua suddenly thought, "The Dragon Bone Divine Statue in the Dragon King Temple!"

This divine statue, made of Dragon Bone, hidden deep in the main hall of the Dragon King Temple, was found by me and hidden in the small fishing village, used as a habitat for the Little Silver Fish.

Then Mo Hua frowned again.

But this Dragon Bone Divine Statue has an ancient aura, mysterious power, and great cause and effect, I may not be able to merge it into my body.

Even if merged, it might not solve the repulsion between the Dragon Bone and the human body.

Moreover, the general method of merging "Copper Skin Iron Bone" should be unable to integrate Dragon Bone into a cultivator’s flesh.

This is "Dragon" Bone.

Even an ordinary River Dragon King’s bones contain powerful forces beyond what cultivators can bear, not to mention, my physical body has always been weak.
