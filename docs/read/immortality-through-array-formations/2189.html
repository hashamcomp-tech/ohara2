Chapter 2189: Chapter 1024: Little Collapse_2

Every victory in each round is as precious as a treasure.

But if we keep losing, there will be nothing left.

The room is quiet, and the air is filled with the scent of tea.

The Three Mountains Sect Leader calmly drinks tea.

However calm they appear on the surface, inwardly they are tormented.

Even more tense than all the cultivators watching the battle.

After drinking tea for a while, the Taia Sect Leader finally couldn’t hold it anymore, coughed, and slowly asked,

"This round... should... be winnable, right?"

As soon as he asked this, the Chongxu Sect Leader and Taixu Sect Master both had a sudden jolt in their hearts.

The Chongxu Sect Leader couldn’t help but say,

"Don’t say it out loud! Blessings and misfortunes depend on each other, wins and losses transform, sometimes when you say you’ll win, you actually lose, when you say you’ll lose, you might end up winning..."

"That’s exactly how it went in the last sword discussion, everyone thought we would lose, but we won in the end."

"Now you say we’ll win, don’t end up losing again later..."

The Taia Sect Leader, feeling somewhat insecure after hearing this, still kept a straight face, "That’s superstition."

The Chongxu Sect Leader replied, "Heavenly Mechanism Causality, mysterious yet even more mysterious, cannot be entirely trusted, but also cannot be completely dismissed. Sometimes, you must believe in a bit of oddity..."

The Taia Sect Leader sighed, "Then pretend I didn’t say anything."

After that, the three continued drinking tea.

But while drinking, the Taia Sect Leader still couldn’t put his mind at ease, so he asked the Taixu Sect Master again, "What do you think, can we win?"

The Taixu Sect Master’s already restless heart was stirred into further unease.

He replied helplessly, "It’s hard to win..."

"Is it because of the ’superstition’ of Heavenly Mechanism, or is it truly hard to win?" the Taia Sect Leader confirmed.

The Taixu Sect Master sighed, "The opponent is from the Qian Taoist Sect, is their strength even in question?"

The Taia Sect Leader frowned.

The Taixu Sect Master said, "The strongest disciple of Qian Taoist Sect is Shen Linsu."

"Although no one openly acknowledges it, this Shen Linsu actually surpasses Xiao Wuchen, Ao Zhan, and Duanmu Qing."

"He’s the strongest among Qianxue Four Talents."

"Although Shen Linsu isn’t present in this sword discussion, there is a Shen Cangfeng."

"Shen Cangfeng is the strongest genius disciple of Shen Family under Shen Linsu."

"Shen Linsu is born with Kirin blood, unmatched in brilliance, overshadowing all the other Heavenly Pride Disciples."

"Shen Cangfeng’s fame is only unrecognized because of the former’s prominence."

"But comparing him to disciples of other sects, Shen Cangfeng is definitely a top Heavenly Pride."

"To defeat him is truly no simple feat."

The Taixu Sect Master said slowly.

The Taia Sect Leader nodded slightly.

The Taixu Sect Master, worthy of being from a major family, has profound insight into these Heavenly Pride Disciples.

But hearing this only made his heart more uneasy.

Having managed to win a round before, will it be just "a flash in the pan," starting a losing streak again?

This is truly distressing.

The Taia Sect Leader sighed.

This is the downside of sword discussions. Watching others fight to the death, experiencing ups and downs, is exciting.

But when it involves your own interests, watching your own sect disciples in the sword discussion, especially when facing consecutive defeats, it truly makes the heart tense and feel hopeless.

This feeling isn’t exclusive to the Taia Sect Leader.

The seemingly calm Taixu Sect Master and even the Chongxu Sect Leader who started researching "Heavenly Mechanism Superstition" also feel despondent.

With individual thoughts and prior calculations, the Three Mountains Sect Leader looked at Mo Hua’s sword discussion, suddenly feeling a sense of mutual understanding and empathy.

Their barriers to one another have decreased.

The atmosphere has also grown significantly warmer.

A true sense of unity within the Three Mountains, sharing both glory and setback, with unified anxiety.

But at such times, they can’t do anything.

They can only sit together, continuously drinking tea, appearing calm, but watching the Fantian Painted Scene without turning an eye.

...

And at this moment, in the Fantian Painted Scene, Mo Hua had already joined hands with Linghu Xiao, grasping the High-Rank Formation.

At this time, the outer city of Qian Taoist Sect had already been breached.

In this sword discussion format, the outer city is hard to defend.

Furthermore, with Linghu Xiao, this Sword Dao Genius, whose Sword Qi is extremely sharp, Qian Taoist Sect symbolically defended and then retreated to the inner city.

The inner city’s gate is the key.

This is also the door deciding the victory of the siege battle.

But when Mo Hua reached the gate of the inner city, he found that there was no one guarding in front of the gate, yet above the gate, the glow of formations, stacked in five layers, flickered.

Among the Four Great Sects, Qian Taoist Sect’s Formation level is ranked first.

The formations on the city gate, although they are only Second Grade Middle-level, are all superior defensive formations.

Stacked in five layers, the defensive effect is naturally strong as well.

At this time, five disciples of Qian Taoist Sect were all guarding inside the inner city.

Leading them was the tall and somewhat brooding Shen Cangfeng.

At this moment, he held a Heavy Sword, secretly gathering power, his powerful Spiritual Power surging, the sharp Sword Qi converging on the sword body.

Solely based on the might of the Sword Qi he held back, he was comparable to, if not more formidable than, Xiao Ruohan of Qian Taoist Sect.

Qian Taoist Sect’s Heaven and Earth Splitting Sword Skill.

This is a Superior Heavy Sword technique.

One of the oldest sword techniques of Qian Taoist Sect, extremely powerful.

But due to its grand and heavy nature, it is quite niche, thus very few in Qian Taoist Sect practice this sword technique.

This sword technique is immensely suitable for Shen Cangfeng.

Shen Cangfeng, because of this Heaven and Earth Splitting Sword Skill, became the first under Shen Linsu within Shen Family and the entire Qian Taoist Sect.

In this sword discussion, although he is on the defending side.

But he doesn’t want to merely defend.

It’s too passive, and it doesn’t showcase his strength.

He wants to turn defense into offense, to annihilate the five from Taixu Gate.
