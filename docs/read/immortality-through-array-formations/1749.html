Chapter 1749: Chapter 885: Four Factions Shaken

"A bit unfamiliar, but not a single stroke wrong, and moreover..."

Moreover, there’s a flow of artistic charm above.

This is not merely "painting a calabash," only depicting its form, but truly comprehending certain laws within the Formation and integrating them into the Formation Patterns, hence the presence of such "artistic charm."

This Formation contains rules.

Although weak, there’s indeed a sign of rules.

"Master Wen, this..."

The group of examiners were uneasy, couldn’t help but all turned to look at Master Wen.

Master Wen took a deep breath, then shook his head and sighed:

"Not a single stroke wrong, painted it out..."

As soon as these words were spoken, the hearts of the people were finally at peace.

"Painted it out?!"

"This can... how, this, this..." Someone stammered, unable to believe.

The shock in the hearts of most examiners was indescribable with words.

Painted through!

In their lifetime, they unexpectedly really saw a disciple thoroughly paint through the exam paper for the Array Discussion Conference at Qian Learning State Boundary!

Moreover, from start to finish, not a single piece missing, not a single detail wrong, not even a single stroke missed?!

Such a difficult peak Formation, cannot even seal the top.

Moreover, he learned it on the spot!

Just by looking once, spending a few cups of tea time, he comprehended the entire Formation Diagram and the laws.

He knew all the Formations being tested.

The Formations he didn’t know, he learned on the spot?!

What the hell, how could just an "Evil Monster" even describe this?

Is there truly such a genius in this world?

The group of examiners were stunned and lost in thought.

They had spent a lifetime learning Formations, lived till now, only to realize at this moment, not only were their experiences shallow, but even their imagination was somewhat impoverished.

Even if they were to daydream, they would definitely not dare to think like this...

A turmoil in the hall.

And at this point, Mo Hua finally spoke, uttering the first words since the Array Discussion Exam began:

"Is there more?"

He was quite calm.

This was already the peak of Nineteen Patterns, also contained Spiritual Power Reverse Transformation Laws, very difficult indeed.

If there were more difficult ones, he wouldn’t be able to paint them.

Furthermore, he started from Sixteen Patterns, painted all the way till now.

This last one consumed a lot of Divine Sense Calculation, painting it completely cost him all his divine sense, even with his strong divine sense, now it was exhausted.

If there are more Formations, he wouldn’t paint them.

Doing within one’s power, was also taught by Elder Master Xun.

But these simple three words caused all the examiners to fall silent.

In this crisp voice, was contained a heavy oppressive power, and exceptionally strong destructive force, making these examiners who are Elders of Formation Arts from various sects, rich in experience and highly respected, almost lose control.

Is there more?

Is there more?!

Is there...

A few Elders’ necks turned red, almost spitting blood out of anger.

But no one dared to speak.

Because, there really was no more...

It really was painted through.

The peak Formation was also painted through...

Not a single piece left...

They couldn’t shamelessly create a Third Grade Array on the spot to test this little Evil Monster, could they?

Master Wen sighed.

He was the chief examiner, despite his heavy mood, he had to speak: "All painted through."

"Then..." Mo Hua said.

"You win." Master Wen said.

Mo Hua’s expression filled with joy, a smile appeared, but he suddenly remembered Elder Master Xun’s reminder to control his emotions, show neither joy nor anger, and maintained a composed demeanor, calmly stood up, and respectfully saluted the examiners above in the hall:

"Thank you, all esteemed masters and elders."

The examiners in the hall were a bit surprised.

They expected this "Evil Monster" with such incredible talent to be cold-attitude, arrogant and rebellious, and disregardful.

But surprisingly, he was quite polite...

The examiners, who were almost spitting blood out of frustration earlier, accepted Mo Hua’s salute from afar, and unexpectedly felt much comforted.

Master Wen was also slightly relieved, then restored his mood, and solemnly supported his cultivation, loudly announcing:

"Qian Learning State Boundary, Array Discussion Conference, concludes hereby."

"The leader of this Array Discussion, Array Leader..."

"Taixu Gate’s, Mo Hua!"

Master Wen’s dignified and stern voice, under the Feather Transformation Realm Spiritual Power’s support, was like a great bell, breaking through the air, instantly spreading across the entire Mount of Tao, resonating endlessly.

Taixu Gate’s, Mo Hua!

Array Discussion leader, Array Leader!

The whole Mount of Tao was boiling with voices, extraordinarily lively.

The disciples of Taixu Gate were cheering their hearts out.

Some disciples even had tears in their eyes, overwhelmed with emotions.

Before this, almost no one could imagine that the Taixu Gate, which was at the end of the Eight Great Gates, almost hanging by a thread, and with an unremarkable Array Dao, could actually... claim the first place in the Array Discussion Conference?!

Even at this moment, they felt like dreaming, unbelievable.

Some normally dignified and serious Elders were now like ordinary disciples, shocked, cheering out loud.

Some Elders, in their excitement, even pulled out their beards.

The high pavilion of the Taoist Court Officials.

Master Xia of the Feather Transformation Realm was shocked and lost in thought.

He took several glances at Mo Hua, even after a long time, was still unable to conceal his inner shock.

"I actually... misjudged..."

This inconspicuous lad, unexpectedly, unexpectedly...

He too was speechless for the moment.

Xia Canonical Official sitting not far away, was also dumbfounded.

She was only routinely attending the Tao Discussion Meeting in Qian Learning State Boundary, but didn’t expect to witness such a scene.

Before this, she knew Mo Hua was proficient in Formation arts, but never completely expected him to be proficient to this extent.

On the Taoist Field, this array "Evil Monster" with an indifferent face, overlooking the group of Heavenly Prides, was this really the familiar, often smiling, kind and adorable Mo Hua she knew?
