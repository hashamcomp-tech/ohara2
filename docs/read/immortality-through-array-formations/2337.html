Chapter 2337: Chapter 1070: Descent (Part 3)

Even if they ultimately fail and fall into the hands of the Evil God, they must fight.

Moreover, many Heaven Void cultivators present have lived for far too long, their knowledge and perceptions have solidified, their minds have preconceived notions, and they remain skeptical, even scornful, of the so-called "Evil God" disaster.

They might believe that the disaster before them originates from the Great Wilderness’s "Evil God."

But they may not truly believe that this so-called Evil God can shake the foundation of their family, bring about the destruction of the state boundary, or cause the downfall of Qian State.

However, given the situation, better safe than sorry. It’s their fear of this "what if" risk that persuades them to heed True Man Situ’s advice and take some countermeasures.

Once everything was discussed and settled.

The matter of "slaying the god" has a plan.

The general attack on the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation was also swiftly arranged.

Above the Qian Learning State Boundary, the air was thick with blood, the Evil Thoughts heavy enough to be suffocating.

A battle concerning both reality and nightmare, affecting the fate of all cultivators in the Qian Learning State Boundary and pulling at the heartstrings of everyone, was slowly descending with the passage of time...

...

Within the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation.

Mo Hua, "sealed" by the blood sacrifice, remained asleep.

Mr. Tu was also preparing for the final ceremony of birth.

Deep within the Blood and Flesh Maze, on the heart above the Formation eye, at the most core part of the giant beast’s skeletal remains, there was a secret hall sealed by White Bone.

Inside the secret hall, Mr. Tu was leading hundreds of Golden Core Demons to arrange the "offerings" for activating the blood sacrifice.

In the grand hall, there was a giant altar.

Upon the altar, there was a terrifying idol of an Evil God with a human face and sheep horns made of White Bone.

Around the altar, the Blood Sacrifice Array was drawn.

At the formation’s nodes, were hundreds of statues of Great Wilderness Demon Monsters, with terrifying and strange appearances.

Each Great Wilderness Demon Monster was bowing toward the center’s highest Evil God idol, but their necks were bent backward, faces turned upward, resembling a "tribute platter."

Hundreds of Golden Core Demons were also kneeling before the Evil God idol, each gripping a living person.

Mr. Tu proclaimed devoutly and frenziedly:

"Start the sacrifice!"

Subsequently, hundreds of Golden Core Demons took action, brutally killing the "living offerings" in their hands.

They employed various methods to kill: some gouged eyes, some sliced noses, some cut ears, some beheaded, and others severed limbs, opened the abdomens, and dug out the viscera...

The bloodshed and cruelty were extreme.

After killing, they followed Mr. Tu’s taught "Divine Thought Technique," reciting incantations, integrating their own "killing intent" with the "fear" of the living before their tragic deaths, transforming it into a "catalyst" injected into the Demon Monster statues before them.

This was both Mr. Tu’s ritual for "summoning the god" and the Golden Core Demons’ ceremony of "allegiance."

Hundreds of Demon Monster statues sensed the believers’ devotion, the cruel killing intent, and the fear and despair of the offerings before their deaths, revealing fierce light in their eyes, and opened their blood-filled mouths.

Each body part sliced from the living offerings: eyes, noses, ears, heads, limbs, viscera... was fed into the mouths of the Demon Monster statues.

These Demon Monster statues closed their bloodthirsty mouths, jaws clamping down. They began to chew.

They merged the faith, fear, and flesh into the power of "blood sacrifice," turning into nourishment for the rebirth of the Great Wilderness God Master.

The Blood Sacrifice Array on the ground also began seeping red light bit by bit, the glow growing stronger and increasingly dazzling.

During this, Mr. Tu kept devoutly kneeling before the idol.

This process lasted a long time.

Until At 22 p.m, the sound of a terrifying heartbeat resonated.

An unbelievable terrifying Divine Thought began to slowly condense upon the altar.

It seemed something had broken free from the shackles of the Great Dao, awoken from the river of time, about to descend upon the world with great terror.

Mr. Tu suddenly became excited, eyes manic.

This day had finally arrived!

In an uncontrollable trembling voice, he slowly recited:

"Heaven and earth are unkind, treating all things as Straw Dogs."

"Deities are unkind, treating all beings as Straw Dogs."

"Today, believers have formed the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, respectfully inviting the Divine Lord to descend!"

"From now on, this Qianxue State Boundary, with its endless beings, thousands of living creatures, will all be ’offerings’ for the Divine Lord, for the Divine Lord’s enjoyment..."
