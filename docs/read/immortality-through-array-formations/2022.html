Chapter 2022: Chapter 970: Slaying Evil_2

The aura of the Evil Embryo began to rise slowly.

Countless Demon Monsters descended from the Divine Authority Tree, landing inside the Divine Temple, glaring menacingly at Mo Hua.

Lord Yellow Mountain saw this, his face bitter, and muttered:

"The Divine Authority Tree, karmic transmission, the Evil God has endless Demon Monsters under his command, impossible to kill..."

Mo Hua’s eyes lit up, "Mountain Lord, hold him off for me..."

Lord Yellow Mountain was stunned, unsure what Mo Hua intended to do, and watched as Mo Hua leapt, bursting through the Demon tides, climbing directly towards the Divine Authority Tree at the center.

Ordinary Demon Monsters were not Mo Hua’s match at all.

Lord Yellow Mountain held off the Evil Embryo.

Mo Hua’s Sword Qi swept through, clearing a path.

Scattered Demon Monsters lunged at Mo Hua, only to be blown apart by his punches and kicks.

Thus, Mo Hua charged into the sea of Demon Monsters, with one person and one sword, climbing upwards until he reached the top of the Divine Authority Tree, then sat directly on the Evil God’s throne in front of Lord Yellow Mountain and the Demon Monsters.

The hall fell silent for a moment.

Then the group of monsters howled, Evil Thoughts surged like a tsunami.

Lord Yellow Mountain’s gaze was startled.

"This kid... actually sat on the Evil God’s throne... truly, so bold!"

And the Evil Embryo was even more enraged.

Mo Hua sat on its throne in front of it, what an insult and desecration!

The Evil Embryo cracked with fury, black blood splattering, flesh expanding, transforming into a malformed Demon Creature, wanting to rush forward to devour Mo Hua alive.

Lord Yellow Mountain sensed trouble, immediately mustered all his strength, wielding an arm of the Evil Embryo, manifested a golden Long Halberd, and without hesitation, stabbed into his own right leg.

The Long Halberd pierced through flesh, pinning the Evil Embryo to the spot.

The Evil Embryo’s rush instantly halted, tearing even the Divine Body, Yelling furiously at Mo Hua.

The Evil Embryo’s roar was ignored by Mo Hua.

He sat on the Evil God’s throne, attempting to communicate with the Divine Authority Tree, to seize control of the Evil God’s power.

But before he could complete the communication, take control, countless vengeful souls and fierce ghosts assailed him.

Most of these vengeful souls and fierce ghosts had met tragic ends at the Solitary Mountain City’s Mining Cultivators.

They were controlled by the Evil Embryo, helpless.

From afar, even more Demon Monsters charged at Mo Hua to stop him.

Mo Hua emanated the aura of Divine Thought Taoism, intimidating these fierce ghosts, then looked at them and asked:

"Do you... not wish to be liberated?"

In the unseen, karmic cycles flowed.

Some fierce ghosts who consumed Shen Qingsheng’s Divine Soul shed their grievances, and their consciousness cleared somewhat.

They were half fearful, half grateful, turned to guard Mo Hua, fending off attacks from other Demon Monsters.

And after one, came a second.

More and more Solitary Mountain City’s fierce ghosts began to rebel, siding with Mo Hua.

They did not wish to become ghostly beings, they wanted liberation from the endless, oppressive, and enslaved nightmare.

The Solitary Mountain City’s fierce ghosts became a "ghost wall," surrounding Mo Hua.

Outside, more and more grotesque Demon Monsters, along the Divine Authority Tree, climbed towards Mo Hua.

These Demon Monsters were Evil Spirits raised at various Evil God Dojos throughout Qianxue State Boundary.

Whether bull-headed, horse-faced, fish-headed, or wolf-bodied, mixed with human limbs, grotesque and hideous, densely packed, crashing against the ghost wall formed by the Solitary Mountain City’s fierce ghosts...

Most Demon Monsters were blocked.

A few breached the ghost wall, reached Mo Hua, only to be incinerated by his Formation.

Mo Hua finally had an opportunity.

He began to meditate with closed eyes, wholeheartedly communicating with the Divine Authority Tree.

Since it wasn’t the first time, Mo Hua, bearing the karmic ties of the Dragon King Temple’s Evil Embryo, did not face rejection from the Divine Authority Tree; it truly treated him as an "Evil Embryo."

The Divine Authority Tree granted him the Evil God’s power.

Mo Hua’s consciousness became the Evil God’s will.

An ancient and evil intent descended upon Mo Hua, at that moment, sitting on the White Bone throne, backed by the Divine Authority Tree, with countless fierce ghosts guarding him, like a true juvenile "Evil God" controlling evil power.

Thousands of fierce ghosts submitted.

Countless Demon Monsters hesitated to advance.

The Evil Embryo roared with rage.

In that instant, Mo Hua felt a strong sense of dominion, as if before him, like clouds pressing down, a limitless army of Demon Monsters were all his slaves.

With just a command.

These Demon Monsters would ravage cities for him, in the Divine Thought World, annihilate all powerful foes, devour the Divine Thought of all Cultivators, turning the entire Qianxue State Boundary into a battlefield of Evil Thoughts, a den of slaughter.

Then, in the Qianxue State Boundary, life would be desolated.

And he would become the True God reigning over this realm, commanding thousands of evil spirits.

That feeling was too intense, so strong that Mo Hua was overtaken by an impulse to destroy everything, to kill everything.

No "human" could resist such temptation.

Fortunately, he was not entirely "human," his Divine Thought partly human, partly Divine, and another part fused with the Heartless Path of Heavenly Demons.

At a crucial moment, his cold Divinity, along with the Heartless Demon nature, restrained his human desire for power, strength, and slaughter.

Mo Hua’s bloodthirst cooled gradually, his Taoist Heart regained clarity.

He sat calmly on the White Bone throne, wielding the Divine Authority Tree, began to order the Demon Monster army to retreat, commanding the remaining Demon Monsters to end themselves.

Later, with a thought, he completely severed the connection between the Gushan Temple and the Divine Authority Tree, cutting off the supply to the Gushan Temple.

The karmic chains were broken.
