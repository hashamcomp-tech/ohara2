Chapter 2360: Chapter 1078: Divine Authority (Part 2)

But the current situation simply cannot afford any hesitation.

True Man Situ exerted all his strength, channeling the Six Yang Vermilion Gold Fire with the power of a Divine Dao Treasure, to burn the Evil God’s true embryo. Even if he couldn’t annihilate it, inflicting severe damage would be beneficial.

The Evil God seemed to have a degree of apprehension toward True Man Situ’s Six Yang Vermilion Gold Fire.

Perhaps because this fire was extremely pure and potent Yang energy.

Or perhaps because True Man Situ was an Innate Primordial Spirit at the Feather Transformation Realm.

The Evil God dared not sit idle, but instead inhaled deeply and expelled a ball of black mist to contest True Man Situ’s Six Yang Vermilion Gold Fire, resulting in a stalemate for the time being.

True Man Situ couldn’t help but twitch his eyelid.

His Six Yang Vermilion Gold Fire was forged with desperate Divine Thought and the Divine Dao Treasure, making its power exceedingly formidable.

Yet, this Evil God True Embryo was merely Third Grade, newly born, inherently inadequate, entirely in an "infant" state, with even its Innate Divine Ability yet to awaken, relying only on casually spewed black mist to block his meticulously refined Six Yang Vermilion Gold Fire.

A nameless fear arose in True Man Situ’s heart.

Should this True God Evil Embryo grow stronger, he could hardly fathom what kind of existence in this world could eliminate such a dreadful Evil God.

"It must be killed!"

True Man Situ immediately resolved to expend his last breath, igniting the Six Yang Red Gold Cup, even his Divine Soul began to burn, determined to annihilate this great calamity from the world in its cradle.

Meanwhile, the four Elders of the Daluo Sect, knowing their incapacity to harbor murderous intent toward the Evil God, felt ashamed but turned their attention to confronting the three demon spawn and numerous other demon monsters hatched from demonic eggs, thus creating an opportunity for True Man Situ.

Relying on the advantage of Fifth Grade Divine Thought, whether it was Divine Thought Sword Light, spells, or runes, they were devastatingly effective across a wide area.

Previously, they might have feared the "pollution" of Divine Thought.

But now, with the Evil God’s rebirth, they were most likely already "polluted," and indifferent to life and death, they could unleash their full might and slaughter without reserve.

Their Taoist Heart was untested, making them hesitant to kill the Evil God, but they showed no mercy against other demon monsters and demon spawn.

The Daluo Sect Ancestor engaged in close combat with the decomposing demon spawn dripping with foul flesh, wielding the Daluo Unification Sword effortlessly across each swing, severing several tentacles from the rotten demon spawn.

Bloody filth tainted his form, corroding his Divine Thought, but he paid no mind.

This could very well be his final desperate fight in this lifetime.

The other Heaven Void Ancestors also began to slay the demon spawn.

The Spirit Talisman Sect Ancestor subdued the evil aura with the Soul Suppressing Talisman; the Little Spirit Sect Ancestor’s array of spells tore through the void; the Ancestor from Kun State wielded a thunderous sword, amidst violent storms, ensuring any evil spirit that touched it disintegrated into ashes.

The remaining Feather Transformation Divine Thought cultivators, centered around Wenren Wan, formed an array to aid in repelling the tidal wave of demon monsters.

In an instant, the tide began to slowly turn.

Countless demon monsters perished under the devastating methods of the Heaven Void Realm Primordial Spirit.

The inherently deficient true embryo of the Great Wilderness Evil God was progressively consumed in the relentless battle against True Man Situ’s soul-burning efforts, gradually eroding its base.

All things in the world are at their most fragile upon birth.

The Evil God is no exception.

With continued assault, should its origin be harmed, even upon rebirth, it would suffer severe depletion of vital energy.

The Evil God True Embryo was furious.

These straw dogs, like mangy dogs, had truly provoked it.

Yet the fury also hastened the transformation of the Evil God True Embryo, awakening its most vital, true divine skills.

The Evil God True Embryo suddenly spat a mouthful of black mist.

True Man Situ, running on fumes, was left with little telekinesis remaining, forcing a temporary retreat, intending to reignite the Six Yang Red Gold Cup for suppression, only to see the Evil Embryo beginning to illuminate with ancient, mottled, and obscure patterns.

True Man Situ’s expression shifted drastically.

But before he could act, the Evil Embryo slowly spoke, in a sharp, raspy voice imbued with an unfathomable divine charm saying:

"Before the deity... all beings are equal."

With the utterance of this phrase, akin to "words born with law," the rules of the dream realm underwent profound transformation.

It seemed an irresistible law enveloped him, causing True Man Situ’s expression to fluctuate wildly.

He could feel that the Divine Thought Realm he had painstakingly cultivated throughout his life was actually...

Gradually descending!

From thirty-nine patterns to thirty-eight, thirty-seven... thirty, then reverse-breaking major realms, continuing the descent to twenty-nine, twenty-eight...

Finally, it sank all the way to twenty-five patterns.

Golden Core Middle Stage, twenty-five-pattern Divine Thought!

He, the Chief Elder of Mystery Valley, at the Feather Transformation Realm, in the blink of an eye, had his Divine Thought plummet from the peak of Feather Transformation, thirty-nine patterns, all the way down to Golden Core!

Not only True Man Situ, but the other four Heaven Void Ancestors, along with the Feathered Immortal, at this moment, were all caught within the Evil God’s domain, and their Divine Thought Realm also collectively plummeted, ultimately falling to various stages between the Golden Core Middle Stage and Late Stage.

An indescribable horror arose in everyone’s hearts.

Even True Man Situ found it unbelievable.

"Is this... the True God’s authority?!"

"Is it the law of divine authority?!"

Before the deity, all differences in realms are erased, creating "equality among all beings," causing all Divine Thought Bodies within the domain of divine authority to exist within the same realm.

True Man Situ’s heart momentarily became ashen.

"Is this... a god..."

He knew, at this point, everything was truly over.

He exhausted all his strength and fought to the extreme, yet he could only struggle to this extent.

But the power of the Evil Path True God within the Divine Thought World transcended the very essence of his comprehension.

From beginning to end, the rules were completely controlled by the Evil God.

They had nothing to fight against.

And indeed, the situation collapsed in an instant.

With the same realm, the cultivators’ Divine Thoughts simply couldn’t match up to the polluted power of the Evil Spirits and Demon Monsters, and even more formidable are the innate fetal demons.

Degraded to the "Golden Core" realm, the Heaven Void Ancestors from the Daluo Sect and the Little Spirit Sect were successively corrupted by the decayed fetal demons, their bodies rotting away.

The Spirit Talisman Sect Ancestor was terrorized by the human-faced spider demon and was devoured by other demon monsters, his body consumed.

The Heaven Void wielding the Thunderwood Sword was kissed on the neck by the Pink Fetal Demon, gradually losing control of his Taoist Heart, and had no resistance left.

The Heaven Void’s "defenses" collapsed.

The remaining Divine Thought Cultivators of the Feather Transformation Realm could not sustain.

The old woman summoning the Six Ding and Six Jia warriors watched as her last few Ding Jia warriors were overwhelmed by the demon army, and she ultimately fell into the mouths of the demon monsters.

Without the Peach Wood Sword, the Taoist was no match for the demon monsters, and his arms were torn off by the demons.

The white-clad True Man’s Divine Thought was exhausted, unable to drive the Three Pure Ones Bell anymore, she could only protect Wenren Wan behind her.

Wenren Wan, grateful for her protection along the way, said, "Recite the incantation, flee quickly."

The white-clad True Man’s face was pale, she smiled bitterly, "No escape for me now."

The Evil God reborn, the energies of the Seven Stars stirred; they had long lost the ability to forcibly break free from the nightmare using the power of the Mystic Sky Beidou Seven Stars Formation.

Wenren Wan’s heart sank.

And in the next instant, before her, suddenly appeared a pale, deformed human face, which let out an extremely piercing scream at her.

Wenren Wan felt dizzy, and upon reopening her eyes, the white-clad True Man had already fallen into the mouth of the human-faced spider demon.

Her Primordial Spirit was wrapped in spider silk and unable to move. Her limb, resembling ice and jade, were being gnawed bloody by the human face growing on the spider.

Wenren Wan’s heart felt as if being gouged by a knife.

By now, she was utterly alone.

The only one left was True Man Situ.

True Man Situ’s face was full of helplessness and bitter hate, but even at the last moment, he still clung to a faint hope, using his last Divine Thought to ignite the Six Yang Red Gold Cup, transforming it into raging flames, and charged towards the Evil God’s True Embryo.

This was the last thing he could do.

But now his Divine Thought was only at the Golden Core, utterly powerless against the Evil God’s True Embryo.

The Evil Embryo acted as usual, casually spewing out a cloud of black mist, extinguishing True Man Situ’s Six Yang Red Gold Fire entirely.

Not only that, the black mist held the deep force of the Evil God, not only quenching the Six Yang Fire, but also corroding the entire Red Gold Cup.

The Six Yang Red Gold Cup shattered.

True Man Situ spurted a mouthful of life soul blood, while bitterly smiling, he also felt regretful, thinking if only Mystery Valley’s sect protecting treasure, Qiankun Clear Light Lamp, was still around, it might have restrained the Evil God, giving him a few more chances today, the outcome might have been different?

But alas... the Qiankun Clear Light Lamp was used to save someone’s life.

Perhaps, this is also the consequence of fate.

To save one’s life, another must die.

Since he forcefully extended another’s life chart, naturally, he must use his own life to repay it.

The Heavenly Dao is, indeed, the most unfeeling...

True Man Situ felt desolate in his heart.
