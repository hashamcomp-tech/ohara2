Chapter 1568: Chapter 830: Forbidden Casket (Part 3)

There are those from the Taixu Gate, the Shangguan Family, the Wenren Family, the Gu Family, and some items scavenged from the island.

From First Grade to High-level Second Grade, they all abound, encompassing a broad range.

The Five Elements of metal, wood, water, fire, and earth, the Gen Mountain Formation in the Eight Trigrams, Fire Formation, Dui Ze Formation, Kan Water Formation, and some Original Magnet Formations, a myriad of arrays.

Everyone took a slight gasp of breath.

Even cultivators who don’t understand formations generally know how profound and complex these formations are.

The more one knows about formations, the more one understands the difficulty of delving into these formation diagrams.

To be able to carry so many, such complex formation diagrams with him, this young brother must indeed have an extremely profound lineage in Formation Study, and his formation attainments are undoubtedly superior. His perseverance and determination in learning formations are also extraordinary. His future in formations is likely unlimited.

A group of Court Officials looked at Mo Hua with eyes full of respect.

Even Xia Canonical Official was somewhat amazed.

A storage bag full of formation diagrams...

No wonder someone so young has such superior attainment in formations.

With such talent, perceptiveness, and diligence, at his age, it’s truly admirable...

Xia Canonical Official sighed in relief.

Previously, she did have some anxiety in her heart.

She thought that after all, Mo Hua was young, lacking restraint, and after turning over the small island, he might ’withhold’ a little.

This is common human behavior, even some Supervisors can’t avoid it.

But she didn’t expect that Mo Hua’s nature was so upright and candid, not taking a penny of illicit gains.

As for these formation diagrams... their origin is already complex and difficult to verify.

Even if some of these formation diagrams were indeed scavenged by Mo Hua from the island, nobody dared to gossip at this point.

A Formation Master is naturally respected.

It is only right for a Formation Master to take a few formation diagrams.

Moreover, Mo Hua broke the formations on the island and did not demand special compensation, so taking a few formation diagrams is naturally justified.

She realized she had judged a noble heart with a petty mind.

With this unexpected guilt, Xia Canonical Official looked at Mo Hua with newfound respect.

This child in the future, if he enters the Taoist Court, to become a Supervisor who upholds his integrity, he might achieve great things.

Xia Canonical Official expressed appreciation in her heart and then glanced at Xiao Tianquan, saying calmly:

"Xiao Zhisi, what do you think?"

Xiao Tianquan’s eyes lingered over Mo Hua’s storage bag several times, finally with some reluctance, but he still cupped his hands and said:

"No problem."

Then he saluted Mo Hua again, smiling and saying: "It was rude of me, please forgive me, young brother."

Mo Hua happily said: "It’s nothing, it’s nothing."

Seeing that Mo Hua didn’t mind, Xia Canonical Official nodded slightly and instructed:

"Alright, let’s head back."

Afterward, everyone returned smoothly without any incident on the way.

But Mo Hua had already confirmed...

This Howling Celestial Dog was indeed looking for something.

And this thing is extremely important.

Even at such a critical moment, it couldn’t help but come forward at the wrong time, risking offending Uncle Gu and Xia Canonical Official to check his storage bag.

"Because if it’s not checked now, once we return to the Qianxue State Boundary and go our separate ways, the Howling Celestial Dog would never know if I really found something on Water Island..."

"What does it want to find on me?"

"What is so crucial?"

"Could it be... that Jade Box from Shui Yan’s lair?"

Mo Hua gently rubbed his thumb, contemplating for a moment, his gaze focused.

"No matter what, this ledger, I’ll remember it..."

"To openly target me, show killing intent to kill me..."

Mo Hua’s eyes turned slightly cold.

...

Arriving at the Qianxue State Boundary, Xia Canonical Official and the others returned to the Taoist Court.

Gu Changhuai, though silent, personally escorted Mo Hua back to the Mountain Gate of the Taixu Gate.

He seemed to have realized something but didn’t say much, only advising Mo Hua:

"Be careful of the Xiao family, focus on your cultivation in the sect, and don’t wander unnecessarily."

"Hmm, don’t worry Uncle Gu," Mo Hua replied.

Gu Changhuai nodded lightly and turned to leave.

Mo Hua then presented the Taixu Token and entered the Mountain Gate alone.

Back at the sect, it was just noon.

Mo Hua first sought out Elder Song to cancel his leave, then returned to the Disciple’s Residence, bathed, burned incense, dispelled blood qi, and changed into the Taixu Gate Taoist Robe, then nonchalantly went to attend classes.

The teaching elder, knowing that Mo Hua had Elder Master Xun’s support and was a ’special case’ in the sect, said nothing more.

The other disciples, knowing that Mo Hua often went out to ’draw formations’, had gotten used to it over time and were therefore not surprised.

Just like that, Mo Hua spent the morning drawing formations to eliminate bandits and returned to the sect in the afternoon to attend class obediently.

In the evening, after dinner, Mo Hua practiced formation with Yu Er for a while, then returned to the Disciple’s Residence, secured the doors and windows, sealed the formations, and then carefully retrieved from the Storage Ring the Jade Box he snatched from Shui Yan.

The Jade Box was heavy in his hand, made of an unknown water-blue jade material from its appearance.

On the Jade Box was engraved a diagram.

In the secrecy of Shui Yan’s lair, Mo Hua had no time to look closely, but back in the sect, he could finally inspect it carefully.

Stern prisons, grim torture devices, various tortured prisoners struggling, crying out, and wailing.

The water-blue Jade Box and the bloody Prison Diagram.

It contained intense evil qi.

"Is this... a Contemplation Map?"

Mo Hua was somewhat puzzled.

He released his divine sense, fully focused, staring at the prison diagram on the Jade Box for a long time, but found nothing unusual.
