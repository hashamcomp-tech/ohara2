Chapter 2226: Chapter 1035: Ominous Sign (Part 2)

He had no authority over other matters.

But at least in terms of the Sword Discussion Conference, Mo Hua’s words were law, and he already exuded the aura of the "Junior Sect Leader" of the Three Mountains.

All elders cooperated.

All disciples also trusted this junior brother of theirs.

Mo Hua’s calculations on the Shura Battle were not yet complete, but the general idea was already formed.

Moreover, some of the preparations he had made for the Tian character Sword Discussion could be directly used.

Mo Hua started the intensive preparations.

Crafting special armor, Taoist robes, and spiritual artifacts.

Gathering formations of the Three Mountains to fill his shortcomings in formation skills.

Especially some rare and obscure formations with special functions, also needed to be supplemented.

The Three Mountains’ heritage was shared, opening some key legacies to the sword discussion disciples to enhance combat power.

Releasing some rare resources for Tao cultivation.

Together with Linghu Xiao, studying the superior sword technique of Mount Chongxu—the Chongxu Sword True Technique.

Thinking about the attribute changes of this sword technique, from the principle of Sword Qi, considering the variation attributes of Water-Moon and the similarities and differences with the Five Elements principle, solving the challenge of Sword Qi amplification...

...

These matters were not solely Mo Hua’s concern.

The entirety of Taixu Gate was mobilized in a step-by-step manner.

And the vast Tao cultivation resources and legacies contained within the Three Mountains Taoist Repository were utilized by Mo Hua through causal calculations and meticulous planning to the fullest extent.

...

At this moment, not only Taixu Gate.

Every sect participating in the Sword Discussion, in preparation for the upcoming Shura Battle, was undergoing meticulous preparation.

Many sects were plotting methods targeting Taixu Gate.

Some wanted revenge, some wanted to eliminate hidden dangers, some hoped to carve out a piece from Taixu Gate, and some simply took advantage of the situation to add fuel to the fire...

And most of the Heavenly Pride Disciples were sharpening their knives against Mo Hua.

The relationship between them and Mo Hua was simple and pure, with the most straightforward "enmity".

No matter what, Mo Hua must die!

The entire Qianxue State Boundary was permeated with a tense and murderous atmosphere.

...

Time ebbed away slowly...

Unknowingly, seven days flew by.

The final battle of the Di character Sword Discussion, the Shura Battle, finally began...

This was a crucial battle.

It’s the Di character showdown.

Winning means continuing the fight; if the defeat is thorough, then the Tian character Sword Discussion can be forgotten.

Taixu Gate.

Before the battle, the Three Mountains Sect Leader personally sent off the sword discussion disciples.

The Taixu Sect Master said things like "fear not failure, do your best" and "regardless of victory or defeat, you are the pride of our sect" to encourage the disciples.

Afterwards, everyone set off for the sword discussion.

But before departing, the Taia Sect Leader couldn’t help but ask Mo Hua in a low voice when no one was looking:

"Can we keep the first position of the Eight Great Gates?"

Mo Hua looked somewhat weary.

These days, he constantly made calculations, his divine sense frequently nearing exhaustion, then recovering through meditation, repeating this cycle.

Even though Mo Hua’s divine sense was strong, he experienced some divine thought exhaustion, his face appearing somewhat pale.

Yet even so, his gaze remained clear and bright.

Due to the mental strain, his deep contemplation, he exuded an aura of calm and steadiness.

"I’ll try my best..."

Mo Hua said to the Taia Sect Leader with a faint smile.

The Taia Sect Leader breathed a slight sigh of relief.

Clearly Mo Hua was just a Foundation Establishment, and only a disciple, but he somehow carried a reassuring demeanor.

His words, and that faint smile, inexplicably granted peace of mind.

"Good."

The Taia Sect Leader nodded.

His only requirement was this.

To do one’s best to hold the first position of the Eight Great Gates...

In the face of such turbulent circumstances, being able to maintain this rank would be like burning high incense and receiving ancestral blessings...

Time was up, the disciples of Taixu Gate embarked on their journey to Mount of Tao.

And at this time, within the Qianxue State Boundary, all sects, all sword discussion disciples, were setting out for the Taoist Field.

Disciples from various sects donned colorful Taoist robes, resembling a dazzling yet distinct multicolored river, orderly and grand, all converging upon the center of the Qianxue State Boundary, the ancient Mount of Tao.

...

Mount of Tao, people like mountains and seas.

The entire Qianxue State Boundary, a mass exodus.

This was the most populated, grandest Sword Discussion.

Atop the Sword Observation Tower.

Distinguished guests entered the tower, ranks clearly defined.

The Emperor’s nobles, aristocratic families of Taoist State, powerful officials of the court, people of high status gathered.

Tall buildings stood, elusive Qi flowed, who knew what hidden masters watched the battle.

And at the center of the upper level, lay a grand hall.

Towers flying, divine architecture, luxurious and ancient.

At this moment, sounds of shattering void rose and fell in the hall.

Elderly figures emerged from the void, seating themselves one after another.

If a knowledgeable observer saw these elders, they would surely be shocked and uneasy, for these elders were all Heaven Void Ancestors.

These were ancestral spectators, extremely rare.

In previous years, even for the finals of the Tian character Sword Battle, not necessarily would the Heaven Void Ancestors personally come to the Sword Observation Tower to watch.

This was the first time that so many Heaven Void Ancestors had gathered in the Sword Observation Tower.

Among the Four Great Sects, Old Ancestor of Shen Family of Qian Taoist Sect, Xiaoo Family Elder of Heavenly Sword Sect, Ao Family Elder of Longding Sect, Duanmu Elder of Wanxiao Sect.

Among the Eight Great Gates, Elder Master Xun of Taixu Gate, Elder Ouyang of Taia Mountain, Linghu Ancestor of Mount Chongxu, as well as Sever Gold Sect, Gui Water Sect, Lingxiao Sect, Vajra Sect, Purple Mist Sect... and many other sect elders.

And below, parts of Twelve Streams sect elders.
