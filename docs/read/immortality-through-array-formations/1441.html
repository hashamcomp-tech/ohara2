Chapter 1441: Chapter 789 19

The Taixu Gate used some unknown means to raise a "fierce god"!

Using a deity to fight a deity.

They wanted to use this "fierce god" to sabotage the Divine Lord’s grand plan.

"Truly a clever trick!"

"The Taixu Gate remains hidden without revealing their strength, yet unexpectedly possesses such profound divine foundation..."

Mr. Tu sneered inwardly.

As he pondered, the next moment, a fierce golden sword qi suddenly struck the large horned door.

Even though the Divine Lock Array on the door contained the "devouring" sealing principle and absorbed part of the power of divine thought, it still couldn’t withstand it.

Several divine lock arrays immediately collapsed.

Mr. Tu’s expression turned sour.

The gentleman beside him also had a face as calm as water.

They were trapped in the hall, and at this moment, a fierce god was breaking in.

They didn’t know the true face of this "fierce god".

And once they confronted this fierce god, in an instant, they would be separated by life and death.

Then came the second sword...

"Mr. Tu..." The gentleman’s expression was stern.

He advanced with divine thought.

The special Eternal Life Rune was embedded in his flesh.

If he truly died here, all his talents, ambitions, and grand plans would be mere talk.

Mr. Tu’s pupils shrank, and he said coldly:

"Unleash the divine arrays and release all the demon wraiths from the great prison to devour this ’fierce god’..."

The gentleman frowned and said: "Once the seal is broken and the demon wraiths are released, chaos will ensue in the Demon Refining Diagram, and the divine foundations here will be utterly destroyed."

These demon wraiths were refined from monster sensing into demon wraiths using the Demon Refining Pot and sealed with a divine seal.

The demon wraiths are ferocious, greedy, and murderous.

On usual occasions, only a small batch is daringly released.

If they were all released, the demon wraiths would go out of control, and no one would have the ability to reseal them.

As a result, the true "Valley of Ten Thousand Demons" would be utterly destroyed.

"There’s no room for such considerations now..." Mr. Tu said.

"Can these demon wraiths really devour the ’fierce god’ outside?" The gentleman’s eyes narrowed slightly.

Mr. Tu’s expression was solemn.

Generally speaking, even a fierce tiger fears a pack of wolves.

Surrounded by so many demon wraiths, even a true "deity" without a way to escape might be completely devoured.

But now, what was hovering outside was a "fierce god".

This fierce god was enshrouded in layers of mist, everything about it was unknown, its appearance, its level, its specific origins, and the divine skills it possessed...

Mr. Tu slowly said: "Not necessarily... even if this ’fierce god’ cannot be devoured, at least it can be stalled, buying time, and that’s enough."

The gentleman slightly nodded.

The knowledge of the Divine Way naturally should be based on Mr. Tu’s judgment.

Mr. Tu made this decision, but for some reason, his heart suddenly sank.

He had a vague foreboding sensation.

He even felt, in some mysterious way, that even if he were to die, he shouldn’t break the seal of these demon wraiths, or it would definitely result in a great disaster.

But there was no other way at this time, the divine bridge had not broken, and the path to the Divine Land is here.

This is a top-secret that absolutely cannot be leaked.

And the noise of door breaking came one after another.

The divine lock arrays on the door broke one by one...

Mr. Tu didn’t have time to think carefully, gritting his teeth, he said:

"Release the demon wraiths!"

The gentleman’s expression intensified, and he nodded.

He took out a divine bone and used it to control the formation pivot, unlocking the divine arrays within the great prison, and thoroughly releasing thousands upon thousands of demon wraiths...

...

Outside the large door, Mo Hua wielded a golden sword, slashing vigorously, but suddenly felt a gust of demonic wind behind him.

He turned to look.

Within the great prison, a gigantic "hum" buzzed suddenly, abundant thought energy circulated, and then the formation patterns of the divine way shattered one by one.

One after another, the chains of the seal were lifted.

Inside the great prison, each demon wraith broke free from its shackles.

They stood up slowly, each with bizarre shapes, emanating monstrous qi, eyes scarlet, staring intently at Mo Hua.

The surrounding demonic aura grew increasingly intense.

In the corner, the sword bone trembled uncontrollably.

Mo Hua, however, paused, his expression peculiar.

"Afraid of me going in, so you release demon wraiths to block me?"

"So many..."

Mo Hua stopped what he was doing, no longer hacking at the door, but looked at the densely packed evil spirits like a surging black cloud, looked into those bloodthirsty eyes almost ready to devour people, and a bright smile suddenly appeared on his small face.

"How very considerate."

"Then I won’t be polite..."

As soon as the words fell, countless demon wraiths surged towards Mo Hua like a tide.

Mo Hua spread his arms, with golden light contained in his eyes, and at the same time, Heavenly secret Calculation and Tricky Calculation churned simultaneously.

Three black shadows, like a mysterious Taoist robe, adorned Mo Hua’s body.

Mo Hua resembled a god, draped in a "demon robe".

His divine thought circulated rapidly, spreading around.

At the same time, the computational power of divine thought was rapidly consuming.

In an instant, a mottled light flickered on the ground.

Like lotus blossoming, series of dazzling formation patterns centered on Mo Hua spread outward layer by layer.

Li Mountain Fire Cremation Compound Formation!

In an instant, the formation exploded.

Within the formation’s range, some weak demon wraiths were immediately obliterated.

The remaining few strong, tiger-leopard-like demon wraiths were also significantly injured.

Mo Hua swiftly "finished off" with a profound and swift fireball technique.

In no time, many demon wraiths were dead or injured.

The surrounding fell into silence, the remaining demon wraiths all showed fear and dared not advance, but in the next moment, their gazes suddenly turned scarlet.

Slain demon wraiths, with evil thoughts spilling everywhere, provoked the ferocity in these demon wraiths.
