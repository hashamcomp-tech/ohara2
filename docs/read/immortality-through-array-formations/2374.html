Chapter 2374: Chapter 1083: Religious Con Men (3)

This is the minion thoroughly devoted to the Evil God, instinctively reacting to a deity.

The deity’s matters need only be carried out faithfully according to the Divine Lord’s will.

If the deity doesn’t speak, you should not ask, nor dare to ask.

But Mr. Tu is not an ordinary servant.

Moreover, he has lived too long, with a complex mind and a lot of suspicion, and in just a moment, he sensed something strange, his gaze vigilantly looking at Mo Hua.

Mo Hua noticed Mr. Tu’s gaze, his heart skipped a beat, guessing that he might be suspicious of him.

If Mr. Tu truly becomes suspicious, controlling him will be troublesome.

Afterward, the development of matters could also deviate from his expectations.

Mo Hua’s expression remained calm, his gaze slightly flickered, gradually deepening as he looked at Mr. Tu, as if trying to see through him.

Then Mo Hua, stroking Yu Er’s cheek, with an eerie and complacent look:

"The Master of the Great Wilderness chose this child as the Divine Fetus, how could I not know?"

"Back then, to avoid causality, you let the lowly Bos Jiang abduct the Divine Fetus, but something went wrong, the Divine Fetus was saved by a passing unnoticed little cultivator..."

"You don’t think this is all a coincidence, do you?"

"Can you guess who saved this Divine Fetus?"

Mr. Tu’s eyes narrowed slightly, looking at Mo Hua.

"You should be able to guess..." Mo Hua’s gaze was bright and playful as he looked at Mr. Tu, "Yes, it was me, personally saving the Divine Fetus."

"Afterward, I infiltrated the Taixu Gate..."

"The Shangguan family sent the Divine Fetus to Taixu Gate to protect its peace, keeping it from nightmare disturbances, and it just so happened... it was placed by my side."

"In other words, during these nearly ten years, your ’Divine Lord’ grew up by my side."

"He listened to my words, received my care, ate and lived with me, his nightmares were dispelled by me, the demon monsters were devoured by me, and even some Evil God’s remains were personally slashed by me..."

"Besides that, the Bi Mountain Demon Cavern, Valley of Ten Thousand Demons, Bloody Fishing Village, Rouge Boat, Dragon King Temple... all were my handiwork in the shadows."

"I used the authority of your Divine Lord to allow countless demon monsters to invade the dreams of the Divine Fetus. When the Divine Fetus was troubled by dreamland, in extreme danger, feeling lonely and helpless, I stepped in to slay these demon monsters and evil spirits one by one..."

"From start to finish, all of this causality, all the incidents, were entirely within my schemes."

"This Divine Fetus views me as the closest person."

"Now, your Divine Lord’s true form has descended into this Divine Fetus, He and the Divine Fetus are one. In other words, He will also view me as ’family.’ Perhaps He is still unfamiliar with me now, but the instincts of the Divine Fetus are here, the causality is here; as long as I carefully nurture, one day He will fall into my hands..."

"And once your Divine Lord fully trusts me, subject to my control..."

Mo Hua faintly smiled, leaving the rest unsaid.

Mr. Tu felt cold all over instantly.

Within his heart, everything suddenly became clear, every previous mystery was unraveled.

All of this, everything, was the conspiracy of this "Vicious God"!

From the very beginning, everything was within His calculations, all the mastermind was Him.

And the vessel of the Vicious God, was this unremarkable little cultivator of Foundation Establishment ahead of him.

No wonder...

No wonder why, no matter how he checked, how he calculated, it seemed like just a misty confusion, with not a single clue.

He was calculating a deity, naturally feeling terrified, unable to start.

And the one he was trying to investigate was merely an insignificant little Foundation Establishment fellow, naturally easily ignored.

No matter how meticulous he was, no matter how he racked his brains, he could never have associated a deity with a little Foundation Establishment fellow, linking these two identities together.

And this little Foundation Establishment fellow... no, this Tai Xu Vicious God, had long anticipated everything and did not hesitate to involve His deity form in the scheme, to approach the "Divine Fetus" of the Divine Lord’s rebirth. His mind was meticulous, his schemes profoundly far-reaching, truly terrifying to the extreme.

Everything in front of us is entirely in the control of this "Vicious God."

Mr. Tu felt dread within his heart, awfully afraid.

Mo Hua saw Mr. Tu being "shocked" by him and truly believing his words, secretly breathed a sigh of relief.

Many things in the Qian Learning State Boundary are merely him tracing the vine to get to the melon, adding some coincidental opportunities, blindly deceiving, accidentally progressing step by step to this state of affairs, how could he calculate those things?

If he could really calculate so precisely, lay out such deep schemes, this Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation would have been obliterated by him before it even started.

This Mr. Tu would have been "calculated" dead by him already.

However, in matters like Heavenly Mechanism Causality, sometimes relying on a glib tongue, being able to speak convincingly, and justifying oneself, that itself is impressive.

At least it seemed Mr. Tu believed it.

Not only did he believe, but his look towards Mo Hua now carried a hint of "fear," as if fearing Mo Hua’s foreknowledge, having long anticipated the current situation, even having prepared a "dead end" for him.

When one’s cultivation is inferior, even in death, at least one can die knowing why.

But when one’s computational power for Heavenly Mechanism Causality is inferior, sometimes even in death, one doesn’t know how they died.

Mr. Tu felt dread in his heart towards Mo Hua, this "deity."

Seeing the timing was right, that Mr. Tu’s mental defense had loosened, Mo Hua’s heartbeat quickened, immediately displaying a solemn and dignified demeanor, speaking like a "religious con man":

"Submit to me, kneel down, I can spare your life."

Furthermore, in these words, Mo Hua included a subtle touch of the "Planting Devil in Taoist Heart" technique.

Mr. Tu’s brows furrowed tightly, his mind wavering between "killing Mo Hua" and "kneeling to Mo Hua." His faith faced an unprecedented severe test.

Unfortunately, the difference in realm was too vast.

This wisp of "Planting Devil in Taoist Heart" had only a slight ability to seduce, even if planted in fear and faith, it could not alter Mr. Tu’s inherent will.

Just as Mr. Tu hesitated, conflicted, indecisive, repeatedly wavering within, unsure how to choose.

A commotion suddenly arose outside the great hall, followed by several extremely powerful cultivation auras entering.

Feather Transformation!

Mo Hua’s face slightly changed, inwardly thinking this is bad.

And being disturbed by these strong cultivation auras, Mr. Tu also regained his senses, awakened from Mo Hua’s faint "Planting Devil in Taoist Heart."

Soon, the aura grew closer and closer.

The doors of the great hall were shaken open by a powerful force, and a line of cultivators marched in.

Leading the group were three Feather Transformations.

A Shangguan family traitor, Shangguan Wang.

The Featherization Old Demon of Yin Corpse Valley.

And the Feather Transformation Sword Slave of Demon Sword Sect.

Along with Feather Transformation Realm’s Mr. Tu, the four most powerful demon path Feather Transformations on the Evil God’s side were now all gathered in this great hall.

Mo Hua’s heart slightly chilled.
