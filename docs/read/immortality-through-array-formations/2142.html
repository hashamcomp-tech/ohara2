Chapter 2142: Chapter 1009: Lu Fairy_2

Mo Hua was a bit confused and asked, "What’s wrong?"

Hao Xuan hesitated for a moment and whispered, "Junior Brother, do you have no sense of tenderness or value for beauty at all?"

Mo Hua was taken aback, "What tenderness? What value for beauty?"

Everyone fell silent, not knowing what to say.

Hao Xuan reminded, "Today in the sword discussion, didn’t you use the Fireball Technique to collect a kill?"

"Mm." Mo Hua nodded.

"You used the Fireball Technique right on the face to collect a kill?"

"Mm." Mo Hua nodded.

"Did you realize that it was a female disciple?"

Mo Hua pondered for a moment, then nodded, "Seems like it was a girl..."

Hao Xuan had a complicated expression, "Do you still remember what she looked like?"

Mo Hua recalled...

Pale skin, curved eyebrows, big eyes, a sharp face, and red lips...

"I have some impression." Mo Hua said truthfully, then asked, "What about it?"

Hao Xuan said, "Junior Brother, do you think she was pretty?"

Mo Hua didn’t understand, "What’s her being pretty have to do with anything? This is a Sword Discussion Conference; prettiness isn’t edible."

Seeing Mo Hua still not understanding, Hao Xuan sighed helplessly and said:

"Junior Brother, do you know who this girl is?"

Mo Hua shook his head.

Hao Xuan sighed, "This female disciple... is the number one beauty of the Purple Mist Sect, named Lu Zhenlong, the legitimate daughter of the prestigious Lu Family of Kun State, both rich and beautiful, famous throughout the Qianxue State Boundary, admired by many, with suitors queuing from the Mount of Tao to the Mistwater River..."

"Today she participated in the sword discussion, attracting numerous people’s admiration, wishing to catch a glimpse of her beauty, and then..."

Then you hit her face with a Fireball Technique and sent her off.

Mo Hua was stunned, then shocked:

"Why didn’t you speak up earlier?"

Hao Xuan smiled wryly.

He wanted to speak up early, but there was no chance at all.

The main thing was, he never thought that the number one beauty of the Purple Mist Sect, Lu Zhenlong, would be someone the Junior Brother hadn’t even heard of.

Even if he hadn’t heard of her...

With such a delicate face in front of you, just one look should reveal her unusual origins, right?

Hao Xuan couldn’t help but ask, "Junior Brother, didn’t you notice her beauty?"

Mo Hua furrowed his brow, then seriously recalled:

"Her looks... seem quite decent, her features are also pretty, but her temperament isn’t good, her temper is bad, her attitude is arrogant, and her divine soul isn’t that attractive either, she’s just a pretty face..."

Not as good as his little senior sister.

Hao Xuan sighed, "Alright, then..."

Junior Brother is not an ordinary person, so he must see things differently.

But he still cautioned, "Junior Brother, you must be careful when you go out these days."

"Why?" Mo Hua asked.

Hao Xuan said, "You were in the discussion arena, so you didn’t know. In the daytime, with that Fireball Technique that hit her face and sent Lu Zhenlong off, the whole outside erupted, like a volcano had exploded, the noise was deafening, and countless male disciples were enraged, wanting to settle accounts with you..."

Mo Hua only felt a headache coming on and couldn’t help but shake his head:

"These people don’t think about cultivation, spending every day with women in their minds. No wonder during the sword discussion, they can only watch from the outside..."

Though Junior Brother’s words can indeed be infuriating at times.

Hao Xuan was startled, quickly covering Mo Hua’s mouth:

"My dear Junior Brother, don’t ever utter such words outside..."

Otherwise, it will really add fuel to the fire.

The volcano ignited by your Fireball Technique won’t only explode; it’ll erupt into the sky.

Mo Hua, having his mouth covered by Hao Xuan, obediently nodded, indicating he wouldn’t say anything.

Only then did Hao Xuan feel relieved.

After finishing their meals and returning to the Disciple’s Residence, the more Mo Hua thought about it, the more upset he felt.

Using the Fireball Technique to collect a kill was originally meant for convenience.

Yet this simple act brought about such a big trouble.

Had he known, he would’ve let Xiao Xiao finish off that Purple Mist Sect person.

Even having Ouyang Xuan finish it would do, as he wasn’t afraid of offending people with his bad reputation.

But on second thought, since he was in a sword discussion, with the sect’s ancestor’s protection, and a fifth-grade formation at the Mount of Tao, no one could really do anything to him.

Thinking this, Mo Hua was reassured.

He began to calmly prepare for the upcoming sword discussion events.

...

Outside the Taixu Gate, because of the Fireball Technique explosion that humiliated the number one beauty of the Purple Mist Sect, Mo Hua had truly earned a wave of deep hatred.

Several sects changed their strategies towards Mo Hua due to this.

They decided to aggressively target Mo Hua.

Partly because Mo Hua’s actions were just too infuriating.

Not "killing" him even once would make it hard to quell the anger.

Throughout the sword discussion, Mo Hua hasn’t "died" once, which many couldn’t accept.

Mo Hua not "dying" is against the heavens.

On the other hand, because of the sword discussion’s tactical needs, it reached a point where they had to "aggressively target" Mo Hua.

Not taking him down wasn’t an option.

Originally, the most feared member of the Taixu Gate team was Linghu Xiao; Mo Hua was not worth mentioning.

But now, Mo Hua’s Five Elements Spells flow ceaselessly, not only being cunning and varied but also extremely suppressive, becoming a major hazard instead.

If left unchecked, his vast array of spells would toy with others effortlessly.

And with Mo Hua’s spells providing support, Linghu Xiao’s sword qi also became unstoppable.

Therefore, between Linghu Xiao and Mo Hua, one had to be killed first.

Aggressively targeting Linghu Xiao would be very challenging.

But targeting Mo Hua would be much simpler.

After all, any cultivator with eyes could see how weak Mo Hua’s physical body and the fragility of his Dao Discussion Jade were.

Killing Linghu Xiao might take a hundred swords.

But to kill Mo Hua, only one sword is needed.

Yet, wanting to target Mo Hua isn’t that simple, at least Linghu Xiao wouldn’t agree, and the other Taixu Gate disciples were even less likely to stand by.
