Chapter 2352: Chapter 1015: "Deicide" (2)

The ancestor of the Little Spirit Sect began using various spells to eliminate the demon monsters hatched from nearby demon eggs.

The ancestors of the Daluo Sect and Kun State continued to restrain the fallen ancestor of the Xiao family.

Other Feathered Immortals also took action, exterminating more and more demon monsters.

But they only dared to attack other demon monsters, not daring to really "kill" Yu Er.

This was especially instructed by True Man Situ before the departure.

Only the Seven Stars Sword could be used to slay the Evil Embryo.

No one else, with any Divine Thought technique or Divine Thought treasure, should touch the body of the Evil Embryo, or it would result in unimaginable consequences.

The Evil Embryo is the embodiment of the Evil God’s descent, the incarnation of the Evil Path Law, not something that can simply be obliterated by the power of Divine Thought alone.

If "killed" improperly, it could result in even more terrifying outcomes.

It might even incur unknowable terrifying karma.

This outcome, this "karma", must be borne by True Man Situ, who faced the "deadly tribulation", alone.

This is his destiny.

It is also one of the greatest challenges in slaying the Evil God.

Therefore, everyone could only target the surrounding demon monsters, fetal demons, and the ancestor of the Xiao family, to create opportunities for True Man Situ.

The ancestor of the Spirit Talisman Sect also only dared to use a Soul Suppressing Talisman to suppress the Evil Embryo, similarly not daring to really "kill" the Evil Embryo.

Numerous accompanying demon monsters hatched, covered in black water, rushed towards everyone.

The old woman summoned the Six Ding and Six Jia, the Taoist controlled a Peach Wood Sword, the three brothers activated Qimen Dunjia, and the white-robed female True Man wielded the Three Pure Ones Bell, each using their Divine Thought skills to exterminate the demon monsters and protect True Man Situ.

True Man Situ disregarded everything around him, his life and death, and his fears.

His gaze was icy, focused solely on slaying the Evil God.

The surrounding overwhelming divine might pressured his Primordial Spirit, causing it to crack.

The all-pervasive intimidation suffocated him.

Yet True Man Situ recited a spell, relying on the Seven Stars Formation and the Red Gold Cup for protection, enduring the immense pressure as he gradually pierced the Seven Stars Sword towards Yu Er’s heart.

"Yu Er’s face showed panic.

A piercing, chilling scream erupted instantly.

And the scream instantly punctured the nearby massive fetal demon eggs.

The demon eggs cracked rapidly, blood splattered everywhere, and amid the surging evil qi, two even more powerful fetal demons hatched from within.

One was a human-faced spider demon.

The face was a patchwork of human faces, with scarlet eyes, a deathly white body, exposed white bones on its limbs, and its abdomen and belly were sewn with grotesque and hideous human faces.

These human faces struggled and screamed, inducing weakness in the Primordial Spirit and instilling terror just by looking at them.

The other was a decaying fetal demon.

With a large head and ears, a body of rotten flesh, oozing pus.

Within this pus lies the power of "corruption", which if touched, would erode the Primordial Spirit with its filthy aura.

These two fetal demons, though at the peak of Third Grade, embodied the evil and filthy laws.

True Man Situ sensed these two formidable threats, and his expression slightly changed.

The other Divine Thought cultivators dared not relax in the slightest.

The Taoist immediately wiped the Peach Wood Sword with Blood of Divine Thought, then wielded the sword, transforming it into a sword light, and directly hacked at the decaying fetal demon.

This sword wielded tremendous power, directly slicing off the right arm of the fetal demon.

But in the next instant, the decaying fetal demon’s pus began to flow, and its flesh started to regenerate.

When the Taoist retracted the Peach Wood Sword, he found it stained with foul blood and rotten flesh, realizing the spiritual essence had been eroded, and he felt deep heartache.

At this time, the human-faced spider demon let out a fearsome roar.

The Taoist, momentarily losing his mental defenses, found himself amid boundless darkness, with all the horrors he experienced over centuries of Tao cultivation emerging from his mind one by one, causing fear to continuously spread from the depths of his heart.

"This isn’t good..."

The Taoist realized something was amiss, trying to protect his true self, but it was already too late. The human-faced spider demon had already closed in on him, its terrifying human face morphing into a gaping black maw, ready to devour the Taoist’s head.

Fortunately, at the critical moment, the old woman summoned Six Ding and Six Jia, protecting the Taoist and forcibly grabbing hold of the human-faced spider demon’s head.

The human-faced spider demon began to roar with fear again.

The white-robed female True Man immediately activated the Three Pure Ones Bell, shattering its demonic sound.

The Taoist’s mind also cleared slightly.

However, just as everyone was about to breathe a sigh of relief, the eldest of the three brothers, who had been blocking the periphery of True Man Situ using Qimen Dunjia to fend off the demon monsters, suddenly shouted in surprise:

"Master Zhao, what are you doing?!"

The one called "Master Zhao" was a burly man covered in runes.

Initially severely injured and resting, he was now stepping towards the pink Evil Embryo suppressed by the ancestor of the Spirit Talisman Sect, as if "possessed", reaching out to undo the sealing talisman on the pink Evil Embryo.

The burly man’s arm was burned by the runes, exposing the white bone beneath.

But his face displayed enamor, as if anything was worth it for his beloved "person", for that soul-stirring desire.

The battle with the accompanying demon monsters and fetal demons was intense and perilous.

No one noticed the burly man’s abnormal behavior.

By the time everyone noticed, it was too late.

The man wasted an arm to undo the runes, freeing the pink Evil Embryo, which immediately embraced the man’s mouth, kissing him.

The man showed an enamored expression, but the flesh on his face began to decay, gradually disfiguring him.

Everyone was horrified.

But before they could react, the fearsome roar of the human-faced spider demon resounded once more.
