Chapter 2396: Chapter 1090: Fateful Evilness of Karma, A Thought Between Immortal and Demon (Part 2)

Mo Hua’s entire being was entwined in a liquid-like black, gray, and blood-red aura, exuding an aura of extreme malevolence, resembling an apocalyptic Demon Head reborn.

And Mo Hua’s Taoist Heart was being devoured by this Demon Thought.

Soaked in Corpse Qi, Evil Qi, and Evil Qi, Mo Hua seemed like an "unprecedented Evil Embryo."

What hatched from this Evil Embryo was an earth-shattering Demon Head.

Perceiving this shocking Demonic Qi, the gates of Taixu began to tremble.

The ancient temple surged with Sword Intent, upholding the way of the heavens.

On the rear mountain, shrouded in mist, the tablets of Taixu Gate’s ancestors began to shiver.

Elder Master Xun’s face twitched in horror and alarm.

Mo Hua’s Life Chart was reversing!

From that pure-hearted genius, determined to master Formation, comprehend the Heavenly Dao, and bring prosperity to all beings of the Righteous Dao, he was undergoing a transformation into an "apocalyptic Great Demon Head" with Corpse Mountain, Blood Sea as companions, with a karmic mutation as a king, and Evil Embryo hatching into a deity!

"This is bad!"

Elder Master Xun immediately touched his forehead with his finger, activating Divine Thought, guiding the Eryi Heavenly Mechanism Lock of Taixu, to lock Mo Hua’s Qi machine and karma, suppressing Mo Hua’s Evil Qi, trying his best to prevent his Taoist Heart from reversing, bit by bit falling into the Abyss.

True Man Situ, whose severe injuries were yet to heal, also held his last breath, enduring the pain of his Sea of Consciousness cracking, striving to protect Mo Hua with Seven Stars and stabilize his spirit with the Soul Lamp.

Yet in the face of the terrifying great karma of Taoist Demon, Evil Embryo, and endless deadly evilness, these heavenly secret resistances were really just a drop in the bucket.

Mo Hua’s Life Chart was still reversing.

His body was also being devoured bit by bit by Corpse Qi, Evil Qi, and Evil Qi...

Looking at Mo Hua’s pitch-black face, Elder Master Xun’s heart was bleeding.

True Man Situ also felt a surge of panic.

At this moment, another twist occurred, and the earth began to tremble.

A thick, ancient, and kindly Taoist Meaning suddenly surged from the earth veins, and the heavy earthly radiance merged into Mo Hua’s body, dissolving the calamity for him.

Elder Master Xun’s face showed astonishment, "The Earth Dao Meaning is protecting Mo Hua..."

Is this... repaying a favor?

Moreover, it’s not just that...

Another ancient and immense aura, at this moment, was gathering between heaven and earth.

Elder Master Xun suddenly looked up, his pupils contracting.

The sky has changed.

Fierce winds arose, and the mountains and forests howled.

Amidst the rising winds and swirling clouds, a vast cloud layer, covering hundreds of miles around, was howling and gathering above Taixu Mountain, forming an unimaginably large whirlpool of a cloud sea, enough to obscure the sky and sun.

In it, the utmost rigid and strong heavenly secret was surging.

Throughout the Qianxue State Boundary, countless cultivators, even all Heaven Void Ancestors, all looked up to the sky towards the direction of Taixu Mountain, their expressions shaken, and their gazes filled with suspicion.

"The movement of the sky is vigorous, a gentleman should strive for self-improvement unflaggingly."

"This is... the heavenly secret of Qian Path!"

"The heavenly secret of Qian Path is gathering above Taixu Gate..."

"What has happened..."

Before the words fell, a pure white auspicious Qi, mighty and vast, descended from the sky, directly illuminating the rear mountain of Taixu Gate, penetrating layers of Formation, and enveloping Mo Hua.

This pure white, utmost rigid and strong Heavenly Dao Qi supported Mo Hua’s Life Chart.

The black deadly evilness instantly began to dissolve.

The reversal of Mo Hua’s Life Chart began to turn back.

Elder Master Xun was shocked, exchanged a glance with True Man Situ, and was suddenly enlightened.

This is... the fortune of Qianxue State Boundary, no, it is the fortune of the entire Qian State, bestowed by Qian Path.

Mo Hua, though he committed countless murders, at the same time, he also saved countless beings.

If it weren’t for him detonating the Large Formation and eradicating all Demon Cultivators, once the Blood Sacrifice Array spread, with Demon Cultivators rampant, Qianxue State Boundary, and even the entire Qian State, would have faced a catastrophe of living beings being devastated.

The balance of karma between heaven and earth.

He killed countless people, committing massive karmic sins, triggering the terrifying "fateful evilness."

But he also saved countless people, the great merit he accrued caused auspiciousness to descend, and Qian Path’s fortune was bestowed upon him.

A Taoist Heart like a pure child, saving beings of a land, a boundary, even a whole state.

This is true "great fortune."

Under the blessing of Qian Path’s fortune, the "Life Chart" of Mo Hua was also undergoing a dramatic transformation.

Each of the opposing Qi forces of good and evil began to vie and slaughter.

The Earth’s Dao Meaning focuses on "defense."

Relying on the life force of "virtue bearing everything," it maintained Mo Hua’s foundation, keeping his Qi machine ceaseless and suppressing the originally accompanying fearful Corpse Qi, karmic Qi, and Evil Qi in his Life Chart.

Qian Path’s fortune focuses on "slaughter."

The pure white Heavenly Dao fortune persisted unflaggingly, balancing with the deep black deadly evilness that continuously emerged, constantly dissipating it.

One comes from the karmic retribution for killing, and the other comes from the merit of saving people;

The apocalyptic evilness and the redeeming fortune, one black and one white, mingled over Mo Hua’s Life Chart, vast and mysterious, resembling Taiji, constantly flowing.

In this way, both maintained a subtle balance, finally congealing and merging into Mo Hua’s Life Chart.

Thus, all karma and fateful evilness dissipated.

Corpse Qi and Evil Qi were suppressed, the Earth Dao Meaning hibernating.

The Evil Qi and heavenly secret intertwined, maintaining balance with each other.

Above Taixu Gate, Qian Path’s fortune dispersed, the auspicious white light faded, and the whirlpool formed by the cloud sea also transformed into scattered clouds, adorning the sky.

The ominous signs in Mo Hua’s Life Chart disappeared.

The black Evil Qi, the blood-red Evil Qi, and the gray Corpse Qi entwining his body also completely submerged.

Mo Hua’s pale face showed redness.

His icy hands and feet regained warmth.

His breath became stable, even issuing a light breathing sound, as if he was sleeping.

Elder Master Xun and True Man Situ were in a state of trance.
