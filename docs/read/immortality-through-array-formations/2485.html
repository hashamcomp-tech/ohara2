Chapter 2485: Chapter 15: Many Thanks (2)

Now, this Young Master Mo has killed Elder Duan and offended the Canglang Sect.

How could he possibly remain uninvolved and put Young Master Mo at a disadvantage?

Furthermore, the people of the Canglang Sect deserve to die!

Luo Master gritted his teeth, his eyes filled with anger, and walked towards Elder Sun with the large ring-saber.

Elder Sun was shocked.

Previously, as an elder of the Canglang Sect, he held a high status and looked down on Luo Master, the "wild" Foundation Establishment who fought alone, never showing him a good face.

But at this moment, Luo Master came toward him with a knife. Elder Sun knew fear and quickly said:

"Luo Chief, let’s talk. Killing me will offend the Canglang Sect completely, think it over."

Seeing Luo Master’s murderous look, Elder Sun secretly readied a Wolf Poison Dart in his right hand, intending to attack, but was maimed in his right arm by a fireball from Mo Hua.

Luo Master had already come close to Elder Sun, raised the saber high, and in Elder Sun’s horrified gaze, chopped off his head.

Elder Sun died instantly.

Luo Master turned to the disciples of the Escort Bureau and said:

"Come over here, each of you stab once."

The disciples of the Escort Bureau hesitated, but thought of the arrogance of the Canglang Sect and the humiliation they had just suffered, they drew their knives and chopped at Elder Sun.

Afterward, the group of escorts killed the other Canglang Sect disciples.

Thus, these two elders of the Canglang Sect, plus several Peak of Qi Refining disciples, were completely "wiped out."

The bodies lay on the ground, blood soaking into the mud.

Luo Master looked at all this, his expression desolate, filled with the pleasure of resisting oppression and rising up, the horror and fear after killing, and the confusion and fear for the future.

Luo Master sighed and cupped his hands to Mo Hua:

"Young Master, we can only escort you this far. We can’t go back to Canglang City, we will now wander the world, until we meet again."

Mo Hua was somewhat surprised, "You’re not going back to Canglang City?"

Luo Master’s expression was complex.

This Young Master Mo looks simple, but his actions are decisive and ruthless.

Though decisive and ruthless, when he speaks, there’s a hint of clear... purity...

"We killed people from the Canglang Sect. Going back to the city is walking into a trap, with no place even to be buried." Luo Master said.

Even reporting this to the Taoist Court would still be their crime.

Moreover, the Taoist Court can’t control the Canglang Sect.

Mo Hua nodded, and asked, "What about your assets in the Canglang Sect?"

Luo Master’s heart ached, yet he was helpless, "It’s gone, where there’s life there are assets, we’ll start over slowly in another place..."

Mo Hua nodded, this Luo Master truly understood things, and his character wasn’t bad.

Mo Hua then said, "No worries, you should go back to the city. Losing assets is a pity, starting from scratch is not easy."

Luo Master looked somewhat resentfully at Mo Hua, and repeated:

"We killed people from the Canglang Sect..."

Mo Hua waved his hand, "If you don’t say it, I don’t say it, then no one knows, furthermore..."

Mo Hua looked confident, "If no one knows we did it, then we haven’t done it!"

Luo Master was shocked.

How could this young man speak such absurd logic with such justification?!

However, upon careful thought, Luo Master felt it seemed... true?

"But..." Ying Lady hesitated, "We’ve left so many clues..."

"No problem," Mo Hua said confidently, "’destruction of evidence’ will solve it..."

This kind of thing, he was familiar with.

Mo Hua threw a few fireballs, simply cremating all the bodies.

Then, he set up a Formation, turning the surrounding soil to quicksand, burying the blood and scorch marks.

And melted the whole small hill, causing a "landslide" that pushed everything down the cliff.

Just like that, everything vanished with the sand and wind.

Evidence and causalities buried beneath the mountain.

The cultivators with Luo Master were all dumbfounded, rendered speechless.

They hadn’t expected there could be someone in this world who would use Fireball Technique and Formations this way...

Fireball to cremate bodies, Formation to bury them.

Luo Master looked at Mo Hua with eyes filled with both "admiration" and "fear," and asked softly:

"Young Master, may I ask what you did before..."

Why is the destruction of evidence so skillfully done?

Mo Hua opened his mouth and said, "Not much, I have an uncle named ’Gu’ who works at the Taoist Court, and my skills were learned from him."

"The Taoist Court..."

Luo Master was enlightened.

This was a family legacy, with a proper inheritance.

It’s not because he has a murderous nature that he has developed such a hobby and habit.

Luo Master breathed a sigh of relief.

Mo Hua said, "You follow me into the city, pretending nothing happened. As long as you don’t leak any information, Canglang Sect won’t be able to trace it to you."

Luo Master was somewhat relieved, yet still had concerns.

He always felt they had just killed elders and disciples of the Canglang Sect, and then swaggering into the city seemed too audacious.

Mo Hua saw through Luo Master’s thoughts, saying to him:

"This is called acting in reverse. The most dangerous actions often also mean the safest, as long as your mind is meticulous, your courage bold, there’s nothing that can’t be done."

For some reason, Luo Master felt that no matter how absurd the words, once spoken by Mo Hua, they seemed particularly reasonable.

He couldn’t help but nod.

"Besides," Mo Hua assured him, "I told you, I have acquaintances in the Canglang Sect who cordially invited me to join."

"This acquaintance has a very high status, strong connections."

"Following me into the city, to the Canglang Sect, you’ll be considered half of ’their own’, they won’t suspect you."
