Chapter 2312: Chapter 1062: Heaven’s Mystery_3

In the Dragon King Temple by the Mistwater River lies the White Bone Maze.

Within the White Bone Maze, although there is an Evil Formation, it has not yet taken shape, clearly intending to build the Heaven’s Mystery Formation, but not yet completed.

And before us, is the Blood and Flesh Maze used for the birth of an Evil God, the core of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, naturally forming the "Mysterious Heaven Great Formation."

The Mysterious Heaven Great Formation is related to the "transformation" of Divine Sense.

The Evil God needs to rely on the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation to be reborn!

So, this Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation is essentially... an Evil Path variation of the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation.

"I should have thought of it earlier..."

Mo Hua’s heart trembled, pupils losing focus.

And his "shocked and dazed" appearance fell into the eyes of Mr. Tu, who was secretly satisfied.

Only a genius could recognize the bewildering nature of this ancient maze formation of blood and flesh before him.

Only true geniuses are attracted to this ancient, mystical, yet murderous and cruel Dao of Formation, feeling deeply shaken.

Ordinary people cannot discern the true essence of this Blood Sacrifice Formation.

Being an Evil God’s servant himself, Mr. Tu had no idea what Mo Hua was really "shocked" about.

After a long time, Mo Hua suppressed the myriad emotions in his heart, letting his face become somewhat calmer.

Mr. Tu nodded slightly, asking Mo Hua:

"How is the formation?"

Mo Hua slightly opened his mouth, almost losing his voice, saying:

"Too... profound, too difficult... such a powerful formation, I... have never seen before..."

Mr. Tu nodded, staring into Mo Hua’s eyes, "Do you... want to learn it?"

Mo Hua’s gaze trembled, "You... truly willing to teach me?"

Mr. Tu replied, "I may be a Demon, but I’m a man of my word, since I promised you, I certainly won’t break my promise."

Mo Hua’s face looked pained.

"Have any concerns?" Mr. Tu asked.

Mo Hua hesitated, "This is the core of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, using blood as Mo, flesh as the medium, it’s a blood and flesh formation. If I learned it, wouldn’t I become... a true Evil Formation Master."

Mr. Tu looked disappointed, "What I told you, did you forget? Sticking to the concept of good and evil, blinded by appearances, how can you see through the Dao of Formation? Is your mindset only this limited?"

Mo Hua’s face showed shame, internally struggling repeatedly, yet he still hesitated.

Mr. Tu sighed, "Forget it, take it step by step then."

He did not force Mo Hua, speaking with a gentle tone:

"Since you have a cleanliness about good and evil, I won’t force you; I’ll teach you the original version of the ancient formation patterns, without touching Evil Power, without using human skin and flesh."

Mo Hua’s spirit was shaken, sincerely grateful, "Thank you, Mr. Tu."

"No need to thank me, but let me remind you," Mr. Tu gazed at Mo Hua, eyes deep, "the original ancient formation patterns, complex and obscure, without external aid, you can’t even comprehend, let alone learn and draw them."

"In this way, even if I teach you, but you can’t learn it, can’t draw it, it’s likewise meaningless. You better have this in mind."

Mo Hua nodded, earnestly saying, "I understand, if I cannot learn it, it is my own inadequacy, no matter what, I will always remember your kindness, sir."

Mr. Tu "hmm"-ed, leading Mo Hua to a corner of the Blood and Flesh Maze, handing him an old scroll with several peculiar formations.

"Comprehend the formation from simple to complex, you start with the most basic..." Mr. Tu said.

"Yes."

Mo Hua nodded, taking the scroll, focusing intently, realizing that these formations, in terms of brushwork and formation style, indeed mirrored those he encountered while breaking through the Heaven Yan Jue, slightly relieving his heart.

Yet his face showed a look of deep thought, as if the formation before him was unfamiliar, extremely difficult.

Mr. Tu looked at Mo Hua, speaking sternly:

"These formations, you must comprehend on your own, but you cannot take them away, only learn within this Blood and Flesh Maze..."

"If you learn them, I will teach you more. How much you can learn depends entirely on your own fortune."

Mo Hua displayed a tense expression, seemingly fearing he might fail to learn, missing this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity, immediately vowing:

"I will do my utmost."

Mr. Tu nodded with satisfaction, then instructed with a solemn face:

"This Blood and Flesh Maze is the forbidden land of the Divine Lord, extremely perilous, as I confine you here, remember not to wander, do not be curious, otherwise, if you incur the wrath of the Divine Lord, I cannot save you."

Mo Hua nodded, "I will remember."

Mr. Tu’s pale palm gently waved, the surrounding formation patterns glowed, flesh wriggled and closed in, enclosing Mo Hua’s surroundings, forming a small "blood and flesh prison," confining him within.

After accomplishing this, Mr. Tu left, seemingly having other matters to attend to.

Mo Hua, meanwhile, sat obediently within the blood and flesh prison, pondering over the scroll given by Mr. Tu, comprehending the formations on it.

After observing for a while, he took out paper and pen, laying them on the ground, trying to draw.

After the first attempt, he failed.

Mo Hua furrowed his brows, starting on the second attempt, then the third...

But the more he drew, the more wrong he became, Mo Hua increasingly felt he couldn’t find the way in, furrowing his brows even more tightly.

Yet he continued laboriously, drawing again and again...

Until the icy feeling of Divine Sense faded away completely, Mr. Tu’s Divine Sense fully withdrawn, only then did Mo Hua slightly relax.

But he was not careless, feigning attempts six or seven times more, each time wrong, confirmed there was no abnormality around him, nor any evil Divine Sense watching him, finally putting down the pen and ink, thoroughly relieved.

The Mysterious Heaven Great Formation, etched in his Sea of Consciousness, just a glance was enough, where was the need to practice so many times...

Despite his breakthrough in Foundation Establishment, integrating the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation in his Sea of Consciousness was of First Grade.

What Mr. Tu had given him was Second Grade.

Yet they shared the same root, making it much easier to learn.

Mo Hua looked around, seeing nothing but nauseating flesh and horrifying blood-colored formation patterns.

"Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, Mysterious Heaven Great Formation, Blood and Flesh Maze..."

Within, there were many deeper secrets Mo Hua couldn’t fathom.

Especially concerning the Heaven Yan Jue cultivation technique given by his master and the origins of the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation related to the Evil God...

But at present, time is pressing, and there’s no leisure to ponder these.

Mo Hua glanced at the bloody and terrifying Blood and Flesh Maze around him.

Based on his many years of experience mingling in the Qian Learning State Boundary, dealing with Evil Gods repeatedly, Mo Hua is almost "professional" in matters concerning Evil Gods.

He could almost instantly know, within this Blood and Flesh Maze, there must be an altar hidden, near the altar, a statue must stand, and upon the altar, there must be sacrifices.

The sacrifice is Yu Er.

And the statue most likely is the horned white bone Evil God statue that the Second Elder mentioned, worshipped by Mr. Tu.

Which means, the person he wants to save, the thing he wants, is already close at hand...

Mo Hua showed a pondering expression, deep within his eyes, sharp golden light flowing.
