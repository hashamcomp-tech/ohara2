Chapter 1783: Chapter 895: God-slaying Dao

The path I walk is the road of Divine Sense Proving the Dao, as a cultivator of Divine Thought Taoism.

I must strictly demand of myself.

I need to become stronger.

Use the Evil Embryo as a touchstone.

Consider the Evil God as the ultimate opponent.

Slaying the Evil Embryo is a minor achievement, but slaying the Evil God is a major success.

If these standards are not met, it is not considered qualified.

Mo Hua nodded.

Now it seems, with the myriad Sword Array, the Sword of Divine Thought transformed, the iterative "Transforming Sword Skill", though powerful, involves a rather weak Origin Power.

Against ordinary Evil Spirits, its killing power is immense.

Yet facing entities like the Evil God, even if victorious, might not necessarily end in death.

Thus, further learning is needed, to acquire advanced sword moves, enhancing the power of the Divine Thought into sword.

And among the Divine Thought into sword True Jue, the most lethal move is ultimately the Divine Thought Sword Form...

Godslaying Sword.

"Yet..."

Mo Hua furrowed his brow, took out the Sword Dao Bamboo Scroll given by Patriarch Dugu, observed the ancient Sword Pattern on it, sensed the Sword Intent within, and murmured softly:

"Using the sword to temper the god, self-slay the Life Soul..."

Indeed, this is how he practiced.

Using the Sword Intent on the bamboo scroll to temper his Divine Thought, to slay his own Life Soul.

Every day a slay, one sword a day, never stopping.

But after all this slaying, aside from turning the Evil Embryo into a "shrinking turtle", and slashing his Life Soul with scars aplenty, there was no other special realization.

Mo Hua just had a vague feeling that he was practicing "Godslaying Sword".

But whether this method was correct, effective, or held the essence of the God Slaying Technique, he still was quite unclear...

This problem, Mo Hua studied for a few more days.

But had no additional thoughts for the time being.

Eventually Mo Hua had to admit, that this unknown, profound Sword Dao Skill is definitely not something he could understand by just shutting himself off from others.

He should honestly consult Patriarch Dugu.

Lest he accidentally veers off track again.

Previously, the ancestor didn’t tell him the exact method of the Godslaying Sword, because his Divine Thought into sword wasn’t well controlled.

But now it’s different.

His Transforming Sword Skill, with Sword Array as the foundational structure, has undergone a transformation.

Going to consult the ancestor again, he should teach him a bit more...

...

A few days later, at Taixu Back Mountain.

Sword Tomb Forbidden Land.

Patriarch Dugu was as usual, in good spirits, which allowed Mo Hua to relax slightly.

Then Patriarch Dugu, as usual, explained the Sword Dao to Mo Hua.

After explaining the Sword Dao, Mo Hua seized the opportunity to quietly ask:

"Ancestor, how is the Godslaying Sword... actually practiced?"

Patriarch Dugu hesitated slightly, then calmly looked at Mo Hua with eyes full of contemplation.

He knew that before Mo Hua’s questioning might stem from curiosity.

But now with Mo Hua asking again, he couldn’t help but realize that this child had already begun learning the God Slaying Sword Style.

Patriarch Dugu sighed internally.

In all his years, it was the first time he saw someone who could advance so swiftly in learning the Divine Thought into sword True Jue, almost progressing at a rate of a thousand miles in one day...

This is the Taixu Divine Thought Sword.

The deepest Divine Thought Sword Skill of Taixu Gate, and more importantly, a Forbidden Sword Skill...

Patriarch Dugu shook his head.

Oh well...

Evil Monsters are perhaps just like this.

He had begun trying to get used to it.

At this point, there’s no need to hide the ultimate sword move.

Patriarch Dugu’s gaze slightly focused, and he asked: "Have you looked at the bamboo scroll I gave you?"

"I have." Mo Hua nodded.

"What have you discerned?"

"There are Sword Patterns on the bamboo scroll, within which there is Sword Intent."

"Do you know what Sword Intent it is?"

Mo Hua pondered for a moment, shook his head, "Disciple only knows, this Sword Intent is very ancient, very profound, not overtly powerful, but it seems to contain... a kind of Sword Dao Origin?"

Otherwise, how could it injure the origin of the Evil Embryo.

Patriarch Dugu furrowed his brows slightly.

Even the Sword Dao Origin can be seen...

Whom exactly did this child learn from to have such deep understanding?

Patriarch Dugu pondered for a moment, slowly nodded, and asked again: "Do you know what kind of Sword Dao Origin this is?"

Mo Hua shook his head.

That he did not know.

He was originally a "Sword Blind", though he learned quite some Sword Dao knowledge from hanging around Patriarch Dugu these days.

Yet far from reaching the level of discerning the Sword Dao Origin...

Patriarch Dugu nodded solemnly and began speaking slowly:

"This Sword Dao Origin has a remarkable background, from our Taixu Gate, before the Three Sects divided, extracted from an ancient ’Divine Sword’, a strand of a Sword Technique-like Taoist Meaning."

"Thereafter the ancestors and predecessors of Taixu Gate poured their lifelong Sword Dao knowledge into this Taoist Meaning."

"Dozens of generations of cultivators, thousands of years of painstaking effort, only then did they successfully nurture this extremely precious ’Sword Dao Origin’."

Patriarch Dugu spoke solemnly, "And this Sword Dao Origin can also be called..."

Mo Hua, hearing this, felt his heart tremble, a term inadvertently surfaced in his mind:

"Sword Stream?"

Patriarch Dugu raised his eyes to glance at Mo Hua, his gaze slightly trembling, then nodded approvingly:

"Correct, it is the Sword Stream, the culmination of Sword Dao within our sect... the source flow of Sword Techniques!"

Mo Hua felt his heart surging.

Sword Stream...

The thing he was pondering daily and using to slash his Divine Soul was unexpectedly a priceless... Sword Stream?

No wonder this thing was ancient and obscure, exceptionally mystical, could even damage the origin of the Evil Embryo.

And such a valuable Sword Stream was given by Patriarch Dugu to him.

Naturally, not passed down, but accurately ’lent’, temporarily lent for his pondering — but also enough to see the ancestor’s regard for him.
