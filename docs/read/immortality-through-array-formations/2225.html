Chapter 2225: Chapter 1035: Ominous Sign

There are seven days left until the Asura Sword Discussion.

These seven days are set by an elder meeting on Mount of Tao.

The aim is for sect disciples to prepare.

Because the Asura War was an unexpected situation, the preparation time given is more ample.

Inside the Disciple’s Residence.

Mo Hua is still concentrating deeply, deducing the battle scenarios, analyzing cause and effect, pondering over issues...

Suddenly, a knocking sound echoes, Mo Hua goes to open the door and is surprised to see the visitor:

"Sect Leader? What brings you here?"

The Taixu Sect Master gazes deeply at Mo Hua, takes out a token from his sleeve, and hands it to Mo Hua.

"This is the Sect Token of the unification of the Three Mountains, newly forged, currently only this one, for you to use first."

"Within the token, the inheritances of the Three Mountains are interconnected."

"You can exchange at will using merit points."

"Moreover, this Sect Token is quite special, the Sect Leaders of Tai’a and Chongxu have given you very high authorization."

"The grade of inheritance you can exchange will not be low."

"With the upcoming Asura War, if you need anything, directly select an inheritance from it."

"Cultivation Techniques, Taoist Skills, Formations, Artifact Refining Methods... etc., can all be exchanged."

"This is for the sword discussion, for the sect, so during the Sword Discussion Conference, you won’t be docked merit points for exchanging inheritances."

"The inheritances you exchange can also temporarily be exceptionally used by other sword discussion disciples, as long as it’s for the upcoming sword discussion, everything is permissible."

"Additionally, I have informed the elders of all this. Anything you need, just speak out. For this sword discussion matter, you are in charge..."

Mo Hua is dazed momentarily, then earnestly accepts the token with both hands.

The Taixu Sect Master says no more, only looks at Mo Hua warmly, pats him on the shoulder, and then turns to leave.

This implies his stance as sect leader, full support for Mo Hua.

Mo Hua feels warmth in his heart, yet at the same time, a heavy weight on his shoulders.

He lowers his head to look at the token in his hand.

The token is ancient and grand, intricately profound and luxurious, a brand new Taixu Gate Sect Token of the unification of the Three Mountains.

This is the template for a standardized token.

Clearly, in the future Taixu Gate will gradually implement this new type of sect token.

Unified sect token, thereby making all Three Mountains disciples belong to a single sect.

As far as Mo Hua knows, with this new sect token, during its forging for differentiation, each sequential mark is engraved inside the token.

Mo Hua lightly strokes the token.

This is the first token forged after the unification of the Three Mountains, containing the inheritances of the Three Mountains, the Taixu Token.

Then the sequence inside this token should be...

"One".

Mo Hua’s gaze shifts briefly, returns to the Disciple’s Residence, seals the doors and windows with formation, then sits at the table, immersing his divine sense into this brand new sect token.

The name of the sect token is still Taixu Token.

But Taixu is divided into Three Mountains.

Not only the original inheritance from Taixu Gate, but also the inheritances of Tai’a and Chongxu branches are categorized and shown in the "Merit Scroll".

Taixu Gate, Tai’a Sect, Chongxu Sect, all used to be part of the Eight Great Gates.

Thus, the Taixu Token he holds now encompasses the rich Taoist Repository of three of the Eight Great Gates, almost equivalent to "half of the landscape" of Qianxue Eight Great Gates’ inheritance, truly luxurious beyond measure.

Moreover, it includes not just ordinary inheritances.

Mo Hua, while browsing the "Merit Scroll" of Mount Chongxu, discovered a special gift.

It appears to be a rare exception from the Chongxu Sect Leader:

"Chongxu Sword True Skill".

This is the most core sword qi technique spell of Mount Chongxu, the sect protecting inheritance of Mount Chongxu, also the superior swordsmanship that Xiaoxiao practices.

Taia Mountain Opening Sword Formation, Chongxu Sword True Skill, Tai Xu Divine Thought into sword.

This truly corresponds to that saying:

"Tai’a Refining Spirit Sword, Rushing Void Proliferating Sword Qi, Tai Xu Sword Intent..."

Mo Hua feels a slight shock in his heart.

Now he truly is the one person embodying the sect protecting inheritance of the three branches.

This is unexpected.

He previously just thought about it, never imagining he would truly have the day when he could gather the inheritance of the Taixu Three Branches.

And, what’s more, obtain it openly and honestly.

All because the upcoming Asura War is critically important.

The same reason is due to the trust and entrustment from the Sect leaders of the Three Mountains.

Behind the scenes, certainly the sect leaders have engaged in extensive discussions and negotiations.

Even Elder Master Xun, undoubtedly has been earnestly strategizing behind the curtains, just for him...

Although they don’t express it.

Mo Hua gently sighs, turns to gaze outside the window, towards Taixu Mountain.

Looking at this mountain gate where he has lived and cultivated for nine years, an unexpected resonance emerges in his heart, as if connected in thoughts with the Taixu Mountain Range.

This is the sect where he studied.

Here, there are fellow disciples, elders, sect leaders, and Elder Master Xun.

These people all treat him very well.

And he too is bound with Taixu Gate, sharing honor and disgrace.

Mo Hua’s eyes are gentle yet resolute, his mind gradually firming in determination.

He must achieve first place.

Even if not for himself, for the junior brothers who accompany him day and night, for the amiable elders, for the sect leaders who trust him, and for the ancestors in the background, who constantly plan carefully and care deeply for him...

He must claim this first place.

To earn a future for Taixu Gate.

To stand at the peak of Qianxue Debate.

And to elevate Taixu Gate to the pinnacle of thousands of sects within the Qian Learning State Boundary.

No one can obstruct him.

The gentleness on Mo Hua’s face fades away, his eyes glittering with golden sharpness.

No matter the Four Sects, Eight Gates, Twelve Streams, if they intend to stop me, then I will... eliminate them all.

...

Afterwards, Mo Hua officially begins preparations for the Asura Sword Discussion.

With the foundation of the Three Mountains Taoist Repository, the full support of the sect leader, and Taixu Gate delegating power, Mo Hua can do much more.
