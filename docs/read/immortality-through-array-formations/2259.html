Chapter 2259 Chapter 1045: Shocking Transformation (Part 3)

Soon, a group of Heaven Void Ancestors made a discovery.

"Southwest direction!"

"The edge of the Third Grade Yanluo Mountain!"

Yanluo Mountain was also where Mo Hua had previously eradicated the Demon Sect.

An ancestor immediately activated the Heaven Peeping Mirror, focusing towards the southwest, covering the marsh full of poison mist and miasma at Yanluo Mountain for eight hundred miles.

A whirlwind swept through the air, and the scene changed.

Then everyone saw, on the Heaven Peeping Mirror, Xuantian Peak standing solitary into the clouds, situated at the edge of the Mountain Realm of the Qian Learning State Boundary, just bordering the Third Grade Yanluo Mountain.

Seeing this terrain, all the Heaven Void Ancestors were greatly shocked.

"This?!"

"Could it be..."

Before they could react, Xuantian Peak indeed, right before their eyes, directly... toppled over.

Accompanied by a huge "boom" sound, the mountains and rivers changed shape, and dust filled the sky.

The towering Xuantian Peak, after being transported to the edge of the Fifth Grade Qian Learning State Boundary by the Fifth Grade Mount of Tao Formation, directly toppled and collapsed towards the Third Grade Yanluo Mountain Realm from the Fifth Grade State Realm.

In this way, the entire Xuantian Peak became like a "bridge" spanning across the Fifth Grade and Third Grade State Realms.

With the restrictions of the Heavenly Dao Laws, in the Third Grade Realm, these ancestors dared not intervene recklessly.

What a grand conspiracy! What deep schemes!

All the ancestors felt a bone-chilling coldness in their hearts for a moment.

...

Almost at the same time, the shocking change at Xuantian Peak attracted the attention of everyone present, from ancestors to ordinary cultivators watching the event.

At the Shangguan Family's viewing platform.

Wenren Wan was similarly shocked by this scene, looking up at the vanished Xuantian Peak, holding Yu Er tightly in her arms.

At the Shangguan Family's viewing platform.

Wenren Wan was similarly shocked by this scene, looking up at the vanished Xuantian Peak, holding Yu Er tightly in her arms.

But in the next instant, a figure flickered, a cultivator suddenly appeared beside Wenren Wan, placing a jade pendant shaped like a "Dao Discussion Jade" on Yu Er's forehead.

Then, with a flash of blue light, Yu Er disappeared without a trace.

Wenren Wan felt her arms suddenly empty, with Yu Er gone, her face turned pale as ash, heart wracked with agony.

She turned her head and looked, her beautiful eyes filled with anger, and cried out in a hoarse, hateful voice:

"Shangguan Wang——"

Shangguan Wang sneered.

This sudden change also drew the attention of other elders of the Shangguan Family, with an elder furious and shocked:

"Elder Wang, what are you doing?"

Shangguan Wang sneered and said, "I am giving the Shangguan Family's direct descendant an opportunity, allowing this child to shed the mortal and become divine."

After speaking, he directly crushed a blood jade, and his whole person also vanished.

Time was precious, only during this brief upheaval at Xuantian Peak would he have this window; once Shangguang Yu was sent away, he had to evacuate immediately, with no time to delay.

If delayed, once any Heaven Void Ancestor regained awareness and noticed him, he wouldn't be able to leave even if he wanted to.

The surroundings instantly emptied.

In just a moment, Yu Er disappeared.

Shangguan Wang also disappeared.

Wenren Wan had no idea where Shangguan Wang had sent her Yu Er.

What would become of Yu Er's fate?

The nightmarish scene resurfaced before Wenren Wan's eyes once again.

Yu Er was drained of all blood, her organs hollowed out, a face full of blood, skin deathly pale, eyes empty, looking at her, asking why, as a mother, she couldn't protect her child...

An endless fear spread from her heart.

Wenren Wan felt like a dagger was slowly cutting into her heart, tearing her apart with pain...

...

After the upheaval.

In a secret chamber of the Qian Learning State Boundary.

Mr. Tu reconstituted himself from a pool of flesh and blood, then donned a sacrificial robe, his overall presence dark and gaunt, like a demon monster shedding human skin, revealing true form, with long fingers and deathly pale face.

"Everything is ready: Divine Fetus, Blood River, Flesh City, Blood Eye, Bone Pivot, and the very top of the Qian Learning State Boundary... the offering."

"Though it was rushed, there's no choice now..."

"That damned little wretch, if it weren't for him..."

Mr. Tu's face twisted with rage, grinding his teeth, then suppressing his emotions, he slowly sighed:

"Forget it, can't wait any longer..."

"Let's begin then..."

Mr. Tu kneeled on the ground, bowing his head to the ground, "The humble mortals respectfully await the birth of the Master of the Great Wilderness..."

His eyes suddenly bulged out, bloodthirsty yet revealing devotion and madness.

"Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation!"

"Activate!"
