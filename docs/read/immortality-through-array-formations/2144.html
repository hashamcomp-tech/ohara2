Chapter 2144: Chapter 1010: Shi Tiangang

Inside the discussion arena, the forest is lush.

Shi Tiangang is chasing, Mo Hua is fleeing.

Mo Hua escapes hastily, his silhouette panic-stricken like a deer running for its life.

Shi Tiangang wears a cold sneer, his expression imposing, his Vajra form tall and mighty, moving like the wind, like a tiger hunting prey.

Countless spectators outside the arena watch the scene, feeling exhilarated.

"Hateful Mo Hua, your day has come."

"Good and evil will be repaid, it’s not that it’s not repaid, but the time hasn’t come."

They can all tell that once Shi Tiangang gets close to Mo Hua, a light squeeze would be enough to kill Mo Hua.

And what they eagerly anticipate is exactly this scene.

In the mountains and forests.

Shi Tiangang indeed is getting closer to Mo Hua step by step, he is a well-trained Body Cultivator, his movement technique is much faster than Mo Hua’s.

Yet Shi Tiangang is not careless.

He knows Mo Hua definitely isn’t as simple as he appears, and surely won’t sit and wait for death.

Moreover, Mo Hua is the Leader of Formations.

Even though in the Sword Discussion Conference, he relies on "Spell" for a living, he must have left some Formation methods.

Sure enough, not long after, the ground swells, the earth sinks, forming a trap that traps Shi Tiangang.

Second Grade Middle-level Formation: Quicksand Mountain Lock Formation.

Shi Tiangang is indifferent.

He swings his arm, smashing the mountain stone chains, his feet vibrating with Blood Qi, dispersing the quicksand, leaping out of the formation trap.

The Sword Discussion Conference has formation limitations, even Mo Hua cannot exert much formation power.

Second Grade Middle-level Formation cannot trap Shi Tiangang for long.

But this delay gave Mo Hua a bit of distance again.

Shi Tiangang continues the chase, striding like a meteor, flickering several times, again approaching Mo Hua.

Mo Hua is shocked, pushing his movement technique to the extreme.

He uses a Water System Body Skill, his steps skillful, speed not weak, when evading, surrounded by flowing water.

The origin of this movement technique, Shi Tiangang and many Elders of Daoist Law outside the arena can instantly see through.

Flowing Water Step.

This is a rare but not particularly advanced Water Series Escape Skill.

Very fitting Mo Hua’s low-level Five Elements, the path that understands all techniques.

Moreover, Mo Hua uses this movement technique very skillfully.

But Shi Tiangang sneers inwardly.

How can mere basic Five Element movement techniques compare to his Vajra Sect’s Gold Element Step?

Shi Tiangang strides to continue the chase, along the way encountering some formations, obstacles, runes, and other petty tricks, but they can’t truly stop him.

Yet Mo Hua quickly runs out of tricks.

Formations and runes are used up, low-level spells are ineffective.

He’s exhausted all his means.

Mo Hua has no choice but to run for his life. But his only remaining movement technique is still inferior to Shi Tiangang’s.

The thin silhouette reveals a trace of despair.

Outside spectators clap and cheer, applauding in celebration.

And soon, the anticipated scene unfolds.

Shi Tiangang approaches Mo Hua, his gaze sharp, body like a tiger or wolf, Vajra spreading over his hand, as if Buddha reborn, grabbing the petty criminal, reaching for Mo Hua.

Everyone is somewhat tense.

But the next instant, a water light flashes at his fingertips, Shi Tiangang’s grasp hits empty air.

Shi Tiangang looks intently, only to see Mo Hua trip on the flat ground, dodging his Vajra hand, then rolling into the nearby bushes, taking advantage of the dense vegetation to conceal his tracks.

Shi Tiangang doesn’t hesitate, immediately punches.

The punch wind plows the land, shattering rocks, pulverizing trees into dust.

Yet behind the bushes, it’s empty, without Mo Hua’s trace.

Shi Tiangang furrows his brows.

He sweeps with Divine Sense, suddenly looking behind, discovering not far away, Mo Hua jumps out of the forest like a deer, frantically fleeing into the distance.

Shi Tiangang immediately follows the pursuit, closing in, throws another punch, golden light spreading.

Yet Mo Hua seems already aware, his form flashes ahead of time, like foreknowledge, avoiding Shi Tiangang’s punch, then dives into the bush again.

Shi Tiangang stamps his foot, Vajra Strength spreads from his sole, cracking open a fissure that snakes to the shrub.

Then the entire shrub is blown away by strength.

Stones fly everywhere, mixed with sand and wood chips, falling down.

But still no sight of Mo Hua.

A wisp of breath comes from behind, Shi Tiangang turns his head and sees a sneaky figure running away.

It’s Mo Hua.

Shi Tiangang grits his teeth, chasing again, almost catching up, Mo Hua dives behind a large stone.

Shi Tiangang punches, shattering the boulder, but behind it, Mo Hua’s shadow is gone.

Not even a hint of breath.

Then after a moment, Mo Hua appears again from somewhere, glances at Shi Tiangang, and runs away.

Even though Shi Tiangang is usually steady, being manipulated by Mo Hua at this time has made him furious.

His eyes like Vajra, breathing like a tiger roar, drawing Spiritual Energy from the Qi Sea, pushing Vajra’s indestructible force to the extreme, deploying it fully to his leg’s foot Sun channel.

Then with forceful step, the ground cracks, gold light flashes, Shi Tiangang shoots toward Mo Hua like a cannonball.

Mo Hua steps lightly, dodging behind a large tree.

The next moment, the entire tree is pulverized by a mighty golden body.

Yet, behind the tree, still no Mo Hua.

Shi Tiangang turns his head, noticing Mo Hua has somehow appeared behind him again, stepping with Flowing Water Step, escaping far away.

Shi Tiangang finally detects something amiss, heart shocked:

"This kid is elusive and enigmatic...what kind of mysterious movement technique is he using?"
