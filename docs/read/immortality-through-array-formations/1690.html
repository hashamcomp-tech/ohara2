Chapter 1690: Chapter 868: Disturbance (Part 2)

But this time there were too many Demon Monsters, and a single layer of the Divine Barrier Array might not stop them all.

Therefore, Mo Hua set up a second layer of the Divine Barrier.

This Divine Barrier was guarded by Big White the dog.

The strength of Big White, who could spar with himself, was beyond doubt.

All the leftover Demon Monsters would end up in Big White’s mouth.

"Steamed buns" for the dog, never to return.

And in the innermost layer, there was a tightly woven Divine Lock Array, heavily surrounding Yu Er, protecting her in the center.

These three intricately constructed Divine Tao Arrays were like three sturdy dams.

Mo Hua personally guarded the gate, with Big White covering the rear, one person and one dog, tightly guarding, waiting to face the impending flood of Demon Monsters.

Time ticked by.

Soon, the sun set, and night fell.

Mo Hua burned incense, lulled Yu Er to sleep, and then sat alone in meditation on the cushion at the Divine Barrier, eyes closed to restore energy.

Big White, following Mo Hua’s instructions, lay down on another cushion not far away.

On the ground, Mo Hua had drawn dense Formation Patterns.

The atmosphere was tranquil, with only the candle flame flickering slightly.

After an unknown period, with its head drooping on the ground, a somewhat bored Big White suddenly opened its eyes wide with a hint of shock.

Mo Hua, who was meditating, also slowly opened his bright eyes.

The thing he had been waiting for had finally come!

A sudden cold wind filled the room, chilling to the bone, as an eerie and oppressive aura arrived.

It seemed something was secretly breeding.

And in Mo Hua’s deep black, white, and gold intertwined eyes, strange Heavenly Mechanism Patterns appeared inside the room, half filthy, half bloody, intertwined in black and red like a spiderweb, emerging from the Void, spreading across the walls.

The branches of the Divine Authority Tree appeared again like Karmic chains.

Black water condensed on the branches, forming embryos without blossoming, resulting in one Demon after another hatching from these embryos.

One by one, Demon Monsters with ox heads, horse faces, human bodies, demon heads, and grotesque features struggled to crawl out from the Void.

Big White widened its eyes.

Inside its body, there remained the Bloodline of a Divine Beast, making it a particularly unique Exotic Beast.

Things that ordinary Cultivators could not see, it could see.

Still, with so many Demon Monsters and ghosts, it was the first time it had seen such a vast number.

Like a little kid who had never witnessed the world’s grandeur, seeing the flourishing world for the first time.

It even drooled.

The atmosphere was eerie, filled with Demon Monsters, Blood Qi permeating, an overwhelmingly cold and oppressive atmosphere.

However, Mo Hua merely smiled slightly.

As if sensing Mo Hua’s disdain, countless Demon Monsters, with fierce gazes, roared and transformed into a terrifying bloody wind, rushing towards Mo Hua...

"Finally, the feast begins..."

...

Meanwhile, in the Forbidden Land of the Taixu Gate.

Sitting in the Sword Tomb, heavily sealed by numerous formations, the long-bearded elder suddenly opened his eyes.

"Finally, it’s here again..."

He had been waiting for a long time.

Waiting exactly for this day.

He wanted to see, this thing that had continuously stirred up trouble within the Taixu Gate and summoned Demons, what exactly it was.

Was it an Exotic Beast that devoured Demons?

Was it the commander of Evil Spirits, or a scheming Evil Demon skilled in Demon Skills plotting against the Taixu Gate?

He had endured for a long time, and this time, whether it was an Evil Demon or a petty villain, none could hide!

A flash of Golden Light appeared in the long-bearded elder’s eyes.

A profound entity, crystallized, Feather Transformation and then shattered, like a sword-shaped Void, flickered in his eyes.

The Divine Sense of the Heaven Void Realm, majestic and with astonishing Sword Intent, swept over from Taixu Mountain.

This Sword Intent was so profound that ordinary disciples were completely unaware.

Only the Heaven Void Ancestor and a few exceptionally gifted mid to late Feather Transformation True Men could sense it.

Sensing this Divine Sense, everyone turned pale, with cold sweat rushing from their hearts.

This was Divine Thought transformed into sword kind of Divine Sense, inherently carrying an incredibly sharp Sword Intent; fortunately, it merely swept across, for if it carried killing intent, like a Divine Sword sweeping over Taixu Mountain, who knows how many disciples would have perished.

"It’s the Forbidden Land’s... Patriarch Dugu..."

"What has happened?"

"Could it be... has the Ancestor’s Taoist Heart cracked again?"

"Is his Divine Thought unstable?"

"Terrible..."

"In the past, the Ancestor occasionally swept his Divine Sense over Taixu Mountain, but never like today, so swift, with a hint of anger..."

"Has the formation sealing the Ancestor failed? Can’t suppress his Evil Thoughts anymore..."

For a moment, from above the Feather Transformation, the true high ranks of the Taixu Gate were all deeply worried.

The Taixu Sect Master, who was perusing the Classics in the library, was no exception.

But besides a solemn expression, he was also somewhat perplexed.

Patriarch Dugu willingly accepted seclusion, guarding the Sword Tomb, and had been at peace for years; no major incidents occurred recently, so why would such an astonishing Divine Sense suddenly be released?

The always composed Taixu Sect Master couldn’t help but furrow his brows.

He put down the Jade Slip, flicked his sleeve to extinguish the lamp, and stepped out of the library, just about to head outside, when he suddenly paused, turning his head towards the direction of the Disciple’s Residence.

If anything indeed happened, it was just one relatively peculiar small matter...

It’s that Mo Hua borrowed that Exotic Beast from him.

Mo Hua suddenly borrowed the Exotic Beast, and the Taixu Sect Master was a bit curious, so he quietly released his Divine Sense, following Mo Hua into the Disciple’s Residence, wanting to see what Mo Hua was up to.

Later, he saw Mo Hua bring the Exotic Beast into the Shangguan Family, into that direct lineage little kid’s room called "Yu Er."
