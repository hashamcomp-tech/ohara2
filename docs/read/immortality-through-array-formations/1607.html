Chapter 1607: Chapter 843: All Here

But at this moment, Water Yama seemed somewhat off.

The Evil Qi around him was heavier than before, his aura somewhat violent, and deep in his eyes was a lustful thought, seemingly due to his lost arm and wounded Vital Energy, causing the Evil Qi cultivated midway through killing to become chaotic.

And this thick mist took advantage of the situation, stirring up the Evil Thoughts buried deep in his heart.

He now looked covetously, his snake-like gaze lingering over Hua Qianqian’s delicate body.

Hua Qianqian shivered all over under his gaze.

Ouyang Feng’s expression turned solemn as he shielded Hua Qianqian behind him.

But his injuries were not light, blood seeping from the wound bit by bit, soaking the sword’s hilt, his hand gripping the sword feeling somewhat numb.

This evil cultivator was a formidable foe!

Up until now, he hadn’t encountered such a powerful Evil Path cultivator with such strong cultivation and Taoist Skill methods.

In direct confrontation, he was completely at a disadvantage.

And this person had even lost an arm.

If not for that, his strength would be even greater!

Ouyang Feng felt heavy-hearted.

Water Yama saw Ouyang Feng blocking Hua Qianqian, unable to see the captivating beauty, he became temporarily angry before laughing out loud instead.

"Good! A haggard beauty and a flower-protecting talent, very good! I have always enjoyed doing this kind of thing in my life."

"In front of a woman, torment her beloved; in front of a man, humiliate his beloved woman."

"And then watch the two of them die with hearts aching, in utter desolation, unwilling even in death..."

Water Yama laughed somewhat neurotically, "Isn’t it a bit perverse?"

"I can’t help it either, having practiced the Demon Path spells, cultivating Evil Qi. This Evil Qi accumulates inside me, and if I don’t find some amusement, torment others to disperse this accumulated Qi, I myself will go mad first."

As Water Yama spoke, his eyes began to show Blood Threads:

"Lately, things have been going poorly for me, this Evil Qi... has weighed heavily deep in my heart for a long time, with no way to relieve it. You pair of Heaven’s Pride young lovers are just right to become my ’woman’ for cultivation venting."

"Moreover..." Water Yama looked at Hua Qianqian’s faintly visible delicate body behind Ouyang Feng, feeling as if his heart was being gnawed by ants, unbearably itchy,

"Such a pure and tender little piece... "

"Blame it only on your poor luck..."

Water Yama’s eyes turned red, his body transforming into Water Shadow, like a yaksha in the water, ferocious and terrifying, rushing towards Ouyang Feng.

Ouyang Feng remained grim, wielding the Sword light, engaging Water Yama in battle.

His first encounter with Water Yama led to an unfamiliarity with his strange methods, and having to care for Hua Qianqian behind him made him inevitably flustered, relying solely on solid cultivation and exquisite Swordsmanship to support himself.

Mo Hua observed all this, eyes slightly cold, thinking:

"This Water Yama seems somewhat unstable, must find a way to kill him..."

A shred of killing intent emerged in his heart.

But just as this killing intent emerged, the Evil Qi invaded the mind, and Water Yama’s exceptionally keen Perception immediately startled.

He immediately ceased his actions, leaping aside, glaring viciously towards the Concealed location of Mo Hua, coldly asking:

"Who?!"

Mo Hua was somewhat surprised.

Had Water Yama suddenly become this alert?

Mo Hua naturally wouldn’t reveal himself.

But Water Yama refused to let it go, almost certain that a faint killing intent had surfaced, making him feel stabbed in the back.

He had suffered losses before and thus would not be careless.

Without waiting for Mo Hua to act, Water Yama took out a Blood Pearl and crushed it directly.

A spray of Blood Mist exploded instantly, permeating the room and engulfing Mo Hua within.

Mo Hua’s form was like water, hurriedly retreating, but the Blood Mist was too thick, a vast expanse, some still clinging to him.

Blood Mist inherently had no killing power, but once adhered, couldn’t be washed off quickly, exposing Mo Hua’s whereabouts.

Mo Hua furrowed his brow slightly.

This Blood Pearl seemed to be some kind of special Evil Artifact.

Water Yama had learned from a previous misstep, having his beloved Water Prison Forbidden Box stolen right before him due to Concealment, thus in agony from the setback, he specifically prepared this Blood Pearl to limit cultivators skillful in Concealment.

This time, it was just used against Mo Hua.

Mo Hua had no choice but to slowly reveal his form.

Water Yama saw his method worked, the sneak being forced to reveal his form, leading him to coldly sneer.

Yet upon seeing Mo Hua’s face, his pupils involuntarily shrank.

Too young.

Like a youth under twenty.

To be adept at Concealment Technique, mastering Invisibility, none were not old and cunning after years in the martial world.

How could he be like this?

Moreover, he didn’t recognize this little imp...

Water Yama frowned, "Who are you?"

Mo Hua’s expression was somewhat nuanced.

This Water Yama, his waterside camp captured by the Taoist Court under himself, his prized Forbidden Box snatched by himself, yet he did not recognize him...

Mo Hua turned his thoughts and roughly understood.

At the dock during the first encounter with Water Yama, he intentionally masked his face, so his appearance wasn’t seen.

The second time, he was lurking in the dark, making a quick move to grab his Water Prison Forbidden Box.

Actions were too quick, leaving too swiftly, the interior dimly lit, he didn’t see clearly again.

But Water Yama was keen of mind, quickly coming to realization despite moments had passed, his pupils suddenly trembled, exclaiming incredulously:

"It’s you!"

He has recognized it!

This face didn’t ring a bell, but this figure, he wouldn’t forget even in death!

The silhouette of Mo Hua before him gradually merged with that of the scoundrel he wished to slaughter a thousand times and grind the bones to dust.
