Chapter 1741: Chapter 883: Monster

Everyone paused, then immediately burst into an uproar.

Like a drop of water falling into a pot of oil, with a "buzz," the area around the Great Dojo stirred into commotion.

"Super Divine Sense!"

The Taixu Sect disciples were even more excited.

"I knew it all along, Junior Brother’s divine sense is ridiculously strong, definitely not simple!" Cheng Mo said with pride.

"You knew it all along and didn’t say anything?"

"How can I casually talk about something like this?"

Cheng Mo replied righteously.

In truth, he wasn’t entirely sure. He had only vaguely speculated that Mo Hua’s divine sense was definitely stronger than average cultivators, but he hadn’t expected it to be this strong.

The higher-level disciple, Murong Caiyun, was shocked but also delighted.

Never did she imagine that this junior brother, who had been tagging along with her for merit points since joining, actually possessed such talent and skill.

If that’s the case, the Taixu Sect’s ranking might be secure.

On the side of the Taixu Sect elders.

Xun Ziyou shook his head and sighed:

"This child truly knows how to endure, and the ancestor is really good at hiding things. If not for the Array Discussion Conference being so significant, the matter of having a Super Divine Sense might still be kept under wraps."

Xun Zixian nodded as well.

"Those with extraordinary talent are bound to be envied. If one doesn’t hide their light and protect themselves, it’s easy to get entrapped. On principle, the deeper the concealment, the better."

"But given the current special situation, whether we can maintain our place among the Eight Great Gates hangs by a thread, leaving no room for error. At such a time, we have no choice but to draw our precious sword and let Mo Hua shine..."

"Indeed," Xun Ziyou sighed, "Our Taixu Sect, is indeed blessed by the ancestors, turning the tides in our favor..."

If Mo Hua hadn’t fallen from the sky, this hurdle of the Dao Conference would be hard to overcome.

...

And at this moment, on the high platform of the families.

The cultivators of the Shangguan Family, Wenren Family, and Gu Family were all silent.

Even Wenren Wan, who was holding Yu Er with a pale face, was momentarily in a daze.

Super Divine Sense?

Mo Hua?

Wenren Wan found it hard to believe.

The hearts of everyone from the families were particularly tumultuous at this moment.

Does it mean... the child whom the Young Madam of the Shangguan Family painstakingly, through many connections, managed to get into the Taixu Sect through the backdoor, is truly a genuine Super Divine Sense formation genius?

Could such a thing be possible?

Shangguan Wang’s face changed, realizing that the situation was not good.

Gu Shouyan furrowed his brow. He wasn’t very skilled in formations, so he was a bit unsure and looked at Gu Changhuai for input.

Gu Changhuai, who was close to Mo Hua, had already vaguely speculated about Mo Hua’s extraordinary divine sense, but discovering his Super Divine Sense publicly, with undeniable evidence, was still shocking.

Seeing the Family Head look toward him, Gu Changhuai pondered for a moment and nodded.

Implying that it is likely true.

Gu Shouyan took a slight breath, sighing:

"Truly... young people are to be feared..."

This child, who often came to the Gu Family, eating, drinking, and receiving gifts, indeed possessed such an incredible Divine Sense talent.

No wonder Elder Master Xun would be so fond of this child.

This also validated that last time the child went to the Gu Family, taking unsigned Heaven Void scripts, saying it was a "gift" on behalf of Elder Master Xun...

It was very likely not just borrowing Ancestor Xun’s name to bail out Wan’er.

But genuinely, it was actually at the behest of Elder Master Xun.

Wenren Jingxuan was also stunned.

He never expected that in a short moment, the situation would change so drastically.

And this child’s talent was indeed unbelievably ridiculous...

Shang Che was visibly stunned for a moment, then his gaze flickered, and he spoke gently to Wenren Wan:

"Wan’er, your position is a bit off, making it inconvenient to observe the ceremony. I’ll have someone change it for you."

The seating order of the families was strictly hierarchical.

Once determined, it generally wouldn’t be altered under normal circumstances.

Of course, this was for normal circumstances.

Clearly, the current situation was not normal.

Soon, attending disciples changed the seats for Wenren Wan and Yu Er.

This seat was no longer on the edge, but closer to the high platform.

The seating height was also elevated, ranked above all regular Golden Core Elders, just below the Feather Transformation Elders.

Shang Che nodded slightly.

In any case, with a Super Divine Sense, the future is promising.

He couldn’t possibly be oblivious to this.

...

Meanwhile, at the seating of the Four Great Sects.

The atmosphere was a bit heavy.

"The Taixu Sect still had this card hidden?" The Elder from the Heavenly Sword Sect frowned.

"Super Divine Sense... it’s impressive that Taixu Sect could find such a talent..." Elder Shen shook his head.

"There is no cultivation technique for Divine Sense; it’s either innately generated, postnatally mutated, or due to some great opportunity. How exactly did he surpass to Super Divine Sense?"

"Unclear..."

"The world is vast and full of wonders."

"It’s a pity his spiritual root is too poor, otherwise with this divine sense aptitude, joining our Qian Taoist Sect would be more than sufficient..."

"Such a talent, instead of hiding and grooming him properly, the Taixu Sect showcased him so early?"

The Longding Sect Elder chuckled softly, "Feeling cornered, maybe they had no other tricks, and without revealing their trump card, they risk dropping out of the Eight Great Gates."

"That’s a good thing."

"Such a disciple, after nurturing for three more years, might indeed become a major threat, perhaps taking a bite of fat meat from our Four Great Sects."

"Now that they’re exposed early, we have a full three years to find ways to counteract."

"Taking advantage of the sect reform, we can modify the Array Competition system in the next term, adding some cultivation level or spiritual root restrictions, or lengthening the assessment items..."

"Yes, knowing beforehand makes it easier to handle. The real fear is being caught off guard when completely unaware."
