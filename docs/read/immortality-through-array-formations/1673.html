Chapter 1673: Chapter 863: True and False (Part 3)

With these words, Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official both had a change in expression.

Even Mo Hua furrowed his brow.

Hua Ruyu glanced at Gu Changhuai’s stiff expression and couldn’t help but laugh flamboyantly, speaking in a sarcastic tone:

"You wouldn’t think that I’m just a simple Sect Instructor, would you?"

"For so many years, I’ve trained so many top-grade girls and served numerous high-ranking sons. Though some are heartless and indifferent, ultimately, there are a few who remember the past and are reluctant to see me die..."

"They cherish and adore, unwilling to see me suffer at the Taoist Court, revealing secrets that shouldn’t be spoken."

"Master Xia is also a person who does great deeds, broad-minded and able to act decisively, not bothered with a little woman like me."

After finishing, Hua Ruyu glanced at Gu Changhuai with quite a bit of satisfaction.

"Unexpected, isn’t it? A place like the Taoist Prison simply can’t hold me. You’ve struggled to investigate for so long, yet you can’t do anything to me. Some things are beyond what you mere errand boys can decide..."

Gu Changhuai’s face was full of killing intent.

Xia Canonical Official also had anger in his eyes.

Mo Hua couldn’t stand her arrogant demeanor and spoke out with concern:

"Sister Hua, what happened to your face? Why has it become so ugly, are you disfigured?"

Upon hearing this, Hua Ruyu immediately reacted like a furious fox, her expression extremely fierce, and rushed viciously towards Mo Hua.

"You little brat, I’ll kill you..."

Before she could get close to Mo Hua, Gu Changhuai reached out with his large hand, twisted her arm, and pushed her back.

Gu Changhuai wished he could slaughter Hua Ruyu.

But he knew his limits; if he really killed Hua Ruyu now, he wouldn’t be able to answer to Master Xia and the Taoist Court.

His position as Supervisor would essentially be over.

Hua Ruyu staggered a few steps before regaining her balance, appearing haggard.

She knew she wasn’t a match for Gu Changhuai and the others, so she just glared at them fiercely.

Especially at that "maliciously spiteful" Mo Hua, she wished she could swallow him whole.

Hua Ruyu sneered coldly:

"Alright, I’ll remember this grudge. Don’t forget, I have someone backing me, it’s his Highness."

"For over a hundred years, how many girls have I trained? Among the many esteemed sons of the Qian Learning State Boundary, who hasn’t experienced bone-chilling ecstasy with me?"

"Now they’re all high-ranked, how could they truly abandon me? Sooner or later, I will make sure you all die without a trace."

After finishing, Hua Ruyu sneered and turned to leave.

Mo Hua and the other two didn’t stop her, just silently stayed in their original spots.

Gu Changhuai was silent for a moment, then spoke coldly: "I’ll go find Master Xia."

Xia Canonical Official held him back, shaking his head: "No need to go."

Gu Changhuai looked silently at Xia Canonical Official.

Xia Canonical Official sighed: "Uncle’s actions are always well thought out, deeply considered; whatever decision he makes, seeking him out is useless."

Gu Changhuai glanced at Xia Canonical Official, a hint of disappointment flashing in his eyes, and spoke softly: "I understand."

Then he said nothing more and turned back inside.

Xia Canonical Official looked at Gu Changhuai, looking forlorn, then also turned and left.

Mo Hua watched the two leave in discontent and couldn’t help but sigh.

Back in his room, Mo Hua felt a bit regretful.

"I really should have blown up Hua Ruyu, what a miscalculation..."

If he had known, he would have arranged more Formations, blowing Hua Ruyu up with the Rouge Boat, ending it once and for all.

Since she survived by chance, not being content to hide, dare to show her face, she really has tired of living.

And he doesn’t even know which noble her "lover" is now.

Even Master Xia must sell his Face.

Mo Hua shook his head, feeling a bit sentimental.

The waters here are really too deep, and too dirty...

"However, Hua Ruyu didn’t die..."

Mo Hua pulled out Boss Jiang’s list from his Storage Bag, seeing that on the list, under Water Yama, Madam Miao’s name was indeed not faded, indicating Hua Ruyu, as Madam Miao, was still alive and well.

"That’s a pity..."

Mo Hua felt somewhat regretful.

Good people don’t live long, evildoers last for a thousand years.

Mo Hua was about to consider whether to eliminate Hua Ruyu but suddenly sensed something amiss.

"Water Yama..."

He looked at the list again, especially the name "Water Yama," and couldn’t help but be shaken.

"Why is Water Yama’s name... still on the list?!"

"Isn’t he dead?"

"What’s going on?"

"Water Yama... he’s not dead?"

Mo Hua’s pupils shook, then he shook his head.

No, impossible!

He was absolutely certain Water Yama was dead.

Killed by him with the Soul-Shocking Sword, destroying his Divine Soul, a backlash from the Pupil Skill, stabbed through the heart by Senior Brother Feng’s Longsword, pierced in the forehead by Sister Qianqian’s Spirit Needle.

Even Xiao Tianquan had given a final stab.

Not only was his body dead, but his Divine Soul also completely annihilated, he couldn’t have been wrong...

"But why?"

"Why is Water Yama’s title not erased from the list, not even slightly faded?"

Mo Hua’s brow furrowed tightly, thoughts racing.

Could it be... that the "Water Yama" who died was not actually Water Yama, at least not the Water Yama who signed a "soul contract" at the altar.

The real Water Yama is someone else?

Who could this person be?

Mo Hua’s head hurt for a while.

Suspects came to mind one after the other, but he couldn’t decide.

He pondered this question the entire night, but could only speculate, unable to find an answer.
