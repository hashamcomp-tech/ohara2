Under the blood-red shroud.

Throughout Qianxue, the bloodthirsty slaughter does not cease, the cruel chaos continues.

Blood flows across the land.

Flesh begins to be piled into walls.

On the altars everywhere, one by one, terrified heads of those who died miserably after remaining, are placed up as offerings to the evil gods...

Blood and slaughter gradually stain the sky red.

The blood omen between heaven and earth, demon scourge suddenly arises, this is a millennial unprecedented upheaval.

If handled improperly, these court leaders face, at best, demotion and investigation, imprisonment; at worst, extreme punishment, decapitation.

Evil demons slaughter cultivators, Taoist Court Tribunal suppresses evil demons.

Deeper within, even stronger demon-monster power silently surges...

An old familiar, bloody, fearsome name floated in their minds:

Demon Path Great Formation!

At this moment, amidst the peaceful world, there are actually people daring, in the Fifth Grade Qianxue State Boundary, at this cultivation paradise where sects stand and aristocratic families gather, under the noses of a group of Fifth Grade Heaven Void Ancestors, to outrageously open the heavily murderous Demon Path Great Formation?!

Absolutely insane, audaciously reckless.

The background of this person, is likely extremely terrifying...

So much so that, although suspicions filled the hearts of all ancestors present, none dared to speak them aloud.

Some things, without solid evidence, cannot be spoken randomly.

They are all ancestors; how could they not understand this principle?

They can only deeply bury this doubt within their hearts.

The current crucial plan is to consider how to quickly destroy the Demon Path Great Formation, quell the demon scourge, this is the most urgent problem.

Once the Demon Path Great Formation gains momentum, the evil disaster spreads, surrounding Qianxue State Boundary, will surely be plunged into devastation.

Even more, each day the Demon Path Great Formation exists, will result in who knows how many needless deaths of cultivators.
