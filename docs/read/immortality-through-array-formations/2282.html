Chapter 2282: Chapter 1053: Great Wilderness Order (2)

Within the Evil Path Great Formation, ordinary Original Magnet fluctuations are certainly isolated.

Since Mr. Tu has laid such a vast plot, he must have planned meticulously. It’s impossible for him to make such a basic mistake, giving them a loophole.

Yet such Original Magnet stuff, they can’t avoid using it.

With such a large conspiracy at play, the operation of this Evil Path Great Formation cannot lack coordination.

Without some means of ’communication’, even unified actions would be impossible.

Mo Hua glanced at the corpse of the Evil Formation Master, then at the spoils he scavenged from him, and said:

"Pry open all the Evil Formation Masters’ teeth, check each one, see if there’s a Token hidden."

"Also, go through these Storage Bags again and find all the Tokens for me."

The crowd was unsure of Mo Hua’s intention, but they followed his instructions, prying teeth, rummaging through Storage Bags, and soon, Mo Hua had seven or eight Tokens in his hands.

Most of these Tokens were made of White Bone, bloody and sinister, all considered to be ’escaped fish’.

After all, the Evil Formation Masters acted cunningly, there were over eighty of them, and time was tight, Mo Hua, despite being clever, unlikely cleared their corpses in one sweep.

Mo Hua rifled through these Tokens one by one, discarding most, retaining only one.

This Token wasn’t a White Bone Order but made of jade, with two ancient, awkward characters inscribed:

Great Wilderness.

"Great Wilderness Order?"

Mo Hua was surprised, then studied it further, realizing this Token was actually a ’Sect Token’.

"Great Wilderness... Sect?"

Mo Hua found the name somewhat familiar but didn’t think much of it at the moment.

Moreover, just based on the words ’Great Wilderness’, he couldn’t confirm outright that the Sect’s name was Great Wilderness Sect.

It could likely belong to some sect power that pays tribute to the Master of Great Wilderness.

"Since it’s a Sect Token, it surely maintains a sect force, has corresponding sect heritage, and can also communicate within the sect."

"This is the Token from an Evil Formation Master, suggesting the Original Magnet information in it is permitted through the Evil Path Great Formation."

Mo Hua’s eyes brightened as he began studying the ’Great Wilderness Order’.

This Great Wilderness Order was encrypted and sealed, forbidden for outsiders to use.

But such encryption couldn’t thwart Mo Hua, being a Thunder Magnetic Formation Master.

With considerable Thunder Magnetism Formation research experience, he’s well-versed in decryption activities.

He stored relevant tools in his Storage Ring, ready for unanticipated needs.

Mo Hua retrieved several Secondary Thunder Pattern Libraries, Jade Slips, Array Plates related to ’Thunder Magnetism’, placed them on the ground, and skillfully commenced, sophisticated and complex pattern unsealing and secret pattern decryption.

These are profound and obscure formation techniques.

Other disciples watched, yet understood nothing, with their minds increasingly numbed.

No matter what formation Mo Hua drew, they weren’t surprised nor did they feel inferior.

You can only compare yourself with other humans, not with ’monsters’.

Different species shouldn’t be forced to compete.

After intricate yet orderly operations, Mo Hua finally broke the sealing patterns, decrypted the secret patterns, and infiltrated the Great Wilderness Order.

Inside the Great Wilderness Order, there were indeed some sect cultivation techniques and Taoist skills, but they were black-grey, as if deliberately ’sealed’, obscuring the heritage within.

Mo Hua was curious but understood the urgency of the situation. It wasn’t the time to break sealing patterns and steal heritage.

Mo Hua focused his mind on the ’communication’ magnetic patterns of the Great Wilderness Order.

In this Great Wilderness Order, there were indeed numerous magnetic paths and densely packed Secondary Thunder Pattern traces, implying the Great Wilderness Order contained many ’fellow’ disciples, and the owner of this Great Wilderness Order frequently communicated with them.

The owner of the Great Wilderness Order was a deceased Evil Formation Master, working for Mr. Tu.

Which meant numerous ’Great Wilderness’ fellow members were under Mr. Tu’s command.

Implying Mo Hua could use the Great Wilderness Order to transmit messages outside.

Mo Hua felt pleased but then stunned:

"Send the message out, but... to whom?"

"Those who receive the Great Wilderness Order’s messages must also be Mr. Tu’s minions; send them letters to rescue me?"

Wouldn’t that be sabotaging oneself?

Mo Hua held his chin, pondering.

Though trapped in the Blood Sacrifice Array, ignorant of external happenings, he could infer generally:

"Hundreds of prodigies across Qian Learning State Boundary were forcibly transferred by Mr. Tu, trapped in the Evil Path Great Formation, various sects, aristocratic families, even the Court Tribunal would surely react..."

"They would surely attempt to break the formation, rescue sect prodigies, or at least capture and kill some Evil God’s minions to weaken the formation’s force."

"If conflict arises, surely some of Mr. Tu’s minions would fall or get caught by the Court Tribunal."

"Then is there a possibility... similar Evil Cultivitors’ ’Tokens’ fell into the hands of the Qianxue Court Tribunal?"

"If I transmit ’help’ magnetic patterns to these Tokens, they could be received by the Court Tribunal, coordinating inside-out to rescue Qianxue sect prodigies..."

But doing this presents another problem:

Mo Hua couldn’t differentiate which Tokens had fallen to the Court Tribunal and which remained with Mr. Tu’s minions.
