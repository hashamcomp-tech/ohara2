Chapter 1875: Chapter 923: Loyalty

Mo Hua felt somewhat incredulous, while simultaneously harboring doubts in his mind.

Why would a Demon Sect that practices blood-sucking cultivation skills actually hide such a top-tier formation that contains powerful variations of spiritual power laws?

How did they acquire this Second Grade, Twenty Patterns Reversed Spirit Formation?

Did they acquire it through killing and robbing?

Did they directly transfer it from the Demon Sect inheritance of a higher level?

Or perhaps someone intentionally hid it inside?

Do they really understand what this formation truly signifies?

Mo Hua flipped through the annotations about the Reversed Spirit Formation in the Demon Sect merit vault:

"Second Grade, Twenty Patterns, alternative formation diagram, contains a certain power of spiritual laws, extremely difficult to comprehend, effects unknown, purpose unknown. Demon Points: Three thousand..."

Extremely difficult to comprehend, effects unknown, purpose unknown...

Mo Hua pondered deeply inside.

It means that these Demon Cultivators fundamentally do not know what the "Reversed Spirit Formation" is, what its purpose is, and no one can learn it.

Moreover, it only requires three thousand Demon Points.

These three thousand points aren’t a small amount in the Demon Sect’s merit vault, but compared to the extremely rare "Reversed Spirit Formation," it’s nothing at all.

Any ultimate formation is a "priceless" treasure.

If there is an opportunity, perhaps you can encounter it; without the opportunity, no amount of Spirit Stones could buy it.

Three thousand as the price is simply an insult to this formation.

"These Demon Cultivators are truly blind, unable to recognize a treasure when they see it..."

Mo Hua murmured to himself, feeling that this Demon Sect is really wasting heavenly treasures, letting such a good thing gather dust in a warehouse.

But upon careful thought, it seems reasonable.

In the Demon Sect, the cultivators practice bloody demon skills, and there are very few who understand formations. It is estimated that none can understand this Reversed Spirit Formation.

In their eyes, this Reversed Spirit Formation might just be an alternative formation, complex and incomprehensible, and its purpose unknown.

Even if they could understand it and learn it, it would still be useless.

Without a strong foundation in formations, without proficiency in Divine Sense Calculation, unable to deduce the Spirit Traces of the formation, it is simply impossible to precisely apply the Reversed Spirit Formation, leading to formation collapse and spiritual power "decomposition," causing devastating killing power.

The principles of the Reversed Spirit Formation are obscure and profound, making its application extremely difficult.

Mo Hua fondled the Guest Elder Token, looking at the three words "Reversed Spirit Formation" on it, his heartbeat quickened a bit.

In the hands of others, the Reversed Spirit Formation might just be a difficult and useless formation.

But in his possession, it is altogether different.

As long as he has the Reversed Spirit Formation, with a Large Formation, he could even destroy the Taoist Demon.

Of course, decomposing a Large Formation is too extravagant.

In this lifetime, he doesn’t know if there will be an opportunity.

But even if he cannot decompose a Large Formation, the Reversed Spirit Formation is extremely powerful.

With his current proficiency in formations, if he truly obtains the Reversed Spirit Array Map, learns the Second Grade Reversed Spirit Formation, plans and arranges in advance, it is completely possible to "collapse and kill" a Golden Core Realm cultivator with Foundation Establishment cultivation.

This is a true cross-border defeat!

Although strictly speaking, he is not relying on his own cultivation.

But Tao cultivation knowledge, in itself, implies power, and a powerful formation means powerful combat power.

"I must get this Reversed Spirit Formation!"

"The Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation is great, but right now, I don’t know where it’s hidden. Instead, the fat meat in front of me has to be firmly bitten."

The fact that he could see this Reversed Spirit Array Map means he has this opportunity at this moment.

Missing it would surely lead to lifelong regret.

If he hesitated even for a moment, and the Demon Sect moved, or got wiped out, if the merit vault was destroyed, the treasure vault seized by the Taoist Court, or if other changes occurred, leaving this formation diagram in someone else’s hands, seeking it again would be like finding a needle in a haystack.

When the opportunity arises, it must be firmly grasped!

Mo Hua no longer hesitated and immediately began contemplating how to acquire this Reversed Spirit Array Map.

But after pondering for a moment, Mo Hua furrowed his brows.

"It seems... it’s not that easy to obtain..."

The Second Grade Reversed Spirit Array Map is something within the Demon Sect merit vault, requiring Demon Points to exchange.

Demon Points are actually manageable. He has now infiltrated the lower levels of the Original Magnet Compound Formation, and theoretically, the amount of Demon Points can be altered through magnetic pattern tampering.

But the problem is, even with Demon Points, how to exchange?

After exchanging, how to get hold of it?

Mo Hua now somewhat understands the Demon Sect and knows that according to the usual process, he first needs a clear, "living" identifiable identity of a Demon Sect cultivator, to use the Demon Sect Token and spend three thousand Demon Points to exchange for this Reversed Spirit Array Map.

After exchanging the formation diagram, this Demon Sect cultivator must personally go into the Demon Sect, to a place akin to the "Merit Pavilion," to retrieve this formation diagram.

Then, he has to deliver this formation diagram into Mo Hua’s hands.

This entire process involves both operations on the Original Magnet level and actions in reality.

On the Original Magnet level, he can forge a "Sequence Magnetic Pattern" to create a nonexistent Demon Sect disciple to exchange for the formation diagram on his behalf.

But on the reality level, he has no way to create a real "person" to go into the Demon Sect and retrieve the Reversed Spirit Array Map.

Moreover, he does not know where the Demon Sect’s stronghold is, even if he could create such a "person," he wouldn’t know where to go to retrieve his Reversed Spirit Array Map.

"It’s a bit troublesome..."

Mo Hua couldn’t help but lie on the ground, with his arms under his head, staring blankly at the ceiling in a trance.

After thinking for a moment, he murmured:

"If only I knew how to ’Planting Devil in Taoist Heart,’ I could ask Uncle Gu to capture a Demon Sect disciple, plant the devil in his Taoist Heart, and have him go to the Demon Sect to help me pick up my ’parcel’..."
