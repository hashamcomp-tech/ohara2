Chapter 1640: Chapter 853: Bloody Battle (Part 2)

Afterwards, he kept his promise and pointed a finger on the shoulders of Ouyang Feng and Hua Qianqian, removing the Water Prison strength from their bodies, then took out a bottle of pills and tossed it to Hua Qianqian.

"Detoxification Pill from Gui Water Sect, it can detoxify."

Hua Qianqian hesitated for a moment, then took out a pill and swallowed it. After a while, she felt the remaining toxins dissipate and let out a slight sigh of relief, nodding to Mo Hua.

Master Xiao looked at Mo Hua seriously and said, "I have done as you said, young brother, do not let me down."

Mo Hua nodded and resolutely said, "Alright, I’ll give you the box!"

Mo Hua reached into the storage bag, then took out the Water Prison Forbidden Box from the Storage Ring, put it into the storage bag, and then "pretended" to take it out from the storage bag, neatly tossing it to Master Xiao.

His actions were so decisive, so crisp and unhesitant.

As if he was tossing not the Sect Protecting Treasure of the Water Prison Sect, but just an ordinary, valueless box.

Master Xiao feared Mo Hua might trick him, so he hesitated for a moment.

But just in that moment of hesitation, a figure suddenly flew forward, wanting to grab the forbidden box right in front of Master Xiao.

It was Xie Liu!

Master Xiao was enraged, "Audacious!"

He immediately thrust his sword with all his strength.

Xie Liu didn’t dodge or evade, allowing Master Xiao’s sword to pierce through his shoulder, but still pressed forward without hesitation to snatch the box.

After grabbing the forbidden box, Xie Liu disregarded his injury, broke free from Master Xiao’s longsword, took a few leaps, and retreated several meters away. Despite the blood flowing from his shoulder, his heart was filled with wild joy:

"The Water Prison Forbidden Box! It’s mine now!"

He laughed madly a few times, then with a face full of grievances, he said angrily:

"With this legacy, I, Xie Liu, will no longer be a nameless nobody! I will never bow to others again, never be bossed around again!"

Seeing his treasure clutched in someone else’s hands, Master Xiao was furious beyond measure, "Seeking death!"

He swung his sword again.

Xie Liu chuckled coldly, leapt away, dodging Master Xiao’s swing.

Seeing his strike miss, Master Xiao’s face fell immediately. He pointed his fingers, and a Water Prison Technique formed out of thin air, enveloping Xie Liu.

Initially, Xie Liu didn’t pay much attention.

Earlier, he had seen Master Xiao using the Water Prison Technique to control that brat without hitting once, so he thought it was a useless spell.

But he didn’t know that he wasn’t Mo Hua. He didn’t have such keen perception of killing intent, nor such profound mastery of the Dao of the Divine, nor the same proficiency with the Water Prison Technique.

Seeing Mo Hua evade so easily, he became a bit careless, but when he truly faced it, in an instant, he was tightly bound by the Water Prison Technique.

Xie Liu’s expression changed dramatically.

Just as Master Xiao’s Sword Qi was about to arrive, Xie Liu’s expression turned frantic, then he gritted his teeth and exerted all his spiritual power, narrowly escaping from the Water Prison Technique.

But his back was still struck by Master Xiao’s sword.

Enduring the severe pain, Xie Liu countered with a backward sword strike, then leapt away, escaping to the distance.

But Master Xiao was somewhat powerless.

No matter how strong he was, he was still only at the Initial Golden Core stage. Frequently using Sword Qi and spells, and even depleting a large amount of spiritual power to perform superior Water Prison Control, his spiritual power was running low, making it very difficult to pursue Xie Liu.

Master Xiao was both shocked and furious.

He never expected that after battling wits with Gu Changhuai and Xia Canonical Official, and the trickery from Mo Hua, all efforts were in vain, letting Xie Liu, a seemingly insignificant pawn, slip through the cracks, pick up the cheap, and snatch the treasure of the Water Prison!

Master Xiao roared, "Xie Liu, return the forbidden box to me, or I will tear you to pieces!"

Upon hearing this, Xie Liu sneered inwardly.

Returning a treasure that’s already in my hands? Does Xiao Zhenhai take me for a fool?

"The box is sealed with secret techniques. Without knowing how to ’unlock’ it, it’s useless to keep." Master Xiao said.

Xie Liu ignored him and just ran forward blindly.

Whether he could open it or not, he decided to hold it firmly in his own hands first.

Seeing the White Bone Boat just ahead, Xie Liu sped up his movement technique, eager to board the boat and flee this place early.

Seeing this, Master Xiao’s expression became extremely terrifying.

His forehead bulged with veins, his face turned livid, his originally squinted eyes suddenly widened, filled with blood threads, and an uncontrollable evil Qi surged around him.

"Xie Liu, die..."

Xie Liu was fleeing well when suddenly he sensed an astonishing killing intent behind him, mixed with frightening evil Qi that sent chills down his spine.

"What’s going on?"

Xie Liu was utterly shocked.

This Xiao Zhenhai... what means does he intend to use to kill me?

The White Bone Boat was just ahead, and once onboard, he could temporarily shake off Xiao Zhenhai. Afterward, he could find an opportunity to leave the Dragon King Temple and devour the Water Prison treasure alone.

But deep down, Xie Liu still felt a bit uneasy.

As a Canonical Official of the Taoist Court, Xiao Zhenhai was deeply scheming, skilled at enduring, with his Taoist skills being fiercely unpredictable.

Xie Liu had no idea what methods Xiao Zhenhai had in his hands, fearing he might be killed in one blow, so he turned his head to take a look.

But just that one glance, changed the game of life and death.

He saw Master Xiao’s eyes.

Those were a pair of blood-red eyes, seemingly containing a Taoist Prison, imprisoning sinners within, who were enduring bloody torture, constantly suffering and screaming.

Blood flowed from the sinners, gradually filling the entire Taoist Prison.

The sinners’ faces were blurred, still struggling, full of despair.

In the next moment, the face of the sinner gradually became clear, suddenly transforming into Xie Liu’s own face.

Xie Liu’s face turned pale, and he was lost in panic.

The torture that the sinner was enduring seemed to "transfer" and inflict pain upon him.
