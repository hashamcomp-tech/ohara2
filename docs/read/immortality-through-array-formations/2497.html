Chapter 2497: Chapter 19: Karmic Gui Tao_2

Mr. Witch nodded and said, "I’m tired."

The Sect Leader of Canglang Sect sensibly got up, "Mr. Duan, I won’t disturb your rest anymore." With that, he quietly and softly left.

Mr. Witch closed his eyes to rest, paused for a moment, and when his Divine Sense slightly recovered, he took out several ancient yellowed scrolls from the wolf skin storage bag and began to read them attentively...

He needed to find a way to break through that fog.

...

Meanwhile, inside the guest room.

Mo Hua also had a strange expression.

"Was there just... someone calculating me?"

"Who?"

This was the first time he had ever had such an obvious feeling of being "divined for causality" by someone.

Before, he was sure that he had been calculated by others.

Mo Hua knows this well.

He is Mr. Zhuang’s direct disciple, with a special identity, and in the Qian Learning state boundary, having stirred such a high-profile commotion in so many events, there must be a group of people secretly deducing his causality.

But Mo Hua previously did not have very obvious feelings.

Firstly, because he was very well protected.

Elder Master Xun cherished him greatly, even gave him the Tai Xu Yin Yang Lock.

Mr. Zhuang’s life was like a dying candle, the fog he laid down had dissipated a lot, but still a part remained in the causality.

The fog in Mo Hua’s life chart was already like the thick fog in the Big Black Mountain deep mountains, forming an extremely strong heavenly secret barrier that hid all the terrifying ominous signs in the depths of the fog.

Ordinary Heavenly Mechanism cultivators couldn’t break this fog layer at all.

Secondly, within the Qian Learning state boundary, most cultivators deducing his causality were of late Golden Core Stage, Feather Transformation, and even Void Realm level.

It was a group of people all calculating him.

These people had very high cultivation and their methods were very subtle.

Therefore, when deduced by these people, Mo Hua couldn’t notice much.

But now it was different.

Leaving the Qian Learning state boundary, free from disturbances, Mo Hua’s causality became much quieter.

And ever since evil qi entered his body, Mo Hua’s life chart became increasingly sensitive, his perception of causality more intricate.

Just then, he clearly and evidently sensed that someone was calculating his causality.

This person’s realm was not much higher than his own.

Their causality skill was not bad, but the refinement was lacking.

Obviously another "rookie."

Moreover, this person was not too far from his position.

In a once quiet life chart, a person, a Heavenly Mechanism "rookie," close to him, deducing his causality... difficult to not notice.

"No wonder Elder Master Xun warned me not to casually calculate others’ causality..." Mo Hua muttered.

You spy on causality, causality spies on you.

If one’s mastery of Divine Thought and causality skill is inferior to others, recklessly calculating someone else, then you’re delivering yourself into their mouth, and it can only result in your own demise.

"Causality is indeed dangerous."

Mo Hua thought with a sigh. Little did he know, he himself was the most dangerous causality.

Mo Hua felt a bit confused.

He could now preliminarily sense being deduced using causality skill...

Then what? What should he do?

Mo Hua frowned and pondered deeply for a while, suddenly realizing he seemed... unable to do anything.

So far, his "defense" on causality seemed all "passive."

It was Elder Master Xun, it was his master, who arranged it for him.

As for within his life chart, there seemed to be even more dangerous and terrifying things lurking.

But these things weren’t within his control, not only not under control, possibly very big "hidden dangers."

These defenses from "hidden dangers" were also "passive."

This made Mo Hua feel very insecure.

One can’t just rely on others to safeguard their own life chart.

One should have the ability to independently protect life, change fate, even defy destiny.

"One’s destiny must be held in one’s own hands. One’s life chart must be controlled by oneself..."

But... how to control?

Not relying on the master, not relying on Elder Master Xun, not relying on those ’hidden dangers,’ only relying on personal capability, how can one counteract others’ causality peering?

Mo Hua was a bit troubled.

His divine sense is very strong, possessing two top-tier heavenly calculation skills, his causality foundation extremely potent.

But unfortunately, he lacks the most "basic" learning on heavenly mechanism causality skill.

His foundation is strong, but basic knowledge is "weak."

In the Qian Learning state boundary, Mo Hua once indirectly sought guidance from True Man Situ, wanting to learn some causality methods.

True Man Situ came from Mystery Valley.

And Mystery Valley stands by its "causality" inheritance.

But True Man Situ was hesitant, clearly not very agreeable.

Firstly, strict rules, these causality methods are Mystery Valley’s exclusive inheritance, not allowed for non-Mystery Valley disciples to learn.

Secondly, he really dared not teach Mo Hua.

Mo Hua’s causality is indeed too great.

Once he learns Mystery Valley’s causality skill, then this "causality" becomes too deeply bound.

Mo Hua’s life chart contains towering evil qi, in a very delicate balance, should the life chart "reverse" in the future, Mo Hua "falling" to become a Great Demon, then Mystery Valley would be "helping the tyrant," breeding a major disaster.

Thus, True Man Situ fundamentally dared not lightly teach Mo Hua anything.

Mo Hua couldn’t help it either.

True Man Situ’s concerns, he also understood.
