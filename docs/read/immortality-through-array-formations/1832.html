Chapter 1832: Chapter 909: Simple Thunder Magnetism Battle_2

But I am different.

I currently don’t know the Thunder Magnetism Formation.

The way I alter the Original Magnet is not through the Thunder Magnetism Formation, but by bypassing the formation and directly returning to the source of the more fundamental Secondary Thunder Pattern, reverting the Secondary Thunder Pattern to the form of a "Source Pattern" of the Thunder Magnetic Miniature.

Others use the Thunder Magnetism Formation, I use the Thunder Magnetic Miniature.

The Thunder Magnetism Formation is strong in its inherited standards, comprehensive system, and diverse functions, being a complete and mature formation system.

In comparison, the strongest aspect of my Thunder Magnetic Miniature lies in its use of "Back Origin Calculation," "Formation Flow," and "Source Pattern," a set of theories regarding the origin stream of formations that the Five Elements Sect does not pass on and even labels as "forbidden."

From the formation principle level, the Thunder Magnetic Miniature, or rather the "Secondary Thunder Source Pattern," is evidently more advanced.

But the problem is that my cultivation level is too low.

The Five Elements Sect exhausted the entire Sect’s power to return to the Five Elements Source Pattern.

However, I am alone, with limited Divine Sense and computational power, insufficient experience, short time, shallow research, and fewer returned formation patterns.

This "Thunder Magnetic Miniature" is currently only a prototype with relatively simple functions. Although the future is promising, at this stage, it cannot compete with the orthodoxly inherited Thunder Magnetic Array Masters.

In this confrontation of thunder and magnetism, it seems I stand no chance...

Moreover, since the opponent is proficient in the Thunder Magnetism Formation, even if it’s a Second Grade Thunder Magnetism Array, they would need at least Golden Core cultivation.

A Golden Core Cultivator, with at least twenty patterns of Divine Sense, possesses stronger Divine Sense than I do.

He knows Thunder Magnetism Formation, I do not.

I have the Thunder Magnetic Miniature but cannot attack or defend.

Furthermore, the confrontation between thunder and magnetism also involves the application of the Thunder Magnetism Array, requiring the accumulation of formation experience, which in itself is profound.

And these are my disadvantages.

Mo Hua sighed after much contemplation.

He had to admit, he indeed was not as skilled as others.

In this thunder and magnetism war, normally, he would not be able to win at all...

Mo Hua felt a bit distressed.

The breakthrough to the Foundation Establishment Late Stage was just two Divine Remains away, and even the day to complete the Godslaying Sword was not far...

At this moment, this Thunder Magnetic Array Master just happened to be in his way, leaving him helpless.

"I need to think of a way..."

But no matter how he thought about it, with his current thunder magnetism level, he was still no match for this Thunder Magnetism Array Master.

Mo Hua racked his brains for a long time and finally came up with a method:

Take out this Thunder Magnetic Array Master!

If he couldn’t win over him in the Thunder Magnetism Formation, then he’d kill him in reality.

If a problem can’t be solved, then solve the person causing the problem!

Find a way, use thunder magnetism tracing and positioning, find the position of this Thunder Magnetic Array Master, then call Uncle Gu and Sister Xia, and take him down together.

In high-level thunder magnetism battles, often the most straightforward means suffice.

Two tigers cannot share one mountain.

A single Original Magnet Compound Formation cannot accommodate two Thunder Magnetic Array Masters.

With him around, I can’t play at all.

This Thunder Magnetic Array Master must die.

Moreover, he works for the Demon Sect, deserving ten thousand deaths.

The problem, however, is that Thunder Magnetism tracing and Original Magnet positioning are also quite high-level formation knowledge...

So, Mo Hua specially consulted Elder Zheng.

Elder Zheng answered all his questions.

Mo Hua also took the opportunity to subtly verify his guess about the Thunder Magnetic Array Master, and upon receiving a positive response, he returned satisfied.

After returning to the Sect, Mo Hua meticulously considered his plan from start to finish:

Firstly, was the problem of Original Magnet positioning.

The Demon Sect Token carried by the Demon Cultivators often changes its location, making it possible but difficult to locate.

Moreover, locating the Demon Sect Token requires operating from the central formation pivot of the Original Magnet Compound Formation.

The Original Magnet Compound Formation pertains to the core of the entire Demon Sect, very hidden, and its position will not easily be revealed.

Mo Hua currently does not have the capability to steal the core authority and locate the central formation pivot of the Original Magnet.

Therefore, the Demon Sect Token and the lower-level Original Magnet Compound Formation cannot be located for now.

But the position of the opposing Thunder Magnetic Array Master can be traced.

Mo Hua asked Elder Zheng and learned that the construction of a Thunder Magnetism Formation has a set of complex procedures.

The whole set of formation media, formation patterns, formation pivot, formation eye... The cost is high, and the structure is precise, integrated with each other. Once set up, unless it’s to completely transfer, it won’t be relocated easily.

This means that the Thunder Magnetic Array Master, along with all his "thunder magnetism" family assets, is currently situated in the Qian Learning State Boundary or a small nearby domain, in some hidden place.

According to another point said by Elder Zheng: Thunder Magnetic Array Masters are not to be disclosed.

Therefore, Mo Hua could also deduce that this Thunder Magnetic Array Master is single-handedly, isolated from others.

Even the Righteous Sect harbors a certain dread towards Thunder Magnetic Array Masters, not to mention the naturally suspicious and murderous Demon Sect.

In the Demon Sect, this Thunder Magnetic Array Master would certainly not easily expose himself.

He most likely won’t have too much contact with Demon Cultivators, and they may not have even seen each other much, keeping only necessary contact methods.

Mo Hua pondered for a moment, nodding his head, believing that his guess should be correct.

Then he began, according to Elder Zheng’s method, to use Thunder Flow tracing to perform "Original Magnet" positioning.

He first took out the map of the Qianxue State Boundary and its surroundings, then marked his position on the map.

After that, he began to orientate.

He infiltrated the Thunder Magnetic Miniature into the Demon Sect Token, perceiving the direction of the peculiar Thunder Magnetism flow.

Thunder Magnetism is also a kind of worldly energy. Since it circulates in the world, scattered among all things, it cannot be without direction.
