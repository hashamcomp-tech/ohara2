Chapter 1632: Chapter 850: Stolen Voices (Extra Chapter for Boss Catnip of the Night)

Ouyang Feng and Hua Qianqian sat in meditation under the protection of two people, one healing and the other suppressing the poison.

Mo Hua also sat for a while and felt that the ground was a bit cold.

This Dragon King Temple is in the middle of the Mistwater River, soaked in water qi, and chillingly cold.

Earlier during the fight and traveling it was fine, but now that everything had quieted down, sitting on the ground, he felt a bit cold.

Mo Hua took out a warm blanket and wrapped himself up, turned to look at Gu Changhuai and the others, thought for a moment, and then took out a few blankets, giving one to each person.

He often went out and camped frequently, so furry blankets were a must-have in his Storage Bag.

This habit developed back when he traveled with his master, junior brother, and junior sister.

Everyone was a bit surprised, but they didn’t refuse, thanking Mo Hua as they received the blankets. After draping them over themselves, they indeed felt warm and couldn’t help but appreciate Mo Hua’s thoughtfulness and care.

Afterward, everyone went silent and meditated to rest.

The surroundings became tranquil.

Mo Hua, wrapped in his blanket, took the opportunity to quietly ponder the matters of the Dragon King Temple.

Some things others might not care about, but he couldn’t let them go unnoticed.

After all, the great dangers within the Dao of Divine Thought, the great crises amongst the Evil Gods and Demon Monsters, none had been witnessed or personally experienced by others, so naturally, they wouldn’t understand.

As for the matters within the Dragon King Temple, at first glance, there might not be much, but upon deeper reflection, there were too many incongruities...

"Is the altar really behind this big door?"

"Could there be other dangers inside?"

"What exactly happened to Mr. Witch, is he hiding other malicious plans?"

"Where is the Dragon Blood?"

"And moreover, what is the reason for all these ruinous remnants... what was being rebuilt? What are they rebuilding?"

"..."

Mo Hua recalled every bit along the way, pondering the answers in his mind and then verifying back and forth.

Lost in thought, Mo Hua suddenly froze, sensing a trace of anomaly.

This... the aura of a Formation?

Someone was secretly setting up a Formation?

Who could it be?

Daring to set up a Formation right under his nose?

Mo Hua quietly turned his head, using the dim light within the Dragon King Temple, quietly looked over, and then discovered that the one setting up the Formation...

was actually Master Xiao!

Mo Hua felt a slight shock in his heart, made a slight calculation, and then gently exhaled.

"It’s a Sound Isolation Formation, not any dangerous Killing Formation, Trapping Formation, or Evil Formation..."

Probably wanting to have a secret conversation, fearing others might eavesdrop.

Mo Hua felt at ease.

He resumed his previous train of thought, continuing to ponder, yet as he thought, Mo Hua paused again.

"A secret conversation?"

Mo Hua suddenly became very curious, his eyes slightly brightening.

Someone set up a Sound Isolation Formation right in front of him, to have an unspeakable secret conversation, how could he resist...

Mo Hua pondered a bit, then secretly took out two blankets, wrapping his head as well, pretending to rest, while quietly slipping out from the blankets, then used the Concealment Technique to sneak away.

Cannonical Official Xia, watching Mo Hua sneak away so openly, couldn’t help but open his mouth.

Gu Changhuai, however, was not surprised, even helping to tuck Mo Hua’s blanket tighter, making Mo Hua look more like a "blanket person."

Mo Hua, hidden, quietly approached Master Xiao, stopping a few feet away.

After all, Master Xiao was a Golden Core, so he dared not get too close.

Mo Hua looked around, found a corner, squatted down, raised his head, and peered stealthily, discovering that Master Xiao was within the range of the Sound Isolation Formation, talking to Water Yama about something.

Their expressions were grave, voices low, clearly discussing something confidential.

"Confidential matter..."

Mo Hua felt an itch in his heart, thought for a moment, and released his Divine Thought very "gently," perceiving the Sound Isolation Formation on the ground.

This Sound Isolation Formation was of the Second Grade.

In the Second Grade Territory, only Second Grade Formations could be used.

Since it was a Second Grade Formation, that made it simple.

Mo Hua performed a slight inference in his mind, calculating the intervals of Spiritual Power flow within the Sound Isolation Formation, which was the internal "flaw" of the Formation.

Then he took out a silver needle inscribed with the "Stealing Sound Array" using fine engraving techniques, flicked it lightly with a finger, and sent the silver needle into the flaw of the Sound Isolation Formation.

The silver needle was very light, and its sound was extremely faint.

And the Sound Isolation Formation insulated sound both inside and out, so Master Xiao remained unaware.

Of course, mainly because he didn’t think anyone could use such a method to eavesdrop on his conversation.

With the silver needle breaking into the Formation, Mo Hua took out the corresponding thread, wrapped it around his ear, and within moments, he could indeed hear sound.

Initially, the sound was somewhat intermittent, then gradually became clear:

"Inadequate, more of a hindrance..."

"...I had already saved you once before, didn’t expect you to still be so incompetent..."

"...if it weren’t for needing to open that thing, I wouldn’t care about your life or death."

Mo Hua was somewhat bewildered upon hearing this.

Open... that thing?

What thing?

Mo Hua thought carefully for a moment, shocked in his heart, "Could it be... to open the Water Prison Forbidden Box?!"

This smiling tiger intends to use Water Yama to open the Water Prison Forbidden Box?

Mo Hua’s heart was heavy for a moment, but after a while, he calmed himself and continued eavesdropping.

"Big Brother, it’s not that I’m incompetent, it’s just that the unexpected changes are too surprising, as if every move has been calculated by someone..."

Water Yama said in a low voice.

Master Xiao’s expression was indifferent, "I won’t listen to excuses."

Water Yama reluctantly closed his mouth, not daring to say more.

Master Xiao turned his head, glanced at Water Yama, "Where is that thing?"

Water Yama’s face instantly turned ashen.

Seeing Water Yama’s expression, Master Xiao slightly frowned, contemplated for a moment, his pupils contracted, eyes widening suddenly, and he said with a sinister tone:

"Don’t you tell me... you lost it..."

"Not lost," Water Yama said tremblingly, "just... it’s not with me right now."

Master Xiao’s eyes flashed with killing intent, anger turning to laughter:

"Well, well, your abilities are indeed great, losing even that thing. Didn’t I warn you, your life can be lost, but that box must not suffer the slightest mishap?"

Water Yama gritted his teeth, "Forgive me, Big Brother, it was my negligence, I fell for a villain’s plot, and the box was snatched away."

"I am doing my utmost to retrieve that box again and will not let Big Brother be disappointed."

Master Xiao frowned, "Who has the box now?"

Water Yama said, "In... in the hands of that brat named Mo Hua."

Mo Hua, listening in stealthily, couldn’t help but shrink back his head.

Master Xiao, instead, looked dumbfounded as if he hadn’t heard clearly, "Who?"

"Mo Hua..." Water Yama said softly.

"Which Mo Hua?"

"The one... who just talked with you, Big Brother... that Mo Hua."

Master Xiao opened his mouth, for a moment, his expression was quite wonderful.

The person who robbed the Water Prison Forbidden Box was just the kid he initially wanted to capture as a hostage but failed, then traveled together for a while, even planning with him how to capture Mr. Witch...

This kid robbed his treasure and then still casually wandered around in front of him for a large half of the day.

And he knew nothing!

At this moment, Master Xiao truly had the urge to kill Water Yama.
