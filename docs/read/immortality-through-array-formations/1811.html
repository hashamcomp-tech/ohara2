Chapter 1811: Chapter 903: Thunder Magnetic Miniature

What... on earth is this?

Mo Hua and the little Thunder Man stared at each other with wide eyes for quite a while.

Mo Hua tentatively said, "Hey."

The Thunder Magnetic Miniature didn’t react, just tilted its head, looking at Mo Hua in confusion.

"Is this little thing alive?"

"Is it living?"

Mo Hua frowned, released his Divine Sense, and carefully sensed for a moment, only then did he come to realize.

No life...

This little thing just looks lively, but in reality, it is still unconscious, unable to act on its own, nor does it make any reactions.

"So does it come under my control?"

Mo Hua moved his thoughts and released his Divine Sense, beginning to control the Thunder Magnetic Miniature.

Soon, a faint connection seemed to extend from the Thunder Magnetic Miniature, merging into Mo Hua’s Divine Thought, as if there was a bond between them of blood and water.

This little figure was personally calculated by Mo Hua using the Back Origin Calculation.

In other words, Mo Hua is its creator.

Their origins are similar.

With a movement of Mo Hua’s Divine Sense, he naturally communicated with this Thunder Magnetic Miniature.

The Thunder Magnetic Miniature slowly stood up, driven by Mo Hua, moved its ’matchstick man’ like body a few steps to the left, then a few steps to the right, and after a little hop, it even posed with hands on hips, looking up at Mo Hua with quite a spirited demeanor.

"It can be controlled by me..."

Mo Hua marveled in amazement.

And then?

This Thunder Magnetic Miniature, created from Secondary Thunder Patterns Back Origin, what specific uses does it have?

Mo Hua, resting his chin, began to make preliminary speculations based on the nature of the Secondary Thunder Patterns and his own understanding of Thunder Magnetic Sensing:

It is formed by the extremely weak Back Origin of Secondary Thunder Patterns, without flesh and blood, formless and intangible, ordinary cultivators cannot see it, and with Thunder Magnetism being obscure, even a formation master with a strong Divine Thought cannot perceive this miniature within their Divine Sense.

Yet it itself is both a Thunder Flow and could be said to be an Original Magnetic Field, a type of Thunder Magnetic energy entity.

Logically, it should be able to infiltrate other Thunder Flows and Magnetic Fields.

In other words, it could infiltrate other Yuan Magnetic Formations, Thunder Magnetic Arrays, and all formation media constructed with the principles of Yuan Magnetic and Thunder Magnetic Arrays.

This Thunder Magnetic Miniature, being a ’collection’ of Secondary Thunder Patterns, adhering to the principle of "using thunder to change magnetism", can generate Thunder Flow to affect Magnetic Patterns.

This also means, if done correctly, this Thunder Magnetic Miniature could alter the fixed Magnetic Patterns of underlying Yuan Magnetic formations...

Altering fixed Magnetic Patterns!

Mo Hua’s eyes shone brightly.

No time to lose, he immediately took out a ’discarded’ Letter Token to begin a preliminary attempt.

First, he disassembled the discarded Letter Token, then extracted a magnetic wire, connecting one end to the Letter Token internally, and the other to the Jade Slip where the Thunder Magnetic Miniature is.

This magnetic wire, made of magnetic material, is a common formation media in Yuan Magnetic formations, not cheap, and hard to buy.

The channels to purchase magnetic wire are quite limited.

Of course, Mo Hua came from a poor background, accustomed to ’thriftiness and household management’.

This magnetic wire was not bought by him at all; he took it apart from other Letter Tokens, counts as ’waste utilization’.

With the magnetic wire connected, Mo Hua used his Divine Thought to control the Thunder Magnetic Miniature.

Within the Jade Slip, the Thunder Magnetic Miniature, existing in the form of a Thunder Flow resulting from the secondary Thunder Pattern Back Origin, began to move under Mo Hua’s Divine Thought guidance, walking out from the Jade Slip, along the magnetic wire, and into the Letter Token.

Upon entering the Letter Token, surrounded by dense magnetic patterns, the little Thunder Man seemed like a fish entering the sea, at its own home, with its aura becoming much more active.

Mo Hua began to manipulate the Thunder Magnetic Miniature to ’infiltrate’ the Letter Token.

In the Divine Sense perspective, microscopically.

The pale blue, spark-flashing, matchstick man-like Thunder Magnetic Miniature directly merged into the Unfixed Magnetic Patterns.

Then just like spring breeze turning into rain, nourishing things quietly, the Thunder Magnetic Miniature penetrated into the Magnetic Patterns with almost no effort.

Then through the Unfixed Magnetic Patterns, traversing the interspace of Magnetic Patterns, without any knowledge or trace, it infiltrated the at the very bottom, the Fixed Magnetic Patterns.

The whole process was natural, like an antelope hanging its horns, leaving no trace, no evidence behind.

If Mo Hua wasn’t controlling it himself, he almost wouldn’t be aware, hidden amongst the densely packed Magnetic Patterns, lies such a Thunder Magnetic Miniature, almost invisible and imperceptible to a cultivator’s Divine Thought.

Mo Hua was initially shocked, then overjoyed.

He then began the formal attempt to use the ’Thunder Magnetic Miniature’ to incite Secondary Thunder Flow and secretly modify the Fixed Magnetic Patterns.

However, this process was not so smooth.

In Mo Hua’s Divine Thought perspective, he could see the Thunder Magnetic Miniature crawling between the Magnetic Patterns, using its little arms and legs dabbing with Magnetic Ink, smearing and modifying things.

But the miniature’s movements were not very coordinated.

Whatever it smeared didn’t quite go as controlled sometimes.

Mo Hua speculated this was because this ’Thunder Magnetic Miniature’ was still just a ’prototype’ of Back Origin, and not enough ’Secondary Thunder Patterns’ were integrated inside.

It is the source flow of Secondary Thunder Patterns, needing to manifest specific Secondary Thunder Patterns to induce corresponding Magnetic Pattern changes.

Each Secondary Thunder Pattern has a unique form, equivalent to a specific change in original magnetism.

Lacking enough Secondary Thunder Patterns, the ’Thunder Magnetic Miniature’ can only cause limited original magnetic changes, naturally unable to modify the Magnetic Patterns at will.

"In the future, I need to back-origin more, integrating as many Secondary Thunder Patterns as possible..." Mo Hua silently thought.

However, the current Letter Token does not involve profound Yuan Magnetic formations.

With the number of Secondary Thunder Patterns back-origin in the Thunder Magnetic Miniature right now, it is barely sufficient.
