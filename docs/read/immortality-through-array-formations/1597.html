Chapter 1597: Chapter 839: Shameless Couple (Part 4)

Mo Hua pondered for a moment, realizing in his heart.

The defense outside the Rouge Boat must be stronger than the inside.

Therefore, someone had laid down a dual Divine Way Array outside this large Rouge Boat, along with sealed demon monsters to prevent others from eavesdropping.

Outside the large Rouge Boat, even with ordinary formations no matter how complex, there would be people who could decipher them.

But the Divine Way Array is different, this formation is nearly lost to history, almost no one knows it, and raising some demon monsters practically guarantees success.

Mo Hua couldn’t help but nod in agreement, "Indeed a good idea."

After that, he unlocked this Divine Way Array, and at the same time, some evil thoughts from the sealed demon monsters in the formation drilled into his Sea of Consciousness, only to be pinched to death in an instant.

For other formations, he would have to exert some effort.

But the Divine Way Array was much simpler.

After breaking the formation, Mo Hua took out a small dagger, quietly dug a small hole in the window, and then peered through it.

Now he could see the scene inside the room.

Pink bedding, a colorful luan screen, a rouge makeup case set in front of the bed, finely crafted jade items placed everywhere, clearly a lady’s boudoir.

At this moment Hua Ruyu was sitting at the table with the female disciple in pink, lips slightly parted, speaking unknown words.

"No sound?"

Mo Hua was startled.

It seemed like there was also a Sound Isolation Formation set up in the room.

Mo Hua thought for a moment, took out a silver needle, wrapped in iron wire, and pierced it into the crucial point of the Sound Isolation Formation through the window gap.

On the silver needle, he carved the sound listening formation with a micro-engraving pen technique.

The end of the silver needle’s thread wrapped around his ear, allowing him to hear the sounds inside the room.

...

"Don’t think so much..."

"What’s wrong with that?"

"Appearance and figure are gifts bestowed by heaven on us women, naturally ’make use of them’..."

"But..."

"There’s no ’but’..."

"Think about it, your background isn’t great, if you marry normally, you’d only be matched with those ordinary men. With your looks, how can those ordinary men be worthy?"

"It’s different on this Rouge Boat, the traveling cultivators are all talented and noble, if not prestigious, then high-status. Even if you can’t marry them, having a few dewdrop encounters wouldn’t be a loss."

"Seize the opportunity, don’t waste your looks..."

"Put aside those meaningless feelings of pride and shame, and you’ll experience the taste of it..."

"Yes, Instructor..."

"Don’t call me Instructor here."

"Yes, elder sister."

"Alright, you go ahead, Young Master Zhao is still waiting for you."

"Hmm."

The pink-clad woman nodded, then whether resigning to her fate or having an epiphany, her expression relaxed somewhat and she gracefully left.

Mo Hua frowned.

What an Instructor Hua, truly knows how to "teach" people.

Being shameless himself, yet using these words to lead others astray.

After the pink-clad woman left, only Hua Ruyu remained in the room. She began to disrobe, preparing to bathe in the room.

Mo Hua felt there was nothing worth watching, so he decided not to look anymore.

Just at this moment, there was a knock on the door again.

Hua Ruyu’s gaze sank slightly, she donned her robe, walked to the door to look out, then with a full smile, welcomed the person outside in.

The newcomer was a man, wearing a mask, with one sleeve empty, seemingly missing an arm.

"Missing an arm..."

Mo Hua’s pupils shrank.

No need for him to think much, as soon as the man entered the room, he took off the mask, revealing a fair handsome face, albeit with a slightly sinister gaze.

Hua Ruyu stepped forward and embraced the man, the two gazed at each other, their brows filled with passion and affection.

"Master Xie, why did you come only now?"

"Did you miss me...?"

"Hmm, missed you dearly."

"My dear Miaoer..."

In their words, all was tenderness.

Mo Hua felt a twinge in his teeth, and somewhat nauseated.

After a moment, he couldn’t help but draw a sharp breath.

It’s really Water Yama!

Moreover, this Water Yama actually has a relationship with Hua Ruyu!

These two despicable people!
