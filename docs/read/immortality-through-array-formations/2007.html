Chapter 2007: Chapter 965: In Hand (Part 2)

Seeing that he was about to succeed, Shen Shouxing, who was fighting with Xun Ziyou, sent out a sword light, slashing through the air towards Shen Tuao.

He wanted to kill Xun Ziyou, but he couldn’t let the Dragon Vein fall into Shen Tuao’s hands.

The Sword Qi of Shen Shouxing at the Golden Core Peak was naturally incomparable to the sword of Young Master Xuan at the Initial Golden Core, especially since he aimed at the vital spot.

Shen Tuao could only retreat to avoid it.

Shen Shouxing’s distraction in swinging the sword also gave Xun Ziyou, who was struggling to hold on, a chance to catch his breath.

Seizing the opportunity, he directly broke free from Shen Shouxing and charged towards the Dragon Coffin.

He wanted to snatch the Dragon Vein.

As a result, if Shen Shouxing wanted to kill him, he had to fight in front of the Dragon Coffin.

Meanwhile, Young Master Xuan, Shen Tuao, and the Bear Demon Cultivator, all three wanted to seize the Dragon Vein, so they had to fight back and forth in front of the Dragon Coffin.

The battlefield, led by Xun Ziyou, was once again mixed together.

And everyone in the battle, more or less, had some unresolved "grievances" against each other, each wishing to put the other to death.

Therefore, as long as they mixed in battle, they would inevitably plot against each other, kill each other, and guard against each other.

For a moment, Shen Shouxing was also distracted, relieving the pressure on Xun Ziyou.

After a brief exchange, the Blood Qi in the air suddenly began to intensify.

Everyone was busy with the fight, with no time to notice, and even inhaled the blood mist without realizing it.

Blood Qi stirred desires.

Everyone’s murderous intent and greed grew increasingly intense, falling into an endless battle, while their Blood Qi rapidly depleted...

At this moment, Elder Bear, seizing the opportunity during the fight, approached the Dragon Vein by chance.

His gaze was greedy, reaching out with a large hand like a Bear Demon towards the Dragon Vein.

But in the next instant, a sword light from Xun Ziyou cut down, severing his hand.

The hand fell to the ground with a sound, blood spilled everywhere, forming a hidden blood mist as it splashed on the ground. Elder Bear clutched his arm, howling in pain.

Xun Ziyou originally just wanted to hold back Shen Shouxing, but after fighting for a while and absorbing the blood mist, his mind was unknowingly affected, and he couldn’t help but reach out to touch the Dragon Vein.

Fortunately, he was quick to alert and immediately reacted:

"What am I doing?"

Xun Ziyou quickly withdrew, but it was still a little late; a dragon-shadow-bearing, strength-surging fist directly struck his back.

Xun Ziyou only had time to turn his body, deflecting part of the force, but was still hit by the punch, his internal organs vibrating, his body like a kite being blown away, landing in the corner of the grand hall.

He spat out a mouthful of blood and half-knelt on the ground.

"A miscalculation..."

The remaining Evil Dragon’s strength tangled in his body, and Xun Ziyou couldn’t move for a while, having to adjust his breathing to restore his strength as soon as possible, otherwise the situation would deteriorate to an irreparable state.

Not to mention Mo Hua, he himself might explain it here.

After punching Xun Ziyou away, Shen Tuao also felt his mind muddled, and he reached out towards the Dragon Vein indiscriminately.

But in his heart, the warning signs grew stronger, though greed compelled him for a moment, he couldn’t suppress it.

Sure enough, the next moment, Shen Shouxing struck with a sword, opening a bloody gash on his back.

Shen Tuao countered with a palm strike, which Shen Shouxing dodged, and Shen Shouxing counterattacked with a sword, using the Shen Family White Jade Sword Skill, a pure white Sword Qi inadvertently pierced through his chest.

Shen Tuao gritted his teeth and slowly fell to the ground.

Until now, only Shen Shouxing was left around the Dragon Coffin.

Inside the Dragon Coffin, the Dragon Vein between Prince Ye’s hands emitted a captivating glow.

Shen Shouxing’s heart followed its beat, uncontrollably walking towards the Dragon Coffin.

This time, no one could stop him; Shen Shouxing successfully extracted the Dragon Vein of the Great Wilderness from the deceased Prince Ye’s hands.

Once holding the Dragon Vein, the aura of the Great Wilderness came rushing.

Strands of stately Dragon Qi followed his palm, entering Shen Shouxing’s blood, flowing along his meridians throughout his body, causing him the agony of tearing pain.

This pain was extremely sharp, accompanied by the deafening Dragon Roar.

It seemed his bloodline was unworthy of possessing the Dragon Vein.

The repulsion force grew stronger.

The Dragon Roar became more rampant, making his scalp tingle.

The hand holding the Dragon Vein also began to be eroded by the Dragon Qi of the Great Wilderness, the flesh gradually rotting, extending bit by bit to the entire arm.

The pain brought Shen Shouxing back to his senses.

Seeing the entire arm about to be crippled, Shen Shouxing’s pupils trembled violently, and he could only release his grip immediately.

Dragon Qi ceased its erosion, but his right arm was ruined more than half.

The Dragon Vein was also thrown into the air, cutting a clear light, and fell to the ground beside, an empty spot.

The grand hall was momentarily deathly silent.

The strongest four Golden Cores were either crippled, severely wounded, or on the verge of death.

This moment, they all sensed something amiss, and then turned to look at Young Master Xuan.

Young Master Xuan’s originally solemn face showed a hint of a cold smile.

"The Dragon Vein of the Great Wilderness isn’t something ordinary people can touch. Without the bloodline of the Great Wilderness, trying to meddle with the Dragon Vein will trigger Dragon Rage, suffering from the backlash of Dragon Qi."

Everyone’s hearts tightened, slowly understanding.

Why did Young Master Xuan use Mo Hua and Shen Qingsheng as leverage, forcing everyone to help him snatch this Dragon Vein?

Not everyone had the qualifications to wield the Dragon Vein.

Except for Shen Tuao, who had royal blood, whoever else among them seized the Dragon Vein would suffer the agony of being devoured by Dragon Qi, bearing the wrath of the Karma Dragon of the Great Wilderness.

Even Shen Shouxing at the Golden Core Peak was forcefully deprived of an arm.

And this secret of the Great Wilderness wouldn’t be spoken by Shen Tuao.

Young Master Xuan had his calculations, and was even less likely to speak.
