Chapter 1618: Chapter 846: Monster (Part 2)

The two of them sat in meditation to heal.

Mo Hua quietly began to gather a Fireball Technique at his fingertips.

But just as he made a move, Xie Liu suddenly opened his eyes and glared fiercely at Mo Hua: "Kid, what are you doing?"

Mo Hua extinguished the Fireball Technique, looking innocent, "I wasn’t doing anything."

Xie Liu sneered, "I’m warning you, don’t try any tricks."

"Oh."

Mo Hua looked indifferent.

Xie Liu gritted his teeth in anger, but healing was urgent, and he couldn’t let this kid disturb his peace of mind.

Xie Liu suppressed his anger and continued to heal.

Mo Hua tested for a moment and realized that although Xie Liu and the others were healing, they were also watching him, hence he restrained himself a bit and indeed stopped making any more moves.

But after a while, Shui Yan suddenly spat out a mouthful of blood, and when he opened his eyes again, they were completely red.

His face was enveloped by deep Evil Qi, and a violent desire surged in his eyes, then he turned his head, staring intently at Hua Qianqian.

Desire burned within him, causing his entire body to tremble.

"Not good!"

Mo Hua’s expression changed, and he quickly pulled Hua Qianqian back a few steps.

The next moment, Shui Yan lunged forward.

Ouyang Feng swung his longsword horizontally, blocking for a moment, but failed to hold him back.

Mo Hua threw a few fireballs, momentarily hindering Shui Yan, but it had little effect. Shui Yan, like an Evil Ghost, ignored everything and continued lunging at Hua Qianqian.

Mo Hua said coldly, "Wretch, don’t you want that box anymore?"

Shui Yan’s eyes remained blood-red, ignoring his words.

Mo Hua’s heart sank, secretly thinking it wasn’t good.

The Water Prison Forbidden Box was important when Shui Yan was rational.

Now this Shui Yan, after many hard battles, had lost his Vital Energy and the bloody monster had soiled his Blood Qi, worsening his injuries, and the Evil Qi gradually got out of control.

Previously, he had inhaled so much dense fog that desire was deeply rooted. Now, it seemed his state of mind was unbalanced, entirely controlled by desire.

"Forget it..."

Seeing Shui Yan charging again, Mo Hua silently thought to himself.

This waste, seems to be useless...

Golden light flickered in his eyes as he began to mobilize his Divine Soul, trying to see if he could use the Soul-Shocking Sword to shatter Shui Yan’s Divine Soul directly.

Just then, a figure stood in front of him, blocking Shui Yan and his line of sight, hindering his Soul-Shocking Sword.

It was Xie Liu.

He protected Mo Hua, swung back with a sword, forcing Shui Yan to retreat, then struck again, slicing Shui Yan’s shoulder.

Shui Yan felt the pain, his Divine Sense clearing up a lot, and he snapped back to his senses, cursing:

"Xie Liu, what the hell are you doing?"

As a Golden Core, being cursed like this made Xie Liu’s face slightly angry, then he sneered, "What are you doing?"

Shui Yan bitterly said: "I can’t hold back the fire, need to vent a bit."

Xie Liu retorted, "No way!"

Shui Yan was furious, speechless, and shouted angrily:

"What’s wrong with you? They are not only unrelated to you, but also your enemies. Why stop me from venting my anger on them?"

Xie Liu pointed his longsword at Shui Yan, sneering, "I’ve been wanting to ask... that box the kid mentioned, what is it?"

Shui Yan hesitated, and his confidence weakened a bit, only saying, "It’s none of your concern."

Xie Liu chuckled disdainfully, "You think I’m an idiot?"

Shui Yan said, "Even if I give that thing to you, you wouldn’t be able to use it."

Xie Liu said, "You don’t give it to me, how do you know I can’t use it?"

Shui Yan gritted his teeth and said helplessly, "That thing is really not something you can learn."

Xie Liu’s face turned cold, "You can learn it, and I can’t? Don’t forget, you’re just Foundation Establishment, and I’m a Golden Core. Tell me... what exactly is that box?"

Shui Yan’s expression turned cold and he said nothing.

Seeing that Xie Liu couldn’t get an answer, Mo Hua "kindheartedly" told him: "It’s the Water Prison Forbidden Box."

Xie Liu was taken aback, "Water Prison Forbidden Box? What is that?"

Mo Hua said: "You know the Water Prison Sect, right..."

When Shui Yan heard this, he immediately shouted angrily: "Kid, shut up!"

Xie Liu slashed a sword light toward Shui Yan, forcing him back a few steps, then sneered and turned to look at Mo Hua, "Continue."

Mo Hua continued: "The Water Prison Sect was the predecessor of the Gui Water Sect. The Water Prison Forbidden Box was the treasure of the top Twelve Streams Sect of its time, containing the Ultimate Technique that only the Water Prison Sect masters could cultivate!"

Xie Liu’s eyes instantly turned red, his breathing grew heavy, his expression filled with disbelief.

Sect Protecting Treasure, Sect Leader’s Ultimate Technique!

He used to be a Sect Disciple, later an Inner Gate Instructor, a step away from becoming an Inner Sect Elder.

Thus, he clearly understood the difficulty of advancement within the Sect and the strict barriers to inheritance.

Sect Protecting Treasure, Sect Leader’s Ultimate Technique, even if he became an Elder, was something he could never touch in his lifetime.

He was not even worthy to catch a glimpse.

But now, such a thing was almost within reach!

Xie Liu trembled, "Where is it?"

Mo Hua glanced at Shui Yan, then at Xie Liu, pondered for a moment and sighed:

"Originally, if Shui Yan hadn’t made a move, I would have given it to him afterward, but he broke his promise, leaving me with no choice. Here’s the deal, as long as you ensure the safety of me and my three senior brothers and sisters, once we leave the Dragon King Temple and reach the shore, I’ll tell you where the Forbidden Box is."

Xie Liu’s gaze was dark, as if lost in thought.

Mo Hua then said: "No matter what ideas you have, they won’t work. If you kill me, I won’t tell, if you threaten me, I still won’t tell. This Forbidden Box is our only bargaining chip for survival; if we give it to you now, the three of us will surely die. So, until we escape safely, I cannot tell you where it is..."

"The safety of the three of us and the Water Prison Sect’s master treasure, you can only choose one."
