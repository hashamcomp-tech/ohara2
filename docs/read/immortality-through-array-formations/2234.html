Chapter 2234: Chapter 1038: Ink Manipulation

Five Brothers of Taia Mountain.

Ouyang Fu, Lu, Shou, Xi, Cai.

These five brothers were originally unknown in Taia Mountain, but under Mo Hua’s formation, spiritual artifacts, and tactical guidance, they formed the "Cleaver Squad". With relentless determination, they gradually began to stand out in the Sword Discussion Conference.

Mo Hua inherited the legacy of the Three Mountains and further perfected their cultivation techniques and swordsmanship. At the same time, he equipped them with good armor and giant swords.

The five brothers were like tigers with wings added.

And they were deeply grateful to Mo Hua.

They remembered the kindness of Junior Brother Mo Hua.

If it weren’t for Junior Brother providing them the opportunity, equipping them with complete spiritual artifacts, formations, and strategies, instilling in them a steadfast resolve, they could never have showcased their skills at the Sword Discussion Conference and gained recognition from the Sect.

And now, Junior Brother was sitting behind them.

The talents of the Four Sects and Seven Gates in front of them wanted to break through the defense line and kill their Junior Brother.

This was absolutely unacceptable.

The five brothers of Taia were enraged, their eyes deep red, and their murderous intent even stronger.

"Anyone who wants to kill Junior Brother must die!"

At this moment, the five were of one mind.

If this were a normal time, dealing with these giants, they could still play a game of "kite".

But this was a siege battle, and the path was narrow. To break through the defense line, one must pass under the giant swords of these five brothers.

The five giant swords were like the guillotine of purgatory, powerful and sharp, ruthlessly harvesting lives.

The spectators outside the field were amazed.

The disciples of the Four Sects and Seven Gates also frowned one after another.

It was only then they remembered, Taixu Gate did not only have Mo Hua, but the other disciples were not to be underestimated.

It was manageable to deal with them individually, but now they gathered around Mo Hua, receiving his overall coordination.

Dealing with them became even more challenging.

"Keep charging!" someone gritted their teeth and said.

"Body cultivators hold the line, and the others rush in!"

A few disciples of the Vajra Sect took a step forward, their muscles like goldstone, joined by other body cultivators, they lifted long shields to withstand the cleaver-like giant swords of the five brothers of Taia.

The giant swords hacked into the vajra bodies of the Vajra Sect disciples, creating deep notches.

Striking the long shields, they also left cracks.

The power of the five giant swords of Taia was unsettling.

But the might of the giant swords was ultimately blocked for a moment.

The other few took advantage of this gap and rushed into the valley.

But as soon as they entered the valley, they were greeted by a brilliant blue sword qi, as beautiful and sharp as the moon.

This sword qi was extremely powerful, instantly slaughtering all the disciples entering the valley, including those Vajra Sect disciples, leaving them with no chance.

The sword qi broke through their vajra bodies.

The five brothers of Taia swung their giant swords, chopping wildly, sending them away.

The area at the mouth of the valley was cleared.

Three more disciples of the Sever Gold Sect came up to fill the gap.

And then they were wiped out by the five brothers of Taia, along with Linghu Xiao’s sword qi.

The remaining disciples, fearing the killing power of Taixu Gate, hesitated and dared not move forward, so they temporarily retreated.

And as they retreated.

Hao Xuan and others, following Mo Hua’s orders, immediately began to set up the formation again at the mouth of the valley.

The broken formation was restored.

The hated formation light once again lit up at the mouth of the valley.

"Damn it."

"All our efforts were in vain..."

The disciples of the Four Sects and Seven Gates cursed inwardly.

But in any case, Taixu Gate barely held the first wave of offense.

Outside the valley, Qin Cangliu’s eyes flashed as he asked everyone:

"What should we do now?"

In fact, he had a plan in mind, but he didn’t speak it out loud.

There were too many talents present, and although his background was not ordinary, with ties to Taoist State, his ancestors having held office in the Taoist Court for generations and being favored by the ancestors.

But the people in front of him also had impressive backgrounds.

The truly outstanding talents of Qianxue each had an ancestor backing them in secret.

It’s not his turn to take the lead.

The talents of the Four Sects and Seven Gates also did not speak rashly.

Ye Qingfeng of the Daluo Sect pondered for a while and then stepped forward.

In such a situation, some words could only be said by him.

The Daluo Sect was the strongest among the Eight Great Gates apart from Taixu Gate.

And he was widely acknowledged as the first under the Qianxue Four Talents.

Shen Linsu, Xiao Wuchen, Ao Zhan, and Duanmu Qing, these four top talents, were above the conflicts, shouldering the mission to compete for the first place in the sword discussion for their respective sects.

They balanced each other, making it impossible to act lightly.

In fact, being among the Four Great Sects, they were happy to see the Seven Great Gates and Taixu Gate locked in deadly conflict.

Once Taixu Gate was destroyed, the Asura War would truly begin.

Only then would the real slaughter start among the four of them.

But under the current circumstances, Taixu Gate had to be removed.

Especially with Mo Hua holding the advantage of the terrain, empowered by formations, and with Linghu Xiao and the five brothers of Taia, these talents with heavy killing power, Taixu Gate’s strength was undeniably the first among the Eight Great Gates.

The Four Great Sects might not regard Taixu Gate highly.

But for the other Seven Gates, climbing upwards required joint effort to eliminate this "formidable enemy" Taixu Gate.

This task could only be led by him. Some unpleasant words had to be spoken by him.

Ye Qingfeng said, "The biggest issue right now is Mo Hua’s formation."

"With the formation blocking the way, Taixu Gate can wait at ease. Even if we manage to break through the formation temporarily and enter the valley, we’ll face interception from Taixu Gate’s Linghu Xiao."

"And after retreating, Mo Hua would just set up the formation again."

"However..." Ye Qingfeng’s eyes sharpened, "There is only one High-Rank Formation Master in Taixu Gate’s camp, and that’s Mo Hua. He’s the only one capable of drawing High-Rank Formations."
