Chapter 1472: Chapter 799 Blood Sacrifice_2

The deities who fall into the evil path will be opposed by the Divine Practitioners.

Formation Masters naturally strive to completely seal them, leaving no loopholes.

Using the "seal" formation as a means to "hide" is truly an ingenious idea.

Lord Yellow Mountain admired it in his heart and glanced at Mo Hua with great curiosity.

He wondered where exactly this Divine Tao Array was learned from...

Lord Yellow Mountain wanted to ask, but wisely chose not to.

Considering the little cultivator’s astuteness, it’s likely he wouldn’t tell him.

Once Mo Hua finished the ink painting, he indeed left an opening in the formation behind the Divine Statue, saying to Lord Yellow Mountain: "Try going inside..."

Lord Yellow Mountain frowned.

Being a deity, it instinctively repelled formations that restrained deities.

Furthermore, this formation came from the mysterious hands of Mo Hua.

Once inside, if he seals it, there would truly be tears with no way out.

But being watched by Mo Hua’s clear and bright eyes, Lord Yellow Mountain felt a bit ashamed.

Oh well...

It gritted its teeth and eventually turned into a wisp of white smoke, trembling, and slipped into the Divine Statue.

Within the statue, the surrounding formations felt like narrow mountain walls, making it somewhat suffocated and uncomfortable.

It had a premonition that if Mo Hua sealed the opening at this moment, it would be trapped inside the statue, sealed by the formation, never to see the light of day again.

Lord Yellow Mountain felt uneasy.

Fortunately, after a while, Mo Hua asked: "How do you feel?"

"It’s... it’s alright..." Lord Yellow Mountain replied.

"Good." Mo Hua nodded, "Then come out. There’s no need to go inside regularly. Only in times of danger should you hide inside."

He didn’t know what exactly Lord Yellow Mountain was hiding from in the dilapidated temple.

But leaving a means of retreat is always good.

Lord Yellow Mountain transformed into green smoke and floated out from the seal, instantly feeling relieved, took a long breath, and then bowed to Mo Hua sincerely:

"Thank you, my friend."

"Alright, I’m leaving now. I’ll visit you again when I have time." Mo Hua waved and then departed from the cave.

Lord Yellow Mountain escorted Mo Hua to the door of the ruined temple, feeling a mix of emotions; after pondering for a long time, he finally spoke:

"In the Qian Learning State Boundary, there is an Evil God..."

"Does the Evil God have goat horns?" Mo Hua asked.

"Yes..." Lord Yellow Mountain nodded, then suddenly paused, "You... you know?"

Not only did he know, he’d seen it and even "experienced" it...

"How do you know?" Lord Yellow Mountain was a bit shocked.

Mo Hua said: "Near the state boundary, I encountered a few evil spirits; they seem to be the minions of this Evil God."

Lord Yellow Mountain looked at Mo Hua with a startled expression and asked:

"Are you alright?"

Mo Hua smiled and replied: "I’m fine..."

Lord Yellow Mountain nodded, thinking that with mastery of the Divine Tao Array, a few evil spirits shouldn’t trouble this "Little Plague God."

Lord Yellow Mountain remained silent for a moment, then spoke with a serious face:

"Minions are not terrifying; what’s terrifying is this Evil God..."

"This Evil God has been asleep for a long time, ancient and full of evil thoughts. Its current strength is feared to be less than one thousandth of its peak state. Once it awakens, even if it births a single Divine Fetus, it would be catastrophic for the world..."

Upon hearing this, Mo Hua’s expression also fell.

Though Lord Yellow Mountain was in decline now, he once had "grandeur," and his insights were still sharp.

His words implied that the true form of the Great Wilderness Evil God is possibly far more terrifying than imagined.

Especially because it might be "awakening"...

"Do you remember what I said to you before?" Lord Yellow Mountain continued.

Mo Hua intended to ask, "Which words?"

He had seen Lord Yellow Mountain many times and said many things, but couldn’t remember which words were being referred to.

Then, pondering the context, a frown emerged as he recalled.

"Are you saying, ’Qian Learning State Boundary is not a place to stay for long’?"

"Correct," Lord Yellow Mountain sighed, "I previously said, cultivate in the sect until the Foundation Establishment Late Stage then leave. Even if delayed for a few years, don’t exceed ten years. Within ten years, it might still be in time..."

"But now, it seems more dangerous."

Mo Hua remained still for a moment, "What do you mean?"

Lord Yellow Mountain looked up at the seemingly clear sky, furrowing his brow:

"Recently, for unknown reasons, the evil thoughts have suddenly become restless; it seems someone is plotting in secret, forcing this Evil God to accelerate the process. Now, it seems it might not last ten years..."

"Someone... plotting in secret?" Mo Hua also furrowed his brow.

Who could be so capable?

To even force the Evil God to accelerate its plans?

As Mo Hua pondered this, his heart suddenly skipped a beat.

The person Lord Yellow Mountain mentioned... could it be myself?

Am I pushing the Evil God?

No way...

Mo Hua remained silent for a moment, then quietly asked Lord Yellow Mountain:

"Mountain Lord, what exactly is the Evil God’s conspiracy? If it wants to awaken, what must it do?"

Lord Yellow Mountain’s expression was conflicted.

"Can’t say?" Mo Hua asked.

Lord Yellow Mountain sighed, "I don’t know the exact conspiracy. I only know that whenever ancient evil gods awaken, it’s often accompanied by a..."

Lord Yellow Mountain’s expression turned solemn; his voice was icy, "A grand blood sacrifice!"

Mo Hua’s pupils constricted.

A grand... blood sacrifice!

In the Qian Learning State Boundary?!

Lord Yellow Mountain looked at Mo Hua, speaking heavily:

"I can only share this much. If it truly happens, once the blood sacrifice begins, life would be devastated, all things wither, my friend... take care of yourself."

Mo Hua furrowed his brow, "But... in the Qian Learning State Boundary, there are many Fourth-grade and even Fifth-grade families and sects, with many Ancestors stationed. How could a blood sacrifice happen?"
