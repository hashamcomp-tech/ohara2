Chapter 2353: Chapter 1075: "Godslayer" (3)

This shout of fear, coupled with the scene of the brute’s disfigured face, deeply shook everyone’s hearts, inducing terror within them.

Fortunately, the white-clothed female True Man took a deep breath, using what little remained of her Divine Thought, and activated the Three Pure Ones Bell once more.

The Three Pure Ones Bell is a supreme treasure, with infinite uses, but it also consumes Divine Thought heavily.

So far, the white-clothed female True Man had activated the Three Pure Ones Bell numerous times to resolve crises, leaving her Divine Thought insufficient, and her Primordial Spirit somewhat scattered.

But even so, she gritted her teeth and, at the crucial moment, once again activated the "Clearing Evil" sound.

The clear bell sound dispelled fear and resisted the cries of the human-faced spider demon.

The Taoist immediately activated the Peach Wood Sword, thrusting it towards the human-faced spider demon, the evil-repelling Peach Wood Sword carrying golden light, piercing directly through one of its faces.

But that face snarled, biting tightly onto the Taoist’s Peach Wood Sword, refusing to let go despite the sword’s light grinding down.

The Peach Wood Sword stained with decaying flesh was further corroded by the human-faced spider demon’s slobber, gradually changing color and transforming into rotten wood.

The Taoist’s expression changed drastically.

Just at this moment, suddenly an angry roar sounded: "Second brother!"

Following the voice, they saw the second brother among the three Qimen Dunjia brothers, with a fresh red lip mark inexplicably appearing on his neck, spreading continuously.

At this moment, the second brother among the three was attacking the other two brothers, madly chanting:

"She is mine, she loves me, you can’t take her, I want you dead!"

The Qimen Dunjia technique emphasizes teamwork of three, brothers united in heart.

Now one of them was poisoned by the evil charm, and the Qimen Dunjia technique broke without attack.

The Qimen Dunjia of these three brothers was the most solid "shield" on this journey, and now with the technique broken, the "defense line" of Divine Thought Cultivators against demon monsters was utterly shattered.

Monsters hatched from eggs surged like a tide...

Accompanied by the three fetus-born demons, everyone’s hearts slowly descended into despair.

Elsewhere, the Daluo Sect Ancestor was battling the Xiao family’s ancestor fiercely. But as he fought, he discovered deep black stains on himself, with his heart’s evil thoughts continuously growing.

Meanwhile, the corrupted Xiao family’s ancestor’s expression became even more insidious and ferocious, as if he had anticipated all of this.

"I’m being assimilated..."

The Daluo Sect Ancestor’s face turned ashen.

True Man Situ, realizing all this around him, felt his heart go cold.

He knew it was all over.

Everyone among them truly was going to die.

"Never mind..." True Man Situ’s gaze suddenly became resolute, his heart calm.

He had come on this trip with the resolve to die, death was not scary, what was scary was, they would die, but the evil embryo wouldn’t.

Then the death of these ten Feather Transformations and four Heaven Voids would be meaningless.

True Man Situ looked at Yu Er with an abrupt and severe expression, "Beast, let’s die together!"

True Man Situ erupted with an incredibly strong will instantly, full of hatred and determined to move forward, he wielded almost all his Primordial Spirit strength to thrust the Seven Stars Sword fiercely.

This sword truly abandoned life and death, containing extraordinary Telekinesis.

Even the divine might of the evil embryo couldn’t withstand it.

The evil embryo screamed wretchedly but couldn’t stop True Man Situ’s absolute intention.

The tip of the Seven Stars Sword moved forward inch by inch.

The stars’ glimmer on the sword tip finally injured Yu Er, injured the evil god’s real embryo.

The light on Yu Er’s chest, burned by the stars, turned red.

And cracks began to appear on his abdomen.

A sharper scream emanated from Yu Er’s mouth, causing the Xiao family’s ancestor, the three fetus-born demons, and many accompanying demon monsters to attack True Man Situ in panic and anger.

Meanwhile, the Daluo Sect Ancestor and other Heaven Voids, along with remaining Feathered Immortals, had a rough premonition of their fate, and now were exerting everything, activating the last Divine Thought Secret Skill to hold off a crowd of evil god minions and demon monsters, buying True Man Situ the final chance.

True Man Situ’s jaw nearly drew blood, withstanding the twisting of the Primordial Spirit under the evil god’s pressure, enduring the tearing pain, he thrust the Seven Stars Sword even deeper.

Wherever the sword’s light reached, Yu Er was constantly screaming, and his abdomen finally broke open, cracking wider and wider.

Wenren Wan’s heart ached as if it were being stabbed by a knife, but she was helpless, could only look on sorrowfully at everything.

Tears surged forth, blurring her eyes, seemingly not wanting to let her see a more cruel scene.

True Man Situ’s eyes flashed with a hint of guilt and a trace of determination.

"I’m sorry..."

Using the last of his Divine Thought, he thrust the Seven Stars Sword sharply, wanting to bore through Yu Er’s chest, end it all.

But just at this moment, a tiny black hand suddenly extended from Yu Er’s abdomen, gripping True Man Situ’s sword tip.

True Man Situ’s pupils constricted sharply.

The Power of Stars surged continuously, slicing at this black hand.

But this tiny hand was as hard as solid stone.

Even its force of Divine Thought was terrifyingly great.

"Divine Body?!"

True Man Situ’s breath was halted, his heartbeat skipped a beat.

A tremendous fear instantly overwhelmed him.

And before total despair, True Man Situ exerted every last ounce of courage, intending to "wrestle" with the evil god, during this last chance, to use the Seven Stars Sword to kill it.

True Man Situ continued to thrust forcefully, but no matter how much he exerted, the Seven Stars Sword seemed embedded in goldstone, unmoving.

The black evil power even infiltrated the Seven Stars Sword.

Suddenly, with a "bang", right in front of True Man Situ, the Seven Stars Sword was completely shattered by that tiny black hand.

The Seven Stars Sword... shattered...

True Man Situ’s face turned pale instantly.

He knew, everything truly... had ended.

After crushing the Seven Stars Sword, the tiny black hand extended from Yu Er’s abdomen.

Then came an arm, a leg, a small body wrapped in membranous tissue filled with sin, soaked in black fetal water, and then, a head.

A small head with a face similar to Yu Er, yet bearing sheep horns.

Time nearly froze.

Accompanied by suffocating and terrifying heartbeats, the true embryo of the Great Wilderness Evil God finally... was born.

It slowly opened its eyes, emitting the first cry.

This cry was not shrill, but it contained great terror that changed the color of the world, spreading along the cause and effect of the Heavenly Dao, in an instant, it echoed throughout the world.

Qian Learning State Boundary, atop the Sword Observation Tower.

All Heaven Void Ancestors, almost simultaneously, felt a terrifying palpitation.

Below the Heaven Void, all cultivators were without exception filled with shock and fear.

Around the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, the righteous and demon paths cultivators fighting sensed this terrible tremor, and all involuntarily stopped, their expressions blank and frightened.

Qianxue cultivators felt as if something profoundly significant had awakened, making their chests feel dazed, nearly suffocating.

While the Demon Path Cultivators sensed this earth-shaking aura, amidst their fear, their expressions grew increasingly fervent.

They knew, the Divine Lord had awoken.

This world would embrace a true storm of blood, would embrace a true feast of slaughter.

...

Inside the Blood Sacrifice Array, in the hall of the Blood and Flesh Maze.

Feeling the terrifying aura of the evil god’s birth, the multitude of demons was similarly driven to madness.

And in the corner of the Blood and Flesh Maze, above the Blood Pool.

Feeling the strong aura of the evil god, Mo Hua slowly opened his eyes.

The evil god had been awakened.

And he was awakened by the evil god.
