Chapter 2148: Chapter 1011: Invisible (Part 2)

The Dragon Cauldron Body Refining method is an extremely superior ancient heritage.

Of course, these inheritances have passed down to the present, and there is certainly very little that truly relates to "dragons".

At most, they have the form, but not the substance.

Mo Hua could think of this without even using his head.

The Longding Sect has passed on for so many years, with so many disciples under its wing. If they all used the Blood of Karmic Dragon for Marrow Cleansing, it would have gone bankrupt a long time ago.

What kind of existence is a dragon?

Even the karma dragon is extraordinarily noble.

Where would there be so much blood to let disciples use for body refinement?

If there were truly so much Blood of Karmic Dragon, the Taoist Court would be the first to close your sect down and make you hand over the Dragon Blood for public use.

If you don’t comply, they would find an excuse to annihilate your entire sect.

As far as Mo Hua knows, the so-called "Blood of Karmic Dragon for Marrow Cleansing" is most likely carried out using spiritual objects that contain the aura of the karma dragon, such as Dragon Blood Fruit or Dragon Blood Grass, the likes of heavenly materials and earthly treasures, for body tempering.

Of course, these heavenly treasures and earthly treasures containing the word "dragon" are also incredibly precious and not something the average cultivator can afford to use.

By tempering the body with these, the cultivated technique is certainly not inferior in power.

Therefore, the Longding Sect is one of the Four Great Sects.

The disciples who join the Longding Sect either come from wealthy or noble backgrounds.

In this encounter with Mo Hua, it was an elite disciple of the Longding Sect, with profound cultivation and a strong body, and Taoist skills that were not common.

The strongest among them still couldn’t compare to the Big Senior Brother Shi Tiangang of the Vajra Sect.

But on average, their strength was slightly better than the previous round’s Vajra Sect.

All five of them were equipped with a Peeping Mirror and carried Revealing Dust.

The Peeping Mirror can see through concealment.

The Revealing Dust can reveal traces.

Their tactics were straightforward, and a bit extreme: all five of them were out to kill Mo Hua.

If any one of them could kill Mo Hua, they would win.

Because this match was a beheading round.

Mo Hua was still the "leader" of the Taixu Gate’s first team.

Five Longding Sect experts, against one Foundation Establishment Realm spiritual cultivator.

This spiritual cultivator was "light-skinned and tender," and could be killed with a single punch, plus, Mo Hua’s rudimentary movement technique was not a threat, and the only thing worth fearing was the concealment technique, which the five targeted specifically.

Not only the five from Longding Sect, but the elders of various sects, the high-level members of aristocratic families, and even the crowd of spectators outside couldn’t imagine what Mo Hua could rely on to survive.

This was almost a certain-death scenario.

Moreover, it happened to be a "beheading battle."

As long as they fought with everything and killed Mo Hua, they could win.

As long as they killed Mo Hua, they wouldn’t even need to deal with the most troublesome Sword Dao prodigy, Linghu Xiao, and the Longding Sect could win.

"Heaven does not favor Taixu Gate, Mo Hua in this match ought to die!"

This thought surfaced in almost everyone’s hearts.

Therefore, as soon as the Sword Discussion began.

The five from Longding Sect coldly shouted in unison, "Kill!"

Then, like dragon roars and tiger growls, their energy and blood surged, and the five of them aggressively attacked Mo Hua.

Mo Hua said nothing, he turned and ran towards the small grove.

Linghu Xiao and the other four took action to try and stop the five Longding Sect disciples.

But these were five body cultivators practicing the Dragon Cauldron Skill, their strength like tigers and dragons, and their bodies like copper cauldrons, with high offense, high defense, and fast movement technique.

Under the strategy of "don’t linger in battle, just behead Mo Hua," within just a few rounds, the five of them broke through Linghu Xiao and the others’ defenses from various angles, and charged into the small grove.

The woods were tranquil, shadows heavy, sunlight shattered into fragments, peaceful and serene, with no sign of Mo Hua.

But the five disciples of the Longding Sect wore cold smiles.

Playing tricks with the concealment technique once was enough.

This is the Sword Discussion Conference; if you don’t rely on hard strength or cultivation and Tao skills, you only think about using these little tricks, it just invites laughter.

Each of the five held a golden jeweled Peeping Mirror, shining like a lake, and from five angles, they illuminated the small grove.

Then the cold smiles on the corners of their mouths froze.

"Check again!"

The five illuminated once more.

The white light swept across, and the Peeping Mirror clearly presented every blade of grass, tree, insect, and stone in the small grove.

But there was no trace of Mo Hua.

Not only were the five disciples of the Longding Sect bewildered, but the elders of Daoist law from various sects also looked at each other in confusion.

The observing cultivators among the audience were even more abuzz with discussion, clearly seeing:

"Isn’t Mo Hua standing right in front of them? What are the five of them doing with the spirit mirrors?"

"The Peeping Spirit Mirror can’t see through concealment? Is it broken?"

"Isn’t the Longding Sect one of the Four Great Sects? They shouldn’t be short of money, so why are they using outdated spiritual artifacts?"

"This is the Sword Discussion Conference, using faulty Peeping Mirrors not to see through concealment is just making fools of themselves, right?"

"At least they are part of the Four Great Sects, it shouldn’t come to this..."

"Then what exactly is the situation now..."

...

In the field, the five disciples of the Longding Sect were equally puzzled.

They never expected a situation where the Peeping Mirror couldn’t see through the concealment.

This changed the entire situation completely.

"Scatter the Revealing Dust..."

The disciple leading Longding Sect immediately said.

The five scattered, chanting spells, activating their divine sense, beginning object manipulation, and scattering the dust from a box into the small grove.

These dust particles hovered in the air, and once adhered to something, could not be easily brushed away for a short time.

Once Mo Hua was enveloped in the dust, his form would be outlined, and hiding would be impossible.

But as the Revealing Dust dispersed through the grove, suddenly a gale arose, howling and sweeping the dust out of the grove into the sky.

"Gale Formation!"

The Longding Sect disciple ground his teeth.

A Gale Formation had been set around the small grove, once activated, it would raise a gale that blew the Revealing Dust completely away.
