Chapter 2271: Chapter 1049: Boss Leader_3

Ye Zhiyuan had a moment of enlightenment and promptly joined Mo Hua.

From this point on, Mo Hua effectively "unified" the Eight Great Gates.

And after the Eight Great Gates, there were the Four Great Sects.

Mo Hua led the disciples of the Eight Great Gates through the Evil Path Great Formation, operating unrestrained, continuously absorbing scattered disciples from various sects, while rescuing those who were not deeply corrupted and could still be saved.

For those who had completely fallen due to an unsteady Taoist Heart and external invasion, they had no choice but to be killed.

Along the way, Mo Hua also gathered some scattered disciples of the Twelve Streams.

The Eight Great Gates assembled, and naturally, the Twelve Streams would not miss the trend, eager to follow Mo Hua.

Thus, Mo Hua gathered more and more talented individuals.

By the time they met the disciples of the Four Great Sects, Mo Hua’s side had amassed over two hundred people, an overwhelming and dark mass.

Among the Four Great Sects, Xiao Ruohan, Ao Zheng, Shen Cangfeng, and others felt their scalps tingle just at the sight.

Since being trapped in the Evil Formation, they sensed something amiss and began gathering their forces, uniting the disciples of the Four Great Sects.

The disciples admitted to the Four Great Sects were all highly talented and proud in nature.

Shen Cangfeng and others were not top-tier prodigies endowed with Bloodline Power like Shen Linsu; despite their strong prowess, they did not possess the ability to truly command respect.

As such, they had only managed to gather around fifty prodigies from the Four Great Sects.

Compared to Mo Hua’s crowd of over two hundred, these fifty prodigies seemed somewhat "insignificant."

Not to mention, facing Mo Hua himself.

During the sword discussion, their fifty prodigies from the Four Great Sects were all obliterated by Mo Hua.

Mo Hua alone was enough to make them wary.

Not to mention now, Mo Hua was surrounded by a full two hundred people.

Shen Cangfeng and his group all wore expressions as dark as water, feeling cold inside, watching Mo Hua tensely.

"Mo Hua, do you intend to start a fight?" Ao Zheng asked with a sharp gaze.

Mo Hua shook his head, "No fight."

Hearing Mo Hua say this, the group of prodigies from the Four Great Sects all breathed a sigh of relief.

The pressure from Mo Hua was simply immense.

Xiao Ruohan spoke calmly, "Since that’s the case, then let’s go our separate ways and keep to ourselves."

Yet Mo Hua shook his head again, "No."

Xiao Ruohan and others felt a slight unease, "What do you mean?"

Mo Hua stated directly, "We can join forces."

Xiao Ruohan and others were stunned, then Ao Zheng sneered, "What kind of alliance? Are you trying to make us follow your orders and do what you say..."

Mo Hua nodded, "If you think that way, that’s fine too."

Ao Zheng was furious, "You’re dreaming!"

Shen Cangfeng also sneered, "Mo Hua, your expertise in formations is indeed exceptional, and your methods are profound, but relying solely on this, do you think the disciples of the Four Great Sects will follow your every command? That would be too far-fetched."

Xiao Ruohan also showed his edge, "You are from the Eight Great Gates, we are from the Four Great Sects, are you trying to overextend your reach by wanting to command the Four Great Sects?"

Mo Hua sighed slightly, shaking his head, "I don’t want to control you guys either, but this is the Evil Path Great Formation, everyone is trapped here on the same string like grasshoppers."

"If I don’t manage you now, after you become corrupted, go mad, or turn into Fallen Demons, you will threaten me, and I will have to kill you."

"Moreover, not only will I kill you, but you will also end up killing each other..."

Mo Hua looked at the bloodstains on Shen Cangfeng and the others’ Taoist Robes, and said indifferently, "You’ve experienced the taste of mutual destruction among fellow disciples, haven’t you?"

Upon hearing this, Shen Cangfeng and others looked grief-stricken, their expressions hiding their pain.

Not long ago, in this eerie Yanluo Mountain, they witnessed firsthand their fellow disciples suddenly going crazy and turning into Fallen Demons.

They fought fiercely, and although reluctant, they had no choice but to painfully slay those Fallen Demon fellow disciples.

Mo Hua had anticipated this.

The disciples of the Four Great Sects, having better origins and greater talents, have an even stronger desire for fame and fortune, which weakens their Taoist Hearts.

Usually, it might be fine, but in this formation filled with Evil Thoughts, a weak Taoist Heart becomes easily invaded by evil desires, leading them step by step into madness.

Even Shen Cangfeng, Ao Zheng, and Xiao Ruohan’s current states were not quite right.

If left unchecked, they might soon fall and become pawns or sacrifices for the Evil God.

Yet the prouder the prodigy, the stronger their self-esteem.

Even knowing that following Mo Hua might be the best choice, the strong sense of self-esteem still made Ao Zheng and others reluctant to associate with Mo Hua.

"Whether we live or die is our concern, not yours. To expect us to submit to you is absolutely impossible."

Ao Zheng sneered.

"Alright," Mo Hua nodded, then asked them, "Do you have the special Eternal Life Rune planted on you?"

Ao Zheng and others frowned, "What do you mean?"

"If you have the Eternal Life Rune planted, that’s okay, if not..." Mo Hua’s eyes turned cold, "I fear once I act, you’ll all die."

"The Eternal Life Rune or your life, in the end, will shatter the same."

These words were indifferent yet extremely domineering.

Ao Zheng and the others were filled with rage, but upon meeting Mo Hua’s gaze, they felt an endless fear.

For Mo Hua indeed had the capability.

Recently, he shattered five special Eternal Life Runes, and they were from the strongest prodigies of their Four Great Sects and Daluo Sect.

Fear brings rationality.

Ao Zheng and others calmed down gradually.

They realized Mo Hua wasn’t joking; if they didn’t go along with his intentions, they truly might be obliterated by him.

Shen Cangfeng sighed and asked Mo Hua:

"Can you really... lead us out?"

Mo Hua responded just as calmly:

"Don’t forget, I am the Formation Leader. With my expertise in formations, not just for you few, even if Shen Linsu or Xiao Wuchen came, they would have to follow my orders."

Formation Leader...

During the previous sword discussion, it was this Formation Leader who directly obliterated him.

And obliterated him twice...

Shen Cangfeng wore a serious expression and nodded, "Fine, I’ll follow you."

With Shen Cangfeng speaking, the others stopped resisting.

Xiao Ruohan and Ao Zheng reluctantly nodded in agreement as well.

Mo Hua nodded in satisfaction.

Thus, the Four Great Sects, Eight Great Gates, and some scattered Twelve Streams were all "unified" by him.

Now he was truly the "leader" of the Four Sects, Eight Gates, and Twelve Streams.
