Chapter 2203: Chapter 1028: Tumo Treasure Book_4

Cheng Mo exclaimed, "Junior Brother, you really have some clout."

"In all the sword discussion conferences, I’ve never heard of creating ’rules’ specifically targeting a particular disciple."

Everyone nodded in agreement, "Junior Brother, you’re truly the ’Sword Discussion Champion’."

"Unprecedented in history..."

"And probably no one will come after you..."

Mo Hua’s face turned dark.

A man fears fame like a pig fears becoming fat.

Being famous invites trouble, just like a fat pig is ready for slaughter.

This is not a good thing.

Mo Hua felt a bit unconvinced, "Has no one ever been targeted like this before?"

"To my knowledge," Hao Xuan shook his head, "no one..."

Mo Hua didn’t understand, "Then why are they targeting me so much?"

"Probably because you’re quite infuriating..."

A group of Taixu Gate junior brothers thought silently to themselves.

Junior Brother Mo Hua, setting aside other skills, is absolutely top-notch at being infuriating.

Even compared to his formation skills, it’s not inferior.

If he were doing it on purpose, that would be one thing.

The key is, sometimes he infuriates people unintentionally.

Unintentionally infuriating is the most infuriating.

Especially when he looks so pure and innocent while doing it, it’s even more maddening.

This was vividly demonstrated during this sword discussion conference.

Of course, they couldn’t say it out loud.

After all, within the Taixu Gate, Junior Brother is still respectable and lovable.

"Maybe it’s because, Junior Brother, you are different from other talents..."

"What do you mean?"

"Other talents are strong in cultivation, which is a strength in hard skills."

"But Junior Brother, you are strong in Taoist skill and formation, which is a strength in tactics."

"Hard skills are hard to target; strong is strong, weak is weak, but tactics can be countered in various ways..."

Some disciples speculated, "It’s probably because of women..."

"Junior Brother, you offended Lu Zhenlong, indirectly offending many of her admirers..."

For most people, offending is just offending, but not with women, especially beautiful women.

Cheng Mo quickly added, "And Duanmu Qing..."

During the sword discussion conference, Duanmu Qing chased Junior Brother relentlessly, as if in a ’no rest until death’ stance.

Someone sighed, "Being chased with resentment by two fairies from the Qianxue Conference simultaneously, Junior Brother, this could be considered ’fated for romantic disaster’..."

Mo Hua’s face turned even darker.

...

After finishing the meal, Mo Hua returned to the Disciple’s Residence.

He had to focus on drawing formations and devise strategies to cope with the next sword discussion.

With this "Rules for Mo Hua", all his skills, especially his conventional formation tactics, were thoroughly studied, making the next sword discussion presumably much tougher.

Yet deep down, he was puzzled.

The formation section of this "Rules" should be from the hands of elders from various sects.

It required seasoned Formation Masters for simulation and deduction of formation encounters.

Then what about the other parts?

Who developed them?

Mo Hua frowned.

Meanwhile, within the Tumo Order, a mysterious figure, blurred like magnetic ink, sent a message with bold ink markings:

"The various strategies aimed at Mo Hua have been compiled into the ’Rules’."

"Each person has a copy of this ’Rules’."

"The subsequent sword discussions will continue to supplement and perfect it."

"Our mission: Mo Hua must die!"

"Our goal: To slay Mo Hua!"

A group of people followed, chanting "Mo Hua must die!"

And this "Rules for Mo Hua", continued to circulate and improve among the Demon Slaying Alliance...

In the hundreds of years that followed, based on this sword discussion conference’s "Rules" as the blueprint, an ever more refined Tao Cultivation Canon emerged, with another well-known name:

"Tumo Treasure Book."
