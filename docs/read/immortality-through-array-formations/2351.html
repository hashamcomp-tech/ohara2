Chapter 2351: Chapter 1075: Godslaying

The Evil God has awakened?!

Looking at those innocent yet sinister eyes, a great shock surged through True Man Situ’s heart, causing his mental defenses to almost falter.

Deep fear rose within him, making him feel icy cold all over.

But True Man Situ was the Chief Elder of Mystery Valley, experienced in the ups and downs of life, and despite the terrifying emotions, he forced himself to calm down and, with the last trace of Clarity in his Divine Thought, struggled to think.

After a few breaths, True Man Situ was suddenly enlightened:

"No, He hasn’t awakened yet!"

"If the Evil God truly awakened, it wouldn’t be just this little commotion; we would definitely be facing a more terrifying killing intent."

"He hasn’t awakened yet!"

True Man Situ looked again at Yu Er’s eyes.

Within these childish eyes lay a hint of evilness, but clearly a trace of confusion and innocence remained, and even a hint of goodness could be seen.

These were not the eyes of the Evil God.

But... they’re close to being so...

True Man Situ could distinctly sense the goodness in Yu Er’s eyes gradually fading away.

Human nature was being extinguished, and evilness was growing.

The innocence and childlike nature were also transforming into indifference and cruelty.

As if all things in the world were mere straw dogs, offerings to Him, sacrifices for His consumption.

And within Yu Er’s Divine Body, there was a slow trembling, as if something was gradually hatching, awakening...

"Quickly kill!"

At this moment, True Man Situ had only this thought.

If not killed now, it would all be over.

He raised the Seven Stars Sword, ready to pierce Yu Er’s chest immediately.

But in the next instant, he suddenly felt a "boom," as if he was drowning in the deep sea, with an overwhelming pressure coming from all directions. Looking up, he found that Yu Er’s innocent face had somehow acquired a terrifying majesty.

In True Man Situ’s heart arose feelings of fear, trepidation, and submission.

As if he was nothing but an ant, a lowly servant, utterly incapable of offending the high and mighty deity.

True Man Situ even felt a compulsion to "kneel" before Yu Er.

His knees bent slightly, and his body began to stoop.

And then, the light from the Six Yang Red Gold Cup flashed, awakening True Man Situ immediately, making him realize what he was doing, sending a cold shiver through his heart.

The divine authority!

Mortal flesh must submit to the deity!

This is the Law of divine power!

A deep sense of terror arose in True Man Situ’s heart.

Not yet born, yet possessing such fearful divine authority, only one gaze was enough to make him feel submissive.

If truly descended, capable of unleashing complete divine power, he feared that he would not dare to even conceive a thought of "resistance."

"Must kill!"

All beings as straw dogs, all lives as sacrifices, are not empty words!

True Man Situ pointed his finger at his forehead, connecting with the Mystic Sky Beidou Seven Star Formation, borrowing the Power of Stars to resist the deity’s majesty.

At the same time, he tried his best to discard his ego, erase all emotions within himself, including fear, tension, and anxiety, and only instilled one belief into his heart:

At all costs, eradicate the Evil God!

The power of divine majesty diminished, and the suffocating pressure around him gradually receded.

True Man Situ suppressed the submission to "kneel" and the fear of "resistance" within him, trembling with his hands, clenched his teeth, and thrust the Seven Stars Sword at Yu Er.

The Seven-Star Sword Aura approached within three inches of Yu Er’s chest.

The sharp blue star aura began threatening the evil intent.

"Yu Er" bore a terrifying expression, letting out an immediate sharp screech.

The voice was filled with rage at the ant-like beings, making True Man Situ’s Blood Qi churn, his head ache, and his Primordial Spirit feel as if it were about to be "torn apart."

Yet he endured all of it, desperately trying to thrust down this sword.

"Yu Er’s" screech suddenly became sharper and more brutal.

This screech seemed to be mixed with blade-like Telekinesis, like a swirling storm, slicing True Man Situ, leaving him covered in wounds.

True Man Situ also felt caught in the fierce storm of Divine Thought, struggling to maintain his footing.

He still wanted to persist, clenching his teeth to persist, believing that perhaps just one more inch deeper, this sword might pierce into the "Evil Embryo’s" chest.

The light of the Seven Stars would eradicate sinful acts and dispel all crises.

But in the next moment, True Man Situ heard a "creak" sound by his ear.

First one, then more followed, resonating in succession.

True Man Situ quickly realized what was happening.

Fetal Demon Eggs!

It was the sound of countless Fetal Demon Eggs around him cracking open.

The yet unborn Evil God seemed to sense the impending danger, hence let out a sharp screech, forcibly commanding his Demon Monster "descendants" to break the eggs prematurely to protect him, the Divine Lord.

Soon, the Fetal Demon Eggs began to crack one after another, black blood water flowed all over the ground, and each Demon Monster emerged entwined with dark qi, carrying an unknown power of "corruption," born from decay, slowly standing up.

Even the three massive Fetal Demon Eggs beside the Evil God began to tremble, their surfaces developing cracks.

Like the red-pink Fetal Demon, the Fetal Demons possessing a part of the Evil God’s power of Law were about to hatch.

They were supposed to descend together with the Evil God, becoming His "trusted aides."

But now, sensing the edge of the Seven Stars Formations, the Evil God had to prematurely break the eggs and summon these "trusted aides."

As the Demon Eggs broke open one by one, waves of corrupt Evil Qi spread out, densely packed Demon Monsters started to emerge...

True Man Situ’s heart trembled and immediately addressed the crowd:

"Exert all strength, assist me in assassinating the Evil God!"

The ancestor of the Spirit Talisman Sect first abandoned the ancestor of the Xiao family and instead used the Soul Suppressing Talisman to suppress Yu Er, who was the Evil Embryo, alleviating True Man Situ’s pressure.
