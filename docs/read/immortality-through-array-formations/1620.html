Chapter 1620: Chapter 847: Chaotic Situation

The spacious room was soaked in blood, with dismembered body parts scattered about, as if some Demon Monster had been feasting inside.

Even Shui Yanluo, an Evil Cultivator who had killed countless, felt somewhat nauseated.

Though he killed, he did not eat people.

But the people in this room had clearly been "gnawed and chewed" by something.

"Could it be that the people in the temple were all eaten by that monster?"

"Was Mr. Witch also eaten?"

Xie Liu and Shui Yanluo’s hearts sank.

"Look around and see if there are any clues," Xie Liu said.

Shui Yanluo nodded.

Then the two of them began searching the messy room full of severed limbs.

Ouyang Feng and Hua Qianqian turned pale instantly when they saw the blood-soaked flesh.

As Sect Disciples, they had never seen such a bloody and terrifying scene.

Hua Qianqian covered her mouth and nose with her hand and closed her eyes, not daring to look any further.

Mo Hua was relatively fine.

Although he felt some discomfort, his short, not even twenty-year-long Tao Cultivation career was already filled with extensive encounters with Heretical Demons.

He had seen similar scenes before.

And he was a Monster Hunter, having witnessed monsters devouring humans since childhood, so he quickly adapted.

Mo Hua thought for a moment, then also began searching the room.

However, with the ground covered in flesh, it was a bit dirty, and he didn’t want to dirty his hands, so he took a stick out of his Storage Bag, poking here and prodding there, looking for clues.

After searching for a while, he indeed found something.

"A large number of Formations were once drawn inside the room, and they were drawn with human blood; these are Evil Formations..."

"These Formations have all been activated."

"After the Formation operated, something seemed to have happened, but to prevent others from knowing, even the Formation Patterns were erased..."

"This Formation... is somewhat complicated."

Mo Hua frowned; with his Formation foundation and experience with Evil Formations, he couldn’t see much more.

Of course, it could also be that the remaining Formation Patterns were already few and blurred by blood, so he couldn’t discern them.

Additionally, Mo Hua also found a small piece of scale within the flesh.

This scale was a blue-ink color, stained with blood, similar to the scales on that monster, but smaller and more crystalline.

The scale lacked the eerie Blood Qi but retained a trace of...

Mo Hua pondered for a moment; it was difficult to describe.

But by intuition, it was a scent he had never perceived from any beast, somewhat ancient, with a hint of sacredness.

Mo Hua sniffed around near the scale.

Besides the strong stench of blood, there was another fresh blood scent.

The strong blood smell was Human Blood.

What was the fresh blood scent...?

Mo Hua furrowed his brows.

But this blood only left a scent, with no liquid, not even a trace, overwhelmed by the stench of Human Blood, making it even more difficult to detect.

Mo Hua shook his head, muttering to himself:

"What on earth happened in this room? What Formation was set up, and what is the origin of this blood scent?"

"What was feeding here?"

"And Mr. Witch, could it be...?"

Mo Hua’s gaze flickered with thought.

On the other side, Shui Yanluo and Xie Liu also finished searching, then gathered together and said:

"These corpses are the temple guards’..."

"There’s no corpse of Mr. Witch."

"He might still be alive."

"Everything else has been destroyed, books, Jade Slips, Tokens, all ruined, seemingly done on purpose by someone..."

The two frowned at this point.

Questions increased, yet what they knew remained very limited.

This Dragon King Temple, more secretive than the Rouge Boat, was shrouded in thick fog, making it difficult for ordinary cultivators to find.

This was a place for the Divine Lord’s banquet, under the protection of the Divine Lord.

Who could have such great power to create such bizarre disturbances, and for what purpose?

Shui Yanluo glanced at the scattered limbs and the sticky flesh underfoot, feeling a bit uneasy, said:

"We shouldn’t stay here too long, let’s leave first."

Xie Liu also felt uneasy, being a Golden Core cultivator, he vaguely sensed some events were beyond his understanding.

The group left Mr. Witch’s main hall and stepped outside; the blood scent faded, but the oppressive feeling grew heavier.

It was as if something was watching them from the shadows.

Shui Yanluo’s eyes darted, and he immediately said: "Let’s get out of this temple!"

They came here to hide.

But now, it seems, hiding in this temple isn’t much safer than encountering the Court Official’s dogs, and the temple itself might be more dangerous.

Unknown dangers lingered.

If so, why stay here?

Xie Liu understood too, responding in a deep voice: "Alright!"

Mo Hua was a bit reluctant.

He had gone through a lot to get into this temple, and now he was about to leave empty-handed.

Mo Hua, with a shift in mind, thought of some little tricks to keep Xie Liu and Shui Yanluo here, but turning to see Ouyang Feng and the pale-faced Hua Qianqian, he softened his heart a bit.

"I don’t mind, but it’s indeed dangerous for senior brother and sister to stay here; it’s better to go out first..."

Shui Yanluo set out, heading towards the temple’s exit.

Mo Hua followed silently.

Ouyang Feng and Hua Qianqian closely followed Mo Hua, while Xie Liu stayed at the back, also closely watching Mo Hua.

The Sect Leader’s legacy was tied to Mo Hua.

He couldn’t let Mo Hua die, nor could he let Mo Hua escape.

Even if death were the only option, it would have to wait until he secured what he needed...

The group proceeded cautiously towards the temple’s exit.
