Chapter 1443: Chapter 789 19_3

In just a moment, these enhanced divine thoughts would be "devoured" by the laws of the Heavenly Dao seal.

Mo Hua kills more, consumes more.

The Heavenly Dao’s sealing laws swallow more.

Mo Hua was initially anxious, wanting to quickly feed this seal to break through the limitations, but as he fed, he suddenly paused.

Mo Hua found that each time he "fed" the Heavenly Dao seal, the "laws" within the seal would circulate once.

The more he fed, the more frequent the display of laws.

Mo Hua suddenly realized...

This is the direct presentation of the Heavenly Dao Laws!

Also, it’s an intuitive display of the core laws of the Divine Tao Array!

In other words, this is a rare opportunity, even a great fortune that countless formation masters dream of!

To frequently and intuitively feel the circulation of the Great Dao Laws and comprehend the intricacies of the Divine Tao Array.

Mo Hua was suddenly enlightened like a baptism of wisdom and realized.

"Such a great opportunity, I almost missed it..."

"Luckily, I’m clever!"

Mo Hua couldn’t help but nod.

Comprehending this sealing principle might mean more to him than a simple divine sense breakthrough...

Mo Hua’s eyes lit up, and he quieted his mind, refining evil thoughts while "feeding" these telekinetic powers to this "seal," letting it eat slowly, circulate slowly.

Meanwhile, Mo Hua seizes the chance to engrave the images into his mind during the transformation between void and reality when the laws are circulating.

Deeply imprint this comprehension of the Great Dao Laws at heart.

Unknowingly, also integrating the comprehended laws more deeply into the Divine Tao Array he laid down.

The effect was immediate.

After fusing the comprehended laws into the Divine Tao Array, Mo Hua suddenly noticed that while the "Divine Lock Array" he laid seemed unchanged, its "resilience" became more robust.

The number of hits it could endure from demon wraith impacts increased.

This means if this "Divine Lock Array" were used to seal evil objects, it would become increasingly unbreakable.

Of course, for now, it would still break.

After enduring nearly a hundred impacts from nearby demon wraiths, the Divine Lock Array shattered again.

Yet the formation’s effectiveness was significantly enhanced.

Then, the demon wraiths rushed towards him again...

Mo Hua began to kill once more.

Though monotonous and seemingly dangerous, Mo Hua gradually found joy in it.

The more demon wraiths he killed, the stronger the devoured evil thoughts.

The telekinetic powers fed to the Heavenly Dao seal continued endlessly, and the power of laws he comprehended gradually deepened.

And so, as time passed...

The void fissure in Mo Hua’s sea of consciousness became shallower, like a torn wound in divine sense, slowly healing.

The "sealing" power in the sea of consciousness was gradually weakening.

While Mo Hua’s control over the sealing laws grew stronger.

Ultimately, the fissure disappeared completely.

The "seal" in the sea of consciousness was utterly erased.

At that moment, Mo Hua felt his divine sense suddenly become unblocked.

A chasm once blocking his sea of consciousness was completely filled in, disappearing without a trace!

He could sense his divine sense was growing again!

Telekinetic powers flowed into his sea of consciousness, nourishing his divine sense, slowly "reviving," and the realm that had been sealed for ages finally began to loosen.

In an instant, the sea of consciousness quaked.

The bottleneck, just a step away from eighteen patterns, was burst through instantly.

Mo Hua’s expression was jubilant, his eyes shining like stars.

Finally, eighteen patterns of divine sense!

Yet it didn’t stop there!

After the Heavenly Dao seal was "fed" and utterly annihilated, the unending stream of telekinetic powers finally benefited Mo Hua himself.

His divine sense realm continued to climb...

From afar, the sword bone was left stunned.

This little ancestor... still growing stronger?!

Meanwhile, outside the nightmare, near the Demon Refining Diagram.

Xun Zixian, who was vigilantly protecting Mo Hua, suddenly paused, turning his gaze upon Mo Hua with a peculiar expression.

Xun Ziyou noticed the anomaly, his expression stiffening as he asked:

"What happened?"

Xun Zixian was silent for a moment before saying, somewhat incredulously:

"Mo Hua’s divine sense... seems to have grown stronger?"

"Divine sense growing stronger?" Xun Ziyou was puzzled, glancing at Mo Hua, questioning, "How did it grow stronger? He didn’t break through his realm, did he? How can divine sense grow stronger in such a short time?"

After all, divine sense differs from cultivation.

A cultivator’s cultivation can suddenly become stronger.

But divine sense, without any cultivation technique, grows exceedingly slowly, only suddenly diminishing, never suddenly growing stronger.

However, he was no formation master, didn’t study the ways of divine thought, so changes like this weren’t particularly apparent to him, certainly not as much as Xun Zixian, a third-grade formation master.

Xun Ziyou scrutinized Mo Hua again, nodding slowly, "It seems... it has grown a bit stronger."

Xun Zixian suddenly shook his head, "No."

Xun Ziyou was confused, "Not what?"

"It’s not ’a bit’ stronger," Xun Zixian said solemnly, "it’s grown a lot stronger, perhaps even..."

"Possibly it’s a breakthrough..." Xun Zixian uttered deeply.

Xun Ziyou was stunned, his mouth agape.

"...A breakthrough?"

Divine sense breakthrough?

Xun Zixian’s eyelids twitched, his mind equally incredulous.

He was about to say something when he was startled again, gazing at Mo Hua with an expression he couldn’t hide any longer: shock.

This is...

Another breakthrough?

In such a short span, two breakthroughs in divine sense?!

Always composed, Xun Zixian was shaken, murmuring:

"This... how is it possible..."

This child, just how strong had his divine sense become...

...

Inside the Demon Refining Diagram.

Mo Hua opened his eyes, and in that instant, his gaze was radiant and complex, containing flowing stars, vanishing golden light, fluctuating heavenly secrets, and shifting Gui Tao.

His aura had become deeper.

An even greater power was his divine thought.

"Nineteen patterns!"

Mo Hua’s little divine thought form seemed to contain rivers of vast telekinetic power.

All around him lay in ruin.

Tens of thousands of demon wraiths had been killed, leaving scarcely any left.

Similarly, he’d "eaten" them until scarcely any were left.

By devouring countless demon wraiths in the Valley of Ten Thousand Demons, Mo Hua fed the Heavenly Dao seal, and beyond that, his divine sense soared.

Breaking through two realms in succession, advancing to nineteen patterns!

He had just entered the Foundation Establishment Middle Stage not long ago but already possessed the divine sense of a Foundation Establishment Late Stage cultivator, even a Foundation Establishment Peak cultivator.

Nineteen patterns of divine sense!

Only one pattern shy of the Golden Core Realm’s twenty patterns!
