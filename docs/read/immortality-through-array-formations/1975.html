Chapter 1975: Chapter 955: Dragon Diagram

In the depths of the tomb pit, the battle atop the bright yellow bronze coffin raged on.

Amidst the blood wind and rain, severed limbs filled the sky.

A gigantic fleshy corpse effigy swung its arms, commanding hundreds upon thousands of corpse demons, roaring in anger like a monster.

And opposite him, the black-robed man carried an Evil Dragon on his back, surrounded by towering Blood Qi. The Four Symbols Green Dragon Evil Formation was operating at full force, its fierce power rivaling the massive corpse effigy.

Each of this man’s punches could unleash a dragon’s shadow.

Each dragon shadow, accompanied by a dragon’s roar, charged forward with astonishing might, crushing onto the enormous fleshy corpse effigy.

The power of the Evil Dragon was terrifying in the extreme, and even the powerful fleshy corpse effigy couldn’t withstand it.

Large masses of fleshy remnants were blasted apart, dissolved, turning into ashes by the Dragon Fist.

Such mighty power not only provoked the flesh corpse effigy into furious anger, but also caused the other people present to feel emotionally turbulent.

Especially Mr. Hui, whose face was full of shock.

"Is this... a dragon?!"

He never expected that the silent man along the way would actually possess such terrifying Evil Dragon power.

Stone was equally shocked.

Even the other three black-robed cultivators were momentarily overwhelmed with various expressions.

The black-robed elder exhibited an arrogant expression tinged with fanaticism.

The black-robed noble sighed enviously, murmuring: "So this is... the dragon of the Great Wilderness..."

Another black-robed man, amidst the fierce battle, also tore off his black robe, revealing a physique as robust as Bear Pi, along with the fierce Monster Pattern on his back.

This black-robed man crushed a corpse demon with his bare hands and then raised his head to look at the figure covered in Dragon Pattern, his eyes full of longing.

And within that longing were traces of jealousy and greed.

The cultivators present, some were shocked while others had different thoughts.

Only Mo Hua was secretly working hard, calculating Formation Patterns, "stealing" the formation.

Using the overwhelming Corpse Qi, along with the surge of Spiritual Power and Blood Qi as a cover, Mo Hua hid at a distance, employing both Heavenly secret Tricky Calculation and Heavenly secret Calculation to amplify each other, spying on this secretive Great Wilderness Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation Diagram.

In his eyes, black, white, and gold, the three colors intertwined.

Powerful Telekinesis poured forth like a flood.

Various ancient and mysterious Formation Patterns, carrying ancient significance, presented themselves in his mind through calculation.

Meanwhile, Mo Hua used his hands to draw on the ground, sketching Mo Patterns, quickly recording these Formation Patterns one by one on the ground.

On the gray-black ground, streaks of ink marks gradually emerged, like Azure Dragon’s scales and claws, piecing together.

First the dragon claws, then dragon scales, dragon tail, dragon body, dragon fins...

A dragon-shaped figure outlined by Formation Patterns gradually appeared... until it reached the dragon head.

But at the dragon head, the entire dragon was stuck.

The calculation suddenly slowed down.

Mo Hua’s Sea of Consciousness began to ache like being pricked by needles, his Divine Sense gradually exhausted.

The calculation empowered by Tricky Calculation surpassing what he could endure began to limit, causing his eyes to swell, as if blood was about to flow...

It seemed like a bottleneck was stuck on the path of calculation.

No matter how hard he tried, how persistent he was, he couldn’t overcome this bottleneck and couldn’t calculate the entire dragon.

Blood almost flowed from the corner of his eyes, the stabbing pain in the Sea of Consciousness became sharper, Mo Hua knew he had truly hit his limit, and had no choice but to cease the calculation.

"The Divine Sense is still not enough, the peak of Nineteen-Pattern also isn’t enough..."

"The Tricky Calculation and the Calculation are still lacking flare."

Mo Hua sighed and then gently rubbed the corner of his eye, lowering his head to look at the Formation Map on the ground.

The Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation, a complete Formation Diagram, presented a dragon shape, but now this dragon lacked a head.

The body part consisted of eighteen patterns.

Mo Hua tried his best but could only deduce to this extent.

The remaining Formation Patterns for the Azure Dragon’s head were beyond the current limits of Mo Hua’s Divine Sense and Calculation powers; he couldn’t deduce them.

Based on the deduced partial diagram, the complete Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation Diagram might be nineteen patterns, but is more likely over twenty patterns, or even twenty-one patterns as an Ultimate Formation.

This means, this Demon Sect Leader’s Green Dragon Formation, is not just a Four Symbols Formation of the Great Wilderness, but also a Divine Beast Formation within the Four Symbols Formation, furthermore, a transmission exceeding a Second Grade Twenty Pattern Absolute Formation.

"What a pity..." Mo Hua sighed inwardly.

If his Divine Sense and Tricky Calculation method were stronger, perhaps he could have completely deduced this Formation Diagram.

Not ending up like now, with claws and tail but no head.

"Though even if calculated, it seems useless..."

Mo Hua examined again the partially deduced Azure Dragon Formation Diagram, gradually frowning.

Though a dragon, but the bones were jagged, patterns reversed, claws and fangs sharp, exuding an aura of evil from its majesty.

"This is an Evil Formation..."

If it was an Evil Formation, then it couldn’t be learned.

Mo Hua would not arrogantly disregard his mentor, Elder Master Xun, and all the elders and seniors’ warnings along the way.

He would not be foolish enough to learn the Formation of the Evil Path acting smart.

Not listening to elders’ words will lead to suffering visibly.

The distinction between good and evil, he could still discern clearly.

"Then what about my Lifebound Formation Map...?"

"How should this incomplete Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation Diagram be handled?"

To transform evil into good?

Where to obtain a complete Formation Diagram?

Surely not, having to kill this powerful Demon Sect Leader, peel the skin off his back, and copy it down?

Mo Hua furrowed his brows tightly.

But with time pressing, he had no space for much thought.

Recording the deduced Formation Diagram onto the Jade Slip, storing it in the Storage Ring, afterward erasing all the traces of calculation on the ground, Mo Hua finally felt at ease, then looked up at the distant battlefield.
