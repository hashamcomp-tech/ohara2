Chapter 2191: Chapter 1025: Everything Blown Up

The dazzling light of Fang Tian’s Shadow blinded everyone, leaving them dumbfounded.

The Sect Leaders of the Taixu Gate’s Three Mountains, holding their teacups, were also stunned on the spot.

The three Sect Leaders, who were previously burdened with heavy thoughts, now stared at the explosion-filled Fang Tian’s Shadow, their minds blank.

On the Sword Observation Tower, some people who had been engrossed in their conversations were startled and couldn’t help but look downwards.

Meanwhile, in front of Fang Tian’s Shadow, the cultivators watching from the mountains were even more bewildered:

"What happened?"

"Did the Mount of Tao explode?"

A moment later, they slowly came to realize:

"Is it... a Formation?"

"Is it that brat Mo Hua’s doing?"

"What kind of Formation is this strong?"

"What’s the status of the sword discussion..."

Everyone sat upright, enduring the piercing light, staring intently at Fang Tian’s Shadow.

After some unknown amount of time, the explosion ceased.

The light from Fang Tian’s Shadow gradually dissipated, the smoke and dust dispersed, revealing the aftermath of the sword discussion venue:

An empty wasteland.

Nothing else remained.

The disciples of the Qian Taoist Sect were gone.

The city gates, the city walls, and the City Lord’s statue were gone as well.

Even Mo Hua and his Taixu comrades were nowhere to be seen.

There was a dead silence in the field, followed by a collective gasp:

"Damn it! Everything’s blown to hell?!"

"Mo Hua, that brat, used a Formation to blow up the entire city along with the disciples of the Qian Taoist Sect?"

"Even his fellow disciples and himself were blown up!"

"I’m impressed, this kid is ruthless."

"So ruthless, he doesn’t even spare himself..."

Then someone exclaimed, "What kind of Formation did he use that has such strong power?"

"Second Grade Nineteen Patterns High-Level Killing Formation..."

"Can a High-Level Killing Formation be this strong?"

"This... I really don’t know..."

"With destruction like this, who actually won?"

After a moment of silence, someone responded, "It should be Taixu Gate..."

Someone was bitter, "Even Taixu Gate’s disciples died, why should they win?"

"This is a siege battle, as long as the city gate is breached and the statue destroyed within the time limit, it’s considered a win."

"Now the time isn’t up, the statue is destroyed, even if all Taixu Gate’s disciples ’died,’ it’s still their win."

"Damn, that brat Mo Hua won again..."

"That brat, what a damn fiend."

Someone sighed, "This is the first time I’ve seen someone siege a city and blow the entire city away..."

"Fortunately, this was just a sword discussion. If it was real Taoist Soldier warfare, capturing cities, then this kid would definitely be a master of city slaughter."

"It’s hard to say, his Formation is so strong, his conscience is bad, his mind is sinister, in the future, he might just become a ’little Demon Head,’ who knows how many murders he’ll commit..."

"In my opinion, Taixu Gate should take preventive measures, abolish this kid’s cultivation, and expel him from the Sect, or else it’s too dangerous..."

A group of people echoed, "Exactly, exactly..."

Someone sneered, "A bunch of idiots, talking nonsense as soon as they open their mouths?"

"Abolish his cultivation, expel him from the Sect, can Taixu Gate bear to do that?"

Of course, Taixu Gate couldn’t bear it.

At this moment, in the Sword Observation Tower.

The Three Mountains Sect Leaders were slowly recovering from their shock.

The Chongxu Sect Leader shook his head, muttering, "This child’s Formation is truly... formidable..."

Even though he was the Sect Leader of Mount Chongxu, at this moment, deeply shocked, he couldn’t find any other word to describe what he had seen and heard.

The Tai’a Mountain Sect Leader was also dazed for a long while, glanced at the Taixu Sect Master, and couldn’t help but say with a hint of admiration:

"This child has been in your Taixu Mountain for nine years and hasn’t blown up your mountain gate; that’s truly fortunate..."

The Taixu Sect Master gave a wry smile.

He didn’t expect Mo Hua to create such a big commotion.

But at the same time, all three Sect Leaders were secretly alarmed.

At the Sword Discussion Conference, using a Second Grade High-Level Formation, he was able to blow up the entire venue into flat land...

No matter what Formation he drew or what Formation principle he used, doing this was quite remarkable.

One could even say it was "terrifying."

And the situation of the cultivation world, these Sect Leaders couldn’t possibly have no premonition.

In future times, if the world were at peace, it would be fine.

But once chaos ensues, order collapses, and Tao Cultivation factions massively merge and fight, with widespread warfare.

This kid, with his supernatural Formation skills, given time, would surely become a city-destroying level terrifying "great weapon."

Even if his cultivation level is not high, it would be enough to make all factions tremble with fear.

The name "Mo Hua," in the minds of these three Sect Leaders, suddenly became weighty...

...

The venue for the sword discussion was blown flat, and the spectators were still in an uproar.

Soon, an elder from the Mount of Tao announced Taixu Gate’s victory.

But the subsequent sword discussion matches were to be postponed for half a day to rearrange the venue.

The elders and disciples from Taixu Gate watching breathed a sigh of relief.

Regardless, Taixu Gate had won another round.

Wenren Wan, Gu Changhuai, Zhang Lan, and others, seeing the huge commotion Mo Hua caused, even causing the schedule to be delayed, couldn’t help but give wry smiles.

No one expected a siege battle to turn out like this.

But thinking about it, this was something Mo Hua did, so somehow... it wasn’t that surprising.

Especially Zhang Lan.

He had seen Mo Hua blow up even bigger things.

So although the scene before him was not small, Mo Hua, this kid, had certainly "restrained" himself quite a bit, otherwise, it might have been even bigger.

Yu Er, on the other hand, was shocked, opening her small mouth wide, even more in awe of Brother Mo.

This sword discussion, thus ended abruptly with Mo Hua’s explosion.

As the crowd was either in awe, puzzled, shocked, or blamed, with various thoughts, Fang Tian’s Shadow dimmed, and the people gradually dispersed in twos and threes.
