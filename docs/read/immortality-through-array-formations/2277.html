Chapter 2277: Chapter 1051: Massacre (Part 3)

But none of their actions escaped Mo Hua’s Divine Sense.

With a flick of Mo Hua’s finger, fireballs rained like fire, interrupting the actions of these Evil Formation Masters.

Some Evil Formation Masters glared angrily, daring to look at Mo Hua.

But they did not know that doing so was tantamount to courting death.

Mo Hua remained motionless, and with a glint of golden light in his eyes, the Seven Souls Tai Xu Sword Prison emanated, intimidating their Divine Souls, leaving them terrified and unable to move.

In the chaos of battle, the other sect’s Heavenly Pride took the opportunity to slay these Evil Formation Masters with a single sword strike.

Some Evil Formation Masters, proficient in formations, still managed to activate the Evil Formation with unfathomable methods.

Blood spread on the ground, summoning white bones that formed into a formation.

A group of Evil Formation Masters gathered together, using the Evil Formation as cover, restructuring their formation to withstand the Heavenly Pride’s assault.

But in the next moment, golden and fiery spiritual ink also began to meander on the ground, forming a high-rank Second Grade Killing Formation almost instantly, the golden light Earthfire Compound Formation, encircling the Evil Formation Masters with a grander and more powerful formation.

Then, the Killing Formation erupted.

The golden light shattered the white bones, and the Earthfire scorched away the blood.

The White Bone Evil Formation was broken.

The group of Evil Formation Masters, who had never seen such magical formation skills, showed expressions of terror, while Qianxue’s Genius brought their slaughter to them, taking advantage of the opportunity...

...

Thus, under Mo Hua’s command and control, a tragic yet covert "slaughter" against the Evil Formation Masters took place within the grand hall.

Before long, the fighting ceased.

The originally blood-red hall of the Evil Formation was now even more littered with corpses, blood flowing profusely, forming rivers.

However, this time the blood shed was no longer that of innocent cultivators, but of these Evil Formation Masters who aided the tyrant.

Nearly eighty Evil Formation Masters were completely slaughtered.

Mr. Tu, not knowing how much time and how many plans it took, after so much effort, meticulously trained such a large group of Evil Formation Masters, only for them to be completely wiped out by Mo Hua and Qianxue’s Genius in one fell swoop, not one remaining.

This was a clean, swift, one-sided annihilation.

It appeared tragic, but in reality, the slaughter was very easy.

The Heavenly Pride of the Four Sects and Twelve Streams, looking at the Evil Formation Masters exterminated before them, were somewhat taken aback and disoriented.

They knew clearly in their hearts that despite slaying these Evil Formation Masters as easily as cutting melons and vegetables, these Evil Formation Masters were not actually "incompetent."

On the contrary, as Evil Formation Masters, they were mostly insidious and vicious, with some sinister methods; if truly executed, they’d be endless trouble.

If Mo Hua had not investigated and planned in advance, using formations as cover and spells as support, even if victory was assured, many lives would inevitably be lost, and the process wouldn’t be this smooth...

Mo Hua had calculated everything, prepared all formations, considered all contingencies, and they only needed to kill at the right time and place.

Everything else required no concern.

Should there be an unexpected situation, Mo Hua would alert them.

In case of an ambush, Mo Hua would interrupt it.

Faced with a formation, Mo Hua would break it.

Even with tough opponents, merely a "look" from Mo Hua would enable them to be slaughtered.

Truly it was as easy as eating or drinking water.

The Heavenly Pride Disciples were suddenly astonished:

Is this what it feels like to be teammates with Mo Hua?

This experience is worlds apart from being his opponent.

As for the outcome of opposing Mo Hua...

They looked again at the Evil Formation Masters lying dead before them, blood flowing abundantly, shuddering once more in their hearts.

Perhaps these Evil Formation Masters met their end opposing Mo Hua.

They died not knowing how they died...

The Heavenly Pride of Qianxue were silent for a moment, feeling their hands and feet cold.

...

Meanwhile, Mo Hua, not sensing their thoughts, had already begun expertly organizing the "aftermath":

"Do not be careless, remember to deal a finishing blow after a chaotic fight."

"Make sure no one is left out, use a spell or sword qi if necessary to ensure they’re really dead."

"Then collect their storage bags."

"Also, don’t miss any Formation Diagrams or Formation Books."

"Some demons turn bones and teeth into storage containers, so check them all over and smash their teeth, break the bones if anything unusual is sensed."

"The heart can be opened and checked, as some people forge magic treasures there..."

...

Mo Hua instructed unreservedly, sharing his lifelong "experience."

The sect’s Heavenly Pride were taken aback by his words.

They silently watched the delicate, fair-skinned Mo Hua, with eyes like paintings, feeling an even deeper chill in their hearts.

Mo Hua furrowed his brow and said, "What are you waiting for? Hurry and do it..."

This is within the Evil Path Great Formation; time is tight, and you never know when unforeseen circumstances or formidable cultivators might be encountered.

So shouldn’t they utilize every second to loot instead of standing there?

These disciples of the Four Sects and Twelve Streams seemed to lack any sense of urgency...

Mo Hua wondered inwardly.

While they spoke, the disciples of Taixu Gate, following Mo Hua’s instructions, continuously "finished off," "looted," and even pried teeth, broke bones, and opened chests to find treasures...

After all, being from the Taixu Gate, having mixed with this Junior Brother Mo Hua for so long, these people had some level of experience.

The other sect disciples, after a moment of silence, also sighed helplessly, then followed Mo Hua’s orders, with reference to the Taixu Gate disciples, looting the Evil Formation Masters present.

Indeed, some Evil Formation Masters were "feigning death."

He seemed to use some "turtle breathing" method, concealing beneath other corpses and blood qi, unnoticed by anyone.

But once "finished off," it was a different story.

The Evil Formation Master was pierced through the chest with a sword, about to have his heart ripped apart, unable to endure it, and leapt up intending to strike, but was entrapped by Mo Hua with the Water Prison Technique and hacked to death by the other disciples.

From then on, all the sect’s Heavenly Pride had more faith in Mo Hua’s words.

They began diligently implementing Mo Hua’s "finishing blow" measures, also looting as Mo Hua instructed, one by one.

They even conducted "cross" inspections.

Each Evil Formation Master was subjected to at least three sect Heavenly Pride’s finishing blows and searches.

Under such circumstances, it’s almost impossible to let anything slip through, be it treasures or Formation Diagrams.

Before long, Mo Hua had a pile of "spoils" before him.

It included Spirit Stones, Evil Pills, Evil Artifacts, Evil Formations, and other Evil Objects.

There were also many Jade Slips, Formation Books, Formation Diagrams, and other formation classics related to Evil Path formations.

Mo Hua nodded, quite satisfied.
