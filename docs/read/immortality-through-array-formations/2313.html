Chapter 2313: Chapter 1063: Mystery Valley

Mo Hua pinched his fingers slightly, did a quick calculation, and frowned, realizing that these matters weren’t so simple.

Rescuing Yu’er is not so easy.

The evil deity statue is also not easy to get hold of.

Even thinking about escaping from Mr. Tu of the Feather Transformation Realm, leaving this Evil God Temple where demons run amok, is fraught with challenges.

These matters still require a lot of Divine Sense and effort, thorough deduction, and careful planning.

Need to think carefully about how to break the situation...

Mo Hua frowned, his eyes glued to the broken Formation Patterns Mr. Tu gave him, occasionally sketching a few strokes, pretending to study formations, but inside his Sea of Consciousness thoughts surged, Divine Sense pouring out, repeatedly sorting through the cause and effect...

No one knows how long it was before Mr. Tu came again.

The arrival of Mr. Tu, accompanied by a cold, sinister energy, interrupted Mo Hua’s thoughts, but Mo Hua feigned ignorance. It was only when Mr. Tu came to him that he seemed to realize it, standing up and cupping his hands, saying:

"Mr. Tu."

Mr. Tu saw the way Mo Hua had previously "contemplated deeply," studying formations, nodded slightly, and asked:

"How did you learn?"

Mo Hua presented several formation sketches to Mr. Tu, with a bitter expression, he said:

"It’s too difficult, I don’t really understand it, I can only copy it to this extent..."

Mr. Tu glanced over them, his heart jumped, slightly astonished.

As expected of the Qianxue Formation Path leader, acknowledged as a Formation Demon, to have mastered this ancient formation to this extent in such a short time.

Even if it’s just a case of mimicry, without understanding the true essence of formations, it still shows his astonishing comprehension.

Mr. Tu said, "Not bad, keep drawing. Draw a hundred times, and its meaning will reveal itself."

"Yes."

Then Mo Hua followed Mr. Tu’s instructions, continuing from the basics, drawing the ancient formation patterns at the core of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation.

Mo Hua also controlled the progress of his "learning" of formations step by step.

Simulating a "gradual" process, he presented his progress, from shallow to deep, little by little for Mr. Tu to see.

Mr. Tu’s face was deathly pale, expression indifferent, but the occasional flicker in his eyes still betrayed appreciation and delight.

As Mo Hua learned the formations better, his demeanor towards Mr. Tu became increasingly respectful.

As if Mr. Tu were a respected "instructor."

And he himself was an industrious and studious "disciple."

The two of them teaching and learning, the atmosphere was harmonious.

Taking advantage of this period of harmony, Mo Hua asked Mr. Tu:

"Sir, to what category do these ancient formation patterns belong, and what is their meaning?"

About the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation, Mo Hua had always been somewhat confused.

Though the entire Mysterious Heaven Great Formation was fused within Mo Hua’s Sea of Consciousness.

Most of the formation patterns he was familiar with, could even paint them easily.

But this level is really just mimicry, knowing its expressions, but not its essence.

The formation patterns in the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation are too unique, obscure, strange, full of confusion, like a series of "riddles" woven into a vast "mist of mystery," concealing some unknown terrible truths.

This is Mo Hua’s impression of the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation.

If only Master were still here, he could ask Master for guidance as he usually did, to clear up some of his doubts.

But now alone, such life-concerning "secrets," he didn’t dare reveal a single shred.

And now, Mo Hua discovered the Great Wilderness Evil God was also constructing the "Mysterious Heaven Great Formation" under the guise of a Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice.

Which means, apart from him, there were "gods" and "people" studying the Mystery Formation, and they’ve already practically applied this set of Mysterious Heaven Great Formation.

Although this is an Evil Path formation, it’s also a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity.

Must seize this opportunity to figure out some deeper mysteries.

The Mysterious Heaven Great Formation is of great importance, not only related to the current situation but also to his future in Tao cultivation.

In Mo Hua’s heart, there was always a vague worry.

His cultivation technique was the ancient Heaven Yan Jue.

The bottleneck of Heaven Yan Jue cultivation is not in Spirit Stone, not in Spiritual Objects, needing no Heavenly Materials and Earthly Treasures, but has a rigid threshold:

Divine Sense.

Or in other words, the formation derived from Divine Sense.

Once, when he broke through the Foundation Establishment, the bottleneck in his Sea of Consciousness was the entire Mysterious Heaven Great Formation.

Now in the Foundation Establishment Late Stage, craving the Golden Core, the bottleneck of forming the core would inevitably still be related to this Mysterious Heaven Great Formation.

The Heaven Yan Jue, this ancient skill, is rather unique.

The stronger the Divine Sense, the stronger the Heaven Yan Jue, correspondingly, the stronger the bottleneck.

Mo Hua now, his Divine Sense sublimated, his Divine Thought is as powerful as a deity’s.

Therefore, cultivating Heaven Yan Jue, breaking through the bottleneck of the Golden Core, would not be simple.

Given the opportunity now, it must be used to thoroughly comprehend this Mysterious Heaven Great Formation, to avoid unpredictable changes when forming the core, failing to achieve the Golden Core and being stuck at the Foundation Establishment Late Stage for a lifetime.

Mr. Tu glanced at Mo Hua, seeing him ask sincerely, and after briefly pondering, decided not to conceal the truth.

Mr. Tu first countered back to Mo Hua:

"Do you know about Mystery Formation?"

Mo Hua nodded, "Formation used for puzzle-solving, enlightenment for children by aristocratic families, for entertainment and mental self-improvement."

Mo Hua thought he answered well.

Unexpectedly, Mr. Tu was stunned, "Who taught you that?"

Master always told me that since I was little...

Mo Hua swallowed this sentence, puzzled: "Isn’t it the case?"

Mr. Tu frowned, "How is it possible? What kind of aristocratic family would use Mystery Formation as a means for entertainment and enlightenment?"
