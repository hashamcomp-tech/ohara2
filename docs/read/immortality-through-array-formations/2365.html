Chapter 2365: Chapter 1080: Maniacal Madness (Part 3)

Godslaying Sword!

The enormous shock battered True Man Situ’s heart; his face was full of disbelief:

"This kid... how could he possibly know the Godslaying Sword?!"

"Is he the heir of Patriarch Dugu of Taixu Gate?"

"That’s impossible too..."

"He’s so young, at such a low Realm, how could he have learned the Tai Xu God-Slaying Sword that only Feather Transformation Realm genius Sword Cultivators of Taixu Gate are qualified to even glimpse?!"

"What’s more, this doesn’t even look like an ordinary Godslaying Sword, this sword is way too big..."

"What in the world... is going on?"

True Man Situ was lost and shaken, his mind in a daze.

On the other side, the Evil God True Embryo, whose face had been full of mockery, also changed color.

From this sword form, It felt a sliver of fear.

This was a terrifying sword move created at great cost by apex human Cultivator powerhouses, specially designed to erase Divine Thoughts, even sufficient to slay a deity.

"Despicable heretic, you’ve been plotting this for a long time, trying to use this sword move to regicide me. Everything before this was just you looking for an opening..."

The Evil God True Embryo’s heart flared with anger, then It looked at Mo Hua and sneered coldly, "An Evil God does not die; you cannot kill me."

"If you do not treat the living beings as Straw Dogs, you cannot forge a divine spark."

"Your deity Dao is far too shallow."

Mo Hua frowned slightly. After pondering for a moment, a flash of insight arose; his mind suddenly cleared, and he understood his own "Dao."

He looked at the Evil God True Embryo of the Great Wilderness, his gaze deep as he slowly said:

"Heaven and earth are not benevolent; they treat all things as Straw Dogs."

"Deities are also among the ten thousand things; that means you’re a Straw Dog too."

"If all living beings are your sacrifices, then you... are my sacrifice!"

As these words fell, the Heavenly secret trembled; in the unseen, mysterious changes of karma stirred, and a wisp of faint, indistinct Heavenly Dao Laws merged into Mo Hua’s whole body, branding themselves into Mo Hua’s Life Chart.

If deities take all living beings as their sacrifices.

Then he will take deities as his sacrifices.

The Evil God True Embryo’s pupils violently trembled, then It instantly flew into a rage, Its expression ferocious, yet in Its eyes, It could not hide the panic as It shrieked loudly:

"Insolence! Arrogance! Ignorance! What are you?!"

Yet in Its heart, a vast, vague horror and dread arose.

"This is absolutely no ordinary heretic!"

"I must strangle this heretic completely here and now, to cut off all future trouble!"

At any cost!

The True Embryo of the Great Wilderness Evil God stared with an utterly terrifying gaze, and even at the cost of depleting Its Vital Energy, began to draw upon Its Origin Power.

A deeper, more profound Evil God Power merged into Its twin horns; between the horns, the Divine Skill Evil Power grew even darker, even carrying a trace of flickering that resembled the void itself.

This power made True Man Situ’s face go completely bloodless.

On the other side, Mo Hua’s expression was equally grave.

But the arrow was nocked and had to be loosed; his Godslaying Sword had already reached full charge, the entire sword body like a heaven-piercing sword, vast and boundless.

In sheer momentum, Mo Hua and this juvenile Evil God were evenly matched.

But in the Origin Power of Divine Thought, Mo Hua was still slightly inferior; if they truly went all out, life and death would be hard to predict.

Just as both sides’ killing moves were about to collide—

Mo Hua’s expression suddenly turned icy, his pupils abruptly turning pitch-black, and an ancient, sinister, demonic aura, one that could sever self, sever emotion, and even slaughter all things, swiftly merged into the Godslaying Sword.

This change in Sword Dao Law was too fast, so fast that True Man Situ didn’t react at all.

The Evil God True Embryo was suddenly stunned, then Its eyes snapped wide open; in those cold, malicious Evil God eyes was nothing but incredulity:

"The aura of a Heavenly Demon?!"

"How is that possible?"

"This brat... just what the hell is he?!"

Before It could react, in the next instant, the "insidious and despicable" Mo Hua let out a cold shout:

"Slash!"

With pure gold Taoization, his Twenty Patterns Golden Core Divine Thought fully fused the Qianxue Five Elements Sword Formation, the Taixu origin-flow Sword Intent, and the terrifying Heavenly Demon Path, condensing all these complex and profound Laws completely into one, forging the largest and strongest "Taixu God-Slaying Giant Sword" Mo Hua had ever wielded.

With that one word—"Slash."

The Giant Sword fell like a suspended Milky Way, the ends of the world overturning as it cleaved down, crashing head-on into the Evil God’s terrifying Divine Skill killing move.

Five-colored interweaving golden light and black-red surging Evil light billowed like world-drowning tsunamis.

The entire nightmare of the Evil God shuddered and warped.

True Man Situ, caught in the aftermath, felt as if he stood at the center of a terrifying Divine Thought storm. His Primordial Spirit, like a candle in the wind, wavered on the verge of annihilation, yet time and again clung to a sliver of life, as though someone were deliberately protecting him so he would not truly be wiped out.

But in True Man Situ’s heart, monstrous waves still surged.

This was the first time in his life he had ever seen a human’s Divine Thought rampage like this, as violent as storm and tidal wave.

After a long period of turbulence, the storm of Divine Thought finally subsided.

As his shaken soul calmed, True Man Situ slowly opened his eyes, only to see that the Evil God’s nightmare altar had already been reduced to ruins.

In the ruins, Mo Hua stood with the Giant Sword in his hand.

And opposite him, the Evil God’s True Embryo had been forcibly cleaved in two from shoulder to abdomen.

True Man Situ’s expression trembled, unable to believe it for a moment. "He actually... split the Evil God open?"

But an instant later, the Evil God’s eyes moved, and True Man Situ’s heart clenched.

Not dead!

This Evil God—It still wasn’t dead!

In the end, Mo Hua’s Realm was still too low. Although the Godslaying Sword had split the Evil God, and the Heavenly Demon Path had prevented the Evil God’s Divine Body from knitting back together, he still could not sever Its Origin.

The Evil God True Embryo’s Divine Body was half-split, Its Origin damaged; from the moment of Its birth, this was the first time It had ever tasted the feeling of "lingering fear."

That mighty Divine Thought flesh-body.

That powerful Godslaying Swordsmanship.

That ancient, bizarre Heavenly Demon Path.

This "heretic" before It—whether he was man, deity, or Demon—was a being enough to make deities themselves feel wary.

However...

The Evil God True Embryo looked at Mo Hua and sneered, "An Evil God does not die. I told you, you cannot kill me..."

Mo Hua’s face was a shade pale, and he nodded. "I know, I can’t kill you."

After he finished speaking, Mo Hua licked his lips.

The Evil God sensed danger; Its face changed. "You... what are you going to do?"

Mo Hua said not a word. He raised the "Giant Sword" condensed from Divine Thought, charged forward, and directly hacked the Evil God’s True Embryo into pieces, black fluid spilling all over the ground.

Then Mo Hua opened his mouth and drew a fierce breath, sucking the Evil God’s "blood" and "flesh" alike into his mouth and swallowing it all down.

Seeing this scene, True Man Situ was so terrified his heart and gall almost burst.

He had never imagined, even in his dreams, that someone in this world would take a newborn Evil God, carve It into eight pieces, and then directly...

Eat It alive?!

This utterly deranged, savagely terrifying scene crashed into True Man Situ’s aged heart, making his whole body crawl with cold goosebumps...
