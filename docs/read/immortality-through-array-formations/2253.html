Mo Hua is the "culprit" who slaughtered their descendants.

Mo Hua is the source of the crisis.

The "instigator" of all this.

"Die!"

The five Heaven Void Dharma Phases, with golden light spreading across the sky, have stern brows and eyes, filled with a chilling killing intent. One commands a flying sword, another holds a heavy sword alone, one wields a longsword to open heaven, another punches to transform dragon, one dusts away the myriad laws, all aiming to kill Mo Hua, the insignificant Foundation Establishment.

They intend to crush Mo Hua, grind him into dust, leaving not even a trace of his Divine Soul!

Yet Mo Hua still stands with his head slightly raised, standing calmly, his gaze serene, without a hint of fear on his fair face.

Just at the moment when the five terrifying Heaven Void Dharma Phases are about to crush and annihilate Mo Hua.

The entire fifth-grade Mount of Tao large formation suddenly erupts with a sky-shaking brilliance.

Vast spiritual power, like a heavenly star river, is devoured by the fifth-grade array patterns, flowing along the formation pivot, powering the entire large formation with full force.

It is as if an ancient beast has awakened.

Between heaven and earth, a massive "humming" sound is heard, the sound of spirit roar when a large formation is activated and massive amounts of spiritual power are exchanged.

Above the Mount of Tao, for a moment the void is vast, resembling a galaxy.

Suddenly, a vast fifth-grade void large formation power descends from the sky, enveloping Mo Hua completely, shielding him from the suppression and killing of the five Heaven Void Dharma Phases.

The Heaven Void Dharma Phase originates from the special Eternal Life Rune, meant to protect the sect’s prodigy.

Mo Hua intends to kill five prodigies.

The Heaven Void Dharma Phase intends to suppress and kill Mo Hua.

However, the initial construction of the fifth-grade Mount of Tao large formation was to protect the sword ceremony disciples.

Mo Hua is a sword ceremony disciple.

Therefore, the fifth-grade Mount of Tao large formation will never allow these five Heaven Void Dharma Phases to harm Mo Hua, the sword ceremony disciple, within the large formation.

This is a contest at the Void Realm Level.

The power of the fifth-grade large formation clashes violently with the five Heaven Void Dharma Phases on Mount of Tao.

For a moment, heaven and earth change colors again, the vast starlight and dharma phase golden light slaughter each other, the astounding spiritual power covers the sky and earth, squeezing and entangling each other, stirring up a terrifying spirit tide, spreading towards the surroundings.

The entire Mount of Tao, all sword ceremony grounds, in an instant are swept flat by this terrifying spirit tide.

All the mountain forests and grounds turn to ashes.

The aftermath still cannot stop, breaking through around the large formation, resembling a ferocious ancient dragon wanting to devour all things in heaven and earth.

And this Void Realm Level spiritual power clash happens entirely within the fifth-grade large formation.

Naturally, it is also restricted by the fifth-grade large formation.

The fifth-grade large formation operates with all its might, the sound of "spirit roar," sharp and piercing, with a powerful formation power, tightly confines the spirit tide generated by the Heaven Void slaughter, not allowing it to spread outside.

Otherwise, the entire Mount of Tao would completely fall.

All cultivators beneath the Heaven Void will be crushed with not even a bone left by the spirit tide resulting from the collision between the fifth-grade large formation and the five Heaven Void Dharma Phases.

At this moment, all cultivators are isolated by the large formation, pale-faced and horrified, watching this terrifying scene unfold.

Watching the Heaven Void Level spiritual power, like a sky-covering tsunami, surging fiercely at them, only to be blocked by the large formation.

Their lives hang by a thread, yet they can do nothing.

In the face of such overwhelming and terrifying power, all living beings are like ants, whether they live or die is not for them to decide.

In the sky above Mount of Tao, half void, half golden light.

The confrontation between the fifth-grade large formation and the Heaven Void Dharma Phases continues.

The spirit tide continues to sweep in wave after wave.

And this is the fifth-grade state boundary, where the Heaven Void Realm can exert full power, the Heaven Void Dharma Phase created by the shattered Eternal Life Rune harbors immense hatred and won’t vanish so easily.

With the "instigator" Mo Hua not dead and the source of the karma not gone, the Heaven Void Dharma Phase won’t dissipate.

The fifth-grade large formation protects Mo Hua, against the five Heaven Void Dharma Phases, enduring wave after wave of Heaven Void Phase attacks.

But this Heaven Void killing intent seems endless.

They won’t rest until Mo Hua is killed.

Finally, the Mount of Tao large formation seems to have "angered."

It won’t allow anyone, any power, to violate the rules of the sword ceremony and harm Mo Hua.

An ancient sword humming sound rises to the sky.

Mount of Tao, White Jade Platform, surrounded by nine towering stone pillars, engraved with majestic divine beasts, covered with auspicious clouds and patterns.

In the center of the platform, a white jade ancient sword over ten meters long, resembling a small mountain peak, is enshrined.

The sword body is transparent and crystalline, emitting a timeless and vast aura.

This sword is the relic of Mount of Tao’s ancestor.

Before each sword discussion, the sword is worshipped.

The altar is opened, offerings are made, blood is smeared, the seal is opened, all Qianxue Debate disciples sincerely worship this sword, to comfort the ancestor’s spirit, seeking Mount of Tao ancestor’s protection.

At this moment, on the White Jade Platform, the auspicious beast pattern’s light suddenly appears.

The massive Dao Discussion Ancient Sword emits a low hum, its jade-like sword qi shoots towards the nine heavens, resonating with the fifth-grade Mount of Tao large formation.

Above the Mount of Tao’s sky, suddenly an elder’s shadow emerges.

This elder’s shadow is formed by the power of the formation, the features are indistinct, but the body is deep and vast like a galaxy, majestic like a giant standing in the sky.

The giant elder, standing tall, waves a hand condensed by void power, grasping the white jade ancient sword as though catching a small mountain peak, then swings it horizontally, the sword qi like the inverted Milky Way, unstoppable, directly slicing the five Heaven Void Dharma Phases in half.

With just this one sword, in an instant all five Heaven Void Dharma Phases are completely extinguished, their spiritual power dissipating invisibly.

The elder returns the sword to the White Jade Platform, and his form gradually dissolves into the world.

The fifth-grade Mount of Tao large formation gradually calms down.

The piercing sound of spirit roar vanishes.

The terrifying spirit tide begins to fade, cease, eventually absorbed and refined completely by the large formation.

Heaven and earth return to silence.

In front of the large formation, all the watching cultivators, including all the esteemed guests in the Sword Observation Tower, are left with lingering shock on their faces, their hearts numb and dazed.

In just half a day, scenes of extreme terror unfolded one after another.

They almost believed that what they were witnessing was not the debate of the prodigies, but a terrifying battle at the Heaven Void Level...
