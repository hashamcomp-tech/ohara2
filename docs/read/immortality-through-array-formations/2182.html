Chapter 2182: Chapter 1022: One Sword from Heaven

First in Qianxue, Formation Leader.

This title echoed in the hearts of most of the cultivators watching outside, weighing heavily on their minds.

Even though this was a Sword Discussion Conference, the words "Leader," even in formations, were not to be underestimated.

On the elevated platform of the Shangguan Family, seeing the formation drawn by Mo Hua withstand the terrifying Sword Qi of the Heavenly Sword Sect, Yu Er was revitalized, her eyes sparkling.

Wenren Wan, Zhang Lan, and the others all felt invigorated.

The disciples of the Taixu Gate were also visibly thrilled.

The elders observing from the Taixu Three Mountains also slowly straightened up, intensely focused on the scene.

Was there a turning point?

The Earth-level sword discussion was extremely difficult, and the Taixu Gate had suffered many defeats.

The sect from top to bottom desperately longed for a victory.

Now that a turning point had appeared, all cultivators of the Taixu Gate felt their hearts beat faster, filled with both expectation and anxiety.

But some knew in their hearts, winning wouldn’t be easy.

After all, the opponent was the Heavenly Sword Sect.

Even though the situation was unfavorable, the Heavenly Sword Sect would never sit idly by; otherwise, they wouldn’t be worthy of being called one of the Four Sects.

Many cultivators in the audience understood this.

And sure enough, from the scene of the drawing, a prodigy from the Heavenly Sword Sect stepped forward.

This person was tall, with sharp features and an extraordinary aura.

Many people’s minds echoed with his name:

"Xiao Ruohan."

The first in the Heavenly Sword Sect was Xiao Wuchen.

Xiao Wuchen’s talent was astonishing, and his Sword Dao was exceedingly profound, even in the Heavenly Sword Sect, he was peerless.

But the Heavenly Sword Sect had deep roots, not having just one Xiao Wuchen.

Beneath Xiao Wuchen, there were still many Sword Dao prodigies.

Xiao Ruohan was one of the strongest among these prodigies besides Xiao Wuchen in the Heavenly Sword Sect.

Xiao Ruohan couldn’t compare with Xiao Wuchen, but compared to other talents of the Four Sects and Eight Gates, he was still among the best.

He was not any less of a prodigy than Linghu Xiao.

This sword discussion team was also led by Xiao Ruohan.

Since he was the elite, the prodigy, when facing a desperate situation, he had to shoulder great responsibilities and turn the tide.

Only thus could he bear the name of "prodigy" and be worshiped by thousands.

Moreover, his true goal was Xiao Wuchen.

The Taixu Gate’s team in front of him had been effortlessly defeated by Xiao Wuchen and had fallen to Xiao Wuchen’s subordinates.

And now, he was about to lose to the "subordinate" of Xiao Wuchen.

This was something Xiao Ruohan could not accept.

He wanted to win.

Not only win, but win in a spectacular manner, and also eliminate Mo Hua!

The team defeated by Xiao Wuchen, he absolutely could not lose to!

At the same time, he must be able to kill who Xiao Wuchen couldn’t!

Only in this way could he keep pace with Xiao Wuchen, gain the approval of the sect elders, and earn the praise of the thousands of cultivators who came to witness the Sword Discussion.

This Mo Hua, as the "Formation Leader," was both his stumbling block in the sword discussion.

And also his stepping stone to surpass Xiao Wuchen’s fame step by step.

Therefore, the gate before him must be broken.

No matter how slight the chance, he could not give up.

In the Sword Discussion Conference, where geniuses clash, often the outcome rests on a knife-edge. If you fight, then you might win.

If you don’t fight, you’re certain to lose!

Moreover, Xiao Ruohan also gradually calmed down.

He realized that in his rush, he might have been bluffed by Mo Hua.

This Mo Hua, his formations were indeed strong, but he shouldn’t be overestimated either.

In such a short time, to be able to draw two High-Rank formations was already unbelievable.

High-Rank formations consume a lot of Divine Sense and take a long time.

Even as the Formation Leader, he could not be so strong as to draw three Second Grade Nineteen Patterns High-Rank formations in a short time.

Merely by strong assault, breaking the formation again, can he charge in with momentum, and cut down Mo Hua, like destroying a city image.

Amidst the chaotic swords, if Mo Hua was slow for even a moment, and the Dao Discussion Jade shattered a moment late, death was certain.

"At all costs, kill!"

Xiao Ruohan’s eyes were cold, then without a moment of hesitation, he crushed the Golden Body Talisman and raised his sword to the sky.

The Golden Body Talisman protected him.

Spiritual power surged like a river, and Sword Qi raged like a stormy wave.

All spiritual power gathered in one body.

All Sword Qi converged into one sword.

His sword emitted a dazzling light, as if starlight was about to fall, radiant and dazzling, imbued with a chilling aura of slaughter.

The observing cultivators were in an uproar, exclaiming:

"One Sword from Heaven!"

"One of the supreme killing moves in the Heavenly Sword Technique..."

"This Xiao Ruohan has actually learned one of the Nine Heavenly Sword Techniques... truly remarkable..."

People shook their heads in amazement, "The Heavenly Sword Sect, after all, is still the Heavenly Sword Sect, worthy of being the leading Sword Dao Sect of Qian Learning State Boundary."

"Among the disciples, apart from Xiao Wuchen, there’s yet another Sword Dao prodigy like this."

"Xiao Ruohan is just slightly inferior to Xiao Wuchen, but it doesn’t mean he isn’t strong; he’s also a top-tier prodigy..."

"This move, One Sword from Heaven, he has yet to use it, right?"

"Indeed, until now, there hasn’t been an opponent in the Sword Discussion worthy of forcing this killing move."

"We’re in for a show..."

"The Taixu Gate’s sword discussion in this round... it’s uncertain again..."

Seeing Xiao Ruohan’s astonishing sword, the entire Taixu Gate, including Yu Er, Wenren Wan, Zhang Lan, all felt a shudder in their heart.

Their previously relaxed hearts tensed up once more.

Inside the Sword Discussion field.

The moment Xiao Ruohan raised his sword to gather power, Mo Hua immediately shouted:

"Interrupt his killing move!"

Linghu Xiao’s Sword Qi was the first to strike.

Then came Situ Jian’s Li Fire Sword.
