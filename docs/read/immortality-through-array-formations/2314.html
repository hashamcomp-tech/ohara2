Chapter 2314: Chapter 1063: Mystery Valley (Part 2)

"Other riddles are for intellectual entertainment. But the Mystery Formation has a far more crucial purpose..."

Mr. Tu’s gaze was solemn, "The so-called Mystery Formation is actually used for Formation Masters to discern the reality and falsehood."

Mo Hua’s pupils narrowed slightly, "Discern... reality and falsehood?"

Mr. Tu nodded, "The ’cover’ of the Mystery Formation is ’false,’ and the ’answer’ is ’true.’ Only by seeing through appearances can one understand the essence of the Formation."

"The ’cover’ is ’virtual,’ and the ’answer’ is ’real.’ Between the virtual and the real lies the profound Dao Laws of transforming virtual into real and vice versa..."

Seeing through falsehood and truth, transforming the virtual and the real.

Mo Hua’s expression changed dramatically, feeling a sudden enlightenment, as if he vaguely grasped a "tail" of a more profound Dao Laws amidst the turbulence in his Sea of Consciousness, glimpsing a corner of the brilliant deity’s Great Dao...

But when he tried to trace the roots and think deeply, he still couldn’t achieve a genuine comprehension and understanding.

Mo Hua knew it was due to his low Realm.

With a low Realm and no cultivation to support, many insights into the Great Dao couldn’t be actualized, transformed, or mastered to wield the power of the Dao truly.

Mo Hua could only temporarily bury this insight in his heart, like planting a seed in his mind.

In the future, if he experiences related events, he could verify and understand gradually.

Mo Hua digested for a moment and then asked Mr. Tu:

"Specifically, what is ’true,’ what is ’false’? What is ’real,’ what is ’virtual’? How do you differentiate between truth and falsehood? And how do you transform between virtual and real?"

Mr. Tu looked deeply into Mo Hua’s eyes, shaking his head, "It is too early for you to ask these questions; these are not things your current Realm can comprehend."

He pointed to the Formation Patterns Mo Hua had drawn, "The reason I told you this is because the core Formation Patterns you’re learning from this Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice are a kind of ’Mystery Formation’ involving truth, falsehood, reality, and the virtual..."

"What you see are merely the surface Formation Patterns, the ’cover’; the true ’answer’ involves the Laws of deity, obscured by the virtual and real, which you cannot see through."

"What you need to do is just to learn these ’covers’; by mastering these ’covers,’ the Laws of the answer are naturally contained within, allowing you to wield some part of the ’deity’s’ power."

"However, you can use this power, but do not delve into it."

"Do not gaze upon the deity, do not explore... the realm of deities, this would violate the taboo, attracting disaster."

Mr. Tu stared into Mo Hua’s eyes, his tone serious and grim.

Mo Hua’s face turned slightly pale, nodding:

"I understand."

Afterward, Mo Hua asked no more but followed Mr. Tu’s instructions, honestly learning only the Formation Patterns of the ’cover,’ knowing what they were but not probing into why they were.

And Mo Hua learned quickly too.

At first, he might have been clumsy, but soon, Mo Hua could replicate most of the Mystery Formation Patterns precisely, though they still lacked spirit.

Mr. Tu was secretly surprised, but given Mo Hua’s talent in Formations, it was not unexpected.

After the basic Formation Patterns came the Formation Pivot of the Mystery Formation.

Mr. Tu did not hold back, starting to pass on the structure of the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, including some core Second Grade Formation Pivots, meticulously to Mo Hua.

These Formation Pivots are drawn with human blood on human skin.

Mr. Tu handed the Formation Pivot skin map to Mo Hua, simply saying, "Study it well yourself, and if you have questions, then ask me."

After saying this, Mr. Tu left.

Within the cage of flesh and blood, only Mo Hua remained.

He endured the discomfort, spreading out the human skin, examining the Formation Pivots on it, immediately frowning.

"Something’s not right..."

These Formation Pivots were not as he anticipated and differed greatly from the structure of the "Mysterious Heaven Great Formation" in his Sea of Consciousness.

"Why?"

Mo Hua frowned, staring at the human skin map, comparing it with the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation in his mind, pondering for a long time, gradually understanding.

The Mysterious Heaven Great Formation is the core, the pivot, the essence of the entire Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation, a "Great Dao model" embodying true and false, virtual and real laws.

This "Great Dao model," despite containing profound and powerful laws, cannot "self-sustain" in its Formation structure.

The "Mysterious Heaven Great Formation" in Mo Hua’s Sea of Consciousness is formed with his own Dao foundation, using his Spiritual Power and Divine Sense as the "source," aided by the mystery of Heaven Yan Jue.

But to apply this Mysterious Heaven Great Formation in the real world, the issue of how to construct a "Formation eye" to "power" the Large Formation must be considered.

This is the most basic Formation logic.

Any Formation, from a Single Formation to a Compound Formation, and even massive Large Formations, must have a "Formation eye" as the core.

With Spiritual Power, Telekinesis, or perhaps Evil Power providing energy, only then can the Formation operate.

And Mr. Tu’s method is the "Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice."

Creating slaughter, letting blood inundate the earth veins.

Using human flesh and bone as a medium, refining the pain, piercing sorrow, fear, and despair from human Divine Soul into Telekinesis, merging them together, refining them into Evil Power, supplying the Mysterious Heaven Great Formation for the birth of the Evil God.

"Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice" is form, method, foundation, a medium for refining Evil Power, powering the Large Formation, and ensuring the operation of the Large Formation as a whole schematics of power circulation Formation Pivot system.

"Mysterious Heaven Great Formation" is core, law, absorbing the Evil Power obtained from the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice, transforming through laws, inversely changing reality, allowing the birth of the Evil God as a "cradle."

This entire Large Formation, with two closely fitted parts, is indispensable.
