Chapter 2245: Chapter 1041: Explosion! (Part 2)

It’s not that they are weak; it’s just that Mo Hua’s formation is indeed peculiar.

Even a punch from his Dragon Cauldron Body Refining Skill could only waste some Formation Patterns but couldn’t break the Formation.

To break the Formation, substantial effort is necessary.

Ao Zhan’s eyes flickered, but he was unwilling to continue attacking. Instead, he stood to the side, waiting for others to make a move.

He didn’t want to waste too much Spiritual Power on breaking Mo Hua’s Formation.

Shen Linsu sneered, drew his longsword, and activated the purple-gold Jinlin Sword Qi, attacking Mo Hua’s Formation.

Then came Duanmu Qing’s Spell, Xiao Wuchen’s Heavenly Sword Technique, and finally Ye Qingfeng’s Daluo Unification Sword Skill.

The four prodigies, along with Daluo Sect’s Big Senior Brother Ye Qingfeng, each took a turn to break the formation.

For a while, powerful Spiritual Power fluctuations surged, and the Compound Formation’s light shifted as the Formation Patterns neared fragmentation, and the cracks grew more numerous...

In the valley, Mo Hua remained calm, engrossed in Drawing Formation, oblivious to everything around him.

Outside, as Qianxue’s Four Talents attacked in unison, many exclaimed:

"To force Shen Linsu and the other top four prodigies to join forces against his Formation, Mo Hua indeed has reason to be proud."

"Indeed..."

"Truly worthy of being the Formation Leader of the Qian Learning State Boundary among countless sects..."

People continued to marvel.

Thus far, the strength of Mo Hua’s Formation far exceeded their understanding.

They couldn’t grasp the deeper intricacies of the Formation Dao; even if they wanted to praise, they didn’t know how, eventually only able to sigh with a single phrase:

Worthy of being the Formation Leader.

Then someone said:

"Once the Four Talents take action, this Shura battle will head towards its conclusion."

"With the Taixu Gate extinguished, the Four Talents, along with Daluo Sect’s Ye Qingfeng, will settle the victory."

"Unexpectedly, the final battle of this Shura encounter arrived so quickly."

"Now, it’s just watching how long the Taixu Gate can hold..."

And as Qianxue’s Four Talents began to break the formation together within, Formation Patterns dimmed, the formations broke, and the Sealing Mountain Formation was slowly weakened.

The Taixu Sect Leader, Elders, disciples including Wenren Wan, Yu Er, Zhang Lan, Gu Changhuai, Murong Caiyun, Hua Qianqian, and other cultivators acquainted with Mo Hua couldn’t help but tense up.

Mo Hua and his team had already shown immense strength.

But alas, their opponents were equally formidable.

Sure enough, after 30 minutes, with a resounding crack, the ground shattered, and the Formation was completely broken.

The Sealing Mountain Compound Formation was destroyed, and the Thread of Heaven was breached.

The imposing and dominant Ao Zhan was the first to step into the valley.

Then followed by Qian Taoist Sect’s prestigious Shen Linsu, Wanxiao Sect’s cold and stunning Duanmu Qing, Heavenly Sword Sect’s ethereal Xiao Wuchen, and Daluo Sect’s restrained Ye Qingfeng.

Behind them, more than a hundred prodigies from the Four Great Sects and Seven Great Gates surged in like a tide, encircling the Taixu Gate.

It felt as though the situation reverted to before.

Taixu Gate against a hundred prodigies of Qianxue.

But this time, the enemies were incomparable to before.

Not only were there the top four Bloodline prodigies of Qianxue, but also Daluo Sect’s Big Senior Brother. The other disciples were largely the true "elites" of the Four Great Sects.

The numbers were similar, but the strength was several times greater than before.

This truly was a force of the most elite disciples gathered from the top Four Great Sects and Seven Great Gates in today’s Qian Learning State Boundary.

This group of prodigies was suffocating just to look at.

On the opposite side, Taixu Gate stood isolated and alone.

After the previous fierce battle, even with Mo Hua’s Five Elements Source Armor and Formation augmentation, Taixu Gate suffered a loss of ten men.

Now Taixu Gate only had twenty remaining.

Twenty against over a hundred, especially when included were Qianxue’s Four Talents among the hundred-plus cultivators, this was truly a despair-inducing situation.

But at this moment, the hundred-plus prodigies didn’t act recklessly.

Because before them lay a vast expanse of dense formations.

All these formations were Mo Hua’s "masterpieces," purely of Second Grade High-Level Formations, even including some Compound Formations, nearly filling the entire valley.

And Mo Hua continued to sit atop that large rock.

The remaining twenty disciples of the Taixu Gate stood guard like a human wall before Mo Hua.

Dense formations surrounded them.

From this setup, only by exterminating Taixu Gate’s disciples and treading over their corpses could they ultimately kill Mo Hua.

And to exterminate Taixu Gate’s disciples, they’d have to carve a bloody path through Mo Hua’s formations.

Faced with the dense array of formations, all disciples from the Four Sects and Seven Gates felt their scalps tingling.

They found it hard to believe.

How strong must Mo Hua’s Divine Sense be to draw so many formations in such a short time...

Is he truly a Foundation Building Cultivator?

But at this moment, it’s kill or be killed, with no room for idle talk.

Shen Linsu simply said:

"Kill..."

With a single command, the prodigies from the Four Sects and Seven Gates began rushing towards Mo Hua’s formations, attacking the Taixu Gate, aiming to kill Mo Hua.

As they stepped into the formations, the ground stones rose, forming barriers.

Earthen prisons erected, forming jails.

Quicksand sank, forming traps.

The vegetation sprouted, becoming thorns.

These stones, earthen prisons, quicksand, and vegetation were all manifestations of Spiritual Power from the formations, being Second Grade Nineteen Patterns close to Golden Core level, already taking some physical form.

These formations were like solid "fortresses," obstructing the advancing Qianxue prodigies.

Qianxue prodigies found themselves bogged down in these dense Defensive and Trapping Formations, struggling step by step.
