Chapter 2305: Chapter 1060: Monster (2)

Insufficient manpower, can only lower the grade and pattern, using a Second-grade Array to fill the structure of the Quasi-Third Grade Grand Array.

Similarly, up to now, the "resources" for the Blood Sacrifice Array are surely running short.

The Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation requires creating massive slaughter.

Only by creating massive slaughter can there be a large amount of human skin, human bones, human flesh, human blood... to be used as Formation media materials for the Evil Formation.

But now the claws of the Evil God are suppressed by the forces from all sides of Qianxue, unable to continue massacring Cultivators.

This means they fundamentally have no way to acquire more "resources" for the Evil Formation.

Even a clever woman cannot cook without rice.

In the case of scarce Evil Path resources, Mr. Tu has no choice but to use Righteous Dao’s array techniques to temporarily bridge as the "bridge" for the Formation Pivot operation.

Lowering the grade and combining the pattern results in unstable structures. Combining righteous and evil forces, Spiritual Power and Evil Power will also clash.

But at least this ensures that the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation is "completed" first.

Complete it first, then consider improvement, then consider perfection.

If it’s not completed, everything is just empty talk.

Mr. Tu is indeed an experienced and immensely knowledgeable old Formation Master, with practical and correct thinking.

Mo Hua nodded slightly.

If it were him, he would probably only reach this level too.

Of course, outwardly he still appeared confused, asking Mr. Tu some "idiotic" questions:

"Isn’t this a Third Grade Grand Array? Is my painting of Second-grade Array useful?"

"Can Righteous Dao formations be compatible with Evil Path formations? What if they’re incompatible?"

"On such a large foundation, why are there only these few Formation Masters painting formations? Where are the other Formation Masters? Have they been killed?"

Within these questions, some basic grand array principles are included, which Mo Hua not understanding is normal, Mr. Tu answered them one by one.

This also contains some "heart-wrenching" phrases, which Mr. Tu pretended not to hear.

After finishing, Mr. Tu handed over a Jade Slip to Mo Hua.

The Jade Slip contained the bulk of the foundational diagram of the Blood Sacrifice Array, with a large portion of the Evil Formation erased by Mr. Tu and replaced with Five Elements and Eight Trigrams formations, which were left specifically for Mo Hua to paint.

"Paint these formations on the cliff in front of you, this is your task," instructed Mr. Tu.

Mo Hua glanced at it, his face immediately showing displeasure, asked Mr. Tu:

"Didn’t you say I would participate in constructing the grand array? Painting these basic arrays isn’t it just making me do ’menial work’?"

I, the esteemed Qianxue Array Dao Leader, doing menial work for you?

Mo Hua expressed dissatisfaction.

Mr. Tu said: "Without accumulating small steps, you cannot reach a thousand miles; without accumulating small streams, you cannot form a river. The grand array is constructed from these fundamental formations. Learning grand arrays, one must begin from the basics, not aiming too high and losing the foundation."

Mo Hua reluctantly nodded, thinking it made sense.

But just as he was about to start working, he seemed to recall something, cautiously said:

"It’s not right, painting these arrays I’ll be equal to aligning with evil demons. If Sect Elders and those gentlemen find out, I’ll definitely be expelled from the Sect, and won’t be able to be a disciple at Taixu Gate in the future..."

Mr. Tu said: "You’re not painting an Evil Formation, it’s a Righteous Dao formation, how would you be expelled from the Sect?"

Mo Hua insisted: "But you are Demon Cultivators, I’m painting formations for you, isn’t this helping the tyrant?"

Mr. Tu slowly corrected: "You’re not ’helping’ us paint formations, you are being coerced; for survival, you have no choice but to paint some formations."

"You are ’coerced’, it’s not your fault."

Mr. Tu emphasized again.

Mo Hua startled, "Truly?"

Mr. Tu nodded, "Truly."

Mo Hua finally let go of his worries, but just as he was ready to begin, he again felt it wasn’t right, suspiciously looking at Mr. Tu:

"Once I’ve finished painting the formations, won’t you turn against me and take my life?"

Mr. Tu was secretly enraged, cursing this little brat for his excessive suspicion.

But at this moment, he could only persuade with kind words:

"Rest assured, your identity is special, it’s of great use to Divine Lord, I won’t kill you. If you can paint formations well, it would be a great achievement, Divine Lord will remember your merits and won’t treat you unfairly..."

So it was, with Mr. Tu’s persuasion, only then did Mo Hua agree to paint formations for him.

Mo Hua finally began his work, but after just sketching two strokes, he paused again, shaking his head:

"Still not right."

At this point, Mr. Tu couldn’t take it anymore, angrily said: "What is it now?"

Mo Hua pointed at the white bone hoop on his head, a little aggrieved and helpless, "My Divine Sense is sealed by you, I can’t paint formations..."

Mr. Tu froze, remembering this lad’s Sea of Consciousness was sealed by the Great Wilderness’s sacred item.

With his Sea of Consciousness sealed, he can’t communicate with deities, and his Divine Sense is limited, naturally unable to paint formations.

But... releasing his seal...

Mr. Tu felt uneasy in his heart.

Yet after hesitating for a moment, he ultimately considered the bigger picture, prioritizing the grand array, extended his pale finger, touched Mo Hua’s forehead, blood thread seeped into the White Bone Tooth Talisman, unsealing Mo Hua.

In that instant, his mind became clear, a flash of brilliance appeared in Mo Hua’s clear eyes, then gradually receded, returning to nothing.

Mr. Tu felt a slight twinge in his heart, but upon a closer look, he detected nothing unusual about Mo Hua, then asked:

"Is it okay now?"

Mo Hua nodded, "No problem."

"Start painting formations."

"Alright."

Mo Hua picked up the brush, dipped it in Spiritual Ink, referred to the Formation Diagram Jade Slip given by Mr. Tu, and methodically began painting formations on the cliff.

With his mastery in formations, for these grand array foundational formations, just a glance would allow him to comprehend them deeply, no need for Mr. Tu to say much.
