Chapter 2324: Chapter 1066: Seven Stars Formations (Part 2)

The rebirth of the Evil God also involves choosing the "right person."

Someone like Yu Er, from an aristocratic family, who bears the Karma Dragon bloodline and has superior Spiritual Roots, is the perfect "Divine Fetus."

Which Evil God would be blind enough to choose someone like me, who has no bloodline, poor Spiritual Roots, a frail body, and comes from a poor background as a Loose Cultivator for rebirth?

My family was so poor when I was young that not even thieves would pay us any mind, let alone the Evil God.

If the Evil God really chose me, they would have to experience the hardships of life from birth, which is truly not worth it.

Upon thinking this, Mo Hua felt much more at ease.

He began to concentrate and continued to familiarize himself with the environment, starting to study the Formation within the Blood and Flesh Maze.

The more he studied, the more he realized.

Yet he dared not study for too long, nor explore too deeply.

Because Mr. Tu could return at any moment, and if he found out that I could break the Seal and run around, he would certainly become extremely vigilant, and perhaps even furious enough to kill me on the spot.

Therefore, it was wiser to be cautious and stop while ahead.

Thus, Mo Hua wandered around for a while, noting many details and questions about Formations, and then returned to the Blood and Flesh prison, obediently wearing the White Bone head ring, his eyes fixed on the Formation Pivot left by Mr. Tu, pretending to study while pondering deeper questions internally.

After a while, a cold aura descended.

Mo Hua immediately sat up straight.

He knew that Mr. Tu was back.

Sure enough, within moments, Mr. Tu appeared outside the door.

Mr. Tu indeed had been delayed by certain matters, and upon his return, the first thing he did was "inspect the room," checking Mo Hua’s condition.

Seeing everything intact, with Mo Hua wearing the White Bone Holy Object, obediently studying the Formation he had set up, Mr. Tu finally felt relieved.

He even thought that he might have been overly cautious.

After all, it was a prestigious Holy Object from the Great Wilderness used to suppress a mere Foundation Building Cultivator; there’s no way it couldn’t keep him in check.

Mo Hua saw Mr. Tu and, fearing he might overthink, quickly asked with a worried expression:

"Mr. Tu, you seem hurried. Did something happen?"

Mr. Tu froze for a moment, his pupils contracted, "Something did happen."

"What happened?" Mo Hua asked quietly.

Mr. Tu frowned, "The Mystic Sky Beidou Seven Star Formation..."

Mo Hua was shocked, "What?"

Mr. Tu also paused, then furrowed his brows tightly with confusion in his heart.

Why did he feel like answering this kid’s question when he asked? Why mention this Formation to him?

Mr. Tu shrugged it off with a shake of his head, "Nothing."

Seeing Mo Hua looking as curious as a cat smelling fish, seemingly wanting to ask more, Mr. Tu first countered his question:

"How is your study of the Formation?"

Mo Hua’s curiosity was indeed interrupted.

When Mr. Tu asked a question, Mo Hua usually had to think carefully before answering, otherwise he might slip up, which would not be conducive to his subsequent plans.

Originally, Mo Hua wanted to say, "This Formation Pivot is too difficult, I haven’t learned it yet," but mid-sentence, he thought of something else and changed his answer temporarily:

"It’s okay, I understood a little bit."

Mr. Tu’s gaze narrowed.

Without utilizing human skin, bones, and blood to activate Divine Sense, enhance Formation skills, and lower barriers, he can still comprehend this highly arcane Blood Sacrifice Formation Pivot...

How exactly did he understand it?

Mr. Tu frowned.

Back in the day, he couldn’t comprehend the essence of this Blood Sacrifice Formation relying solely on his own strength, so he succumbed to the Evil God’s "bewitchment," making a fatal mistake that led to his current plight. Hence, he was deeply resentful of Mo Hua’s kind of "insight."

Feelings of jealousy reignited.

Mr. Tu struggled to suppress the jealousy buried within.

The rebirth of the Divine Lord is a significant matter, and he cannot let narrow-mindedness disrupt the grand plan laid out for the Divine Lord.

Moreover, he truly didn’t have much time to waste on Mo Hua.

"Keep studying."

Mr. Tu left that sentence and then departed.

Mo Hua then followed his instructions, continuing to study the Blood Sacrifice Formation Pivot.

In the days that followed, Mr. Tu would still check on Mo Hua every so often to ensure he didn’t cause any disruptions.

However, these monitoring sessions grew increasingly infrequent.

Mo Hua’s "free activity" opportunities also increased.

Apart from the deepest part of the Maze, which Mo Hua hadn’t ventured into and dared not go, he had visited almost all the surrounding areas, and the structure of the Formation Pivot had been thoroughly examined.

To others, those intricate mazes, writhing blood and flesh, and identical bone walls might appear gory and oppressive, making it impossible to distinguish anything.

But in Mo Hua’s eyes, they felt as familiar as his own "home."

Just by glancing, he could determine, based on the aura of the flesh and the internal Formation Patterns, which piece of flesh corresponded to which location in the Maze.

Observing the surface, he discerned the essence of the Formation.

Using Formation Patterns, he identified positional layouts.

To know what others don’t, that is the power of a Formation Master.

And since Mr. Tu appeared less frequently, seemingly busier and more troubled, Mo Hua speculated that he must have encountered some difficulties.

"Mystic Sky Beidou Seven Star Formation..."

Mo Hua muttered quietly.

This name, he heard from Mr. Tu.

In recent days, it seemed this Mystic Sky Beidou Seven Star Formation exerted immense pressure on Mr. Tu, making him look solemn and distracted from other matters.

"Seven Stars..."

Mo Hua frowned, gradually recalling the Formation knowledge taught by Elder Master Xun in Taixu Gate’s Formation classes:

"The Seven Star Formation is a major category of Formation, crafted based on the Big Dipper formations—Tian Shu, Tianxuan, Phecda, Tianquan, Yuhen, Kaiyang, Yaoguang—to condense celestial star patterns, thus constructing the Formation."
