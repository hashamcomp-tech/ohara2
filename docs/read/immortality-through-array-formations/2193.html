Chapter 2193: Chapter 1025: Blow It All Up_3

Who is this disciple?

What outrageous thing did he do again?

Everyone knew it well inside.

After all, such a big "firework" launched on the Fang Tian Painting Shadow, anyone could see it.

Mo Hua had truly set a precedent.

Yet, he was still rather depressed.

Mo Hua sighed.

He felt targeted once again.

He had actually planned it all out.

Through this Sword Discussion, he roughly estimated the explosive power generated by the High-Rank Formation within the city walls.

This power was truly immense.

The kind that leads to mutual destruction.

But this was the Sword Discussion, he wouldn’t really die anyway, so "mutual destruction" didn’t matter much.

Moreover, as long as the city wall exploded, he would surely win.

Next time in a siege battle like this, using the Killing Formation to ignite and explode the city walls, no one could stop him.

Even if it were Xiao Wuchen and Ao Zhan from the Four Great Sects, if they were unlucky enough to encounter him during a siege battle,

Once the Killing Formation was triggered and the city wall exploded, they would also meet their end.

In the face of a massive explosion, all are equal.

No matter how high their cultivation or strong their Taoist Skill, it wouldn’t matter.

"What a pity..."

This great plan had yet to be implemented and was already rooted out entirely.

No one gave him another chance to exploit the loophole.

"The elders of Mount of Tao are really petty..."

Mo Hua muttered to himself.

However, the matter had reached this point, and his dissatisfaction was of no use.

He could only suppress his "explosive" impulse and refocus on the upcoming Sword Discussion.

...

Meanwhile, at Qian Taoist Sect.

Shen Cangfeng, a prodigy of the Qian Taoist Sect, felt equally resentful and frustrated.

This is the Sword Discussion Conference!

He didn’t even get to meet Mo Hua.

Not even a single sword was swung.

Then the entire person, along with the whole city, was blown away with a "boom."

By the time he regained his senses, he had already lost.

The Heaven and Earth Splitting Sword Skill he had spent years refining didn’t even have the opportunity to display itself.

Shen Cangfeng was nearly spitting blood and silently vowed:

"Mo Hua, one day I will tear you to pieces..."

...

Thus, within the Tumo Order, there was yet another person named "Fallen before drawing the sword," silently following the crowd, repeating:

"Mo Hua must die!"

However, for Mo Hua, being the one involved, these felt less apparent.

Moreover, he focused solely on the Sword Discussion, with no mind to deal with such matters.

In contrast, the meticulous Situ Jian saw everything clearly.

Only he knew how long the list of grudges in his Junior Brother’s "Sword Discussion Enmity Record" really stretched...

Situ Jian quietly sighed.

...

Afterward, there were two more Earth-level Sword Discussions.

One with the Eight Great Gates, another with the Four Great Sects.

The opponents were not weak.

But during the siege battle with Qian Taoist Sect, Mo Hua leveled the entire Sword Discussion arena using a few Killing Formations, truly causing all sects to break into a sweat.

Although afterward, the elders of Mount of Tao explained that it was a "coincidence," a result of the imbalance of formations within the city walls causing the explosion.

It wasn’t Mo Hua’s fault.

Theoretically, it was indeed impossible for Mo Hua to have done it.

Yet other sects were still cautious.

They placed great emphasis on Mo Hua’s formations.

The deployment of all tactics practically revolved around one point:

To limit Mo Hua’s formations.

With Mo Hua’s formations being targeted, Linghu Xiao felt less pressured and was instead liberated.

After many failures and difficult duels against prodigies, Linghu Xiao’s swordsmanship was also honed, becoming increasingly powerful step by step.

Mo Hua absorbed a lot of firepower through his formations.

Linghu Xiao just happened to unleash Sword Qi, wreaking havoc everywhere.

Besides, Ouyang Xuan had decent talent, and his swordsmanship was not weak; although he wore a sullen face, he also helped quite a bit.

As for Cheng Mo and Situ Jian, they seemed rather ordinary.

But having faced off against so many top prodigies, both their mentality and Taoist Skill had been tempered.

Although unable to truly rely on strength to stand alone, they became much smoother and more natural in coordination and support, showing a bit of a "seasoned veteran" demeanor.

Moreover, even when facing top prodigies, they showed no fear.

If they could fight, they would, if not, they would find an opportunity.

Winning was naturally good, and they did their best even when losing.

This down-to-earth mentality also made their demeanor more resolute, and their performance in the Sword Discussion increasingly steady.

Thus, through Mo Hua’s planning and the concerted battles of Linghu Xiao and others, the five-man team secured two more victories in the Earth-level Sword Discussion.

The Sect Leaders of the Three Mountains, especially those of Tai’a and Chongxu Mountains, felt more pleased and agreeable when looking at Mo Hua.

Previously, there were rumors that Mo Hua was the "illegitimate child" of the Taixu Sect Master. They found it amusing at heart, but now they genuinely felt envy.

But the overall ranking for Taixu Gate in the Sword Discussion was still not too optimistic.

Thanks to these two victories, Taixu Gate’s victories were slightly stabilized.

But behind them, Daluo Sect began to catch up.

Daluo Sect, a Sword Dao Sect.

Over two thousand years ago, Daluo Sect ranked among the "Four Great Sects" in Qianxue, known then as Daluo Sword Sect.

But later, it fell out of the Four Great Sects and never climbed back up.

Throughout the past two thousand years, Daluo Sect has been among the top sects within the Eight Great Gates.

The strongest sect among the Eight Great Gates today.

Currently, Daluo Sect is closely trailing Taixu Gate in rankings.

A slight slack, and Taixu Gate would drop from fifth to sixth place.

Which also means Taixu Gate would lose its title as "The First among the Eight Great Gates."

Now, as the Earth-level Sword Discussion progresses, there are not many rounds left.

Truly, it’s one round less each time.

If Mo Hua can win them all, it would be fine.

But any loss could allow Daluo Sect to overtake, which would be troublesome.

Thus, the upcoming matches are almost all tough battles...
