Chapter 2251: Chapter 1043: Sever!

In the Sword Observation Tower, the Master of Hundred Flower Valley saw Mo Hua in this state and felt a sharp pain in her chest, her eyes moist, almost unable to breathe.

Inside the Tian Shu room, the Supervisor looked at Mo Hua with an unruly and disdainful world-conquering demeanor, caught off guard and puzzled.

He couldn’t figure out who Mo Hua resembled in his "youth"...

Inside the sword discussion arena.

Mo Hua’s words were extremely arrogant, looking down on everyone.

Even Shen Linsu and the others, born with a haughty disdain for the mundane, were thoroughly enraged.

Shen Linsu couldn’t help but sneer:

"If you used this sword earlier, wouldn’t killing the five of us be better?"

Mo Hua said contemptuously, "The power of this sword is immense, if used early, it would cut down those mediocre ones as well."

"My sword does not cut mediocrity, only you few are worthy to die under my sword."

Shen Linsu felt a surge of hot blood.

Even Duanmu Qing, Xiao Wuchen, and Ao Zhan, driven by hatred, their eyes slightly red.

"Good, good..." Shen Linsu laughed angrily, "Today I want to see how you strike this sword..."

"Let’s see how you, a Formation Master without a sword, strike down us five strongest prodigies of Qianxue."

Mo Hua nodded slightly, "Then you better open your eyes wide, make sure to watch carefully, make sure..."

Mo Hua’s voice carried a hint of eeriness, "...don’t blink."

Then he didn’t hesitate anymore, his eyes deep, and slowly pointed his fingers between his eyebrows.

...

Outside the sword discussion arena, everyone clearly saw Mo Hua’s strange motion and couldn’t help but frown.

"What does Mo Hua mean by this...?"

"What’s he trying to do?"

"Is he really trying to take on five and kill the five major prodigies of Qianxue?"

"Impossible, is he out of his mind?"

"The formation is gone; how can he kill?"

...

Elders and disciples of Taixu Gate, at this moment, were also looking at each other, puzzled, not understanding what Mo Hua was doing.

Only Xun Ziyou suddenly remembered something, his pupils widened.

He remembered this familiar action of Mo Hua.

Naturally, he also remembered the pig monster in Refining Demon Mountain, with not a single scar, but suddenly dropping dead in front of Mo Hua...

That third-grade Golden Core Realm Pig Monster...

Xun Ziyou felt his scalp explode, his whole body trembling uncontrollably.

In the Sword Observation Tower, inside the Heaven Void Hall.

Many Heaven Void Ancestors frowned slightly, equally shocked in their hearts.

They were Heaven Voids, unable to interfere with the disciples’ sword discussion beyond the fifth-grade formation, but still could sense the atmosphere inside through the formation.

Mo Hua’s flesh is weak, spiritual power is low, sword dao foundation is shallow, no sword qi on him, nor a single spirit sword in hand.

What sword technique could such a disciple know?

What bizarre trick is this strange kid playing?

Then, within an instant, an abyss-like sea of aura rose from Mo Hua. An ancient and deep sword intent slowly ascended.

Sensing this sword intent, all Heaven Voids were stunned.

"This aura is..."

Some Sword Dao Heaven Void Ancestors, after careful sensing, immediately changed color dramatically, looked incredulously at Elder Master Xun:

"Is it Taixu Gate’s... Divine Thought into sword?!"

This statement caused all Heaven Void Ancestors present to change dramatically.

They were Heaven Void Ancestors, having lived for many years, how could they not have heard of this shocking name?

Taixu Gate’s forbidden sword technique:

Taixu Mind Transforming Sword True Jue!

This Taixu Gate’s kid is using that near-lost and banned divine thought into sword True Jue that shakes the world and gods?!

How is this possible?!

This sword technique is a profound sect protecting sword technique.

Hard to learn, hard to practice, hard to pass on, if slightly careless, it could lead to divine obliteration and death.

How could a Foundation Establishment disciple possibly learn it?!

Who dares let a Foundation Establishment disciple learn it?

Even these Heaven Void Ancestors, deep-minded, couldn’t help but feel shocked internally at this moment.

Even Elder Master Xun frowned, his mind fluctuating.

His greatest fear has come true.

Mo Hua truly learned Divine Thought into sword True Jue.

Then this sword technique must have been taught by his senior brother, who is half-step into Heavenly Demon, unknown whether alive or dead, uncertain if demon or human.

And the sword technique, Mo Hua actually learned it indeed.

He became the only existing Taixu Gate divine thought into sword forbidden technique inheritor.

Elder Master Xun’s heart trembled, not knowing whether to be sad or happy.

Quickly, some ancestors realized a more severe issue.

An elder suddenly stood up, terrified: "Not good! Fifth-grade Mount of Tao formation does not defend against divine thought!"

"Quick! Stop him!"

"Under no circumstances let him strike this sword!"

Faced with Divine Thought into sword True Jue, all flesh and spiritual power are reduced to illusions.

No matter how good the spiritual root, how strong the flesh, if the Taoist Heart is not firm, divine thought not solid, cannot resist the erasure by Taixu Sword Intent.

That is to say, he truly can "kill" people inside the Dao Discussion Formation.

And who he wants to kill...

All Heaven Void Ancestors were shocked.

Immediately, several Heaven Void Ancestors forcefully acted, trying to surpass the fifth-grade formation to stop Mo Hua from striking this sword.

But their Heaven Void power touched the formation and was inevitably dissipated by the formation patterns, deep and complex, fused into the void.

The fifth-grade Mount of Tao formation prohibits anyone from interfering with the sword discussion process.

Even if these people are Heaven Void Ancestors.

Inside the sword discussion arena, as Mo Hua’s sword intent ascends.

Shen Linsu and the others instantly realized the danger, even sensing a hint of fear from the bloodline forewarning.
