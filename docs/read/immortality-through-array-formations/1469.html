Chapter 1469: Chapter 798 Eyes_2

Lord Yellow Mountain did not want to answer.

Especially about the matter of "Divine Thought into Sword," which was a lifelong pain.

But having eaten, it was difficult to refuse.

Moreover, the aura of majesty around Mo Hua was becoming more intense, and it dared not refuse.

"Ask, young friend, this minor god will tell you everything."

"Hmm." Mo Hua nodded and asked Lord Yellow Mountain the questions he had pondered early:

"Mountain Lord, for Divine Thought into Sword, must it be used with a sword?"

"Of course, otherwise, how can it be called Divine Thought into ’Sword’?"

"What I mean is, after transforming Divine Thought into ’Sword Intent,’ must it be attached to Sword Qi or Spirit Sword in order to be performed? Can it be used directly?"

"This..." Lord Yellow Mountain hesitated, somewhat troubled:

"I haven’t learned it, so I’m not sure, but the Sword Cultivator I fought did indeed use a sword to carry Sword Intent."

"Moreover, without relying on Sword Qi or Spirit Sword to perform Sword Intent, even if it’s possible, you would need to cultivate the Divine Thought into Sword method to a very advanced level..."

"If you are just starting, having just learned everything, and your Sword technique is not yet proficient, considering using Divine Thought Sword Intent directly without relying on Swordsmanship, isn’t it like trying to run before learning to crawl?"

Mo Hua realized, "That’s true..."

Sword Qi and Spirit Sword are like crutches.

With crutches, one can better control Sword Intent and activate the Divine Thought into Sword spell point.

Once cultivated to a deep level, with Sword Dao being extremely proficient, one can consider abandoning the crutches and relying solely on Divine Thought Sword Intent for attacks.

Furthermore, even then, when facing a strong enemy, one cannot solely rely on Sword Intent.

It still requires merging Spirit Sword, Sword Qi, and Sword Intent into one to unleash the greatest power of the Taixu Mind Transforming Sword True Jue.

"Taixu Divine Thought Sword, requires ’sword’ as a medium..."

Mo Hua muttered softly, and then asked again:

"If not using the sword as a medium, what is Divine Thought into Sword like? Is it like this..."

Mo Hua pointed his fingers, gesturing stylishly in the air.

"... With one point, ’whoosh,’ Divine Thought into Sword flew across, just like Sword Control?"

Lord Yellow Mountain rubbed his chin, "It should not be... but I don’t remember clearly."

"Think carefully," Mo Hua said, "recall more, especially when you were slashed by Divine Thought into Sword, what was the scene like."

Lord Yellow Mountain: "..."

This kid always likes to rub salt into people’s wounds.

So it endured the heartache and tried to recall, bringing out that unwanted memory of a deadly battle from almost forgotten recollections.

Little bits of past events surfaced in its heart.

The aura of Lord Yellow Mountain changed, seemingly no longer a destitute minor mountain god, its brows and eyes gaining an unfathomable majesty.

Lord Yellow Mountain spoke slowly:

"In that battle, I was polluted by evil thoughts, lost my mind, and fought to the death with that expert Sword Cultivator, almost battling purely by instinct."

"And back then, that Sword Cultivator relied on an ancient treasure sword bound to his life and spirit."

"His Sword Intent was vast, seemingly like the void, merged within the ancient sword, extremely sharp. With mere human flesh and the spell point of Sword Dao cultivators, he could injure me, this deity."

"I’m unfamiliar with this Sword technique, but from the experience of engaging him, the Divine Thought into sword True Jue is still considered a special type of Sword technique. It’s just that on the sword, it has the ’Sword Intent’ attached to the Divine Thought, and when performed, it still must involve the ’sword.’

"On the outside, it seems like an ordinary Sword Cultivator using swordsmanship."

"Only deities or cultivators in Divine Thought can perceive its might..."

Mo Hua sighed, somewhat disappointed, "No matter how you say it, still can’t get away from swordsmanship..."

This is considered his weakest among weaknesses.

Although his Sword Control is impressive, Mo Hua also knows in his heart, his "Sword Control" is merely surface-level, essentially not swordsmanship, but a method of "Divine Thought Object Manipulation," which merely appears like Sword Control.

"Not exactly..."

Lord Yellow Mountain frowned.

Mo Hua was astonished, "What do you mean?"

Lord Yellow Mountain tried recalling again, its gaze was dignified, and then it exclaimed:

"I remembered, when I fought that Sword Cultivator, my fierce nature erupted, and I once slapped his ancient sword away."

"That Sword Cultivator’s ancient sword was knocked out of hand, losing its dependency on the sword, immediately falling into a disadvantage. I wanted to seize the opportunity for a thorough kill, but at that moment, the Sword Cultivator’s eyes shone brightly..."

Mo Hua was shocked, "Eyes?"

"Yes," Lord Yellow Mountain nodded, "...Eyes shone brightly, as if Sword light condensed in them, extremely sharp. I just glanced, felt my eyes stinging, and my whole divine thought felt the pain of being ravaged by a thousand swords..."

"Taking advantage of this gap, that Sword Cultivator used Divine Sense Sword Control to summon the ancient sword back to his hand, reorganized his stance, and continued fighting with me."

"But this move of condensing sword light in the eyes, he only used it once at that critical moment. After that, when fighting to the exhaustion of each other’s oil and light, he never used it again."

"Which means..." Mo Hua’s gaze widened, "In the true technique of Divine Thought into Sword, even without a sword, one can use ’eyes’ to perform the attacking Sword Intent?"

Lord Yellow Mountain shook his head, "I don’t know, I just saw that person use this move, but only that one time."

"I don’t even know if this is a move from Divine Thought into Sword true jue..."

But regardless of what Lord Yellow Mountain said, Mo Hua already considered along this line of thought by himself.

Eyes, sword light, attacks...

Yet he still couldn’t understand, so he asked Lord Yellow Mountain:

"Why the eyes? Not the mouth, nose, or ears? Because the eyes are the window of Divine Sense?"

Lord Yellow Mountain nodded, "Correct, a cultivator’s Divine Sense resides in the Sea of Consciousness, can sense external surroundings through externalization, and the strongest place to externalize a cultivator’s Divine Sense is the eyes."
