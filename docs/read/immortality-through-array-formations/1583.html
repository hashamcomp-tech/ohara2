Chapter 1583: Chapter 835: White Dog (Part 2)

Mo Hua raised an eyebrow and pointed at it:

"Vile creature, such nerve!"

The big dog seemed to understand a little human nature, and knew Mo Hua was scolding it. Immediately, its white hair stood on end, it slowly stood up, looking at Mo Hua with a fierce gaze.

When it stood up, it revealed its figure, taller than Mo Hua by a head, baring its teeth, indeed somewhat intimidating.

Yet Mo Hua remained unafraid.

Because the dog was chained.

Even though he was shorter by a head, he still faced off with this Big White dog with a commanding presence.

But after watching for a moment, Mo Hua felt a bit conflicted.

What to do next?

Beat up the dog?

Or find a way to slaughter it, stew it into a meat pot, and calm Yu Er?

As if sensing Mo Hua’s "great disrespect," this Big White dog suddenly became enraged, its expression turning even more hideous, a powerful, intimidating pressure spreading from its body.

Within its vertical pupils, something seemed to be swirling with light.

Mo Hua was startled, feeling uneasy in his heart.

This Big White dog doesn’t seem like an ordinary dog?

Not a Monster Beast, nor does it resemble a Spirit Beast, could it be... some kind of special Exotic Beast?

Exotic Beast? Likely not easy to deal with...

Should he just let it go?

As soon as this thought emerged, it seemed the big dog sensed it.

It looked at Mo Hua with a gaze mixed with disdain and contempt.

Seeing this, Mo Hua became angry.

A mere guard dog, actually looking down on me?

Fine!

Can’t fight, can’t kill, can’t stew, then I’ll scare you, make you suffer a bit, know the mountain beyond the mountain, the person beyond the dog!

Mo Hua snorted coldly, focusing his gaze, Divine Thought Manifestation Soul-Shocking Sword, operating Seven Souls Pupil Skill, sharp light erupted from his eyes, like Sword Qi swirling, threads of evil leaked out.

Soul-Shocking Sword!

This was the first time he performed the Soul-Shocking Sword Skill, not to a person, but to a dog.

According to Mo Hua’s expectation, this big dog should instantly be blinded, then howl for mercy.

But this Big White dog actually had its vertical pupil tremble, pure white light swirling in its eyes, transforming into a blurry virtual shadow, rising and falling, constantly changing.

In front of the secluded and beautiful library pavilion, one person and one dog glared at each other.

One is a young boy, one is a Big White dog, both opposing each other.

It seemed childish, but where their gazes crossed, invisible to ordinary Cultivators, faint golden sword light and pure white virtual shadow constantly entangled, ebbing and flowing, slaughtering without end.

Moments later, it was a draw.

Both sides gave up.

Mo Hua pointed at it and said: "You wait for me, after class tomorrow, I’ll come find you again."

The Big White dog also looked at Mo Hua disdainfully, as if saying, "I’m waiting, come if you dare."

Mo Hua snorted coldly, then turned and left.

Returning to Disciple’s Residence, Mo Hua collapsed on a chair, looking dejected, feeling greatly defeated.

"How absurd, couldn’t beat a dog..."

He thought that with his Soul-Shocking Sword complete, once used, he could "dominate all," but ended up humiliated by a mere dog...

Strictly speaking, Mo Hua felt his Sword Dao Pupil Skill, in the confrontation, ought to slightly surpass this big dog, but a loss is a loss, can’t make excuses for himself.

"What exactly is this big dog’s background..."

Mo Hua frowned, but the more he thought, the more annoyed he became.

"No, no matter what its background, have to show it something."

Otherwise, his original creation, the fusion of Tai Xu Shocking God Sword Skill and Seven Souls Blood Prison Pupil Skill, wouldn’t it be a great embarrassment.

First battle failed, can’t even handle a dog.

Mo Hua rekindled his fighting spirit, determined to compete with this big dog.

"This Soul-Shocking Sword needs more practice..."

Mo Hua took out the Water Prison Forbidden Box and began visualizing again.

The Seven Souls Blood Prison transformed into the Seven Souls Sword Prison.

Ideally, to integrate Corpse Dog, Fushi, Spirit of Sparrow Shade, Swallow Thief, Non-Toxic, Remove Filth, Stinking Lung all Seven Souls into Sword Prison Heart Image, sensing each, activating the Divine Soul, then using both eyes as apertures, transforming into "Sword Intent," merging the Evil Qi Divine Soul as a means of slaughter.

There are eight in the Sword Prison, first integrate Seven Souls, then integrate Life Soul.

Previously, Mo Hua only engraved a Sword Prison Heart Image in the Divine Soul, integrated one soul of "Corpse Dog," beyond that, must integrate the other six souls, for this improved Soul-Shocking Sword to truly mature.

With Seven Souls integrated, then Life Soul, thus completing the construction of the Sword Prison, Sword Fiend unified, this Soul-Shocking Sword can be considered grandly successful.

Mo Hua’s mood gradually calmed down.

"Still early..."

Whether the Soul-Shocking Sword or Seven Souls Pupil Skill, countless profound secrets remain undiscovered, many deep mysteries to learn, not something solved just integrating one soul.

Currently, only learned the beginning.

Must continually refine, perfect the Soul-Shocking Sword!

Mo Hua cast aside all distractions, focused intently, continued delving deeper into Seven Souls Soul-Shocking Sword.

Using Life Soul as a foundation, building Seven Souls Sword Prison, forming the Soul-Shocking Sword technique.

"Corpse Dog Soul, has integrated into Heart Image, forming one Sword Prison, next soul is... Fushi Soul..."

"Visualize Forbidden Diagram, replace instrument with Sword Weapon, use sword instead of punishment..."

"Use sword in Heart Image, merge Heart Image with Divine Soul, lead Divine Soul to Soul Emergence..."

...

Mo Hua gradually comprehended, repeatedly practicing, the more he grasped, the more he could experience the profoundness and mystery inherent in the Soul-Shocking Sword Skill and Seven Souls Blood Prison Pupil Skill.

His Sword Prison of Fushi Soul also began taking shape bit by bit.

The pace was considerably slower.

It seemed that this Divine Thought Technique, inherently progresses slower as cultivation deepens.

Yet, even though slower, Mo Hua spent an entire night, still integrated half of it.
