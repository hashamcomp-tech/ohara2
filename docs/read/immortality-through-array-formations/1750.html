Chapter 1750: Chapter 885: Four Factions Shaken

Master Xia’s thoughts were somewhat sluggish, unable to regain composure for a long time.

Master Xia silently glanced at Master Xia, sighing inwardly.

Indeed, a person’s fortune and fate are hard to predict...

...

On the high platform of the families.

Wenren Wan couldn’t help but cover her mouth, her eyes filled with disbelief.

The Array Leader is... Mo Hua?!

Even though expectation is just expectation, when the anticipated scene becomes reality, it still feels unbelievable to her.

Yu Er didn’t care much about these details.

Of course, he didn’t know what the Array Discussion Conference meant, only feeling happy for Mo Hua, clapping joyfully:

"Brother Mo won, Brother Mo is first!"

"No one is as amazing as Brother Mo!"

The family heads of the Shang, Wenren, and Gu families, including all senior elders, were stunned, shaken to the core.

"He really... came first..."

Even if they were beaten to death, they wouldn’t have thought the Array Discussion Conference would end with this result.

But the Gu family was the first to feel joy.

Because many elders of the Gu family knew Mo Hua.

Some even treated him to meals, gave him small gifts, and chatted or had tea with him.

Recalling these moments now, they seemed gilded with a golden edge, shimmering brightly, making one feel proud...

This was the Array Leader.

Qianxue’s first!

Something to boast about!

Gu Shouyan, always silent and rigid, not quite understanding formations, couldn’t help but feel deeply comforted.

Their Gu family was the place Mo Hua freeloaded meals.

This was a great fortunate connection.

Shang Che also started to reflect inwardly on whether he had been impolite or too cold towards Mo Hua.

How should he subtly show favor in the future...

Wenren Jingxuan hesitated for a moment, his expression a bit odd, glancing at Shangguan Wang, he couldn’t help but confirm aloud:

"Are you saying... my daughter exerted connections and spent great favors to forcefully introduce the Array Leader, ranking first in Qianxue’s state boundary among thousands of sects, into Taixu Gate?"

"Are you saying, after Taixu Gate finds out about this, shouldn’t they treat my daughter as a highly esteemed guest?"

Shangguan Wang’s face flushed crimson, looking as ugly as a pork kidney.

...

In the Taoist Field of Array Competition, at the Hundred Flower Valley’s location.

Hua Qianqian looked at Mo Hua, covering her mouth, her beautiful eyes filled with shock.

Above, a Hundred Flower Valley Master, dressed in an elaborate robe, face icy and exceedingly beautiful, also gazed at Mo Hua’s figure with a dazed expression.

"This child seems to be..."

A moment later, a peculiar expression appeared on her exceedingly beautiful face.

On the other side, the elder of Ten Thousand Formations Sect had reddened eyes.

Now his heart was truly filled with jealousy, bitterly exclaiming:

"Pressure of the Four Sects, winning the first in formations, such an astonishing formation genius, why would he be in Taixu Gate? Our Ten Thousand Formations Sect, besides the Four Great Sects, ranks first in formations, he should have been our disciple!"

Beside him, the instructor of Ten Thousand Formations Sect hesitated for a moment, then softly said: "Originally he was..."

The elder of Ten Thousand Formations Sect froze, "What?"

The instructor whispered: "Elder, have you forgotten, a few years ago, a little disciple from another region wanted to join our Ten Thousand Formations Sect, had great formation talent, but poor Spiritual Root."

"Sect rules say no. But I later thought about it, felt it was a pity, and I confirmed with you again."

Afterward, he was scolded by the elder out of habit.

The instructor said: "You said back then, it’s hard to find a frog with only two legs, are two-legged Formation Masters rare? Rules are rules, a Second Grade Formation Master, one more or one less, has little impact, not worth breaking the rules..."

"The disciple rejected...," the instructor quietly pointed, "is this one here, the one who won first place, the youth from Taixu Gate..."

The elder of Ten Thousand Formations Sect’s expression changed dramatically, as if struck by lightning.

"I... rejected?"

A Qianxue first, Array Leader, once wanted to join his Ten Thousand Formations Sect, but... was rejected by him?

The elder of Ten Thousand Formations Sect’s eyes darkened, Blood Qi surged into his head, and his intestines turned green.

The bitterness from his stomach couldn’t help but rise.

...

Among the crowd, Elder Zheng also stood shocked and dazed.

Others may not understand, even typical elders of formations might not see clearly.

But he was different, he was once an elder of the Qian Taoist Sect, one of the Qianxue’s Four Great Sects, bearing the Zheng family’s Thunder Formation legacy, knows the terrifying strength Mo Hua displayed in this competition.

Divine Thought was unfathomable.

Formation Study was profound and erudite.

Super Divine Sense, exceeding by at least three ranks!

With a Foundation Building Middle Stage, overshadowing the Four Sects, even mastering Calculation, capable of comprehending Spiritual Power rules in an extremely short time...

If he hadn’t seen it with his own eyes, he wouldn’t dare imagine it.

This Evil Monster, was indeed the child he had previously seen, born from Loose Cultivation, with a steadfast Taoist Heart and grand vision...

Elder Zheng stood in place, stunned and dazed.

Simultaneously, a spark of hope lit up in his eyes, like a blazing thunderous fire...

...

With a burst of commotion, amidst countless exclamations and disbelief, the Array Discussion Conference ended completely.

Everything settled.

Foundation Building Middle Stage, the first in formations.

Overshadowing the Four Sects, winning the Leader position.

The name "Mo Hua" of Taixu Gate, resonating like the bell of Dao Discussion, completely shook the entire Qianxue state boundary.

For a moment, the winds and clouds shifted, four directions trembled.
