Chapter 2108: Chapter 998: Sect Taoist Soldiers 4

The flickering lights in the Disciple’s Residence.

Mo Hua, following the planned order, diligently studied and comprehended the several Ultimate Formations he currently possessed, over and over again.

Until he finished practicing the Reversed Spirit Formation and moved on to the Four Symbols Monster Pattern, transforming it back into the Dragon Pattern, Mo Hua paused, suddenly thinking of a problem:

Lifebound Formation Map!

Lately, he had been too occupied with the matter of the sword discussion, almost forgetting it.

His Lifebound Formation Map is still unresolved...

Mo Hua furrowed his brow.

He still remembered the message the Second Elder conveyed before being reduced to ashes:

Mr. Tu holds a statue of an Evil God, inside which hides the Ancient Wilderness Formation Map found by a remarkably talented Great Witch from the depths of the barbaric wilderness, pertaining to the Great Wilderness Lineage.

This Ancient Wilderness Formation Map is related to his Lifebound Formation Map...

"Mr. Tu, Evil God statue..."

But... where exactly is this Mr. Tu?

Where could the Evil God statue be hidden?

What would his Lifebound Formation Map be?

Mo Hua furrowed his brow, thinking for a long time without any clues.

"Forget it, I’ll deal with it after the sword discussion..."

"Right now, the sword discussion is the most important matter. After the sword discussion, there should still be some time to find someone to hunt down Mr. Tu, beat him up and kill him, then figure out a way to get the ’Evil God statue’..."

"If it’s really difficult, asking for help from the ancestors and Sect Leader might work, but who knows if the ancestors have the time..."

"Moreover, this concerns an Evil God, it’s very dangerous, must be cautious and careful..."

Mo Hua pondered silently in his heart, then temporarily set this matter aside, focusing on practicing formations, while also looking forward to the sword discussion conference tomorrow...

...

At an unknown place within the Qian Learning State Boundary.

In a bloody secret chamber.

Mr. Tu, covered in wounds, with a patchy Divine Soul, kneels on the ground.

The death of the Solitary Mountain Evil Embryo had subjected him to an unprecedented severe "Divine Punishment", both his physical body and Divine Soul were in extreme pain, as if plunged into an endless purgatory, ceaselessly.

Mr. Tu had become numb.

Vaguely, he sensed that time was running out for him.

Not only because of Taixu Gate but also within Taixu Gate, there lurks a fierce god watching intently...

And something even more terrifying...

"Lately, I’ve had a feeling, as if something, a pair of... dark, eerie eyes, were secretly watching me..."

"But I have no idea who he is..."

"Who could he be?"

"Has a terrible cultivator mighty entered the fray... or has he been part of the fray all along?"

"I don’t know..."

"I... don’t have time, I..."

Mr. Tu’s tone suddenly pauses, his eyes dim, revealing a bizarre depth.

After a moment, he slowly furrows his brows:

"Just now... what was I saying?"

"Am I faltering?"

"No, impossible."

"My loyalty to the Divine Lord is witnessed by heaven and earth. No one, no demon monster, no deity, can shake my devout faith."

"There’s nothing in this world truly fearworthy."

"As long as the Divine Lord revives, all wicked ghosts and monsters will be obliterated under the Divine Lord’s brilliance..."

"Yes, it’s true..."

Mr. Tu mutters with frenzy, then kneels on the ground, his back bloody, and the wounds on his Divine Soul faintly aching.

Yet in Mr. Tu’s heart, a pleasure arises.

Thunder and dew, all are divine grace.

This is the Divine Lord’s "blessing".

His pain is proof of the Divine Lord’s existence, a sign of the Divine Lord’s favor on him.

"The revival of Solitary Mountain failed, because not enough people died..."

"This time, it will definitely succeed..."

"As long as enough die, it is sure to succeed..."

"And Divine Lord you will eventually awaken, tasting the most delicious offerings in this world with your flesh..."

Mr. Tu kneels devoutly.

In his hand, a blood-soaked human skin is held, painted with Green Dragon Formation Patterns, flickering with a green and red sinister light.

And whom he worships, is a deity statue.

A huge, terrifying, chilling statue of an Evil God with a human face and goat horns and white bone.

Within this statue, hides a terrifying Evil God aura and an ancient secret unknown to anyone...
