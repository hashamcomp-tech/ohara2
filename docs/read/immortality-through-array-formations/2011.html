Chapter 2011: Chapter 966: My_3

In this way, the situation reversed again.

The situation was back in Mo Hua’s hands.

Young Master Xuan’s face turned pale instantly.

Mo Hua silently scrutinized him for a moment, contemplating whether to eliminate Young Master Xuan first.

This Young Master Xuan is cunning, with strange methods, and being a descendant of Xuan Sanren, the karmic threads suggest he could be dealt with first.

But after pondering for a while, he decided the Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation was most important.

Besides, there wasn’t much time...

"Help me restrain Shen Tuao, I’ll copy the Dragon Patterns on him..."

Mo Hua instructed Elder Bear, then turned around to see Young Master Xuan’s thoughts stirring, not knowing what trick he intended to play now, Mo Hua warned him:

"I won’t act against you for now, but don’t push your luck."

"After copying the Dragon Patterns, we leave. Whatever needs to be discussed, we’ll do it outside."

"Don’t forget, there’s still an Evil Embryo in this tomb, that’s the real beast."

The Second Elder used his own life to temporarily isolate the Evil Embryo’s perception, but only for an hour.

Now calculating, the remaining time is less than an hour.

"If you cause more trouble, delay time, and let the Evil Embryo awaken, everyone will die."

"A Third Grade peak Evil Embryo, even if your ancestor comes, it’s useless," Mo Hua said coldly.

Young Master Xuan looked displeased, but his heart was full of apprehension, and indeed dared not make further moves.

Then Elder Bear followed Mo Hua’s instructions to suppress Shen Tuao.

Shen Tuao was already heavily injured and near death, unable to resist.

But Mo Hua dared not take risks.

These Demon Cultivators, each one hiding schemes, who knows if he is truly heavily injured or just pretending.

Therefore, having Elder Bear suppress him was a precaution.

Xun Ziyou had been recuperating until now, his injuries had recovered somewhat, seeing this he propped himself with his sword, standing beside Mo Hua to prevent anyone from harming Mo Hua.

Mo Hua, after enduring countless hardships, finally laid eyes on the genuine Dragon Diagram.

On Shen Tuao’s back at this time, a whole body presented an ancient patterned, majestic, and ferocious green Dragon Diagram.

Gathering the strengths of myriad monsters, forming a dragon shape.

Using the power of the Dragon Vein, manifesting dragon energy.

Due to resonating with the Dragon Vein, cleansing Monster Qi, assimilating Monster Patterns, the Evil Dragon Formation thus "reformed", officially turning into the Great Wilderness’s orthodox Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation.

This was Mr. Tu’s painstaking "Myriad Monsters Raising Dragon" method.

Mo Hua’s Lifebound Formation Map finally found its destination.

Moreover, upon closer inspection, this Four Symbols Green Dragon Formation was clearly a Second Grade Twenty-One Pattern Ultimate Formation.

Great Wilderness Dragon Pattern, royal inheritance.

Still an Ultimate Formation!

Mo Hua’s eyes shone brightly, unable to contain excitement, immediately taking out a Jade Slip, ceaselessly focused on transcribing the Four Symbols Green Dragon Ultimate Formation.

Transcribing a Formation Diagram is no simple matter, requiring deep foundational knowledge of formations to truly restore the original diagram without error, and record it down.

Moreover, this is an Ultimate Formation, making it even more difficult.

Mo Hua’s eyes focused, Divine Thought as a pen, stroke by stroke.

The complete Four Symbols Green Dragon Ultimate Formation, pattern by pattern, without error, imprinted onto the Jade Slip in his hand.

But as he drew, a sense of foreboding gradually welled up in Mo Hua’s heart.

As if something sinister was slowly approaching him.

What is it...

Mo Hua furrowed his brows, heart pounding suddenly, alarm bells ringing as he looked up, losing color:

"Elder Xun, be careful!"

Xun Ziyou hadn’t sensed anything happening, seeing Mo Hua look up, not knowing what he saw, his face drastically changing, relying on years of experience, instinctively drew his longsword, slashing upward.

The sound of sword edge intersecting with bone echoed.

Xun Ziyou abruptly pulled Mo Hua behind him, then retreated several meters gracefully, only then raising his head to look.

At a glance, his pupils constricted involuntarily.

On the temple’s golden beam, without knowing when, hung a pale four-limbed creature, half-human-half-demon, with a distorted face, resembling a "Human Demon."

The others also noticed, their expressions changing drastically.

"What is this?!"

The pale human demon let out a chilling laugh, blood seeping out from its skin, transforming into a blood mist that spread around.

Mo Hua saw this blood mist, and his heart trembled, only then realizing that the blood mist used by Young Master Xuan to plant the devil in the Taoist heart was not entirely his own doing.

A portion of the blood mist was actually from this human demon.

This human demon had been in this great hall for a long time.

It covertly manipulated and lured everyone into a violent battle for the dragon vein.

"But... why did no one notice?"

Mo Hua frowned.

As the blood mist spread, the pale human demon gradually lost its trace, disappearing from everyone’s perception.

Even when divine sense swept across, there was nothing.

Not only Mo Hua, but the hearts of a group of Golden Core cultivators also filled with a haze of gloom.

This pale human demon not only looked terrifying but also exuded eeriness everywhere.

Everyone unleashed their divine senses, scanning the surroundings, fully on guard, yet still could not find the figure of that human demon.

At that moment, a flash of golden light flickered deep within Mo Hua’s eyes, and he immediately glimpsed a sly blood shadow.

"Elder Bear, behind you!"

Elder Bear Pi was instantly alert, and almost in an instant, his arm transformed into a python, swinging backhandedly.

The python seemed to bite onto something, but the skeleton and flesh were too hard to bite through.

Very quickly, the pale human demon broke free.

As it broke free, Mo Hua immediately sidestepped, dodging behind Xun Ziyou.

In just a breath, a deathly pale arm reached out from the blood shadow, grasping at where he had originally stood.

But Mo Hua, as if foreseeing this, dodged in advance.

This grasp caught nothing but air.

Xun Ziyou’s longsword immediately stabbed forward.

The sword edge struck the deathly pale arm, leaving a long blood stain. The monster let out a roar, leapt into the air, and disappeared again.

Mo Hua’s golden eyes, filled with majesty, scanned the surroundings, searching for the evil demon’s trace.

A moment later, Mo Hua pointed a finger, a fireball shot through the air, flying toward Fan Jin’s side.

Fan Jin quickly dodged, while Xun Ziyou coordinated, cleaving with a sword.

The fireball exploded, revealing a deformed human figure, and then sword Qi struck its body. The pale human demon staggered back several steps, its eyes blood-red, staring at Mo Hua fiercely, its voice tearing:

"Kid, what exactly are you?"

Why can you perceive its existence.

Why can you see through its attacks.

Mo Hua didn’t answer, instead began gathering the Fireball Technique at his fingertips again.

The pale human demon’s deformed face, seemingly pieced together from a corpse, grew increasingly ugly, but after a moment, the anger on its face disappeared, replaced with a sly smile.

The pale human demon slowly opened its mouth, sticking out a crimson tongue, which shot toward the distance like a blood spear.

Mo Hua immediately understood its intention, his forehead throbbing, he shouted:

"Stop it!"

Elder Bear Pi was puzzled, reacting a beat too slow.

Master Gu and Fan Jin didn’t have the strength to stop it.

But Elder Xun Ziyou listened to Mo Hua, acting first, though ultimately a bit too late, his sword Qi sliced through the air, splitting the blood spear in half.

Half of the blood spear was shredded by the sword Qi, the other half still flew forward.

It flew toward the Second Elder, clad in black robes, sacrificing his life to isolate the Evil Embryo’s aura, who was already deceased.

The Second Elder’s corpse was instantly pierced by the blood spear.

His flesh was corroded by the blood, polluting the dragon bones before him.

His sorcery was destroyed.

The Second Elder’s flesh was immediately sucked dry, withering away.

The white bone lock chain sealing the Evil Embryo turned to dust.

The remaining time instantly became zero.

An evil, intense, chaotic, gruesome, fearfully deep aura spread throughout the Divine Temple, filling every corner.

Mo Hua’s pupils trembled.

The Third Grade peak Evil Embryo had awakened...
