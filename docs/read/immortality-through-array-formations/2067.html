Chapter 2067: Chapter 985: God-slaying Soul Projection (2)

Although the power may diminish, only the strength fully controlled by oneself is one’s true skill.

With the exquisite Divine Thought into sword, if it can go beyond the sheath, half of its power is actually enough.

Remove the Taishang Emotion Cutting, remove half of the Tai Xu Sword Intent, the Dao integrated is halved, correspondingly reducing the load, making strikes faster, and improving actual combat effectiveness.

Among all magical techniques, only speed is unbeatable.

This was taught to me by Grandpa Gui a long time ago.

Better to be faster by a fraction, rather than stronger by an inch.

The problem with condensing the Godslaying Sword seems to be solved.

The next issue is the problem of its release.

To release it, the eyes must serve as the outlet, breaking the boundary between the Sea of Consciousness and the real world, only then can the Divine Thought into sword be fully released.

In a certain sense, this issue is more critical.

If at the crucial moment, the transmission of divine thought is blocked, the boundary between reality and illusion cannot be broken, failing to release the Sword of Divine Thought, then it’s over.

It’s like two cultivators fighting fiercely with swords.

If the opponent’s sword has already reached your neck, and your sword cannot leave its sheath, then you are doomed to die foolishly.

Mo Hua’s expression was grave.

He definitely doesn’t want to die "foolishly."

"I must find a way that can ensure the Divine Thought into sword leaves its sheath one hundred percent..."

However, releasing the spell point is not something Mo Hua can learn at his realm.

He has "exceeded the syllabus" too much, having no precedent to follow, he can only explore bit by bit, improving based on his own situation.

Mo Hua pondered for a moment, recalling some teachings from Patriarch Dugu in the past.

According to Taixu Gate’s most orthodox Divine Thought into sword True Jue, a "sword" is needed as a medium for the release.

Mo Hua is not an orthodox Sword Cultivator and does not possess this sword.

Therefore, he can only use his eyes as the medium.

But using the eyes as the outlet, Divine Thought into sword is evidently a more complex and profound spell point.

With Mo Hua’s current cultivation level and prowess in Sword Dao, it’s impossible to stably control it.

Instability easily leads to failure. Once failed, it spells doom.

"I need to add another medium to ensure stability..."

But what could be added?

He doesn’t possess his own Magical Treasure, not even nurturing an embryo.

Aside from the eyes, his physical body offers no other suitable medium...

Frowning, Mo Hua racked his brains for a long time, and finally, leveraging his profound and advanced knowledge of formations, he found some inspiration from the Five Elements Source Formation Law with twenty-three patterns of First Grade.

Spiritual Power!

The core of the Five Elements Source Formation is utilizing divine thought, through formation transformation, amplifying the power of the Five Elements, thereby enhancing the power of Five Elements class Taoist Skill.

This indicates that Spiritual Power and Telekinesis, seemingly two distinct Tao Cultivation energies, share a mutual origin.

Spiritual Power can integrate with divine thought.

To some extent, it means Spiritual Power can act as a medium for "divine thought."

This process is complex and surely involves certain Dao Laws, otherwise, the Five Elements Source Formation wouldn’t be the Sect Protecting Ultimate Formation of the Five Elements Sect.

But Mo Hua doesn’t need overly complex transformation.

He just needs to leverage his Spiritual Power to guide his Divine Sense.

Spiritual Power and Divine Sense are inherently his, possessing a certain affinity, making mobilization without mutual rejection.

"Spiritual Power guides Divine Sense..."

Mo Hua contemplated for a moment, suddenly startled, thinking about his cultivation technique—Heaven Yan Jue.

Practicing Heaven Yan Jue, during realm breakthrough, Spiritual Power always recedes to the Heavenly Gate, flows into the Sea of Consciousness, then contacts Divine Sense and constructs a Spirit Screen made up of Mystery Formation to serve as the bottleneck for breakthrough.

Does this imply...

The operation of Heaven Yan Jue involves the resonance of Spiritual Power and Telekinesis... or a certain fusion?

Mo Hua’s expression slightly changed, feeling quite shaken.

This touches upon the fusion of the most fundamental power of a cultivator.

The ancient methodology his master imparted indeed contains significant mysteries.

Unfortunately, his current cultivation is still shallow, only able to vaguely perceive the gist, yet unable to fathom the deeper essence of Heaven Yan Jue.

"Continue cultivating slowly, perhaps as cultivation heightens, wisdom deepens, I’ll gradually uncover the secrets of this ancient methodology..."

Mo Hua temporarily set aside the confusion regarding Heaven Yan Jue, continuing to diligently research the Divine Thought into sword release.

After pondering for a while, he had already thought out most issues, constructing a method based on his knowledge and experience.

Now it’s merely a matter of trying it out.

Mo Hua calmed his mind, dismissing all distractions, then his gaze gradually sharpened, his whole being like a treasured sword sheathed with hidden murderous intent.

In the Sea of Consciousness, Mo Hua raised both hands high.

The sword arrays of Gold Cutting, Mountain Opening, Gui Water, and Li Fire, relentlessly gathered between his hands.

A wisp of Tai Xu Sword Intent was also merged into it.

Because it only incorporated part of the Sword Dao, this Godslaying Sword formed very quickly.

After the Godslaying Sword formed, its sword intent was fierce, poised to strike.

Mo Hua’s eyes became increasingly radiant, sharp and glaring.

He pointed to his brow with two fingers.

A wisp of Spiritual Power connected to the Sea of Consciousness, gradually "guiding" the telekinetic force within his Sea of Consciousness.

Mo Hua could distinctly sense the boundary between reality and illusion.

Using the eyes as the outlet, Spiritual Power as the guide, Mo Hua’s Sword of Divine Thought began gradually breaking through the barrier between reality and illusion.

Mo Hua experienced all this.

Meticulously appreciating the flow and changes between Spiritual Power and Telekinesis within.

Also continuously pinpointing the path, direction, and target for his Godslaying Sword release, understanding the virtual-real transformation within Sword Dao.
