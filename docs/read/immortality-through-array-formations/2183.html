Chapter 2183: Chapter 1022: One Sword from Heaven (Part 2)

Cheng Mo raised his large axe, roared loudly, and swung it fiercely at Xiao Ruohan.

Ouyang Xuan also activated his movement technique, the Taia Sword flashed with a cold gleam, aiming straight for Xiao Ruohan’s heart.

Mo Hua acted quickly, tossing several fireballs over.

But his fireballs were blocked by a Golden Body Talisman, failing to interrupt Xiao Ruohan’s sword moves, causing only minor fire damage.

Linghu Xiao and Situ Jian’s Sword Qi were intercepted by two disciples of the Heavenly Sword Sect, each with a sword.

Cheng Mo and Ouyang Xuan were also entangled by two other disciples of the Heavenly Sword Sect, preventing them from getting close to Xiao Ruohan.

Mo Hua then shouted, "Xiaoxiao, strike him quickly!"

Linghu Xiao understood and immediately grasped a Golden Body Talisman, raised his Chongxu Sword, unleashing all his Spiritual Power, forming a beautiful but deadly Water Moon-colored Chongxu Sword Breath, with equally terrifying might facing Xiao Ruohan.

Both exhibiting Superior Sword Techniques.

Both were Sword Dao geniuses.

The two were evenly matched, hard to distinguish who had the upper hand at the moment.

The Heavenly Sword Sect disciples realized the danger and immediately shifted their focus to attack Linghu Xiao.

Situ Jian wielded the Li Fire Sword, charging forward.

In the clash of sword edges, after several rounds of exchange, the heritage of the Li Fire Sword couldn’t compare to the Heavenly Sword Technique. Situ Jian was caught in a bind, unable to defend.

Cheng Mo and Ouyang Xuan had no choice but to turn around and support Linghu Xiao.

The scene was extremely chaotic for a moment.

Sword Energy Skills clashed.

Disciples on both sides engaged in a deadly struggle.

While each fought for their lives, Xiao Ruohan’s sword moves had gathered enough power. Without hesitation, he instantly cleaved with his sword’s edge, a magnificent sword light, like a comet descending from the sky, One Sword from Heaven, aimed at the city gate.

Ouyang Xuan protected Linghu Xiao, unable to break free.

Cheng Mo and Situ Jian saw this and could only grit their teeth and charge at the sword light, using their lives to block.

But this deadly strike from the Nine Heavenly Sword Techniques was truly powerful.

The sword light surged forward, directly eliminating Cheng Mo and Situ Jian, then continuing forward, striking solidly at the city gate protected by a High-Rank Gold Stone Formation.

At the same time, witnessing Cheng Mo and Situ Jian’s sacrifice, Linghu Xiao’s heart burned with anger, his eyes icy cold. He then held nothing back, slicing at Xiao Ruohan with the chilling and sharp Chongxu Sword Qi like moonlight.

Xiao Ruohan’s sword move was unfinished, with nowhere to escape.

The other two disciples of the Heavenly Sword Sect immediately moved forward, activating Sword Qi, and forcibly took Linghu Xiao’s Chongxu killing move.

But Linghu Xiao’s strike was equally formidable.

One Heavenly Sword Sect disciple was slaughtered by the Sword Qi.

Another was severely wounded.

The remnants of the Sword Qi struck at Xiao Ruohan.

Both Sword Dao geniuses wielded Superior Sword Techniques and killing strikes, despite obstructions, managed to hit their targets.

Then the Sword Qi exploded violently.

Two thunderous boom sounds erupted, accompanied by heaven and earth spinning, earth and stone cracking, thick smoke rolling.

Amidst the smoke and dust, astonishing sword lights intertwined, dazzling and bright.

On the screen of Fang Tian, the air was filled with thick smoke, earth and stone, and sword light killing and illuminating the scene with flickering brightness.

Everyone held their breath, in tense anticipation.

They were eager to know, what was the outcome?

Was the city gate broken?

Was Xiao Ruohan dead?

Between the Heavenly Sword Sect and Taixu Gate, who was the winner?

In the midst of collective expectation, the smoke slowly dissipated, and the sword light gradually vanished.

On the screen of Fang Tian, clearly displayed was the result after the Superior Daoist Magic clash.

The city gate was broken.

Xiao Ruohan was not dead.

Xiao Ruohan had forcibly withstood one of Linghu Xiao’s Chongxu Sword Qi killing moves.

But the Chongxu Sword Qi was largely neutralized by the disciples of the Heavenly Sword Sect.

The remaining Sword Qi, like a dying wrecker, simply couldn’t kill him.

Meanwhile, after Xiao Ruohan’s Nine Heavenly Sword Techniques eliminated Cheng Mo and Situ Jian, it solidly struck the city gate.

Cheng Mo and Situ Jian’s cultivation was ultimately a step behind the disciples of the Heavenly Sword Sect.

Therefore, Xiao Ruohan’s Sword Qi, despite their utmost effort, only partially blocked it.

This was one reason.

Another reason was that the city gate was too large and immobile, thus like a "target," it fully absorbed Xiao Ruohan’s immensely powerful sword strike.

Not a bit of Sword Qi was wasted.

This was also the disadvantage of the defending side in a siege.

In a typical sword discussion, if you’re losing, you can run away.

But in a siege, with such a large city gate standing there, you can’t run or evade.

Having borne the brunt of Xiao Ruohan’s powerful sword, even with High-Rank Formation’s support, the city gate was still breached.

The Gold and Stone Formation shattered.

A large gap tore open on the city gate of the inner city.

"The city gate is broken!"

Cultivators outside the field exclaimed:

"One man, one sword, One Sword from Heaven!"

"Just this one sword, killed two people, broke the formation, opened the city gate!"

"The power of this sword is so strong, truly worthy of being a Sword Dao genius of the Four Great Sects!"

"In the face of adversity, save the crumbling situation single-handedly, this is truly a Sword Dao prodigy!"

"Xiao Ruohan, through this battle, will surely gain wide fame. Everyone will know that apart from Xiao Wuchen, the Heavenly Sword Sect also has Xiao Ruohan..."

"The Four Great Sects, truly are cradles of geniuses, making me envious of our sect..."

...

The outside cultivators were not stingy with their praise, discussing incessantly.

Inside the field, Mo Hua sighed.

He looked at the huge Sword Qi gap on the city wall, feeling a bit helpless:

"No doubt it’s the Qianxue Debate, these prodigies, each more fierce than the other, can’t underestimate them at all..."

"And no laziness can be afforded."

"Winning a round is really tough..."

If only two formations were drawn, then it would be truly over.
