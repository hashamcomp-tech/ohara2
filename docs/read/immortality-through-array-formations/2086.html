Chapter 2086: Chapter 991: Wood’s Rejuvenation (Part 3)

"Didn’t I say it already? I don’t want you to develop any thoughts of ’slaughter and victory seeking’... Moreover, the Heaven, Earth, and Man Three Talents Formation, the Taoist Court dares to offer, but do you dare to learn it?"

"That’s true..."

"I still find it somewhat strange..."

The disciples discussed spiritedly.

As they chatted, Cheng Mo looked towards Mo Hua and asked, "Junior Brother, your formations are impressive, do you know what formation this might be?"

Mo Hua furrowed his brows and shook his head, "The Wood Revitalization Formation, it sounds like a healing formation... But formations are profound, and many share the same name, without seeing the specific Formation Diagram, I can’t be certain."

"Oh..." Cheng Mo nodded.

After chatting for a while, everyone didn’t pay much attention to it.

In the sword competition’s rewards, the formation’s allure was the weakest for them.

Cultivation Techniques, Taoist Skills, treasured artifacts, Pills, etc., were all much more practical.

Besides, to gain first place in the sword competition, and receive this reward, was far too distant for them.

Though they had bursts of interest in discussing it, once that faded, they forgot about it.

Mo Hua, however, was still mulling over the matter of the formation in his mind.

He found it very peculiar.

"Wood Revitalization Formation"...

Wood, belongs to the category of the Five Elements, Revitalization, is the use of the formation.

Inferring from the formation principle, it should be a healing formation.

A healing formation... up until now, Mo Hua hasn’t really studied this type of formation much.

Killing Formation, Trapping Formation, Sword Array, Industry Formation... and others, he has delved into, but healing formations, he has almost never touched.

Healing formations are very rare, and the formation formats passed down are not many.

But this does not mean formations are difficult, rather most healing formations have been "phased out."

It’s not the formations that phased out healing formations, but Alchemy.

With the development of the Tao cultivation industry and the maturity of alchemical techniques, most of the injuries and diseases cultivators suffer can be treated directly with Spiritual Medicine and refined Pills.

Most cultivators also prefer to use Pills for healing, as they are not only cheap but also simple and quick.

If injured, just pop a Pill, why bother setting up a formation with effort?

Thus, the need for using formations for healing is very rare.

Healing formations, once had a specialized name, called "Medical Formations."

This is what Mo Hua read in some formation history books.

But as formations are no longer circulated, the name "Medical Formation" is rarely mentioned.

This is the part that intrigued Mo Hua.

As a reward for winning the Formation Competition, why choose such an obscure, outdated, and already "phased out" category of formations?

This question lingered in Mo Hua’s mind.

"Wood Revitalization" these four words seemed to possess a mysterious power, persistently tugging at Mo Hua’s heartstrings.

At night, Mo Hua sat at the table in the Disciple’s Residence, perusing formation books, yet he couldn’t help but think of this matter again.

"Wood Revitalization Formation... there must be something uncanny hidden within..."

"What could it be?"

Mo Hua pondered repeatedly, unable to understand it, yet couldn’t let it go, and eventually decided to calculate it.

Since this is something from the Dao Court Tian Shu Pavilion, he did not dare to delve deeply, just took out a Copper Coin, planning to "barely touch the edges" and glimpse the causality.

The aura of the Causality Copper Coin flowed.

Mo Hua silently recited "Wood Revitalization Formation," and in an instant, his heart shrank drastically.

Before his eyes, in a hazy vision, a graceful, scholarly, and celestial figure appeared.

This figure stood arrogantly between the heavens and earth, as sharp as a sword, exuding an invincible aura of arrogance.

Yet, upon turning around, all its edge was retracted, appearing gentle, with a slight smile in the eyes, looking at Mo Hua with reluctance.

Mo Hua could hardly believe it and was momentarily dazed.

"Master..."

Enveloped in a pale yellow memory, retracing from causality.

Mo Hua seemed to see the every moment in Tongxian City.

He saw his master smiling gently at him.

He saw himself waiting outside the bamboo house, waiting for his master to rest, to consult about formations.

He saw himself and his junior brothers and sisters doing their homework under the big locust tree, with master sitting in the bamboo house watching them from afar...

He also saw, during his travels, the days and nights spent enduring the elements, the people and events he encountered along the way.

He saw, in the Five Elements Sect, with the Demon sword suspended, and the sky covered with a bloody banner, the last meeting he had with his master before parting, and the earnest, reluctant exhortations...

The sour, bitter pain spread in his heart.

Mo Hua’s eyes watered.

A teardrop fell from his cheek.

After what seemed like an eternity, Mo Hua slowly came back to his senses, his gaze gradually brightening.

"The Wood Revitalization Formation... is connected with master’s causality..."

"Can this formation... save master?"

Mo Hua’s expression gradually firmed.

If that’s the case...

Then this formation must, at all costs, be obtained.

All obstacles in the way, must be flattened.

All enemies, even if they are the most outstanding talents among the Four Great Sects within the Qian Learning State Boundary, must all be cut down!

Mo Hua’s eyes glimmered with pure golden and resplendent sword light, exuding a terrifying edge.

"This formation will inevitably be mine!"

...

Meanwhile, far away in the Taoist State.

Heaven Shu Pavilion.

The drowsing Pavilion Elder suddenly awakened, opened his eyes, and saw the black game piece before him glowing with the utmost pure golden light, punctuated with dragon chants, feeling slightly relieved.

"Finally... hooked, have you..."

The Pavilion Elder murmured, then sighed.

"It seems to be... a big dragon still..."

The Pavilion Elder gazed at the black piece, observing the golden light on the piece, watching the dragon-like shape within.

As he watched, the Pavilion Elder’s expression changed slightly.

Inside the black piece, it seemed to be a dragon, but there was also a vague, savage aura trapping the dragon inside.

"This is not a dragon...?"

The Pavilion Elder paused, his gaze deepening, exuding an eerie aura.
