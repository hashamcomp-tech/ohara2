Chapter 1823: Chapter 906: The Manipulator (Part 2)

But just having information is not enough.

Setting up a plan like this can’t only rely on information; someone has to push it forward and implement it, otherwise it’s purely "talking on paper".

But he was sitting on Taixu Mountain, usually very busy, completely unable to extricate himself, and unable to take action personally.

Even if he personally took action, he was a Foundation Establishment, unable to do anything against a Golden Core.

"No ’pawns’..."

Mo Hua muttered with a frown.

He had been pondering this matter for several days.

Until this day during the rest period, Mo Hua was fiddling with the Thunder Magnetic Miniature when the Letter Token trembled slightly, bringing a message.

The message was from Gu Changhuai, saying he wanted to invite him to a delicious meal.

Mo Hua was a bit surprised and, after thinking for a moment, gladly accepted the invitation.

If you’re not enthusiastic about eating, there’s definitely something wrong with your brain.

And since Uncle Gu was inviting, it must be a big meal.

Mo Hua replied: "Okay!"

He fiddled with the Thunder Magnetic Miniature again that morning, studied the Formation for a while, and as it was nearing noon, Mo Hua got a little hungry, packed up his things, and got up to head to Tai Xu City.

Gu Changhuai, knowing that Mo Hua needed to cultivate, didn’t make him travel far, and chose the largest Food Building nearby in Tai Xu City for the banquet.

The name of the Food Building was Tai Xu Restaurant.

The restaurant was grand and magnificent, with an ancient and elegant style and was skilled in various ancient dishes.

Gu Changhuai, being wealthy and generous, ordered a whole table full of dishes.

When Mo Hua arrived, he found that Canonical Official Xia was also there.

He looked at Canonical Official Xia, then at Gu Changhuai, and asked in confusion: "Uncle Gu, Sister Xia, are you inviting me together? Is there a happy event?"

Canonical Official Xia paused.

Gu Changhuai grumbled, "What are you thinking? Just eat."

"Oh." Mo Hua nodded.

During the meal, Gu Changhuai and Canonical Official Xia barely touched their chopsticks, most of the time just watching Mo Hua eat.

Mo Hua, on the other hand, was not shy and enjoyed the meal heartily.

The food at Taixu Sect was also good, but it was mainly for disciples and not overly extravagant.

You get what you pay for with Spirit Stones, so it definitely wasn’t as good as specialized Food Buildings.

What’s more, someone else was treating him, so Mo Hua found the food especially delicious.

After eating for a while, with his cheeks puffed out, Mo Hua suddenly remembered something and asked Gu Changhuai:

"Uncle Gu, is there something you need me for since you’re inviting me to this meal?"

Gu Changhuai hesitated for a moment, not knowing how to start, while Canonical Official Xia hesitated briefly before asking:

"Mo Hua, was the message from the Letter Token sent by you?"

She didn’t specify what message, but everyone understood.

Mo Hua candidly replied, "I sent it."

Canonical Official Xia and Gu Changhuai were taken aback.

A moment later, Canonical Official Xia frowned and asked, "How did you know about the Demon Sect...?"

Mo Hua remained candid, "I can’t say."

"Then when the Demon Sect retreated later..."

Mo Hua still didn’t say, just shook his head.

Gu Changhuai and Canonical Official Xia couldn’t help but exchange a glance.

Mo Hua didn’t say it, but they had some idea in their hearts, yet they still found it quite incredible.

They couldn’t figure out how Mo Hua knew the Demon Sect’s plan.

Nor how he managed to confuse and "command" the Demon Sect, resolving this killing game...

But since Mo Hua didn’t mention certain secrets, they indeed couldn’t push too hard.

In any case, Mo Hua could be said to have saved their lives.

Canonical Official Xia raised her glass, her appearance dignified and graceful, and solemnly said, "For such a great favor that goes beyond words, Mo Hua, I toast to you."

Gu Changhuai said nothing, silently raising his glass as well.

Some things he didn’t say out loud but would remember silently in his heart.

Mo Hua laughed, "We’re all familiar with each other, no need to be formal."

However, he still raised his glass, clinking it with Gu Changhuai and Canonical Official Xia, acknowledging the sentiment.

Mo Hua wasn’t a pretentious person; having done a good deed and being appreciated by others made him happy.

After drinking, Mo Hua pondered for a moment, had a thought, and quietly said, "Uncle Gu, Sister Xia, do you want to deal with that Demon Sect?"

Gu Changhuai and Canonical Official Xia were taken aback by his words.

Canonical Official Xia nodded, "Naturally, it’s the Taoist Court’s duty to eradicate those Demon Cultivators completely!"

"That’s good..." Mo Hua whispered, "We’re all familiar with each other, so I won’t hide it from you; I indeed have a way and channels to obtain information on the Demon Sect. I can’t reveal the source, but it’s likely credible..."

After all, he really obtained the information from within the Demon Sect.

He knew more than most Demon Sect disciples.

Mo Hua "persuaded," "Sister Xia, if you and Uncle Gu can keep it secret and not let my identity slip, I’ll provide you the information, so you can eradicate the Demon Sect and get rid of the Demon Cultivators, eliminating harm for Qianxue State Boundary."

Canonical Official Xia was a bit tempted but puzzled, so she asked Mo Hua, "But if you do this, what will you gain?"

Mo Hua righteously replied:

"Heretical Demon, everyone should punish them!"

"I am a disciple of Taixu Sect, a descendant of the prestigious Eight Great Gates, and should uphold the Sect’s teachings, exuding righteousness, eradicating demons to protect the Dao, without seeking anything in return!"

Canonical Official Xia was taken aback by Mo Hua’s aura of righteousness.

She hadn’t expected Mo Hua, with his clean and gentle appearance, to have such firm bones, magnanimity, and hatred for evil.

Truly, one should not judge a book by its cover...

Only Gu Changhuai sighed silently on the side.

Influenced by Mo Hua, Canonical Official Xia resolutely said:

"Alright, you provide the information, we’ll deal with the Demon Cultivators, and we’ll definitely eradicate the Demon Sect that harms people with blood-sucking practices!"

Mo Hua couldn’t help but nod.

He then remembered something again, pretending to be in a difficult position, and said: "But, there’s a problem..."
