Chapter 2310: Chapter 1062: Heaven of Mysteries

Qian Learning State Boundary, Mount of Tao, Sword Observation Tower.

A group of aristocratic families and sects’ ancestors stayed in the main hall, from this highest observation tower on Mount of Tao, they discerned the situation, overseeing the entire scene, and constantly issued orders, commanding all forces of Qian Learning to suppress the Desolate Heaven Blood Sacrifice Formation.

But now, the situation has changed again.

The Blood Sacrifice Array, originally suppressed by various forces and slaughtered by the Taoist Court, had its spread trend curbed. It suddenly started to radiate brilliance again, carrying blood light and evil qi, continuing to spread around Qian Learning.

Many ancestors frowned slightly.

"The majority of the Evil Formation Masters have died or been injured; the Blood Sacrifice Array should have been on the verge of collapse, why has it revived?"

"Is there someone secretly aiding these evil demons again?"

"Who is it?"

Elder Master Xun pondered slightly, and his heart suddenly skipped a beat.

Then he concealed his hand in his sleeve, using it to pinch a Jue, calculating the cause and effect, his face remained unchanged but his heart gradually sank.

"Could it be... Mo Hua child is helping?"

Elder Master Xun’s eyelids twitched.

If that is true, then the situation is extremely severe.

No matter the reason or motive, helping the Demon Cultivators, constructing a Blood Sacrifice Array is an unforgivable crime.

Even as an ancestor, he could not save him.

This child!

Elder Master Xun’s face turned solemn, and his heart was also suspended.

Regarding this matter, most Heaven Void ancestors present couldn’t calculate and thus didn’t quickly guess it.

But there were some who knew slightly about the Heavenly secret skill, or although they didn’t understand Heavenly Mechanism Skill but were thoughtful Heaven Void ancestors who noticed something.

Though their faces remained unchanged and they said nothing, they looked at Elder Master Xun with veiled glances, containing a hint of schadenfreude.

This Mo Hua child is too prominent.

Not only is his talent in Formation considered monstrous, he also mastered a terrifying Forbidden Technique Taoist Skill almost similar to the Taixu Mind Transforming Sword True Jue. If left unchecked, they truly couldn’t know what kind of monster he would cultivate into.

Yet, they were all Heaven Void Ancestors, highly respected, and at least outwardly had to act according to the laws of the Taoist Court, unable to truly do as they please, and unlikely to face a junior in the Foundation Establishment Realm directly.

Furthermore, Mo Hua recently saved their sect and family’s direct descendants from the Blood Sacrifice Array, which is considered not a small "favor."

If they return kindness with enmity now, breaking this cause and effect, the consequences are unpredictable.

Looking at the current situation, it seems there is no need for their concern.

The brilliant righteous path disciple falls into the Demon’s Cave, associating with evil demons, it is certain that they will face innumerable enticements such as cultivation, fame, power, and women.

As long as they are a normal "person," they won’t be able to hold it.

Even if he resists, he cannot guarantee not making mistakes.

Now it seems, what Mo Hua did was more "excessive" than they thought, he dares to help these evil demons draw an Evil Path Great Formation.

Indeed, the ignorant are fearless, incredibly audacious.

But regarding this matter, a group of Heaven Void ancestors actually welcomed this outcome.

If Mo Hua became a Demon Cultivator, they could justifiably order to kill Mo Hua using the pretence of eliminating demons and protecting the Dao.

Righteous and Demonic do not coexist, no one can say anything.

Even if Mo Hua retained his conscience and was unwilling to associate with Demon Cultivators, his "crimes" are clear—an entire covering Blood Sacrifice Array, which cannot be argued, and he ultimately could not escape death.

At least his cultivation must be abolished and expelled from the sect.

In fact, they don’t need these Heaven Void ancestors to act personally, just borrowing the situation to let the Taoist Court "eliminate demons," or force the Taixu Gate to "clean their house."

In this way, the threat of Mo Hua is resolved.

And their hands remain clean.

Gradually, more and more Heaven Void ancestors understood this reasoning.

Naturally, more schadenfreude gaze fell on Elder Master Xun of Taixu Gate.

Of course, there were also some with complicated looks.

A genius appears, a genius is caught, a genius falls into the demon path, a genius perishes...

The Taixu Gate will face so many ups and downs in a short period, making people’s inner heart sigh.

The face of Elder Master Xun naturally grew heavier.

Such thoughts lingered in the minds of all the ancestors present.

Of course, regardless of Mo Hua’s outcome, they could not ignore the Blood Sacrifice Array.

"Send more manpower to kill the Formation Masters inside the Large Formation."

"All Cultivators around the Evil Path Formation, as well as those along the line, evacuate towards Qian Learning State Boundary, do not allow Demon Cultivators to continue killing people to nourish the evil qi of the formation."

"Seal the choke points of the formation, if any evil demons emerge, strike immediately..."

"Construct additional formations along the route, curb the formation’s trend..."

Of course, although these measures are effective, they are "treating the symptoms but not the root cause."

The Evil Path Great Formation is certainly tricky, but the most troublesome may be the terrifying existence being nurtured inside the formation.

All present Heaven Void ancestors, more or less, anticipated something.

"The girl from the Wenren family is protected, and also..." A Heaven Void Ancestor with a thin face and a celestial aura, spoke, "Has someone been sent to Mystery Valley to invite Old True Man Situ?"

Another Heaven Void Ancestor nodded, "Invited."

"When will they arrive?"

"In these few days."

The thin-faced ancestor nodded slightly.

They are Heaven Void, with high realms and strong cultivation, but it doesn’t mean they can master all aspects of Cultivation World.

The progress in cultivation alone requires devoting most of their time and effort.

Secular affairs of the sect and family also exhaust the mind.
