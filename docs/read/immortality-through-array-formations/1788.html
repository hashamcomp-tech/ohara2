Chapter 1788: Chapter 896: Supreme Emotion Cutting_2

Unable to eliminate the Evil Embryo, unable to convert calamity into Divine Marrow, unable to further Dao Hua the Divine Thought, it’s uncertain when I can break through the Foundation Establishment Late Stage...

Mo Hua felt slightly numb on his scalp, couldn’t help but sigh.

But merely sighing is useless, the Dao must still be cultivated, the Sword must still be practiced, and life must still go on.

"Next time I go to the Forbidden Land, I’ll consult the Ancestor again..."

Mo Hua forcibly put this matter aside, calmed his mind, and continued his cultivation lessons.

Two days passed like this.

Mo Hua got up in the morning, followed the routine of cultivation, and after finishing his training, he took out the bamboo slip as usual. Using the Sword Intent on the bamboo slip, he slashed at his own Life Soul, to suppress the Evil Embryo and refresh his mind.

This set of actions was entirely due to inertia.

But after slashing, Mo Hua suddenly realized that he no longer needed to slash.

The God-slaying Sword isn’t practiced like this.

It’s not about truly slashing the Life Soul.

Mo Hua sighed again, feeling somewhat agitated, then submerged his Divine Sense into the Sea of Consciousness to check the injuries on his Divine Soul.

Fortunately, his Divine Soul, having been slashed for so long, was accustomed to it. Though there were injuries, they weren’t too severe.

Mo Hua breathed a sigh of relief, then inwardly viewed with his Divine Sense, and glanced at his Divine Soul.

On the soul, sword marks remained, mottled like fish scales.

These were all Mo Hua’s own "masterpieces," left from daily self-slashing.

"Sometimes being too diligent seems not to be a good thing..."

Mo Hua muttered in his heart, and then intended to exit the Sea of Consciousness.

But just at this moment, Mo Hua caught a glimpse, suddenly froze.

He saw that on the wound of his Divine Soul, Sword Intent remained.

This was nothing unusual.

Using Tai Xu Sword Intent to slash his own Divine Soul, while leaving a wound, would naturally also leave some Sword Intent on the wound.

This was originally a normal thing.

However, looking at it now, Mo Hua felt his heart tremble violently, as if an astonishing thought slowly hovered in his mind...

"Using the Sword to quench the soul, self-slashing of the Life Soul... Slash the ego, observe the Sword Stream, comprehend the origin, integrate the Sword Dao..."

"Self-slashing of the Life Soul is a means; the ultimate result is to integrate the Sword Dao origin into the Divine Soul..."

"If that’s the case..."

Like a bolt from the blue, Mo Hua’s expression shook:

"Then using the Tai Xu Sword Intent to slash my own Divine Soul, doesn’t it mean... I’ve engraved the Sword Intent in the Divine Soul?"

"Sword Intent contains the Sword Dao origin."

"This also means, am I engraving the Sword Dao origin... into my Divine Soul through the sword marks and wounds?"

"Afterwards, as long as I thoroughly integrate this origin with the Divine Soul, won’t the final effect be the same?"

The difference is, the orthodox practice of the God-slaying Sword is to first cultivate the "ego" Sword Dao, then slash the ego, to comprehend the Sword Stream, integrate the Sword Dao.

While I skipped all the steps from cultivating the "ego" to comprehending the Sword Stream, directly reaching the integration of the Sword Dao!

The absence of the ego’s Sword Dao, doesn’t matter.

Not understanding the Sword Stream, has no impact.

The inability to comprehend the Sword Dao origin, doesn’t matter either.

All these steps are discarded, directly using Sword Intent to self-slash.

Sword Intent self-slashing engraves sword marks on the Divine Soul, leaving wounds.

Thus, the Sword Dao origin in the Tai Xu Sword Flow can naturally integrate with your Divine Soul along with the wounds!

This is a form of "cheating" even more thorough than "slashing the self to become a saint!"

And this "cheat," only I can do...

Others can’t withstand the slashing of Tai Xu Sword Intent.

But I can.

Others can’t repair the injuries of the Divine Soul.

But I can.

And the Tai Xu Sword Flow, I don’t need to comprehend it, as long as I integrate the Intent into my Divine Soul, I can directly use it.

The Ancestor himself said, "The gentleman’s nature is not different, he is adept at utilizing external things."

To seek the Great Dao with the method as a raft.

Having gained the Great Dao, discard the method and raft.

Form matters not.

Mo Hua’s heart beat rapidly.

Until now, he truly sensed that the gate to the secret of the God-slaying Sword was gradually opening to him.

With the decision made, Mo Hua immediately concentrated, visualizing himself.

With Divine Thought introspection, perceiving the wounds on the Divine Soul, while also perceiving the Sword Intent within the wounds.

The wounds were fragmented, with stabbing pains.

The Sword Intent was profound, hard to erase, within which the Sword Dao origin persistently circulated.

Mo Hua endured the severe pain, directly integrating the Sword Intent through the wounds into his Divine Soul.

This process was extremely painful, but Mo Hua gritted his teeth.

The ancient Sword Intent seeped into the wounds of the Divine Soul, bringing pain, but gradually merged with Mo Hua’s Divine Soul.

Like blades flowing into flesh, circulating internally.

But these "blades" also resembled ancient Immortal Pill; with each circulation, a strand of Sword Intent transformed, blending seamlessly with Mo Hua’s Divine Soul.

This was the ancient Sword Dao origin of Taixu Gate, after Dao Hua with Mo Hua, comparable to the deity’s Divine Soul fusion.

Mo Hua’s Divine Thought into sword True Jue also finally began to truly step into the Realm of "God-slaying."

Afterwards, only one sword needs to be slashed every day, tempering a portion of the origin, accumulating over time.

Integrating the Sword Dao origin into the flesh of the Divine Soul, tempering the God-slaying Sword.

Tempering until the true "God-slaying Sword" is born...

That day will also be the deadline for the Evil Embryo.

Mo Hua was mentally invigorated, eyes shining brightly.

Then according to the method he summarized, he tempered his Divine Soul for a while more, then temporarily stopped.

Because it was truly too painful.

Using Tai Xu Sword Intent to slash his own Divine Soul is painful enough.

Now having to integrate the Sword Intent fragments into the "flesh" of the Divine Soul, felt somewhat like "lingering death."

Therefore, it can only be done step by step, not too hastily.
