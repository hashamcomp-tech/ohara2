“So… does that mean I’ll never be able to use magic for the rest of my life…?”

Just because I couldn’t use magic didn’t mean I couldn’t survive, but I had lost one of the blessings I should have gained by coming to this world.

“Listen carefully. Like I said earlier, you can’t use most magic, but you should still be able to use magic itself.”

“Just what kind of environment did you grow up in…?”

“It’s a beginner’s magic book that I wrote. Try various things while looking at it.”

“Hey, Gramps. What kind of magic can I use?”

“It’s been a long time since I’ve seen one… A person with no aptitude…”

“It’s been since you, huh… A person like that.”

He reminisced about memories from over a thousand years ago.
