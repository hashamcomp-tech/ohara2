"You think I'll let you escape?"

From behind the fleeing pig, the blue-haired woman thrust her slender sword straight through his chest.

The tip of the sword emerged from the front of his body, right in front of my eyes. The pig coughed up a spray of blood from his mouth.

Immediately after, the blue-haired woman pulled the sword out of his body and flicked it once, shaking off the blood in a clean motion.

"Suffer as much as you can on your way to hell."

Even though the third pig had been stabbed through the heart, unlike the other two, he was still faintly breathing. His fallen body twitched irregularly, and his arms moved ever so slightly.
